Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Maria, thank you for scheduling this call! I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to chat with you. To start, can you tell me a bit about what prompted you to reach out to me?"
YOU:
"I appreciate you sharing that. It takes courage to acknowledge these challenges. What specifically resonated with you about my work when you saw my ad?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling overwhelmed. Can you describe what that looks like on a typical day?"
YOU:
"And how long have you been feeling this way? What impact is it having on your relationships and well-being?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about your current situation, what would it be?"
Phase 3: Presenting the Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Maria, what I'm hearing is that you're feeling overwhelmed, disconnected, and longing for more peace and presence in your life. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is to help you reconnect with your inner wisdom and develop practical tools for managing stress and cultivating self-compassion. We'll work together to identify what's truly important to you and create a life that aligns with your values. Many of my clients report feeling significantly calmer and more grounded within just a few weeks."
YOU:
"This involves a personalized 1:1 coaching program where we meet weekly for 60 minutes and you have access to me via email for support in between sessions."
Phase 4: Closing the Call 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this could be a good fit for you? ... Wonderful! Let's talk about the program details and how we can get started."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Welcome & Connection, Deep Dive into Challenges, Presenting the Solution, and Closing the Call.

2. How should you handle the objection, "I need to talk to my partner about it?"

Acknowledge their need to discuss it, ask what key points they want to cover with their partner, offer to provide information or answer questions, and suggest a brief call together.

3. What is a realistic monthly income potential with 5 new clients per month?

With a program fee of $1,500, 5 clients per month could generate $7,500 in monthly income.
