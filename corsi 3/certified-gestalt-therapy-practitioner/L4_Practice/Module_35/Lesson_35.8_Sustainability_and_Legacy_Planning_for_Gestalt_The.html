Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call close?

A clear summary of the client's needs, a confident presentation of your solution, and a direct call to action.

2. How can you address a client's concern about the cost of your program?

Acknowledge their concern, reframe the investment in terms of value and potential outcomes, and offer payment options if available.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful responses.
  • Present your pricing with conviction, highlighting the value you provide.
  • Close the call with a clear call to action, inviting the client to take the next step.
  • Remember that client acquisition is a skill that improves with practice.
