Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of using Gestalt therapy techniques in the initial stages of a coaching session?

The primary goal is to increase the client's self-awareness and understanding of their present experience, including their feelings, sensations, and thoughts.

2. Why is it important to avoid jumping to solutions too quickly when using Gestalt therapy?

Jumping to solutions before fully exploring the client's present experience can prevent them from gaining deeper insights and understanding of the root causes of their issues.

Key Takeaways

  • Focus on increasing the client's present awareness.
  • Validate their feelings and experiences.
  • Avoid rushing the process or jumping to solutions.
  • Use open-ended questions to encourage exploration.
  • Trust in the client's ability to find their own answers.
