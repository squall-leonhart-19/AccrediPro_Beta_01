The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a powerful 4-tier system, each building upon the last to create a comprehensive and deeply impactful learning experience:

  1. Tier 1: FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15): This is where we lay the groundwork. You'll delve into the core methodology, explore fundamental concepts, and build a solid understanding of conscious parenting principles.
  2. Tier 2: ADVANCED (Modules 16-23): Ready to go deeper? In this tier, we tackle more complex cases, explore clinical depth, and refine your coaching techniques to address a wider range of challenges.
  3. Tier 3: MASTER (Modules 24-29): Here, you'll hone your skills to train others and provide supervision. You'll learn to mentor aspiring coaches and elevate the field of conscious parenting.
  4. Tier 4: PRACTICE (Modules 30-36): It's time to put your knowledge into action! We'll guide you through building your business, attracting clients, and creating a thriving practice that aligns with your values and goals.
