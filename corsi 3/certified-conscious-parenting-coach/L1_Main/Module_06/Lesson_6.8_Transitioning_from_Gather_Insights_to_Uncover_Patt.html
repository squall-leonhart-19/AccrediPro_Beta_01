Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate a client's feelings before offering solutions?

Validating their feelings builds trust and rapport, making them more receptive to your guidance. It shows you understand their experience and are not just there to give advice.

2. What's a better alternative to judging a parent for their reliance on screens?

Acknowledge the challenges of modern parenting and offer alternative strategies that can help them reduce screen time while still meeting their needs.

Key Takeaways

  • Prioritize building rapport and trust before offering solutions.
  • Validate the client's feelings and acknowledge their challenges.
  • Focus on small, sustainable shifts that can bring more joy and connection.
  • Avoid judgment and create a safe space for open communication.
  • Empower parents to find their own solutions and celebrate their successes.
