Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Framework

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call! I'm so excited to connect with you. I know you found me on Instagram, but tell me, what specifically resonated with you and made you book this call?"
YOU:
"It sounds like you're juggling a lot right now. Before we dive in, can you tell me a bit more about what a typical day looks like for you?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"What are the biggest challenges you're facing as a working mom? What feels the most overwhelming?"
YOU:
"When you feel that parenting guilt, what thoughts or feelings come up for you? How does it affect your interactions with your children?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about your current situation, what would it be?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're feeling overwhelmed, guilty, and stretched too thin. Many of my clients come to me with similar challenges. Does that resonate with you?"
YOU:
"The Conscious Parenting Coaching program is designed to help working moms like you find more balance and joy in parenting. We'll work together to identify your core values, develop practical strategies for managing your time and energy, and cultivate a more mindful and present approach to parenting. My clients typically see significant improvements in their stress levels and family relationships within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"Specifically, we'll use the GUIDE Method to gather insights, uncover patterns, inspire awareness, develop strategies, and empower execution. This proven framework will help you create lasting change."
Phase 4: Invite to Enroll 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I truly believe I can help you create the fulfilling family life you desire. Are you ready to explore how we can work together?"

Handling Common Objections

Overcoming Objections with Confidence

"I need to think about it."
"I understand. It's a big decision. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the time commitment, the investment, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make the best choice for yourself and your family."
"It's too expensive."
"I hear you. Can I ask ‚Äì what value would you place on feeling less stressed, more present with your children, and more fulfilled as a working mom? What if we could create that within the next 12 weeks? What would that be worth to you?"
"I don't have the time."
"I understand that time is precious, especially as a working mom. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your busy schedule. We can work together to identify small, manageable steps that will make a big impact over time. Plus, think about the time you'll save by being more organized and efficient!"
"I'm not sure if coaching is right for me."
"That's a valid concern. Coaching isn't for everyone. However, if you're truly committed to making a change and willing to invest in yourself, I believe you'll see incredible results. I offer a satisfaction guarantee, so if you're not happy with the program, I'll refund your money."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's challenges, presenting your solution, and inviting them to enroll.

2. How can you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Focus on the value and ROI of the program, and ask the client what they would be willing to pay for the desired outcome.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is essential for building a thriving coaching practice.
