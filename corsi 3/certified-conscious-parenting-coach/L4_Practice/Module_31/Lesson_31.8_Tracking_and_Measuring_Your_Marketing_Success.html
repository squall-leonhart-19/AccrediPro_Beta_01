Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I'm Emma. I help women just like you rediscover their passion and purpose. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me on Facebook. What caught your eye about my ad?"
Phase 2: Understand Her Needs 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about what's going on in your life right now. What's been challenging since the kids left home?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to find that spark again?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your life look like six months from now?"
Phase 3: Position Your Program 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're at a crossroads and ready for a new chapter. Many of my clients in your situation feel the same way. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My Conscious Reinvention Program is designed to help you clarify your values, identify your passions, and create a concrete plan to pursue them. We work together over 12 weeks, with weekly one-on-one coaching sessions, personalized exercises, and a supportive community. We use the GUIDE method, which you learned in the course. Many of my clients start feeling a renewed sense of purpose within just a few weeks."
Phase 4: Close the Deal 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for? ... Great! Let me tell you about the program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections Like a Pro

Navigating Common Concerns

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand your concern. The program requires about 3-4 hours per week, including our session. But think about it this way: what if investing that time now gave you back hours of wasted time and energy from feeling unfulfilled? We can also work together to find creative ways to fit it into your schedule."
"I'm worried about the cost."
"I appreciate that. Many of my clients initially feel that way. But let's look at the potential ROI. What's the cost of staying stuck? What's the value of rediscovering your passion and creating a life you love? We also have payment plan options to make it more manageable."
"I need to think about it and talk to my family."
"Of course. I want you to feel 100% confident. What specific questions do you have for your family? Perhaps I can provide some information to help with that conversation, or we could even schedule a brief call together."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can guarantee my commitment to providing you with the tools, support, and guidance you need. My clients who fully engage with the program consistently report significant positive changes in their lives. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee; if you're not happy after the first two weeks, you'll receive a full refund."

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is essential for building a successful coaching practice.
  • A well-structured discovery call helps you connect with potential clients and understand their needs.
  • Confidently handling objections builds trust and demonstrates your value.
  • Clearly presenting your pricing and call-to-action increases enrollment rates.
