Feeling overwhelmed, constant power struggles with her 6-year-old, unsure how to set boundaries effectively.

"I'm willing to invest in my family, but I need to see a clear return. I've tried other things that didn't work."
Decision Style
Needs to feel understood and supported. Values practical strategies and a personalized approach.
Her Goal
"I want to create a more peaceful and cooperative home environment. I want to enjoy being a mom again."

Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Rapport (5 minutes) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you so much for scheduling this call. I'm Emma, and I'm so glad we connected. To start, could you tell me what prompted you to reach out to a parenting coach?"
YOU:
"I understand. It takes courage to seek support, and I commend you for taking this step. So, tell me a bit about what's been happening at home."
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Challenges (10 minutes) 10 min
YOU:
"Can you give me a specific example of a recent power struggle you experienced with your child? What happened, and how did you react?"
YOU:
"And what have you already tried to address these challenges? What felt helpful, and what didn't make a difference?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, what impact will this have on your family in the long term? Conversely, if you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about your parenting, what would it be?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Unique Approach (10 minutes) 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're feeling [validate her feelings] and struggling with [summarize her key challenges]. Is that a fair assessment?"
YOU:
"My conscious parenting program takes a different approach. We focus on understanding the 'why' behind your child's behavior, building connection, and teaching cooperation rather than just compliance. It's a 12-week journey where we work together to create a personalized plan that aligns with your values and your child's needs. For example, Sarah, a client of mine, was in a similar situation, and after only 6 weeks, she reported a 70% reduction in daily arguments."
Phase 4: Closing the Call (5 minutes) 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with you? Do you see how this could be a good fit for your family? ... Wonderful! Let's talk about the program details and how we can get started."

Handling Objections with Grace

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I need to discuss it with my partner."
"That's perfectly understandable. To make that conversation easier, I can send you a detailed program overview, including testimonials from other parents who have gone through similar situations. Would that be helpful? Or, perhaps we could schedule a brief call where both you and your partner can ask questions."
"I'm not sure if I have the time for this right now."
"I appreciate your honesty. Many of my clients are busy parents. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your existing routine. We can schedule sessions at times that work best for you, and the strategies we implement are designed to save you time and energy in the long run by reducing conflict and increasing cooperation. Think of it as an investment in your family's well-being and your own peace of mind."
"It's more expensive than I anticipated."
"I understand that the investment is a consideration. However, let's look at the value you're receiving. This isn't just about quick fixes; it's about creating lasting change in your family dynamics. Think about the cost of continuing down the current path ‚Äì the stress, the arguments, the impact on your relationship with your child. My program offers personalized support, proven strategies, and ongoing guidance to help you create a more harmonious and fulfilling family life. Many of my clients find that the investment pays for itself in terms of reduced stress, improved communication, and a stronger family bond."
"I've tried other parenting programs, and they didn't work."
"I'm sorry to hear that. It sounds frustrating. Conscious parenting is different. It is not about time-outs or reward charts. It is about understanding the root cause of the behavior and meeting the child where they are. We will create a personalized plan for your family. What have you tried in the past?"

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call helps you effectively assess a prospect's needs and determine if your services are a good fit.
  • Confidence in presenting your pricing is essential for attracting clients who value your expertise.
  • Handling objections with empathy and providing valuable information can turn potential concerns into commitments.
  • Realistic income goals and success stories can motivate you to build a thriving conscious parenting coaching practice.
