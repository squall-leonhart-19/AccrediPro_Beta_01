Handling Objections

Common Questions & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. The program requires about 3 hours per week, including the group session. Think of it as an investment in *yourself*. Can you carve out that time to prioritize your well-being and future happiness?"
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you participate fully in the program and don't feel you're getting value, I'll offer a full refund. I'm confident in the program's effectiveness, and I want you to feel secure in your decision."
"I'm worried about the group dynamic."
"I carefully curate the group to ensure a supportive and positive environment. I also facilitate the sessions to encourage open communication and respect. Many women find the group connection to be one of the most valuable aspects of the program."
"It's more than I was expecting to spend."
"I understand that it's a significant investment. Let's break it down. This program includes 8 weeks of group coaching, personalized exercises, and access to a supportive community. Many of my clients say the value they receive far exceeds the financial investment, in terms of increased confidence, clarity, and renewed purpose. What is gaining that clarity and excitement about your life worth to you?"

Case Study: Maria, 48

Maria, a former teacher, felt unfulfilled after retiring. She joined a "Rediscover Your Passion" group program led by a certified conscious parenting coach. Through the program, Maria reconnected with her love of art and started teaching painting classes to seniors. This not only brought her joy but also generated an income of $2,000 per month.

Case Study: Emily, 55

Emily, a stay-at-home mom for many years, felt lost when her children left for college. She enrolled in a "Purposeful Living" workshop and discovered her passion for helping other women navigate the empty nest phase. She launched her own coaching practice, specializing in this niche, and now earns $5,000 per month running group programs and workshops.

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call for filling a group program?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's needs, presenting the program as a solution, and confidently inviting them to enroll.

2. How can you effectively address budget concerns during a discovery call?

Acknowledge their concerns, highlight the value and potential ROI of the program, and offer payment options or early bird discounts.

Key Takeaways

  • Group programs offer a leveraged way to serve more clients and increase your income.
  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for attracting the right participants.
  • Address objections with empathy and confidence, highlighting the value of your program.
  • Practice presenting your pricing clearly and confidently.
  • Remember, you're not just selling a program; you're offering a transformation.
