1. How does the ACCEPT Framework‚Ñ¢ help in this scenario?

The ACCEPT Framework‚Ñ¢ provides a structured approach to understanding and addressing the client's challenges by Acknowledging their experience, Cultivating curiosity about their behaviors, and Empowering them with effective strategies.

2. Why is it important to Cultivate Curiosity before Empowering a client with strategies?

Cultivating curiosity helps to uncover the underlying needs and motivations behind the client's behaviors. This ensures that the strategies are tailored to address the root causes, rather than just the symptoms.
