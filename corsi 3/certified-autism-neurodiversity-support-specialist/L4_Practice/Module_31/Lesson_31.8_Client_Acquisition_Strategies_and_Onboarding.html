Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the initial phase of a discovery call?

Key elements include expressing gratitude for their time, asking about their background and how they found you, and actively listening to their initial responses to build a connection.

2. How should you handle the objection: "I need to ask my partner"?

Acknowledge their need to consult with their partner and offer support by providing information they can share or scheduling a call where their partner can ask questions directly.

Key Takeaways

  • Prepare a structured discovery call script to ensure a smooth and effective conversation.
  • Practice handling common objections with confident and empathetic responses.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and its potential return on investment.
  • Confidently present your pricing and offer flexible payment options.
  • Always end with a clear call-to-action, inviting the prospect to take the next step.
