Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Context 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan! Thanks for connecting after the workshop. What resonated with you most from the session?"
YOU:
"I'm glad you found it helpful. So, tell me a bit about what prompted you to book this call. What's been going on for you at work?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling overwhelmed. Can you give me a specific example of when that happens?"
YOU:
"What strategies have you tried so far to manage your ADHD at work? What's worked, and what hasn't?"
YOU:
"And what's the impact of these challenges on your career and your overall well-being?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution & Value 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're dealing with [summarize her key pain points]. Is that a fair assessment?"
YOU:
"The approach I use is designed to help you develop personalized strategies for managing your ADHD symptoms in the workplace. Over 12 weeks, we'll work together to identify your strengths, create systems for focus and organization, and build your confidence. Clients typically report a 20-30% increase in productivity and a significant reduction in stress within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"I've helped other professionals, like Sarah, a 48-year-old marketing manager, who, after our work together, received a promotion and now thrives in her role. It's all about finding what works for you."
Phase 4: Close the Sale 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with you and address the challenges you're facing? ... Great! Let me share the details of the program and how we can move forward."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand your concern about time. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. We'll have one 60-minute session per week, and I'll provide you with tools and strategies you can implement in just a few minutes each day. Think of it as an investment that will ultimately save you time and increase your productivity."
"I'm worried it won't work for me. I've tried so many things already."
"I hear your frustration. What sets my approach apart is that it's highly personalized and evidence-based. We'll start by identifying your unique strengths and challenges, and then create a plan that's tailored to your specific needs. Plus, I offer ongoing support and accountability to help you stay on track. Many of my clients, like Maria, a 45-year-old teacher, felt the same way before starting, but now rave about their transformations."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I'm confident that you'll see significant improvement if you fully participate in the program and implement the strategies we develop together. I have a proven track record of helping clients achieve their goals, and I'm committed to providing you with the support and guidance you need to succeed. A 2023 study in the 'Journal of Attention Disorders' showed that personalized coaching programs led to a 40% improvement in focus and task completion for adults with ADHD."
"I need to discuss this with my partner."
"That's perfectly understandable. Would you like me to provide you with some information you can share with them? Perhaps a program overview and some testimonials from past clients? I'm also happy to schedule a brief call with both of you to answer any questions they may have."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport & Context, Deep Dive into the Problem, Present Your Solution & Value, and Close the Sale.

2. What is a good strategy for handling the "I need to think about it" objection?

A good strategy is to ask clarifying questions to uncover the specific concerns the prospect has, such as the investment, the time commitment, or something else. This allows you to address their concerns directly.

3. What is the importance of practicing your pricing presentation?

Practicing your pricing presentation helps you deliver your price with confidence, making it more likely that the prospect will perceive the value of your services and move forward with the sale.
