Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. As a fellow healthcare professional, I understand the demands you face. How has your week been?"
YOU:
"I appreciate you reaching out. What specifically prompted you to seek herbal support at this time?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the insomnia. How long has it been going on, and what have you tried to address it?"
YOU:
"What impact is the burnout and lack of sleep having on your professional and personal life?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal health situation look like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're experiencing significant burnout and insomnia. My approach focuses on addressing the root causes of these issues through personalized herbal protocols and lifestyle adjustments. Does that resonate with you?"
YOU:
"I offer a 12-week program that includes comprehensive assessments, customized herbal formulations, and ongoing support. This approach is different because we're not just masking symptoms; we're restoring balance to your system."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, I believe my program can provide the support you need. Are you interested in learning more about how we can work together?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand that time is a precious commodity. My program is designed to fit into your busy schedule. We can schedule sessions at times that work best for you, and the herbal protocols can be easily incorporated into your daily routine. Think of it as an investment in your long-term well-being."
"I'm already taking supplements. How is this different?"
"That's a great question. My approach is different because it's highly personalized. I don't just recommend generic supplements; I create custom herbal formulations based on your specific needs and imbalances. We also focus on addressing the underlying causes of your symptoms, rather than just masking them."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can assure you that I'm committed to providing you with the highest level of care and support. Most of my clients experience significant improvements in their symptoms and overall well-being within the 12-week program. I encourage you to review the testimonials on my website to see what others have experienced."
"I need to research more about herbal medicine."
"That's a very smart approach. I can provide you with some reputable resources and research articles that support the use of herbal medicine for your specific concerns. Would you like me to email those to you after our call?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call, and what is the approximate time allocation for each?

The four phases are: 1) Build Rapport (5 min), 2) Understand the Problem (10 min), 3) Present Your Approach (10 min), and 4) Close (5 min).

2. What is a good strategy for handling the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need for time, then ask probing questions to understand the specific concerns they need to consider (e.g., investment, time commitment). Offer to provide additional information or schedule a follow-up call to address their concerns.

Key Takeaways

  • Master the Discovery Call: A well-structured discovery call is crucial for qualifying prospects and showcasing your expertise.
  • Handle Objections with Confidence: Prepare for common objections and develop thoughtful responses that address client concerns.
  • Present Your Value: Clearly communicate the value of your program and how it addresses the client's specific needs.
  • Practice Your Pitch: The more you practice, the more confident and natural you'll become in presenting your pricing and call-to-action.
  • Believe in Your Potential: With consistent effort and a focused client acquisition strategy, you can achieve your income goals.
