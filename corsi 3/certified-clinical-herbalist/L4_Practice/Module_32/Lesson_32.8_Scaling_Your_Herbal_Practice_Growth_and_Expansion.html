Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Outline

Phase 1: Welcome & Build Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call! I'm excited to learn more about you and how I can help. To start, what prompted you to reach out?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me on Instagram. What caught your eye?"
Phase 2: Explore Her Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the anxiety and sleep issues. How long has this been going on?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to manage these symptoms? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1-10, how much are these issues impacting your daily life? What would it be like to drop that number down by 5 points?"
Phase 3: Present Your Herbal Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're dealing with a combination of stress, hormonal changes, and sleep disruption. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach focuses on addressing the root causes of these imbalances using herbal medicine, lifestyle adjustments, and nutritional support. I create a personalized plan for you, and we work together over [duration of program, e.g., 12 weeks] to achieve lasting results."
YOU:
"For anxiety, we might use herbs like Skullcap or Lemon Balm. For sleep, Valerian or Passionflower. And for hormonal support, herbs like Vitex can be very effective. But it's all tailored to your unique needs."
Phase 4: Transition to Offering & Close 5 min
YOU:
"Does this approach resonate with you, Susan? Is it something you could see yourself trying?"
YOU:
"Great! Then let me tell you more about my [program name] program and how we can get you started."

Handling Objections Like a Pro

Navigating Common Concerns

"I'm not sure if herbal medicine really works."
"I understand your skepticism. Many of my clients felt the same way initially. But herbal medicine has been used for centuries, and there's growing scientific evidence supporting its effectiveness. I am a Certified Clinical Herbalist and stay up-to-date on the latest research. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you don't see results within [timeframe], we can adjust the plan or offer a refund."
"I don't have time for another program."
"I hear you. Time is precious. My program is designed to be manageable and fit into your busy life. The consultations are [duration] long, and the herbal regimen can be easily incorporated into your daily routine. Plus, think of the time you'll save by finally addressing these issues and feeling more energized and focused."
"I'm worried about side effects."
"That's a valid concern. While herbal medicine is generally safe, it's important to work with a qualified practitioner like myself. I carefully assess your health history and potential interactions with any medications you're taking. We start with low doses and gradually increase as needed, monitoring your response closely."
"Can I just try a single consultation first?"
"I understand wanting to start small. The best results come from the comprehensive program because it allows us to do a deep dive into your health and create a long-term plan. However, I do offer a limited number of single consultations per month at a rate of $[price]. If, after that consultation, you decide to join the full program, I'll credit the consultation fee towards the total program cost."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problems, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive?"

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value of your program in terms of the long-term benefits and the cost of *not* solving their problem.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script regularly to build confidence.
  • Prepare confident responses to common objections.
  • Clearly communicate the value and benefits of your herbal programs.
  • Remember, you're not just selling a service; you're offering transformation.
