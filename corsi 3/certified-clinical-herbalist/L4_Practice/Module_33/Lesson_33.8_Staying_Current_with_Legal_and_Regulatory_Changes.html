Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I'm Sage, and I'm excited to learn more about how I can help you. To make the best use of our time, let's quickly cover what we'll do today. First, I'd love to hear more about what you're experiencing. Then, I'll share how my program works and how it's helped others. Finally, if it feels like a good fit, we can discuss next steps. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"Great! So, tell me, what prompted you to reach out about your digestive health?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned bloating and gas. Can you describe when these symptoms started and what seems to trigger them?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? Did anything provide temporary relief?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the worst, how much do these digestive issues impact your quality of life?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and resolve this, what would your life look like? What activities would you enjoy again?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're experiencing significant discomfort and frustration. What I do is different. Instead of just masking the symptoms, I use a functional approach to identify the root causes of your digestive issues. We'll look at your diet, lifestyle, and even run targeted lab tests to understand what's really going on."
YOU:
"My program is a 12-week personalized journey. We'll create a tailored plan that includes dietary changes, herbal remedies, and lifestyle adjustments to restore balance to your gut. Many clients experience significant relief within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"For example, I recently worked with a client, Sarah, who had similar issues. After 12 weeks, she reported a 70% reduction in bloating and was able to enjoy social events without fear. Does this approach resonate with you?"
Phase 4: Close the Sale 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I'm confident I can help you achieve your goals. Are you ready to take the next step and learn more about the program details?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for a 12-week program."
"I understand. Time is valuable. The program is designed to be flexible. We can schedule sessions around your availability, and the dietary and lifestyle changes are gradual and sustainable. Think of it as investing in your long-term health and well-being. How much time are you currently spending feeling unwell?"
"I've tried so many things already, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. That's why I take a different approach. We're not just treating symptoms; we're identifying the root causes through targeted testing and personalized recommendations. This isn't a one-size-fits-all solution. It's a tailored plan designed specifically for you. What if this is the thing that finally works?"
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes because everyone responds differently, I can promise you my full commitment to your success. I'll provide you with the tools, knowledge, and support you need to achieve your goals. And, based on my experience with other clients facing similar challenges, I'm confident you'll see significant improvements if you follow the plan."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What aspects are you unsure about? Perhaps I can provide more information or clarify any concerns you have. Is it the investment, the commitment, or something else?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value by focusing on the long-term benefits and potential ROI.

3. What's a good strategy for presenting your pricing with confidence?

Clearly state the price, highlight the inclusions, and offer flexible payment options. Practice your script until it feels natural.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call process increases your chances of converting prospects into clients.
  • Handling objections effectively is crucial for overcoming sales barriers.
  • Confidently presenting your pricing reflects the value you provide.
  • Focus on the long-term benefits and transformation your program offers.
  • Consistent practice builds confidence and improves your client acquisition skills.
