Upselling Strategy: Ongoing Wellness Program

Your 15-Minute Upsell Conversation

Phase 1: Acknowledge Progress & Reiterate Goals 3 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah! It's wonderful to connect again. I'm so thrilled to see the progress you've made during our initial program. You've truly embraced the herbal approach. As we wrap up, I wanted to discuss how we can maintain this momentum and support your long-term wellness goals."
YOU:
"Remember when you first came to me, you were struggling with [mention specific issue]? Now you're [mention specific positive outcome]. What are your biggest takeaways from the program?"
Phase 2: Introduce the Ongoing Wellness Program 5 min
YOU:
"Many of my clients find that ongoing support is crucial for sustaining their results and optimizing their health as life changes. That's why I've created an Ongoing Wellness Program. It's designed to provide continuous personalized support, proactive adjustments to your herbal protocol, and ongoing education to empower you on your wellness journey."
YOU:
"The program includes [list key features, e.g., monthly consultations, personalized herbal adjustments, access to exclusive content, priority email support]. It's all about staying ahead of the curve and ensuring you continue to thrive."
Phase 3: Address Concerns & Present Value 5 min
YOU:
"Sarah, I understand that investing in ongoing support is a decision. But think about the time and money you've already invested in your health. This program is designed to protect that investment and maximize your long-term well-being. We'll proactively address any challenges that arise and fine-tune your herbal protocol as needed."
YOU:
"Imagine continuing to feel this good, or even better, a year from now. No more slipping back into old patterns. Just consistent progress and vibrant health. Does that sound appealing?"
Phase 4: Close 2 min
YOU:
"So Sarah, based on your success so far and your desire for long-term wellness, I truly believe the Ongoing Wellness Program is the perfect next step for you. Are you ready to continue this journey together?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I think I can manage on my own now."
"I admire your confidence, Sarah. And you absolutely have the tools to manage your health. However, life throws curveballs. This program is like having an insurance policy for your wellness. We can proactively adjust your protocol as needed, ensuring you stay on track no matter what."
"I'm not sure I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Let's look at the value proposition. How much would it cost you to slip back into your old patterns? Doctor visits, supplements that don't work, lost productivity? This program is an investment in your long-term health and well-being, which ultimately saves you money. We also have a payment plan available to make it more accessible."
"What if I don't see results?"
"Sarah, you've already experienced significant results during our initial program. This program is designed to maintain and optimize those results. We'll continuously monitor your progress and make adjustments as needed. Plus, you have my ongoing support and expertise to guide you every step of the way. I'm confident we can continue to achieve your wellness goals."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision. I'm happy to answer any further questions you may have."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of an effective upselling strategy for existing clients?

Acknowledging past successes, reiterating client goals, presenting a high-value offer, addressing concerns, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How can you address the objection that a client "can manage on their own now"?

Acknowledge their capabilities but emphasize the value of proactive support, personalized adjustments, and ongoing expertise to navigate life's curveballs and maintain long-term wellness.

Key Takeaways

  • Upselling existing clients is a highly effective strategy for scaling your practice.
  • Focus on providing ongoing value and personalized support to maintain client engagement.
  • Address client concerns with empathy and confidence, showcasing the long-term benefits of your services .
  • Present your pricing confidently and highlight the return on investment for your clients.
  • By offering continuous support, you foster client loyalty and build a sustainable, thriving practice.
