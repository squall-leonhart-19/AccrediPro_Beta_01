Your Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Call Structure & Key Dialogue

Phase 1: Welcome & Rapport (5 min)
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sage, and I'm excited to learn more about how I can help you. To start, what prompted you to reach out to me?"
YOU:
"I appreciate you sharing that. It takes courage to acknowledge these challenges. I see you're in [city/state]. What's the weather like there today?" (Casual connection)
Phase 2: Problem Deep Dive (10 min)
YOU:
"You mentioned hormonal imbalance. Can you tell me more about the specific symptoms you're experiencing, like the hot flashes, anxiety and insomnia? When did they start, and how are they impacting your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? Any medications, supplements, or lifestyle changes?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being completely debilitating, how would you rate the impact of these symptoms on your overall quality of life?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution (10 min)
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I understand you're dealing with hormonal imbalance symptoms that are significantly impacting your daily life. You're looking for a solution that addresses the root cause and provides lasting relief. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because I focus on personalized herbal protocols that support your body's natural ability to heal. We'll work together to identify the underlying factors contributing to your hormonal imbalance. My program typically involves a 3-month commitment with weekly consultations, customized herbal blends, and lifestyle recommendations. Many of my clients experience noticeable improvements within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"Does this approach resonate with you, Susan?"
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps (5 min)
YOU:
"Great! I'm confident I can help you regain your balance and feel like yourself again. Would you like to learn more about my 3-month program and how we can get started?"
YOU:
"Wonderful. Let me share the details of the program, including the investment and what's included..."

Handling Common Objections

Overcoming Hesitations with Confidence

"I need to think about it."
"I understand. Making a decision about your health is important. What specific aspects do you want to consider further? Perhaps I can provide more clarity on those points."
"It's more expensive than I expected."
"I appreciate your honesty. Let's break down the value you receive in my program. It includes personalized herbal blends, one-on-one consultations, and continuous support. Think of it as an investment in your long-term well-being, rather than a quick fix. How does that reframe it for you?"
"I'm not sure if I have the time commitment."
"I understand that time is valuable. The weekly consultations are designed to be efficient and focused on your specific needs. Most of my clients find that even with busy schedules, prioritizing their health for a few hours a week makes a significant difference in their overall energy and productivity. How can we tailor the program to fit your lifestyle?"
"Will this really work for me? I've tried so many things."
"That's a valid concern, Susan. What sets my approach apart is the personalized nature of the herbal protocols. We'll delve deep into your unique situation, identify root causes, and create a customized plan. While I can't guarantee results, my clients have experienced significant improvements in their hormonal balance and overall well-being. I'm confident that we can find a solution that works for you. Are you willing to give it a try?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your solution, and closing the call with a clear call-to-action.

2. How should you handle the objection, "It's too expensive?"

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the investment by highlighting the value they'll receive, such as personalized herbal blends, consultations, and support. You can also offer payment plans.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script until it feels natural.
  • Address objections with empathy and confidence, highlighting the value you provide.
  • Clearly communicate your pricing and offer payment options.
  • Believe in the value of your services and the positive impact you can have on your clients' lives.
