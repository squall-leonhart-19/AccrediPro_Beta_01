You Belong Here (The "Am I Too Old?" Section)

Let's address the elephant in the room. I know some of you might be thinking, "Am I too old to start something new?" or "Do I have the right experience?" The answer is a resounding NO, you are not too old, and YES, you have exactly the right experience. In fact, your life experience is your superpower.

The field of sex practice needs empathetic, wise, and experienced practitioners. Your years of navigating relationships, overcoming challenges, and learning about yourself are invaluable assets. Don't underestimate the power of your lived experience. A 2022 study in the Journal of Sexual Medicine found that clients often prefer practitioners who have a diverse range of life experiences, as they are perceived as more relatable and understanding.

This program is designed for women of all ages and backgrounds. We have students who are recent college graduates, stay-at-home moms looking for a new purpose, and seasoned professionals seeking a career change. What unites them all is a passion for sexual wellness and a desire to make a difference.
