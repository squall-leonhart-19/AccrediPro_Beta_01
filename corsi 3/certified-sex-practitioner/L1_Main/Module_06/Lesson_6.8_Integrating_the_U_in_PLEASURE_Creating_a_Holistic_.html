Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to start uncovering potential blocks. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for being so open. It sounds incredibly frustrating to feel like you're repeating the same patterns. Before we talk tactics, let's explore what beliefs might be driving these choices. I often find that our unconscious beliefs have a bigger impact than we realize."
MARIA:
"I guess I haven't really thought about my beliefs. I just thought I was unlucky."
YOU:
"That's a common feeling. Let's try a little exercise. Complete this sentence for me: 'Men who are loving and supportive are usually...'"
MARIA:
"(Pauses) 'Men who are loving and supportive are usually...boring.'"
YOU:
"Thank you for sharing that honestly, Maria. Where do you think that belief comes from?"
MARIA:
"I don't know...maybe from watching my parents? My dad was reliable but not very exciting. I think I unconsciously equated excitement with someone who wasn't dependable."
YOU:
"That's incredibly insightful. So, let's reframe that belief. What if loving and supportive could also be exciting in different ways? What would that look like for you?"
