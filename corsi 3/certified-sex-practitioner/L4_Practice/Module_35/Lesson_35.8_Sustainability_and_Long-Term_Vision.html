Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm so glad you reached out. To start, can you tell me a bit about what led you to book this consultation?"
YOU:
"I appreciate you sharing that. I work with many women in similar situations. It takes courage to address these issues. Before we dive deeper, is now still a good time for you? Do you have any distractions?"
Phase 2: Understanding the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned low libido and feeling disconnected. Can you elaborate on that? How long has this been going on, and how is it impacting your relationship?"
YOU:
"Have you tried anything to address this already? What were the results?"
YOU:
"I understand. Many women feel lost and confused by this. If things continue as they are, what's the long-term impact on your life and relationship? What would your life look like if you could wave a magic wand and restore your desire and connection?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, what I'm hearing is that you're experiencing a significant decline in libido and connection, which is causing distress in your relationship. You've tried [mention what she's tried] without success, and you're longing for a solution to restore intimacy and desire. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is holistic and personalized. We address the root causes of low libido, considering hormonal imbalances, emotional factors, and relationship dynamics. I offer a 12-week program that includes weekly coaching sessions, personalized exercises, and resources to help you reconnect with your body and your partner. Many of my clients report significant improvements in desire and intimacy within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"I want to share a quick story. I had a client, let's call her Mary, 52, who was in a similar situation...[tell a brief success story about a past client]."
Phase 4: The Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I genuinely believe my program can help you reignite your desire and reconnect with your husband. Does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"It's a big investment. I need to think about it."
"I understand. It's important to consider the financial aspect. When you say 'think about it,' what specifically are you weighing? Is it the cost, the time commitment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make the best decision for yourself and your relationship."
"I'm not sure if it will work for me. I've tried so many things already."
"That's a valid concern, Susan. I understand your hesitation. My program is different because we focus on personalized solutions. We don't just offer generic advice; we identify the root causes of your specific challenges and create a tailored plan to address them. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you don't see any improvement within the first 30 days, I'll refund your investment. How does that sound?"
"I need to discuss it with my husband."
"That's perfectly reasonable. I encourage you to have that conversation. Would it be helpful if I sent you a detailed program overview that you can share with him? Or, I'd be happy to schedule a brief call with both of you to answer any questions he may have. What would be most helpful for you both?"
"I don't have the time right now."
"I understand that time is precious. However, what I find is that my clients are able to find the time for things that are important to them. If this program can help you reconnect with your husband, would that be worth finding the time for? Also, my program is very flexible. We can work around your schedule."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the initial minutes of a discovery call?

Express genuine interest, actively listen, acknowledge their feelings, and find common ground.

2. How can you address a prospect's concern about the cost of your program without immediately lowering your price?

Focus on the value and ROI of the program, share success stories, and offer payment plans or financing options.
