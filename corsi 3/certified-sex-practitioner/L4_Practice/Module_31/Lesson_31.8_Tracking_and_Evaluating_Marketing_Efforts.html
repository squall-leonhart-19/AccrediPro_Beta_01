Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, it's so great to finally connect! Thank you for scheduling this call. I understand [Friend's Name] referred you. How do you know her?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! So, tell me a little bit about what brought you here today. What are you hoping to achieve?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive & Uncovering Needs (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling unfulfilled. Can you elaborate on that? What areas of your life feel most affected?"
YOU:
"And what have you already tried to address these feelings? What worked, what didn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal life look like in six months?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Sarah, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're seeking a deeper connection with yourself, renewed confidence, and a more fulfilling intimate life. Do I have that right?"
YOU:
"My approach is holistic, focusing on mind-body connection, emotional well-being, and practical techniques to enhance intimacy. I work with clients over 12 weeks to create personalized plans that address their unique needs. Many of my clients, like you, are women in their 40s and 50s who are ready to rediscover their sexuality and confidence."
Phase 4: Closing & Next Steps (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Sarah, does this approach resonate with you? Do you see how this could help you achieve your goals?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me briefly explain the program and how we can move forward."

Handling Common Objections

Mastering Objection Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this right now."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy women. That‚Äôs why the program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. We can tailor the sessions to your schedule, and you'll have access to resources you can use at your own pace. Plus, think about the time you're currently spending feeling unfulfilled. What if we could reclaim that time and energy?"
"It's a lot of money. I need to think about it."
"I appreciate that. Investing in yourself is a big decision. Can I ask, what's the biggest hesitation? Is it the financial investment itself, or are you unsure if this is the right fit for you? Perhaps we can explore a payment plan to make it more manageable. And consider this: what's the cost of *not* addressing these issues? What will it cost you in terms of your happiness, your relationships, and your overall well-being?"
"I've tried so many things before, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. It sounds like you've been through a lot. My approach is different because it's highly personalized and focuses on the root causes, not just the symptoms. We'll work together to create a plan that's tailored specifically to your needs and goals. Plus, you'll have ongoing support and guidance throughout the process. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee - if you are not satisfied after the first month, I will refund your money."
"Can I get back to you next week?"
"Of course! I understand you need time to consider. What specific questions do you have that I can answer now to help you make a decision? Also, my schedule tends to fill up quickly, so I can't guarantee availability next week. However, if you're seriously considering this, I can offer you a limited-time bonus if you enroll within the next 48 hours."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of an effective discovery call?

The four phases are: Connection & Rapport, Deep Dive & Uncovering Needs, Presenting Your Solution, and Closing & Next Steps.

2. What is a good strategy for handling the "It's too expensive" objection?

A good strategy is to acknowledge their concern, explore payment plan options, and highlight the value and long-term benefits of your program, focusing on the cost of *not* addressing the underlying issues.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key. Know your prospect, your script, and your pricing.
  • Focus on connection and understanding your client's needs.
  • Address objections with confidence and empathy.
  • Believe in your value and present your pricing with conviction.
  • Consistent effort leads to sustainable income growth.
