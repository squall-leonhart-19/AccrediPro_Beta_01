The Importance of Self-Care

Supervision should emphasize the importance of self-care for both supervisors and supervisees. Burnout is a significant risk in sex therapy, so prioritize:

  • Boundary Setting: Establish clear boundaries to prevent emotional exhaustion.
  • Stress Management: Practice relaxation techniques and mindfulness.
  • Peer Support: Engage in regular consultation with colleagues to process challenging cases. A 2023 study in "Professional Psychology: Research and Practice" found that therapists who engaged in regular peer consultation reported 30% lower rates of burnout.
