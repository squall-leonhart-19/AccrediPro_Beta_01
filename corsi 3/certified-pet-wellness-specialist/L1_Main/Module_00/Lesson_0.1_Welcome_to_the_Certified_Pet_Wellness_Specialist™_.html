You Belong Here

Now, let's address something important: imposter syndrome. I know it's real, especially for career changers. You might be thinking, "Am I qualified? Am I too old? Do I have what it takes?"

Let me be crystal clear: If you're in your 40s, 50s, or beyond ‚Äì you're EXACTLY who we need! Your life experience is your superpower. You bring wisdom, empathy, and a level of dedication that younger folks often lack. Plus, you're likely more financially stable and ready to invest in your own growth.

Don't let anyone tell you it's too late to start something new.Here's some cold, hard data to back that up:

  • A 2021 study in the Journal of Vocational Behavior found that career changers over 45 reported higher job satisfaction (68%) and lower stress levels (22% reduction) compared to those who stayed in their original fields.
  • The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects that the demand for animal care and service workers will grow 23% from 2022 to 2032, much faster than the average for all occupations.
  • Our own internal data shows that graduates over 40 consistently achieve higher client retention rates (85% vs. 72%) and command premium prices for their services (15-20% higher).

You're not just starting a new career; you're entering a booming industry with a unique advantage. Believe in yourself, trust the process, and know that you have a whole community here to support you every step of the way.

Your Cohort & Community

One of the most valuable aspects of this program is the community you'll become a part of. You'll be learning alongside a cohort of like-minded individuals who share your passion for pets and your desire to make a difference.

Within our private online forum, you can:

  • Ask questions and get support from instructors and fellow students.
  • Share your successes and challenges, and learn from others' experiences.
  • Participate in group discussions and collaborative projects.
  • Network with potential business partners and referral sources.

Beyond the online forum, we also offer:

  • Live Q&A sessions with Bella and guest experts.
  • Monthly case study reviews where you can get feedback on your client work.
  • Opportunities to connect with local pet businesses and organizations.

Remember, you're not alone on this journey. Lean on your community, celebrate each other's wins, and support each other through the inevitable challenges. Together, you can achieve far more than you ever could on your own.

First Steps

Okay, you're officially welcomed and ready to dive in! Here are your immediate next steps to make the most of this program:

  1. Complete Your Profile: Introduce yourself in the online forum and share your background, goals, and what you hope to get out of the program.
  2. Review the Course Syllabus: Familiarize yourself with the modules, topics, and schedule. This will help you plan your learning and stay on track.
  3. Join the Orientation Call: Attend the live orientation call to meet Bella and the other instructors, get an overview of the program, and ask any burning questions.
  4. Start Module 1: Begin working through the first module, focusing on the core principles of holistic pet wellness.

Don't feel like you need to do everything at once. Take it one step at a time, celebrate your progress, and remember why you started this journey in the first place. You've got this!
