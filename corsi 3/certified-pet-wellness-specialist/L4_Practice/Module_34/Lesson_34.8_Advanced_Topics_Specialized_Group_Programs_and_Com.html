Enrollment Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Recap 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah! It's great to connect with you again after the Metabolism Reset workshop. What was your biggest takeaway from the workshop?"
YOU:
"Wonderful! I'm so glad you found it valuable. Today, we'll explore how the group program can help you build on those insights and achieve your goals. Sound good?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Needs 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned wanting to lose weight and increase your energy. Can you tell me more about the challenges you've faced in achieving those goals?"
YOU:
"And what does preventing diabetes mean to you personally? How would your life be different if you knew you were significantly reducing your risk?"
YOU:
"What have you tried in the past, and what were the results? What worked, what didn't?"
Phase 3: Program Overview & Benefits 10 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, Sarah, the 'Metabolism Makeover' group program is designed to address those exact challenges. It's a 10-week program where we dive deep into personalized nutrition, mindful movement, and stress management techniques."
YOU:
"Unlike generic diets, we focus on YOUR unique metabolic needs. Plus, the group setting provides incredible support and accountability. 85% of our participants report significant weight loss and increased energy within the first 6 weeks, and 70% have improved blood sugar levels."
YOU:
"We also have weekly Q&A sessions and a private online community where you can connect with other members, share your successes, and get your questions answered."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"So Sarah, based on what we've discussed, do you see how this program could help you achieve your weight loss, energy, and diabetes prevention goals?"
YOU:
"Wonderful! The program starts next Monday. Let's talk about the investment and how you can secure your spot."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I can commit to the time."
"I understand. The program requires about 3-4 hours per week, including the group sessions and personal practice. However, think about the time you're currently spending worrying about your weight and health. This program is an investment in reclaiming that time and energy. Plus, the sessions are recorded, so you can catch up if you miss one."
"I'm worried I won't be able to keep up with the group."
"That's a common concern, Sarah. But our program is designed for all levels. We provide modifications and personalized support to ensure everyone can participate comfortably. And remember, the group is there to support and encourage you, not to judge. Many of our participants form lasting friendships."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, absolutely! We offer a payment plan of three monthly installments to make it more accessible. Would you like me to walk you through those options?"
"I need to think about it."
"I understand completely. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can provide some additional information or address any remaining concerns you have right now."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key benefits of offering a payment plan for your group program?

Offering a payment plan can make your program more accessible to clients on a budget, increasing enrollment rates. It also demonstrates your understanding and empathy towards their financial situation.

2. How can you effectively address a prospect's concern that they won't have enough time for the group program?

Acknowledge their concern, then highlight the time-saving benefits of the program compared to their current struggles. Emphasize the flexibility of recorded sessions and the long-term value of improved health and energy.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your enrollment call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful, empathetic responses.
  • Clearly communicate the value and benefits of your group program.
  • Confidently present your pricing and payment options.
  • Remember, enrolling clients is about helping them achieve their health goals.
