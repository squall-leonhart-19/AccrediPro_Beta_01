Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call! I'm so glad you saw my ad. To start, can you tell me what resonated with you about the ad and why you decided to reach out?"
YOU:
"That's great to hear. I work with many women in their 40s and 50s who are experiencing similar challenges. Before we dive deeper, is now still a good time for you to chat?"
Phase 2: Uncover Pain Points & Goals 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, what are the biggest frustrations you're currently facing with your hormone health? How are these symptoms impacting your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to manage these symptoms? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and completely resolve these hormone issues, what would your life look like in 6 months? How would you feel?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution & Expertise 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her key pain points]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach focuses on identifying the root causes of hormone imbalances through comprehensive testing and personalized wellness plans. I use a combination of [mention your modalities, e.g., nutrition, lifestyle modifications, targeted supplementation]. Many of my clients experience significant improvements in their symptoms within the first few weeks. For example, I recently worked with a client, Maria, 48, who was experiencing severe night sweats and insomnia. After 8 weeks in my program, she reported a 90% reduction in night sweats and was finally sleeping through the night."
YOU:

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time for this."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The program requires approximately 3-4 hours per week, including our weekly sessions and implementing the personalized plan. However, think about the time you're currently spending dealing with these symptoms ‚Äì the doctor's appointments, the sleepless nights, the lack of energy. This program is designed to help you reclaim that time and energy."
"I need to research this more."
"Of course! What specific information are you looking for? I'm happy to provide case studies, testimonials, or research articles that support my approach. I can also send you my free guide on [Relevant Topic] to help you get started."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific results (as everyone's body responds differently), I can guarantee that I'll provide you with the tools, knowledge, and support you need to achieve your goals. I have a proven track record of helping women like you regain control of their hormone health. I'm confident that if you commit to the program and follow the plan, you'll see significant improvements."
"I've tried everything, and nothing seems to work."
"I hear your frustration. It sounds like you've been through a lot. What makes my approach different is that we focus on identifying and addressing the root causes of your hormone imbalances, rather than just masking the symptoms. We use comprehensive testing to get a clear picture of what's going on inside your body, and then we create a personalized plan that's tailored to your unique needs."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: (1) Rapport & Connection, (2) Uncover Pain Points & Goals, (3) Present Your Solution & Expertise, and (4) Close with Confidence.

2. What is one effective way to respond to the objection "I need to think about it"?

A good response is: "I completely understand. This is an important decision. Can I ask ‚Äì what specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need."

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is essential for building a thriving practice.
  • A well-structured discovery call can significantly increase your conversion rate.
  • Handling objections with empathy and confidence is crucial for closing deals.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your services and the ROI for your clients.
  • Believe in yourself and your ability to help others achieve their wellness goals.
