Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Michelle, thank you for sharing all of that. It sounds incredibly frustrating to feel like your body is working against you. I want to assure you that these symptoms are common, especially during this phase of life, and there are definitely steps we can take to bring you back into balance."
MICHELLE:
"I've tried a few things my doctor suggested, but nothing seems to be working. I'm starting to lose hope."
YOU:
"I understand. Many conventional approaches focus on symptom management, but we're going to dig deeper to address the root causes. Based on what you've told me, I'd like to explore the connection between your hormones, your gut health, and your body's natural detoxification processes. How does that sound?"
MICHELLE:
"That makes sense. I've heard those things are connected, but I don't really understand how."
YOU:
"Great. Let's start by talking about your diet and lifestyle in a bit more detail. I'm going to ask some specific questions, and as we go, I'll explain how each factor impacts your hormones, gut, and detox pathways. Sound good?"
MICHELLE:
"Yes, that sounds perfect. I'm ready to get started."

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate Michelle's feelings before diving into solutions?

Validating her feelings builds trust and rapport, making her more receptive to your recommendations. It also acknowledges the real impact of her symptoms on her life.

2. What are the three key areas you want to explore with Michelle, and why are they important?

Hormones, gut health, and detoxification. These three areas are interconnected and play a crucial role in Michelle's symptoms. Addressing them holistically is key to achieving lasting results.

3. What is a better way to say "enhance phase 2 liver detoxification" to a client like Michelle?

"Supporting your liver's detox process" or "Helping your liver clear out toxins more effectively."

Key Takeaways

  • Always validate the client's feelings and experiences before offering solutions.
  • Focus on identifying and addressing the root causes of the client's symptoms.
  • Speak in plain language that the client can easily understand.
  • Acknowledge the emotional component of the client's symptoms and offer support.
  • Emphasize the interconnectedness of hormones, gut health, and detoxification.
