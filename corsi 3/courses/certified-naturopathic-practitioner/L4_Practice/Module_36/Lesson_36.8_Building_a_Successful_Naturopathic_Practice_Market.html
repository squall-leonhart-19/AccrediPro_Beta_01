Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for joining me! I'm Sarah. I appreciate you taking the time after attending my workshop. What resonated with you the most?"
YOU:
"That's great to hear! So, tell me a bit more about what prompted you to book this call. What's been going on for you lately?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned hot flashes and insomnia. How frequently are you experiencing those, and how are they impacting your day-to-day life?"
YOU:
"And what have you already tried to address these symptoms? What worked, what didn't, and for how long?"
YOU:
"If these symptoms continue, where do you see yourself in six months or a year? And if you could magically eliminate these challenges, what would your life look like and feel like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, from what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing significant hormonal imbalances that are disrupting your sleep, mood, and overall well-being. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because we focus on identifying the root causes of these imbalances, rather than just masking the symptoms. Over 12 weeks, we'll use targeted testing, personalized nutrition, and lifestyle adjustments to rebalance your hormones naturally. Most of my clients report a significant improvement in their symptoms within the first month."
YOU:
"I also provide ongoing support and resources to help you maintain these results long-term."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this approach aligns with what you're looking for? ... Wonderful! Let me share the details of the program and how we can get you started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The program is designed to be flexible, with weekly sessions that fit into your schedule, and the lifestyle changes we implement are gradual and sustainable. Think of it as investing in your long-term health and well-being, which will ultimately give you more time and energy."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can tell you that I have a strong track record of success with clients who are committed to the program. We'll closely monitor your progress, make adjustments as needed, and I'll provide you with all the support you need to succeed. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee [explain details if applicable]."
"I need to talk to my family about it."
"Of course. I'm happy to provide you with more information to share with them. Would it be helpful to schedule a brief call with them to answer any questions they might have? Or, I can send over a detailed brochure outlining the program and its benefits."
"I've tried so many things before without success."
"I hear your frustration. It sounds like you've been through a lot. What makes my approach different is that we don't just treat symptoms; we dig deep to identify the underlying causes of your hormone imbalances. We use comprehensive testing and personalized protocols tailored to your unique needs. I also provide ongoing support and accountability to help you stay on track and achieve lasting results. This isn't just another quick fix; it's a holistic approach to restoring your health and vitality."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of building rapport during a discovery call?

Building rapport involves active listening, showing empathy, finding common ground, and making a genuine connection with the prospect.

2. How can you effectively address the objection that the program is "too expensive?"

Address cost concerns by emphasizing the long-term value, potential savings on medications, the program's unique approach, and the benefits to their overall quality of life.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare compelling responses.
  • Present your pricing with confidence and emphasize the value of your program.
  • Focus on building genuine connections with your prospects and understanding their needs.
  • Remember that consistent effort and persistence are essential for client acquisition.
