Maria Rodriguez

48 years old

  • V: Maria valued feeling energetic and being able to enjoy activities with her grandchildren.

  • T: Targeted therapies included a gut-healing protocol with probiotics and prebiotics, dietary modifications to reduce processed foods and increase fiber, and stress management techniques like meditation.

  • L: Long-term lifestyle changes focused on sustainable dietary habits, regular exercise, and stress reduction practices.

Outcome: After three months, Maria reported significant improvements in her energy levels, digestive function, and overall well-being. She successfully transitioned to a healthier lifestyle and continues to thrive.
