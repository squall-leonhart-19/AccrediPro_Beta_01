Handling Objections

"I'm not sure if I have the time."

"I'm worried I won't fit in with the other participants."
"That's a valid concern. Our community is incredibly welcoming and supportive. Many participants share that the connections they make are invaluable. Check Your Understanding

1. What are three key elements of a successful discovery call for a group program?

Building rapport, understanding client needs, and presenting the program as a solution.

2. How can you address the objection that the program is too expensive?

Focus on the value and ROI, offer payment plans, and highlight the potential savings in other areas (e.g., healthcare costs).
