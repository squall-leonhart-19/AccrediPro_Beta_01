Meet Your Prospect

üë©

Susan, 42

A busy marketing executive, feeling overwhelmed and exhausted. Found you on Instagram.

Her Situation
Constant stress, poor sleep, relies on caffeine, gaining weight, feels "burnt out."
Budget Concern
"I'm willing to invest in my health, but I need to see a clear return on investment."
Decision Style
Analytical, needs data and a structured plan. Appreciates efficiency.
Her Goal
"I want to regain my energy, manage stress effectively, and feel like I'm thriving, not just surviving."

Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Intro 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you so much for scheduling this call. I know your time is valuable, so I appreciate you making the time. I saw you found me on Instagram; what caught your eye?"
YOU:
"Great! And what prompted you to book this call specifically? What are you hoping to achieve?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Her Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling burnt out. Can you tell me more about that? What does a typical day look like for you?"
YOU:
"And what have you already tried to address these issues? Supplements, diet changes, exercise? What were the results?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 1 being 'not at all' and 10 being 'completely debilitating,' how would you rate the impact of these challenges on your overall quality of life?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're dealing with significant stress and fatigue, and you're looking for a sustainable solution, not just a band-aid. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach is to identify the root causes of your symptoms through comprehensive testing and personalized nutrition. I create a customized plan that addresses your unique needs. Clients typically report significant improvements in energy, sleep, and stress management within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"This isn't a one-size-fits-all program. It's tailored specifically to your body and your lifestyle."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, I'm confident I can help you achieve your goals. Does this approach resonate with you, Susan?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me briefly outline the program, its investment, and how we can get started."

Handling Common Objections

Prepare for These Objections

"I'm not sure I have the time for this right now."
"I understand completely. Many of my clients are incredibly busy professionals. The program is designed to be efficient and integrated into your existing lifestyle. And think about it this way: what's the cost of NOT making time for your health? How will you perform at work if you continue to feel burnt out?"
"I've tried so many things before, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. What makes my approach different is that we focus on identifying the underlying causes of your symptoms, not just masking them. We use advanced testing to get a clear picture of what's happening in your body. I'm confident we can find solutions that haven't been explored before. Plus, 85% of my clients report significant improvement within 12 weeks."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can guarantee that I will provide you with a personalized plan, ongoing support, and the tools you need to succeed. Your success depends on your commitment to the program, but I'll be with you every step of the way. Most clients see a positive return on investment within a few weeks, feeling more energetic and productive."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What aspects are you still considering? Is it the financial investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision. Perhaps I can answer any remaining questions right now?"

The Power of Discovery Calls

Discovery calls are a critical step in converting potential clients into paying ones. They are your opportunity to connect with prospects, understand their challenges, and demonstrate how your services can provide solutions. A well-executed discovery call can significantly increase your conversion rate and build a strong foundation for long-term client relationships.

According to a 2023 study by HubSpot, companies that excel at lead nurturing generate 50% more sales-ready leads at a 33% lower cost. Discovery calls are a crucial part of that lead nurturing process.

Crafting Your 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

Time is precious, both yours and your prospect's. That's why it's essential to structure your discovery calls efficiently. This sample script is designed to maximize impact in just 30 minutes.

Sample Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Intro 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for taking the time to speak with me today. I appreciate you reaching out."
YOU:
"Before we dive in, I'd love to hear a little bit about what prompted you to schedule this call."
YOU:
"Great. And just to confirm, we have about 30 minutes to chat. Does that still work for you?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Her Challenges 15 min
YOU:
"So, Susan, tell me more about what you're currently experiencing. What are your biggest frustrations when it comes to your health and energy levels?"
YOU:
"When did you first start noticing these symptoms?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these issues?"
YOU:
"What are your health goals? What does success look like for you?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how important is it for you to achieve these goals?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 5 min
YOU:
"Thank you for sharing that, Susan. Based on what you've told me, it sounds like you're dealing with [summarize her key challenges]."
YOU:
"I specialize in helping people just like you overcome these challenges through a personalized, holistic approach."
YOU:
"My program focuses on identifying the root causes of your symptoms using advanced testing, creating a customized nutrition plan, and providing ongoing support to help you achieve lasting results."
YOU:
"Unlike other approaches you may have tried, we focus on [highlight your unique selling proposition, e.g., personalized care, gut health optimization, mind-body connection] to create a truly tailored solution that fits both your body and your lifestyle."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, I'm confident I can help you achieve your goals. Does this approach resonate with you, Susan?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me briefly outline the program, its investment, and how we can get started."
