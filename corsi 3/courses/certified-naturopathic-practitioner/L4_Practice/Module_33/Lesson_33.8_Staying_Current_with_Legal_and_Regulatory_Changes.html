Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to learn more about your gut health goals. To make the most of our time, let's spend a few minutes understanding your current situation, then I'll share how my program can help, and finally, we'll discuss if it's a good fit for you. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"Great! I noticed you found me on Instagram. What specifically caught your attention?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Her Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the bloating and digestive discomfort. When did it start, and what does a typical day look like for you in terms of symptoms?"
YOU:
"What diets have you tried, and what were the results? What did you like or dislike about them?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being perfect gut health, where would you rate yourself now? And where would you *like* to be?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution & Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her key pain points] and you're looking for a sustainable solution. Is that an accurate summary?"
YOU:
"My gut health program is a 12-week personalized approach that focuses on identifying the root causes of your digestive issues. Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time commitment."
"I understand. It does require a commitment, but we break it down into manageable steps. The weekly sessions are [duration], and the daily implementation of the plan is designed to fit into your existing routine. Think of it as investing time *now* to gain more energy and well-being in the long run. Many of my clients, even with busy schedules, find the program to be surprisingly easy to integrate."
"I've tried so many things before that haven't worked."
"I hear your frustration. What makes my approach different is that we don't just focus on symptom management; we dig deep to identify the underlying causes of your gut issues. We use [mention specific testing or methodologies] to create a personalized plan tailored to *your* unique needs. Plus, you'll have my ongoing support and guidance throughout the entire process."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer a payment plan option. Instead of paying the full amount upfront, you can split it into [number] monthly installments of [amount]. This makes the program more accessible while still allowing you to achieve your gut health goals. Would you like me to send you the details of the payment plan?"
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"I'm confident that you'll see results if you fully commit to the program. However, I understand your concern. That's why I offer a [mention guarantee or refund policy, e.g., satisfaction guarantee, partial refund]. My goal is for you to be completely satisfied with your experience."

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for converting prospects into clients.
  • Understanding the prospect's challenges and goals is essential for tailoring your presentation.
  • Addressing objections with confidence and empathy builds trust and increases your chances of closing the sale.
  • Clearly communicating the value and benefits of your program is key to justifying the investment.
  • Consistent client acquisition efforts can lead to a significant and sustainable income for your practice.
