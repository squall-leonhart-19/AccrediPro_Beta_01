Check Your Understanding

1. What are some key elements to building rapport during a discovery call?

Show genuine interest, actively listen, and find common ground to establish trust and connection.

2. How can you address the "I need to think about it" objection effectively?

Acknowledge their need for time, then gently explore the specific concerns or questions they have to provide clarity and support their decision-making process.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call helps you understand your prospect's needs and present your services effectively.
  • Anticipating and confidently handling objections can significantly improve your conversion rate.
  • Clearly communicating the value of your program and payment options makes it easier for clients to invest in their health.
  • Consistent client acquisition efforts can lead to a substantial and sustainable income for your practice.
