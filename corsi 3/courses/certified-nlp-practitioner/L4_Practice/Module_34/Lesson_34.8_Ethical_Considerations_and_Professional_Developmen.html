Pre-Workshop Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Maria, thanks so much for registering for the 'Stress-Free Living' workshop! I'm Sarah, and I'm thrilled you're interested. To start, tell me a bit about what resonated with you about the workshop description."
YOU:
"That's great to hear! What are you hoping to get out of the workshop specifically?"
Phase 2: Understand Her Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"Can you tell me more about the specific stressors you're facing right now? What does a typical stressful day look like for you?"
YOU:
"What have you tried in the past to manage your stress? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, where 10 is completely overwhelmed, where would you rate your stress level currently?"
Phase 3: Highlight Workshop Benefits 10 min
YOU:
"Maria, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're feeling incredibly overwhelmed and are looking for practical ways to regain control and find more peace. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"In the workshop, we'll be diving deep into proven NLP techniques for stress management, including anchoring and reframing. You'll learn how to quickly shift your state, manage negative thoughts, and create lasting positive change. Plus, you'll connect with a supportive group of women who understand what you're going through."
YOU:
"Many participants report feeling significantly calmer and more in control within just a few days of implementing these techniques. Imagine feeling that way!"
Phase 4: Address Concerns & Close 5 min
YOU:
"So Maria, does this sound like the kind of support and guidance you've been looking for?"
YOU:
"Great! I understand you mentioned budget concerns. Let's talk about that and see how we can make this work for you."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Time is precious. The workshop is designed to be highly efficient, giving you practical tools you can use immediately. Think of it as an investment in your well-being that will actually create more time by reducing stress and increasing your focus. Plus, you'll have access to recordings, so you can review the material anytime."
"It's too expensive."
"I hear you. When you compare the cost of the workshop to the ongoing expenses of managing stress ‚Äì like medications, therapy, or even just the cost of feeling drained and unproductive ‚Äì it's actually a very valuable investment. I also offer a payment plan to make it more accessible. Would you like to hear about that?"
"I'm not sure if it will really work for me."
"That's a valid concern. I've seen incredible results with clients using these techniques. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you attend the entire workshop and don't feel you've received value, I'll gladly refund your money. How does that sound?"
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the content, the time commitment, or the financial investment? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision. Perhaps I can answer any remaining questions?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are three key benefits you can highlight to a potential workshop participant?

Practical tools and techniques, a supportive community, and a clear return on investment in their well-being.

2. How can you address a potential participant's concern about the workshop being too expensive?

Compare the cost to the ongoing expenses of managing stress, offer a payment plan, and highlight the value and long-term benefits.

Key Takeaways

  • Group workshops are a powerful way to scale your impact and generate revenue.
  • Pre-workshop discovery calls are essential for building rapport and addressing concerns.
  • Confidently state your price and highlight the value participants will receive.
  • Address objections with empathy and offer solutions.
  • Remember, you are providing a valuable service that can transform lives.
