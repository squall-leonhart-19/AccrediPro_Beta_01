Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I enjoyed your participation in the workshop! To start, can you tell me a bit about what brought you here today?"
YOU:
"That's interesting. What specifically resonated with you from the workshop?"
Phase 2: Problem Exploration 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling stuck. Can you elaborate on what that feels like for you?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address this feeling of being stuck?"
YOU:
"And if you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about your career right now, what would it be?"
Phase 3: Solution & Value Proposition 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're looking for a career that's both fulfilling and energizing. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach is designed to help you uncover your core values and align them with a new career path. I work with you to identify limiting beliefs and create a clear action plan. Many clients find clarity and renewed purpose within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"Would you like to hear how this process could specifically benefit you?"
Phase 4: Closing & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, I believe I can help you achieve your career goals. Are you interested in learning more about my program and how we can get started?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Time is valuable. The program is designed to fit into your schedule, requiring just a few hours per week. Plus, think about the time you're currently spending feeling unfulfilled. What if we could redirect that energy into something you love?"
"I need to research other options first."
"That's smart. What specific information are you looking for? I'm happy to provide testimonials, case studies, or answer any questions you have right now."
"Can I get a discount?"
"My program is priced to reflect the value and personalized support you'll receive. While I don't offer discounts, I do have a payment plan option that can make it more accessible. Would you like to hear about that?"
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"I'm confident in my program's effectiveness, and I'm committed to your success. I offer ongoing support and guidance throughout the process. Many of my clients have achieved significant career transformations, and I'm here to help you do the same."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's problem, presenting your solution, and closing with a clear call-to-action.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, highlight the value of your program, offer a payment plan, and emphasize the potential return on investment.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition and sales is crucial for building a thriving practice.
  • A structured discovery call helps you connect with prospects and present your value effectively.
  • Confident objection handling can turn potential "no's" into "yes's".
  • Clearly communicate your pricing and the value your program provides.
  • Consistent effort and practice will lead to increased client acquisition and income potential.
