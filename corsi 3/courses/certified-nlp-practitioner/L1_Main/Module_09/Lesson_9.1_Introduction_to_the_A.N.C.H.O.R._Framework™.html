Breaking Down the Acronym

Each letter in the A.N.C.H.O.R. Frameworkâ„¢ represents a critical stage in the coaching process:

  • A - Assess: Thoroughly evaluate the client's current state, challenges, and desired outcomes.
  • N - Neutralize: Identify and neutralize limiting beliefs, negative emotions, and unhelpful patterns.
  • C - Create: Co-create a compelling future vision and empowering beliefs.
  • H - Harness: Discover and harness inner resources, strengths, and positive experiences.
  • O - Optimize: Develop and optimize strategies, behaviors, and action plans.
  • R - Reinforce: Solidify positive changes through anchoring, future pacing, and ongoing support.
