Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, so glad you could make it! Thank you for investing this time in yourself. I saw you're in [Susan's city] - I love [local landmark]! How's your day going so far?"
YOU:
"Before we dive in, tell me a little about what prompted you to book this call. What's been on your mind lately?"
Phase 2: Uncover Needs & Desires 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling a little lost. Can you elaborate on that? What does that look and feel like for you?"
YOU:
"What have you tried in the past to address these feelings? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and create your ideal life, what would that look like? What would you be doing, feeling, and experiencing?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're craving purpose, connection, and a renewed sense of self. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My program is designed to help women just like you rediscover their passions and create a life they truly love. Over 12 weeks, we'll work together to identify your core values, overcome limiting beliefs, and design a personalized action plan. Many of my clients experience a significant shift in their mindset and confidence within the first month."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I genuinely believe I can help you achieve your goals. Does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for? ... Great! Let me tell you a bit more about the program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time."
"I understand. Time is precious. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your existing lifestyle. We can work together to create a schedule that works for you. Plus, think of the time you'll save by finally feeling more focused and energized!"
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. That's perfectly reasonable. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the financial investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make the right decision for you."
"I'm not sure I'm worth the investment."
"Susan, you are absolutely worth investing in. You deserve to feel happy, fulfilled, and excited about your life. Think of this as an investment in your future self. What would it be worth to finally feel like YOU again?"
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer a flexible payment plan. Let's discuss the options and find one that suits your budget."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport & Connection, Uncover Needs & Desires, Present Your Solution, and Close & Next Steps.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then ask clarifying questions to understand their specific concerns (e.g., financial investment, time commitment). Offer to provide additional information or schedule a follow-up call to address their concerns.
