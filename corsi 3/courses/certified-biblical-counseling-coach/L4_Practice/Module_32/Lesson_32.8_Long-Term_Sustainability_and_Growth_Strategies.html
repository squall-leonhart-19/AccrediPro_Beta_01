The Discovery Call: Your Roadmap

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, it's so great to finally connect with you! Thank you for scheduling this call. I understand you found me through the local group. What resonated with you from my profile?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! I'm passionate about helping women like you. Tell me, what prompted you to reach out for support at this time?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges & Aspirations 10 min
YOU:
"Can you elaborate on what feeling 'lost' looks like for you on a daily basis? What activities or roles used to bring you joy?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these feelings? Any books, workshops, or self-help approaches?"
YOU:
"If we were having this conversation a year from now, and you were thrilled with the progress you've made, what would be different in your life?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Unique Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, if I'm hearing you correctly, you're feeling a lack of purpose and are seeking a fulfilling path forward. You've tried some self-help methods, but are looking for more personalized guidance. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is rooted in biblical principles and integrates proven coaching techniques to help you uncover your God-given talents and passions. We'll work together to create a roadmap that aligns with your values and aspirations. Many of my clients experience a renewed sense of purpose and direction within the first few months."

Overcoming Objections

Navigating Concerns with Grace

"I'm not sure if I have the time commitment right now."
"I understand. Time is precious. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. Can you tell me more about what your typical week looks like? Perhaps we can identify some areas where we can carve out dedicated time for your personal growth. Remember, investing in yourself is an investment in all areas of your life."
"I need to pray about it."
"That's wonderful, Susan. I encourage you to seek God's guidance. Would it be helpful if I sent you a detailed program outline and testimonials from other clients to support your prayerful consideration? I'm confident that God will lead you to the right decision."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you fully participate in the program and don't see significant progress within the first month, we can discuss a refund. I'm committed to your success and providing you with the support you need to achieve your goals. I also have several client success stories I'd be happy to share with you."
"I'm worried about the cost."
"I understand that investing in coaching is a significant decision. Let's talk about the value you'll receive in return. Consider the cost of staying where you are ‚Äì the emotional toll of feeling unfulfilled, the missed opportunities, and the potential impact on your relationships. My program is designed to provide you with a clear path forward and equip you with the tools to create lasting change. We also have payment plan options available."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Warm Welcome & Connection, Deep Dive into Challenges & Aspirations, Presenting Your Unique Approach, and Confident Close & Next Steps.

2. What is the importance of handling objections effectively?

Handling objections effectively builds trust, addresses concerns, and ultimately increases the likelihood of converting prospects into clients.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is essential for client acquisition.
  • Building rapport and understanding the prospect's needs are crucial for success.
  • Confidently presenting your pricing and handling objections effectively are key to closing the deal.
  • Practice and preparation are essential for delivering a compelling and persuasive discovery call.
