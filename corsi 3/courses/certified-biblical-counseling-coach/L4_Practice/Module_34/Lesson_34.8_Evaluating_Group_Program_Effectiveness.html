Common Objections & Confident Responses

Handling Objections with Grace

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Time is precious. Think of this not as taking time away from your life, but investing time in your life. Two hours a week for six weeks can lead to significant changes in your overall well-being. Plus, the group dynamic provides accountability and support."
"I'm not good at group settings."
"That's perfectly okay! Many women feel that way initially. This group is designed to be a safe and non-judgmental space. I facilitate the discussions to ensure everyone feels comfortable sharing at their own pace. It's more about connection and support than forced participation."
"Can I get a discount?"
"While I don't offer discounts on the core program, I do have a few spots open for a scholarship opportunity. To apply, you'd need to fill out a brief form explaining why this workshop is important to you right now. Would you like me to send you the link?"
"I need to think about it."
"Absolutely. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the financial commitment, the time, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make the right decision for you."

Check Your Understanding

1. What's a key benefit of offering a payment plan for your group program?

A payment plan removes a financial barrier and makes the program more accessible to potential clients.

2. How can you address a prospect's concern about not being "good at" group settings?

Reassure them that the group is designed to be a safe and non-judgmental space, and that you facilitate the discussions to ensure everyone feels comfortable sharing at their own pace.

Key Takeaways

  • Focus on understanding the prospect's needs and connecting with them on a personal level.
  • Address objections with empathy and provide solutions, not just rebuttals.
  • Confidently state your price and highlight the value of your group program.
  • Practice your closing lines to make them feel natural and authentic.
  • Remember, group programs offer a fantastic opportunity to create impact and generate income.
