Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I appreciate you taking the time. To start, can you tell me what resonated with you from my Facebook ad?"
YOU:
"I understand. Many women in a similar stage of life find themselves feeling that way. What specifically prompted you to book this call now?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling stressed and overwhelmed. Can you elaborate on that? What areas of your life are contributing to those feelings?"
YOU:
"And what does a typical day look like for you? What are some of the biggest challenges you face?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and change one thing about your current situation, what would it be?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're experiencing a common feeling of being 'lost' after a significant life transition. My approach focuses on helping you reconnect with your values, identify your strengths, and create a roadmap for a fulfilling future. Does that resonate with you?"
YOU:
"My 12-week program is designed to provide structure and support as you navigate this transition. We'll use mindfulness techniques, goal-setting exercises, and personalized coaching to help you rediscover your purpose and create a life you love. Many of my clients, women just like you, report feeling significantly more confident and energized within the first few weeks."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, Susan, I believe my program can provide the support and guidance you're looking for. Are you open to learning more about how we can work together?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand that time is a valuable resource. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your existing schedule. We can work together to prioritize your goals and create a manageable plan. Many of my clients find that by investing just a few hours a week, they actually gain time by becoming more focused and efficient."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can assure you that I'm committed to providing you with the best possible support and guidance. My program is based on proven methodologies, and I'll work closely with you to personalize the approach to your unique needs and goals. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied after the first month, I'll provide a full refund."
"I need to research other options first."
"I respect that. What specifically are you looking to research? I'm happy to provide you with any information you need to make an informed decision. I can also share some testimonials from past clients who have achieved remarkable results."
"I'm worried about the financial commitment."
"I understand. Investing in yourself is a big decision. Let's break down the cost and look at the value you'll receive. The program includes 1:1 coaching, group sessions, personalized resources, and ongoing support. Think of it as an investment in your future happiness and well-being. Many of my clients find that the benefits far outweigh the cost. And I offer flexible payment plans to make it more accessible."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, reframe the cost as an investment, highlight the value they'll receive, and offer flexible payment options.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call script will keep you on track.
  • Practice handling common objections to build confidence.
  • Present your pricing with conviction and highlight the value.
  • Focus on the client's needs and goals to build trust.
  • Consistent effort in client acquisition leads to financial success.
