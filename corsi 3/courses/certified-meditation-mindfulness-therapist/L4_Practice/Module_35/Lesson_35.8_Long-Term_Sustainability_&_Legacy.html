Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I understand you found me on [meditation app]. What prompted you to reach out today?"
YOU:
"That's interesting. Many of my clients in similar roles experience the same challenges. Can you tell me more about what a typical stressful day looks like for you?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"When did you first notice these feelings of overwhelm? How does it impact your sleep and overall well-being?"
YOU:
"Have you tried any stress reduction techniques in the past? What were the results?"
YOU:
"Imagine a year from now, if things continue as they are. What does that look like? Now, imagine you've successfully overcome this stress. What's different?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing significant stress that's impacting your sleep and well-being. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach integrates mindfulness and meditation techniques tailored to high-stress professionals. We'll work together to develop personalized strategies for managing stress, improving sleep, and enhancing your overall sense of calm. A 2023 study in the Journal of Applied Psychology found that mindfulness interventions reduced perceived stress by 27% in finance professionals (n=120, CI 95%)."
YOU:
"Typically, clients start experiencing noticeable improvements within the first few weeks of the program."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with what you're looking for? ... Excellent! Let me share the details of the program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand. Many of my clients are concerned about the time commitment. However, the program is designed to be flexible and adaptable to your schedule. We can break it down into manageable segments, and even 15 minutes of daily practice can make a significant difference. Plus, think about the time you'll save by being more focused and less stressed."
"I'm worried it won't work for me."
"That's a valid concern. However, my program is based on evidence-based techniques and personalized to your specific needs. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not seeing results within the first month, we can adjust the program or offer a refund."
"I need to discuss it with my family."
"That's perfectly reasonable. Would you like me to provide you with some information that you can share with them? I'm also happy to answer any questions they may have. We can even schedule a brief call together."
"There are so many meditation apps, why should I choose your program?"
"That's a great question. While apps can be a good starting point, my program offers personalized guidance and support, helping you to overcome specific challenges and achieve deeper levels of calm and focus. Plus, I bring my years of experience and training to tailor the practice specifically to you."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How should you handle the objection, "I need to think about it?"

Acknowledge their need to think, then ask clarifying questions to understand their specific concerns. Offer to provide additional information or answer further questions.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is crucial for client acquisition.
  • Addressing objections with confidence builds trust and increases conversion rates.
  • Clearly communicating the value of your program justifies your pricing.
  • Consistent effort in client acquisition leads to a sustainable and thriving practice.
