In-depth Exploration of Splenic Authority

The Splenic Authority is characterized by its immediacy and reliance on intuition. It's a clear, here-and-now knowing that speaks in the moment. Individuals with this Authority experience a gut feeling or instinctual awareness that guides them toward what is healthy and safe. Navigating Emotional Authority

Emotional Authority, rooted in the Emotional Solar Plexus Center, operates on an emotional wave. Clarity isn't immediate; it requires time to process emotions and arrive at a neutral perspective. Making decisions in the heat of the moment is discouraged for those with Emotional Authority. The key is to ride the wave, allowing emotions to rise and fall before making a clear decision. This process ensures that decisions are not driven by temporary emotional states but by a deeper understanding of one's true feelings. Embracing this process aligns with the 'Witness Patterns' aspect of the A.W.A.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢.

Consider Sarah, a 52-year-old Manifesting Generator with Emotional Authority. She was presented with an investment opportunity that seemed promising. Instead of immediately committing, she allowed herself to experience her emotional wave. Over a few days, she felt excitement, doubt, and eventually, a sense of calm. From this place of neutrality, she recognized red flags and decided to pass on the investment, saving herself from potential financial loss.

Self-Projected Authority

The Self-Projected Authority relies on finding clarity through verbal processing and reflection. Individuals with this Authority need to talk through their decisions with trusted individuals to hear their own truth. This Authority is connected to the G Center, which governs identity, love, and direction. It's not about seeking advice but about using conversation as a mirror to reflect one's inner knowing. The key is to listen to how the words sound and feel, not just what others say. This process aligns with the 'Reflect and Integrate' aspect of the A.W.A.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢.
