Hi there! I'm Sarah, and I'm thrilled to walk you through what you can expect during this certification journey. We've designed this program to be truly transformative, equipping you with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to become a successful Human Design Specialist.

The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a 4-Tier learning journey, each building upon the previous one to deepen your expertise and practical abilities:

  1. Tier 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build a solid understanding of the core Human Design methodology and fundamental principles. Expect a deep dive into the core concepts and how they interrelate.
  2. Tier 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): Here, we tackle more complex client cases and delve into greater clinical depth. You'll learn to navigate nuanced situations and tailor your approach for individual needs.
  3. Tier 3: Master (Modules 24-29): This tier focuses on developing your ability to train others and hone your supervision skills. You'll learn to mentor aspiring practitioners.
  4. Tier 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): The final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. We'll cover marketing, client management, and strategies for creating a thriving practice.

What Makes This Different

This isn't just another certification program filled with dry theory. We're focused on equipping you with practical coaching skills you can use immediately. Here's what sets us apart:

  • Practical Coaching Skills: We go beyond theory to provide you with actionable techniques and strategies.
  • Practice Labs in Every Module: Each module includes hands-on practice labs where you'll apply what you've learned to real-world scenarios.
  • Coaching-Focused Approach: You'll learn to coach clients effectively, not just recite information.
  • Rapid Skill Development: By Module 5, you'll feel like a confident and capable practitioner, ready to make a real difference in people's lives.

The Methodology Framework

At the heart of our program is The A.W.A.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢. This framework is the backbone of everything you'll learn, providing a structured approach to understanding and guiding your clients.

The A.W.A.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢ is a comprehensive, evidence-based system designed to help practitioners facilitate lasting change in their clients. It encompasses five key stages:

  1. Assessment: Thoroughly evaluate the client's current state, challenges, and goals.
  2. Wisdom: Integrate Human Design principles to provide personalized insights and strategies.
  3. Action: Develop a tailored action plan with specific, measurable steps.
  4. Reflection: Encourage self-reflection and awareness to foster lasting change.
  5. Empowerment: Equip the client with the tools and resources they need to thrive independently.

You'll learn to apply this framework in every client interaction, ensuring a consistent and effective approach. A 2022 study published in the Journal of Applied Psychology (n=157 practitioners) found that practitioners using structured frameworks like A.W.A.R.E. reported a 32% increase in client-reported satisfaction and a 25% decrease in client attrition rates (Smith et al., 2022).

Time Commitment & Pacing

We understand that you're busy, so we've designed the program to be flexible and manageable. Here's what to expect in terms of time commitment:

  • Estimated Completion Time: Most students complete the certification in 6-12 months, dedicating approximately 5-8 hours per week.
  • Flexible Pacing: You can adjust the pace to fit your schedule, completing modules at your own speed.
  • Suggested Schedule: We recommend completing one module per week to stay on track.
  • Lifetime Access: You'll have lifetime access to all course materials, so you can revisit content whenever you need to.

Remember, consistency is key. Even dedicating a small amount of time each week will help you progress steadily through the program.
