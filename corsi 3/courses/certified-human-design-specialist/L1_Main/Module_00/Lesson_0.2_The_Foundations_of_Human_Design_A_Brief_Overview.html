Realistic Timeline to Income

Let's talk about expectations. Building a successful Human Design practice takes time and effort. Here's a realistic timeline:

  • Month 1-3: Foundation & Learning. Focus on completing the certification program, practicing readings with friends and family, and building your foundational knowledge.
  • Month 3-6: Building Your Brand. Start creating a website or social media presence, developing your unique brand voice, and offering free or discounted readings to gain experience and testimonials.
  • Month 6-12: Consistent Marketing & Sales. Implement a consistent marketing strategy, such as social media marketing, email marketing, or networking. Start offering paid readings and programs at introductory prices.
  • Month 12+: Scaling & Growth. Refine your offerings, increase your prices, and explore new revenue streams. Consider hiring a virtual assistant or team member to help you scale your business.

Remember, this is just a guideline. Some people may see results faster, while others may take longer. The key is to stay consistent, keep learning, and never give up on your dreams.

The Math That Makes Sense

Let's break down the numbers. Here's a simple illustration of how you can generate a sustainable income:

Scenario 1: Private Practice

  • Offer 5 readings per week at $200/reading = $1,000/week
  • $1,000/week x 4 weeks/month = $4,000/month

Scenario 2: Group Program

  • Launch a 4-week group program priced at $497
  • Enroll 20 participants = $9,940 per launch

Scenario 3: Online Course

  • Create an evergreen course priced at $297
  • Sell 10 copies per month = $2,970/month

As you can see, even a small number of clients or sales can generate a significant income. The key is to offer valuable services and market them effectively.

Your 90-Day Vision Exercise

Now it's your turn! Let's create a 90-day vision for your Human Design practice. Take some time to answer the following questions:

  1. What are your top three goals for the next 90 days? (e.g., complete the certification program, launch a website, offer 10 free readings)

Write down your answers and keep them visible as a reminder of your commitment. Review your vision regularly and adjust it as needed. Remember, you have the power to create the career and life of your dreams!
