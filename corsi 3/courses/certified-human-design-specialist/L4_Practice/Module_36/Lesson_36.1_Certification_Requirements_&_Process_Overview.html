Certification Requirements

To achieve certification, you must successfully complete the following:

  • Case Study Submissions: Submission of three comprehensive case studies demonstrating your ability to apply Human Design principles with real clients.

  • Written Exam: A comprehensive assessment of your knowledge of Human Design theory and principles.
  • Ethical Guidelines Adherence: A signed statement confirming your commitment to ethical conduct as a Human Design practitioner.

Certification Process

The certification process is structured to ensure a fair and thorough evaluation of your skills. Here's a step-by-step breakdown:

  1. Application Submission: Submit your certification application through the AccrediPro Academy portal.
  2. Review Timeline: Your application will be reviewed within 7-10 business days to ensure all prerequisites are met.
  3. Examination Scheduling: Upon approval, you will be able to schedule your practical and written exams. You have 30 days from approval to schedule.
  4. Notification of Results: Exam results will be communicated within 5-7 business days of completion.

Maintaining Certification

Certification is valid for two years. To maintain your certification, you must:

  • Complete 20 hours of continuing education: Focus on advanced Human Design topics or related modalities (e.g., coaching, counseling).
  • Submit proof of continuing education: Provide certificates or transcripts documenting your completed coursework.
  • Pay a renewal fee of $97: This fee covers administrative costs associated with maintaining your certification.

Continuing education ensures you stay current with the latest developments in Human Design and maintain a high level of expertise. It's an ongoing Embodiment of your commitment to growth and excellence.

Practical Exam Preparation Tips

The practical exam evaluates your ability to conduct a Human Design reading. Here are some preparation tips:

  • Practice Readings: Conduct practice readings with friends, family, or study partners.
  • Record Yourself: Record yourself conducting a reading and review the recording to identify areas for improvement.
  • Seek Feedback: Ask experienced practitioners to observe your readings and provide constructive feedback.
  • Familiarize Yourself with Common Questions: Anticipate common questions clients may ask and prepare thoughtful responses.

The practical exam is your opportunity to shine. Embody your knowledge and skills and demonstrate your ability to provide valuable insights to others.
