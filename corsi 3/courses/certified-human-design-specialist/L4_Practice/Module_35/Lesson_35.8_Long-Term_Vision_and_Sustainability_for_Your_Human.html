Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during a discovery call?

Expressing genuine interest, actively listening, asking open-ended questions, and finding common ground.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it?"

Acknowledge their need for time, then gently inquire about the specific concerns they have. Offer to provide more information or schedule a follow-up call.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful responses.
  • Confidently state your pricing and highlight the value you provide.
  • Focus on building genuine connections with potential clients.
  • Remember, every "no" brings you closer to a "yes."
