Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Discovery 7 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about your goals. First, what inspired you to explore Human Design as a business model?"
YOU:
"Tell me about your career transition. What were you doing before, and what are you hoping to create now?"
YOU:
"What are some of the biggest challenges you're facing as you build this business?"
Phase 2: Value Proposition 10 min
YOU:
"Many career-changers face similar challenges: imposter syndrome, lack of clarity, and marketing confusion. How do those resonate with you?"

"The 'Certified Human Design Specialist' program helps you overcome these challenges by providing a proven framework, personalized mentorship, and a supportive community. It's designed to take you from feeling unsure to confident in your ability to build a thriving practice."
YOU:
"Imagine having a clear roadmap, step-by-step guidance, and the support you need to launch your business successfully. How would that feel?"

YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the type of support you've been looking for?"

YOU:

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I need to compare other programs first."
"I understand. What other programs are you considering? What specific features are you comparing?"
"I'm not sure if I have enough time."
"The program is designed for busy individuals. How many hours per week can you dedicate to your business? We can tailor the program to fit your schedule."
"Can I see some success stories?"
"Absolutely! Let me share a few stories of women just like you. For example, Maria, 45, a former teacher, launched her practice within 3 months and now earns $5,000/month."
"Is there a guarantee?"
"We are confident that if you implement what you learn in the course, you will see results. We offer personalized support, and our students see significant progress with client acquisition and income generation. While we don't offer a money-back guarantee, we are dedicated to helping you succeed."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's needs, presenting your value proposition, handling objections, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How can you address the "I need to think about it" objection?

Acknowledge their need for time, then ask clarifying questions to understand the specific concerns they have. Offer to provide additional information or schedule a follow-up call to address those concerns.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script regularly to build confidence and fluency.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful, empathetic responses.
  • Confidently state your pricing and highlight the value you provide.
  • Focus on building genuine connections with potential clients.
  • Remember that every "no" brings you closer to a "yes."
