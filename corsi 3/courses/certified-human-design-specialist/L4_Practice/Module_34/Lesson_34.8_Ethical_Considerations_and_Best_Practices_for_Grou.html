Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during a discovery call?

Show genuine interest, actively listen, find common ground, and empathize with the prospect's situation.

2. How can you address budget concerns during a discovery call for a group program?

Acknowledge their concerns, highlight the value and potential ROI of the program, offer payment plans or early bird discounts, and focus on the long-term benefits.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for converting prospects into group program participants.
  • Building genuine connection and understanding the prospect's needs are paramount.
  • Addressing objections with empathy and confidence is essential for overcoming resistance.
  • Clearly presenting the value and benefits of the group program is key to motivating prospects to join.
  • Following up with interested prospects can significantly increase enrollment rates.
