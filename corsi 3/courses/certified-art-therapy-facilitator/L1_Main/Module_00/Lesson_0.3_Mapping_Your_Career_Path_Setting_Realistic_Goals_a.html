The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a comprehensive 4-Tier system, designed to take you from foundational knowledge to mastery and practical application. Hereâ€™s a breakdown of what you can expect at each level:

  1. Tier 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build your core understanding of the art therapy methodology and fundamentals. We'll cover the essential principles, techniques, and ethical considerations. You'll gain a solid foundation to build upon.
  2. Tier 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): In this tier, we delve into more complex case studies and explore advanced techniques for addressing a wide range of client needs. You'll learn how to apply your knowledge in diverse clinical settings and deepen your clinical depth.
  3. Tier 3: Master (Modules 24-29): This tier focuses on developing your ability to train and supervise others. You'll learn how to effectively communicate your knowledge, mentor aspiring facilitators, and ensure the highest standards of practice. This level is about developing your leadership skills.
  4. Tier 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): The final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. We'll cover marketing strategies, business management, and practical tips for establishing a successful art therapy practice. This is where you'll gain business acumen.

Certification Requirements

To earn your professional certification, you'll need to meet the following requirements:

  1. Complete all 36 modules of the program.
  2. Pass the end-of-module quizzes with a score of 80% or higher.
  3. Successfully complete the final certification exam.
  4. Submit a portfolio of your work, demonstrating your ability to apply the art therapy techniques effectively.
  5. Complete 20 hours of supervised practice with clients.

Once you've met these requirements, you'll receive your official Certified Art Therapy Facilitatorâ„¢ certificate, recognizing your expertise and commitment to excellence in the field.
