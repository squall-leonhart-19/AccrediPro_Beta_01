8 Career Paths (With Income Ranges)

The beauty of this certification is its versatility. You're not limited to one path. Here are just a few options, along with realistic income ranges:

  1. Private Practice (1-on-1): Work directly with clients, creating personalized plans. Example: Offering a 3-month program for $1,500. With just 5 clients, you're at $7,500/month. Potential: $5,000 - $20,000/month.
  2. Group Programs: Lead group coaching sessions or workshops. Example: Launching a 6-week online program for $497. With 50participants, that's $24,850. Potential: $3,000 - $30,000+/month.
  3. Online Courses: Create and sell self-paced courses. Example: A course priced at $297, selling 20 copies per month = $5,940. Potential: $1,000 - $10,000+/month (passive income).
  4. Speaking Engagements: Present at conferences, workshops, or corporate events. Example: Charging $1,000 - $5,000 per speaking engagement. Potential: $1,000 - $10,000+/month (depending on frequency).
  5. Corporate Wellness Programs: Partner with companies to provide menopause support to employees. Example: Securing a corporate contract for $5,000/month. Potential: $3,000 - $20,000+/month.
  6. Affiliate Marketing: Recommend relevant products and services and earn a commission. Example: Earning 10% commission on a $100 product, selling 100 units = $1,000. Potential: $500 - $5,000+/month.
  7. Content Creation (Blog, Podcast, YouTube): Monetize your content through ads, sponsorships, or product sales. Example: Building a YouTube channel with 10,000 subscribers and earning $500/month from ads. Potential: $500 - $10,000+/month.
  8. Collaborations with Other Practitioners: Partner with doctors, nutritionists, or therapists to offer comprehensive care. Example: Receiving referrals and sharing revenue on client cases. Potential: Varies widely, but can significantly boost income.

These are just starting points! The possibilities are truly endless, and you can combine multiple paths to create a unique and fulfilling career.

Realistic Timeline to Income

Let's be real: you won't become a millionaire overnight. Building a successful practice takes time and effort. Here's a realistic timeline:

  • Month 1-3: Complete the certification, refine your niche, and start networking. Focus on building your knowledge and confidence. Expect minimal income during this phase.
  • Month 3-6: Start offering introductory services (e.g., free consultations, low-cost workshops). Build your email list and gather testimonials. Expect to generate some income, but primarily focus on marketing and building relationships.
  • Month 6-12: Launch your core services (e.g., 1-on-1 coaching, group programs). Refine your pricing and marketing strategies. Expect to see a significant increase in income.
  • Year 1+: Continue to grow your practice, expand your services, and increase your rates. Focus on building a sustainable and profitable business.

Important: This is just a guideline. Your individual timeline may vary depending on your experience, marketing efforts, and the specific career paths you choose. But remember, consistency and persistence are key!

Your 90-Day Vision Exercise

Now it's time to get practical! Let's create a 90-day vision for your practice and income:

  1. Define Your Ideal Client: Who do you want to serve? What are their biggest challenges and desires?
  2. Choose Your Core Services: What services will you offer in the next 90 days? (e.g., 1-on-1 coaching, group program, online course).
  3. Set Your Pricing: How much will you charge for each service? Research your market and consider your value proposition.
  4. Create a Marketing Plan: How will you attract clients? (e.g., social media, email marketing, networking).
  5. Set an Income Goal: How much money do you want to earn in the next 90 days? Be realistic but ambitious.
  6. Break It Down: What specific actions do you need to take each week to achieve your income goal?

Write down your 90-day vision and review it regularly. This will help you stay focused, motivated, and on track to achieving your goals.
