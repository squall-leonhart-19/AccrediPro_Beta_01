Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to learn more about what you're experiencing. To start, what prompted you to reach out to me specifically?"
YOU:
"I understand. It takes courage to seek help. What are you hoping to achieve from our conversation today?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned hot flashes and sleep disturbances. Can you describe how these symptoms are impacting your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to manage these symptoms? What were the results?"
YOU:
"Imagine you wake up three months from now, and these symptoms are significantly reduced. What would that mean for you? How would your life be different?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her key challenges and goals]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach focuses on identifying the root causes of your symptoms through comprehensive testing and personalized lifestyle interventions. I work with clients over 12 weeks to create a sustainable plan that addresses their unique needs. 85% of my clients report significant symptom improvement within 8 weeks."
YOU:
"This isn't about quick fixes; it's about empowering you with the knowledge and tools to manage your health long-term."
Phase 4: Confident Close (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with you? Do you see how it could help you achieve your goals? ... Excellent! Let's discuss the program details and how we can get started."

Handling Objections with Grace

Common Concerns & Empowered Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand that time is precious. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. Many of my clients are busy professionals who find that investing in their health actually *increases* their productivity and energy levels. We can work together to create a schedule that works for you. Think of it as an investment in reclaiming your time and energy."
"I've tried so many things before that haven't worked."
"I hear your frustration. That's why my approach is different. We don't just treat symptoms; we dig deeper to identify the underlying causes. We use evidence-based testing and personalized protocols to create a plan that's tailored to your specific needs. This isn't a one-size-fits-all approach."
"I need to compare your program to others."
"Absolutely. What specific aspects are you comparing? I'm happy to provide more information about my program's unique features, such as [mention specific benefits, testing options, or personalized support]. Ultimately, the best program is the one that feels like the right fit for you and your goals."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes (because everyone responds differently), I can guarantee my commitment to providing you with the highest quality care and support. 90% of my clients report feeling significantly better within 12 weeks, and I stand behind my program. I offer ongoing support and adjustments to ensure you're making progress towards your goals."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Connection & Rapport, Deep Dive into Challenges, Present Your Solution, and Confident Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need for time, then gently inquire about the specific concerns they need to consider (investment, time commitment, etc.) to ensure they have all the necessary information.

3. What is a good strategy for presenting your pricing confidently?

Clearly state the price, highlight the value and inclusions, and frame it as an investment in their health and well-being. Connect the investment to the potential ROI in terms of symptom relief and improved quality of life.

Key Takeaways

  • Master the Discovery Call: Practice your script and objection handling techniques to confidently guide potential clients.
  • Embrace Confident Pricing: Know your worth and present your program value with assurance.
  • Focus on Value: Highlight the tangible benefits clients will experience through your program.
  • Believe in Your Potential: Embrace the income possibilities and visualize your success.
