Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm so glad we're connecting. To start, can you tell me what led you to reach out to me specifically?"
YOU:
"That's great to hear you found me through my website. What resonated with you most? And what are you hoping to achieve by the end of our call today?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned hot flashes, night sweats, and mood swings. Can you describe how these symptoms are impacting your daily life and work?"
YOU:
"What natural remedies or conventional treatments have you already tried, and what were the results? What are your biggest frustrations?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, where do you see yourself in six months or a year? And if you could wave a magic wand and resolve these issues, what would your ideal life look like?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her key challenges]. Is that a fair assessment?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because it's tailored to your unique needs. I specialize in helping women navigate menopause naturally through personalized nutrition plans, lifestyle adjustments, and targeted supplement recommendations. My 12-week program focuses on addressing the root causes of your symptoms and providing you with the tools to manage them long-term. Many of my clients report significant improvements within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"For example, I recently worked with a 52-year-old client named Maria who had similar symptoms. After just six weeks in the program, she reported a 70% reduction in hot flashes and improved sleep quality."
Phase 4: Closing the Call (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"So Susan, based on our conversation, do you think this approach could be a good fit for you? ... Great! Let me share the details of the program and how we can move forward."

Handling Objections

Addressing Concerns & Building Confidence

"I'm not sure if I have the time for a 12-week program."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. We can schedule sessions at times that work best for you, and you'll have access to resources and support in between sessions. Think of this as an investment in your long-term health and well-being ‚Äì what is that worth to you?"
"I'm worried about the cost. I've already spent so much on supplements and doctors."
"I hear you. It's frustrating to invest in solutions that don't deliver results. This program is different because it's personalized and evidence-based. We focus on addressing the root causes of your symptoms, which can lead to long-term cost savings by reducing the need for expensive treatments and unnecessary supplements. Let's talk about the value you'll receive and compare it to the ongoing costs of managing your symptoms without a comprehensive plan. For instance, a 2023 study in the Journal of Women's Health found that personalized nutrition plans can reduce healthcare costs by 20-30% in women experiencing menopause symptoms."
"I need to discuss this with my partner before making a decision."
"That's perfectly reasonable. Would you like me to send you some information that you can share with your partner? Or, perhaps we could schedule a brief call together so that he can ask any questions he may have? My goal is to ensure you both feel comfortable and confident in this decision."
"What if the program doesn't work for me?"
"I understand your concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I'm committed to providing you with the best possible support and guidance. My program is based on evidence-based practices and personalized to your unique needs. I also offer ongoing support and adjustments to the plan as needed. Many of my clients experience significant improvements within the first few weeks, and I'm confident that you'll see positive changes as well. Plus, I have a satisfaction guarantee, and if you're not completely satisfied after the first 30 days, I'll refund your investment."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's challenges, presenting your solution, and closing the call with a clear call to action.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, highlight the value and long-term cost savings, and offer flexible payment options.

3. What is a realistic monthly income goal for a new menopause specialist?

A realistic income goal for a new menopause specialist can range from $5,000 to $12,500 per month with 2-5 clients.

Key Takeaways

  • Master the Discovery Call: Structure your calls to build rapport, understand challenges, present solutions, and close effectively.
  • Handle Objections with Confidence: Prepare responses to common concerns and reframe them as opportunities to highlight the value of your program.
  • Price Your Services Strategically: Communicate your value proposition clearly and offer flexible options to meet client needs.
  • Set Realistic Income Goals: Understand your income potential and create a plan to achieve your financial objectives.
  • Believe in Your Value: Your expertise and dedication can transform women's lives during menopause.
