Research Review

While research on Reiki and addiction is still emerging, several studies suggest its potential benefits. A 2015 pilot study published in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine found that Reiki significantly reduced anxiety and depression symptoms in individuals undergoing substance abuse treatment. The study reported a 50% reduction in anxiety scores and a 40% reduction in depression scores after four weeks of Reiki treatment. A meta-analysis of 12 studies (n=547) published in Complementary Therapies in Medicine in 2018 concluded that Reiki has a moderate effect on reducing pain and anxiety. These findings suggest that Reiki can be a valuable tool for supporting individuals in addiction recovery.
