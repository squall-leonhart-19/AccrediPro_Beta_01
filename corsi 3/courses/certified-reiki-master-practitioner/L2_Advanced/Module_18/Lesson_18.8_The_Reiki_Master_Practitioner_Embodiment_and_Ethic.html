Differential Considerations

What Else Could Be Going On?

1

Hormonal Imbalance

Irregular cycles, endometriosis, and migraines could indicate significant hormonal fluctuations beyond what's managed by birth control pills. Consider further testing with an endocrinologist.

2

Mast Cell Activation Syndrome (MCAS)

History of allergies, GERD, and migraines can be associated with MCAS. Recommend further evaluation if symptoms worsen or new triggers emerge.

3

Adrenal Fatigue/Dysregulation

High stress, insomnia, and feeling overwhelmed may indicate adrenal dysregulation. Consider adrenal saliva testing and lifestyle modifications.

4

Cervical Instability

Migraines and low back pain might be related to upper cervical instability. Screen for symptoms and consider referral to a specialized chiropractor or osteopath.
