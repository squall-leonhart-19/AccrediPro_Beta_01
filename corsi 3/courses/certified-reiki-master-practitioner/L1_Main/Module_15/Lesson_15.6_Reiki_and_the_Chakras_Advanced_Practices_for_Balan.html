  • Sacral Chakra Imbalance: Manifests as creative blocks, emotional instability, reproductive issues, or lower back pain.

Reiki Chakra Meditations

Reiki chakra meditations are powerful tools for clearing and energizing the chakras. These meditations typically involve visualizing each chakra, focusing on its color, and using Reiki energy to dissolve any blockages. Here's a basic structure:

  1. Begin by grounding yourself and connecting to Reiki energy.
  2. Visualize each chakra, starting with the root chakra, and focus on its corresponding color.
  3. Send Reiki energy to each chakra, visualizing any blockages dissolving.
  4. Use affirmations related to each chakra's function (e.g., "I am safe and grounded" for the root chakra).
  5. Continue this process for each of the seven chakras.
  6. End by grounding yourself again and expressing gratitude.
