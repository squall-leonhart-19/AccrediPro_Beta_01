Energy Clearing

Energy clearing involves removing stagnant, negative, or unwanted energies from spaces, objects, and individuals. This practice is vital for maintaining a healthy and vibrant energy field, which can positively impact physical, emotional, and mental well-being.

Techniques for Clearing Spaces:

  • Smudging: Using sage, palo santo, or other sacred herbs to cleanse the space with smoke. The smoke helps to lift and transmute negative energy.
  • Sound Healing: Using singing bowls, bells, or chimes to break up stagnant energy and restore harmony. The vibrations penetrate and clear the energy field.
  • Salt: Placing bowls of salt in corners of the room to absorb negative energy. Salt is a natural purifier and can draw out unwanted energies.
  • Reiki Infusion: Infusing the space with Reiki energy, visualizing the energy filling the room with light and positivity. Use the distance healing symbol (Hon Sha Ze Sho Nen) to send Reiki to the space from a distance.

Techniques for Clearing Objects:

  • Reiki Application: Holding the object and infusing it with Reiki energy, visualizing any negative energy being released.
  • Salt Water Soak: Soaking the object in salt water to draw out negative energy.
  • Sunlight or Moonlight: Placing the object in direct sunlight or moonlight to cleanse and recharge it.

Techniques for Clearing Individuals:

  • Reiki Shower: Visualizing Reiki energy raining down on the individual, washing away any negative energy.
  • Sweeping: Using your hands to sweep the energy field, removing any stagnant or unwanted energy.
  • Chakra Balancing: Balancing the chakras to restore the natural flow of energy throughout the body.
