Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to start empowering Maria. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, it's wonderful that you're experiencing the benefits of Reiki. Now, let's bridge the gap between those sessions and your daily life. Think of Reiki as the seed, and our coaching as the soil and sunlight to help it grow. How does that resonate with you?"
MARIA:
"That's a great analogy. I feel like the seed is just sitting there, not sprouting."
YOU:
"Exactly. We're going to create a plan to nurture that seed. First, let's identify one insight or feeling you consistently experience during your Reiki sessions. What comes to mind?"
MARIA:
"A sense of calm and clarity. Like I can see things more clearly."
YOU:
"Perfect. Now, how can we bring that calm and clarity into your daily routine? Let's brainstorm three small, actionable steps you can take this week. No pressure, just ideas."

  • Maria actively brainstorms ideas and takes ownership of the process.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to bridge the gap between Reiki sessions and daily life?

Because the goal of Reiki is not just temporary relaxation, but lasting empowerment and positive change.

2. What is the key difference between giving advice and empowering a client?

Giving advice tells the client what *you* think they should do. Empowering them helps them find their *own* solutions.
