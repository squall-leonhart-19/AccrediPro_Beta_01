Gassho Meditation and Centering

Before beginning any Reiki session, it's vital to center yourself and connect with the Reiki energy. Gassho meditation is a simple yet powerful technique to achieve this.

To practice Gassho:

  1. Sit comfortably with your spine straight.
  2. Bring your hands together in prayer position at the center of your chest.
  3. Focus on the point where your middle fingers touch.
  4. Breathe deeply and allow your mind to quiet.
  5. Visualize the Reiki energy flowing through you.

Practicing Gassho for a few minutes before each session enhances your focus and strengthens your connection to the universal life force energy. This aligns with the Awareness stage of the A.W.A.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢, ensuring you are fully present and attuned to your client's needs.

Integrating Breathwork

Breathwork is a powerful tool that complements Reiki by enhancing relaxation and energy flow. Integrating simple breathwork techniques into your Reiki sessions can deepen the healing process.

Here are two effective techniques:

  • Diaphragmatic Breathing: Encourage your client to breathe deeply into their abdomen, allowing their belly to rise and fall. This promotes relaxation and reduces stress.
  • Alternate Nostril Breathing (Nadi Shodhana): Guide your client to close one nostril with their finger and inhale through the other, then switch nostrils and exhale. This balances the nervous system and promotes mental clarity.

Instruct your clients to focus on their breath throughout the Reiki session. This helps them stay present, release tension, and receive the energy more fully. Breathwork aligns with the Acceptance phase of the A.W.A.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢, encouraging clients to fully embrace the healing process.
