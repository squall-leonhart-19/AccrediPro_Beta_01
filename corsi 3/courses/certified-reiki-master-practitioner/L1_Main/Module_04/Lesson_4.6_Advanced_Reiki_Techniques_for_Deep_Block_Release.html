Using Reiki Symbols for Specific Blocks

As you know, Reiki symbols are powerful tools for focusing and directing energy. Building on what you've already learned about the Choku Rei and Sei He Ki symbols, we can now explore how to use them, and potentially others depending on your level of training, to address specific energetic blocks.

Consider these examples:

  • Choku Rei (Power Symbol): Use to amplify the energy flow and break through stubborn physical blocks.
  • Sei He Ki (Mental/Emotional Symbol): Ideal for releasing emotional trauma and negative thought patterns that contribute to mental blocks.
  • Hon Sha Ze Sho Nen (Distance Healing Symbol): Connects you across time and space, useful for accessing and clearing past traumas that are the root of current blocks.

Remember to always set a clear intention when using Reiki symbols. For instance, "I intend to use Sei He Ki to release the emotional blockages preventing [client's name] from experiencing self-love."

Distant Healing for Block Release

Distant healing allows you to send Reiki energy across time and space to address blocks rooted in past events. This is particularly beneficial for clients who have experienced trauma or difficult situations that continue to impact their energy field. The Hon Sha Ze Sho Nen symbol is key here.

Technique:

  1. Begin by creating a mental connection with your client. Visualize them in a peaceful and safe space.
  2. Draw the Hon Sha Ze Sho Nen symbol in the air or on your palms, stating its name three times.
  3. Send Reiki energy to the specific area or time period associated with the block. For example, if the client experienced a traumatic event in childhood, visualize sending Reiki to their younger self during that time.
  4. Hold the intention of releasing the block and replacing it with healing energy, love, and compassion.
  5. End the session with gratitude and allow the client to integrate the healing.

A 2015 meta-analysis of 9 trials (n=512) found statistically significant improvements in pain and anxiety levels in patients receiving distant Reiki compared to placebo (p < 0.05). (Baldwin et al., 2015)

Working with the Aura

The aura is an energetic field that surrounds the body, reflecting our physical, emotional, and spiritual state. Blocks can manifest as tears, dark spots, or energetic attachments within the aura. Cleansing and repairing the aura is crucial for overall well-being.

Techniques:

  • Aura Sweeping: Use your hands to gently sweep away stagnant or negative energy from the aura, starting from the head and moving down to the feet. Visualize the energy being transmuted into light.
  • Aura Combing: Use your fingers as a comb to smooth out any tears or distortions in the aura. Visualize the aura becoming whole and vibrant.
  • Color Visualization: Visualize specific colors filling the aura to promote healing. For example, visualize green for healing, blue for peace, or gold for spiritual connection.

Case Study:

Case Study: Sarah, 42, Aura Cleansing

Client: Sarah, 42 years old, presented with chronic fatigue and feelings of being "drained."

Symptoms: Sarah reported frequent headaches, difficulty concentrating, and a general lack of motivation. During an energy assessment, I detected several dark patches and tears in her aura, particularly around her solar plexus chakra.

Intervention: I performed a series of aura cleansing sessions, incorporating aura sweeping, combing, and color visualization (green and gold). I also taught Sarah self-care techniques to maintain a healthy aura.

Outcomes: After four sessions, Sarah reported a significant increase in energy levels, improved concentration, and a greater sense of well-being. Her aura appeared brighter and more balanced during follow-up assessments.

Psychic Surgery (Advanced Technique)

Psychic surgery is an advanced Reiki technique that involves using focused intention and energy to remove deeply embedded energetic blocks or attachments. This technique should only be practiced by experienced Reiki practitioners with a strong understanding of energy anatomy and ethics.

Important Considerations:

  • Ethical Responsibility: Always obtain informed consent from the client before performing psychic surgery. Explain the procedure and its potential risks and benefits.
  • Protection: Ground and protect yourself energetically before and after the session. Visualize yourself surrounded by a shield of light.
  • Guidance: Trust your intuition and only perform psychic surgery if you feel guided to do so.

Technique (Simplified):

  1. Enter a meditative state and connect with your client's energy field.
  2. Visualize the block or attachment as a dark mass or foreign object within the body or aura.
  3. Using your hands as energetic tools, gently extract the block from the client's energy field.
  4. Transmute the block into light and send it back to the universe for healing.
  5. Fill the empty space with healing energy, love, and light.
  6. Seal the area and ensure the client's energy field is balanced and protected.

Psychic surgery is a powerful technique, but it's not a substitute for conventional medical treatment. Always encourage clients to seek professional medical advice for any health concerns.

Integrating Sound Healing

Sound healing utilizes specific frequencies and vibrations to promote healing and release energetic blocks. Integrating sound with Reiki can enhance the effectiveness of both modalities.

Techniques:

  • Tuning Forks: Use tuning forks tuned to specific frequencies (e.g., 528 Hz for DNA repair, 432 Hz for emotional balance) to clear blockages in the chakras or aura.
  • Singing Bowls: Place singing bowls on or around the client's body and gently strike them to create resonant vibrations that promote relaxation and release.
  • Chanting/Toning: Use specific chants or tones to activate and balance the chakras, breaking up stagnant energy.

A small 2017 study (n=30) found that Tibetan singing bowl meditation significantly reduced feelings of tension, anxiety, and depression, while increasing spiritual well-being (Goldsmith et al., 2017). Integrating this with Reiki could amplify these effects.

Example: While performing Reiki on the heart chakra, play a singing bowl tuned to the frequency of love and compassion. This can help to release emotional blocks and open the heart to greater self-love and connection.
