Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal when initially addressing a client's energy blocks?

The primary goal is to create a safe space for the client to explore their feelings and experiences related to the block, building trust and rapport before diving into healing techniques.

2. How can you help a client identify the emotional root of a physical symptom?

Use open-ended questions that encourage the client to connect their physical sensations with associated emotions and memories. For example, "What emotions come up when you focus on the pain in your shoulder?"

Key Takeaways

  • Physical discomfort can often be a manifestation of blocked energy.
  • Resentment and other strong emotions can create significant energy blocks.
  • Gentle techniques, like visualization, can help clients release these blocks.
  • It's crucial to address the emotional root of a block before attempting Reiki healing.
  • Patience and empathy are essential for guiding clients through the process of releasing blocks.
