Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a successful discovery call?

The key phases are: Building rapport, understanding the problem, presenting your approach, and closing.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "It's too expensive?"

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value by highlighting the long-term benefits and the cost of not solving the problem.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key. Understand your prospect's needs and goals before the call.
  • Listen more than you talk. Let the prospect share their story and pain points.
  • Present your solution as a transformation, not just a service. Focus on the results they'll achieve.
  • Be confident in your pricing. Know your value and communicate it clearly.
  • Practice, practice, practice! The more you practice, the more natural you'll sound.
