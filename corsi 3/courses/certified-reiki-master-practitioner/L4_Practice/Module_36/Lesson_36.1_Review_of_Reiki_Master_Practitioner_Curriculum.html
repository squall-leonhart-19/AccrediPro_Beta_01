Ethical Considerations

Upholding ethical standards is paramount. Remember these key principles:

  • Client Autonomy: Respecting the client's right to make informed decisions about their care.
  • Confidentiality: Maintaining the privacy of client information.
  • Scope of Practice: Practicing within the boundaries of your training and expertise. Reiki is a complementary therapy and does not replace conventional medical treatment.
  • Professional Boundaries: Maintaining appropriate relationships with clients.
