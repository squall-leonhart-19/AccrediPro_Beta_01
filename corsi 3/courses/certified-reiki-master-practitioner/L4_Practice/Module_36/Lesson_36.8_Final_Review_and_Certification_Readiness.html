Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I believe in Reiki."
"That's perfectly understandable. Many people are skeptical at first. I encourage you to read some of the testimonials on my website from clients who were also unsure but experienced significant benefits. Ultimately, the best way to know if Reiki is right for you is to experience it firsthand. I offer a money-back guarantee if you don't feel any benefit after the first session."
"I don't have time for six sessions."
"I understand that time is a precious commodity. However, think of these sessions as an investment in your well-being. We can schedule them at your convenience, and the self-healing techniques you'll learn will save you time and energy in the long run by reducing stress and improving your overall health. We can also spread the sessions out over a longer period if that works better for you."
"It's more expensive than I expected."
"I appreciate your honesty. While the upfront cost may seem higher than a single massage, remember that this is a comprehensive program designed to provide lasting results. Consider the long-term value ‚Äì reduced stress, improved sleep, and increased energy. Many of my clients find they save money on other stress-related expenses once they start practicing Reiki regularly. I also offer payment plans to make it more accessible."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can promise to provide you with my full attention, expertise, and support throughout the program. Most of my clients experience significant improvements, and I'm confident that if you commit to the process, you'll see positive changes as well. I encourage you to read the testimonials on my website to hear about the experiences of other clients."

    Handling Objections

    "I need to think about it."
    "I understand. It's important to make the right decision. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can provide some additional information or address any remaining concerns you have right now. I'm here to help you make an informed choice."
    "I don't have the budget right now."
    "I understand that budget is a consideration. Let's explore some options. We can discuss a payment plan, or perhaps focus on the most pressing issue first and then address other concerns later. Ultimately, investing in your well-being can save you money in the long run by reducing stress-related health issues. Would you like to explore a tailored plan that fits your budget?"
    "I've tried other things that didn't work."
    "I'm sorry to hear that. It sounds like you've been through a lot. Reiki is different because it works on a holistic level, addressing the root cause of the issue rather than just the symptoms. Many of my clients have found relief with Reiki after other methods have failed. I encourage you to read their testimonials. Would you be open to trying a session and experiencing the difference for yourself?"
    "My doctor doesn't believe in Reiki."
    "I respect your doctor's opinion, and I always recommend consulting with them regarding your health. However, many healthcare professionals are becoming more open to complementary therapies like Reiki. It's important to remember that Reiki is not a replacement for medical treatment, but rather a supportive therapy that can enhance healing and well-being. I can provide you with research articles and testimonials that you can share with your doctor. Ultimately, the decision to try Reiki is yours."
