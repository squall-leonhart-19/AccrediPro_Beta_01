Practical Demonstration Guidelines

During this session, you will conduct a simulated Reiki treatment. Here are the key guidelines:

  • Simulated Client: You will be paired with another participant who will act as your client.
  • Treatment Time: Each session will last approximately 30 minutes.
  • Focus Areas: Include energy assessment, intention setting (weaving intention as per the A.W.A.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢), Reiki activation, and techniques for releasing blocks.
  • Communication: Maintain clear and compassionate communication with your simulated client throughout the session.
