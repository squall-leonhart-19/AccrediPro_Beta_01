Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for connecting with me! I'm excited to chat with you today. First, I'd love to hear a little about what prompted you to reach out."
YOU:
"I noticed you're on LinkedIn. What kind of content do you usually engage with?"
Phase 2: Uncover Needs & Desires 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about what 'feeling disconnected' looks like in your relationship. What's missing?"
YOU:
"What have you and your husband tried to improve things? What was the outcome?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal relationship look and feel like in 6 months?"
Phase 3: Position Your Program 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're longing for deeper connection, more fun, and renewed intimacy. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My couples intimacy program is designed to help couples like you rediscover that spark. Over 10 weeks, we'll work through proven exercises to improve communication, reignite passion, and create lasting intimacy. Many couples report feeling significantly closer and more in love by the end of the program."
YOU:
"Unlike traditional therapy, we focus on practical tools and actionable strategies you can implement immediately."
Phase 4: Transition to Offer & Close 5 min
YOU:
"Does this approach resonate with you, Susan? Would you like to hear more about the program details and how we can get started?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"We're really busy right now. I don't know if we have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients lead busy lives. That's why the program is structured to be manageable, with just a few hours of focused work each week. Think of it this way: what's the cost of *not* investing in your relationship? What could happen if you continue feeling disconnected? This program is an investment in your long-term happiness."
"I'm not sure my husband would be open to this."
"That's a common concern. Would it be helpful if I shared a brief overview of the program that you could share with him? It highlights the benefits for both partners. Or, I'm happy to have a quick chat with both of you to answer any questions he might have."
"It seems like a lot of money."
"I understand that the investment is significant. However, consider the value you receive: personalized guidance, proven strategies, and a renewed sense of connection with your husband. Many of my clients find that the increased happiness and intimacy are priceless. Plus, I offer flexible payment plans to make it more accessible."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes (every couple is different), I can guarantee that if you fully participate in the program and implement the strategies, you'll see positive changes in your relationship. I'm committed to supporting you every step of the way."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a 30-minute discovery call, and what is the goal of each phase?

The key phases are: 1) Rapport & Connection (build trust), 2) Uncover Needs &Desires (identify pain points), 3) Present Solution (tailor your offer), and 4) Transition to Offer & Close (ask for the sale).

2. Describe three effective strategies for handling common objections during a discovery call.

Strategies include: 1) Acknowledging the objection and empathizing with the client, 2) Reframing the objection as an opportunity, and 3) Providing social proof or testimonials.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is crucial for converting leads into paying clients.
  • A structured approach, with clear phases, ensures a smooth and effective conversation.
  • Anticipating and handling objections with confidence is essential for overcoming resistance.
  • Presenting your pricing clearly and demonstrating the value of your services builds trust.
  • Consistent practice and refinement of your discovery call skills will lead to increased client enrollment.
