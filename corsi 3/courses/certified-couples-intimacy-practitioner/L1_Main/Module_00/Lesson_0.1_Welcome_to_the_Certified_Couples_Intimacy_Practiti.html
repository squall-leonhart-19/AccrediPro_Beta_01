Your Cohort & Community

One of the most valuable aspects of this program is the community you'll be joining. You're not alone on this journey. You'll be surrounded by a cohort of like-minded women who share your passion for helping others and building fulfilling careers.

You'll be assigned to a Private Practice Group (POD), a small group of fellow students who will serve as your accountability partners, support system, and brainstorming buddies. You'll connect with your POD regularly to discuss course materials, share experiences, and offer each other encouragement.

In addition to your POD, you'll have access to our vibrant online community forum, where you can connect with students from around the world, ask questions, share resources, and celebrate your successes. This community is a safe and supportive space where you can learn, grow, and build lasting relationships.
