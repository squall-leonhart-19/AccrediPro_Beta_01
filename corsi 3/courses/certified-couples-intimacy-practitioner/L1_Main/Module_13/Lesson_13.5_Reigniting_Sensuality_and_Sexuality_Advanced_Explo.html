Case Study: Rekindling the Flame

Client: Sarah and David

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah, 42, and David, 45, had been married for 15 years. They reported a significant decline in their sexual intimacy over the past few years, with Sarah experiencing decreased desire and David feeling rejected. They both expressed a desire to reconnect but felt unsure how to reignite their spark.

Intervention:

  • Mindfulness Exercises: Introduced daily mindfulness exercises to help them become more present during intimate moments and reduce stress.
  • Communication Training: Facilitated open and honest conversations about their sexual desires, fantasies, and boundaries using "I" statements.
  • Sensate Focus: Guided them through sensate focus exercises to rediscover pleasure without the pressure of intercourse.
  • Addressing Stress: Helped them identify and manage stressors in their lives, such as work-related stress and childcare responsibilities.

Outcomes: After three months of therapy, Sarah and David reported a significant increase in their sexual intimacy and overall relationship satisfaction. Sarah experienced increased desire and David felt more connected to Sarah. They both expressed gratitude for the tools and techniques they learned to communicate openly and prioritize their sexual relationship. They reported a 40% increase in frequency of sexual activity and a 60% increase in reported satisfaction.
