Case Study: Applying the Framework

Case Study: Sarah, Age 34

Client: Sarah, a 34-year-old woman, presented with concerns about decreased sexual desire and difficulty experiencing orgasm after the birth of her second child.

Assessment: Using the P.L.E.A.S.U.R.E. Frameworkâ„¢, the practitioner explored Sarah's:

  • Personal Profile (P): History of anxiety and perfectionism.
  • Lived Experience (L): Impact of childbirth and motherhood on her body image and sexual self-esteem.
  • Education & Empowerment (E): Addressing misconceptions about postpartum sexuality.
  • Attunement & Alignment (A): Exploring her values and desires related to sex and intimacy.
  • Sensory Exploration (S): Encouraging self-pleasure and experimentation with different sensations.
  • Unleash Desires (U): Identifying barriers to desire and exploring ways to reignite passion.
  • Relationship Enhancement (R): Improving communication with her partner about sexual needs and preferences.
  • Embodiment & Evolution (E): Helping Sarah reconnect with her body and embrace her evolving sexuality.

Intervention: The practitioner used a combination of education, communication skills training, and sensate focus exercises to address Sarah's concerns.

Outcomes: After 12 sessions, Sarah reported a significant increase in sexual desire, improved ability to experience orgasm, and enhanced overall satisfaction with her sexual relationship. She also reported feeling more confident and empowered in her sexuality.
