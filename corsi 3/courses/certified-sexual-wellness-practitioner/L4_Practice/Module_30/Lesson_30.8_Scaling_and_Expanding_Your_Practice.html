Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of building rapport in the first 5 minutes of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves actively listening, showing empathy, and finding common ground with the prospect. Ask open-ended questions, validate their feelings, and share a personal anecdote if appropriate.

2. How can you effectively address the "It's too expensive" objection?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the cost as an investment in their long-term well-being and quality of life. Highlight the value they'll receive, offer payment plans, and ask what the value of solving their problem would be worth.
