Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I know it takes courage to reach out. First, tell me a little about yourself and what brought you here today."
YOU:
"I'm so glad you found me on Instagram. I love connecting with women who are ready to reclaim their sexual wellness. What specifically resonated with you?"
Phase 2: Uncover the Pain (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned low libido. Can you tell me more about what that looks like for you? How long has this been going on?"
YOU:
"How is this affecting your relationship with your husband? What have you tried so far to improve things?"
YOU:
"What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now? If we could wave a magic wand, what would you want your sex life to look like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution (10 min) 10 min

YOU:
"Great! I have two spots opening up next month. Let's discuss the investment and see if this is the right fit for you."

Handling Objections Like a Pro

Common Concerns & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. It's an investment of time, but think about the time you're already spending feeling frustrated and unhappy. My clients find that by prioritizing their sexual wellness, they actually have *more* energy and focus in other areas of their life. Plus, our sessions are flexible and can be scheduled around your needs."
"I need to talk to my partner first."
"That's completely reasonable. It's important to be on the same page. I'm happy to answer any questions he might have. Would you like me to send you some information you can share with him, or would you prefer we schedule a brief call together?"
"What if it doesn't work?"
"I understand your concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can tell you that my program is based on proven strategies and personalized support. I'm committed to working with you every step of the way, and I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the program after the first 30 days, I'll refund your investment."
"It's more than I was expecting to spend."
"I get it. It's an investment in your health and happiness. Let's break it down. How much have you already spent on things that *haven't* worked? What's the cost of continuing to feel this way? My program is designed to provide lasting results, and many clients find that the increased intimacy and confidence are priceless. I also offer payment plans to make it more accessible."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Connection & Rapport, Uncover the Pain, Present Your Solution, and Enrollment.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "It's more than I was expecting to spend?"

Acknowledge their concern, break down the value, compare it to the cost of staying where they are, and offer payment options.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key. Know your ideal client and anticipate their concerns.
  • Focus on building rapport and understanding their pain points.
  • Present your program as a solution to their specific needs.
  • Handle objections with empathy and confidence.
  • Practice your pricing presentation until it feels natural.
