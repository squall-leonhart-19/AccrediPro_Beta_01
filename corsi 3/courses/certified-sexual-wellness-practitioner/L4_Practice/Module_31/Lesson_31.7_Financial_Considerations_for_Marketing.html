Calculating Return on Investment (ROI)

Calculating ROI is essential to determine the effectiveness of your marketing efforts. The basic formula is:

ROI = (Revenue Generated - Marketing Cost) / Marketing Cost

For example, if you spend $1,000 on a Facebook ad campaign and it generates $3,000 in revenue, your ROI is:

($3,000 - $1,000) / $1,000 = 2 or 200%

This means you're getting a $2 return for every $1 you spend. Track ROI for each marketing strategy to identify whatâ€™s working and whatâ€™s not. According to a study by Nielsen, campaigns optimized for ROI can increase sales by 20-40%.

Exploring Funding Options

If you need additional funds for marketing, consider these options:
