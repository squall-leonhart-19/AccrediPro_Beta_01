Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during the initial minutes of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves expressing genuine interest in the prospect, asking open-ended questions, actively listening to their responses, and finding common ground to establish a connection.

2. How can you address the common objection that your program is "too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the investment by highlighting the long-term value and benefits they will receive, such as improved health, increased energy, and a boost in confidence. You can also offer flexible payment options to make it more accessible.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is essential for scaling your sexual wellness practice.
  • A well-structured discovery call helps you understand your prospect's needs and present your solution effectively.
  • Handling objections with empathy and confidence can turn potential "no's" into "yes's."
  • Clearly communicating your pricing and the value you offer will attract ideal clients willing to invest in their well-being.
