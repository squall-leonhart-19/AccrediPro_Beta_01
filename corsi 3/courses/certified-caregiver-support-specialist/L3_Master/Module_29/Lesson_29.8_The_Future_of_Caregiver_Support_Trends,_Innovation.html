Guiding Olivia: Teaching Points

Essential Guidance for Your Mentee

1

Emphasize Individual Variability

Remind Olivia that what works for one person may not work for another. Reactions to supplements can vary widely.

2

Discuss Potential Interactions

Explain that high doses of B vitamins or omega-3s can sometimes cause side effects like headaches, especially if there are underlying sensitivities. Nutrient interactions are critical to consider.

3

Highlight the Importance of Baselines

Discuss the need for a thorough baseline assessment, including potential nutrient deficiencies and underlying conditions, before recommending supplements.

4

Encourage Gradual Titration

Advise starting with lower doses of supplements and gradually increasing them while closely monitoring the client's response. This minimizes the risk of adverse reactions.
