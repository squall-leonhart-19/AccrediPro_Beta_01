Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing all of that. It sounds like you're carrying a lot right now. I want you to know that it's completely normal to feel overwhelmed in your situation. Many of my clients in similar circumstances feel the same way."
MARIA:
"It's just... I feel like I'm failing at everything. I love my mom, but I also need to work and take care of myself."
YOU:
"I understand. What if we could find a way for you to feel more balanced and less stressed? Let's focus on creating a plan that honors your commitments to your mother, your job, and yourself."
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, I'd like to focus on three key areas: time management, stress reduction, and setting realistic expectations. How does that sound to you?"
MARIA:
"That sounds perfect. I need help with all of those things."
YOU:
"Great. Let's start by exploring your typical day. I want to understand where your time is going and identify any potential areas for improvement. Sound good?"

You'll Know It Worked When...

  • Maria expresses relief: "I feel like someone finally understands what I'm going through."
  • She's actively engaged in the conversation and asks clarifying questions.
