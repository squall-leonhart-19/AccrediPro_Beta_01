Safety Precautions and Contraindications

Patient safety is paramount. Before applying any advanced Tui Na technique, it's crucial to assess for contraindications, such as:

  • Acute Injuries: Avoid direct manipulation of acute sprains, fractures, or dislocations.
  • Skin Infections: Do not perform Tui Na on areas with active skin infections or open wounds.
  • Certain Medical Conditions: Use caution with patients who have bleeding disorders, cancer, or severe osteoporosis.

Always communicate clearly with your client, explaining the procedure and potential sensations. Adjust your pressure and technique based on their feedback. Remember, the goal is to promote healing, not to cause pain or discomfort.
