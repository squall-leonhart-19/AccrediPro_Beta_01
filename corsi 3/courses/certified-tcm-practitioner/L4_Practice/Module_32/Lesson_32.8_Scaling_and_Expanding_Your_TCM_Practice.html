Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call! I'm excited to learn more about what brought you here. To start, could you share what resonated with you on my website?"
YOU:
"That's great to hear! Many women find that this approach is really effective. Before we dive in, is now still a good time for you?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the weight gain and low energy. When did you first notice these changes? What impact are they having on your life?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and feel completely different, what would that look like for you?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've said, it sounds like you're experiencing [briefly summarize her challenges] due to hormonal changes and lifestyle factors. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My program takes a holistic approach, combining TCM principles with modern nutritional science. We'll focus on balancing your Qi, supporting your hormones, and creating a sustainable lifestyle plan. Most clients report noticeable improvements in energy and weight within the first few weeks."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this program could be a good fit for you? ... Great! Let me explain the program details and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your existing lifestyle. We can tailor the sessions to your schedule, and the lifestyle changes are gradual and sustainable. Plus, think of the time you'll save by having more energy and focus!"
"I'm worried I won't be able to stick with it."
"That's a valid concern. That's why I provide ongoing support and accountability throughout the program. We'll work together to create realistic goals and address any challenges that come up. I'm here to guide you every step of the way. Plus, my adherence rate is over 85%, because I focus on small, manageable changes that build momentum."
"I need to discuss it with my family."
"That's perfectly fine. I can provide you with a detailed program outline that you can share with them. Would you also like to schedule a brief call where they can ask me any questions directly?"
"I've tried so many things before that haven't worked."
"I understand your frustration. What makes my approach different is that we focus on the root causes of your weight gain and low energy, not just the symptoms. TCM offers a unique perspective by balancing your body's systems and promoting natural healing. Many of my clients who have tried everything else find success with this approach."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value of your program by highlighting the long-term benefits and potential ROI. Ask them to consider what their life would look like if they solved their problem.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is crucial for building a sustainable practice.
  • A structured discovery call helps you understand your prospect's needs and present your program effectively.
  • Handling objections with confidence and empathy is essential for converting prospects into clients.
  • Clearly communicating the value of your program justifies your pricing.
  • Consistent effort and belief in your services will lead to a thriving practice.
