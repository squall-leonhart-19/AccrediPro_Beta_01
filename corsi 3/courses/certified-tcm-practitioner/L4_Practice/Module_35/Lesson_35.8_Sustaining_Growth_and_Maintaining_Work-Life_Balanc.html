Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Qualify 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I appreciate you taking the time. I saw you responded to my ad about hormonal balance - what resonated with you?"
YOU:
"That makes sense. Before we dive deeper, can you tell me a little bit about what you've already tried to address these issues?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the sleep issues. How many hours are you getting, and what does a typical night look like?"
YOU:
"And how is the stress impacting your daily life? Are you noticing any specific physical symptoms?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the most important, how important is it for you to resolve these issues in the next 90 days?"
Phase 3: Position Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're dealing with a complex set of challenges. What I specialize in is helping women over 40 find balance and reclaim their energy through a personalized approach that combines TCM principles, lifestyle adjustments, and targeted herbal support. "
YOU:
"Unlike conventional medicine, we don't just treat the symptoms. We identify the root imbalances in your body and create a plan to restore harmony. This typically involves a comprehensive assessment, customized herbal formulas, dietary recommendations, and stress management techniques."
Phase 4: Present Offer & Close 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, I believe my 12-week program would be a great fit for you. It's designed to address all the areas you're struggling with and help you achieve sustainable results. Are you open to learning more about it?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this right now."
"I understand. Time is valuable. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your existing schedule. We can work together to create a plan that works for you, and most of my clients find that the increased energy and focus they experience actually *saves* them time in the long run. How does spending 30 minutes a day for 12 weeks sound?"
"I need to research other options first."
"That's a smart approach. What specific information are you looking for in your research? Perhaps I can provide some resources or answer any questions you have right now to help you make an informed decision. I can also share some case studies from women just like you!"
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer a payment plan option. Instead of paying the full amount upfront, you can break it down into smaller, more manageable monthly payments. Would you like me to share the details of the payment plan with you?"
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"I am confident in the program's effectiveness, and I am committed to your success. I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you fully participate in the program and don't see any improvement within the first 30 days, I'll gladly refund your money. How does that sound?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the "Build Rapport & Qualify" phase of the discovery call?

To establish a connection with the prospect and determine if they are a good fit for your services.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I'm not sure if I have the time for this right now?"

Acknowledge their concern, highlight the program's flexibility, and emphasize that the increased energy and focus they'll gain will ultimately save them time.
