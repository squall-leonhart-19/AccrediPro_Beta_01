Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on yielding and transformation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your story with me. It takes courage to navigate such significant life changes. The insomnia and anxiety you're experiencing are completely understandable given what you've been through."
MARIA:
"It's been really tough. I feel like I'm constantly on edge, and the lack of sleep is making everything worse."
YOU:
"I understand. Many women find themselves at a crossroads during this stage of life, and it's an opportunity to yield to the present moment and transform into a stronger, more resilient version of themselves. We can use TCM principles to support this process."
YOU:
"Let's explore how we can work with your body's natural rhythms to calm your mind, improve your sleep, and help you embrace this new chapter with confidence. Does that sound like a path you'd like to explore?"
MARIA:
"Yes, absolutely. I'm open to anything that can help me feel better."
YOU:
"Wonderful. We'll start by looking at your daily habits and routines, and then we'll identify specific TCM techniques to help you yield to the present and transform your energy. How does that sound?"

Your Turn ‚Äî Practice Out Loud

4

Once you're comfortable with the script, adapt it to your own unique style. Keep the core message about yielding and transformation, but use your own words to express it.
