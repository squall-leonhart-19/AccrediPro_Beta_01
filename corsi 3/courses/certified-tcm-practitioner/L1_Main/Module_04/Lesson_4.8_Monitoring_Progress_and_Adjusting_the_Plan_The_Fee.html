Key Takeaways

  • Address adherence challenges with empathy and understanding.
  • Focus on small, sustainable changes rather than drastic overhauls.
  • Help clients identify specific, measurable action steps.
  • Create accountability systems to track progress and provide support.
  • Celebrate small victories to build momentum and confidence.
