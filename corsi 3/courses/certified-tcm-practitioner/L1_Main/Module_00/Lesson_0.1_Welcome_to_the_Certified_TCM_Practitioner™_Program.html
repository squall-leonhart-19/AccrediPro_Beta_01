What You'll Learn & Become

This program is designed to transform you into a confident and competent TCM practitioner. You'll gain a deep understanding of:

  • TCM Theory: The fundamental principles of Yin and Yang, the Five Elements, Qi, Blood, and Body Fluids.
  • Diagnostic Skills: Tongue diagnosis, pulse diagnosis, and questioning techniques to identify patterns of disharmony.
  • Acupuncture: Point location, needling techniques, and treatment protocols for a wide range of conditions.
  • Herbal Medicine: The properties and uses of common Chinese herbs, formula construction, and herbal modifications.
  • Business & Ethics: Setting up a successful TCM practice, marketing your services, and adhering to ethical guidelines.

But it's not just about knowledge. You'll also develop essential skills like:

  • Critical Thinking: Analyzing complex cases and developing effective treatment plans.
  • Communication: Building rapport with clients and explaining TCM concepts in an accessible way.
  • Hands-On Practice: Mastering needling techniques and herbal preparation through supervised practice.
  • Self-Care: Maintaining your own health and well-being to avoid burnout.

By the end of this program, you'll be able to:

  • Confidently assess clients and identify TCM patterns of disharmony.
  • Develop personalized treatment plans using acupuncture, herbal medicine, and lifestyle recommendations.
  • Effectively communicate with clients and build strong therapeutic relationships.
  • Run a successful and ethical TCM practice.
