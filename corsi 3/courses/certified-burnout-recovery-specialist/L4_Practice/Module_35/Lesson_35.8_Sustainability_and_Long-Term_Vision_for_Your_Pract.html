Business Practice Lab

Let's build systems to scale your practice. This lab focuses on automating key business tasks to free up your time.

Your Practice Vision

Before diving into systems, let's revisit your vision. Scaling isn't just about more clients; it's about creating a sustainable, impactful practice that aligns with your goals. Imagine you want to work only 20 hours a week, serving high-value clients who are truly committed to their burnout recovery. How do you get there?

Identifying Bottlenecks

Where are you spending the most time *outside* of client sessions? Common bottlenecks include:

  • Scheduling and reminders
  • Client onboarding
  • Invoicing and payments
  • Marketing and lead generation

This lab will focus on creating systems to streamline these tasks.

System 1: Automated Scheduling

Imagine never having to manually schedule another appointment. Tools like Acuity Scheduling or Calendly integrate with your calendar and allow clients to book directly.

System 2: Client Onboarding

Create a streamlined onboarding process. This could include:

  • Automated welcome email with program details
  • Digital intake forms (using tools like Jotform or Google Forms)
  • Pre-recorded welcome video explaining your approach

System 3: Streamlined Payments

Use a platform like Stripe or PayPal to automate invoicing and payments. Set up recurring billing for ongoing programs.

System 4: Content Marketing Automation

Consistent content marketing is crucial. Use a tool like Buffer or Hootsuite to schedule social media posts in advance. Create email sequences using Mailchimp or ConvertKit to nurture leads.

Case Study: Maria's Transformation

Client: Maria, 48, former teacher

Challenge: Overwhelmed by administrative tasks, spending more time on business management than client work.

Intervention: Implemented automated scheduling, digital intake forms, and a streamlined payment system.

Outcome: Maria freed up 10 hours per week, allowing her to focus on client care and develop new program offerings. Her monthly income increased by 30% within three months.

Key Takeaways

  • Identify your biggest time-wasters outside of client sessions.
  • Automate scheduling, onboarding, and payments.
  • Use content marketing automation to nurture leads.
  • Scaling your practice is about creating a sustainable system, not just working harder.
