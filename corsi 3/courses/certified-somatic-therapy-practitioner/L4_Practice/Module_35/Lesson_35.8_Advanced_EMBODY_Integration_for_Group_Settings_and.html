Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, so glad you could make it! Thanks for downloading my guide. What resonated with you most?"
YOU:
"I work with women just like you, who are feeling stressed and disconnected. Before we dive in, tell me a bit about what's been going on for you lately."
Phase 2: Uncover the Pain 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling overwhelmed. Can you describe what that feels like in your body?"
YOU:
"And how is this impacting your daily life? What are you missing out on?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address this? What worked, and what didn't?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, what I'm hearing is that you're experiencing [summarize her pain points] and that's leading to [impact on her life]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"The reason my approach is so effective is that we go beyond surface-level solutions. We use somatic techniques to address the root causes of your stress and disconnection, helping you reconnect with your body and emotions in a safe and sustainable way. My 12-week program is designed to do just that."
YOU:
"I've seen women like you go from feeling completely overwhelmed to feeling calm, confident, and in control in as little as 6 weeks. Imagine what that would be like for you."
Phase 4: The Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I truly believe I can help you. Does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me tell you a bit more about the program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. It's about prioritizing. Many of my clients are busy professionals just like you. We can tailor the program to fit your schedule. How much time per week do you currently spend feeling stressed or overwhelmed? What if we could reclaim that time and turn it into productive, joyful moments?"
"I've tried other things before, and they didn't work."
"I hear you, and it's frustrating to invest time and money without seeing results. The difference with somatic therapy is that we're addressing the root cause, not just masking the symptoms. We're working WITH your body, not against it. Many of my clients come to me after trying everything else, and they're amazed at the results."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer a payment plan to make it more accessible. We can break it down into [number] monthly installments of [amount]. Would that work better for you?"
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"I'm confident that you'll see results if you fully commit to the process. I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you complete all the sessions and don't feel a significant improvement, I'll offer you a partial refund. I'm that confident in the power of this work."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Welcome & Connection, Uncover the Pain, Present Your Solution, and The Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need for time, then ask clarifying questions to understand their specific concerns. Offer to provide additional information or schedule a follow-up call.

3. What is the importance of confidently stating your pricing?

Confidently stating your pricing demonstrates your belief in the value you offer. It also sets the tone for a professional and respectful transaction.

Key Takeaways

  • Master the Discovery Call: Structure your calls for maximum impact.
  • Handle Objections with Grace: Prepare for common objections and have confident responses ready.
  • Price with Confidence: Know your value and communicate it clearly.
  • Practice Makes Perfect: The more you practice, the more natural and effective you'll become.
  • Believe in Your Value: Your clients are investing in a transformation.
