The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured into four distinct tiers, each building upon the previous one to provide a holistic and in-depth learning experience:

  1. L1 FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15): This tier lays the groundwork for your somatic therapy practice. You'll learn the core methodology, fundamental principles, and essential techniques. Think of it as building a solid foundation for a beautiful house.
  2. L2 ADVANCED (Modules 16-23): In this tier, we delve into more complex cases and clinical depth. You'll explore advanced techniques, learn to navigate challenging client situations, and refine your diagnostic abilities. This is where you start to develop your expertise.
  3. L3 MASTER (Modules 24-29): The Master tier focuses on leadership and mentorship. You'll learn how to train others, develop supervision skills, and contribute to the growing field of somatic therapy.
  4. L4 PRACTICE (Modules 30-36): The final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. We'll cover marketing strategies, ethical considerations, and practical tips for establishing a thriving somatic therapy practice.
