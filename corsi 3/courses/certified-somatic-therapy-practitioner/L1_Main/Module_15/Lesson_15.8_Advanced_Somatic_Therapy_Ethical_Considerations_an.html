Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, incorporating somatic resonance. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your story. I can sense the weight of these transitions in your voice and posture. Before we dive in, let's take a moment to just *feel* that. Can you place a hand on your heart and describe what sensations you notice?"
MARIA:
"Um... I guess a little tightness in my chest. And my shoulders feel really tense."
YOU:
"That's perfect. There's no right or wrong answer. Now, let's gently shift our focus. Imagine a time when you felt truly confident and at peace. Where do you feel *that* in your body?"
MARIA:
"Hmm... maybe a sense of lightness in my stomach? And my breathing feels easier."
YOU:
"Excellent. We're already accessing your inner resources. Now, keeping that sense of lightness in mind, let's explore how we can bring more of *that* feeling into your daily life and decision-making process."
YOU:
"How does that sound as a starting point?"
