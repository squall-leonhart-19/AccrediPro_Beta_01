Key Takeaways

  • Prioritize Validation: Always validate the client's experience before offering solutions.
  • Define Integration: Clearly explain what integration means in a somatic context.
  • Emphasize Self-Compassion: Encourage the client to be kind and patient with herself.
  • Practical Application: Focus on how to integrate somatic practices into daily life.
  • Listen to the Body: Guide the client to reconnect with and listen to her body's signals.
