Your Coaching Script

Here's a powerful opening to help Diane feel seen and understood. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Diane, thank you for being so open. It takes courage to admit you're feeling lost, especially after dedicating so much to others. I want you to know that feeling this way is incredibly common as women enter this new chapter of life."
DIANE:
"It's nice to hear I'm not alone. I feel like everyone else has it figured out."
YOU:
"That's a common illusion, Diane. The truth is, many women are quietly grappling with similar questions. The good news is, this is an incredible opportunity to design a life that truly reflects your desires and values."
YOU:
"Let's start by exploring what truly lights you up. What activities, even from your childhood, bring you joy and energy?"
DIANE:
"Wow, I haven't thought about that in years... I used to love painting, and I was really into hiking."
YOU:
"Wonderful! Let's explore how we can weave those passions back into your life, even in small ways. We'll also address those fears you mentioned, step by step. How does that sound?"
