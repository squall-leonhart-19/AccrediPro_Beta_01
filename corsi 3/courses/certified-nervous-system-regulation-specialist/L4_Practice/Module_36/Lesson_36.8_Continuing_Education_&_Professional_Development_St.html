YOU:
"Great! And what prompted you to schedule this call now? What's the biggest challenge you're facing?"

Handling Objections

Addressing Common Concerns

"I'm not sure I have the time for this."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. This program is designed to integrate seamlessly into your life. We'll work together to find small, sustainable changes that fit your schedule. Plus, feeling less stressed and more energetic will actually give you MORE time in the long run."
"I've tried other things, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. The key difference is that we're not just treating symptoms. We're addressing the root causes by regulating your nervous system. This creates a fundamental shift in your body's ability to handle stress and restore balance. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee, so you can feel confident in your investment."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes (because everyone is different), I can guarantee my commitment to providing you with the best possible support and guidance. Most of my clients experience significant improvements in their energy, sleep, and stress levels within the first few weeks. I also provide regular check-ins and adjustments to the program to ensure you're on track."
"I need to discuss it with my partner."
"That's completely understandable. To help with that conversation, I can send you a detailed program overview and some client testimonials. Would you also like to schedule a brief call where all three of us can discuss any concerns or questions?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the first 5 minutes of a discovery call?

Focus on genuine connection. Ask about their background, how they found you, and their initial interest in your services. Show empathy and active listening.

2. How can you address the "I need to think about it" objection effectively?

Acknowledge their need for time. Ask clarifying questions to understand their specific concerns (investment, time commitment, etc.). Offer additional information or support to help them make a confident decision.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is crucial for converting prospects into paying clients.
  • Empathy and active listening are essential for building trust and understanding client needs.
  • Confidently presenting your pricing and addressing objections can increase your conversion rate.
  • Realistic income goals are achievable with consistent effort and effective client acquisition strategies.
