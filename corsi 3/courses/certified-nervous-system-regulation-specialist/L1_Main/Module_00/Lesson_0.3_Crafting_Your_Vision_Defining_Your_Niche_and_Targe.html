The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a 4-tier learning journey, designed to build your expertise progressively:

  1. Level 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build a solid foundation in our core methodology and fundamental principles. You'll learn the essential concepts of nervous system regulation and how they impact overall health and well-being.
  2. Level 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): In this tier, we delve into more complex cases and explore clinical applications in greater depth. You'll learn how to adapt your approach to address a wider range of client needs.
  3. Level 3: Master (Modules 24-29): Here, you'll develop the skills to train others and provide supervision. This level focuses on leadership and mentorship, preparing you to guide other practitioners.
  4. Level 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): This final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. We'll cover marketing strategies, business development, and ethical considerations to help you create a thriving practice.
