Your Teaching Approach

Key Points to Cover with Aisha

1

Your Feedback Dialogue

How to Deliver Constructive Feedback

Start with Affirmation
"Aisha, it's wonderful that you're seeking guidance. Handling strong emotional releases can be challenging, even for experienced practitioners. You‚Äôre already showing great initiative."

"Remember, emotional releases are a natural part of chakra healing. Preparing clients beforehand and having a toolkit of supportive techniques can make these moments more manageable for both of you."

"Let's brainstorm some grounding techniques Sarah can use. We'll also explore ways to help her verbalize her emotions without feeling overwhelmed."

"How do you envision the next session with Sarah? What specific steps will you take to support her and yourself?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the most important thing to remember when a mentee presents a challenging case?

The most important thing is to create a safe and supportive environment where the mentee feels comfortable sharing their concerns and learning from the experience.

2. How can you help a mentee who is struggling with setting boundaries with clients?

You can help by sharing your own experiences, providing specific examples of healthy boundaries, and role-playing difficult scenarios.

Key Takeaways

  • Mentoring is about guiding, not dictating. Encourage critical thinking and self-reflection.
  • Creating a safe and supportive environment is crucial for mentee growth.
  • Share your own experiences and challenges to normalize the learning process.
  • Emphasize the importance of boundaries and self-care for both the practitioner and the client.
  • Remember, effective mentoring multiplies your impact on the world.
