Vibrational medicine operates on the principle that everything in the universe vibrates at a specific frequency. When these frequencies become imbalanced, it can manifest as physical, emotional, or spiritual dis-ease. In chakra healing, we use various vibrational modalities to restore harmony.

Using dowsing, the root chakra was identified as significantly blocked, linked to past feelings of insecurity and lack of grounding. The heart chakra also showed imbalances, reflecting difficulties in self-love and emotional expression.

Intervention:

  • Shamanic Journeying: Maria was guided on a shamanic journey to connect with her power animal, helping her reclaim her personal power and sense of grounding.
  • EFT: EFT was used to release emotional blockages related to past insecurities and self-doubt, focusing on the root and heart chakras.

Outcomes: After several sessions, Maria reported a significant increase in energy, reduced anxiety, and a renewed sense of purpose. She began exploring new career opportunities and reported feeling more connected to herself and her loved ones. This case highlights how integrating shamanic practices and EFT can address deep-seated energetic imbalances and promote profound healing.
