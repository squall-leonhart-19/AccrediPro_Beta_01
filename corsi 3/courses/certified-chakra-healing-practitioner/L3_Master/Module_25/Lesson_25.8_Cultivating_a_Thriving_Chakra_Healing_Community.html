Her Question

The Case She Presents

Sarah's Client Intake Form Review

What Sarah Asks You:

"I've attached my new client intake form. I'm not sure if I'm asking the right questions or if I'm missing anything important. Also, what chakra assessments would you recommend for a first session?"

What You Need to Explore:

  • Is the intake form comprehensive enough to gather relevant client history?
  • Are there any questions that might be leading or biased?
  • Does Sarah understand the purpose of each question and how it relates to chakra assessments?
  • Is she clear on the scope of practice and what types of information she can and cannot gather?

Your Teaching Approach

Key Points to Cover with Sarah

1

Validate Her Concerns

"It's completely normal to feel nervous before your first client. We all start somewhere, and it's great that you're seeking support."

2

Review the Intake Form

Go through each section of the intake form, explaining the rationale behind each question and suggesting improvements for clarity and relevance. For example, you might suggest adding questions about past trauma or major life events.

3

Recommend Chakra Assessments

Suggest a combination of simple and insightful assessments. For example, a pendulum reading, a body scan, or guided visualization to identify potential chakra imbalances. Explain how to interpret the results and use them to guide the session.

4

Emphasize Intuition

Encourage Sarah to trust her intuition during the session. Remind her that her unique perspective and insights are valuable assets. Share examples of how you've used your intuition to guide your own sessions.

Your Feedback Dialogue

How to Deliver Constructive Feedback

Start with Appreciation
"Sarah, I really appreciate you sharing your intake form with me. It shows that you're committed to providing the best possible care for your clients."
Ask for Her Perspective
"Before I give my feedback, what are your thoughts on the intake form? Is there anything you're particularly unsure about?"
Provide Specific Suggestions
"I noticed that you don't have any questions about past trauma. It might be helpful to add a section on that, as past trauma can often manifest as chakra imbalances."
Offer Encouragement
"You're doing great, Sarah! Remember to trust your intuition and have confidence in your abilities. You've got this!"
