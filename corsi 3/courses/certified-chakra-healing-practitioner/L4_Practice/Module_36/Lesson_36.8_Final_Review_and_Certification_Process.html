Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. It's about prioritizing. My program requires about 2-3 hours per week. Think of it as investing in your well-being, which will ultimately make you more productive and present in all areas of your life. Plus, we can work together to schedule sessions that fit your lifestyle. How does that sound?"
"I need to think about it."
"I respect that. What specifically do you need to think about? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision. Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else?"
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't *guarantee* specific outcomes because everyone's journey is unique, I can tell you that my clients consistently report significant improvements in their stress levels, sleep quality, and overall well-being. In fact, a recent client survey showed that 92% of my clients reported feeling less stressed after completing my program. I'm confident that if you commit to the process, you'll see positive changes."
"I'm worried it won't be effective for me."
"That's a valid concern. My approach is tailored to each individual. We start with a thorough assessment to understand your unique energy system and identify specific imbalances. We then create a personalized healing plan that addresses your specific needs. Plus, I offer ongoing support and guidance throughout the program to ensure you get the most out of it. How does that sound?"

Acknowledge their need, then ask clarifying questions like, "What specifically do you need to think about?" to address their underlying concerns.
