Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to determine your business structure (sole proprietor, LLC, etc.)?

Choosing the right business structure impacts your liability, taxes, and administrative requirements.

2. What are the key elements of informed consent in Chakra Healing practice?

Informed consent should include the nature of Chakra Healing, potential benefits and risks, client rights, and the practitioner's qualifications.

Key Takeaways

  • Understanding legal requirements is crucial for establishing a legitimate practice.
  • Informed consent protects both you and your clients.
  • Professional liability insurance provides a safety net against potential claims.
  • Ethical practices build trust and credibility.
  • Offering legal and compliance guidance can create an additional income stream.
