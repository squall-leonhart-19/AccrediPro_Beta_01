Finding your niche is about identifying a specific segment of the market that you can serve exceptionally well. This allows you to focus your marketing efforts and become known as the go-to expert for a particular type of client or issue. For chakra healing, this could involve targeting specific client demographics (e.g., entrepreneurs, athletes, creatives) or specializing in particular chakra imbalances (e.g., anxiety, burnout, relationship issues).
