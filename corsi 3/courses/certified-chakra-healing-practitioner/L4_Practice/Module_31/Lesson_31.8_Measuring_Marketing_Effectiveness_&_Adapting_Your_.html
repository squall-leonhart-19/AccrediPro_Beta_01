Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the "Warm Welcome & Connection" phase of the discovery call?

To establish rapport with the potential client and understand their initial reason for seeking chakra healing.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I'm not sure if I have the time for this right now"?

Acknowledge their concern and suggest exploring ways to integrate sessions into their schedule without adding extra stress. Emphasize the potential time and energy gains from reduced stress and improved focus.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key: Thoroughly understand your ideal client's needs and challenges.
  • Structure your discovery call: Follow a clear outline to guide the conversation and ensure you address all key points.
  • Master objection handling: Anticipate common concerns and prepare confident, empathetic responses.
  • Present your value clearly: Confidently articulate the benefits of your program and the investment required.
  • Practice your call-to-action: Close the call with a clear and compelling invitation to begin working together.
