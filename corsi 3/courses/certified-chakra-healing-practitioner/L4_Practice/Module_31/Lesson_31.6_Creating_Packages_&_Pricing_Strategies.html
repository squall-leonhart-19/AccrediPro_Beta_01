6. Clear Pricing & Payment Policies

Avoid confusion and build trust by clearly communicating your pricing and payment policies to clients upfront. Display your pricing information prominently on your website and in your marketing materials.

Consider offering payment plans or sliding scale options to make your services more accessible to clients with limited budgets.
