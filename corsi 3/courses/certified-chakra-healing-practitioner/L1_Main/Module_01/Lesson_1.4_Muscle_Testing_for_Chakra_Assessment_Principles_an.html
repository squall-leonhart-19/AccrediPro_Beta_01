Introduction to Applied Kinesiology

Applied Kinesiology is based on the principle that muscle strength is directly related to the body's overall energy flow. When a chakra is balanced and energy is flowing freely, the corresponding muscle will test strong. Conversely, if a chakra is blocked or imbalanced, the muscle will test weak. This is the foundation upon which you will Assess imbalances.

Dr. George Goodheart, a chiropractor, developed AK in the 1960s. Since then, it has been adapted and refined by many practitioners. While some consider AK controversial, many holistic practitioners find it a valuable tool when used ethically and with proper training. Remember from Lesson 1, always practice within your scope.

For many women like you, this skill can be a game-changer. Imagine being able to provide clients with immediate, tangible feedback about their energetic state! This can be incredibly empowering for both you and your clients.

Proper Muscle Testing Technique

Accuracy is paramount in muscle testing. Hereâ€™s a step-by-step guide to ensure you obtain reliable results:

  1. Client Positioning: Have your client stand or sit comfortably. Ensure they are well-hydrated, as dehydration can affect muscle strength.
  2. Muscle Isolation: Typically, the arm is used. Have the client extend their arm straight out to the side, parallel to the floor.
  3. Applying Pressure: Explain to the client that you will apply gentle, steady pressure downwards on their arm. Instruct them to resist the pressure and hold their arm strong.
  4. Consistent Pressure: Apply pressure gradually and consistently. Avoid jerky or sudden movements. The goal is to assess the muscle's ability to maintain its position against resistance.
  5. Interpreting Results: A "strong" muscle resists the pressure and maintains its position. A "weak" muscle gives way under pressure, indicating a potential energetic imbalance.

Important Note: Itâ€™s crucial to differentiate between a muscle that is genuinely weak due to energetic imbalance and one that is weak due to physical fatigue. Always start with a neutral test to establish a baseline.

Using Surrogate Testing

Surrogate testing is a valuable adaptation of muscle testing that allows you to assess clients who are unable to be tested directly due to physical limitations, such as infants, elderly individuals, or those with disabilities. In surrogate testing, a healthy individual acts as an intermediary, holding the client or maintaining physical contact while you test the surrogate's muscle strength.

The principle behind surrogate testing is that the surrogate's energy field becomes temporarily linked to the client's, allowing you to assess the client's energetic state through the surrogate's muscle response. While this technique requires practice and sensitivity, it can be a powerful tool for assessing individuals whoare otherwise difficult to test.

Example: To perform surrogate testing on an infant, the parent can hold the baby while you test the parent's arm. When questioning the infant's chakra health, the parent's muscle response will reflect the infant's energetic state. Ensure you have consent from both the surrogate and, if possible, the client or their guardian.

Formulating Clear Questions

The clarity of your questions is critical for accurate muscle testing. Avoid vague or ambiguous inquiries. Instead, formulate specific questions that target individual chakras and potential imbalances.

Here are some examples of effective questions:

  • "Is [client's name]'s root chakra balanced?"
  • "Is there an energetic blockage in [client's name]'s sacral chakra?"
  • "Is [client's name]'s solar plexus chakra associated with feelings of overwhelm?"

Remember to frame your questions in a way that elicits a clear "yes" or "no" response, which can then be correlated with the muscle test result. A strong muscle indicates a "yes" response (chakra is balanced), while a weak muscle indicates a "no" response (chakra is imbalanced).

Pro Tip: Before beginning, clearly state your intention to receive accurate and truthful information. This helps to establish a clear energetic connection and minimize interference.
