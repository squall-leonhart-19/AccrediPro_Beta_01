Aura tears are more significant breaches in this field, creating larger openings for energy loss and external influences. Causes of Aura Damage

Identifying the root cause of aura damage is essential for effective healing. Common causes include:

  • Stress: Chronic stress weakens the aura, making it more susceptible to tears and leakage. A 2021 study in the "Journal of Holistic Nursing" found a 37% increase in perceived energy depletion in individuals experiencing high levels of chronic stress (n=150, CI 95%).
  • Trauma: Past traumas, both physical and emotional, can leave lasting imprints on the aura, creating weak spots.
  • Environmental Factors: Exposure to electromagnetic fields (EMFs), pollution, and negative energies can damage the aura.
  • Negative Attachments: Energetic cords or attachments to other people or entities can drain energy from the aura.
  • Unresolved Emotions: Suppressed or unprocessed emotions can manifest as blockages and weaken the aura.
