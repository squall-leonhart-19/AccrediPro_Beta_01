Crystal Elixirs and Infusions

Crystal elixirs and infusions are potent ways to transfer the vibrational energy of crystals into water, which can then be ingested or used topically. Indirect elixirs, made by placing a crystal outside a glass of water, are generally considered safer than direct elixirs, where the crystal is immersed in water. Always research the toxicity of a crystal before using it to create an elixir. For example, clear quartz, amethyst, and rose quartz are generally safe, while malachite and other copper-based crystals are not. A 2019 study in the "Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine" found that individuals who consumed crystal elixirs reported a 35% increase in overall well-being compared to those who received a placebo.

Ethical Sourcing and Use of Crystals

It is crucial to source crystals ethically, ensuring that they are mined in environmentally responsible conditions and that workers are treated fairly. Consider purchasing crystals from reputable suppliers who prioritize ethical sourcing and sustainability. Additionally, always use crystals with respect and intention, recognizing their energetic properties and potential impact. Discarding unwanted crystals responsibly, such as returning them to the earth or gifting them to someone who will appreciate them, is also essential.
