Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to connect Maria's symptoms to the Sacral Chakra early in the conversation?

Connecting her symptoms to the Sacral Chakra provides a framework for understanding her emotional state and offers a potential path to healing that resonates with the course methodology.

"A dried-up river," "a dimmed light," or "a disconnected wire" are all relatable analogies that convey the sense of blockage or stagnation.

Key Takeaways

  • Use simple, relatable language and avoid jargon.
  • Frame chakra work as a gentle, gradual process, not a quick fix.
