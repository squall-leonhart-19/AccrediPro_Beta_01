Check Your Understanding

1. What is the most important thing to do BEFORE suggesting any chakra work?

Build rapport and validate the client's feelings. Showing empathy is crucial before introducing any specific techniques.

2. Why is it important to address the root cause (disconnection) rather than just the symptoms (sleep, digestion)?

Treating only the symptoms provides temporary relief, but the underlying issue will persist. Addressing the root cause leads to more lasting and meaningful healing.

Key Takeaways

  • Acknowledge and validate the client's feelings before offering solutions.
  • Focus on the root cause of the issue, not just the symptoms.
  • Use chakra work to help clients reconnect with their authentic selves.
  • Offer a path toward healing and reconnection, not a quick fix.
  • Practice empathy and create a safe space for clients to share their experiences.
