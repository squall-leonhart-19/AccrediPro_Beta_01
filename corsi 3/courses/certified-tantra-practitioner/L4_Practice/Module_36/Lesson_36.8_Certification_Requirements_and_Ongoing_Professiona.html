Handling Objections

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Life gets busy. My program is designed to be flexible. We can work around your schedule. How about we identify 2-3 hours per week that you can dedicate to yourself? Remember, investing in yourself is an investment in all areas of your life."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How can you address a prospect's concern about the cost of your program?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the cost as an investment in their well-being and highlight the potential return on investment (ROI) in terms of improved quality of life.
