Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during the initial minutes of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves expressing gratitude for their time, creating a connection by referencing shared experiences (e.g., the online workshop), and setting a clear intention for the call.

2. How can you effectively address a potential client's concern about the time commitment required for your program?

Acknowledge their concern, emphasize the program's flexibility, tailor sessions to their schedule, provide self-paced resources, and address the specific part of the time commitment that feels most challenging.

Key Takeaways

  • Focus on building genuine connections with potential clients by understanding their desires and challenges.
  • Address objections with empathy and provide clear, confident responses that highlight the value of your services.
  • Present your pricing with conviction and emphasize the transformative outcomes your clients can achieve.
  • Practice your call-to-action to confidently invite potential clients to embark on a journey with you.
  • Remember that sustainable growth comes from consistent effort, dedication, and a genuine commitment to your clients' well-being.
