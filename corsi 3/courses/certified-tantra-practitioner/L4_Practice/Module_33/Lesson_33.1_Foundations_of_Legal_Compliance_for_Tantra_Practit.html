Instead, focus on the potential benefits of tantra practices, such as increased self-awareness, enhanced intimacy, and a deeper connection to one's body. Professional Liability Insurance

Professional liability insurance, also known as malpractice insurance, is essential for mitigating potential risks associated with tantra practice. While the risk of legal action may seem low, even unfounded claims can be costly to defend. Insurance protects you from financial losses resulting from allegations of negligence, misconduct, or harm. This aligns with the Safety (S) principle of the S.A.C.R.E.D. Frameworkâ„¢, protecting both you and your clients.

Types of Coverage:

  • General Liability: Covers bodily injury or property damage that occurs on your premises.
  • Professional Liability: Covers claims of negligence or errors in your professional services.
  • Cyber Liability: Protects against data breaches and cyberattacks, especially if you store client information electronically.

A 2022 survey by the "American Association of Professional Liability Insurers" found that the average cost to defend a professional liability claim was $18,000, even if the claim was ultimately dismissed. (AAPLI, 2022)
