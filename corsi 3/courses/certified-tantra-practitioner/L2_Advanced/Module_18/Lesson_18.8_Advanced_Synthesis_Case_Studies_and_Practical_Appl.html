Clinical Reasoning & Tantric Integration

Unpacking Eleanor's Case

Step 1: Identify Core Imbalances
Eleanor presents with a complex interplay of physical pain (endometriosis), hormonal influences (OCP use), emotional distress (anxiety, disconnection), and relational challenges. The core imbalances appear to be a disconnection from her body's sensations and a stagnation of sexual energy.
Step 2: Apply Tantric Principles
From a tantric perspective, we can view Eleanor's symptoms as manifestations of blocked energy flow within the Swadhisthana Chakra (sacral chakra), associated with sexuality, creativity, and emotional well-being. Her disconnection from her body reflects a diminished capacity to experience pleasure and intimacy.
Step 3: Consider the Impact of Medications
The oral contraceptive pill, while managing endometriosis symptoms, can suppress natural hormone fluctuations and potentially diminish libido and vaginal lubrication. Propranolol may also contribute to sexual dysfunction. It is important to work with her physician.
Step 4: Develop a Holistic Intervention Strategy
A comprehensive approach should address the physical pain, hormonal balance, emotional well-being, and relational dynamics. This requires a phased protocol incorporating tantric practices, lifestyle modifications, and potentially collaboration with other healthcare providers.

Explore potential past trauma (sexual, emotional, or physical) that may be contributing to her disconnection from her body and sexuality.
