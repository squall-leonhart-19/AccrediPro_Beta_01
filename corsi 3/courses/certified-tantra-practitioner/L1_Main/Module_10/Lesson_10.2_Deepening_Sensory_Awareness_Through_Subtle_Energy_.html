Refining Sensory Awareness

Let's delve into refining your sensory awareness to perceive subtle energy. This involves specific practices to heighten your senses beyond their ordinary capacity. Remember, as women over 40, our intuition and sensory abilities are often heightened, offering a unique advantage in this field. Embrace your innate wisdom and allow it to guide you.

Here are some practices to try:

  • Hand-Sensing Exercises: Hold your hands a few inches apart, palms facing each other. Focus on the space between your hands. What sensations do you notice? Heat? Tingling? Pressure? Practice this daily for 10-15 minutes.
  • Chakra Awareness: Sit comfortably and bring your awareness to each of your seven major chakras, one at a time. Visualize each chakra as a spinning wheel of light. Notice any sensations associated with each chakra. Are they open and flowing, or blocked and stagnant?

These exercises are designed to awaken your subtle senses. Don't be discouraged if you don't feel anything immediately. Like any skill, it takes practice. The key is to approach these exercises with patience, curiosity, and a willingness to explore the unknown.

Partner Exercise: Sensing Energy Fields

Now, let's put these principles into practice with a partner exercise. This exercise is designed to help you sense and map each other's energy fields with compassionate awareness. Remember, this is an opportunity for exploration and discovery, not judgment or criticism.

Instructions:

  1. Find a partner and designate one person as the "sensor" and the other as the "receiver."
  2. The receiver stands comfortably with their eyes closed.
  3. The sensor stands a few feet away from the receiver and slowly moves their hands around the receiver's body, paying attention to any sensations they feel.
  4. The sensor maps the receiver's energy field, noting any areas of heat, tingling, pressure, or other sensations.
  5. After 5-10 minutes, the sensor shares their observations with the receiver.
  6. Switch roles and repeat the exercise.

Important Considerations:

  • Approach this exercise with an open mind and a compassionate heart.
  • Respect your partner's boundaries and ask for permission before touching them.
  • Focus on sensing the energy field, not judging or analyzing it.

This exercise provides a tangible experience of sensing energy fields and reinforces the importance of compassionate awareness in energy work. It also allows you to practice differentiating between your own energy and the energy of another person.
