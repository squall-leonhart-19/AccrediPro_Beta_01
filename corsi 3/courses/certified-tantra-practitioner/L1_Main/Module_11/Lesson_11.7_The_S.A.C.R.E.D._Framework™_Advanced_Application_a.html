Case Study: Sarah, 38, Facing Career Burnout

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah, a marketing executive, reported chronic fatigue, cynicism, and a sense of detachment from her work and relationships. She felt overwhelmed and lacked motivation, experiencing frequent anxiety and difficulty sleeping.

S.A.C.R.E.D. Intervention:

  1. Sensory Awareness: Guided Sarah through mindful body scans to reconnect with physical sensations, noticing areas of tension and discomfort.
  2. Authentic Expression: Explored her core values and passions, identifying a disconnect between her current career path and her authentic self.
  3. Conscious Connection: Facilitated exercises to improve communication and build deeper connections with colleagues and loved ones.
  4. Release & Reframe: Used cognitive restructuring techniques to challenge negative thought patterns and limiting beliefs about her capabilities and worth.
  5. Embodied Integration: Introduced somatic movement practices to release pent-up emotions and integrate newfound insights into her physical being.
  6. Divine Union: Encouraged Sarah to explore her spiritual beliefs and connect with a sense of purpose beyond her career.

Outcomes: After 12 weeks, Sarah reported a significant reduction in burnout symptoms, improved sleep quality, and a renewed sense of purpose. She made proactive changes in her work environment, delegating tasks and setting healthier boundaries.
