Troubleshooting Common Obstacles

Even with careful planning, you may encounter obstacles. Here are some common challenges and strategies for overcoming them:

Obstacle Strategy
Resistance to Vulnerability Build trust gradually. Start with less vulnerable exercises and emphasize the importance of self-compassion.
Difficulty with Sensory Awareness Begin with simple grounding techniques. Use guided meditations to enhance focus and awareness.
Emotional Overwhelm Provide a safe space for processing emotions. Teach coping skills and grounding techniques.
Lack of Time Break down the framework into smaller, manageable steps. Integrate practices into daily routines.
