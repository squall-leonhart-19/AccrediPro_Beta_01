Your 90-Day Vision Exercise

Now itâ€™s time to start creating your own vision for the next 90 days. Answer the following questions in as much detail as possible:

  1. What are your top 3 career goals for the next 90 days? (e.g., launch your website, attract 5 new clients, create an online course)
  2. What specific actions will you take each week to achieve these goals? (e.g., write 2 blog posts, network with 3 potential clients, record 1 video lesson)
  3. What limiting beliefs or fears might hold you back? (e.g., fear of failure, imposter syndrome, lack of confidence)
  4. How will you overcome these challenges? (e.g., seek support from mentors, celebrate small wins, focus on your strengths)
  5. What resources do you need to succeed? (e.g., time, money, support, training)
  6. How will you measure your progress and stay accountable? (e.g., track your results, set deadlines, find an accountability partner)

Take some time to reflect on these questions and create a clear action plan. Remember that your vision is a living document that can evolve as you grow and learn.
