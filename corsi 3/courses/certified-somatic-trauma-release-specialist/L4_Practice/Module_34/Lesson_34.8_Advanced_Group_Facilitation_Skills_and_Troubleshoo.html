Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for your interest in the 'Release & Renew' workshop! I'm excited to chat with you. How did you hear about the workshop?"
YOU:
"That's great! What specifically about the workshop caught your eye?"
Phase 2: Understand Needs & Desires 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me a bit about what's been going on in your life lately. What are you hoping to gain from a workshop like this?"
YOU:
"What does 'feeling connected to others' look like for you? What kind of community are you seeking?"
YOU:
"What are your biggest fears or hesitations about joining a group workshop?"
Phase 3: Present the Workshop 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, the 'Release & Renew' workshop seems like a great fit. It's designed to help women like you who are seeking connection and rediscovering themselves."
YOU:
"During the weekend, we'll be using somatic techniques to release stored emotions, gentle movement to reconnect with your body, and group sharing to build a supportive community. We create a safe and nurturing environment where you can truly be yourself."
YOU:
"One of our past participants, Maria, a 48-year-old teacher, said the workshop helped her 'finally let go of the guilt and shame she had been carrying for years' and 'felt more alive and connected than she had in decades.'"
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the kind of experience you're looking for? ... Great! Let's talk about the investment and how you can reserve your spot."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"It's too expensive for me right now."
"I understand. I offer a payment plan of two installments to make it more accessible. Think about what you'd invest in a new wardrobe or a weekend getaway. This workshop is an investment in YOU, in your well-being, and in creating lasting connections. Is the payment plan something that would help?"
"I'm not sure if I'm comfortable sharing my feelings in a group."
"That's a very common feeling, Susan. We create a very safe and supportive environment. Sharing is always optional, and you're welcome to simply observe and participate at your own pace. Many women find that the group dynamic is actually very healing and empowering. Would you feel more comfortable if I shared some testimonials about the group dynamic?"
"I need to check my schedule."
"Of course! The workshop is filling up quickly, and I wouldn't want you to miss out. If you reserve your spot today, I can offer you a full refund if you need to cancel within 48 hours. How does that sound?"
"I'm worried it won't be worth it."
"I understand your concern. I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you attend the entire workshop and don't feel you received value, I'll refund your money. I'm confident you'll find it transformative. What would make you feel more confident in the value of the workshop?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of the discovery call structure outlined in this lesson?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand Needs & Desires, Present the Workshop, and Close.

2. What is a good response to the objection "It's too expensive for me right now?"

A good response is to offer a payment plan and reframe the workshop as an investment in their well-being and creating lasting connections.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the art of enrolling clients into group programs requires empathy, active listening, and confidence.
  • Addressing objections with understanding and offering solutions can turn hesitation into enrollment.
  • Presenting your pricing with clarity and highlighting the value of the workshop is crucial.
  • Group programs and workshops can be a significant income generator for your practice.
  • Remember, you are offering a transformative experience that can positively impact people's lives.
