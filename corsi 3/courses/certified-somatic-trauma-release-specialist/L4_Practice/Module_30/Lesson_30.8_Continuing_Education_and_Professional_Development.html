Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a successful discovery call?

The key phases are: Welcome & Connection, Deep Dive into Challenges, Presenting Your Solution, and The Invitation.

2. How should you handle the objection, "I need to think about it?"

Acknowledgee their need to think, and then ask clarifying questions to understand what specifically they need to consider (e.g., the investment, the process). This allows you to provide targeted information and address their underlying concerns.
