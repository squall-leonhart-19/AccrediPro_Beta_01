Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand, Susan. Many of my clients have similar concerns. Let's explore this a bit further. What specific financial constraints are you facing right now? Perhaps we can discuss payment plan options or tailor the program to fit your budget. Remember, investing in your well-being is an investment in your future."
"I need to think about it."
"I respect that, Susan. It's a big decision. What aspects are you still unsure about? Is it the time commitment, the financial investment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed choice. Perhaps we can schedule a brief follow-up call to address any lingering questions."
"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"Time is precious, Susan, and I appreciate you bringing that up. Our 12-week program is designed to fit into your busy schedule. The sessions are [duration] long, and you'll have access to recordings and resources to review at your convenience. Think of it as carving out dedicated time for your well-being, which will ultimately increase your productivity and overall quality of life."
"I've tried other things before, and they didn't work."
"I hear your frustration, Susan. It's discouraging when previous attempts haven't yielded the desired results. Somatic trauma release is different because it addresses the root causes of your anxiety and sleep issues by working directly with your body's nervous system. It's a gentle and effective approach that empowers you to develop long-term coping mechanisms. I'm confident that with my guidance and your commitment, you can achieve the results you're looking for."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need for time, ask what specifically they need to think about, and offer a follow-up call to address any questions.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is essential for building a thriving practice.
  • A well-structured discovery call can effectively convert prospects into clients.
  • Confidently addressing objections builds trust and demonstrates your expertise.
  • Clearly communicating the value and benefits of your program justifies your pricing.
  • Consistent effort and dedication to sales will lead to increased income and success.
