Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate a client's experience before suggesting solutions?

Validating their experience builds trust and rapport, making them more receptive to the coaching process.

2. What is the key difference between talk therapy and Somatic Trauma Release?

Talk therapy focuses on cognitive understanding, while Somatic Trauma Release addresses the physical sensations and imprints of trauma in the body.

Key Takeaways

  • Always begin by validating the client's experiences and emotions.
  • Explain Somatic Trauma Release in simple, accessible language.
  • Obtain consent before initiating any exercises or interventions.
  • Focus on building trust and creating a safe space for the client.
