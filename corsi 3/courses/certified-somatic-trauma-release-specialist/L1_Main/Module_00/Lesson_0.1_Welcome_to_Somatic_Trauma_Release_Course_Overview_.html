First Steps: How to Maximize Your Learning

Ready to dive in? Here's how to get started and make the most of your learning experience:

  1. Set aside dedicated time for learning: Treat this program like a job. Schedule specific blocks of time each week to watch the videos, complete the assignments, and participate in the community. Consistency is key! A 2021 study in *Educational Psychology Review* found that students who engage in regular, spaced repetition learning demonstrate significantly higher retention rates compared to those who cram information at the last minute.
  2. Actively engage with the material: Don't just passively watch the videos. Take notes, ask questions, and participate in the discussions. The more actively you engage with the material, the more you'll learn and retain.
  3. Practice, practice, practice: The best way to master these techniques is to practice them. Work with practice clients, volunteer your services, and get feedback from your peers and mentors.
  4. Take care of yourself: Working with trauma survivors can be emotionally demanding. Make sure you prioritize self-care, set healthy boundaries, and seek support when you need it.
  5. Don't be afraid to ask for help: We're here to support you every step of the way. If you're struggling with something, don't hesitate to reach out to your instructors, mentors, or peers.

Remember, this is your journey. Embrace the challenges, celebrate the successes, and trust in your ability to create a meaningful and fulfilling career as a Certified Somatic Trauma Release Specialistâ„¢.
