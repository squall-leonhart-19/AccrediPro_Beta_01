Discovery Call Script: Your Blueprint for Success

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for reaching out! I appreciate you taking the time to connect. To start, could you share a bit about what prompted you to seek support right now?"
YOU:
"I understand. It takes courage to reach out. I admire you for taking action. What have you already tried to support your son?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Challenge 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the specific challenges your son is facing in school. What does a typical day look like for him?"
YOU:
"And how is this impacting him emotionally? What are his biggest frustrations?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's challenges, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the deal.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive?"

Acknowledge their concern, reframe the cost as an investment, highlight the long-term benefits, and offer payment options.

3. What is the importance of practicing your pricing presentation?

Practicing helps you deliver your price confidently and clearly communicate the value of your program.
