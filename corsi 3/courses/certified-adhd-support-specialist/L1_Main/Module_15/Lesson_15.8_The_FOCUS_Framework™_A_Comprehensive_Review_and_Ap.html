Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, it sounds like you're going through a challenging time. Thank you for sharing what's been going on. Many people experience similar issues with focus and sleep when facing significant changes or increased stress. Let's work together to find some strategies that can help you regain your sense of calm and control."
MARIA:
"I appreciate that. I feel like I'm constantly running on empty."
YOU:
"I understand. We can explore some simple, practical techniques to help you manage the overwhelm and improve your sleep. Would you be open to starting with a brief assessment to identify your primary challenges and strengths?"
MARIA:
"Yes, that sounds good. I'm willing to try anything at this point."
YOU:
"Great. Let's begin by discussing your daily routine and how you're currently managing your time and energy. This will give us a clearer picture of where we can make the most impactful changes. How does that sound?"

You'll Know It Worked When...

  • Maria expresses relief and a sense of being understood.
