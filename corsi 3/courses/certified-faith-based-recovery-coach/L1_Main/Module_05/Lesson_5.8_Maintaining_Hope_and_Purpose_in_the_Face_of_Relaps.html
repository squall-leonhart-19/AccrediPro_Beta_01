Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on instilling hope and exploring purpose. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your story with me. It takes courage to acknowledge feeling lost, especially after such a significant life change. I want you to know that it's absolutely possible to rediscover your purpose and find joy again, even with the challenges you're facing."

"It's hard to believe that right now. Everything feels so different."
YOU:
"I understand. But think of this as a new chapter, not the end of the book. We can explore what truly brings you alive, what gifts you have to offer the world, and how your faith can support you on this journey. How does that sound?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal when coaching a client who feels lost and without purpose?

The primary goal is to instill hope and help the client rediscover or redefine their sense of purpose, connecting it with their values, faith, and potential for new beginnings.

2. What are some key phrases to use when coaching a client to explore their values and potential purpose?

Key phrases include: "What truly brings you alive?", "What gifts do you have to offer the world?", "How can your faith support you on this journey?", and "What activities or roles have given you the greatest sense of fulfillment in the past?".

Key Takeaways

  • Instilling hope is crucial for clients who feel lost and without purpose.
  • Help clients connect their past experiences with potential new directions.
  • Encourage exploration of values, gifts, and faith to uncover renewed purpose.
  • Avoid imposing your own beliefs about purpose; guide clients to discover their own.
  • Recognize even small clues can point towards a renewed sense of purpose.
