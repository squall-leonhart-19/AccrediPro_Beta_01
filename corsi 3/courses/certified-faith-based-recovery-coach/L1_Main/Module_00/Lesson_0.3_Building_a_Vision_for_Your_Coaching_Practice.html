The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is designed as a comprehensive journey, broken down into four distinct tiers, each building upon the last to create a well-rounded and highly competent coach:

  1. Level 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where we lay the groundwork. You'll learn the core methodology, fundamental principles of faith-based recovery, and essential coaching skills. Think of it as building a strong and stable base for everything that follows.
  2. Level 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): Now we delve into more complex scenarios and clinical depth. You'll explore advanced coaching techniques, learn to navigate challenging client situations, and refine your ability to tailor interventions to individual needs.
  3. Level 3: Master (Modules 24-29): At this level, you'll develop the skills to train and supervise other coaches. You'll learn how to impart your knowledge and expertise, mentor aspiring practitioners, and contribute to the growth of the field.
  4. Level 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): This final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. We'll cover marketing strategies, ethical considerations, and practical tips for establishing a thriving coaching practice. Many of our graduates, like Sarah (age 48, former teacher), have found financial freedom and meaningful work, earning $60,000 - $80,000 per year within their first two years.

Time Commitment & Pacing

We understand that you have a busy life, so we've designed the program to be flexible and self-paced. On average, students who dedicate 5-7 hours per week complete the certification in 6-9 months. However, you can adjust the pace to fit your individual needs and schedule.
