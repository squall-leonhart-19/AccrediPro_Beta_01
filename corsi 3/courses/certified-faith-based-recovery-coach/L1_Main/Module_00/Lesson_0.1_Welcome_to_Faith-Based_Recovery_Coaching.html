You Belong Here - Overcoming Imposter Syndrome

Let's address something important right away: imposter syndrome. It's that sneaky voice that whispers, "Who are you to do this? Are you qualified enough? What if you fail?" Especially as women, and often as Christian women, we've been conditioned to doubt ourselves.

But I want you to know this: your life experience, your struggles, your triumphs â€“ they are your qualifications. You don't need to be perfect to guide others. You need to be real, compassionate, and willing to learn.

According to a 2019 study in the Journal of Behavioral Science, approximately 70% of people experience imposter syndrome at some point in their lives. Know that you're not alone, and more importantly, you can overcome it.

Remember the "wounded healer" archetype. Your scars aren't liabilities; they're sources of strength and empathy. They allow you to connect with your clients on a deeper level and offer hope based on genuine understanding.

Throughout this program, we'll help you recognize your unique gifts, build your confidence, and silence that inner critic. You ARE enough. You ARE capable. You ARE called.
