Discovery Call Script: Group Program Enrollment

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for reaching out and expressing interest in the Finding Freedom group program. I'm Grace, and I'll be leading the group. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"I understand you're interested in the Finding Freedom group program. What specifically drew you to this program?"
Phase 2: Understanding Her Needs 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about what's been going on for you and your struggles with codependency. What does that look like in your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these patterns?"
YOU:
"What are you hoping to gain from participating in a group program like this?"
Phase 3: Program Overview & Addressing Concerns 10 min
YOU:
"The Finding Freedom group program is a 10-week journey where we delve into the root causes of codependency through a faith-based lens. We'll explore scripture, share experiences, and learn practical tools for setting boundaries and building healthy relationships. It's a safe space where you'll find support and encouragement."
YOU:
"I understand cost is a concern. The investment for the 10-week program is $797, which includes weekly group sessions, a workbook, and access to our online community. Think of it as an investment in your long-term well-being and freedom."
YOU:
"Are there any other questions or concerns you have about the program?"
Phase 4: Call to Action & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I truly believe the Finding Freedom group program can provide the support and tools you need to break free from these patterns and build healthy relationships. Are you ready to take this step and join us?"
YOU:
"If you're ready, I can send you the enrollment link and payment information. The group starts next week, and spots are limited."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I'm ready to share my struggles in a group."
"I understand that vulnerability can be scary. The group is designed to be a safe and supportive space. Sharing is always optional, and you can participate at your own pace. Many find that hearing others' experiences is incredibly validating and empowering."
"I'm worried I won't have time with my busy schedule."
"I hear you. Time is precious. The sessions are 90 minutes a week, and we offer flexible scheduling options. Think about what you're currently sacrificing because of these patterns. Investing in this program is investing in reclaiming your time and energy in the long run."
"The cost is a bit more than I was expecting."
"I understand. Let's talk about the value you're receiving. This program includes 10 group sessions led by a certified coach, a comprehensive workbook, and access to a supportive online community. Many clients find the accountability and connection alone are worth the investment. We also offer payment plans to make it more accessible."
"I need to think about it and pray on it."
"That's completely understandable. I respect your process. What specific questions or concerns can I address now that would help you in your prayerful consideration? I'm happy to provide more information or answer any lingering questions."

    Enrollment Strategies for Group Programs

    Now that you've designed your group program, it's time to enroll clients! This lesson focuses on how to adapt your discovery call script to highlight the unique benefits of group participation and confidently close the sale. Remember, effective enrollment is about connecting with potential clients, understanding their needs, and showcasing the transformative power of your group program.

    Adapting Your Discovery Call Script

    While the core elements of a discovery call remain the same (building rapport, identifying needs, presenting solutions), you'll want to emphasize the group aspect when speaking with prospects interested in this format.

    Tips for Group Program Discovery Calls

    • Highlight Community: "Many clients find the connection and support within the group to be invaluable. How important is having a supportive community to you in this journey?"
    • Address Concerns About Vulnerability: "It's natural to feel hesitant about sharing in a group. I create a safe and confidential space where everyone can participate at their own pace."
    • Emphasize Shared Experience: "In a group setting, you'll learn from others who are facing similar challenges. What are you hoping to gain from connecting with others?"

    Case Study: Sarah's Hesitation

    Client: Sarah, 42, recently divorced

    Presenting Symptoms: Feeling isolated, struggling with codependent patterns in relationships

    Objection: "I'm not sure I'm comfortable sharing my personal life in a group."

    Intervention: The coach addressed Sarah's concern by emphasizing the confidentiality and supportive nature of the group. She shared testimonials from past participants who had initially felt the same way but found the group to be incredibly healing.

    Outcomes: Sarah enrolled in the group and reported feeling less alone and more empowered to set healthy boundaries. She valued the shared experiences and support from other members.

    Addressing Common Objections

    Be prepared to address common objections that may arise during the enrollment process. Here are some examples:

    "I'm not sure if group therapy is right for me."
    "That's a great question. Group programs offer a unique opportunity to connect with others who understand what you're going through. It can be incredibly validating and empowering to share your experiences and learn from others. We also incorporate individual coaching elements to tailor the experience to your specific needs."
    "I'm worried about being vulnerable in front of strangers."
    "I understand. It takes courage to be vulnerable. The group is designed to be a safe and supportive space. Sharing is always optional, and you can participate at your own pace. Many find that hearing others' experiences is incredibly validating and empowering."
    "I'm worried I won't have time with my busy schedule."
    "I hear you. Time is precious. The sessions are 90 minutes a week, and we offer flexible scheduling options. Think about what you're currently sacrificing because of these patterns. Investing in this program is investing in reclaiming your time and energy in the long run."
    "The cost is a bit more than I was expecting."
    "I understand. Let's talk about the value you're receiving. This program includes 10 group sessions led by a certified coach, a comprehensive workbook, and access to a supportive online community. Many clients find the accountability and connection alone are worth the investment. We also offer payment plans to make it more accessible."
    "I need to think about it and pray on it."
    "That's completely understandable. I respect your process. What specific questions or concerns can I address now that would help you in your prayerful consideration? I'm happy to provide more information or answer any lingering questions."
