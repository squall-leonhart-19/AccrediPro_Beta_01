Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection; Phase 2: Uncovering the Pain Points; Phase 3: Presenting Your Unique Approach; Phase 4: Confident Close.

2. What are some key strategies for handling the "It's too expensive" objection?

Acknowledge their concern, reframe the value, highlight the long-term benefits (beyond just money), and offer payment plan options.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script until it feels natural and conversational.
  • Prepare confident responses to common objections.
  • Clearly articulate the value of your program and the transformation it offers.
  • Believe in your ability to help your clients achieve their goals.
  • Consistency is key. Regularly reach out to potential clients and nurture those relationships.
