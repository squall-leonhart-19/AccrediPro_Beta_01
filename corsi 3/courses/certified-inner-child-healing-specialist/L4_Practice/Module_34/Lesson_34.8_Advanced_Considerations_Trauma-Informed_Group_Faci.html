Handling Objections

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful group program enrollment call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's desires and challenges, presenting the program as a solution, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How can you address a prospect's budget concerns during an enrollment call?

Acknowledge their concerns, highlight the potential ROI of the program, offer payment plans, and provide a satisfaction guarantee.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your enrollment script regularly to build confidence and fluency.
  • Actively listen to your prospects and tailor your presentation to their specific needs.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare confident, empathetic responses.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your group program and its potential impact on participants' lives.
  • Believe in the transformative power of your work and let that passion shine through!
