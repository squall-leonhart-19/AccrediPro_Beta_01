Your Coaching Script

Here's a script to guide your initial conversation. Remember, you can customize this using the client intake forms and assessment templates from the DFY Accelerator!

"Diane, thank you for sharing your story. It takes courage to acknowledge these feelings. What you're experiencing is common, especially after significant life transitions. I want you to know that healing is possible, and you're not alone."

"Thank you. It's hard to admit that I still feel like a lost little girl sometimes."

"It means becoming the loving, supportive parent your inner child always needed. YOU:
"Does that sound like a path you'd be willing to explore, Diane?"
