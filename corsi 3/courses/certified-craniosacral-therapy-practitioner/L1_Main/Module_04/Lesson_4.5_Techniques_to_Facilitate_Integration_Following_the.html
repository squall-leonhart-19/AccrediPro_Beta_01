Techniques for Enhancing the Inner Tide

Working with Resistance

Resistance to the inner tide can manifest as areas of tension, restriction, or inertia within the body. It's important to approach resistance with patience, respect, and a non-judgmental attitude. This aligns with the Guidance aspect of the A.L.I.G.N. Frameworkâ„¢, guiding the client through their healing process with sensitivity and skill. Here's how to work with resistance effectively:

  1. Acknowledge the Resistance: Recognize that resistance is a natural part of the healing process and may represent a protective mechanism.
  2. Reduce Input: Minimize any external pressure or force that could exacerbate the resistance.
  3. Find the Fulcrum: Identify the point of greatest tension or restriction within the area of resistance.
  4. Wait for Release: Gently support the fulcrum and wait for the tissues to soften and release on their own.
  5. Follow the Unwinding: As the tissues release, gently follow the unwinding process, allowing the body to guide the treatment.
