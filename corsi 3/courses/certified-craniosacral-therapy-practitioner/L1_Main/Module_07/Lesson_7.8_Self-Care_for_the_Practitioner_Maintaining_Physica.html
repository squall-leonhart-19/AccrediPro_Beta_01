Your Coaching Script

Here's a sample script to guide the initial conversation with Maria. Remember to adapt it to your own style and intuition:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for coming in today. I understand you've been dealing with chronic headaches, neck stiffness, and anxiety. It sounds like it's significantly impacting your life."
MARIA:
"Yes, it is. I'm constantly on edge, and the headaches are debilitating sometimes."
YOU:
"I want you to know that Craniosacral Therapy can be very effective in addressing these issues. We'll be working to gently release tension in your body, particularly around your head, neck, and spine, which can help alleviate your symptoms and promote a sense of calm."
YOU:
"Before we begin, do you have any questions about Craniosacral Therapy or what to expect during the session?"
MARIA:
"I've read a little about it, but I'm not sure I fully understand how it works."
YOU:
"That's perfectly fine. I'm happy to explain further. Essentially, we'll be working with the craniosacral system, which includes the bones of your skull, spine, and sacrum, as well as the membranes and fluid that surround and protect your brain and spinal cord. By gently releasing restrictions in this system, we can improve its overall function and help your body heal itself."
