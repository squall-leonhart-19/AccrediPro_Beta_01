Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call! I saw you found me on Instagram. What caught your eye?"
YOU:
"I'm curious, what made you decide to reach out now? What's been going on in your life that led you here?"
Phase 2: Uncovering the Pain 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about this stress you're experiencing. What does it feel like in your body?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to alleviate this stress? What has worked, and what hasn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal state of well-being look and feel like?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I understand you're feeling stressed and disconnected, and you're looking for a way to feel more grounded and in control. Is that right?"
YOU:
"My approach with Craniosacral Therapy is different. We're not just treating the symptoms of stress, but addressing the root causes by gently releasing tension patterns in your body. I work with clients over 8 weeks to help them reconnect with their inner resources and build resilience. Many clients report feeling a significant shift within the first few sessions."
Phase 4: Closing the Call 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with you? ... Wonderful! Let me share the details of my program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The sessions are [time commitment], and we can work together to find a time that fits your schedule. Think of it as an investment in your well-being that will ultimately make you more productive and present in all areas of your life."
"I've never tried Craniosacral Therapy before."
"That's perfectly fine. Many of my clients are new to it. It's a gentle, non-invasive approach that works with your body's natural healing mechanisms. I'm happy to answer any specific questions you have or provide you with resources to learn more."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer a payment plan option where you can split the investment into [number] installments. This makes it more accessible for those who prefer to spread out the cost."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"I am confident in the approach and the results my clients achieve. I offer a satisfaction guarantee where, if you are not satisfied after the first two sessions, you will receive a full refund. I want you to feel completely comfortable and confident in your investment."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's pain points, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the call.

2. How should you handle common objections during a sales conversation?

Acknowledge the client's concerns, address them with empathy and confidence, and offer solutions.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call helps you effectively connect with potential clients.
  • Understanding your ideal client's needs and desires is crucial for successful acquisition.
  • Confidently addressing objections builds trust and increases conversion rates.
  • Clearly presenting your pricing and the value you offer is essential for closing the sale.
  • Consistent effort and effective strategies can lead to a thriving practice and financial freedom.
