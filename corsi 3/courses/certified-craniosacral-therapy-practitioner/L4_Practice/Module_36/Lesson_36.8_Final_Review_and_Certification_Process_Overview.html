Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Maya, and I'm excited to learn more about what you're looking for. To make the best use of our time, let's spend a few minutes understanding your current situation, then I'll share how my Craniosacral Therapy program can help, and finally, we'll discuss if it's a good fit for you. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"Great! To start, what prompted you to reach out to me specifically?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned headaches and poor sleep. Can you tell me more about those? How frequently do they occur, and how do they impact your daily life and work?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to alleviate these issues? Were there any treatments or strategies that provided temporary relief, or did they fall short of your expectations?"
YOU:
"Imagine you wake up three months from now, and these issues are significantly reduced or gone. How would that change your life? What would you be able to do differently?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're dealing with a lot of stress manifesting as physical symptoms. Craniosacral Therapy can be incredibly effective in these situations. It's a gentle, hands-on approach that releases tension in the central nervous system, allowing your body to naturally restore balance."
YOU:
"My program is designed to address the root causes of your symptoms, not just mask them. Over 8 weeks, we'll work together to release restrictions, improve your sleep, reduce headaches, and help you reconnect with your body. Most of my clients experience noticeable improvements within the first few sessions. For example, a 2021 study in the Journal of Bodywork and Movement Therapies found that Craniosacral Therapy significantly reduced headache frequency and intensity (p<0.05)."
YOU:
"How does this approach resonate with what you're looking for?"
Phase 4: Offer & Close 5 min
YOU:
"Great! Based on what you've shared, I'm confident that my program can help you achieve your goals. Are you ready to discuss the details and see if it's the right fit for you?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The sessions are designed to be deeply relaxing and restorative, so they can actually help you manage your time more effectively. Plus, we can work together to find appointment times that fit your schedule. What specific time constraints are you concerned about?"
"I've never tried Craniosacral Therapy before. I'm a little skeptical."
"That's perfectly normal. It's a gentle modality, but it can have profound effects. I'd be happy to share some testimonials from other clients who were initially skeptical but experienced significant benefits. Also, I offer a satisfaction guarantee: if you don't feel any improvement after the first two sessions, I'll refund your money."
"It seems a bit expensive."
"I understand that it's an investment. However, consider the long-term benefits: reduced stress, improved sleep, fewer headaches, and a greater sense of well-being. Many clients find that they save money on other treatments and medications as a result of the program. Plus, I offer payment plans to make it more accessible. Let's explore those options."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes (as everyone responds differently), I can guarantee my commitment to providing you with the highest quality care and personalized attention. My program is structured to maximize your chances of success, and I'll be with you every step of the way. Based on my experience and the positive results my clients have achieved, I'm confident you'll experience significant benefits."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport & Set Agenda, Deep Dive into the Problem, Present Your Solution, and Offer & Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection "It seems a bit expensive"?

Acknowledge the concern, highlight the long-term benefits and potential savings, and offer payment plan options.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is crucial for converting prospects into clients.
  • Building rapport and understanding the client's problems are essential for a successful call.
  • Confidently present your solution and handle objections with empathy and understanding.
  • Practice your pricing presentation and closing lines to increase your conversion rate.
  • Remember that consistent effort in client acquisition can lead to a thriving practice and significant income.
