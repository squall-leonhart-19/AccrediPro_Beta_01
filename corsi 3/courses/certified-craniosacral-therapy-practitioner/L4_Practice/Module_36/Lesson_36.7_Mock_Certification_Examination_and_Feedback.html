Administering the Mock Examination

The mock examination will mirror the format and content of the actual certification exam. This includes both written and practical components, covering the key areas we've explored throughout this course, from assessment techniques to treatment protocols. Treat this as the real deal. Find a quiet space, set a timer, and focus. The goal is to experience the exam environment and identify any areas where you may need additional review.

Individualized Feedback and Performance Analysis

Following the mock examination, you will receive detailed, individualized feedback. This feedback will highlight your strengths, pinpoint areas for improvement, and offer specific recommendations for further study. Consider this constructive criticism as a valuable tool for refining your skills and optimizing your preparation. We will focus on areas related to The A.L.I.G.N. Case Study: Maria's Mock Exam Experience

Client: Maria, Age 45

Presenting Symptoms: Maria felt anxious about the practical portion of the exam, particularly the assessment of cranial rhythm.

Intervention: Maria participated in the mock examination, receiving feedback from the instructor on her hand placement and palpation skills. She also practiced self-regulation techniques to manage her anxiety during the exam.

Outcomes: Maria's confidence increased after receiving specific feedback and practicing relaxation techniques. She felt more prepared for the actual certification exam and successfully passed the practical component.

Group Review and Clarification

In addition to individualized feedback, we will conduct a group review of the mock examination results. This session will provide an opportunity to clarify any common errors or misconceptions, as well as to learn from each other's experiences. We will also discuss effective test-taking strategies and techniques for managing test anxiety. This collaborative approach ensures that everyone has a solid understanding of the material and feels confident in their ability to apply it.

We are committed to providing you with all the support and resources you need to succeed.
