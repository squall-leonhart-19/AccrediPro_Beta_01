Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for being so open about this. It takes courage to admit these feelings, and I want you to know you're not alone. Many new mothers experience a gap between their expectations and reality."
MARIA:
"It's just... all the books and blogs make it seem so natural and instant. I feel like I'm failing."
YOU:
"Those portrayals can be incredibly misleading. Bonding is a process, not an event. And your difficult labor and postpartum period likely added extra layers of challenge. Let's explore what might be impacting your connection with your baby, without any judgment. Does that sound okay?"
YOU:
"I'd like to gently ask some questions about your daily interactions with your baby, your self-care practices, and your support system. These areas often hold clues to strengthening that bond. Are you comfortable with that?"
MARIA:
"Yes, I'm willing to try anything at this point."
YOU:
"Great. Let's start with a typical day. Tell me about the moments when you feel most and least connected to your baby."
