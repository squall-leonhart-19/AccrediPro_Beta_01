The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a transformative 4-Tier system, designed to build your skills and confidence step-by-step:

  1. L1 FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build your core knowledge and learn the fundamentals. You'll dive deep into our methodology and establish a solid base for more advanced concepts.
  2. L2 APPLICATION (Modules 16-30): Here, you'll learn how to apply what you've learned in real-world scenarios. We'll focus on practical application, case studies, and interactive exercises to ensure you're ready to work with clients.
  3. L3 INTEGRATION (Modules 31-45): This tier is all about integrating your knowledge and skills into a cohesive practice. You'll learn how to tailor your approach to individual client needs and create personalized recovery plans.
  4. L4 MASTERY (Modules 46-60): The final tier is where you'll master advanced techniques and strategies. You'll learn how to handle complex cases, build a thriving practice, and become a leader in the field of postpartum recovery.

The B.L.O.O.M. Framework‚Ñ¢

At the heart of our certification is The B.L.O.O.M. Framework‚Ñ¢. This comprehensive methodology guides you through every aspect of postpartum recovery coaching:

  • B - Bonding: Facilitating secure attachment between mother and child.
  • L - Lifestyle: Promoting healthy habits and routines.
  • O - Optimizing Nutrition: Providing guidance on postpartum nutrition and supplementation.
  • O - Overcoming Challenges: Addressing common postpartum challenges such as sleep deprivation, anxiety, and depression.
  • M - Mindset & Mental Health: Cultivating a positive mindset and supporting mental well-being.

This framework ensures that you're equipped to address the diverse needs of your clients and provide comprehensive support.

Time Commitment & Pacing

Certification Requirements

To achieve certification as a Postpartum Recovery Coach‚Ñ¢, you'll need to meet the following requirements:

  • Complete all modules and coursework.
  • Pass the certification exam with a score of 80% or higher.
  • Complete a minimum of 20 hours of supervised coaching practice.
  • Submit a final case study demonstrating your competence and application of The B.L.O.O.M. Framework‚Ñ¢.

Upon successful completion of these requirements, you'll receive your official certification and be recognized as a Certified Postpartum Recovery Coach‚Ñ¢.

Case Study: Client Sarah, 32

Client: Sarah, a 32-year-old first-time mother.

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah presented with symptoms of postpartum anxiety, sleep deprivation, and difficulty bonding with her baby.

Intervention: Using The B.L.O.O.M. Framework‚Ñ¢, the coach implemented the following strategies:

  • Bonding: Guided Sarah through attachment-based exercises and facilitated skin-to-skin contact with her baby.
  • Lifestyle: Helped Sarah establish a consistent sleep routine and incorporate self-care practices into her daily life.
  • Optimizing Nutrition: Provided Sarah with a personalized nutrition plan to support her postpartum recovery and breastfeeding.
  • Overcoming Challenges: Taught Sarah coping strategies for managing her anxiety and addressing her sleep deprivation.
  • Mindset & Mental Health: Encouraged Sarah to practice mindfulness and engage in supportive therapy sessions.

Outcomes: After 12 weeks of coaching, Sarah reported a significant reduction in her anxiety symptoms, improved sleep quality, and a stronger bond with her baby. She also felt more confident and empowered as a mother.
