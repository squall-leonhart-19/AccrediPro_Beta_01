Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Framework

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, it's so lovely to connect with you. Thank you for scheduling this call. How are you doing today, aside from the usual mom-exhaustion?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me through the moms' group. That's a great community! What specifically prompted you to reach out for postpartum support?"
Phase 2: Understanding Sarah's Needs 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling overwhelmed. Can you tell me more about what that looks like in your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to improve your sleep and nutrition? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal postpartum experience look like?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Sarah, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're struggling with [summarize her key challenges]. Is that a fair assessment?"
YOU:
"My 12-week postpartum recovery program is designed to address those exact challenges. We focus on creating a personalized plan that incorporates evidence-based strategies for sleep, nutrition, and emotional well-being. Many of my clients, like you, are able to regain their energy and enjoy motherhood again."
YOU:
"I understand that you value evidence-based approaches. My program is rooted in research and clinical guidelines, and I can share some resources with you if you'd like."
Phase 4: Invitation & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Sarah, does this sound like the kind of support you've been searching for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let's discuss the program details and see if it's the right fit for you."

Overcoming Objections

Common Concerns & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand that time is precious, especially with a little one. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your busy life. We can work together to create a schedule that works for you, and the benefits of improved sleep and energy will actually give you MORE time in the long run. Plus, think of the time saved not endlessly researching solutions that don't work!"
"It sounds great, but I need to talk to my partner about the investment."
"That's completely reasonable. I'm happy to provide you with a detailed program overview that you can share with your partner. I'm also available for a quick call with both of you to answer any questions and address any concerns. Would either of those options be helpful?"
"I've tried other programs before, and they didn't work."
"I hear your frustration. What I offer is different because it's not a one-size-fits-all approach. We focus on identifying your unique needs and creating a personalized plan. I also provide ongoing support and accountability to help you stay on track. Plus, I am a Certified Postpartum Recovery Coach, trained to use evidence-based methods to help you recover. We can pinpoint what didn't work before and make sure that this experience is different."
"What if I don't see results?"
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can promise that I will dedicate my expertise and support to help you achieve your goals. I track progress closely and make adjustments as needed. Many of my clients experience significant improvements in their sleep, energy, and overall well-being within the first few weeks. And, if you fully commit to the program and don't see the progress you expect, we can discuss a tailored solution. I'm invested in your success."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Warm Welcome & Connection, Understanding the Prospect's Needs, Presenting Your Solution, and Invitation & Next Steps.

2. How can you handle the objection "I need to talk to my partner about the investment?"

Offer to provide a detailed program overview or schedule a call with both the prospect and their partner to answer any questions.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for converting leads into clients.
  • Understanding your prospect's needs and challenges is essential for presenting a relevant solution.
  • Confidently address objections and present your pricing with clarity.
  • Practice your closing lines to increase your chances of securing a new client.
  • Visualize your income potential to stay motivated and focused on your business goals.
