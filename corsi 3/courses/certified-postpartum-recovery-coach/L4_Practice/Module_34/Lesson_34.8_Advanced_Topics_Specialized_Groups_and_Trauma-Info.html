Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, thanks for reaching out! I'm so glad you're interested in the Mindful Motherhood workshop. I saw you found me on Instagram ‚Äì what caught your eye?"
YOU:
"It's wonderful you're prioritizing your well-being as a new mom. What are some of the biggest challenges you're facing right now?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the anxiety. When does it tend to surface? How does it impact your ability to enjoy motherhood?"
YOU:
"And what have you tried so far to manage it? Have you explored mindfulness practices, therapy, or other support systems?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, what's the potential impact on your relationship with your baby, your partner, and yourself?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Sarah, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're feeling overwhelmed and disconnected, and you're looking for practical tools to manage anxiety and cultivate mindfulness. Does that resonate?"
YOU:
"The Mindful Motherhood workshop is designed to provide you with exactly that. Over three hours, we'll explore evidence-based mindfulness techniques, gentle movement practices, and strategies for creating a more peaceful and connected postpartum experience. You'll also connect with other moms who understand what you're going through."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Sarah, does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for? ... Great! Let me tell you about the workshop details and how you can register."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time."
"I understand, Sarah. Motherhood is demanding! This workshop is designed to give you practical tools you can integrate into your daily life, even with limited time. Think of it as an investment in your well-being that will ultimately make you more present and efficient."
"I'm not sure it's worth the money."
"I hear you. Many moms feel that way initially. But consider the cost of NOT addressing these challenges. What's the value of feeling more calm, connected, and confident? The workshop provides you with evidence-based tools you can use for years to come."
"I'm nervous about attending a group workshop."
"That's completely normal. I create a safe and supportive environment where everyone feels comfortable sharing. Many moms find that connecting with others who understand their experience is one of the most valuable aspects of the workshop."
"Can I get a discount?"
"I appreciate you asking. While I don't typically offer discounts, I do have an early bird special that expires [Date]. If you register by then, you'll receive [Benefit, e.g., a free bonus resource]."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call for a postpartum workshop?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's challenges, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How can you address budget concerns when selling a postpartum workshop?

Focus on the value of the workshop, the potential cost of NOT addressing the challenges, and offer early bird pricing or bonus resources.

Key Takeaways

  • Workshops are a valuable way to generate income and build community.
  • Discovery calls are essential for understanding your prospect's needs and presenting your workshop as the solution.
  • Confidently handle objections by addressing concerns and highlighting the value of your workshop.
  • Practice your pricing presentation to confidently state your price and encourage registration.
