Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call! I know you're busy. I saw you found me on Instagram, what caught your eye?"
YOU:
"Oh, you saw my post about stress and aromatherapy? Great! Tell me a bit about what's been going on with you and what led you to reach out today."
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned you're a school principal. That must be incredibly demanding. What does a typical day look like, and how does stress manifest for you?"
YOU:
"And how is your sleep? Are you having trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or both?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to manage your stress and improve your sleep? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"Imagine you could wave a magic wand and wake up feeling refreshed and calm every day. What impact would that have on your work, your relationships, and your overall well-being?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if essential oils really work."
"I understand your skepticism. Many people are unsure at first. Aromatherapy has been used for centuries, and current research supports its benefits for stress reduction and sleep improvement. My approach is based on understanding your individual needs and using evidence-based practices to create a personalized plan that works for you. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee."
"I'm too busy to commit to another program."
"I hear you. Time is precious. The beauty of aromatherapy is that it can be easily integrated into your daily routine. We'll work together to find simple, effective ways to incorporate essential oils into your life, so it doesn't feel like a burden. Many of my clients are busy professionals who find that aromatherapy actually helps them become MORE productive by reducing stress and improving focus."
"Can I get the same results just by buying essential oils online?"
"While you can certainly purchase essential oils on your own, my program provides a personalized approach that considers your specific needs and goals. I can also ensure that the oils are high quality and properly diluted for safe use. You also receive ongoing support and guidance to help you stay on track and achieve the best possible results. It's like the difference between trying to bake a cake from a recipe versus having a personal chef guide you through the process."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How can you address the objection, "I'm not sure if essential oils really work?"

Key Takeaways

  • Personalize Your Approach: Tailor your discovery call to each prospect's unique needs and goals.
  • Address Objections Confidently: Prepare thoughtful responses to common objections to build trust and credibility.
  • Communicate Your Value: Clearly articulate the benefits of your program and how it can transform your client's life.
  • Practice Your Closing Lines: Confidently ask for the sale and guide your prospects towards taking the next step.
