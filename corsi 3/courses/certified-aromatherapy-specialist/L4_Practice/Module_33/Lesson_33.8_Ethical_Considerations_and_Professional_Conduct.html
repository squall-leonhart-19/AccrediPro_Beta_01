Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan! It's so great to connect with you. Thanks for scheduling this call. I enjoyed our interaction at the workshop. How did you find the workshop overall?"
YOU:
"Fantastic! So, what specifically made you decide to book this call? What are you hoping to achieve?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the stress you're experiencing. What does a typical day look like for you? What are the biggest stressors?"
YOU:
"And how is this stress affecting your sleep? What have you tried to improve your sleep quality?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, what impact will this have on your health and well-being in the long term? If you could change one thing about your current situation, what would it be?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're dealing with significant stress that's impacting your sleep and overall well-being. You're looking for natural solutions to regain control. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach focuses on identifying the root causes of your stress and creating a personalized aromatherapy plan to address them. We'll use a combination of essential oils, lifestyle adjustments, and relaxation techniques to help you manage stress, improve sleep, and enhance your overall well-being. Most clients experience noticeable improvements within the first few weeks of the program."
YOU:
"For example, a 2023 study in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine found that aromatherapy with lavender oil reduced anxiety scores by 32% in participants with generalized anxiety disorder."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with you? Do you think this could be the solution you've been searching for? ... Excellent! Let's discuss the program details and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Time is valuable. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. We'll work together to create a plan that works for you, with manageable steps and realistic goals. Plus, think of the time you'll save by being more focused and less stressed!"
"I've tried aromatherapy before, and it didn't work."
"I hear you. It's important to remember that not all aromatherapy approaches are created equal. My program is different because it's highly personalized and based on a thorough assessment of your individual needs. We'll use specific blends and application methods tailored to your unique situation. Plus, we track your progress and make adjustments as needed."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can guarantee that I'll provide you with the knowledge, tools, and support you need to succeed. My clients typically experience significant improvements in their stress levels, sleep quality, and overall well-being. If you follow the program and commit to the process, you're likely to see positive results. Check out the testimonials on my website!"
"I need to think about it and do some more research."
"I understand. What specific research are you looking to do? Perhaps I can point you to some resources, case studies, or even connect you with a past client who had a similar experience. Would that be helpful?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value by highlighting the long-term benefits and potential ROI. For example, "I understand. However, consider the cost of not addressing your stress. What is your health worth?"

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for client acquisition.
  • Understanding your prospect's needs and pain points is essential.
  • Practice handling common objections with confidence and empathy.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and its potential ROI.
  • Believe in your ability to help clients achieve their goals.
