Handling Objections

"That's a valid concern. Aromatherapy is very individualized, and that's why I create custom blends for each client. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you don't see improvements within the first month, we can adjust the plan or offer a partial refund."
"I need to research aromatherapy more before committing."

"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer a payment plan of three monthly installments. This makes the program more accessible while still allowing you to get the full benefits."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a 30-minute discovery call, and how much time should you allocate to each?

The key phases are: (1) Warm Welcome & Connection (5 min), (2) Deep Dive into Needs (10 min), (3) Connecting Aromatherapy to Goals (10 min), and (4) Transition to Offer & Next Steps (5 min).

2. What's a good strategy for addressing the objection, "I'm not sure if I have the time for this right now?"

Acknowledge their concern, emphasize the program's flexibility, and highlight the time they'll GAIN from reduced stress and better sleep.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call helps you understand the client's needs and present your services effectively.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare confident, empathetic responses.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and its potential impact on the client's life.
  • Practice your pricing presentation to convey confidence and professionalism.
  • Client acquisition is a skill that improves with practice. Keep refining your approach!
