Case Study: Emily, 42, Chronic Anxiety

Client: Emily, a 42-year-old marketing executive, presented with chronic anxiety and sleep disturbances.

Assessment: Using the S.C.E.N.T. Frameworkâ„¢, I assessed Emily's sensory preferences and identified lavender, chamomile, and frankincense as calming scents.

Intervention: I recommended a personalized aromatherapy routine, including a bedtime diffuser blend, a pulse point roll-on for daytime anxiety, and a guided meditation with essential oils.

Outcomes: After 8 weeks, Emily reported a 50% reduction in anxiety symptoms and improved sleep quality, as measured by the Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7-item (GAD-7) scale and the Pittsburgh Sleep Quality Index (PSQI).

Case Study: David, 68, Post-Operative Pain

Client: David, a 68-year-old retired teacher, experienced post-operative pain following a hip replacement.

Assessment: I identified peppermint, eucalyptus, and rosemary as potentially helpful for pain management and circulation.

Intervention: I created a massage oil blend for topical application and instructed David's caregiver on proper massage techniques. I also emphasized the importance of consulting with his physician regarding pain medication.

Outcomes: David reported a 30% reduction in pain levels, as measured by the Visual Analog Scale (VAS), and reduced reliance on opioid pain medication. This was achieved in conjunction with physical therapy and prescribed medication.
