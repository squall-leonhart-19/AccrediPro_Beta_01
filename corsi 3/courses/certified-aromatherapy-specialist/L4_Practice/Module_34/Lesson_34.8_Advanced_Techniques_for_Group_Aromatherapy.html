Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. It's important to prioritize your time. The program requires just a few hours per week, and many participants find that it actually *creates* more time by reducing stress and increasing focus. Plus, all sessions are recorded, so you can catch up if you miss one."
"It's more expensive than I thought."
"I hear you. Let's look at the value you're receiving. This isn't just about learning about aromatherapy; it's about transforming your well-being. Consider the cost of ongoing stress ‚Äì lost sleep, decreased productivity, potential health issues. This program is an investment in your long-term health and happiness. We also have a payment plan available if that makes it more accessible."
"I'm not sure if it will really work for me."
"That's a valid concern. I can share testimonials from other participants who were initially skeptical but experienced significant positive changes. Also, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you participate fully in the program and don't see results, I'll offer a full refund."
"Can I think about it and get back to you?"
"Of course! I want you to feel completely comfortable with your decision. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can provide some more information or answer any remaining questions you have right now."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful group program sales call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's needs, presenting the program as a solution, handling objections effectively, and confidently stating the price.

2. How can you address a prospect's concern about the cost of your group program?

Highlight the value they're receiving, offer a payment plan, and emphasize the long-term benefits of the program.

Key Takeaways

  • Group programs offer a scalable way to increase your income and impact.
  • A structured enrollment call helps guide the conversation and address potential concerns.
  • Confidence in your pricing and the value you provide is essential for successful sales.
  • Handling objections with empathy and providing solutions can turn skeptics into clients.
  • Remember to focus on the transformation you offer, not just the features of the program.
