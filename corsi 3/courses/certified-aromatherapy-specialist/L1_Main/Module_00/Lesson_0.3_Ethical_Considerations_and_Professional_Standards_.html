The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a 4-tier learning journey, each building upon the previous one to transform you into a confident and competent aromatherapy specialist:

  1. Level 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build your core knowledge of aromatherapy, including essential oil profiles, safety guidelines, and fundamental application techniques. Think of it as building the strong foundation for your aromatherapy practice.
  2. Level 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): You'll delve into more complex cases and explore the clinical depth of aromatherapy. You'll learn how to address specific health conditions and tailor your approach to individual client needs.
  3. Level 3: Master (Modules 24-29): This level focuses on honing your skills to train others and provide supervision. You'll learn how to effectively communicate your knowledge and guide aspiring aromatherapy practitioners.
  4. Level 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): We'll focus on building your business and attracting clients. You'll learn marketing strategies, ethical considerations, and practical tips for creating a thriving aromatherapy practice.
