Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Diane, thank you for sharing all of that with me. It takes incredible courage to talk about these experiences. I want you to know that what you're feeling is a normal response to what you've been through. You are not broken, and you are not alone."
DIANE:
"Thank you. I feel so much shame and guilt. Like I should be over it by now."
YOU:
"That's a very common feeling, Diane. But trauma doesn't work on a timeline. It impacts our nervous system and our beliefs about ourselves. We're going to work together to help you re-regulate your nervous system and rewrite those beliefs."
YOU:
"I want to assure you that our work together will be approached with the utmost sensitivity. We will move at your pace, and your safety and comfort are my top priorities. Does that sound okay with you?"
DIANE:
"Yes, that makes me feel a lot better."
YOU:
"Wonderful. To start, let‚Äôs explore some of the physical sensations you experience when you feel anxious. This will help us understand how your body is holding onto this trauma, and how we can begin to release it."
