Using Constellations to Resolve Career Challenges

Case Study: Chronic Relationship Conflict

Case Study: Chronic Relationship Conflict

Client: Sarah, 38 years old

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah reported chronic conflict with her partner, Mark. They argued frequently about finances, household responsibilities, and parenting styles. Despite attempts at couples therapy, the conflicts persisted, leaving them both feeling frustrated and disconnected.

Intervention: A Family Constellation was conducted to explore the underlying dynamics contributing to the conflict. Representatives were chosen for Sarah, Mark, Sarah's mother, and Mark's father. The constellation revealed that Sarah was unconsciously mirroring her mother's critical and controlling behavior towards her father, while Mark was reacting in a similar way to his father's passive and withdrawn behavior.

Outcomes: Through the constellation, Sarah and Mark gained a deeper understanding of the transgenerational patterns influencing their relationship. They were able to express empathy for their parents' experiences and release the unconscious loyalties that were driving their conflict. Following the constellation, they reported a significant decrease in arguments, improved communication, and a greater sense of connection and intimacy. Sarah and Mark reported a 40% reduction in conflict frequency within one month of the intervention.
