The Methodology Framework

At the heart of our certification program lies a robust methodology framework, built upon the principles of systemic thinking, phenomenology, and trauma-informed care. This framework provides a solid foundation for understanding and applying Family Constellations Therapyâ„¢ effectively.

The framework consists of three key pillars:

  1. Systemic Perspective: Understanding individuals within the context of their family system and recognizing the interconnectedness of all members.
  2. Phenomenological Approach: Focusing on the client's subjective experience and honoring their unique perception of reality.
  3. Trauma-Informed Lens: Recognizing the potential impact of trauma on individuals and families and approaching therapy with sensitivity and awareness.

By integrating these three pillars, our methodology framework enables you to work with clients in a holistic and compassionate manner, addressing the root causes of their challenges and facilitating lasting healing.
