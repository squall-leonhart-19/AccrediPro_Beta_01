Here's a script to guide your initial conversation with Diane. Remember to adapt it to your own style and intuition.

YOU:
"Diane, thank you for sharing your story with me. It takes courage to explore these deeper feelings. I understand you're feeling stuck and unfulfilled, and I want to assure you that Family Constellations can offer a unique perspective on this."
DIANE:
"I've read a little about it, but I'm not sure I fully understand how it works."

"That's perfectly fine. In essence, Family Constellations helps us uncover unconscious patterns and entanglements within your family system that may be influencing your present-day experience. We'll explore how these patterns might be contributing to your feeling of stuckness."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the initial consultation with Diane?

The primary goal is to establish rapport, understand Diane's chief complaint, and introduce the concept of Family Constellations as a potential path to healing.

2. Why is it important to avoid jumping to conclusions about Diane's family dynamics?

Jumping to conclusions can invalidate Diane's experience and create a sense of mistrust. It's crucial to allow her to guide the exploration and reveal information at her own pace.
