The certification exam consists of two parts: a written component and a practical component. The written exam assesses your understanding of the core concepts, principles, and ethical considerations of Family Constellations Therapy. Effective Exam Preparation

Effective preparation is key to success. Here are some strategies to help you prepare:

  • Review Course Materials: Go through all modules, focusing on key concepts and principles.
  • Practice Constellations: Facilitate constellations with friends, family, or study groups.
  • Time Management: Practice answering questions under timed conditions.
  • Stress Reduction: Incorporate relaxation techniques such as meditation or deep breathing exercises. A 2021 study in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine found that mindfulness meditation significantly reduces test anxiety (Grossman et al., 2021).
