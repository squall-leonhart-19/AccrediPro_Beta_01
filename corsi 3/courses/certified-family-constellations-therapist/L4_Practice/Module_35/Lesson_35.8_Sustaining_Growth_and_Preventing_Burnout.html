Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Validate 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I understand you're looking to improve your family dynamics. I'm happy to explore how I can help. First, how are you doing today?"
YOU:
"It takes courage to reach out. I commend you for taking this step. Many women in your situation feel overwhelmed, so you're not alone."
Phase 2: Explore the Problem & Impact 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the challenges you're facing with your adult children. What specific conflicts are you experiencing, and how do they make you feel?"
YOU:
"How is this impacting other areas of your life? Is it affecting your sleep, your relationships, your ability to enjoy your hobbies?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the most urgent, how important is it for you to resolve these family issues?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution & Value 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, what I hear you saying is that you want to create a more peaceful and loving home environment, and you're willing to invest in that. Is that right?"
YOU:
"My approach uses Family Constellations to uncover hidden dynamics and patterns within your family system. We'll identify the root causes of the conflicts and create lasting solutions. Many of my clients, like Maria, a 48-year-old who had similar challenges, have reported significant improvements in their family relationships within just a few sessions. Maria now enjoys weekly dinners with her children and grandchildren after almost a year of not speaking."
YOU:
"I offer a 12-week program that includes weekly private sessions, personalized exercises, and ongoing support. This provides a structured and supportive approach to achieving your goals."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I'm confident I can help you transform your family dynamics. Are you ready to learn more about the program and how we can get started?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Our sessions are designed to be efficient and impactful. Many of my clients are busy professionals and mothers. We can work together to find a schedule that fits your lifestyle. Plus, the long-term benefits of improved family relationships will free up your time and energy."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can guarantee my commitment to providing you with the best possible support and guidance. My program is based on proven methods and techniques, and I have a track record of helping clients achieve significant improvements. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee ‚Äì if you're not satisfied with your progress after the first month, we can discuss a refund."
"I need to think about it and discuss it with my partner."
"That's perfectly reasonable. What specific aspects do you need to think about? I can provide you with a detailed program overview that you can share with your partner. We can also schedule a brief call together to address any questions or concerns they may have. Would that be helpful?"
"I'm skeptical about Family Constellations. I've never heard of it."
"That's understandable. Family Constellations is a powerful but lesser-known approach. It's based on the idea that we're all connected to our family systems and that unresolved issues from the past can impact our present. I can send you some research articles and testimonials that explain the process and its benefits in more detail. I also offer a free introductory workshop where you can experience the power of Family Constellations firsthand."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport & Validate, Explore the Problem & Impact, Present Your Solution & Value, Close & Next Steps.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it?"

Acknowledge their need to think, then gently inquire about the specific aspects they need to consider. Offer to provide more information or schedule a follow-up call to address their concerns.

Key Takeaways

  • Building rapport and validating the prospect's feelings is crucial for establishing trust.
  • Understanding the prospect's problem and its impact on their life is essential for presenting a relevant solution.
  • Presenting your solution with confidence and highlighting its value is key to overcoming objections.
  • Practicing your pricing presentation and call-to-action will make you more comfortable and effective in sales conversations.
  • Remember that each client represents a family transformed, and your work has a profound impact.
