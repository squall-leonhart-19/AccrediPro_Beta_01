Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to learn more about what's going on for you. To start, what prompted you to reach out?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me on Instagram. What resonated with you from my content?"
Phase 2: Explore the Challenges (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the challenges you're facing with your daughter. What does a typical day look like in your household?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to improve the situation? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, what impact will this have on your family in the long term? What's the ideal outcome you're hoping for?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're feeling overwhelmed and exhausted from the constant conflict. I understand. Many of my clients come to me with similar challenges. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"In my practice, I use Family Constellations to uncover the hidden dynamics that are contributing to these conflicts. We'll work together to identify the root causes and create a pathway toward healing and harmony. My 8-week program provides a supportive and transformative experience."
Phase 4: Confident Close (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I genuinely believe I can help you create the peaceful family environment you desire. Does this sound like the support you've been looking for? Great! Let's discuss the program details and how to get started."

Handling Objections Like a Pro

Common Objections & Effective Responses

"I'm not sure if Family Constellations is right for me. I don't really understand it."
"That's perfectly understandable. It's a unique approach. The best way to understand it is to experience it. I offer a free introductory workshop where you can participate in a mini-constellation and see for yourself how powerful it can be. Would you be interested in attending?"
"I need to talk to my partner about this first."
"I completely respect that. Is there anything specific you want to discuss with your partner? Perhaps I can provide some information or answer any questions they might have. We can even schedule a brief call together."
"I'm worried it won't work for my family."
"I understand your concern. Every family is unique, and there are no guarantees. However, Family Constellations has helped countless families resolve deeply rooted issues and create stronger connections. I offer a satisfaction guarantee ‚Äì if you're not completely satisfied after the first two weeks, you'll receive a full refund. How does that sound?"
"I don't have the time right now."
"I hear you. Life gets busy. However, think about the long-term impact of continuing down the same path. Investing in your family's well-being now can save you time and stress in the future. Plus, my program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. We can work together to find times that work for you."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Warm Welcome & Rapport, Explore the Challenges, Present Your Solution, and Confident Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I need to talk to my partner about this first?"

Acknowledge their need to consult with their partner, inquire if there's specific information you can provide, and offer to schedule a brief call with both of them.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is essential for building a thriving practice.
  • A well-structured discovery call can significantly increase your chances of converting prospects into clients.
  • Handling objections with confidence and empathy builds trust and demonstrates your expertise.
  • Practice presenting your pricing with assurance to convey the value of your services.
  • Realistic income goals are achievable with consistent marketing and client acquisition efforts.
