Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I need to think about it."
"I understand completely, Susan. This is an important decision, and I want you to feel 100% confident. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the financial investment, the time commitment, or something else? Knowing that will help me provide you with the information you need."
"I don't know if I can afford this right now."
"I hear you, Susan. Many of my clients initially feel that way. Let's explore this a bit. What are you currently spending on things like stress relief, sleep aids, or other solutions that aren't truly working? What if, instead of just managing the symptoms, we could actually address the root cause of your stress and create lasting change? What would that be worth to you in terms of your health, happiness, and overall quality of life?"
"I'm not sure if this is really for me."
"I appreciate your honesty, Susan. What specifically makes you feel that way? Is it something about my approach, the time commitment, or something else? I'm happy to address any concerns you have and help you determine if this is the right fit for you."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, absolutely! I offer a payment plan option of three monthly installments to make it more accessible. Would you like me to share those details with you?"
