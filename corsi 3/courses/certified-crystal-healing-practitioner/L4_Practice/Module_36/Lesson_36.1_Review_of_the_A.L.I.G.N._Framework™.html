Assessing Energy Fields

Accurate assessment is the foundation of effective crystal healing. Remember the various methods we've covered:

  • Intuitive Scanning: Using your intuition to sense imbalances or blockages in the client's energy field.
  • Dowsing: Employing a pendulum or other dowsing tool to identify areas of energetic disturbance.
  • Kinesiology: Utilizing muscle testing to assess the body's response to different stimuli and identify areas of weakness or imbalance.
  • Observation: Paying close attention to the client's physical posture, facial expressions, and verbal cues.

A key skill is to triangulate information from multiple assessment methods. Don't rely solely on one technique. For example, if intuitive scanning suggests a blockage in the solar plexus, confirm this with dowsing or kinesiology.
