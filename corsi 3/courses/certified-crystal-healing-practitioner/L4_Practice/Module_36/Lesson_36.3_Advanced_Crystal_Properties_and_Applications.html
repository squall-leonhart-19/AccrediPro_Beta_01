Exploring Uncommon Crystals

  • Moldavite: Known as the "Stone of Transformation," moldavite is a tektite formed from a meteorite impact. It's believed to accelerate spiritual evolution and facilitate deep healing.

  • Selenite: Though relatively common, selenite's powerful cleansing and purifying properties are often underestimated. It's excellent for clearing energetic blockages and enhancing intuition.

Scientific Understanding of Crystals

While crystal healing is often associated with metaphysical concepts, there's also a growing body of scientific evidence supporting their properties. Key scientific principles include:

  • Piezoelectricity: Some crystals, like quartz, generate an electrical charge when subjected to mechanical stress. This property is used in various technologies, including watches and electronic devices.
  • Vibrational Resonance: Crystals vibrate at specific frequencies, which can interact with and influence the human energy field.
  • Water Structuring: Studies have shown that crystals can alter the structure of water, potentially influencing its properties and benefits.

Understanding these scientific principles can enhance your credibility and provide a more grounded explanation of how crystals work. As you Assess a client, remember that scientific principles don't negate the value of intuition and experience, but rather complement them.
