Case Study: Overcoming Client Resistance

Client: Sarah, 38 years old, presenting with anxiety and difficulty sleeping.

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah reported feeling overwhelmed, experiencing frequent panic attacks, and struggling with insomnia. She was initially skeptical about crystal healing but open to exploring alternative therapies.

Intervention: During the initial consultation (Alignment phase), Sarah expressed her doubts about the effectiveness of crystals. The practitioner acknowledged her concerns and focused on building trust by explaining the scientific principles behind crystal energy and its potential impact on the body's energy field. A personalized crystal layout was designed, incorporating amethyst (calming), lepidolite (stress relief), and clear quartz (energy amplification). The session focused on gentle energy work and guided meditation.

Outcomes: After the first session, Sarah reported feeling more relaxed and grounded. Over the course of four sessions, her anxiety levels decreased significantly, and she experienced improved sleep quality. She also developed a greater appreciation for the potential benefits of crystal healing.
