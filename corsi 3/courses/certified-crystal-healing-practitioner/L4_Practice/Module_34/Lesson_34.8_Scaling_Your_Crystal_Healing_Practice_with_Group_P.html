Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Reconnect & Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Linda! It's so great to connect with you again after the workshop. How are you feeling since then?"
YOU:
"I'm so glad you enjoyed it! What was the most valuable thing you took away from the workshop?"
Phase 2: Explore Her Needs & Desires 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned wanting to rediscover yourself. What does that look like for you? What are you hoping to achieve?"
YOU:
"What's been holding you back from achieving this so far?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your life look and feel like in 6 months?"
Phase 3: Connect to the Program & Benefits 10 min
YOU:
"Linda, based on what you've shared, I really believe the Rediscover You group program can provide the support and tools you need to achieve your goals. It's designed to help women in your exact situation find their purpose and connect with like-minded individuals."
YOU:
"Specifically, in the program, we will [mention 2-3 key modules/activities that align with her needs]. How does that sound to you?"
YOU:
"Imagine yourself 12 weeks from now, feeling energized, connected, and excited about your future. That's what this program can help you create."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"So Linda, are you ready to join the Rediscover You program and start your journey?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Time is valuable. The program is designed to be flexible, with modules that you can access at your own pace. Plus, the community support can actually save you time by providing resources and solutions. How about we look at your schedule together and see where we can fit it in? Even 1-2 hours a week can make a huge difference."
"It's too expensive."
"I hear you. Let's break it down. The program is an investment in yourself and your future. Think about the cost of staying where you are ‚Äì the lost opportunities, the lack of fulfillment. We also have payment plan options available to make it more accessible. Would you like to hear more about those?"
"I'm not sure if it's right for me."
"That's a valid concern. What specifically makes you unsure? Perhaps I can address those concerns. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If, after the first two weeks, you don't feel the program is a good fit, I'll provide a full refund. How does that sound?"
"Can I think about it?"
"Of course! I want you to feel completely comfortable with your decision. What are the key things you're considering? Is there any additional information I can provide to help you make a decision?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call for group program enrollment?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's needs and desires, connecting the program to their specific goals, addressing objections, and confidently presenting pricing.

2. How can you handle the objection "It's too expensive" when promoting a group program?

Break down the investment, highlight the value and ROI, and offer payment plan options.

Key Takeaways

  • Group programs offer a scalable way to increase your income and impact.
  • Discovery calls are crucial for converting interested leads into paying clients.
  • Practice your script and pricing presentation to build confidence.
  • Address objections with empathy and offer solutions.
  • Focus on the transformation and the value you provide.
