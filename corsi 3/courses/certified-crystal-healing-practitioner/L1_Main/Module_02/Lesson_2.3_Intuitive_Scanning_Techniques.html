Hand Scanning Techniques

Hand scanning, also known as energy scanning, involves using your hands to sense the energy field around a person or object. This is a foundational skill for locating blockages within the A.L.I.G.N. Frameworkâ„¢.

Here's how to perform hand scanning:

  1. Preparation: Ground yourself by visualizing roots extending from your feet into the earth. Center yourself by taking a few deep breaths.
  2. Positioning: Hold your hands a few inches away from the body, palms facing the area you are scanning.
  3. Sensing: Slowly move your hands over the body, paying attention to any sensations you feel.

Common sensations you might experience include:

  • Temperature Variations: Hot or cold spots can indicate areas of congestion or depletion.
  • Tingling or Buzzing: Thesesensations can signify energy flow.
  • Pressure or Resistance: These feelings may indicate blockages or stagnant energy.
  • Pulsations: A rhythmic pulsing sensation can indicate a strong energy center or chakra.

Visual Scanning Techniques

Visual scanning involves using your inner eye to perceive the energy field or aura around a person. While it may seem challenging at first, with practice, you can learn to see subtle energetic patterns and distortions.

Here's how to develop your visual scanning abilities:

  1. Soft Gaze: Relax your eyes and soften your gaze. Avoid focusing too intently on any one point.
  2. Peripheral Vision: Use your peripheral vision to observe the area around the person.
  3. Color Perception: Pay attention to any colors you see in the aura. Different colors are associated with different emotions and energies. For example, blue often represents communication and peace, while red can indicate passion or anger.
  4. Pattern Recognition: Look for any distortions, breaks, or imbalances in the aura. These can indicate areas of blockage or disharmony.

A 2018 study published in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine explored the relationship between perceived aura colors and emotional states (Benor, 2018). While the study was small, it suggested a correlation between certain aura colors and reported feelings, providing a foundation for further research in this area.

Auditory Scanning Techniques

While less commonly discussed, auditory scanning involves listening to the subtle sounds and intuitive messages that arise when you focus on a person's energy field. This goes beyond simply hearing external sounds; it's about tuning into your inner guidance.

Here's how to practice auditory scanning:

  1. Quiet Environment: Find a quiet space where you won't be disturbed.
  2. Inner Listening: Close your eyes and focus your attention on the person's energy field. Listen for any inner sounds, words, or phrases that come to mind.
  3. Intuitive Messages: Pay attention to any intuitive messages or insights you receive. These may be related to the person's emotional state, physical health, or life purpose.

Remember that auditory scanning is highly subjective and requires practice to develop accuracy and discernment. It's crucial to differentiate between genuine intuitive messages and your own thoughts or biases.

Integrating Multiple Senses

The most effective energy assessment involves integrating multiple senses to gain a comprehensive understanding of a person's energy field. By combining hand scanning, visual scanning, and auditory scanning, you can create a more complete and accurate picture of any blockages or imbalances.

Here's how to integrate your senses:

  1. Start with Hand Scanning: Use hand scanning to get a general sense of the energy field and identify any areas of interest.
  2. Follow with Visual Scanning: Use visual scanning to further investigate these areas and look for any distortions or imbalances in the aura.
  3. Listen for Auditory Cues: Finally, tune into your inner guidance and listen for any intuitive messages or insights that arise.
