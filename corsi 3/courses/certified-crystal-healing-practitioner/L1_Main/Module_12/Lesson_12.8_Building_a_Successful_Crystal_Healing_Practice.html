Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on crystal healing. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing so openly. It sounds like you're at a crossroads, seeking deeper meaning. I want to introduce the idea of using crystals as a tool to reconnect with your inner self and reignite your passions."
MARIA:
"Crystals? I've seen them in stores, but I don't really know anything about them."
YOU:
"That's perfectly fine! Many people are new to this. Think of crystals as amplifiers of energy and intention. We can use them to tap into your own innate wisdom and clarity."
YOU:
"Based on what you've told me, I'd suggest exploring crystals that resonate with emotional healing, self-discovery, and joy. Rose Quartz for self-love, Amethyst for spiritual connection, and Citrine for optimism. How do those sound to you?"
MARIA:
"Those sound...interesting. I'm willing to try something new."
YOU:
"Great! We can start with a simple meditation, holding a Rose Quartz, and focusing on releasing any self-doubt or negativity. Would you be open to experiencing that together?"

Key Takeaways

  • Crystals can be powerful tools for emotional healing and self-discovery.
  • It's essential to meet clients where they are, respecting their beliefs and openness.
  • Introduce crystals in a grounded, accessible way, focusing on intention and personal experience.
  • Suggest a few relevant crystals to start, avoiding overwhelm.
