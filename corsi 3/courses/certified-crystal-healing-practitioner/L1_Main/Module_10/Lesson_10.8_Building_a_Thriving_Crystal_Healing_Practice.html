Chief Complaint
Feeling overwhelmed and disconnected. "My classroom is chaotic, and I feel completely drained by the end of the day."

Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing. It sounds like you're navigating a lot right now. It's completely understandable that you feel overwhelmed. The good news is, your crystals can be powerful allies in helping you find that sense of calm and connection you're seeking."
MARIA:
"I hope so! I bought them a while ago, but I'm not really sure what to do with them. It feels like just another thing on my to-do list."
YOU:
"I understand. Let's reframe that. Instead of adding *more* to your list, we'll find simple ways to weave crystals into your existing routine. We'll focus on making it easy and enjoyable, not a chore."
YOU:
"Given your amethyst, rose quartz, and clear quartz, let's explore how we can use those to create a sense of peace in your classroom, support your sleep, and boost your overall sense of wellbeing. How does that sound?"
MARIA:
"That sounds wonderful. I'm especially interested in bringing more peace into my classroom. It's so chaotic!"
YOU:
"Perfect. Let's start there. Tell me about your classroom setup. Where do you spend most of your time, and what are some of the biggest challenges you face each day?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What's the most important thing to do before suggesting specific crystal placements in Maria's classroom?

Understand the layout of her classroom and where she spends most of her time.

2. Why is it important to focus on the crystals Maria already owns, rather than suggesting new ones?

To keep things simple and avoid overwhelming her. It's also a great way to help her appreciate the resources she already has.

Key Takeaways

  • Start by acknowledging the client's current challenges and validating their feelings.
  • Focus on simplicity and ease of integration when suggesting crystal practices.
  • Use the crystals the client already owns whenever possible.
  • Empower the client to make their own choices and adjust the suggestions to fit their needs.
  • Remember that empathy and understanding are just as important as crystal knowledge.
