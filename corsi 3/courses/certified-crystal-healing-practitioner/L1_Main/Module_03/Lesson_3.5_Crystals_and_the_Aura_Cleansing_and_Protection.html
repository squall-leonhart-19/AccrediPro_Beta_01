Detecting Auric Imbalances

Before we can cleanse and protect the aura, we need to be able to identify when it's out of balance. There are several methods for detecting auric imbalances, ranging from intuitive sensing to more structured techniques.

  • Intuitive Sensing: Developing your intuition is key. Pay attention to your gut feelings and any sensations you experience when interacting with someone. A sense of unease, heaviness, or discomfort could indicate an imbalance in their aura.
  • Dowsing: Using a pendulum or dowsing rods can help detect energetic blockages or weak spots in the aura. Hold the pendulum over different areas of the body and observe its movement. Erratic or sluggish movement can indicate an issue.
  • Kinesiology (Muscle Testing): This technique involves testing the strength of a muscle in response to a stimulus. A weakened muscle can indicate an energetic imbalance.
  • Visual Observation: While it takes practice, some individuals can see the aura visually. Look for areas of dimness, discoloration, or distortion in the energetic field.

Often, physical, emotional, or mental symptoms can be indicators of a compromised aura. Remember that the A.L.I.G.N. Frameworkâ„¢ emphasizes a holistic approach; addressing imbalances in the auric field can positively impact other areas of well-being.

Crystals for Auric Cleansing & Repair

Certain crystals possess powerful cleansing and restorative properties that can help heal and strengthen the aura. Here are a few examples:

  • Selenite: A powerful cleanser that can clear negative energies and blockages from the aura. It promotes clarity, peace, and spiritual connection.
  • Clear Quartz: Amplifies energy and can be programmed to cleanse and heal the aura. It brings balance and harmony.
  • Amethyst: Transmutes negative energy into positive energy and promotes relaxation and spiritual awareness. It can help repair tears or holes in the aura.
  • Black Tourmaline: A protective stone that absorbs negative energy and shields the aura from psychic attacks and electromagnetic smog.

Application: To cleanse the aura, hold the chosen crystal in your hand and visualize its energy radiating outwards, filling your energetic field with light and clearing away any darkness or stagnation. You can also create a crystal grid around yourself or your client, focusing on intention and visualization to amplify the cleansing effect.

Creating Crystal Shields

A crystal shield is a protective barrier that surrounds your aura, deflecting negative energies and preventing psychic attacks. It's like an energetic bodyguard, keeping you safe and secure.

Crystals for Protection:

  • Black Obsidian: Forms a powerful shield against negativity and psychic attacks. It helps to ground and protect your energy field.
  • Labradorite: Creates a shield of light around the aura, deflecting unwanted energies and enhancing intuition.
  • Smoky Quartz: Grounds and protects against negative energies while promoting emotional balance.
  • Hematite: Forms a protective barrier and helps to deflect negative energy back to its source.

Creating a Shield: To create a crystal shield, choose one or more of the protective crystals listed above. Hold the crystal in your hand and visualize a bubble of protective light surrounding your aura. Feel the energy of the crystal strengthening your energetic boundaries. You can carry the crystal with you throughout the day or place it in your environment to maintain the shield.

Crystals & Smudging

Combining crystals with smudging (the practice of burning sacred herbs like sage) can amplify the cleansing and protective effects on the aura. Smudging helps to clear away stagnant energy and create a clean slate for the crystals to work their magic.

How to Combine:

  1. Light a smudge stick (sage, palo santo, etc.) and allow the smoke to fill the space.
  2. Hold your chosen crystal in your hand and visualize its energy combining with the smoke to cleanse and purify the aura.
  3. Gently waft the smoke around your body, focusing on areas where you feel blockages or stagnation.
  4. As you smudge, affirm your intention for cleansing and protection.

Safety Note: Always practice smudging in a well-ventilated area and never leave a burning smudge stick unattended.
