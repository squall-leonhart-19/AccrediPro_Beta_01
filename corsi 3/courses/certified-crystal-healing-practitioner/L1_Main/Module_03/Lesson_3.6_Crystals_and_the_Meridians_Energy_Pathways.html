Stimulating Meridian Flow with Crystals

Now that we've identified some key points, let's explore how to use crystals to stimulate meridian flow. The goal is to gently encourage the smooth circulation of Qi, removing any stagnation or blockages. Remember, as a crystal healing practitioner, your intention and intuition are paramount. Trust your inner guidance to choose the right crystals and techniques for each client.

Here are some techniques you can use:

  1. Crystal Placement: Place the chosen crystal directly on the acupressure point, securing it with medical tape if needed.
  2. Crystal Massage: Gently massage the crystal over the acupressure point in a circular motion. This helps to stimulate the flow of Qi and break up any blockages.
  3. Crystal Tapping: Use a crystal point to gently tap the acupressure point. This sends vibrations into the meridian, encouraging energy flow.
  4. Crystal Grids: Create a crystal grid around the client, focusing on specific meridians or organ systems. This amplifies the healing energy and promotes overall balance.
