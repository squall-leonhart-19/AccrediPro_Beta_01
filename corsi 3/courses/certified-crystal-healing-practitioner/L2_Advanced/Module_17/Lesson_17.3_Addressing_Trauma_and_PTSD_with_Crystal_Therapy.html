Gentle and Grounding Techniques for Client Safety

Safety is paramount when working with clients who have experienced trauma. Employ gentle and grounding techniques to create a secure and supportive environment. Here are some strategies:

  • Establish clear boundaries: Explain the session process and ensure the client feels in control.
  • Use grounding crystals: Place grounding crystals like Smoky Quartz or Black Tourmaline near the client's feet.
  • Incorporate breathwork: Guide the client through slow, deep breaths to regulate their nervous system.
  • Offer choices: Allow the client to choose the crystals used and their placement.
  • Regular check-ins: Ask the client how they are feeling throughout the session.

Remember to move at the client's pace and honor their boundaries. If they become overwhelmed, pause the session and offer support.
