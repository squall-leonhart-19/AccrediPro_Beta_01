Review of Chronic Fatigue Syndrome (CFS)

Chronic Fatigue Syndrome (CFS), also known as Myalgic Encephalomyelitis (ME/CFS), is a complex, debilitating disorder characterized by persistent fatigue that is not relieved by rest and is not directly caused by other medical conditions. It's essential to understand that CFS isn't just feeling tired; it's a profound and persistent exhaustion that significantly impacts daily life. Energetically, CFS often manifests as a severely depleted energy field, with blockages in the chakras and meridians that impede the flow of vital force. Individuals with CFS often experience a disconnect between their mind and body, leading to further energetic stagnation.
