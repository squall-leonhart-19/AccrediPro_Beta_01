Contraindications and Precautions

While crystal healing is generally safe, there are certain contraindications and precautions to consider when working with clients undergoing addiction treatment. It's essential to prioritize the client's safety and well-being at all times. Crystal healing should not replace medical treatment or therapy. It is a complementary modality that supports the client's overall recovery journey.

Avoid using crystals that stimulate energy too strongly, such as high-energy crystals like Fire Agate, in clients experiencing severe anxiety or agitation. Always obtain informed consent from the client before beginning any crystal healing session. Be mindful of any potential allergies to metals or minerals in the crystals. If the client has a history of psychosis or severe mental illness, proceed with caution and consult with their healthcare provider. Grounding techniques, such as using Hematite, are essential to keep the client connected to the earth and prevent overstimulation.
