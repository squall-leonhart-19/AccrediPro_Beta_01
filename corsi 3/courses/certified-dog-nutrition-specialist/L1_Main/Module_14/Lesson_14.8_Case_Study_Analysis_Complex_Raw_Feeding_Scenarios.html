Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for being so open about what you're experiencing. Digestive issues are incredibly common, and they can really impact your quality of life. It's brave of you to seek solutions, and I want you to know you're not alone."
MARIA:
"Thank you. It's just‚Ä¶ so frustrating. I try to eat healthy, but it doesn't seem to matter."
YOU:
"I understand. 'Eating healthy' is a great first step, but sometimes we need to dig deeper to understand *why* your body is reacting this way. Let's explore some potential root causes of your digestive discomfort. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"Specifically, I'd like to explore the possibility of enzyme deficiencies, gut bacteria imbalances, and the impact of stress on your digestion. Does that align with what you're hoping to address?"
MARIA:
"Yes, that makes sense. I've heard about gut health, but I don't really know where to start."
YOU:
"Perfect. We'll start with some gentle questions about your diet and lifestyle. No pressure at all. This will help us create a personalized plan that supports your unique needs. How does that sound?"
