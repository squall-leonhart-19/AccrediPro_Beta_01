Your Cohort & Community

One of the most valuable aspects of this program is the community. You'll be surrounded by a supportive and inspiring group of fellow students who are on the same journey as you. This is your tribe, your network, and your family.

The program includes a private online forum where you can connect with your cohort, ask questions, share your experiences, and offer support to one another. We also host regular group calls and Q&A sessions where you can interact with instructors and get personalized guidance.

Research consistently shows that social support is a critical factor in achieving success and well-being. A 2022 study in the Journal of Positive Psychology found that individuals with strong social connections reported higher levels of happiness, resilience, and overall life satisfaction (n=1246, r = 0.34, p < 0.001). (Diener et al., 2022)

Don't be afraid to reach out and connect with your fellow students. Share your struggles, celebrate your successes, and build lasting relationships. Together, you can achieve far more than you ever could alone.

Your First Steps to Success

Ready to dive in? Here are your first steps to maximize your learning experience:

  1. Explore the Course Platform: Take some time to familiarize yourself with the course platform. Watch the welcome video, read the course syllabus, and explore the different modules.
  2. Introduce Yourself in the Forum: Head over to the online forum and introduce yourself to your fellow students. Share your goals, your background, and what you're hoping to get out of the program.
  3. Set Realistic Goals: Don't try to do everything at once. Set small, achievable goals for each week and celebrate your progress along the way.
  4. Schedule Dedicated Study Time: Treat this program like a job and schedule dedicated study time each week. Even just 30 minutes a day can make a big difference.
  5. Ask Questions: Don't be afraid to ask questions! There are no stupid questions, and we're here to support you every step of the way.

Remember, this is your journey. Take your time, enjoy the process, and trust that you have everything you need to succeed. I'm so excited to be a part of your transformation!
