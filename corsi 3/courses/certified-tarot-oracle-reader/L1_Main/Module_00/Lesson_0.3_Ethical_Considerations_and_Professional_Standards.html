The 4-Tier Transformation

This certification program is designed around a 4-Tier Transformation model, a structured approach to guide clients through profound personal growth using tarot and oracle readings as a catalyst. Each tier builds upon the previous one, creating a cohesive and effective coaching process.

  1. Tier 1: Foundation & Clarity. Establishing a solid understanding of the client's current situation, identifying core challenges, and setting clear intentions.
  2. Tier 2: Exploration & Insight. Utilizing tarot and oracle cards to uncover hidden patterns, gain new perspectives, and explore potential pathways.
  3. Tier 3: Empowerment & Action. Translating insights into actionable steps, fostering self-efficacy, and creating a personalized plan for progress.
  4. Tier 4: Integration & Growth. Supporting the client in integrating new awareness into their daily life, tracking progress, and celebrating achievements.

This tiered approach ensures that readings are not just insightful but also lead to tangible and lasting positive change for your clients. Each module in this course will delve deeper into these tiers, providing you with the knowledge and skills to effectively guide your clients through each stage.

What Makes This Different

While many tarot and oracle courses focus solely on card meanings and spreads, this certification emphasizes the coaching aspect of reading. We believe that a truly effective reader is also a skilled coach, able to empower clients to take ownership of their lives and create positive change.

Here's what sets this program apart:

  • Focus on practical coaching skills: You'll learn how to ask powerful questions, actively listen, provide constructive feedback, and motivate clients to take action.
  • Integration of evidence-based coaching techniques: We incorporate principles from positive psychology, cognitive behavioral coaching, and motivational interviewing to enhance your effectiveness.
  • Emphasis on ethical practice: You'll learn how to conduct readings with integrity, respect client autonomy, and avoid potential harm.
  • Real-world case studies and practice sessions: You'll have ample opportunities to apply your knowledge and skills in realistic scenarios.

The Methodology Framework

The core methodology framework we use is based on the GROW model, a widely recognized and effective coaching framework. We've adapted it to specifically address the unique nuances of tarot and oracle readings.

The GROW model stands for:

  • Goal: What does the client want to achieve?
  • Reality: What is the client's current situation?
  • Options: What are the possible pathways to achieve the goal?
  • Will: What actions will the client take?

By integrating the GROW model with the 4-Tier Transformation, you'll have a powerful framework for guiding clients through a structured and effective process of self-discovery and personal growth. You'll learn how to use tarot and oracle cards to illuminate each stage of the GROW model, providing clients with deeper insights and clarity.
