Legal Considerations

The legality of offering tarot and oracle readings as a coaching service is generally straightforward, but it's wise to be aware of basic legal principles to protect yourself and your clients.

Here's a quick overview:

  • Health Coaching Legality: Health coaching is generally legal as long as you stay within your scope of practice.
  • Insurance and Liability: While you likely won't need malpractice insurance (as you're not practicing medicine), general liability insurance is a smart move to protect against claims of property damage or bodily injury.
  • Staying in Your Lane: Never provide medical or psychological advice. Always refer clients to qualified professionals when necessary.

Ethics & Professionalism

Upholding ethical standards is crucial for building a thriving and respected practice. Here are some key principles to guide you:

  • Client Confidentiality: Always maintain strict confidentiality. What is shared in a reading stays in the reading.
  • Professional Conduct: Be punctual, prepared, and respectful. Maintain clear boundaries.
  • When to Refer Out: Recognize when a client's needs are beyond your expertise. Have a network of trusted professionals to whom you can refer.
  • Building Trust and Credibility: Be authentic, honest, and transparent. Let your clients know that you are there to empower them.

These principles will help you become a trusted guide for your clients.
