Former teacher, strong intuition, loves helping people.

Excellent card knowledge, empathetic, good listener.
Growth Areas
Trouble with nuanced interpretations, overwhelmed by large spreads.
Her Question
"How do I handle a client who's asking about a very complicated relationship?"

The Case She Presents

Client: Emily, 48, Seeking Clarity on a Love Triangle

Sarah's Initial Reading:

"I did a Celtic Cross spread for Emily. The cards showed conflicting energies: The Lovers, The Tower, and The Hierophant all came up. I felt confused and couldn't give her a clear answer. I'm afraid I made things worse!"

Key Areas to Explore:

  • How did Sarah define the positions in the Celtic Cross?
  • What were her initial interpretations of the individual cards?
  • How did she attempt to synthesize the conflicting cards?
  • Did she set clear boundaries with Emily about the reading's scope?

Your Teaching Approach

Key Points to Cover with Sarah

1

Deconstruct the Spread

Review each card's position and meaning. Ask Sarah to explain her initial interpretations without judgment.

2

Focus on the Core Issue

Help Sarah identify the central theme: Emily's confusion and need for clarity. This simplifies the interpretation.

3

Highlight Card Relationships

Explain how the cards interact. The Lovers and The Hierophant could indicate a choice between passion and commitment, while The Tower suggests disruption.

4

Emphasize Ethical Boundaries

Remind Sarah to avoid giving advice. Instead, empower Emily to make her own decisions based on the insights gained.

Your Feedback Dialogue

How to Deliver Constructive Feedback

Start with Appreciation
"Sarah, thank you for sharing this reading. It takes courage to ask for help, and I admire your dedication to providing the best possible service."
Encourage Self-Reflection
"What did you find most challenging about this reading? Where did you feel most uncertain?"
Offer Alternative Interpretations
"The Lovers, Tower, and Hierophant together suggest a powerful conflict between desire, stability, and unexpected change. How does that resonate with you?"
Reinforce Empowerment
"Remember, our role is to provide insights, not solutions. How can you empower Emily to make her own choices, based on these insights?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What's the most important aspect of providing supervision to a new Tarot/Oracle reader?

Creating a safe space for them to learn and grow, focusing on their strengths, and reinforcing ethical boundaries.

2. How can you help a mentee navigate a complex reading without giving them the "answer?"

By deconstructing the spread, focusing on the core issue, highlighting card relationships, and encouraging self-reflection.

Key Takeaways

  • Supervision is about empowering new practitioners, not dictating their process.
  • Focus on their strengths and provide constructive feedback to build confidence.
  • Reinforce ethical boundaries to ensure responsible and client-centered readings.
  • Ask open-ended questions to encourage self-reflection and independent thinking.
  • Celebrate their growth and progress to foster a positive learning environment.
