Handling Objections Like a Pro

Navigating Common Objections

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Life gets busy. The program requires about 2-3 hours per week, including our sessions. Think of it as an investment in your future happiness and fulfillment. Could you carve out that time if it meant creating a life you truly love? Many of my clients find that prioritizing this actually *creates* more time and energy in other areas of their lives."
"I'm worried it won't work for me."
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can tell you that my program is tailored to each individual's needs. We'll work together to create a personalized plan that aligns with your unique goals and circumstances. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied after the first two weeks, you'll receive a full refund."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, absolutely! I offer a payment plan of three monthly installments to make it more accessible. Would you like me to share the details of that option?"
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can provide more information or address any remaining concerns you have. I want you to feel completely comfortable and confident in your decision."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Connection & Rapport, Exploring the Problem, Presenting Your Solution, and Closing.

2. How should you respond to the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then ask what specifically they need to consider. Offer to provide more information or address any remaining concerns.

3. What is the purpose of practicing your pricing presentation out loud?

Practicing your pricing presentation builds confidence and helps you deliver it smoothly and naturally, making you more persuasive.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call helps you understand your prospect's needs and present your services as the ideal solution.
  • Handling objections effectively is crucial for converting prospects into paying clients.
  • Confidence in presenting your pricing is essential for conveying the value of your services.
  • Even a few clients per month can generate a significant income.
  • Consistent practice and refinement of your sales process will lead to increased success.
