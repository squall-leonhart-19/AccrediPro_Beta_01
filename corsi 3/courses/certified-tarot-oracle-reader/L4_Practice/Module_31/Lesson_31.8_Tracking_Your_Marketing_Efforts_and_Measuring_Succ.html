Handling Objections with Grace

Common Concerns & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure I have the skills or talent for this."
"That's a common concern, Susan! But the beauty of Tarot & Oracle reading is that it's about intuition and connection, not innate talent. My program provides a step-by-step framework, and I offer personalized guidance every step of the way. Many of my most successful students started with zero experience."
"I'm worried about the cost."
"I understand. Investing in yourself is a big decision. Let's look at it this way: what's the cost of staying stuck and unfulfilled? My program isn't just about learning a skill; it's about rediscovering your passion and creating a life you love. Plus, many of my students start generating income through readings even before the program is over!"
"I need to talk to my family about it."
"Of course! It's important to have their support. How about I send you a detailed program overview that you can share with them? Also, I'd be happy to schedule a brief call with you and your family to answer any questions they might have. Would that be helpful?"
"I'm just not sure if now is the right time."
"I hear you. Sometimes, timing is everything. But what would need to happen for it to feel like the right time? What are you waiting for? If you keep waiting, will you miss out on an opportunity that could change your life?"

Key Takeaways

  • Building rapport is crucial for establishing trust and connection.
  • Uncovering your prospect's deepest needs is essential for presenting a relevant solution.
  • Handling objections with empathy and confidence is key to overcoming resistance.
  • Presenting your pricing with assurance demonstrates your value and expertise.
  • Mastering the call-to-action is vital for guiding your prospect towards a decision.
