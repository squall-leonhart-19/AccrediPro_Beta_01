Case Study: Emily, 38, Bloating & Fatigue

Client: Emily, a 38-year-old marketing manager.

Presenting Symptoms: Chronic bloating, fatigue, and brain fog for over six months. She had seen her primary care physician, who diagnosed her with IBS and recommended a low-FODMAP diet, which provided minimal relief.

Intervention:

  1. Comprehensive stool test revealed dysbiosis with overgrowth of opportunistic bacteria and low levels of beneficial bacteria.
  2. Personalized protocol including:
    • Dietary modifications: Elimination of gluten, dairy, and processed foods. Introduction of bone broth and fermented foods.
    • Supplementation: Probiotic blend with multiple strains (Lactobacillus and Bifidobacterium), digestive enzymes, and L-glutamine for gut lining repair.
  3. Stress management techniques: Daily meditation and gentle exercise.

Outcomes: Within four weeks, Emily reported significant reduction in bloating, improved energy levels, and clearer thinking. After three months, a follow-up stool test showed improved gut microbiome balance. She was able to reintroduce some previously restricted foods without experiencing symptoms. She reported feeling "like a new person" and was able to return to her normal activities with renewed energy and enthusiasm.
