Olivia's Client Case: Sarah, 48

What Olivia Tells You:

"Sarah, 48, has IBS-D, chronic fatigue, and anxiety. She's tried everything ‚Äì low-FODMAP, gluten-free, you name it. Her stool test shows dysbiosis with elevated Klebsiella. I'm not sure how to prioritize or what supplements to recommend. I feel lost."

Key Areas to Explore:

  • Has Olivia considered Sarah's stress levels and their impact on gut motility?
  • Did she review Sarah's medication list for potential gut disruptors?

  • Does Olivia have a clear strategy for rebalancing the gut microbiome beyond general probiotics?

Your Feedback Dialogue

How to Deliver Constructive Feedback

Acknowledge Her Effort
"Olivia, I can see you've put a lot of thought into Sarah's case. It's great that you're diving into the stool test results. Let's break this down into manageable steps."
Guide Her Thinking
"Instead of focusing on *what* to do, let's think about *why* we're doing it. How does Sarah's stress impact her gut, and what can we do to address that first?"
Share Your Experience
"In my experience, clients with chronic IBS often need more support with lifestyle factors than targeted supplements. It's about building a resilient foundation."
Instill Confidence
"You have the knowledge, Olivia. Now it's about applying it strategically. Let's create a step-by-step plan together. You've got this!"
