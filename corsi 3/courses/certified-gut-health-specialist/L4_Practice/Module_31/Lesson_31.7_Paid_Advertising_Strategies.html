Keyword Research

Keyword research is the foundation of a successful paid advertising campaign, especially on platforms like Google Ads. It involves identifying the terms your target audience uses when searching for information or solutions related to gut health. Effective keyword research ensures your ads appear to the right people at the right time.

Hereâ€™s how to approach keyword research:

  • Brainstorm: Start by listing all the terms you think potential clients might use. Think about symptoms (e.g., "bloating," "IBS," "acid reflux"), conditions (e.g., "SIBO," "leaky gut"), and solutions (e.g., "gut health diet," "probiotics").
  • Use Keyword Research Tools: Tools like Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, and Ahrefs can help you discover new keywords, analyze search volume, and assess competition.
  • Analyze Competitors: See what keywords your competitors are targeting. This can give you valuable insights into whatâ€™s working in your niche.

Setting Up Conversion Tracking

Conversion tracking allows you to measure the effectiveness of your advertising campaigns. A conversion is a desired action, such as booking a consultation, signing up for a newsletter, or making a purchase. By tracking conversions, you can see which ads are driving the most valuable results and optimize your campaigns accordingly.

Hereâ€™s how to set up conversion tracking:

  • Google Ads: Use Google Tag Manager to install conversion tracking tags on your website. Track form submissions, phone calls, and other key actions.
  • Facebook Ads: Use the Facebook Pixel to track website visitors and conversions. You can also track events within your app if you have one.
  • LinkedIn Ads: Use LinkedIn Insight Tag to track website conversions and retarget website visitors.

Ensure your tracking is accurate. A 2023 report by Nielsen found that up to 40% of online advertising spend is wasted due to poor targeting and tracking. Don't let this happen to you!

  • A/B Testing: Continuously test different ad copy, visuals, and landing pages to improve performance.
