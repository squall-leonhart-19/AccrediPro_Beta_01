Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set the Stage 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to learn more about what you're experiencing. To make the best use of our time, I'll ask some questions, share how I can help, and see if we're a good fit. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"Before we dive in, tell me a little bit about yourself. What do you enjoy doing when you're feeling your best?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Her Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned bloating and digestive discomfort. Can you describe what that feels like and when it typically occurs?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? What were the results?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the most bothersome, how would you rate the impact of these symptoms on your daily life?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your ideal digestive health look like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution & Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're struggling with [summarize her key pain points and desired outcomes]. Is that a fair assessment?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because I focus on identifying the root causes of your digestive issues through comprehensive testing and personalized protocols. We'll work together over 12 weeks to create a tailored plan that addresses your specific needs. Clients typically experience a 50-70% reduction in symptoms within the first month."
YOU:
"This includes [briefly mention key program components, e.g., personalized diet plan, targeted supplements, stress management techniques]. We'll also track your progress with regular check-ins and adjustments as needed."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this approach could be a good fit for you?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me share the program details and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time commitment."
"I understand. The program requires about 3-5 hours per week for meal prep, implementing protocols, and our coaching sessions. However, many clients find that by improving their digestion and energy levels, they actually become MORE productive and efficient in other areas of their lives. How important is it for you to prioritize your health right now?"
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can assure you that I'm committed to providing you with the highest level of support and guidance. My clients who fully implement the program typically experience significant improvements in their symptoms and overall well-being. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee ‚Äì if you're not happy with the program after the first 30 days, I'll provide a full refund."
"I need to discuss it with my partner."
"That's perfectly fine. To help with that conversation, I can send you a summary of the program and its benefits. Would it also be helpful to schedule a brief call with both of you to answer any questions your partner might have?"
"I'm worried about the cost."
"I understand that the investment is a consideration. Let's break down what's included and the value you're receiving. This program not only addresses your digestive issues but also helps you gain long-term knowledge and tools for maintaining optimal health. Think about the potential savings on doctor visits, medications, and supplements that haven't worked in the past. We also have payment plan options available to make it more manageable."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, identifying the client's challenges, presenting your solution, handling objections, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How can you effectively address the objection "It's too expensive?"

Break down the value of the program, compare it to the cost of ineffective treatments, offer payment plans, and highlight the long-term benefits.

3. Why is it important to practice your pricing presentation?

Practicing your pricing presentation builds confidence, ensures you can clearly articulate the value of your services, and helps you overcome any hesitation you might have about asking for what you're worth.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for converting prospects into clients.
  • Effective objection handling requires empathy, understanding, and a confident response.
  • Your pricing presentation should clearly communicate the value and benefits of your program.
  • Consistent practice is essential for mastering client acquisition and sales skills.
  • Realistic income goals can motivate you to build a thriving practice.
