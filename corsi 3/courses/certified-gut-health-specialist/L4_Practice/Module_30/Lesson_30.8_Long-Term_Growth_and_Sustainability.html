Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Connection & Intro, Problem Exploration, Solution & Framework, and Enrollment & Next Steps.

2. How should you respond to the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the investment in terms of the value they'll receive (e.g., improved health, reduced medical expenses, increased energy). You can also offer payment plans.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is crucial for client acquisition.
  • Prepare a structured script to guide the conversation effectively.
  • Practice handling common objections with confident and empathetic responses.
  • Clearly articulate the value of your program and present your pricing with confidence.
  • Remember, consistent effort and a genuine desire to help your clients will lead to success.
