Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's challenges, presenting your solution, and confidently closing with a clear call to action.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive?"

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value by focusing on the long-term benefits and the cost of *not* solving the problem.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Prepare responses to common objections so you can address concerns effectively.
  • Know your pricing and present it with conviction, highlighting the value you provide.
  • Focus on connecting with your prospect and understanding their needs.
