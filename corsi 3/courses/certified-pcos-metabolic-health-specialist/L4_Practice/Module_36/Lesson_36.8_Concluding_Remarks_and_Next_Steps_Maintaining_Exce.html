Your Discovery Call Script

Here's a structured script to guide your 30-minute discovery call with Susan. Remember to adapt it to your own style and Susan's specific needs.

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Introduction 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call with me. I'm Sarah, and I'm thrilled to connect with you. To start, can you tell me a bit about what prompted you to reach out?"
YOU:
"That's helpful to know. I work with many women facing similar challenges. Before we dive deeper, is there anything specific you'd like to cover during this call?"
Phase 2: Uncovering the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"I understand you're dealing with PCOS and metabolic issues. Can you describe the symptoms that are impacting your life the most right now?"
YOU:
"And what have you already tried to address these symptoms? What were the results?"
YOU:
"How are these challenges affecting your overall quality of life ‚Äì your energy levels, your relationships, your work?"

YOU:

Handling Common Objections

Be prepared to address common objections with confidence and empathy. Here are a few examples:

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand that investing in your health is a significant decision. Let's look at it this way: how much have you already spent on treatments that haven't worked? My program is designed to get to the root cause, potentially saving you money in the long run. Plus, I offer flexible payment plans to make it more accessible."
"I need to think about it."
"That's perfectly fine. What specifically do you need to think about? Is there any information I can provide to help you make a decision? I want to make sure you feel completely comfortable and informed."
"I'm not sure if I have the time commitment."
"I understand that time is precious. My program is designed to be integrated into your existing lifestyle, not to take over it. We'll work together to create a sustainable plan that fits your schedule. Most of my clients spend just a few hours per week on the program, and the results are well worth the investment."
"I've tried everything, and nothing seems to work."
"I hear your frustration, and I know it can be discouraging. But my approach is different. I focus on personalized solutions based on your unique needs and biochemistry. I've seen success with clients who have tried everything else. One client, Sarah, a 48-year-old woman, had been struggling with PCOS for over 10 years. After completing my program, she reported a 70% reduction in her symptoms and significant weight loss."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your solution, and closing the call with a clear call to action.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need for time, ask what specifically they need to think about, and offer to provide additional information.
