Check Your Understanding

1. What are the potential risks of long-term oral contraceptive use in Eleanor's case?

Long-term oral contraceptive use can deplete key nutrients, disrupt the gut microbiome, and potentially exacerbate estrogen dominance. It is crucial to monitor nutrient levels and support detoxification pathways.

2. How would you differentiate between estrogen dominance and MCAS in Eleanor's presentation?

Estrogen dominance often presents with cyclical symptoms related to the menstrual cycle, while MCAS may involve a broader range of triggers (foods, environment) and more varied symptoms (skin reactions, digestive upset). Testing can help differentiate.

3. What key lifestyle factors should be addressed in Eleanor's protocol, and why?

Stress management and sleep hygiene are crucial. High stress can worsen hormonal imbalances, gut health, and neurological symptoms. Prioritizing sleep supports hormone regulation, detoxification, and overall resilience.
