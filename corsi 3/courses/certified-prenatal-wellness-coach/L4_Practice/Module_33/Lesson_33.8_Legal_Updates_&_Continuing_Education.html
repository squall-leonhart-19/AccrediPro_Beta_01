Check Your Understanding

1. What are three key elements to include in a discovery call to convert a prospect into a client?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's needs and pain points, and presenting your program as the solution with a confident close.

2. How should you handle the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then gently probe to understand the specific concern (budget, time, etc.). Offer additional information or a follow-up call to address the concern.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key: Thoroughly understand your ideal client and their needs.
  • Structure your calls: Follow a proven script to guide the conversation and ensure you cover all essential points.
  • Handle objections with confidence: Anticipate common concerns and prepare thoughtful, empathetic responses.
  • Believe in your value: Confidently present your pricing and the transformative benefits of your program.
  • Practice makes perfect: Rehearse your script and closing lines until they feel natural and authentic.
