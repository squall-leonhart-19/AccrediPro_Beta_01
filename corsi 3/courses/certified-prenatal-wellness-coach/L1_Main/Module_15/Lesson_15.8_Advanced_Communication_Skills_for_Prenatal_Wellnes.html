Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your story. It takes courage to acknowledge these challenges. Many women experience similar feelings when trying to conceive again, especially with the demands of a young child and a career. I want you to know that you're not alone, and we can work together to create a supportive plan."
MARIA:
"Thank you. It's just... different this time. I feel more stressed and less hopeful."
YOU:
"I understand. The emotional aspect of trying to conceive is often overlooked, but it's incredibly important. We'll focus on creating a holistic approach that addresses both your physical and emotional well-being. How does that sound to you?"
MARIA:
"That sounds wonderful. What would that involve?"
YOU:
"We'll start by exploring your current stress levels, sleep patterns, and nutritional habits. We can also integrate visualization techniques to help you connect with your body and reduce anxiety. YOU:

You'll Know It Worked When...

  • Maria expresses relief and gratitude for your understanding.

Check Your Understanding

1. How can you create a safe space for clients to share their fertility challenges?

By actively listening, validating their feelings, and offering empathy and support. Avoid judgment or unsolicited advice.

2. What are some key areas to explore when coaching a client struggling with secondary infertility?

Stress levels, sleep patterns, nutritional habits, emotional well-being, and relationship dynamics.
