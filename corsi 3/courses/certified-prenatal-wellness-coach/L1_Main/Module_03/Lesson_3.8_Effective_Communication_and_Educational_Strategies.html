Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate Maria's feelings at the beginning of the coaching session?

Validating her feelings helps build trust and rapport, making her more receptive to your guidance. It also shows that you understand and acknowledge the challenges she's facing.

2. What are some key areas to focus on when educating Maria about hormonal changes?

Focus on explaining the impact of hormonal shifts on cognitive function, mood, and physical symptoms. Provide clear, simple explanations and avoid overwhelming her with technical jargon.

Key Takeaways

  • Validate Feelings: Always begin by acknowledging and validating the client's feelings.
  • Simplify Information: Present complex information in an easy-to-understand manner.
  • Offer Practical Advice: Provide actionable steps that the client can implement immediately.
  • Address Holistic Well-being: Consider both the physical and emotional aspects of the client's experience.
  • Provide Resources: Offer resources and support systems to assist the client in their journey.
