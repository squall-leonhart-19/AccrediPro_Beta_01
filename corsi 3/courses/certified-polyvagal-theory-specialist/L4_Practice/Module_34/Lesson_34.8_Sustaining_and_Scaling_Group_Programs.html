Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call for a group program?

Building rapport, uncovering specific challenges, presenting the group program as a solution, and confidently inviting enrollment.

2. How can you address the objection "I'm not sure if I'm a 'group person'"?

Emphasize the safe and supportive environment, the focus on connection, and the shared experiences of other participants.

Key Takeaways

  • Group programs offer a powerful way to scale your impact and income.
  • Discovery calls for group programs should focus on building connection and highlighting the community aspect.
  • Addressing common objections with confidence is crucial for successful enrollment.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and confidently state your price.
  • Remember, you're not just selling a program; you're offering a transformation and a supportive community.
