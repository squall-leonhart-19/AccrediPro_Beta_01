Designing Individual Coaching Packages

Individual coaching allows for personalized attention and tailored interventions. When structuring your sessions, consider:

  • Session Length: Will you offer 60-minute, 90-minute, or other customized session lengths?
  • Package Duration: Will you offer a single session, a 3-month package, or a longer engagement?
  • Session Focus: Will each session focus on a specific aspect of the S.A.F.E. Framework‚Ñ¢?

Pricing strategies can vary based on your experience, location, and target audience. Here are some potential income examples:

  • Entry-Level: $75-$125 per session
  • Mid-Range: $150-$250 per session
  • Experienced: $250+ per session

Outcome-based goals are crucial for demonstrating the value of your services. For example, clients might aim to:

  • Reduce anxiety symptoms by 30% within 3 months.
  • Increase feelings of safety and connection in relationships.
  • Improve sleep quality and duration.

Creating Group Programs and Workshops

Group programs and workshops leverage the power of co-regulation and shared experiences. Consider offering:

  • Introductory Workshops: A 2-3 hour workshop covering the basics of Polyvagal Theory and the S.A.F.E. Framework‚Ñ¢.
  • Themed Group Programs: A 6-8 week program focused on specific topics like stress management, relationship building, or trauma recovery.
  • Specialized Retreats: A weekend retreat offering immersive experiences and deeper exploration of Polyvagal principles.

A 2021 study in the Journal of Consulting and Clinical Psychology found that group interventions are highly effective for reducing symptoms of anxiety and depression (Richards et al., 2021).
