The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a 4-Tier progressive learning journey, designed to build your expertise step-by-step:

  1. Tier 1: FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build a strong base in the core methodology and fundamental principles of Polyvagal Theory. We'll cover the nervous system, trauma, and basic coaching skills.
  2. Tier 2: ADVANCED (Modules 16-23): We'll delve into more complex cases and explore clinical applications in greater depth. You'll learn how to address nuanced client presentations and refine your coaching techniques.
  3. Tier 3: MASTER (Modules 24-29): This tier focuses on developing your ability to train others and provide supervision. You'll learn how to mentor aspiring practitioners and lead workshops.
  4. Tier 4: PRACTICE (Modules 30-36): The final tier is all about building your practice and attracting clients. We'll cover marketing, business development, and strategies for creating a thriving professional life.

What Makes This Different?

This certification program isn't just about theory; it's about developing practical coaching skills. Here's what sets us apart:

  • Practical Coaching Skills: We emphasize hands-on application, not just memorization of facts.
  • Practice Labs in Every Module: You'll work through real-world scenarios and role-play client interactions.
  • Coaching Focus: You'll learn to coach, not just know information. This means youwill learn how to guide clients through powerful transformations.
  • Transformative Learning: We focus on your transformation as a practitioner. You'll deepen your self-awareness and ability to connect with clients.
  • Rigorous Standards: We maintain high standards for competence and ethical practice. You'll be prepared to provide exceptional service.

The Methodology Framework

The core of our program is built around a proven methodology framework that integrates Polyvagal Theory with practical coaching techniques. This framework includes:

  1. Assessment: Accurately assess a client's nervous system state and identify key areas for intervention.
  2. Intervention: Apply specific techniques to regulate the nervous system and promote resilience.
  3. Integration: Help clients integrate new skills and strategies into their daily lives.
  4. Maintenance: Support clients in maintaining their progress and building long-term well-being.

Time Commitment & Pacing

To successfully complete the certification, plan to dedicate approximately 8-10 hours per week to the course. This includes:

  • Watching video lessons
  • Reading course materials
  • Participating in practice labs
  • Completing assignments
  • Engaging in group discussions

The program is designed to be completed in 6-9 months, but you can adjust your pace based on your individual needs and schedule.

Resources Included

Throughout the certification, you'll have access to a comprehensive suite of resources, including:

  • Video Lessons: Engaging video lectures from expert instructors.
  • Downloadable Workbooks: Practical exercises and tools to reinforce your learning.
  • Practice Labs: Hands-on opportunities to apply your skills.
  • Community Forum: A supportive online community where you can connect with other students and instructors.
  • Live Q&A Sessions: Regular opportunities to ask questions and get personalized feedback.
  • Mentorship Program: Connect with experienced practitioners for guidance and support.

Certification Requirements

To earn your certification, you'll need to meet the following requirements:

  • Complete all modules and assignments.
  • Participate in at least 80% of the practice labs.
  • Pass the final certification exam.
  • Complete a supervised coaching practicum.
  • Adhere to the ethical guidelines of the profession.
