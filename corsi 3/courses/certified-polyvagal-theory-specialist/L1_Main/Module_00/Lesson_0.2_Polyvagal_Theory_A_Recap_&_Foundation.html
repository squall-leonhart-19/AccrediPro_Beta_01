Realistic Timeline to Income

It's important to have realistic expectations about how long it will take to start generating income from your Polyvagal Theory expertise. While some people may see results quickly, it's more common to see a steady increase over time. Here's a general timeline:

  • Months 1-3: Learning & Implementation. Focus on completing the certification, practicing the techniques, and developing your niche.
  • Months 3-6: Building Your Foundation. Create your website, build your social media presence, and start networking with potential clients and partners.
  • Months 6-12: Generating Momentum. Offer introductory sessions, run workshops, and actively market your services.
  • Months 12+: Scaling Your Practice. Refine your offerings, increase your rates, and explore new income streams.

A 2022 survey of new therapists found that it takes an average of 6-12 months to build a sustainable caseload. (Source: Barnett et al., 2022) This timeline can be shorter or longer depending on your individual circumstances, but it's important to be patient and persistent.
