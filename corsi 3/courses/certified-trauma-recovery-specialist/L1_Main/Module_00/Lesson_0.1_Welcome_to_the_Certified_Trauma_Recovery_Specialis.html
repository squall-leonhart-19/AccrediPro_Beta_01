Your Cohort & Community

You're not alone on this journey. One of the most valuable aspects of this program is the incredible community of like-minded individuals you'll connect with.

You'll be assigned to a Private Practice Group (POD), a small cohort of fellow students who will become your support system throughout the program. You'll share experiences, ask questions, offer encouragement, and celebrate each other's successes.

Think of your POD as your personal advisory board, your accountability partners, and your cheerleaders all rolled into one. These are the people who will understand what you're going through, who will challenge you to grow, and who will be there to support you every step of the way.

Plus, you'll have access to our exclusive online community forum, where you can connect with students and alumni from around the world. You can ask questions, share resources, and network with potential collaborators and referral partners.

This isn't just a course; it's a community. And we're all here to support you.

First Steps

Ready to dive in? Here's what to do right now to get the most from this program:

  1. Introduce yourself in the community forum. Tell us a little about yourself, your background, and your goals for this program.
  2. Connect with your POD. Reach out to your fellow group members and introduce yourself. Schedule a virtual coffee chat to get to know each other better.
  3. Set aside dedicated study time each week. Consistency is key to success. Block out time in your calendar and treat it like an important appointment.
  4. Engage actively in the course materials. Don't just passively watch the videos or read the transcripts. Take notes, ask questions, and participate in the discussions.
