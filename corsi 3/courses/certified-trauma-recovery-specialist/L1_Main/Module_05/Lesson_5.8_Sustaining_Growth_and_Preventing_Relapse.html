Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to start this coaching session. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Diane, thank you for being so open about feeling stuck. It takes courage to admit that, especially after dedicating so much of your life to teaching. I want you to know that feeling unfulfilled at this stage is incredibly common, and it's a sign that you're ready for something new."
DIANE:
"It's scary, though. I've been a teacher for so long, I don't even know who I am without it."
YOU:
"That's completely understandable. We're not going to erase your past, we're going to build upon it. Think of your teaching career as a strong foundation. Now, we get to design the house you truly want to live in."
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, I see three key areas we can explore: reconnecting with your core values, identifying untapped passions, and creating a vision for your ideal future. Does that resonate with you?"
DIANE:
"Yes, it does. I've lost touch with what truly matters to me."
YOU:
"Perfect. Let's start with a simple exercise to help you reconnect with your values. It's a powerful way to gain clarity and direction. Sound good?"

Your Turn ‚Äî Practice Out Loud

1

Read the script silently first

Understand the flow and the intention behind each statement. Pay attention to the tone and empathy conveyed.

2

Now speak it out loud

Imagine Diane is sitting across from you. Use a warm, encouraging tone. Maintain a calm and supportive demeanor.

3

Record yourself (optional but powerful)

Use your phone to record, then listen back. Analyze your pacing, tone, and level of confidence. Did you sound empathetic and supportive?

4

Adapt it to your voice

Once you've practiced the script, modify it to sound like YOU. Keep the structure, but use your own words and expressions.

Key Takeaways

  • Validate the client's feelings and experiences.
  • Focus on building upon their existing strengths and foundation.
  • Explore core values, passions, and future vision.
  • Encourage small, manageable steps.
  • Provide ongoing support and guidance.
