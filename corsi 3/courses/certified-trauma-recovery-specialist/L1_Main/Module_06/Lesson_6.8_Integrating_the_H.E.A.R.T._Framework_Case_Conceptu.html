Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate Maria's experience before offering solutions?

Validating her experience builds trust and rapport. It assures her that you understand her feelings and are not dismissing her concerns. This makes her more receptive to your guidance.

2. What are some practical techniques you can introduce to Maria to help regain control in the moment of a panic attack?

Practical techniques include grounding exercises (like the 5-4-3-2-1 method), mindful breathing (such as box breathing), and progressive muscle relaxation. These techniques help to calm the nervous system and bring her back to the present moment.

Key Takeaways

  • Empathy is crucial in trauma recovery coaching.
  • Validating the client's experience builds trust.
  • A structured approach provides a sense of safety and control.
  • Practical techniques offer immediate relief.
  • Setting clear expectations is essential for a successful coaching relationship.
