Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's problem, presenting your approach, and making an offer.

2. How should you handle the objection, "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the investment in terms of the value they'll receive and the long-term benefits.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence.
  • Prepare for common objections and craft compelling responses.
  • State your pricing with conviction and highlight the value you offer.
  • Focus on the HEART methodology to create lasting change for your clients.
  • Client acquisition is a skill that improves with practice and dedication.
