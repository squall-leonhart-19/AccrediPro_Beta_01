Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to build rapport before diving into dosha assessment?

Building rapport creates trust and allows the client to feel safe and understood, which leads to more honest and open communication during the assessment process.

2. What's a good way to explain the concept of "doshas" to a new client?

Explain that doshas are like unique energies or constitutions that influence our physical, mental, and emotional characteristics. Understanding them helps tailor a personalized approach to well-being.

Key Takeaways

  • Building rapport is crucial before assessing doshas.
  • Explain doshas in simple, relatable terms.
  • Frame Ayurveda as a way to understand and balance the client's unique constitution.
  • Practice active listening and empathy.
  • Adapt the coaching script to your own authentic voice.
