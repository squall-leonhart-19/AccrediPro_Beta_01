30-Minute Discovery Call Script

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you so much for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about what you're experiencing. To start, what sparked your interest in Ayurveda and what are you hoping to achieve?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me on Instagram, what post or content resonated with you the most?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned digestive issues and low energy. Can you describe those symptoms in more detail? When did they start, and what makes them better or worse?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these issues? What worked, what didn't, and why do you think that is?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the most debilitating, how would you rate the impact these symptoms have on your daily life?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her key concerns]. Is that an accurate summary?"
YOU:
"My approach to digestive health is rooted in Ayurveda, which focuses on identifying the root cause of imbalances and creating a personalized plan to restore balance. I work with clients over 12 weeks, using diet, lifestyle, and herbal support to address the underlying issues. Many clients experience significant improvements in energy and digestion within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"Does this approach resonate with you?"
Phase 4: Enrollment & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared and the approach I use, I'm confident I can help you achieve your health goals. Would you like to learn more about my 12-week program and how we can get started?"

Handling Common Objections

Practice Responding to Concerns

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand that time is valuable. Our program is designed to be flexible and integrates into your current lifestyle. The weekly sessions are [duration], and the daily practices can be tailored to fit your schedule. What specific concerns do you have about the time commitment?"
"I've tried so many things before, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. Ayurveda is a holistic approach that addresses the root cause of imbalances, rather than just masking symptoms. We'll work together to create a personalized plan that's tailored to your unique needs. What specifically have you tried in the past, and what do you think didn't work for you?"
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific results, I can promise that I will provide you with the tools, knowledge, and support you need to make lasting changes in your health. My clients who fully commit to the program and follow the recommendations consistently experience significant improvements. What specific results are you hoping to achieve?"
"I need to talk to my partner about the investment."
"That's perfectly understandable. Would it be helpful if I sent you a detailed program overview that you can share with your partner? Or, I'm happy to schedule a brief call with both of you to answer any questions."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the initial phase of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves actively listening, showing empathy, finding common ground, and creating a comfortable and trusting environment. Ask about their interests, what led them to you, and acknowledge their challenges.

2. How can you address a client's concern that they've "tried everything before" and nothing has worked?

Acknowledge their frustration and explain that Ayurveda takes a holistic approach, focusing on identifying and addressing the root cause of imbalances. Ask them about what they have tried and why they think it didn't work for them. Emphasize that you will create a personalized plan tailored to their unique needs.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is essential for client acquisition and building a thriving practice.
  • Building rapport, actively listening, and addressing client concerns are crucial for establishing trust and confidence.
  • Confidently presenting your pricing and handling objections are key to successful enrollment.
  • Your income potential as an Ayurveda practitioner is significant and directly tied to your client acquisition efforts.
