Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Intro 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm passionate about helping women thrive through menopause using Ayurveda. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"Great! To start, can you tell me a little bit about what led you to reach out to me?"
Phase 2: Problem Exploration 10 min
YOU:
"I understand. Menopause can be challenging. Can you describe the symptoms you're experiencing in more detail? When did they start, and how are they impacting your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to manage these symptoms? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and alleviate these symptoms, what would your life look like? How would you feel?"
Phase 3: Present Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her key symptoms and frustrations]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is rooted in Ayurveda, which focuses on balancing your doshas to address the root cause of your symptoms. I offer a 12-week personalized program that includes [list key program features like consultations, customized diet and lifestyle plans, herbal recommendations, etc.]. Many of my clients see significant improvements in their symptoms within the first few weeks."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Does this approach resonate with you, Susan? Do you feel like this could be the solution you've been looking for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me share the details of the program, including the investment and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I need to think about it."
"I understand. It's a big decision. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the time commitment, the investment, or something else? Knowing that will help me provide you with the right information."
"It's more expensive than I expected."
"I appreciate that. Let's break down the value you're receiving. The program includes [reiterate key program features and benefits]. Think about the cost of *not* addressing these symptoms ‚Äì the ongoing discomfort, the impact on your quality of life. This is an investment in your well-being."
"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"That's a valid concern. The program requires [estimate time commitment per week]. However, many of my clients find that by prioritizing their health, they actually gain *more* time and energy in the long run. We can also tailor the program to fit your schedule. Would you be open to discussing how we can make the time commitment manageable for you?"
"I've tried so many things before, and nothing has worked."
"I understand your skepticism. Ayurveda is different because it focuses on addressing the root cause of imbalances, rather than just masking symptoms. My program is personalized to your unique needs and dosha. I'm confident that with a consistent commitment to the program, you'll see positive results. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee, so you can feel confident in your investment."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Break down the value, emphasize the long-term benefits, and offer payment options.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence.
  • Prepare for common objections and craft compelling responses.
  • Confidently state your prices and highlight the value you provide.
  • Focus on the client's needs and offer personalized solutions.
  • Remember that every "no" gets you closer to a "yes."
