Discovery Call Script: Your 30-Minute Framework

YOU:
"That's wonderful! And what are you hoping to achieve through our work together?"

"Let's dive deeper into what you're experiencing. Can you describe your hot flashes? When do they occur, and how intense are they on a scale of 1 to 10?"

"And how is your sleep? Are you having trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or both?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to manage these symptoms?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the initial phase of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves expressing gratitude for their time, understanding their motivation for seeking Ayurvedic guidance, and creating a comfortable, trusting environment.

2. How should you address pricing concerns during the call?

Address pricing concerns by emphasizing the value and long-term benefits of your program. Highlight the personalized approach, potential symptom reduction, and the overall investment in their well-being.

3. What is the importance of practicing your closing lines before a discovery call?

Practicing closing lines helps you deliver them confidently and naturally, increasing the likelihood of converting a prospect into a client. It ensures you are prepared to guide the prospect towards a decision smoothly.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call script is crucial for converting prospects into clients.
  • Building rapport and active listening are essential for understanding client needs.
  • Addressing objections with confidence and empathy can turn concerns into opportunities.
  • Clearly articulating the value and benefits of your program justifies the investment.
  • Consistent practice and belief in your services will lead to increased success.
