Optimizing Your Website for Search Engines (SEO)

Having a beautiful website is only half the battle. You need to ensure potential clients can find it! Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is the process of improving your website's visibility in search engine results pages (SERPs). This aligns with the "Acknowledge the Inquiry" stage of the A.C.C.E.S.S. Frameworkâ„¢ - ensuring your website appears when potential clients search for Akashic Records readings.

Key SEO Strategies:

  • Keyword research: Identify the terms people use when searching for Akashic Records readings (e.g., "Akashic Records reading near me," "past life reading online"). Use tools like Google Keyword Planner to find relevant keywords.
  • On-page optimization: Incorporate your target keywords naturally into your website's content, including page titles, headings, meta descriptions, and body text.
  • Link building: Acquire links from other reputable websites in your niche. This signals to search engines that your website is trustworthy and authoritative. Guest blogging and directory listings are effective link-building strategies
  • Mobile optimization: Ensure your website is responsive and looks great on all devices, including smartphones and tablets. Mobile-friendliness is a ranking factor for Google.
  • Page speed optimization: Optimize your website's loading speed by compressing images, minifying code, and leveraging browser caching. Faster websites rank higher in search results.

Expert Tip: Focus on local SEO if you offer in-person readings. Claim your Google My Business listing and optimize it with relevant keywords, photos, and your service area.

Email Marketing: Connecting with Your Audience

Email marketing is a powerful way to nurture leads, promote your services, and build relationships with your audience. This aligns with the "Serve with Grace" and "Sustain" stages of the A.C.C.E.S.S. Frameworkâ„¢ - continuing to serve your audience and sustaining long-term client relationships.

Building Your Email List:

  • Offer a freebie: Create a valuable resource, such as a guided meditation or an ebook, and offer it in exchange for email addresses.
  • Use opt-in forms: Place opt-in forms on your website and social media pages.
  • Run contests and giveaways: Collect email addresses as part of the entry process.

Email Marketing Strategies:

  • Welcome email: Send a welcome email to new subscribers, introducing yourself and your services.
  • Newsletter: Share valuable content, such as blog posts, tips, and insights.
  • Promotional emails: Promote your services and special offers.
  • Personalized emails: Segment your email list and send targeted emails based on subscribers' interests and needs.

Email Marketing Platforms:

  • Mailchimp: A popular email marketing platform with a free plan for up to 2,000 subscribers.
  • ConvertKit: A powerful email marketing platform designed for creators.
  • GetResponse: An all-in-one marketing platform with email marketing, landing pages, and automation features.
