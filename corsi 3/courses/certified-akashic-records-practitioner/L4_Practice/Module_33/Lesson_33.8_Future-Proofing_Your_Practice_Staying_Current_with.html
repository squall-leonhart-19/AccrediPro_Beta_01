Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key legal considerations when working with clients in the Akashic Records?

Confidentiality, informed consent, scope of practice, data privacy regulations, and professional liability insurance.

2. How do you address a client's concerns about the legitimacy of your services?

Provide credentials, testimonials, adhere to a code of ethics, and offer a clear service agreement.

Key Takeaways

  • Address client concerns about confidentiality upfront.
  • Be transparent about your credentials and ethical practices.
  • Provide a clear service agreement outlining the terms of service.
  • Carry professional liability insurance to protect yourself and your clients.
  • Remember, building trust through compliance builds a strong practice.
