Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to chat with you. To start, what sparked your interest in exploring the Akashic Records?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful. So, Susan, tell me a bit about what's going on in your life right now that led you to reach out?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling a bit lost in retirement. Can you elaborate on what that feels like for you day-to-day?"
YOU:
"What have you considered doing to find that sense of purpose again?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and have clarity on your next steps, what would your ideal life look like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're seeking clarity and direction as you navigate this new chapter of retirement. You want to find something meaningful that excites you again. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My Akashic Records readings can help you tap into your soul's purpose and uncover hidden talents and passions. We'll explore your past lives, identify limiting beliefs, and gain insights into your unique gifts. Many of my clients, especially women in their 40s and 50s, find this process incredibly empowering and clarifying."
YOU:
"I offer a Soul Purpose Discovery Package, which includes three in-depth readings over six weeks, personalized action steps, and email support between sessions."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this Soul Purpose Discovery Package sound like it resonates with what you're looking for? ... Wonderful! Let me share the investment details and how we can get started."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: 1) Build Rapport, 2) Understand the Problem, 3) Present Your Approach, and 4) Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, and then ask clarifying questions such as, "What aspects are you still considering?" This helps you address their specific concerns.

Key Takeaways

  • Building rapport is crucial for creating a connection with potential clients.
  • Understanding the client's problem deeply allows you to tailor your approach effectively.
  • Presenting your pricing with confidence is essential for conveying the value of your services.
  • Practice objection handling to address concerns and build trust.
  • Consistent action, including conducting discovery calls, leads to a sustainable and thriving practice.
