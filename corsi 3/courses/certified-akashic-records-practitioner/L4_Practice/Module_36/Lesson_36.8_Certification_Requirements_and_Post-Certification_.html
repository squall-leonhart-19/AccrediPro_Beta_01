Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about what you're hoping to gain from an Akashic Records reading. I saw you found me on Facebook; what specifically caught your eye?"
YOU:
"That's interesting! So, tell me a little more about what's going on in your life right now that led you to reach out."
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling stuck in your career. Can you elaborate on that? What specifically feels unfulfilling?"
YOU:
"And what have you already tried to address this feeling? Have you considered career counseling or other self-help methods?"
YOU:
"If you continue down this path, where do you see yourself in a year? And on the flip side, if you COULD magically find clarity and purpose, what would that look and feel like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Okay, Susan, let me summarize what I'm hearing: You're feeling unfulfilled in your career as an empty-nester and seeking clarity on your next steps. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"An Akashic Records reading can provide a unique perspective. We can access information about your past lives, soul's purpose, and potential future paths. This isn't about fortune-telling, but about gaining insights to make empowered choices. Many of my clients experience a profound sense of clarity and direction after a session."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, does this sound like the type of guidance you're looking for? ... Wonderful! Let me explain how my readings work and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure I believe in all this 'spiritual' stuff."
"I understand your skepticism. Think of the Akashic Records as a vast database of information about your soul's journey. You don't have to 'believe' in it for it to provide valuable insights. Many of my clients are initially skeptical but find the experience surprisingly helpful."
"How is this different from a psychic reading?"
"That's a great question. While some psychics make predictions, Akashic Records readings are more about understanding your soul's journey and empowering you to make conscious choices. The focus is on insight and personal growth, not predicting the future."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What aspects do you need to consider further? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need."
"It's more expensive than I expected."
"I understand. Let's talk about the value you'll receive. This isn't just a one-time reading; it's an investment in gaining clarity and direction for your future. What would it be worth to you to finally feel aligned with your purpose and create a fulfilling second chapter?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's more expensive than I expected"?

Focus on the value the client will receive and what it's worth to them to solve their problem. Discuss payment options or packages.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key: Know your prospect and anticipate their concerns.
  • Focus on the client's needs: Understand their pain points and desires.
  • Confidently present your value: Highlight the benefits of your services.
  • Practice your closing lines: Be prepared to ask for the sale.
  • Believe in yourself: Your confidence will inspire trust in your clients.
