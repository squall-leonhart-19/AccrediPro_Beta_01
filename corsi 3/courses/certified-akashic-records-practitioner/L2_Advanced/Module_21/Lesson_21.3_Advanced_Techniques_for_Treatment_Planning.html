Time-Based Treatment Planning

Structuring sessions over a specific timeframe with clear objectives for each stage can enhance the effectiveness of Akashic Records readings. This approach allows for a more structured and focused exploration of the client's issues. Consider a 3-month or 6-month treatment plan, with specific goals and objectives for each session.

A sample timeline might look like this:

Session Objective Techniques
Session 1 Acknowledge the Inquiry: Identify core issues and set intentions. Initial consultation, question formulation, connecting to the Akashic Records.
Session 2 Connect to the Records: Explore past lives or ancestral patterns. Past life regression, family genogram, Akashic Records reading.
Session 3 Clarify the Information: Identify energetic imbalances. Energy scanning, chakra assessment, muscle testing.
Session 4 Empower through Understanding: Develop action steps. Integration techniques, goal setting, affirmation creation.
Session 5 Support Integration: Implement energy clearing techniques. Reiki, sound healing, guided meditation.
Session 6 Seal the Connection: Review progress and plan for future. Final integration session, review of action steps, sealing prayer.
