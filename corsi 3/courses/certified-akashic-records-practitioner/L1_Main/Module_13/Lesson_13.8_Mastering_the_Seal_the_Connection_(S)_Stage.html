Your Coaching Script

Here's a sample script to guide your opening conversation with Maria. Remember to adapt it to your own style and intuition. Focus on creating a safe and supportive space for her to explore her Akashic Records.

YOU:
"Maria, welcome. Thank you for choosing to explore your Akashic Records with me. I understand you're feeling a bit stuck and searching for more meaning in your life. Is that right?"
MARIA:
"Yes, exactly. I feel like I'm at a crossroads, and I don't know which way to go."
YOU:
"I understand. Many people reach this point in their lives. The Akashic Records can offer clarity and guidance by revealing your soul's unique journey and purpose. Are you familiar with the Akashic Records?"
MARIA:
"I've read a little bit about them, but I'm not really sure what to expect."
YOU:
"That's perfectly fine. Think of the Akashic Records as a vast library containing information about every soul's past, present, and potential future. We'll be accessing this library to gain insights into your soul's purpose and identify any blocks that might be holding you back."
YOU:
"Before we begin, is there anything specific you'd like to focus on during this reading? Any questions you'd like to explore?"
MARIA:
"I guess I'd like to know what my true purpose is. What am I really meant to be doing with my life?"
YOU:
"That's a beautiful question, Maria. Let's open your Records and see what guidance is available for you."

Once you've practiced the script, modify it to sound like YOU. Keep the structure, but use your own words and expressions. Authenticity is key!

‚ùå Making assumptions about the client's beliefs
