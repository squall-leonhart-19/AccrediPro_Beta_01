Adapting the Framework

While the A.C.C.E.S.S. Framework‚Ñ¢ provides a structured approach, it's essential to adapt it to suit individual client needs and reading styles. Consider the following:

  • Client's Communication Style: Some clients prefer direct and concise information, while others benefit from a more nurturing and supportive approach.
  • Client's Belief System: Tailor the language and concepts to align with the client's spiritual or philosophical beliefs.
  • Reading Modalities: Integrate other modalities, such as tarot or oracle cards, to enhance the reading experience.
  • Time Constraints: Adjust the depth and scope of the reading to fit within the allotted time.

Remember, the A.C.C.E.S.S. Framework‚Ñ¢ is a guide, not a rigid formula. Trust your intuition and adapt the framework to create a personalized and transformative experience for each client.
