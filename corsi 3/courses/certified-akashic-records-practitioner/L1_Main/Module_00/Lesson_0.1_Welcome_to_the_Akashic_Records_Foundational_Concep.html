Your Cohort & Community

One of the most valuable aspects of this program is the community you'll become a part of. You'll be surrounded by fellow students who are just as passionate about the Akashic Records as you are. This is a safe space to ask questions, share your experiences, and support one another.

We'll have regular group calls, online forums, and opportunities to connect with your classmates. You'll find lifelong friends, accountability partners, and a network of support that will help you thrive long after the program ends.

First Steps

Okay, you're officially in! Here are your first steps to ensure you get the most out of this program:

  1. Complete Your Profile: Make sure your profile is up-to-date with a photo and a brief bio. This will help you connect with other students.
  2. Join the Community Forum: Introduce yourself and start engaging with your classmates.
  3. Review the Course Materials: Familiarize yourself with the course structure, the schedule, and the resources available to you.
  4. Set Your Intention: What do you want to achieve through this program? What kind of impact do you want to make on the world? Write it down and keep it visible as a reminder of your goals.

Remember, this is your journey. Take your time, be patient with yourself, and enjoy the process. I'm here to support you every step of the way. Welcome to the family!
