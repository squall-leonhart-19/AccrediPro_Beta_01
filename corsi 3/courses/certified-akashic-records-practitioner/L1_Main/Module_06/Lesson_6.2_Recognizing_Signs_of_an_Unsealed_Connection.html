Identifying Physical Symptoms

One of the first indicators of an unsealed connection can manifest physically. After conducting an Akashic Records reading, pay close attention to any unusual physical sensations. These might include:

  • Headaches: Particularly persistent or unexplained headaches.
  • Fatigue: Feeling unusually drained or exhausted, even after adequate rest.
  • Unusual Sensations: Tingling, pressure, or discomfort in specific areas of your body.

These symptoms can arise from the lingering energetic link with the client and the information accessed during the reading. Sealing the connection is essential to prevent these energetic residues from affecting your physical state.

Emotional Indicators

Beyond the physical realm, emotional indicators can also signal an unsealed connection. Be mindful of the following emotional states:

  • Feeling Overwhelmed: Experiencing a sense of being emotionally flooded or burdened.
  • Anxiety: Increased anxiety levels without an obvious trigger.
  • Carrying Client's Emotional Burden: Feeling as though you've absorbed the client's emotional issues and are carrying them yourself.

Itâ€™s crucial to differentiate between empathy and absorption. While empathy is a valuable trait for an Akashic Records practitioner, absorbing a client's emotions can be detrimental. The A.C.C.E.S.S. Frameworkâ„¢ emphasizes energetic boundaries to avoid this.

Mental Signs

An unsealed connection can also impact your mental state. Watch out for:

  • Difficulty Concentrating: Trouble focusing on tasks or maintaining attention.
  • Intrusive Thoughts: Unwanted thoughts or mental images related to the client or the reading.
  • Confusion: Feeling mentally foggy or disoriented.

These mental signs suggest that your mind is still actively processing the information from the Akashic Records, even after the session has concluded. Sealing the connection helps to clear your mental space and restore clarity.

Dream Patterns

Our dreams often reflect our subconscious mind. Notice if you experience:

  • Dreams Related to the Client: Dreams featuring the client or their life circumstances.
  • Dreams Related to the Reading: Dreams that replay or expand upon the information received during the session.

These dream patterns may indicate that the energetic connection remains open, and your subconscious is still processing the information. Itâ€™s a clear sign that additional sealing techniques may be required.

Intuitive Signals

Trust your intuition. You might experience:

  • Persistent Thoughts About the Client: A nagging feeling or recurring thoughts about the client, even when you're not actively thinking about the session.
  • Sense of Unease: A general feeling of discomfort or unease that you can't quite explain.
  • Feeling Pulled Back to the Reading: An urge to revisit the information from the Akashic Records, even when it's not necessary.

These intuitive signals are your inner guidance alerting you to the fact that the connection hasn't been fully severed. Pay attention to these subtle cues and take appropriate action.
