Distinguishing Between Intuition and Information

One of the most crucial skills for an Akashic Records Practitioner is the ability to differentiate between your personal intuition and the direct information coming from the Records. Intuition is your own inner guidance, colored by your experiences and beliefs. Akashic information is objective data from the Records themselves. Blurring these lines can lead to inaccurate readings.

Ask yourself: Does this information feel like a thought originating within me, or does it feel like a download from an external source? Does it align with the client's questions and energy, or is it something I'm projecting onto them? Trusting the Records involves setting aside your own agenda and allowing the information to flow freely, a core component of the Clarify step in the A.C.C.E.S.S. Frameworkâ„¢.

Utilizing Pendulums and Dowsing Rods

Divination tools, such as pendulums and dowsing rods, can be valuable aids in clarifying information received from the Akashic Records. These tools work by amplifying subtle energetic responses, providing a physical manifestation of yes/no answers or directional guidance.

Similarly, dowsing rods can be used to locate energy blockages or identify areas of focus within a client's energy field.
