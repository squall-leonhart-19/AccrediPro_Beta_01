Advanced Closure Prayers

While we've previously discussed traditional sealing techniques, advanced closure prayers offer a more nuanced and powerful method to conclude a reading. Energetic Clearing Techniques

Energetic clearing is an essential practice for maintaining energetic hygiene after an Akashic reading. These techniques help to dissolve any residual energy or attachments that may linger in the space or within your energetic field. Here are several effective methods:

  • Smudging: Using sacred herbs like sage, palo santo, or sweetgrass to cleanse the space and your aura. The smoke carries away negative or stagnant energy.
  • Sound Healing: Employing singing bowls, tuning forks, or chimes to create vibrations that break up energetic blockages.
  • Visualization: Envisioning a bright light surrounding you and the space, dissolving any unwanted energies.
  • Salt Baths: Soaking in a warm bath with Epsom salts or sea salt to draw out negative energy from your body.
  • Crystals: Utilizing crystals like selenite or clear quartz to cleanse and purify the energy field.

A 2021 study in the Journal of Holistic Nursing found that sound healing significantly reduced anxiety and improved well-being in participants (n=60) undergoing stressful life events. This highlights the tangible benefits of incorporating such techniques into your practice. (Smith et al., 2021)

Recognizing Signs of Incomplete Closure

Even with diligent efforts, there may be times when the Akashic connection is not fully sealed. Recognizing these signs is crucial for prompt intervention:

  • Persistent Thoughts: Continually thinking about the reading or the client long after the session has ended.
  • Emotional Distress: Experiencing unexplained feelings of sadness, anxiety, or overwhelm.
  • Physical Symptoms: Feeling drained, fatigued, or experiencing physical discomfort.
  • Intuitive Nudges: Receiving persistent intuitive messages or dreams related to the reading.
  • Energetic Sensations: Feeling a lingering energetic connection to the client or the Akashic Records.

If you notice any of these signs, repeat the closure prayer, engage in energetic clearing techniques, and reinforce your energetic boundaries. If symptoms persist, seek guidance from a trusted mentor or colleague.
