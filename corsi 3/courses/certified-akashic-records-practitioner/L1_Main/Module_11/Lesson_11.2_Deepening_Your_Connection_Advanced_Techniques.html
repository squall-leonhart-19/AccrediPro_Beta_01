Energetic Hygiene

Maintaining a clean and clear energetic field is crucial for accurate and unbiased readings. Here are some effective energetic hygiene practices:

  • Smudging: Use sage, palo santo, or other cleansing herbs to clear your personal energy field and your workspace.
  • Salt Baths: Epsom salt or sea salt baths can help to draw out negative energy and toxins.
  • Visualization: Visualize a golden light surrounding you, dissolving any negativity or unwanted energy.

Make these practices a regular part of your routine, especially before and after Akashic readings. A clean energetic field will allow you to access the Records with greater clarity and accuracy.
