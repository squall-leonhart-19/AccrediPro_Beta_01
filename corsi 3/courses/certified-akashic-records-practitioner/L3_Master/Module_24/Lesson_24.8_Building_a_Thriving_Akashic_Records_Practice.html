Emily's Pricing Question

What Emily Asks:

"I want to make Akashic Records readings accessible, but I also need to value my time and energy. I've seen prices ranging from $75 to $300 per session. How do I find the sweet spot?"

Your Teaching Approach

Key Points to Cover with Emily

1

Deconstruct the Money Mindset

Help Emily identify and challenge any limiting beliefs about money and worthiness. Many new practitioners struggle with undervaluing their services.

2

Research Market Rates

Encourage Emily to research the typical rates for Akashic Records readings in her geographic area or online. This provides a baseline for her pricing.

3

Consider Value-Based Pricing

Help Emily understand the value she brings to her clients. What transformation or insights do her readings provide? Price accordingly.

4

Structure Pricing Options

Suggest offering different packages or session lengths at varying price points. This allows clients to choose what best fits their needs and budget.

Your Feedback Dialogue

How to Guide Emily to Find Her Pricing

Start with Empathy
"Emily, I completely understand your concern. Pricing can be tricky, especially when you're starting out. It's a balance between honoring your value and making your services accessible."
Explore Her Beliefs
"What does money mean to you? What are your beliefs about charging for spiritual services? Sometimes, our beliefs can unconsciously influence our pricing decisions."
Offer Practical Advice
"One approach is to calculate your expenses (marketing, software, etc.) and then determine an hourly rate that covers those costs and provides a profit. Another is to research what other practitioners in your area are charging."
Empower Her Decision
"Ultimately, the best price is one that feels aligned with your values and attracts the clients you want to work with. Experiment and see what resonates!"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the most important aspect of mentoring a new practitioner?

Creating a safe and supportive environment where they feel comfortable asking questions and exploring their skills.

2. How can you help a mentee overcome limiting beliefs about pricing?

By exploring their beliefs about money and worthiness, and encouraging them to value the transformation they provide to clients.
