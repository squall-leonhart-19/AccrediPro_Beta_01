Case Study: Clearing a New Home

Case Study: The Anxious Abode

Client: Sarah Miller, 35 years old.

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah recently moved into a new home but experienced persistent feelings of anxiety, sleep disturbances, and a general sense of unease. She suspected the house held negative energies.

Intervention: Using the Akashic Records, the practitioner discovered the house had been the site of a bitter divorce and subsequent financial hardship for the previous owners. The emotional residue of anger, resentment, and fear was palpable. The practitioner used a combination of smudging with sage, sound healing with Tibetan singing bowls, and a crystal grid to clear the negative energies. They then led Sarah in a guided meditation to infuse the space with intentions of peace, love, and abundance.

Outcomes: Within a week, Sarah reported a significant reduction in her anxiety levels. She began sleeping soundly and felt a sense of peace and comfort in her new home. The practitioner recommended ongoing maintenance with regular smudging and affirmations to sustain the positive energy.

Case Study: Revitalizing the Workplace

Client: "Tech Solutions Inc.", a company with 50 employees.

Presenting Symptoms: The company was experiencing a decline in productivity, increased employee absenteeism, and a general lack of enthusiasm. Management suspected the office space was contributing to the problem.

Intervention: An Akashic Records reading revealed the building was built on a site that had previously been a hospital during a major epidemic. The energetic imprint of suffering and loss was impacting the employees. The practitioner worked with the management team to implement a comprehensive space clearing protocol. This included deep cleaning, decluttering, incorporating plants, and using essential oil diffusers with uplifting scents. The practitioner also led a group meditation to clear the collective energy of the workplace and infuse it with intentions of creativity, collaboration, and success.

Outcomes: Within a month, the company saw a noticeable improvement in employee morale and productivity. Absenteeism decreased, and employees reported feeling more energized and engaged. The company implemented a regular maintenance program to sustain the positive energy, including weekly smudging and monthly group meditations.
