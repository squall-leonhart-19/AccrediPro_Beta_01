Discovered you through a local wellness fair. Booked a free discovery call.

Her Situation
Stressed from work, trouble sleeping, feeling disconnected. Empty-nester syndrome.
Budget Concern
"I'm willing to invest in my health, but I need to see the value."
Decision Style
Analytical, needs social proof (testimonials). Values personalized attention.

"I want to feel calm, centered, and find joy in this next chapter of my life."

Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to learn more about you. How was the wellness fair for you?"
YOU:
"Great! So, tell me, what specifically made you want to book this call today? What are you hoping to achieve?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned stress and trouble sleeping. Can you elaborate on that? How long has this been going on, and how does it impact your day-to-day?"
YOU:
"And how does the empty-nester syndrome play into this? What are you missing or longing for?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these issues? What worked, what didn't, and why do you think that is?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're dealing with a combination of stress, sleep issues, and a sense of loss as you navigate this new phase of life. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach to sound healing goes beyond just relaxation. We use specific frequencies and vibrations to address the root causes of your stress, rebalance your nervous system for better sleep, and help you reconnect with your inner joy and purpose. It's a personalized journey to restore your well-being. Many clients report feeling significantly calmer and more grounded within just a few sessions."
YOU:
"I have a 12-week program designed to help you achieve those goals. It includes [list 2-3 key inclusions like weekly sessions, personalized soundscapes, support calls]."
Phase 4: The Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like it could be the right fit for what you're looking for? ... Excellent! Let me tell you about the program investment and how we can get you started on this path to feeling your best."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Life gets busy. The program requires [X hours] per week, including the session and listening to your personalized soundscapes. But think about it this way: how much time are you currently losing to stress and sleeplessness? This is an investment in reclaiming that time and energy."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can tell you that most of my clients experience significant improvements in stress levels and sleep quality within the first month. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee: if you're not completely happy after the first [X sessions], we can discuss a refund. I'm committed to your success."
"It seems a bit pricey."
"I hear you. However, let's break down the value. This isn't just about sound healing sessions. It's about creating a lasting transformation in your well-being, reducing stress, improving sleep, and finding joy again. It's an investment in yourself, and many of my clients find that the value far outweighs the cost. What number were you hoping to invest?"
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can provide some additional information or address any remaining concerns to help you make a confident decision."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then break down the value of your program and emphasize the long-term benefits. Ask what number they were hoping to invest.

3. What are some realistic income scenarios for a sound healing practitioner?

With 2 clients per month, you could earn around $5,000. 5 clients per month could generate $12,500, and 10 clients per month could lead to $25,000 in income.

Key Takeaways
