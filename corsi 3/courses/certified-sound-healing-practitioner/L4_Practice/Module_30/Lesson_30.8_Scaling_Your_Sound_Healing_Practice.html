Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Intro 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call! I'm Sarah, and I'm passionate about helping people like you find inner peace through sound. How's your day going so far?"
YOU:
"Great! I'm so glad you found me on Instagram. What specifically caught your eye and made you book this call?"
Phase 2: Uncover Needs 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about what's been going on with your sleep and stress levels. When did you start noticing these issues?"
YOU:
"What have you tried already to address these problems? What has worked, and what hasn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and feel completely different, what would that look like for you?"
Phase 3: Present Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're dealing with significant stress and sleep issues that are impacting your overall well-being. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My sound healing approach is different because we address the root cause of your stress and disconnection. Over 8 weeks, we'll use personalized sound frequencies to restore balance and promote deep relaxation. Many clients report improved sleep and reduced stress within the first few sessions."
YOU:
"I also provide access to guided meditations and personalized soundscapes that you can use at home between sessions to deepen your healing experience."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, do you think this approach could be a good fit for you? ... Wonderful! Let me explain the program details and how we can get you started."

Handling Common Objections

Mastering Objection Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The sessions are designed to be deeply restorative, so even a single session can provide significant benefits. Plus, the at-home resources can be used whenever you have a few free minutes. How about we explore how to fit this into your schedule?"
"I've never tried sound healing before. I'm skeptical."
"That's perfectly normal! Sound healing is relatively new to many people. I'm happy to share some client testimonials and research articles that demonstrate its effectiveness. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you don't feel a positive shift after the first few sessions, I'll offer a full refund."
"It seems a bit pricey."
"I understand your concern about the investment. Think about how much you're currently spending on things that *aren't* truly solving the problem ‚Äì perhaps sleep aids, stress relief products, or even lost productivity at work. This program is designed to provide lasting results, not just temporary fixes. What value would you place on consistent, restful sleep and reduced stress levels?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of an effective discovery call?

The four phases are: Rapport & Intro, Uncover Needs, Present Solution, and Close & Next Steps.

2. How should you respond to a client who says your program is "too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value by highlighting the lasting benefits and comparing the investment to other less effective solutions.
