Your Discovery Call: A Step-by-Step Guide

30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, I'm Sarah! Thank you for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about what brought you here. To start, what resonated with you from my Instagram post?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! I love helping women find natural ways to manage stress. Tell me, what's been happening in your life that made you seek this kind of support now?"
Phase 2: Dive Deep into Her Needs 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling overwhelmed. Can you describe a typical day and where you feel the most stress?"
YOU:
"And how is this affecting your sleep? What have you tried so far to improve it?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand, what would your life look like without this stress and sleep deprivation?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Sound Healing Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, you're looking for a natural way to manage stress and improve your sleep, without relying on medication. Is that right?"
YOU:
"My sound healing approach is designed to do just that. We'll use specific frequencies and vibrations to calm your nervous system, release tension, and promote deep relaxation. We'll work together over 8 weeks to create a personalized plan that fits your lifestyle. Most clients experience noticeable improvements in their sleep and stress levels within the first few sessions."
Phase 4: Confident Enrollment 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for? ... Excellent! Let me share the details of the program and how we can get you started."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Warm Welcome & Connection, Dive Deep into Her Needs, Presenting Your Sound Healing Approach, and Confident Enrollment.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I'm not sure if I have the time?"

Acknowledge their concern, highlight the flexibility of your program, and emphasize that the benefits (reduced stress, better sleep) will ultimately create more time and energy.

Key Takeaways

  • Structure your discovery calls into four key phases for optimal flow.
  • Prepare confident responses to common objections to address client concerns effectively.
  • Practice stating your pricing with assurance to convey the value of your services.
  • Focus on building genuine connections with potential clients to establish trust and rapport.
  • Remember that consistent practice and dedication are essential for building a successful practice.
