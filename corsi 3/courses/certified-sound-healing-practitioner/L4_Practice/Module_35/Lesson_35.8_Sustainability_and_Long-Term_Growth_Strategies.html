Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm thrilled to connect with you. I saw you attended my Facebook workshop. What resonated with you most?"
YOU:
"Great! Today, let's discuss your sleep and stress challenges, explore how sound healing can help, and see if my program is a good fit. Sound good?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about your sleep issues. How long has this been going on, and how does it impact your work and personal life?"
YOU:
"And what about stress? What are your biggest stressors right now, and how do you typically cope with them?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being 'completely overwhelmed,' where would you rate your current stress level?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, what I'm hearing is that you're struggling with chronic sleep deprivation and high stress levels, impacting your overall well-being. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My 8-week Sound Transformation Program is designed to address these exact challenges. We use targeted sound frequencies to calm your nervous system, promote deep relaxation, and improve sleep quality. Many of my clients report feeling significantly calmer and more rested within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"For example, Client Jane, a 48-year-old executive, had similar issues. After completing the program, she reported a 70% reduction in stress and consistently slept through the night. I can share her testimonial with you."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, do you see how this program could help you achieve your goals of feeling calmer and more in control?"
YOU:
"Wonderful! Let's talk about the program details and how we can get you started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. The program requires about 2 hours per week. However, think about the time you're currently losing due to stress and lack of sleep. What if this investment of time actually *gave* you more time by increasing your focus and productivity?"
"I've tried other things that didn't work."
"I hear you. Many of my clients have felt the same way. What makes sound healing different is that it works directly with your nervous system to promote relaxation at a cellular level. It's not just a temporary fix; it's a foundational shift. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee."
"It sounds interesting, but I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically are you unsure about? Is it the investment, the process, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision."
"How is this different from just listening to relaxing music?"
"That's a great question! While relaxing music can be helpful, sound healing uses specific frequencies and vibrations that are scientifically proven to impact brainwave activity and promote deep relaxation. It's a targeted therapeutic approach."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are three key elements to include in your discovery call script?

Building rapport, identifying the client's challenges, and presenting your solution.

2. How should you handle the objection, "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then focus on the value and ROI of your program. Ask what the value of solving their problem would mean to them.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call process increases your chances of converting prospects into clients.
  • Anticipating and handling objections confidently builds trust and demonstrates your expertise.
  • Clearly articulating the value of your program and its potential ROI is crucial for justifying the investment.
  • Consistent practice and refinement of your sales skills will lead to increased client acquisition and revenue.
