You can use both weighted and unweighted tuning forks together to create a more layered and nuanced sound experience.

Precautions and Contraindications

While tuning fork therapy is generally safe and well-tolerated, there are certain precautions and contraindications to be aware of:

  • Pregnancy: Avoid using tuning forks on the abdomen or lower back during pregnancy.
  • Epilepsy: Use caution when using tuning forks with clients who have epilepsy, as the vibrations may trigger seizures in rare cases.
  • Pacemakers: Avoid placing tuning forks directly over pacemakers or other implanted medical devices.
  • Acute Infections: Avoid using tuning forks on areas of acute infection or inflammation.
  • Severe Mental Health Conditions: Use caution when using tuning forks with clients who have severe mental health conditions, such as psychosis or severe anxiety, as the vibrations may exacerbate their symptoms.

It is always best to consult with a qualified healthcare professional before using tuning fork therapy, especially if you have any underlying health conditions.
