Precautions and Ethical Considerations

While tuning fork therapy is generally safe, it's essential to be aware of potential precautions and ethical considerations.

  • Pregnancy: Avoid applying tuning forks directly to the abdomen of pregnant women.
  • Epilepsy: Use caution with clients who have epilepsy, as certain frequencies may trigger seizures.
  • Metal Implants: Be mindful when applying weighted tuning forks near metal implants, as the vibrations may cause discomfort.
  • Client Consent: Always obtain informed consent from your clients before beginning a session, and respect their boundaries at all times.
  • Scope of Practice: Clearly define your scope of practice and refer clients to other healthcare professionals when necessary.
