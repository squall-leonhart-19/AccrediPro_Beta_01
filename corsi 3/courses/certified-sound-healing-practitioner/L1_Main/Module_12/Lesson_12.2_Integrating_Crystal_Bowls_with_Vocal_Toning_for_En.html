Practical Exercise: Stress Reduction & Emotional Release

Let's put these concepts into practice with a guided exercise designed to reduce stress and promote emotional release. This exercise combines crystal bowls and vocal toning to create a powerful healing experience. Remember to create a comfortable and safe environment for yourself or your clients before beginning.

  1. Preparation: Find a quiet space where you can relax undisturbed. Sit comfortably or lie down, close your eyes, and take a few deep breaths.
  2. Grounding: Begin by playing a crystal bowl tuned to the root chakra (e.g., a bowl around 396 Hz or in the key of C). As you play the bowl, tone the vowel sound "OO." Visualize a connection to the earth, feeling grounded and stable.
  3. Heart Opening: Next, transition to a bowl tuned to the heart chakra (e.g., a bowl around 639 Hz or in the key of F). As you play this bowl, tone the vowel sound "AH." Focus on feelings of love, compassion, and self-acceptance.

  5. Integration: Finally, return to the root chakra bowl and tone the "OO" sound. Allow the vibrations to integrate and ground you back into your body.
  6. Reflection: Take a few moments to reflect on your experience. Notice any shifts in your energy or emotional state.

This exercise can be adapted to suit individual needs. Encourage your clients to experiment with different vowel sounds and crystal bowl combinations to find what resonates best for them. The S.O.N.A.R. framework will help you to adapt this exercise to suit each client.
