Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your experience. It sounds like you're feeling a bit stuck right now. Before we jump to solutions, let's explore what 'stuck' means to you. What does it feel like in your body when you think about launching your practice?"
MARIA:
"Honestly, it's like a weight in my chest. My stomach gets tight, and I just want to avoid it. It feels so overwhelming."
YOU:
"That's a very real physical response, Maria. It sounds like there's some resistance there. Let's gently explore what might be causing that resistance. Would you be open to that?"
MARIA:
"Yes, I think so. I just don't want to feel this way anymore."
YOU:
"Great. Let's start with a simple question: What's the worst thing that could happen if you launched your practice tomorrow?"
YOU:
"Sometimes, bringing those fears into the light can help us understand what's really holding us back. Take your time, and just let whatever comes to mind surface."

Your Turn ‚Äî Practice Out Loud

4

Modify the script to sound like YOU. Keep the structure, but use your own words and phrasing.
