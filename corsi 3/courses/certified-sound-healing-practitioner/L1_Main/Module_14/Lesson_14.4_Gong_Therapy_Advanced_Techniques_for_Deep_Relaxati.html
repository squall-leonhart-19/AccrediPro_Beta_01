Addressing Client Sensitivities and Contraindications

Safety is paramount. It's crucial to assess client sensitivities and contraindications before introducing gong therapy. This aligns with the 'S' - Sound Assessment - component of the S.O.N.A.R.Frameworkâ„¢. Some individuals may be more sensitive to sound vibrations than others.

  • Pregnancy: Avoid strong vibrations near the abdomen during pregnancy.
  • Epilepsy: Sound-induced altered states may trigger seizures in individuals with epilepsy.
  • Tinnitus or Hyperacusis: Exercise caution with individuals who have sound sensitivities.
