Advanced Tuning Fork Techniques

  • Binaural Beat Induction: Using two tuning forks with slightly different frequencies to create a binaural beat effect, which can entrain brainwave activity and promote relaxation or focus.
  • Point-Specific Application: Applying the tuning fork directly to specific acupuncture points or trigger points to release blockages and stimulate energy flow.

Experiment with these techniques to discover what resonates best with you and your clients. Remember, your intuition is a valuable guide in this process.

Precautions & Contraindications

While tuning fork assessments are generally safe, it's crucial to be aware of certain precautions and contraindications. This aligns with the 'A' (Activate Healing) aspect of the S.O.N.A.R. Frameworkâ„¢, ensuring you activate healing in a safe and responsible manner.

  • Pregnancy: Avoid direct application of tuning forks to the abdomen during pregnancy.
  • Metal Implants: Use caution when applying tuning forks near metal implants, as the vibrations may cause discomfort.
  • Acute Inflammation: Avoid applying tuning forks directly to areas of acute inflammation, as it may exacerbate the condition.
  • Epilepsy: Binaural beat induction may not be suitable for individuals with epilepsy.

Always prioritize your client's safety and comfort. If you're unsure whether a particular technique is appropriate, err on the side of caution.
