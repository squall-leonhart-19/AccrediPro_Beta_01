Cymatics and Vibrational Imaging

Cymatics is the study of visualizing sound and vibration. By placing particles (such as sand or water) on a vibrating surface, patterns emerge that correspond to the frequency of the sound. This visual representation can be incredibly insightful, helping you and your clients understand the tangible effects of sound. Vibrational imaging takes this concept further, using advanced technology to create detailed images of vibrational patterns in the body.

While not directly a healing technique, cymatics can be used as an educational tool to demonstrate how different frequencies create different patterns, fostering a deeper understanding of sound's impact.
