Your Coaching Script

Here's a script to guide your initial conversation. Remember, this is a starting point, so adapt it to your unique style:

YOU:
"Maria, I understand you're feeling anxious, having trouble sleeping, and experiencing irritability. Thank you for sharing that with me. It takes courage to seek support."
MARIA:
"It's been tough. I'm just so tired of feeling this way."
YOU:
"I hear you. Many people experience similar challenges, especially with the demands of work and life transitions. I want you to know that adaptogens can be a gentle yet powerful tool to support your body's natural ability to cope with stress."
MARIA:
"Adaptogens? I've heard of them, but I don't really know what they do."
YOU:
"That's perfectly fine! Think of adaptogens as herbs that help your body adapt to stress, like a thermostat that regulates temperature. They can help balance your energy levels, improve your sleep, and reduce anxiety without the side effects of some medications."
YOU:
"Would you be open to exploring how adaptogens, along with some lifestyle adjustments, could help you regain a sense of calm and improve your sleep?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the initial consultation with Maria?

The primary goal is to build rapport, validate her experience, and educate her about the potential benefits of adaptogens as part of a holistic approach to address her anxiety and sleep issues.

2. Why is it important to avoid overpromising results when discussing adaptogens with Maria?

Overpromising can erode trust and set unrealistic expectations. It's crucial to emphasize that adaptogens are most effective when combined with lifestyle adjustments and other supportive practices.
