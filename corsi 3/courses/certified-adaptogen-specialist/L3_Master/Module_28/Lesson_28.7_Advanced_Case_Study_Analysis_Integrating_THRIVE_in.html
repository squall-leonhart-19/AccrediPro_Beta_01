Case Study

Severe Anxiety Attack Post-Traumatic Event

AS

Ava Sterling, 48

Experiences a severe anxiety attack following a car accident.

Ava, a 48-year-old marketing executive, experienced a traumatic car accident. While physically unharmed, she developed severe anxiety attacks, characterized by palpitations, shortness of breath, and intense fear. Her symptoms disrupted her sleep, work, and overall quality of life. She sought help to manage her anxiety without relying solely on pharmaceutical interventions. Ava's case highlights the critical need for integrative strategies in crisis management.

Applying the THRIVE Framework

Let's walk through how we can apply the THRIVE Frameworkâ„¢ to Ava's case:

  • T: Targeted Assessment: Identify Ava's primary stressors (trauma-induced anxiety), physiological symptoms (palpitations, shortness of breath), and lifestyle factors (work-related stress).
  • H: Holistic History: Gather information about Ava's medical history, lifestyle, diet, sleep patterns, and previous experiences with anxiety management.
  • R: Realistic Goals: Set achievable goals with Ava, such as reducing the frequency and intensity of anxiety attacks, improving sleep quality, and enhancing overall well-being.
  • I: Integrative Protocol: Design a personalized adaptogen protocol incorporating complementary practices like mindfulness and breathing exercises.
  • V: Validate Progress: Regularly monitor Ava's progress, gather feedback, and make necessary adjustments to the protocol based on her response and tolerance.
  • E: Educate and Empower: Provide Ava with education about adaptogens, anxiety management techniques, and self-care strategies to empower her to take control of her health and well-being.

Adaptogen Selection and Dosage Rationale

Based on the THRIVE assessment, the following adaptogens were selected for Ava:

Adaptogen Dosage Rationale Timing
Rhodiola Rosea 200mg twice daily Helps reduce stress and fatigue, improves cognitive function and mood. A 2017 study in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine showed significant reduction in anxiety symptoms with Rhodiola supplementation (Bystritsky et al., 2017). Morning and early afternoon
Ashwagandha 300mg twice daily Reduces anxiety and improves sleep quality. A 2019 meta-analysis in Nutrients found that Ashwagandha significantly reduced stress and anxiety scores (Salimbeni et al., 2019). Morning and before bed
Lemon Balm 600mg daily Provides calming effects and reduces anxiety. A 2014 study in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine demonstrated that lemon balm reduced anxiety symptoms (Cases et al., 2011). Before bed

Dosage Rationale: Dosages were chosen based on clinical research and established guidelines for adaptogen use. It's crucial to start with lower doses and gradually increase to assess tolerance and minimize potential side effects.

Integrative Strategies for Crisis Management

In addition to adaptogens, Ava was introduced to the following integrative strategies:

  • Mindfulness Meditation: Practicing mindfulness meditation for 10-15 minutes daily to reduce anxiety and improve emotional regulation. Studies indicate that mindfulness can reduce anxiety symptoms by up to 58% (Grossman et al., 2004).
  • Deep Breathing Exercises: Performing diaphragmatic breathing exercises to activate the parasympathetic nervous system and promote relaxation.
  • Gentle Yoga: Engaging in gentle yoga poses to release tension and improve overall well-being.
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Techniques: Identifying and challenging negative thought patterns to reduce anxiety triggers.

Monitoring and Adjusting the Protocol

Regular monitoring and adjustments are crucial for optimizing the adaptogen protocol. Ava was instructed to keep a journal to track her symptoms, mood, and sleep quality. Weekly check-ins were scheduled to assess her progress and make necessary adjustments. After two weeks, Ava reported a 30% reduction in anxiety attack frequency and improved sleep quality. The dosage of Rhodiola was slightly increased to 250mg twice daily to further enhance its effects.

Expected Outcomes and Long-Term Management

With consistent use of adaptogens and integrative strategies, Ava can expect to experience:

  • Reduced frequency and intensity of anxiety attacks.
  • Improved sleep quality and duration.
  • Enhanced mood and emotional regulation.
  • Increased resilience to stress.

Long-term management involves maintaining the adaptogen protocol, continuing integrative practices, and seeking ongoing support from a healthcare provider.
