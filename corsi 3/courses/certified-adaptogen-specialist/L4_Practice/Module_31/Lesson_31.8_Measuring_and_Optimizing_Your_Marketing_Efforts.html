Discovery Call Script: Your 30-Minute Roadmap

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport (5 min) 5 min

YOU:
"I saw you're a marketing manager. That sounds demanding! What does a typical day look like for you?"
Phase 2: Uncover Challenges & Goals (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned stress and sleep issues. Can you elaborate on that? When did these issues start, and how are they impacting your life?"

"What have you already tried to address these challenges? What worked, and what didn't?"

"If you could wave a magic wand and feel completely better, what would that look like for you? What would you be able to do that you can't do now?"

Handling Objections: Turn Doubts into Opportunities

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. We can work together to find realistic ways to incorporate these strategies into your daily routine. Plus, imagine how much *more* productive you'll be when you're not constantly battling stress and fatigue."
"I need to research adaptogens more."
"That's a great idea! I'm happy to share some credible resources with you. However, keep in mind that research alone isn't enough. It's about finding the *right* adaptogens and dosages for *your* specific needs, and that's where my expertise comes in. I can guide you through that process."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"I understand your concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I'm confident in my approach. I have a proven track record of helping clients like you. We'll continuously monitor your progress and adjust the plan as needed. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the program after the first 30 days, I'll refund your money."
"I need to talk to my partner about it."
"That's perfectly reasonable. Would it be helpful if I sent you a summary of the program and its benefits that you can share with them? Or, I'm happy to schedule a brief call with both of you to answer any questions they may have."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a successful discovery call?

The key phases are: Building rapport, Understanding the problem, Presenting your approach, and Closing.

2. How should you respond to the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value by focusing on the potential ROI in terms of improved health, productivity, and reduced costs in other areas.

3. What is the purpose of practicing your call-to-action?

Practicing your call-to-action helps you deliver it confidently and naturally, making it more likely that the prospect will say "yes".

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key. Know your prospect and your program inside and out.
  • Structure your discovery calls to maximize your time and ensure you cover all the important points.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare confident, value-driven responses.
  • Practice your pricing presentation until you can deliver it with unwavering confidence.
  • Close with a clear and compelling call-to-action.
