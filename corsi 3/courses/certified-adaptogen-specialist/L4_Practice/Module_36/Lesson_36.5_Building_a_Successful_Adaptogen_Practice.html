Establishing Your Adaptogen Practice

The foundation of a successful practice lies in defining your niche and creating a solid business plan. Consider these steps:

  1. Define your ideal client: Are you passionate about working with women over 40 experiencing hormonal imbalances, athletes seeking performance enhancement, or individuals managing chronic stress?

  3. Set up your workspace: Whether it's a dedicated room in your home or a rented office space, ensure it's conducive to client consultations and reflects your professional brand.
  4. Obtain necessary licenses and insurance: Research local regulations and secure professional liability insurance to protect your practice.
