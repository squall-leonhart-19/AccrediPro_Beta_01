5. Offering Incentives and Referral Programs

Incentives and referral programs can be a great way to attract new participants and build a loyal customer base. Consider these options:

  • Introductory Discounts: Offer a discount for first-time participants.
  • Early Bird Specials: Provide a lower price for those who sign up early.
  • Bundle Packages: Offer discounts for signing up for multiple workshops.
  • Referral Programs: Reward participants who refer new customers. For example, offer a discount on their next workshop or a free gift.
  • Scholarships: Offer partial or full scholarships to those who cannot afford the full price, increasing accessibility to your workshops.

A study by the Wharton School of Business found that referred customers have a 16% higher lifetime value than non-referred customers (Schmitt et al., 2011).
