Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for connecting! I saw your message on Instagram, and I appreciate you reaching out. I know how stressful it can be when our feline friends are anxious. How long have you had Whiskers?"
YOU:
"He sounds like a real sweetheart! What made you decide to reach out to a cat wellness specialist now?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about Whiskers' anxiety. What does it look like? When do you notice it most?"
YOU:
"You mentioned calming treats. Have you tried anything else to help him relax?"
YOU:
"If Whiskers continues to be anxious, what impact will that have on his quality of life? And on yours?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're a very caring cat parent, and you want the best for Whiskers. I understand your concerns about his anxiety and your desire to find a solution that truly works. Is that right?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because I focus on understanding the root cause of the anxiety. We'll look at his environment, diet, routines, and even his past experiences to develop a personalized plan. I work with clients over 8 weeks to implement these strategies. Many clients see a significant reduction in anxiety within the first few weeks."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach sound like something that resonates with you and your goals for Whiskers?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me tell you more about my 8-week program and how we can get started helping Whiskers feel more relaxed and secure."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in your cat's well-being is a big decision. Let's talk about the long-term benefits. Reducing Whiskers' anxiety can improve his overall health and potentially reduce vet bills down the road. We can also explore payment plan options to make it more manageable."
"I need to do some more research."
"That's perfectly reasonable. What specific information are you looking for? I'm happy to provide you with some resources or answer any questions you have. I also have testimonials from other clients who have seen great results with their anxious cats."
"I'm worried it won't work for my cat."
"I appreciate your concern. Every cat is different, and there are no guarantees. However, my approach is highly personalized, and I tailor the plan to Whiskers' specific needs. I'm confident that we can make a positive impact on his anxiety levels. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee, so if you're not happy with the results after a certain period, we can discuss a refund."
"I don't have time for another program."
"I understand that you're busy. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. The weekly sessions are only 60 minutes, and I provide you with actionable steps that you can easily incorporate into your daily routine. Think of it as an investment in Whiskers' well-being and your peace of mind."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's needs, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle budget objections during a discovery call?

Acknowledge the client's concerns, highlight the long-term value of your services, and offer flexible payment options.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful responses.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your cat wellness program.
  • Confidently state your pricing and close the sale.
