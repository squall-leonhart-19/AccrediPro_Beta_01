Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I'm Maya, and I'm excited to chat with you. To make the most of our time, I'd like to understand what you're looking for, share how I can help, and if it feels like a good fit, discuss next steps. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"Before we dive in, I always like to ask: what prompted you to reach out to me specifically?"
Phase 2: Explore Challenges & Desired Outcomes 10 min
YOU:
"So, Susan, tell me more about what's been going on. What challenges are you currently facing in achieving that work-life balance you mentioned?"
YOU:
"What does 'fulfillment' look like to you? What would you be doing, feeling, and experiencing if you achieved it?"
YOU:
"And what have you already tried to address these issues?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being 'absolutely essential,' how important is it for you to resolve these challenges?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're feeling overwhelmed by the demands of your career and are searching for a way to create more balance and purpose in your life. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My hypnotherapy program is designed to help you identify and overcome the limiting beliefs and subconscious patterns that are contributing to your stress and preventing you from achieving your goals. We'll work together to create a personalized plan that addresses your specific needs and helps you unlock your full potential. Many of my clients report feeling significantly calmer, more focused, and more empowered within just a few weeks."
YOU:
"Does this approach resonate with what you're looking for?"
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Great! Let me briefly explain the program and how we can get started. It's a 12-week program with weekly sessions, personalized recordings, and ongoing support. Would you like to learn more about the investment?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. We can work together to find a schedule that fits your needs. And consider this: how much time are you *losing* right now due to stress and lack of focus? This program is designed to help you become more efficient and productive."
"I'm skeptical about hypnotherapy."
"That's perfectly normal. Many people are initially skeptical. Hypnotherapy is a scientifically-backed technique that utilizes the power of your subconscious mind to create positive change. I can share some research and testimonials with you, and I encourage you to do your own research as well. The American Psychological Association recognizes its efficacy for various conditions."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the process, or something else? Knowing that will help me provide you with the information you need to make an informed decision."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer a payment plan option. Instead of paying the full amount upfront, you can pay in three monthly installments. This makes the program more accessible while still allowing you to achieve your goals."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's challenges and goals, presenting your solution, handling objections, and confidently stating your price.

2. How can you address a prospect's skepticism about hypnotherapy?

Acknowledge their skepticism, share research and testimonials, explain the scientific basis of hypnotherapy, and offer to answer their questions.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is crucial for building a thriving hypnotherapy practice.
  • A structured discovery call helps you understand your prospect's needs and present your solution effectively.
  • Handling objections with confidence and empathy is essential for closing sales.
  • Confidently stating your price and highlighting the value of your program is key to earning your worth.
  • Consistent practice and a positive mindset will help you attract and enroll more clients.
