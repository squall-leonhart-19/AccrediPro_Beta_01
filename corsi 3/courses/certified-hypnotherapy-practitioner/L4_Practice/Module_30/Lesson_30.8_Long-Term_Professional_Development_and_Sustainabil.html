Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during a discovery call?

Building rapport involves active listening, empathy, and finding common ground with the prospect. Start with a warm welcome, ask open-ended questions, and show genuine interest in their story.

2. How can you effectively address the "time" objection during a sales conversation?

Acknowledge their concern, emphasize the program's efficiency, and highlight the long-term benefits of investing in their health. Frame it as a way to reclaim their time and energy, rather than another burden on their schedule.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call helps you understand your prospect's needs and present your solution effectively.
  • Anticipating and handling objections with confidence is crucial for closing the sale.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and confidently state your price.
  • Focus on the transformation you offer, not just the features of your program.
  • Consistent effort in client acquisition can lead to a thriving and fulfilling practice.
