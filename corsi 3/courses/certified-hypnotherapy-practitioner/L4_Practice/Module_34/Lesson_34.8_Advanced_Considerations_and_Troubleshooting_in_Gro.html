Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the initial phase of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves expressing genuine interest in the prospect, actively listening to their story, and finding common ground to establish a connection.

2. How can you effectively address the "It's too expensive" objection without immediately lowering your price?

Reframe the cost as an investment in their well-being, highlight the long-term value and benefits they'll receive, and offer payment plans to make it more manageable.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the art of enrollment is crucial for filling your group programs and workshops.
  • Building rapport and understanding your prospect's needs are essential for effective sales.
  • Confidently addressing objections and presenting your pricing will increase your conversion rates.
  • Consistent practice and a genuine desire to help your clients will lead to success.
