Key Takeaways

  • Empathy is crucial: Build rapport by genuinely listening to your prospect's concerns.
  • Address root causes: Highlight how hypnotherapy differs from other approaches.
  • Practice objection handling: Prepare confident responses to common objections.
  • State your price confidently: Believe in the value you provide.
  • Close with a clear call to action: Guide your prospect toward the next step.
