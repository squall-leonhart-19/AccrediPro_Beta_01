Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of empowerment coaching?

The primary goal is to help clients recognize and cultivate their inner strengths and resources to achieve their goals and live a more fulfilling life.

2. Why is it important to connect a client's actions to their values?

Connecting actions to values increases motivation and commitment, as it creates a deeper sense of purpose and meaning.

Key Takeaways

  • Empowerment coaching focuses on helping clients tap into their potential.
  • Validating a client's feelings is crucial for building trust and rapport.
  • Focus on what the client *wants*, not what they *should* do.
  • Small, incremental steps lead to sustainable progress.
  • Connecting actions to values increases motivation and fulfillment.
