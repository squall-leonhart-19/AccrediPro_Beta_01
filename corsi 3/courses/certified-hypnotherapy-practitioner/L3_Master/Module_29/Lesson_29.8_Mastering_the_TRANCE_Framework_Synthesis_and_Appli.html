Key Takeaways

  • Mentoring new practitioners is a valuable way to give back to the community and help shape the future of hypnotherapy.
  • Focus on providing practical, actionable advice that addresses their specific needs and challenges.
  • Encourage them to find their own unique style and approach to hypnotherapy.
  • Celebrate their progress and successes along the way to build their confidence and motivation.
