Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I appreciate you taking the time to connect. To start, what prompted you to reach out to me specifically?"
YOU:
"That's interesting that you found me through [mention how she found you]. What are you hoping to achieve by the end of our call today?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the brain fog, Susan. How long has it been going on? What does it feel like?"
YOU:
"And what about your energy levels? When are they at their lowest? How does this impact your daily routine?"
YOU:
"What strategies have you already tried to address these issues? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and eliminate these challenges, what would your ideal day look like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her pain points]. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach focuses on identifying the root causes of these issues using a personalized, holistic strategy. We'll explore how medicinal mushrooms can support your cognitive function, energy levels, and stress response. I'll create a customized 12-week protocol tailored to your specific needs and goals."
YOU:
"Typically, my clients report noticeable improvements within the first few weeks, such as increased focus, sustained energy, and reduced anxiety. How does that sound to you?"
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, considering everything we've discussed, do you believe this approach aligns with your goals and expectations? ... Excellent! Let's discuss the program details and how we can get you started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if medicinal mushrooms are right for me."
"That's perfectly understandable. Many people are unfamiliar with their benefits. Medicinal mushrooms have been used for centuries and are backed by scientific research. I can provide you with studies and testimonials to help you make an informed decision. Additionally, we will start with a very low dose to ensure you tolerate it well."
"I'm worried about the cost."
"I appreciate your concern. Let's break down the investment. My 12-week program is designed to provide long-term solutions, potentially reducing your reliance on other expensive treatments or supplements that haven't worked. What if you could regain your focus and energy, leading to increased productivity and career advancement? What would that be worth to you?"
"I need to research medicinal mushrooms more."
"Of course! I encourage you to do your research. I can send you some reputable resources to get you started. Would you also be open to a follow-up call to discuss any questions you may have after your research?"
"I don't have time for a 12-week program."
"I understand your time is valuable. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your busy schedule. We can adjust the frequency and duration of our sessions to accommodate your needs. Plus, the benefits you'll experience ‚Äì increased energy and focus ‚Äì will actually help you become more efficient and productive in other areas of your life."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a successful discovery call?

The key phases are: Building Rapport, Understanding the Problem, Presenting Your Approach, and Closing.

2. How should you handle the objection "I'm worried about the cost?"

Acknowledge their concern, break down the investment, and focus on the long-term value and potential ROI. Ask what achieving their health goals would be worth to them.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call helps you understand the prospect's needs and present your program effectively.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare confident, value-driven responses.
  • Practice stating your price clearly and confidently, emphasizing the long-term benefits.
  • Focus on the potential return on investment for the client, both in terms of health and overall well-being.
