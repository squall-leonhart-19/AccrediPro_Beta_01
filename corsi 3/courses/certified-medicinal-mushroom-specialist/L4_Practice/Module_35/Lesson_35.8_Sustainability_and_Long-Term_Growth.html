Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if this will really work for me."
"I understand your hesitation. Many of my clients felt the same way initially. What I can tell you is that my approach is highly personalized. We'll continuously adapt the program based on your progress and feedback. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you don't see improvements within the first month, we can adjust the plan or discuss a refund."
"I'm concerned about the cost."
"I appreciate your honesty. Investing in your health is a big decision. Let's break down what you're getting: personalized consultations, a tailored wellness plan, ongoing support, and potentially reduced reliance on other costly treatments. Many clients find that the long-term benefits outweigh the initial investment. We also have flexible payment options available, if that helps."
"I need to do some more research."
"That's perfectly fine, Susan. What specific information are you looking for? I can provide you with some research articles and testimonials from past clients. Would that be helpful?"
"I don't have time right now."
"I understand that timing is important. The program is designed to fit into your busy lifestyle. We can schedule sessions at convenient times, and the daily practices can be integrated into your existing routine. How about we schedule a follow-up call in a week to see if the timing is better then?"
