Check Your Understanding

1. What is the difference between a health claim and a structure/function claim?

A health claim suggests a product can treat, cure, or prevent a disease. A structure/function claim describes the role of a nutrient or ingredient in maintaining normal structure or function of the body.

2. Why is it important to include a disclaimer on marketing materials?

A disclaimer helps protect you legally by stating that your statements have not been evaluated by the FDA and your services are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Key Takeaways

  • Focus on structure/function claims rather than health claims in your advertising.
  • Always include a disclaimer on your marketing materials.
  • Offer compliance services as a valuable addition to your mushroom practice.
  • Stay updated on the latest FDA regulations and guidelines.
