Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Introduction 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm a Certified Medicinal Mushroom Specialist. It's great to connect with you. To start, can you tell me what prompted you to reach out to me specifically?"
YOU:
"That's interesting you found me through [keyword she used]. What are your initial thoughts or expectations about working with medicinal mushrooms?"
Phase 2: Problem Exploration 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned brain fog, sleep issues, and anxiety. Can you elaborate on when these symptoms started and how they impact your day-to-day life?"
YOU:
"Have you consulted with other healthcare professionals or tried any specific treatments for these symptoms?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the most disruptive, how would you rate the impact of these symptoms on your overall well-being?"
Phase 3: Solution Presentation 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing common menopausal symptoms significantly impacting your daily life. My approach uses medicinal mushrooms to target the root causes of these issues, like hormonal imbalances and inflammation. Does that resonate with you?"
YOU:
"My 12-week program involves a personalized protocol using specific mushroom extracts, dietary adjustments, and lifestyle recommendations tailored to support your unique needs. Many of my clients report noticeable improvements in sleep, mental clarity, and mood within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"For example, I worked with a 48-year-old client experiencing similar symptoms. After 8 weeks on a Reishi and Lion's Mane protocol, she reported a 60% reduction in anxiety and improved sleep quality. Would you be interested in hearing more about how this could work for you?"
Phase 4: Call to Action & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, do you feel that this approach aligns with your goals and values for managing your symptoms naturally?"
YOU:
"Great! I have two options for my program. Let me briefly explain them, and then you can decide which is the better fit for you."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if medicinal mushrooms are right for me."
"I understand your hesitation. Many people are unfamiliar with medicinal mushrooms. However, they have a long history of traditional use and are now backed by scientific research. I can provide you with some resources to learn more about their benefits for menopausal symptoms, and we can discuss any specific concerns you have."
"I need to do more research."
"That's perfectly reasonable. What specific information are you looking for? I can point you to reputable scientific articles and studies. Also, keep in mind that my program includes ongoing support and education to ensure you feel confident and informed every step of the way."
"I'm concerned about potential side effects."
"It's wise to be cautious. Medicinal mushrooms are generally well-tolerated, but like any natural substance, some individuals may experience mild side effects. We'll start with a low dose and gradually increase it while monitoring your response. I'll also provide guidance on potential interactions with any medications you're taking."
"I'm not sure I have the time for a 12-week program."
"I understand that time is a valuable resource. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your busy schedule. The weekly commitment is approximately 1-2 hours, including supplement intake, dietary adjustments, and check-in calls. Plus, the potential benefits ‚Äì improved sleep, mental clarity, and reduced anxiety ‚Äì can actually free up more time and energy in the long run."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's problems, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the "It's too expensive" objection?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value by focusing on the potential ROI in terms of improved health, reduced healthcare costs, and increased quality of life.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the discovery call is crucial for client acquisition and building a thriving practice.
  • Focus on understanding your prospect's needs and pain points to tailor your solution effectively.
  • Practice handling common objections with confidence and empathy.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and confidently state your price.
  • Remember, every "no" gets you closer to a "yes." Stay persistent and believe in your ability to help others.
