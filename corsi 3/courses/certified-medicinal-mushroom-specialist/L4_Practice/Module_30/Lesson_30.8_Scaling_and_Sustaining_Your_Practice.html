Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I appreciate you taking the time to connect. I saw on your Instagram that you love gardening; I'm a big fan myself! What are you growing this season?"
YOU:
"That sounds lovely! So, Susan, what prompted you to reach out? What's been going on?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the brain fog. When did you first notice it? How does it impact your work and family life?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, how do you see yourself a year from now? And if you could magically eliminate the brain fog and regain your energy, what would your life look like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Unique Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I understand you're experiencing [summarize her key challenges]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach focuses on identifying the root causes of your symptoms rather than just masking them. Over 12 weeks, we'll create a personalized protocol using medicinal mushrooms and lifestyle adjustments to address the underlying imbalances. Many of my clients report significant improvements in energy and mental clarity within the first month."
YOU:
"For example, I worked with a client, Sarah, 48, a busy marketing executive, who had similar symptoms. After 8 weeks on a tailored reishi and lion's mane protocol, she reported a 60% reduction in brain fog and significantly improved sleep."
Phase 4: Confident Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with you? Do you feel like this could be the solution you've been searching for? ... Wonderful! Let me share the details of my program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I need to think about it."
"I understand completely. It's a big decision. What specifically do you need time to consider? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed choice."
"It's more expensive than I anticipated."
"I appreciate your honesty. Let's look at the value you're receiving. The program includes [list key features and benefits]. What's the value of regaining your mental clarity and energy? What's the cost of continuing to struggle with these symptoms? We also offer payment plans to make it more accessible."
"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand that time is valuable. The program is designed to be integrated into your existing lifestyle. The time commitment is approximately [state time commitment] per week. Think of it as an investment in your well-being, which will ultimately increase your productivity and allow you to enjoy your life more fully."
"I'm skeptical if this will really work for me."
"That's a valid concern. I encourage you to read testimonials from other clients who have experienced similar challenges and achieved significant results. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the program after [state timeframe], I'll [state guarantee]. My goal is to help you achieve your health goals, and I'm confident we can do that together."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's problem, presenting your approach, and closing with a call to action.

2. How can you address the objection "It's too expensive?"

Focus on the value they're receiving, the potential return on investment, and offer payment plan options.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is crucial for building a thriving practice.
  • A structured discovery call helps you connect with potential clients and understand their needs.
  • Confidently handling objections is essential for converting prospects into clients.
  • Practicing your pricing presentation will help you convey the value of your program.
  • Consistent action and a belief in your services will lead to success.
