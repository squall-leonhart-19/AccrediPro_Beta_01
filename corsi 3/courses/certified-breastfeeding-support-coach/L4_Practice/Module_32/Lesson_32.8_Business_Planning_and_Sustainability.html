Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Welcome & Connection, Understand Challenges, Offer Solutions & Expertise, and Close with Confidence.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I need to talk to my partner first?"

Acknowledge their need and offer to answer questions for them or schedule a call with both of them.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for client acquisition.
  • Empathy and active listening build trust and rapport with potential clients.
  • Confidently presenting your pricing and handling objections are essential sales skills.
  • Consistent effort and a client-focused approach lead to a thriving practice.
  • Practice your script and responses to build confidence and fluency.
