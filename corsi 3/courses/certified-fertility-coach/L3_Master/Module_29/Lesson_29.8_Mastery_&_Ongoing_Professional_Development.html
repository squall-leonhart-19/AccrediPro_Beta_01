Your Feedback Dialogue

Delivering Constructive Feedback to Sarah

Start with Encouragement
"Sarah, it's fantastic that you're reaching out! It shows you care deeply about your clients and are committed to providing the best support."
Ask Open-Ended Questions
"What are your initial thoughts about Emily's reaction? What possibilities have you considered?"
Offer Targeted Education

"Based on our discussion, what adjustments do you think would be most helpful for Emily? How can you modify your approach to better support her?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the MOST important thing to do when a mentee presents a challenging client case?

The most important thing is to create a safe space for them to share their concerns without judgment. Validate their feelings and emphasize that challenging cases are a normal part of the learning process.

2. How can you help a mentee build confidence in their pricing structure?

Share your own pricing journey, discuss the value they provide, and help them articulate their unique selling points. Role-play difficult conversations about pricing. Remind them that earning $75-$150 per session is achievable with the right skills and confidence.

Key Takeaways

  • Effective supervision involves creating a safe and supportive environment for mentees.
  • Asking open-ended questions encourages critical thinking and problem-solving skills.
  • Providing targeted education and resources empowers mentees to expand their knowledge base.
  • Affirming strengths and celebrating successes builds confidence and resilience.
  • Remember, the goal is to guide, not dictate, empowering mentees to develop their unique style and approach.
