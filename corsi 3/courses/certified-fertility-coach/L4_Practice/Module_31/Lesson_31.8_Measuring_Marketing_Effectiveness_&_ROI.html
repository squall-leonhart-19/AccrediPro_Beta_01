Handling Objections with Grace

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I need to think about it."
"I understand completely. It‚Äôs a big decision. To help you with your decision, is there anything specific you need to think about? Perhaps the investment, the time commitment, or something else? Knowing that will help me provide you with the right information."
"It's too expensive."
"I hear you. Let's reframe it. How much have you already spent on [supplements, doctors, etc.] without seeing lasting results? My program is an investment in a long-term solution that addresses the root cause of your PCOS. Think about the value of regaining control of your health and feeling like yourself again. What's that worth to you?"
"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand that time is valuable. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. The weekly coaching sessions are [duration], and the action steps are manageable. Plus, think about the time you're currently spending on managing symptoms without a clear plan. This program can actually save you time in the long run."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, my program is based on evidence-based strategies and personalized to your unique needs. I have a track record of helping women with PCOS manage their symptoms and improve their quality of life. I also offer [mention any guarantee or support]. I'm confident that if you commit to the program, you'll see positive changes."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are three key elements to include in your discovery call script?

Building rapport, uncovering challenges and goals, and presenting your solution.

2. How can you address the "It's too expensive" objection effectively?

Reframe the investment by focusing on the long-term value and potential cost savings from ineffective treatments.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is Key: Thoroughly prepare your discovery call script and anticipate common objections.
  • Focus on Value: Highlight the value of your program and how it addresses the client's specific needs.
  • Confidence is Contagious: Project confidence in your pricing and your ability to help the client.
  • Practice Makes Perfect: Practice your script and objection handling responses until they feel natural.
  • Follow Up: Always follow up with potential clients after the discovery call.
