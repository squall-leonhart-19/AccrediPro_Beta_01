Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you so much for scheduling this call. I know your time is valuable. I'm excited to chat with you. To make the most of our time, I'd love to understand what brought you here today. How did you find me on Instagram?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! I'm glad my content resonated with you. So, Susan, what's been happening that made you decide to reach out now?"

YOU:
"Tell me more about your fertility journey. When did you start trying to conceive? What challenges have you faced?"
YOU:

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in your fertility is a big decision. Let's explore some options. I offer payment plans to make it more accessible. Also, consider the potential cost of *not* addressing this ‚Äì the continued emotional stress, the potential for more expensive interventions down the road. What feels like a comfortable investment for you to prioritize your fertility and wellbeing?"
"I need to do more research."
"That's perfectly reasonable. What specific information are you looking for? I'm happy to provide resources or answer any questions you have. I also have client testimonials and case studies that might be helpful."
"I'm not sure if this is the right time for me."
"I appreciate your honesty. What makes you feel like it's not the right time? Sometimes, addressing fertility concerns proactively can reduce stress and improve your overall well-being, making it *easier* to conceive. We can always adjust the pace of the program to fit your needs."
"I've tried so many things already, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. It sounds like you've been through a lot. My approach is different because I focus on identifying the *root causes* of your fertility challenges. I don't just offer generic advice; I create a personalized plan based on your unique situation. Many of my clients have had success after trying other methods that failed."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then ask clarifying questions to understand their specific concerns and provide additional information.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence.
  • Prepare confident responses to common objections.
  • State your prices clearly and with conviction.
  • Focus on the value you provide to potential clients.
  • Remember that client acquisition is a crucial skill for scaling your practice.
