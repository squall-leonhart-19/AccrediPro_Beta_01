Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Emma, and I'm a Certified Fertility Coach. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"I understand you're exploring fertility coaching. What prompted you to reach out now?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Can you tell me more about your journey with secondary infertility? How long have you been trying?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far? What were the results?"
YOU:
"What's the biggest challenge you're facing right now?"
YOU:
"Imagine you're holding your newborn baby in your arms. What does that feel like? How would that change your life?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're feeling overwhelmed, unsupported, and unsure of what to do next. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is holistic and personalized. I work with women like you to identify the root causes of infertility, create a tailored plan using nutrition, lifestyle modifications, and targeted techniques, and provide unwavering support throughout the process. My clients often experience positive results within 3-6 months."
YOU:
"I also provide resources on legal and ethical issues surrounding fertility so you can make informed decisions."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the type of support you've been searching for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me tell you about my coaching program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I need to think about it."
"I understand. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the financial investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision. I'm here to answer any questions you may have."
"It's too expensive."
"I understand that it's an investment. Think about what you've already spent on treatments with no results. My program offers a personalized approach that addresses the root cause of your fertility issues. What is conceiving and holding your baby worth to you? Many of my clients find that the value they receive far outweighs the financial investment."
"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand your concern about time. My program is designed to fit into your busy lifestyle. The weekly coaching calls are flexible, and the action steps are manageable. Remember, this is an investment in your future and your family. Imagine how much time you'll save if you conceive sooner rather than later."
"What if it doesn't work?"
"I understand your hesitation. While I can't guarantee pregnancy, I can guarantee that you'll receive personalized support, a tailored plan, and evidence-based strategies to improve your fertility. I have a proven track record of helping women like you achieve their dreams of motherhood. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with the program, I'll work with you to make it right."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. What's a good way to handle the objection "It's too expensive?"

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the investment by highlighting the value they'll receive and the potential cost of *not* solving their problem.

Key Takeaways

  • Master the Discovery Call: Practice the script to confidently guide potential clients.
  • Handle Objections with Grace: Prepare thoughtful responses to common objections.
  • State Your Price with Confidence: Believe in the value you offer and communicate it clearly.
  • Close the Deal: Practice your call-to-action to convert prospects into clients.
