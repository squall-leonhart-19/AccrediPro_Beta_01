Case Study: Maria, 72

Client: Maria, a 72-year-old woman with terminal breast cancer.

Presenting Symptoms: Anxiety, fear of death, strained relationship with her daughter, and a sense of unfinished business.

Intervention: Using the S.O.U.L.S. Frameworkâ„¢, the doula:

  • Support System Scan: Identified that Maria's daughter, although caring, was overwhelmed and struggling to cope. The doula facilitated a conversation between Maria and her daughter to address their strained relationship.
  • Opening to Emotions: Provided a safe space for Maria to express her fears and anxieties about death. Used mindfulness techniques to help her manage her emotions.
  • Understanding Values: Explored Maria's values and helped her identify what was most important to her in her final weeks.
  • Legacy & Meaning-Making: Assisted Maria in creating a memory box for her grandchildren, filled with photos, letters, and small mementos.
  • Spiritual Exploration: Facilitated conversations about Maria's spiritual beliefs and helped her connect with her faith community.

Outcomes: Maria experienced a significant reduction in anxiety and fear. Her relationship with her daughter improved, and she found peace and meaning in her final weeks. The memory box brought her a sense of comfort and purpose.
