Check Your Understanding

1. What is the most important thing to establish in the initial conversation with a grieving client?

Establishing trust and validating their feelings are crucial. Avoid minimizing their grief or offering unsolicited advice.

2. How can you help a client identify their passions after a significant loss?

Encourage them to reflect on activities and interests that brought them joy before the loss, and explore new possibilities for connection and fulfillment.

Key Takeaways

  • Grief has its own timeline, and there's no "should" involved.
  • Focus on rediscovering passions, exploring new possibilities, and creating a lasting legacy.
  • Balance exploring the past with creating a vision for the future.
  • Tailor your approach to the client's unique experiences and preferences.
