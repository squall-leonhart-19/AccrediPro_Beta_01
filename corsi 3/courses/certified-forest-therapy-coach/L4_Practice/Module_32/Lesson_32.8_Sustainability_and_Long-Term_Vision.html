Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to learn more about what brought you here. To start, can you tell me a little bit about yourself and what you're hoping to achieve?"
YOU:
"That's great. Before we dive deeper, is it okay if I take some notes during our conversation?"
Phase 2: Understanding Needs (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling stressed and disconnected. Can you elaborate on that? What does a typical week look like for you, and how does that impact your well-being?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to manage stress and reconnect with nature? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and create your ideal life, how would it feel? What would be different?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're seeking a way to reduce stress, reconnect with nature, and improve your overall well-being, but haven't found a sustainable solution. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My forest therapy coaching program is designed to address those needs. It's an 8-week program that combines guided forest walks with mindfulness practices to help you reduce stress, enhance your connection with nature, and cultivate a greater sense of well-being. We meet once a week for a 2-hour guided walk, and you'll also receive weekly exercises to integrate nature connection into your daily life. A 2023 study in the Journal of Environmental Psychology found that regular forest bathing significantly reduces cortisol levels and improves mood (Park et al., 2010)."
Phase 4: Closing (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me tell you about the program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The 2-hour weekly walks are designed to be a dedicated time for self-care, and the benefits often translate into increased productivity and reduced stress throughout the week. Think of it as an investment in your overall well-being, which can ultimately save you time and energy."
"I'm not sure if it's worth the investment."
"I appreciate that. I encourage you to consider the long-term benefits of reduced stress, improved well-being, and a stronger connection with nature. These benefits can positively impact your health, relationships, and overall quality of life. Plus, I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied after the first two sessions, I'll refund your investment."
"Can I think about it?"
"Of course! What aspects are you wanting to consider further? Perhaps I can provide more information to help with your decision?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's needs, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need for time, inquire about the specific concerns they have, and offer additional information or support.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful responses.
  • Confidently present your pricing and demonstrate the value of your program.
  • Focus on building genuine connections with potential clients.
  • Remember that every "no" gets you closer to a "yes."
