The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a progressive 4-Tier system, designed to build your knowledge and skills step-by-step:

  1. Tier 1: FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15): This is where we establish your core understanding of forest therapy principles and our unique coaching methodology. You'll learn the fundamentals of connecting people with nature for healing and well-being.
  2. Tier 2: ADVANCED (Modules 16-23): We delve into more complex client cases and explore the clinical depth of forest therapy. You'll learn to adapt your coaching to diverse needs and challenges.
  3. Tier 3: MASTER (Modules 24-29): This tier focuses on developing your ability to train others and supervise aspiring coaches. You'll hone your leadership and mentoring skills.
  4. Tier 4: PRACTICE (Modules 30-36): We'll equip you with the tools and strategies to build a thriving forest therapy coaching practice. You'll learn about marketing, client acquisition, and business management.

Time Commitment & Pacing

To maximize your learning and integration of the material, we recommend dedicating approximately 5-7 hours per week to the course. This includes watching video lessons, completing practice labs, and engaging with the community forum. The entire program is designed to be completed within 6-9 months, but you can adjust the pace to fit your schedule.

Here's a sample pacing guide:

  • Weeks 1-12: Focus on Tier 1 (Foundation). Aim to complete 1-2 modules per week.
  • Weeks 13-18: Dive into Tier 2 (Advanced). Dedicate more time to complex case studies.
  • Weeks 19-24: Master Tier 3 (Master). Practice your training and mentoring skills.
  • Weeks 25-36: Build your practice in Tier 4 (Practice). Implement marketing and business strategies.
