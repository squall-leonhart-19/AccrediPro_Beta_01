Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's problem, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then gently inquire about what specifically they need to consider (time, investment, etc.) to address their concerns.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script until it feels natural and conversational.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare confident, empathetic responses.
  • Clearly articulate the value of your program and how it addresses the client's specific needs.
  • Present your pricing with confidence and offer payment options if needed.
  • Remember that every "no" gets you closer to a "yes."
