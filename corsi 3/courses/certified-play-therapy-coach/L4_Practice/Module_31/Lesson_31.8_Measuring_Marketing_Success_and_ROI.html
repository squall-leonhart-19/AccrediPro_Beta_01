Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Connect 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, it's Sarah! Thanks for scheduling this call. I'm excited to chat with you. To start, what caught your eye about my services?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! I understand wanting to find a new passion after such a big life change. On a scale of 1-10, how fulfilled are you feeling right now?"
Phase 2: Uncover Pain Points & Desires 10 min
YOU:
"What's missing? What does an 'ideal' day look like for you?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to fill that void? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If you don't find something fulfilling, what will that cost you in the long run? What's at stake here?"
Phase 3: Present Solution & Value 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I believe my 1:1 coaching program can help you rediscover your passions and create a fulfilling new chapter."
YOU:
"My program is designed to help you gain clarity, build confidence, and take action. We'll identify your unique strengths, explore new possibilities, and create a roadmap to achieve your goals. Imagine waking up each day feeling energized and excited about what's to come."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like it resonates with what you're looking for? If so, I'd love to share more details about my program and how we can work together."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy women. This program is designed to fit into your schedule. We can work together to prioritize and manage your time effectively. Plus, think of the time you'll save once you're clear on your direction and motivated to take action."
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can promise that I'll be fully committed to your success. I offer personalized support, proven strategies, and a safe space for you to explore your potential. And, I have many testimonials from women who have achieved incredible transformations through my program."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course! I want you to feel 100% confident in your decision. What specific questions or concerns do you have that I can address right now to help you make a more informed choice?"
"Can I get a discount?"
"My program is priced to reflect the value and personalized support I provide. However, I do offer a payment plan to make it more accessible. Let's discuss if that's a good option for you."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during a discovery call?

Building rapport involves active listening, asking open-ended questions, finding common ground, and showing genuine empathy.

2. How can you effectively handle the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge the client's need for time, then ask clarifying questions to understand their specific concerns and address them directly. Offer additional resources or support if needed.

Key Takeaways
