The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is meticulously structured into four distinct tiers, each designed to build upon the previous one. This progressive approach ensures you develop a deep understanding of play therapy coaching and the practical skills to excel.

  1. L1 FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15): This tier lays the groundwork. You'll master the core methodology, fundamental principles, and essential techniques of play therapy coaching. Think of this as building a strong base for your future practice.
  2. L2 ADVANCED (Modules 16-23): Here, we delve into more complex cases and clinical depth. You'll learn to navigate challenging situations, refine your coaching strategies, and expand your knowledge of specific therapeutic interventions.
  3. L3 MASTER (Modules 24-29): This is where you step into a leadership role. You'll develop the skills to train others, provide supervision, and mentor aspiring coaches. You'll become a true expert in the field.
  4. L4 PRACTICE (Modules 30-36): The final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. We'll cover marketing strategies, ethical considerations, and practical steps to establish a thriving play therapy coaching practice.
