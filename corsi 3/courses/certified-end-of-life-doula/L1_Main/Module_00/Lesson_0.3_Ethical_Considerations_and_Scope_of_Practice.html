The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is thoughtfully structured into four distinct tiers, each building upon the last to provide you with a comprehensive and deeply enriching learning experience.

  1. Tier 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15)

    This is where we lay the groundwork. You'll delve into the core methodology and fundamentals of end-of-life doula work. Think of it as building a strong and reliable base for your future practice. You'll gain a solid understanding of the principles, practices, and ethical considerations that underpin this meaningful work.

  2. Tier 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23)

    Here, we explore more complex cases and delve into greater clinical depth. You'll learn how to navigate challenging situations, support diverse client needs, and deepen your understanding of the physical, emotional, and spiritual aspects of end-of-life care. This is where you'll truly refine your skills and expand your knowledge.

  3. Tier 3: Master (Modules 24-29)

    This tier is all about leadership and mentorship. You'll learn how to train others and develop supervision skills. Whether you aspire to lead workshops, mentor new doulas, or contribute to the growth of the profession, this tier will equip you with the tools and confidence to do so.

  4. Tier 4: Practice (Modules 30-36)

    Now it's time to build your business and start working with clients. You'll learn practical strategies for marketing your services, establishing your brand, and creating a sustainable and fulfilling doula practice. This is where your passion transforms into a viable and rewarding career.

Maria, a 78-year-old woman with terminal breast cancer, expressed fear and anxiety about dying alone. Through active listening and validation, the doula helped Maria explore her fears and identify her sources of support. The doula then facilitated a family meeting to ensure Maria's wishes were understood and honored, resulting in Maria feeling more at peace and supported in her final days.

The Methodology Framework

At the heart of our certification is the P.E.A.C.E. Frameworkâ„¢ â€“ a compassionate and comprehensive approach to end-of-life care. This framework guides you in providing holistic support to clients and their families, addressing their physical, emotional, spiritual, and practical needs.

The P.E.A.C.E. Frameworkâ„¢ stands for:

  • Presence: Being fully present with your client, offering your undivided attention and compassion.
  • Empathy: Understanding and validating your client's emotions and experiences, creating a safe and supportive space for them to share their feelings.
  • Advocacy: Advocating for your client's wishes and preferences, ensuring their voice is heard and respected.
  • Comfort: Providing physical and emotional comfort, helping your client manage their symptoms and find peace.
  • Education: Educating your client and their family about the dying process, empowering them to make informed decisions and navigate this challenging time with knowledge and confidence.
