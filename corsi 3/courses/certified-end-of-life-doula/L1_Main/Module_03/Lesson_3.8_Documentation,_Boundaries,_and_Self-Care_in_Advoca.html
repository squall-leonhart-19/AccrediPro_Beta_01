Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to explore the client's emotions related to their legacy project?

Exploring emotions helps uncover the underlying motivations and values driving the client's desire for a legacy project. This allows for more targeted and meaningful coaching.

2. What are some examples of smaller, achievable actions that can build momentum for a legacy project?

Examples include: writing a letter to a loved one, volunteering for a cause they care about, starting a journal to capture their memories, or sharing their skills with others.

Key Takeaways

  • Focus on the client's values and emotions when exploring legacy work.
  • Help the client identify smaller, achievable actions.
  • Validate their fears and hesitations.
  • Remain neutral and curious.
  • Encourage self-compassion and celebrate small wins.
