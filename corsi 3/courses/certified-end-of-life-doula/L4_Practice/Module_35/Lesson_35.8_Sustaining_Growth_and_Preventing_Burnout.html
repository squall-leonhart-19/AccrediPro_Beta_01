Crafting Your Elevator Pitch

Your elevator pitch is a concise and compelling summary of your services, designed to capture someone's attention in a short amount of time. It should clearly articulate what you do, who you serve, and the unique value you provide.

Example Elevator Pitch

"I'm an end-of-life doula. I provide emotional, spiritual, and practical support to individuals and families facing a terminal illness. I help create peaceful and meaningful end-of-life experiences by facilitating conversations, honoring wishes, and offering compassionate care during a challenging time."

The Initial Consultation: A Step-by-Step Guide

The initial consultation is your opportunity to connect with potential clients, understand their needs, and demonstrate how you can help. This script provides a framework for conducting a successful consultation.

Consultation Script: Talking with the Family

Phase 1: Welcome & Introductions 5 min
YOU:
"Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. I'm , and I'm an end-of-life doula. I understand you're seeking support for your mother, Eleanor. Is that correct?"
YOU:
"Before we begin, I want to acknowledge the difficulty of this time and commend you for seeking support. My goal is to provide compassionate care and guidance to Eleanor and your family."
Phase 2: Needs Assessment 15 min
YOU:
"To best understand how I can help, can you share a bit about Eleanor's current situation, her wishes, and your family's needs?"
