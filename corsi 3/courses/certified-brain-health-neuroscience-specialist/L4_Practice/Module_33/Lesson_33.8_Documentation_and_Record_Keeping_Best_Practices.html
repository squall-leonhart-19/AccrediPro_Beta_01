Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to have a client agreement and disclaimers in your brain health coaching practice?

Client agreements and disclaimers protect you legally, set clear expectations with clients, and help prevent misunderstandings or disputes. They outline the scope of your services, payment terms, confidentiality, and limitations of your expertise.

2. What are some key considerations when choosing a business structure (e.g., sole proprietorship, LLC) for your practice?

Key considerations include liability protection, tax implications, administrative complexity, and long-term business goals. An LLC, for example, offers personal liability protection but involves more paperwork than a sole proprietorship.

3. How can you address a potential client's concern that your legal and compliance package is too expensive?

Emphasize the value of the package in terms of risk mitigation, time savings, and peace of mind. Highlight the potential costs of non-compliance, such as fines or lawsuits. Offer payment plans to make it more affordable. Share testimonials from other practitioners who have benefited from your services.
