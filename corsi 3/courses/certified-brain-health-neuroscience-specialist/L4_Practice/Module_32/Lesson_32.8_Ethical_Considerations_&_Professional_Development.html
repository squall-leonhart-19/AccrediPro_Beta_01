Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I understand you're experiencing brain fog and memory issues. I'm Sarah, and I help women in their 40s and 50s regain their cognitive clarity and feel like themselves again. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me on Facebook. What specifically caught your eye about my offer?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Can you tell me more about the brain fog? When did it start, and how does it impact your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address this? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and eliminate the brain fog, what would that allow you to do that you can't do now?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing significant cognitive challenges related to perimenopause, and you're looking for a natural, effective solution. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because we focus on the root causes of your brain fog, not just masking the symptoms. We'll use targeted nutrition, lifestyle adjustments, and specific brain-training exercises to optimize your cognitive function. I've seen amazing results with women just like you."
YOU:
"My signature program is 12 weeks, one-on-one, completely customized to you. It includes weekly coaching calls, personalized protocols, and unlimited email support."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this program could be the solution you've been looking for?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals and moms. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. We can work together to create a plan that works for you. Plus, think about the time you're wasting now because of the brain fog. What if we could reclaim that time?"
"It's a lot of money."
"I hear you. Investing in your brain health is an investment in your overall well-being and future. Think about the cost of continuing to struggle with brain fog: lost productivity, strained relationships, and decreased quality of life. This program is designed to provide lasting results. Plus, I offer payment plans to make it more accessible."
"Will this really work for me?"
"That's a valid question. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can tell you that I've helped many women in their 50s overcome similar challenges. My program is based on proven neuroscience principles and personalized to your unique needs. I'm confident that if you commit to the process, you'll see significant improvements. And I'll be there to support you every step of the way."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision. Is it the financial investment, the time commitment, or something else?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the investment as a cost savings compared to the ongoing costs of not addressing their problem. Highlight the value and ROI of your program.

3. What is the purpose of practicing your pricing presentation?

Practicing your pricing helps you deliver it with confidence and conviction, making potential clients more likely to invest in your services.
