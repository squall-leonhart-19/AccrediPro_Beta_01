You Belong Here

Consider this:

  • A 2021 study in the *Journal of Applied Gerontology* found that older adults reported higher levels of trust and connection with healthcare providers who were closer to their own age.

Your Cohort & Community

One of the most valuable aspects of this program is the community you'll be joining. You'll connect with fellow students from diverse backgrounds, all united by a shared passion for brain health.

This is your safe space to ask questions, share challenges, celebrate successes, and build lasting relationships. Lean on each other, support each other, and learn from each other. Remember, you're not alone on this journey.

We'll have:

  • Live Q&A Sessions: Get your questions answered by Sarah and guest experts.
  • Peer Coaching Groups: Practice your skills and receive feedback in a supportive environment.
  • Online Forum: Connect with classmates, share resources, and stay updated on the latest research.

Research consistently shows that social support is crucial for success in any learning endeavor. A 2018 meta-analysis in *PLoS One* (n=28,457) found that students who participated in online learning communities reported higher levels of satisfaction and academic achievement.

Don't be afraid to reach out, ask for help, and offer your own expertise. Together, we'll create a vibrant and supportive community that empowers each of us to thrive.
