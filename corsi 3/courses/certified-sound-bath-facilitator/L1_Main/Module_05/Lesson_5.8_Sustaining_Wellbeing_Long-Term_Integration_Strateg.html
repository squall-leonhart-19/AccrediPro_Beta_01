Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to focus on small, manageable steps when coaching a client struggling with burnout?

Starting with small steps prevents overwhelm and increases the likelihood of the client sticking to the plan. It's about creating sustainable habits, not drastic changes.

2. What are three key areas you can explore to help a client cultivate wellbeing?

Sensory experiences, mindful movement, and connection are three powerful areas to explore. These can be incorporated into small, daily practices.

Key Takeaways

  • Validate the client's feelings and experiences. Show empathy and understanding.
  • Focus on small, sustainable actions rather than overwhelming changes.
  • Explore sensory experiences, mindful movement, and connection as pathways to wellbeing.
  • Help the client identify activities that used to bring them joy.
  • Encourage self-compassion and kindness throughout the process.
