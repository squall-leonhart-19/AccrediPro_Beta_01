Gong Size, Material, and Frequency Range

The physical characteristics of a gong significantly influence its sonic properties. Understanding these factors allows you to select the right gong for specific therapeutic goals:

  • Size: Larger gongs generally produce lower frequencies and longer sustain, creating a deeper, more grounding experience. Smaller gongs offer higher frequencies and quicker decay, which can be more stimulating and uplifting.
  • Material: The alloy used in the gong's construction affects its timbre and resonance. Bronze gongs tend to have a warm, complex sound, while nickel-silver gongs are brighter and more focused.
  • Frequency Range: Each gong has a unique frequency range, which can be measured using a spectrum analyzer. Matching the gong's frequency to specific binaural beats or isochronic tones can enhance its therapeutic effects.
