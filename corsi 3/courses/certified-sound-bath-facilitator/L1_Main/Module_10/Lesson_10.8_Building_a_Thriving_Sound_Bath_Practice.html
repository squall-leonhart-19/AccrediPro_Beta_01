Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate Maria's feelings at the beginning of the session?

Validating her feelings helps build trust and rapport, making her feel heard and understood.

2. How can you set realistic expectations for sound bath sessions with Maria?

Explain that sound baths are a tool for relaxation and stress reduction, not a cure-all, and that individual results may vary.

Key Takeaways

  • Acknowledge and validate the client's feelings to build trust.
  • Position sound baths as a supportive tool for well-being.
  • Set realistic expectations for the benefits of sound baths.
  • Address any concerns or questions the client may have.
  • Focus on helping the client reconnect with their inner self.
