Key Takeaways

  • Building rapport is crucial, especially with HR managers who need to justify their wellness investments.
  • Understanding the specific pain points of the company and its employees allows you to tailor your pitch effectively.
  • Addressing budget concerns with flexible options and ROI data can help overcome financial objections.
  • Providing a clear call-to-action, such as scheduling a free introductory session, encourages prospects to take the next step.
