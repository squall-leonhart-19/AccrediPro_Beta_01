Handling Objections Like a Pro

Common Objections and Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if it will work for me."
"I understand your hesitation. Sound baths are a unique experience, and it's natural to be unsure. I offer a satisfaction guarantee ‚Äì if you don't feel a difference after your first session, I'll refund your money. How about you try it and see for yourself?"
"It's too expensive."
"I appreciate that you're mindful of your budget, Susan. Let's break down the value. My program is an investment in your wellbeing, leading to reduced stress, better sleep, and increased productivity. Think about how much you're already spending on temporary fixes like sleep aids or stress-related health issues. My clients often find that the long-term benefits outweigh the cost."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically would you like to think about? Is it the time commitment, the financial investment, or something else? I want to make sure I've addressed all your concerns."
"I don't have time."
"I understand that time is precious. What if you dedicated just one hour a week to deep relaxation and stress reduction? My clients often find that by prioritizing their wellbeing, they actually become MORE productive and efficient in other areas of their lives. Plus, I offer flexible scheduling options, including evening and weekend sessions."
