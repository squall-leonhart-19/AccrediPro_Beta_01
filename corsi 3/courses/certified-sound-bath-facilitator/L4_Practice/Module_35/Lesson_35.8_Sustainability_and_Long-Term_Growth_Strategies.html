Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Framework

Phase 1: Connection & Intention 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call! I'm Sarah. I'm excited to learn more about what brought you here. To start, can you tell me a bit about what you're hoping to achieve?"
YOU:
"Great. I want to make sure this call is valuable for you. We'll explore your stress levels, how sound baths can help, and if we're a good fit to work together. How does that sound?"
Phase 2: Exploring the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"So, Susan, you mentioned stress. Can you describe what that feels like in your body? What are your biggest stress triggers?"
YOU:
"How long has this been going on? And what have you tried so far to manage your stress? What's been effective, and what hasn't?"
YOU:
"And what's the impact of this stress on your daily life? On your sleep, your relationships, your work?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her pain points]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"Sound baths can be a powerful tool for stress relief. The vibrations help to calm the nervous system, reduce anxiety, and promote deep relaxation. Many of my clients report sleeping better, feeling calmer, and having more energy after just a few sessions."
YOU:
"My program is designed to [explain key benefits and features of your offer]. We'll work together to create a personalized plan that addresses your specific needs and goals."
Phase 4: Invitation to Work Together 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like something that resonates with you? Do you see yourself benefiting from this?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me share the details of the program and how we can get started. I have a few spots available in the next month."

Objection Handling: Be Prepared

Navigating Common Concerns

"I'm not sure if sound baths are really for me."
"I understand your skepticism. Many people are new to sound baths. I offer a trial session. You can experience it firsthand and see if it resonates with you. How does that sound?"
"It's a lot of money."
"I understand that it's an investment. Let's break it down. Consider what you're currently spending on stress relief ‚Äì massages, supplements, sleepless nights. This program is designed to provide lasting results, not just temporary fixes. The value you receive in terms of reduced stress, improved sleep, and increased energy far outweighs the cost."
"I don't have the time."
"I hear you. Time is precious. The sessions are designed to be deeply relaxing and efficient. Many clients find that the time they invest in sound baths actually *creates* more time in their lives by increasing their focus and productivity. We can also work with your schedule to find times that work best for you."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes (because everyone is different), I can guarantee my commitment to providing you with the highest quality care and support. Most of my clients experience significant improvements in their stress levels, sleep, and overall wellbeing. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with the program after [specify timeframe], I'll [specify refund or other offer]."
