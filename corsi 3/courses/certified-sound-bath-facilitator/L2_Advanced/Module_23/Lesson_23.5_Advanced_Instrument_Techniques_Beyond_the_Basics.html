Integrating Less Common Instruments

Expand your sonic palette by incorporating less common instruments like the monochord, didgeridoo, or shruti box. Each instrument offers unique tonal qualities and resonant properties. The monochord, with its multiple strings tuned to the same note, creates a rich, drone-like sound that can induce deep relaxation. The didgeridoo's circular breathing technique produces a continuous, grounding vibration. The shruti box provides a harmonium-like drone that supports vocal toning and meditation. These instruments enhance the "C" (Cultivate Space) stage of the S.O.N.I.C. Frameworkâ„¢, creating a unique environment for healing.
