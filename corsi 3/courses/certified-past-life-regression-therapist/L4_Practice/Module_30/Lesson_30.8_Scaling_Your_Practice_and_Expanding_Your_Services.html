Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Time is valuable. This program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. How much time per week are you realistically willing to dedicate to finding your new path?"
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. I have a strong track record of success with clients like you. I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not happy with the progress after the first 30 days, I will refund your money."
"I need to research other options."
"I encourage you to do your research. What specific information are you looking for? Perhaps I can provide it to you directly and save you some time?"
"I don't know if I believe in past life regression."
"That's perfectly fine! Many of my clients are skeptical at first. We can focus on other techniques that help you gain clarity. The past life regression is simply another tool to help you uncover what is already within you."

Case Study

Case Study: Sarah, 35, Unfulfilled Marketing Manager

Presenting Problem: Sarah felt trapped in her marketing job, experiencing burnout and a lack of purpose. She had tried various self-help books and online courses, but nothing provided lasting clarity.

Intervention: Sarah enrolled in a 90-day Career Clarity Program. The intervention included past life regression, strengths assessments, and values clarification exercises. She also received weekly coaching calls.

Outcomes: Within the first month, Sarah identified her core values and discovered a passion for environmental sustainability. By the end of the program, she had transitioned into a marketing role at a non-profit organization focused on conservation. Sarah reported feeling more fulfilled, energized, and aligned with her purpose. She also received a 15% salary increase in her new role.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured sales process is crucial for converting potential clients.
  • Empathy and active listening are essential for building trust and understanding client needs.
  • Addressing objections effectively can overcome barriers to enrollment.
  • Confidently presenting your pricing demonstrates your belief in the value of your services.
  • Consistent effort and a clear call to action are vital for achieving your income goals.
