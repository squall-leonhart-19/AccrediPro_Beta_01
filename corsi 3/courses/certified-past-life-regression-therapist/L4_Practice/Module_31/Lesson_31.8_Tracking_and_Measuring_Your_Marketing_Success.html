Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Your Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. I'm Sarah, and I'm a Certified Past Life Regression Therapist. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"I'm glad you found my website. What specifically resonated with you and made you want to book this call?"
Phase 2: Uncover Challenges & Desires 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling stuck and struggling with anxiety. Can you tell me more about that? When did this start, and how does it affect your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these challenges? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and resolve this anxiety and feeling of being stuck, what would your life look like? What would you be doing differently?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, from what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her pain points]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"Past Life Regression can help you uncover the root causes of your anxiety and feeling stuck by exploring past experiences that may be influencing your present life. Through a series of sessions, we'll work together to identify patterns, heal emotional wounds, and empower you to move forward with clarity and confidence."
YOU:
"My program is designed to guide you through this process safely and effectively. We'll meet weekly for 90-minute sessions over three months. I also provide email support between sessions to answer any questions and offer guidance."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this approach resonates with you and could potentially help you achieve your goals?"
YOU:
"Great! Let's talk about the investment and how we can get started."

Handling Objections with Grace

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I believe in Past Life Regression."
"I understand your skepticism. It's natural to be curious about something unfamiliar. While belief isn't a prerequisite for the therapy to be effective, I can share some client testimonials and research that demonstrate the positive impact it can have on people's lives. Ultimately, it's about whether you're open to exploring new possibilities for healing."
"It's more expensive than I expected."
"I appreciate your honesty. Let's break down the value you're receiving. My program includes [list inclusions: e.g., weekly 90-minute sessions, personalized guidance, email support, access to resources]. Many of my clients find that the investment pays for itself through reduced anxiety, improved relationships, and a greater sense of purpose. We can also discuss payment plan options to make it more manageable."
"I need to think about it."
"I completely understand. This is an important decision. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need. Perhaps we can schedule a brief follow-up call to address any remaining questions?"
"I'm worried it won't work for me."
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can share that many of my clients have experienced significant positive changes in their lives through Past Life Regression. I'm committed to providing you with the best possible support and guidance throughout the program. If, after the first few sessions, you don't feel like it's a good fit, we can discuss alternative options."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call for Past Life Regression therapy?

Building rapport, uncovering challenges and desires, presenting your solution, and closing with a clear call to action.

2. How should you respond to a prospect who says, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, ask what specifically they need to consider, and offer a follow-up call to address any remaining questions.

3. What are some realistic income goals you can set for your Past Life Regression practice?

Starting with 2 clients per month ($5,000) and gradually increasing to 5-10 clients per month ($12,500 - $25,000) is a realistic goal.

Key Takeaways

  • Master the art of the discovery call to effectively connect with potential clients.
  • Practice handling common objections with confidence and empathy.
  • Present your pricing clearly and confidently, highlighting the value you provide.
  • Set realistic income goals and track your progress to stay motivated.
  • Remember that client acquisition is a skill that improves with practice and dedication.
