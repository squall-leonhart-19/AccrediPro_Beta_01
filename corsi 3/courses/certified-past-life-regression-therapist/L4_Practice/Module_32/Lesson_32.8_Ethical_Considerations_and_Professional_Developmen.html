Discovery Call Script: A 30-Minute Journey

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, thank you so much for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about you. I saw you found me on Instagram - what resonated with you?"
YOU:
"Tell me, Sarah, what prompted you to reach out now? What's been on your mind?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned anxiety. Can you describe what that feels like for you? When does it typically surface?"
YOU:
"And how has this anxiety impacted your daily life, your relationships, your sense of self?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and eliminate this anxiety, what would your life look like? What would you be doing differently?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Sarah, what I'm hearing is that you're experiencing [summarize her pain points]. Does that resonate with you?"
YOU:
"My approach to past life regression is unique because [explain your methodology and what makes it effective]. I guide you through a journey to uncover the root causes of your anxiety, empowering you to release those patterns and reconnect with your authentic self. Many of my clients experience a profound sense of peace and clarity within just a few sessions."
YOU:
"This isn't just about managing anxiety; it's about reclaiming your power and living a more fulfilling life."
Phase 4: Confident Close (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Sarah, based on what you've shared, I truly believe I can help you. Does this sound like the support you've been searching for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me share with you how my program works and how we can get you started on this transformative journey."

Handling Objections: Turning "No" into "Yes"

Common Concerns & Your Empathetic Responses

"I'm not sure if I believe in past lives."
"That's perfectly okay. Many of my clients are skeptical at first. Think of it as a powerful metaphor for understanding your subconscious patterns. The goal is to heal, regardless of whether you believe in past lives literally."
"I'm worried I won't be able to be hypnotized."
"Hypnosis is a very natural state, like daydreaming. Everyone can be hypnotized to some degree. I'll guide you gently, and we'll work at your pace. It's about relaxation and focused attention, not mind control."
"It's a big investment. I need to be sure it will work."
"I understand. You're investing in yourself, and that's a big decision. I offer a free consultation to ensure we're a good fit. I also have testimonials from other women who have transformed their lives through this work. Ultimately, it comes down to whether you're ready to commit to your own healing."
"I need to talk to my partner about it."
"Of course. Would it be helpful if I sent you some information to share with them? Or perhaps a brief call together to answer any questions they may have? I want you both to feel comfortable and confident in this decision."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: 1) Connection & Rapport, 2) Deep Dive into the Problem, 3) Presenting Your Solution, and 4) Confident Close.

2. How should you handle the objection, "I'm not sure if I believe in past lives?"

Acknowledge their skepticism and reframe it as a powerful metaphor for understanding subconscious patterns, emphasizing that the goal is healing regardless of literal belief.

3. What's a good closing line to use at the end of a discovery call?

"I truly believe this work can help you reclaim your joy and confidence. Are you ready to embark on this journey with me?"

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is crucial for building a sustainable practice.
  • A well-structured discovery call helps you connect with potential clients and understand their needs.
  • Handling objections with empathy and confidence can turn skepticism into commitment.
  • Presenting your pricing clearly and confidently demonstrates your value.
  • Consistent client acquisition efforts lead to a steady stream of income and a thriving practice.
