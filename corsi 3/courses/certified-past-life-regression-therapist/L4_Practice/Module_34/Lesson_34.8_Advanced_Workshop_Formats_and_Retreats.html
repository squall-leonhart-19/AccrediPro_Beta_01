Consultation Call Script

30-Minute Consultation Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for your interest in the 'Past Life Solutions for Modern Anxiety' program! I'm excited to chat with you. To start, what resonated with you about the program description?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful. I designed it to help people just like you. What are your biggest challenges with anxiety right now?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Anxiety 10 min
YOU:
"Can you describe what a typical anxiety episode feels like for you? What triggers it?"
YOU:
"What have you tried to manage your anxiety in the past? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"If your anxiety disappeared, what would be possible for you in your life?"
Phase 3: Program Overview & Benefits 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like anxiety is preventing you from fully enjoying your retirement. Is that fair to say?"
YOU:
"This group program is designed to address the root causes of anxiety by exploring past lives. We'll meet weekly for 90 minutes, and you'll receive personalized guidance, support, and tools to manage your anxiety. Plus, you'll be part of a supportive community. Participants often report feeling calmer, more connected, and empowered within just a few weeks."
Phase 4: Enrollment & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this group program sound like a good fit for what you're looking for? ... Great! Let me tell you about the investment and how to secure your spot."

Handling Objections

Common Concerns & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I believe in past lives."
"That's perfectly okay! You don't need to believe in past lives for this to be effective. Many participants come in skeptical, but they find the techniques helpful for accessing subconscious patterns and emotions, regardless of their beliefs. It‚Äôs more about the therapeutic process."
"I'm worried I won't be comfortable sharing in a group setting."
"I understand. It can be intimidating. The group is a safe and supportive space, and you're welcome to share only what feels comfortable for you. There's no pressure to disclose anything you're not ready for. Plus, many people find the group dynamic to be incredibly healing and empowering."
"I'm concerned about the cost."
"I appreciate your honesty. While the program is an investment, consider the value you'll receive in terms of reduced anxiety, increased well-being, and a supportive community. Think of the money you might be spending on other things to manage anxiety that aren't truly solving the problem. We also have a payment plan available to make it more accessible."
"I need to talk to my family about it first."
"That's completely understandable. Would it be helpful if I provided you with a program overview and some testimonials from past participants that you could share with them? I'm also happy to answer any questions they might have."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the most important element of a consultation call for a group program?

Building rapport and understanding the prospect's specific needs and challenges is crucial for demonstrating how the group program can help them.

2. How can you address a potential client's skepticism about past lives during a consultation call?

Emphasize that belief in past lives is not required for the program to be effective. Focus on the therapeutic techniques and the benefits of accessing subconscious patterns.

Key Takeaways

  • Prepare a structured consultation call script to guide the conversation.
  • Anticipate and address common objections with confident and empathetic responses.
  • Clearly communicate the value and benefits of the group program.
  • Present pricing with confidence and offer flexible payment options.
  • Practice your closing lines and be ready to invite prospects to join the program.
