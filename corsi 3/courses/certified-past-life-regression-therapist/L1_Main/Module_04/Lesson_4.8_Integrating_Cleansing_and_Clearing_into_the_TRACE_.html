Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on cleansing and clearing. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Diane, thank you for sharing your experiences with me. It sounds like you've been carrying a lot, and it's completely understandable that you're feeling stuck. Many of us hold onto energetic residue from past experiences, especially in spaces we associate with those times."
DIANE:
"Yes, exactly! I feel like my house is filled with the memories, and not the good ones. I've tried rearranging things, but it doesn't really help."
YOU:
"Rearranging can be helpful, but sometimes we need to go deeper and address the energetic imprints. We're going to explore some cleansing and clearing techniques that can help you release that old energy and create a fresh start in your home and in your life."
YOU:
"Does the idea of energetically cleansing your space resonate with you? Have you tried anything like that before?"
DIANE:
"I've heard of smudging, but I don't really know where to start. And honestly, I'm a little skeptical."
YOU:
"That's perfectly okay to feel that way. We can approach this in a way that feels comfortable and aligned with your beliefs. We'll focus on the intention behind the practice, rather than just the ritual itself. How does that sound?"

Once you've practiced the script, modify it to sound like YOU. Keep the structure, but use your own words. For example, instead of "energetic imprints," you might say "emotional echoes."

You'll Know It Worked When...

  • Diane expresses a willingness to try something new.
  • She identifies specific areas in her home that feel particularly "heavy."
  • She starts to connect her external environment to her internal feelings.
  • She feels empowered to take action, even if it's a small step.
  • She says, "I feel like I can actually do this."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the most important thing to validate with a client who is skeptical of cleansing rituals?

Their feelings and beliefs. Acknowledge that skepticism is normal and that there are many ways to approach cleansing and clearing.

2. What are three different methods of energetic cleansing you could suggest to a client?

Smudging with sage, sound clearing with singing bowls, intentional decluttering, salt cleansing, or sunlight exposure.

Key Takeaways

  • Cleansing and clearing involves releasing stagnant energy from spaces and lives.
  • Address skepticism with empathy and offer choices.
  • Link external cleansing to internal emotional release.
  • Focus on intention, not just ritual.
  • Empower clients to create a positive, peaceful environment.
