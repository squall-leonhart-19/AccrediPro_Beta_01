Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of using the Affect Bridge technique?

To help the client access the root cause of an emotion by focusing on the feeling itself.

2. Why is it important to avoid "leading" the client during the regression?

Leading the client can contaminate the process and prevent them from accessing their own authentic memories and experiences.

Key Takeaways

  • The Affect Bridge technique uses emotions as a pathway to past experiences.
  • It's crucial to validate the client's feelings without judgment.
  • Avoid leading questions and allow the client's subconscious to guide the process.
  • Practice creating a safe and supportive environment for deep emotional work.
