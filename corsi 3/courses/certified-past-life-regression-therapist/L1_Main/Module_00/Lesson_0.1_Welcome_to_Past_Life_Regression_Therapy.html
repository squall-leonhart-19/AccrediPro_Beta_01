Case Study: Maria, 52

Client: Maria, a 52-year-old woman, presented with chronic anxiety and a feeling of being "stuck" in her life. She had tried various therapies without lasting relief.

Intervention: Maria underwent a series of three past life regression sessions. In her first session, she experienced a past life as a soldier during wartime, witnessing intense trauma and loss. In subsequent sessions, she explored themes of self-sacrifice and unresolved grief.

Outcomes: After the sessions, Maria reported a significant reduction in anxiety levels. She gained a deeper understanding of the root causes of her fears and developed coping mechanisms to manage her emotions. She also reported feeling more empowered to make positive changes in her life. Follow-up assessments at 3 and 6 months confirmed sustained improvements in her mental well-being.
