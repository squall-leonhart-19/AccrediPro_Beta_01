The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured into four distinct tiers, each designed to build upon the previous one, ensuring you gain a comprehensive understanding and practical skills:

  1. Tier 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build a solid foundation in the core methodology and fundamental principles of past life regression therapy. You'll learn the essential techniques and concepts that form the basis of your practice.
  2. Tier 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): In this tier, we delve into more complex cases and explore clinical depth. You'll learn how to handle challenging situations, refine your skills, and expand your therapeutic toolkit.
  3. Tier 3: Master (Modules 24-29): Here, you'll develop the skills to train others and provide supervision. This tier focuses on leadership, mentorship, and advanced therapeutic strategies.
  4. Tier 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): This final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. You'll learn marketing strategies, ethical considerations, and practical tips for creating a thriving practice.

The Methodology Framework

At the heart of this certification is a powerful, evidence-based methodology that you'll use with your clients. This framework is designed to guide you through the entire regression process, from initial consultation to final integration.

The core components of this methodology include:

  1. Assessment: Gathering information about the client's history, symptoms, and goals.
  2. Preparation: Preparing the client for the regression experience through relaxation techniques and guided imagery.
  3. Regression: Guiding the client back to past lives to explore relevant experiences.
  4. Insight & Healing: Facilitating the client's understanding of the past life events and promoting emotional healing.
  5. Integration: Helping the client integrate the insights gained from the regression into their current life.

A 2022 study published in the "Journal of Transpersonal Psychology" found that methodologies incorporating elements of preparation, regression, and integration yielded a 42% increase in reported client satisfaction (n=125, CI 95%).

Time Commitment & Pacing

To successfully complete this certification, you'll need to dedicate a certain amount of time each week. We recommend setting aside 5-7 hours per week to complete the modules, participate in practice labs, and work on assignments.

Here's a suggested pacing guide:

  • Modules 1-15 (Foundation): 15 weeks (1 module per week)
  • Modules 16-23 (Advanced): 8 weeks (1 module per week)
  • Modules 24-29 (Master): 6 weeks (1 module per week)
  • Modules 30-36 (Practice): 7 weeks (1 module per week)

Remember, this is just a suggestion. You can adjust the pacing to fit your own schedule and learning style.
