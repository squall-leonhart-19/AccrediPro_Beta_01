4. Case Study: Career Direction

Case Study: Sarah - Finding Career Clarity

Client: Sarah, 34 years old

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah felt deeply unfulfilled in her current accounting job. She experienced chronic boredom, lack of motivation, and a sense that she was not using her true potential. She sought PLR to gain clarity on her career path.

Intervention: During the PLR session, Sarah accessed a past life where she was a renowned architect, designing beautiful and innovative buildings. She felt a deep sense of passion and fulfillment in this role. She also connected with a spirit guide who encouraged her to explore her creative talents in her current life.

Outcomes: Inspired by her past life experience, Sarah enrolled in evening classes in interior design. She discovered a hidden talent for spatial planning and a deep love for creating beautiful spaces. Within two years, she transitioned to a career as an interior designer, where she felt much more fulfilled and engaged. Sarah reported a significant improvement in her overall well-being and a greater sense of purpose.
