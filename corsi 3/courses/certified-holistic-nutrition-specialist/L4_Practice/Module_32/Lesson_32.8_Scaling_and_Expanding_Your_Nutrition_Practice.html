Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about your health goals. To make the best use of our time, I'd like to spend a few minutes understanding what's been going on, then share how I can help, and finally, discuss next steps. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"I saw on your intake form that you're experiencing hot flashes and mood swings. Can you tell me more about when these started and how they're impacting your day-to-day life?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Her Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned weight gain. Have you noticed any changes in your diet or exercise routine?"
YOU:
"And what about your sleep? Are you finding it difficult to fall asleep or stay asleep?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? What worked, and what didn't? What did the doctors recommend?"
Phase 3: Present Your Holistic Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're experiencing a complex interplay of hormonal imbalances, sleep disturbances, and weight fluctuations. My approach focuses on identifying the root causes of these issues through comprehensive testing and a personalized nutrition plan."
YOU:
"Unlike conventional medicine, which often focuses on symptom management, I take a holistic approach that considers your diet, lifestyle, stress levels, and environmental factors. I use targeted testing to identify specific nutrient deficiencies, hormonal imbalances, and gut health issues that may be contributing to your symptoms."
YOU:
"My 12-week program provides you with one-on-one support, personalized meal plans, supplement recommendations, and lifestyle modifications to help you restore hormonal balance, improve sleep, and achieve sustainable weight loss. Many of my clients, like Maria, a 48-year-old teacher, report significant improvements in their symptoms within the first few weeks. Maria lost 15 pounds and reported a 70% reduction in hot flashes after just 6 weeks."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, Susan, I'm confident that my program can help you achieve your health goals. Does this sound like the type of support you've been looking for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let's discuss the program in more detail and see if it's a good fit for you. I have two options available..."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time commitment."
"I understand. It's important to prioritize your time. The program requires a few hours per week for meal prep, exercise, and our coaching calls. However, many of my clients find that investing in their health actually *increases* their productivity and energy levels. What if, by prioritizing this now, you could reclaim those lost hours due to fatigue and brain fog?"
"I'm concerned about the cost."
"I appreciate your honesty. Can I ask, how much have you already spent on supplements, doctor visits, and other remedies that haven't worked? This program is an investment in a long-term solution, not just a quick fix. Plus, I offer flexible payment plans to make it more accessible. Let's explore those options together."
"I need to think about it and do more research."
"I respect that. What specific information are you looking for? Perhaps I can provide you with some additional resources or testimonials from past clients. I'm also happy to answer any further questions you may have. What would make you feel most comfortable moving forward?"
"My husband/partner handles the finances, and I need to discuss it with him first."
"That's perfectly fine. Would it be helpful if I sent you a detailed program outline and pricing information that you can share with him? Or, perhaps we could schedule a brief call together so he can ask me any questions directly? I want to make sure you both feel confident in this decision."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four key phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport & Set Agenda, Deep Dive into Her Challenges, Present Your Holistic Approach, and Close & Next Steps.

2. How should you respond to the objection "I'm concerned about the cost"?

Acknowledge their concern, ask about previous spending on ineffective remedies, emphasize the long-term investment, and offer flexible payment plans.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for converting prospects into paying clients.
  • Building rapport and understanding the client's challenges are essential for establishing trust.
  • Presenting your unique value proposition and confidently stating your pricing are key to closing the sale
  • Handling objections with empathy and providing solutions can overcome potential roadblocks.
  • Focus on the client's desired outcomes and the transformation your program offers.
