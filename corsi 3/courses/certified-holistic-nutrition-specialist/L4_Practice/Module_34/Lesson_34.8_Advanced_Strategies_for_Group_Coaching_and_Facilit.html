Discovery Call Script: Group Program Enrollment

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Recap 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, it's so great to connect with you! Thanks for joining my workshop. What was your biggest takeaway from the session?"
YOU:
"That's fantastic! I'm glad you found it valuable. Today, I want to understand your goals better and see if my group program, "Vitality After 50," is the right fit for you."
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Needs 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling sluggish. Can you tell me more about that? How does it impact your daily life?"
YOU:
"And what does 'healthy weight' mean to you? What have you tried in the past to achieve that?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, how committed are you to making a change in the next 90 days?"
Phase 3: Present the Group Program 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I believe "Vitality After 50" can be a game-changer for you. It's a 12-week program designed specifically for women like you who want to boost energy, manage weight, and navigate the complexities of aging with confidence."
YOU:
"In the program, you'll receive weekly group coaching calls, personalized meal plans, access to a private online community for support, and monthly one-on-one sessions with me. We focus on sustainable lifestyle changes, not quick fixes. In fact, 85% of our participants report increased energy levels within the first month."
YOU:
"What questions do you have about the program?"
Phase 4: Invitation & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on our conversation, I truly believe this program can help you achieve your goals. Are you ready to join "Vitality After 50" and start feeling your best?"
YOU:
"Great! To secure your spot, I just need to take your payment information. We can do that now, or I can send you a secure link to complete the enrollment process. Which would you prefer?"

"I'm worried I won't have time to commit."

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering the sales script is crucial for converting leads into paying clients.
  • Address objections with empathy and provide value-driven solutions.
