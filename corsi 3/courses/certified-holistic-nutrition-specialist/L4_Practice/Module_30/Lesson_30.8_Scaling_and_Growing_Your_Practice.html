Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for joining me today! I'm Sarah, and I'm thrilled to connect. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"Great! So, the purpose of this call is to understand your health goals and see if my program is the right fit for you. Does that sound good?"
YOU:
"I also want to let you know at the end of our call, if it feels like a good fit, I'll share how we can work together. Does that sound fair?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Susan's Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, I saw you attended my workshop on hormone harmony. What resonated with you the most?"
YOU:
"Tell me more about the symptoms you're experiencing. What's been the most challenging?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to manage these symptoms? What has worked, and what hasn't?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being 'completely in control' and 1 being 'totally overwhelmed,' where would you rate your current health?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and feel amazing again, what would that look like for you?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach & Expertise 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, thank you for sharing all of that. I understand how frustrating it can be to feel like your body is betraying you."
YOU:
"Based on what you've told me, it sounds like you're experiencing a common set of challenges related to hormonal shifts. My approach is to address the root causes of these imbalances through personalized nutrition, lifestyle adjustments, and targeted supplementation."
YOU:
"Unlike conventional approaches that often focus on symptom management, I take a holistic approach, considering your unique biochemistry, lifestyle, and emotional well-being."
YOU:
"I've helped many women in their 40s and 50s navigate this transition and regain their vitality. For example, I had a client, Maria, who was experiencing similar symptoms. After working together for 12 weeks, she reported a 70% reduction in hot flashes, improved sleep, and increased energy levels."
Phase 4: Transition to Offer 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach resonate with you? Do you see how this could be different from what you've tried before?"
YOU:
"Great! I'd love to share more about my 12-week "Hormone Harmony" program and how we can work together to achieve your health goals."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand that investing in your health is a big decision. Let's break down the value you'll receive in the program. It includes personalized nutrition plans, one-on-one coaching sessions, access to exclusive resources, and ongoing support. Many of my clients find that they actually save money by reducing doctor visits and unnecessary supplements. What part of the investment is most concerning?"
"I need to think about it and do some research."
"I respect that. What specific information are you looking for? I'm happy to provide any additional details or resources you need to make an informed decision. Also, thinking about it, what would you need to know to say yes today?"
"I'm worried I won't have the time to commit to the program."
"I understand that time is valuable. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. We can work together to create a schedule that works for you, and the results are worth it! Remember, you can space the 12 weeks out for a longer duration. What would make you feel more comfortable about the time commitment?"
"I've tried so many things before, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. It sounds like you've been on a long journey. The difference with my approach is that we focus on identifying and addressing the root causes of your imbalances, rather than just masking the symptoms. We'll work together to create a personalized plan that's tailored to your unique needs. Plus, I'll be there to support you every step of the way. Ready to try something different?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of building rapport during a discovery call?

Building rapport involves active listening, empathy, sharing personal stories, and identifying common ground. Make sure to personalize your approach to each prospect.

2. How do you handle the "I need to think about it" objection?

Acknowledge their need for time, then ask clarifying questions to uncover the specific concerns. Offer additional resources or schedule a follow-up call to address their doubts.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for converting prospects into clients.
  • Building rapport through active listening and empathy fosters trust.
  • Addressing objections with confidence and providing value demonstrates your expertise.
  • Clearly presenting your pricing and call-to-action increases conversion rates.
  • Believe in your value and the transformative impact you can have on your clients' lives.
