You Belong Here

Maybe you're thinking, "I'm not a doctor or a registered dietician. Can I really do this?" The answer is a resounding YES! Your life experience, your passion for health, and your empathy are your greatest assets. In fact, many of the most successful functional medicine practitioners come from non-traditional backgrounds.

Consider these points:

  • Empathy & Connection: You've likely faced your own health challenges or supported loved ones through theirs. This gives you a unique ability to connect with clients on a deeper level and understand their struggles.
  • Real-World Experience: You bring a wealth of knowledge from your own life, including cooking, budgeting, time management, and stress management. These are all essential skills for helping clients make lasting lifestyle changes.
  • Fresh Perspective: You're not bound by the limitations of conventional medicine. You're open to new ideas, innovative approaches, and the power of holistic healing.

A 2022 study published in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine found that health coaches with lived experience were significantly more effective at promoting behavior change than those without (OR = 2.45, 95% CI: 1.88-3.21). Your personal journey is your superpower.
