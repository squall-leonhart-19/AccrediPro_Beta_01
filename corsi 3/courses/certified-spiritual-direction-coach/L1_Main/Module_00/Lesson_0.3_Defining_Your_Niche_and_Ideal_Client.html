The 4-Tier Transformation

Our certification program is structured around a powerful 4-Tier framework, designed to take you from foundational knowledge to masterful practice. Each tier builds upon the previous one, ensuring a comprehensive and practical learning experience.

  1. Tier 1: FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build a solid understanding of the core methodology and fundamentals of spiritual direction coaching. You'll learn the essential principles, theories, and techniques that form the bedrock of your practice.
  2. Tier 2: ADVANCED (Modules 16-23): In this tier, we delve into more complex client cases and explore advanced coaching strategies. You'll gain a deeper clinical understanding and learn how to navigate challenging situations with confidence.
  3. Tier 3: MASTER (Modules 24-29): This tier focuses on developing your leadership and mentoring skills. You'll learn how to train others in spiritual direction coaching and develop the skills to supervise other practitioners.
  4. Tier 4: PRACTICE (Modules 30-36): The final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. You'll learn practical marketing strategies, business development techniques, and ethical considerations for establishing a thriving coaching practice.

The Methodology Framework

At the heart of our certification program lies The G.U.I.D.E. Frameworkâ„¢, a proprietary methodology designed to provide a structured and effective approach to spiritual direction coaching. G.U.I.D.E. stands for:

  • G: Grounding: Establishing a safe and supportive space for clients to explore their spirituality.
  • U: Understanding: Deeply understanding the client's unique beliefs, values, and experiences.
  • I: Illuminating: Helping clients gain insights into their spiritual journey and identify areas for growth.
  • D: Directing: Guiding clients towards their spiritual goals through powerful questions and supportive practices.
  • E: Empowering: Empowering clients to take ownership of their spiritual journey and live authentically.
