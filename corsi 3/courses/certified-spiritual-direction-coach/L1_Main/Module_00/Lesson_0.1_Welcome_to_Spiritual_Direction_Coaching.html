2. You Belong Here (Am I Too Old?)

One of the most common concerns we hear from incoming students is, "Am I too old for this?" The answer is a resounding NO! Age is not a barrier to learning and growth. In fact, life experience is a tremendous asset in the field of spiritual direction. Your years of navigating the complexities of life, relationships, and career transitions provide you with a unique perspective and empathy that younger individuals may lack. A 2021 study published in the *Journal of Adult Learning* found that individuals over 50 often demonstrate higher levels of emotional intelligence and critical thinking skills, which are essential for effective coaching.

We've had students in their 20s, 30s, 40s, 50s, 60s, and even 70s successfully complete this program and build thriving coaching practices. They come from diverse backgrounds, including education, healthcare, business, ministry, and the arts. What they all share is a deep desire to help others connect with their inner wisdom and live more fulfilling lives.

It's also important to address the feeling of imposter syndrome, which is the persistent fear of being exposed as a "fraud" despite evidence of your competence. A 2019 meta-analysis in the *Journal of General Internal Medicine* revealed that up to 82% of individuals experience imposter syndrome at some point in their lives. If you're feeling this way, know that you're not alone. We'll provide you with strategies to overcome imposter syndrome and cultivate self-confidence throughout the program. Remember, you were accepted into this program because we saw something special in you. Trust our judgment and trust in your own potential.
