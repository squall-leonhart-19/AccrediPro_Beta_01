Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to acknowledge the client's grief before suggesting solutions?

Acknowledging their pain builds trust and rapport. It shows you understand their experience and are not just trying to "fix" them.

2. How can you leverage the client's bond with their animal in a coaching session?

Explore activities they enjoy doing together, identify ways the animal provides comfort and support, and use the bond as motivation for positive change.
