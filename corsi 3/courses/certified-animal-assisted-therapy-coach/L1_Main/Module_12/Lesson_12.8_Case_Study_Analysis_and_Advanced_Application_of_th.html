Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Diane, it sounds like you're really missing that special bond with Buster. It's completely understandable to feel this way, especially given how much you two mean to each other. Many of my clients experience similar feelings when life gets busy. Let's explore some ways to reignite that connection."
DIANE:
"I just don't know what to do. I feel like I've tried everything."
YOU:
"I hear you. It's frustrating when the usual things don't work. Sometimes, a fresh perspective can make all the difference. We're going to look at this with new eyes, focusing on Buster's needs and how you can both benefit. How does that sound?"
YOU:
"I want to start by understanding Buster's daily routine. Can you walk me through a typical day for him ‚Äì from morning until bedtime? Don't worry about 'right' or 'wrong' answers; just give me the details. This will help us identify any subtle shifts in his behavior or environment that might be contributing to this disconnection."
DIANE:
"Okay...well, he usually wakes up with me around 6:30..."
YOU:
"Perfect. Let's start there. Pay attention to the small details, Diane. They often hold the key."
