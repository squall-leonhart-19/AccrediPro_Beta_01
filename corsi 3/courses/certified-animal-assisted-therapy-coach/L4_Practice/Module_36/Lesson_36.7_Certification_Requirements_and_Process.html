Continuing Education

To maintain your certification, you are required to complete 20 hours of continuing education every two years. This ensures that you stay up-to-date with the latest research and best practices in animal-assisted therapy coaching.

Continuing education activities may include:

  • Attending workshops and conferences.
  • Completing online courses and webinars.
  • Publishing articles or presenting at professional events.
  • Participating in research projects.

AccrediPro Academy offers a variety of continuing education opportunities to help you meet this requirement. Check our website for upcoming events and courses.

Insurance & Ethics

Maintaining professional liability insurance is a critical aspect of being a Certified Animal-Assisted Therapy Coachâ„¢. This insurance protects you in the event of a claim arising from your professional activities.

When selecting an insurance provider, ensure that the policy covers animal-assisted therapy coaching specifically. Many providers offer tailored policies for professionals in the animal-assisted therapy field. A typical policy may cost between $500 and $1,000 per year, depending on the coverage amount.

Adhering to the ethical guidelines outlined in Module 2 is also essential. These guidelines cover areas such as client confidentiality, informed consent, and avoiding conflicts of interest. Failure to comply with these ethical standards can result in disciplinary action, including revocation of your certification.
