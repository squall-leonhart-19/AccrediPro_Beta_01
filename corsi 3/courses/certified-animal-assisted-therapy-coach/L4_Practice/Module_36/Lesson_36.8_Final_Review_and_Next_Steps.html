Discovery Call Script

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during the first 5 minutes of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves expressing appreciation for their time, asking about their pet in a positive context, and understanding what prompted them to seek your services.

2. Describe an effective way to address the objection "It's more than I was expecting to spend."

Break down the program's components and value, highlight the long-term benefits, compare it to the cost of inaction, and offer payment plans to make it more accessible.
