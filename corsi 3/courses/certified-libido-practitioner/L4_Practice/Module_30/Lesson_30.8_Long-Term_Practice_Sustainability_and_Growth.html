Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to learn more about what you're looking for. I understand you saw my ad on Facebook. What caught your eye?"
YOU:
"Great! So, in the next 30 minutes, I'd like to hear about your situation, explain my approach, and see if we're a good fit. Does that sound good?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling 'blah' and having low energy. Can you tell me more about that? When did you start noticing these changes?"
YOU:
"How is this impacting your daily life and your relationships?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these issues?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being your ideal self, where would you rate your current energy and libido?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing common symptoms related to hormonal changes and decreased libido post-menopause. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My program is designed to address these issues by focusing on personalized nutrition, lifestyle adjustments, and targeted strategies to boost libido and energy. We'll work together for 12 weeks to create a plan that's tailored to your unique needs. A 2023 study published in the *Journal of Sexual Medicine* found that women who participated in similar programs reported a 40% increase in sexual desire and a 30% increase in energy levels."
YOU:
"This isn't a quick fix. It's about creating sustainable changes that will help you feel like yourself again."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, do you think this program could be a good fit for you?"
YOU:
"Great! I have two options available. The first is my standard 12-week program, and the second is a VIP option with additional one-on-one coaching and resources. Let me explain the pricing."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in your health is a big decision. Can I ask, what dollar amount *would* feel comfortable for you? Often we can explore a payment plan. Also, consider the cost of *not* addressing this. How much is your low energy and decreased libido costing you in terms of your relationships, career, and overall happiness?"
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the time commitment, the financial investment, or something else? I want to make sure I've answered all your questions."
"I've tried other things before, and they didn't work."
"I hear you. It's frustrating when things don't work out. My program is different because it's personalized to your specific needs and focuses on addressing the root causes of your symptoms. Plus, I provide ongoing support and accountability to help you stay on track. I also provide a satisfaction guarantee, so you can feel confident in your investment."
"I'm not sure I have the time for this."
"I understand that time is valuable. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. We can schedule sessions at times that work best for you, and I provide resources that you can access at your own pace. Think of it as investing in your future self ‚Äì a self with more energy, more passion, and more time to enjoy life."
