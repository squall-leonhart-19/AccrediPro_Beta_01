Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call! I'm Sarah, and I'm excited to chat with you. I saw you found me on Instagram - what resonated with you from my post?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful to hear. So, Susan, tell me a little bit more about what brought you here today. What's been going on?"
Phase 2: Uncover the Pain 10 min
YOU:
"When did you start noticing the changes in your libido? How is it impacting your relationship?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address this? What were the results?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, where do you see yourself and your marriage in a year? What's the ideal outcome you're hoping for?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, from what you've shared, I understand you're feeling disconnected in your marriage and struggling with low libido, despite trying [mention her previous attempts]. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because we focus on a holistic plan to ignite your inner fire. We'll explore the physical, emotional, and relational factors affecting your libido. The program is 12 weeks long, and most women experience significant improvements within the first 4-6 weeks."
Phase 4: The Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this is the kind of support you've been looking for? ... Wonderful! Let me explain the program details and how we can get you started."

Handling Objections with Grace

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Time is valuable. The program requires [X] hours per week, which includes [list key activities]. Many of my clients are busy professionals and mothers. How about we explore ways to integrate this into your existing schedule, and see if it's feasible for you? What would need to shift to make this work?"
"I need to think about it."
"Of course! This is a big decision. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the process, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make the right choice for you."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, I offer flexible payment plans. We can break it down into [X] monthly installments. Would you like to discuss the different options available?"
"I've tried so many things before that didn't work."
"I hear your frustration. It's disheartening to invest time and money without seeing results. My program is different because [explain your unique approach and focus on root causes]. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not completely satisfied after [X] weeks, we can discuss a refund."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Connection & Rapport, Uncover the Pain, Present Your Solution, and The Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then ask clarifying questions like, "What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the process, or something else?" to address their concerns directly.

Key Takeaways

  • A well-structured discovery call is crucial for client acquisition.
  • Practice handling common objections with confidence and empathy.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and its potential impact.
  • Visualize your income potential to stay motivated and focused.
