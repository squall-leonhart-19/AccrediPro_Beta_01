Case Study: Sarah M., 45

Client: Sarah M., a 45-year-old woman experiencing decreased libido after menopause.

Presenting Symptoms: Reduced sexual desire, vaginal dryness, and difficulty achieving orgasm.

Intervention: Sarah engaged in a 12-week program that combined hormone therapy, mindfulness exercises, and communication skills training. The practitioner focused on building Sarah's confidence and empowering her to communicate her needs to her partner.

Outcomes: After the program, Sarah reported a significant increase in her sexual desire, improved vaginal lubrication, and enhanced orgasmic function. She also felt more confident and empowered in her relationship.

Client: David L., a 52-year-old man struggling with erectile dysfunction and decreased libido due to stress.
