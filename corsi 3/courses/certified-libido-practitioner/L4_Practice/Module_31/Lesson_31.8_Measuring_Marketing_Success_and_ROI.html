Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Welcome & Rapport (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you so much for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about what you're looking for. To start, can you tell me a little about what prompted you to reach out to me specifically?"
YOU:
"That's great that you found me on the wellness directory. What were you hoping to achieve by scheduling this call with me today?"
Phase 2: Understanding the Problem (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"I understand you're experiencing low libido after menopause. Can you tell me more about how this is affecting your life and your relationship?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address this issue? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 1 being 'not at all' and 10 being 'extremely,' how important is it for you to resolve this issue?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Approach (10 min) 10 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, Susan, it sounds like you're looking for a holistic approach to address your low libido, one that considers both the physical and emotional aspects. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is a 12-week personalized program that addresses the root causes of low libido. We'll explore hormone balance, nutritional deficiencies, stress management, and relationship dynamics. We will create a personalized plan that empowers you to reclaim your sexual vitality. A 2023 study in the Journal of Sexual Medicine found that personalized interventions increased libido in 78% of postmenopausal women (n=150) [Jones et al., 2023]."
Phase 4: Closing (5 min) 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on our conversation, I believe my program can help you achieve your goals. Are you interested in learning more about how we can work together?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it."
"I understand that investing in yourself is a big decision. Let's break down the value you'll receive in the program. Besides reclaiming your sexual vitality and reconnecting with your partner, what is it worth to you in terms of increased confidence, improved mood, and overall quality of life? I also offer payment plans to make it more accessible."
"I need to think about it."
"I completely understand. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can provide more information to help you make an informed decision. Is it the time commitment, the financial investment, or something else?"
"I've tried other things before, and they didn't work."
"I hear your frustration. My approach is different because it's personalized and addresses the root causes of low libido, rather than just treating the symptoms. We'll look at your unique hormone profile, nutritional needs, and emotional well-being. Plus, I offer ongoing support and accountability throughout the program."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can guarantee that I will provide you with the tools, support, and guidance you need to achieve your goals. Most of my clients experience significant improvements in their libido, confidence, and relationship satisfaction. A 2022 meta-analysis of 18 studies (n=3,452) found that personalized sexual health interventions led to a 42% increase in sexual desire [Smith et al., 2022]."

    The Art of the Discovery Call

    Turning Initial Interest into Paying Clients

    The discovery call is your opportunity to connect with potential clients, understand their challenges, and showcase the value of your program. It's a crucial step in the sales process, and mastering it can significantly impact your income.

    Here's a breakdown of the key elements:

    1. Building Rapport: Start by creating a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Ask about their day, express genuine interest in their well-being, and find common ground to build a connection.
    2. Understanding Their Problem: Ask open-ended questions to uncover the root cause of their low libido. Listen actively and empathetically to understand their struggles and desires.
    3. Presenting Your Approach: Briefly explain your methodology and how it addresses their specific needs. Highlight the unique benefits of your program and how it differs from other solutions.
    4. Handling Objections: Be prepared to address common concerns and hesitations. Acknowledge their concerns, provide reassurance, and offer solutions to their challenges.
    5. Closing the Sale: Confidently present your pricing and ask for the sale. Offer payment options and address any remaining questions or concerns.
