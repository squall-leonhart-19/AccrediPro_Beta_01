Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on validation and hope. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for being so open with me. What you're describing is incredibly common, and you're not alone in feeling this way. Many women in their late 40s and early 50s experience a range of changes that can impact their libido and overall well-being."
MARIA:
"It's just so frustrating. I feel like I've lost a part of myself."
YOU:
"I understand. And it's completely valid to feel that way. The good news is that there are effective strategies to help you regain that connection with yourself and rediscover intimacy, if that's what you desire. It's about understanding what's happening in your body and finding solutions that work for you."
YOU:
"Before we dive into specific strategies, I want to assure you that this isn't just about 'trying harder' or 'relaxing more.' We're going to look at the underlying factors that are contributing to these changes, like hormonal shifts, stress levels, and even nutritional needs. Does that sound like a comprehensive approach you'd be comfortable with?"
MARIA:
"Yes, that sounds much better than just being told to 'relax'."
YOU:
"Great. Then let's start with a few questions to get a clearer picture of your overall health and lifestyle. We'll explore everything from your sleep patterns to your dietary habits, so we can create a personalized plan that addresses your specific needs. How does that sound?"
