The 4-Tier Transformation

Our program is structured around a proven, four-tier learning journey. Each tier builds upon the previous one, ensuring you gain a deep and practical understanding of the material.

  1. Level 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build a solid base in our core methodology and the fundamentals of libido coaching. Expect to dive into the science, psychology, and relational aspects of sexual wellness.
  2. Level 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): We'll tackle more complex cases and delve into greater clinical depth. You'll learn to address nuanced challenges and tailor your approach to individual client needs.
  3. Level 3: Master (Modules 24-29): Here, you'll develop the skills to train others and provide supervision. You'll hone your leadership abilities and become a mentor in the field.
  4. Level 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): This final tier focuses on building your business and attracting clients. You'll learn marketing strategies, ethical considerations, and practical tips for creating a successful practice.
