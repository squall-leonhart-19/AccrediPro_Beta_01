8 Career Paths as a Libido Practitioner

Here are just a few ways you can leverage your certification:

  1. Private Practice (1-on-1): Work directly with clients, creating personalized plans. Income: $5,000 - $20,000/month. Imagine, like Maria, a practitioner in Austin, who helps women reclaim their sexual confidence, charging $250/session.
  2. Group Programs: Lead group coaching or workshops. Income: $3,000 - $50,000/launch. Think of launching a 6-week program, "Ignite Your Inner Spark," for $797 per person.
  3. Corporate Wellness: Offer workshops or coaching to companies. Income: $2,000 - $10,000/month retainer. Consider partnering with a local tech company to provide stress-reduction and intimacy workshops.
  4. Integrative Clinic Teams: Join a clinic as a specialist. Income: $40,000 - $120,000/year salary. Envision working alongside doctors and therapists, offering a holistic approach to patient care.
  5. Online Courses: Create and sell online courses. Income: $1,000 - $10,000+/month passive income. Develop a self-paced course, "The Libido Reset," and market it to a global audience.
  6. Speaking Engagements: Share your expertise at events and conferences. Income: $500 - $5,000+/speaking engagement. Become a sought-after speaker on topics like female sexuality and empowerment.
  7. Writing & Content Creation: Write articles, blog posts, or books. Income: $50 - $500+/article, $1,000 - $10,000+/book advance. Establish yourself as an authority by contributing to online publications.
  8. Affiliate Marketing: Partner with related brands and products. Income: Varies, but can be significant passive income. Promote products you believe in and earn a commission on each sale.

These are just a few ideas to get you started! The possibilities are truly endless, and your unique skills and passions will shape your individual path.

Realistic Timeline to Income

Let's talk realistically about how long it takes to start generating income. Here's a general timeline:

  1. Month 1-3: Complete your certification, define your niche, build your website and social media presence. Focus on learning and setting up your foundation. You may earn a small amount from early clients ($0 - $500).
  2. Month 3-6: Start marketing your services, network with other professionals, and offer introductory sessions. Build your client base and refine your offerings. Expect to earn $500 - $2,000/month.
  3. Month 6-12: Continue building your client base, explore different income streams (group programs, online courses), and refine your marketing strategy. Aim to earn $2,000 - $5,000+/month.
  4. Year 1+: Scale your business, diversify your income streams, and become a recognized expert in your niche. The sky's the limit! Many practitioners earn $5,000 - $20,000+/month.

This is just a guideline, and your individual timeline may vary depending on your dedication, marketing efforts, and the specific career path you choose. Remember, consistency is key!

Your 90-Day Vision Exercise

Now it's your turn! Let's create a 90-day vision for your practice:

  1. Define Your Niche: Who do you want to work with? What specific problems do you want to solve?
  2. Set Income Goals: How much money do you want to earn in the next 90 days? Be realistic but ambitious.
  3. Identify Income Streams: What services or products will you offer? Will you focus on private practice, group programs, online courses, or a combination?
  4. Create a Marketing Plan: How will you attract clients? Will you use social media, networking, advertising, or referrals?
  5. Take Action: Commit to taking consistent action every day to move closer to your goals.

Write down your 90-day vision and keep it visible as a reminder of what you're working towards. Review it regularly and adjust it as needed.
