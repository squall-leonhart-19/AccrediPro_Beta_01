The Case She Presents

Aisha's Client Case

What Aisha Tells You:

"I have a client, Sarah, 52, who reports low libido after menopause. She's tried hormone therapy with limited success. She feels disconnected from her partner and is open to exploring holistic options. I'm just not sure which protocols to prioritize from L1."

What You Need to Explore:

  • Has Aisha considered Sarah's relationship dynamics?
  • Did Aisha assess Sarah's stress levels and coping mechanisms?
  • Is Aisha familiar with the research on phytoestrogens for post-menopausal libido?
  • Does Aisha understand the importance of starting with small, achievable steps?

Your Teaching Approach

Key Points to Cover with Aisha

1

Emphasize the Importance of Holistic Assessment

Remind Aisha that low libido is rarely a purely physical issue. Encourage her to explore Sarah's emotional, relational, and lifestyle factors.

2

Review the L1 Protocols for Post-Menopausal Libido

Walk Aisha through the relevant protocols, highlighting the importance of personalized selection based on Sarah's unique needs and preferences.

3

Discuss the Role of Phytoestrogens and Adaptogens

Explain the potential benefits of incorporating phytoestrogen-rich foods and adaptogenic herbs to support hormonal balance and stress resilience.

4

Encourage Gradual Implementation and Ongoing Monitoring

Advise Aisha to start with one or two simple interventions and closely monitor Sarah's response before adding more complex protocols.

Your Feedback Dialogue

How to Deliver Constructive Feedback

Start with Appreciation
"Aisha, thank you for bringing this case to me. It shows your commitment to providing the best possible care for your clients. It's common to feel overwhelmed when starting out."
Identify Strengths
"I noticed you've already considered hormone therapy. That's great! Now, let's broaden the lens."
Offer Guidance
"Remember our discussion on the Biopsychosocial model? How might Sarah's relationship or stress levels be impacting her libido? Let's brainstorm some assessment questions."
Empower Decision-Making
"Based on what we've discussed, what initial steps feel most aligned with Sarah's needs and preferences? What resources from L1 can you use to support your plan?"
