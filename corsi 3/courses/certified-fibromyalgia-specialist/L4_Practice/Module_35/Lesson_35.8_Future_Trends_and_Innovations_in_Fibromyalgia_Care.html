Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call. I know your time is valuable, so I appreciate you making the time. I saw you found me through Facebook, what caught your eye?"
YOU:
"That's great to hear. Before we dive in, tell me a little bit about what's been going on and why you decided to reach out to me."

YOU:

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a typical discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. What is a good strategy for handling the objection "I need to think about it?"

Ask clarifying questions to understand what specifically they need to think about (e.g., the investment, the time commitment). Offer to provide additional information or address any concerns.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is crucial for building a thriving practice.
  • A well-structured discovery call can help you build rapport, understand your prospect's needs, and present your services effectively.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare confident responses.
  • Practice stating your prices with confidence and highlighting the value you provide.
