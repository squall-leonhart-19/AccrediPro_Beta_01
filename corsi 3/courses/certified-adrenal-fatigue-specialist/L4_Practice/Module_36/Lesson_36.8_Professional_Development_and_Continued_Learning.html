Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Expectations 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I appreciate you taking the time. I know your time is valuable, so I want to be respectful of that. During this call, I want to understand more about what you're going through, and then I'll share how I can help. Sound good?"
YOU:
"I noticed you found me through my blog. What resonated with you in particular?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Susan's Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about your sleep issues. How long have you been experiencing them, and what does a typical night look like?"
YOU:
"You mentioned brain fog. Can you describe how that impacts your day-to-day activities?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the most impactful, how would you rate the effect your symptoms have on your overall well-being?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Empathetic Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand budget is a concern. Many of my clients initially feel the same way. Let's look at it this way: you've already invested in supplements and other solutions that haven't worked. What if, instead, you invested in a program that gave you lasting results, improved your productivity, and reduced your reliance on those other expenses? We also have payment plan options available."
"I need to think about it and do more research."
"I respect that. What specific information are you looking for? I'm happy to provide more details or answer any questions you have. Sometimes, too much information can be overwhelming. Remember, I offer a satisfaction guarantee, so you can try the program risk-free."
"I'm not sure if I have the time to commit to a program like this."
"I understand that time is a valuable resource. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. We'll work together to create a schedule that works for you. Consider this: what's the cost of *not* addressing these issues? How much time are you losing now due to fatigue and brain fog?"
"I've tried so many things before, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. It's discouraging when you've tried various approaches without success. My program is different because it's personalized to address your specific needs and root causes. We use a functional medicine approach, supported by scientific evidence, to get lasting results. Plus, you'll have my support every step of the way."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a 30-minute discovery call?

The key phases are: building rapport and setting expectations, understanding the client's challenges, presenting your personalized approach, and transitioning to program details and closing.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, ask what specific information they're looking for, offer to provide more details, and remind them of any guarantees or risk-free options.

Key Takeaways

  • Focus on understanding the client's needs and challenges during the discovery call.
  • Present your approach as a personalized solution that addresses the root causes of their symptoms.
  • Practice confidently stating your prices and handling common objections with empathy and understanding.
  • Remember that you're offering a valuable service that can significantly improve your clients' lives.
  • Consistent practice and refinement of your sales skills will lead to increased client acquisition and income potential.
