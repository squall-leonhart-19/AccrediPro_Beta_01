Discovery Call Script: Your Roadmap to Success

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set the Stage 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call with me. I'm excited to learn more about your health goals and how I can help. To start, can you tell me what prompted you to reach out to me today?"
YOU:
"I understand you found me through a Facebook group. What resonated with you about my approach or the testimonials you saw?"
Phase 2: Uncover Susan's Challenges & Goals 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, you mentioned fatigue and brain fog. Can you describe how these symptoms impact your daily life? What activities are you missing out on?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these issues? What worked, and what didn't? What are your biggest frustrations?"
YOU:
"Let's paint a picture of success. If you could wave a magic wand and eliminate your fatigue and brain fog, what would your life look like in six months? What would you be doing differently?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution & Address Concerns 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're experiencing [summarize her pain points and goals]. Is that an accurate summary?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because we focus on identifying and addressing the root causes of your fatigue and brain fog, rather than just masking the symptoms. I use a personalized, holistic approach that integrates nutrition, lifestyle modifications, and targeted interventions. I work with clients over 12 weeks to create lasting changes. Many of my clients report significant improvements in energy levels and cognitive function within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"You mentioned your concern about investing in another program that doesn't deliver results. I understand your hesitation. My program is designed to provide you with personalized support and guidance every step of the way. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your progress after the first 30 days, I'll provide a full refund."
Phase 4: Close with Confidence 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on everything we've discussed, do you feel like this program could be the solution you've been searching for? ... Great! Let me explain the program details and how we can get started."

Mastering Objections: Turning "No" into "Yes"

Conquer Common Objections with Grace and Confidence

"I need to think about it."
"I understand, Susan. It's a big decision. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed choice. Perhaps I can address those concerns right now?"
"It's too expensive."
"I understand your concern about the investment. Let's reframe it. You've already invested time and money in solutions that haven't worked. What if this program could actually solve your fatigue and brain fog in 12 weeks? What would that be worth to you in terms of increased energy, improved focus, and overall quality of life? We also have flexible payment plans available to make it more accessible."
"I don't have the time."
"I understand that time is valuable. This program is designed to be flexible and fit into your busy schedule. The sessions are [length of sessions] and can be scheduled at your convenience. Think of it this way: how much time are you currently losing due to fatigue and brain fog? This program will help you reclaim that time and become more productive."
"I'm not sure if it will work for me."
"I understand your skepticism. Every client is different, and results vary. However, my program is based on evidence-based principles and tailored to your individual needs. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your progress after the first 30 days, I'll provide a full refund. You have nothing to lose by giving it a try."
