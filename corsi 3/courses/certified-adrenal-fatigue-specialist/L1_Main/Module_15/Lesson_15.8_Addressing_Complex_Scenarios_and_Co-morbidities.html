Your Coaching Script

This script helps you transition from initial conversation to a focused assessment. Remember to speak with compassion and confidence.

YOU:
"Jennifer, thank you for being so open. It takes courage to acknowledge these challenges, especially with the demands of your career. I want you to know that you're not alone. Many women in high-stress roles experience similar symptoms. We can definitely work together to find solutions."
JENNIFER:
"That's a relief to hear. I was starting to think I was just getting old and lazy."
YOU:
"Not at all! Your body is communicating something important. Instead of masking the symptoms, let's uncover the root causes. I'm confident we can identify key areas to support your energy, sleep, and overall well-being."
YOU:
"To get started, I'd like to ask some questions about your daily habits, stress levels, and dietary choices. This will help me create a personalized plan that addresses your specific needs. Does that sound good to you?"
JENNIFER:
"Yes, absolutely. I'm ready to do whatever it takes."
YOU:
"Great! Let's begin with your sleep routine. Can you describe a typical night for me?"
