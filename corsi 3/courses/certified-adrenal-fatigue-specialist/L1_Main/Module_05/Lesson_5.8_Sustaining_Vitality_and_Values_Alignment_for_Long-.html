Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to avoid imposing your own values on a client during a values alignment coaching session?

Imposing your values can invalidate the client's own experiences and desires, leading to solutions that don't truly resonate with them. The goal is to empower the client to discover and honor their authentic values.

2. What are some practical ways to help a client clarify their core values?

Practical exercises include: imagining an ideal day, identifying role models, reflecting on past experiences, and completing values clarification worksheets. Asking open-ended questions and actively listening are also crucial.
