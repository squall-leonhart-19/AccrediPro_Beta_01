Check Your Understanding

1. Why is vulnerability important in building trust with clients?

Vulnerability demonstrates authenticity and creates a safe space for clients to share their own struggles. It fosters a deeper connection and strengthens the coaching relationship.

2. How can you ensure your vulnerability is helpful and not harmful?

Ensure your sharing is relevant to the client's situation, keeps the focus on them, and is genuine. Avoid oversharing or using vulnerability as a manipulative tactic.

Key Takeaways

  • Vulnerability builds trust and connection.
  • Share personal experiences to show empathy.
  • Respect client boundaries and pace.
  • Focus on empowering the client, not yourself.
  • Authenticity is essential for successful coaching.
