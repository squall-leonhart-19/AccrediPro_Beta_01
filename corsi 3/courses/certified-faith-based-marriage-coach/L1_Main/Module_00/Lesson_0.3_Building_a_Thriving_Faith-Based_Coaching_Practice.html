The 4-Tier Transformation

This certification program is structured around a 4-Tier Transformation model, designed to provide a comprehensive and practical learning experience. Each tier builds upon the previous one, ensuring a deep understanding of the principles and techniques of faith-based marriage coaching.

  1. Tier 1: Foundational Principles. This tier covers the core principles of marriage from a faith-based perspective. You'll learn about the biblical foundation of marriage, the roles and responsibilities of spouses, and the importance of spiritual intimacy.
  2. Tier 2: Coaching Skills. This tier focuses on developing essential coaching skills, such as active listening, powerful questioning, and effective communication. You'll learn how to create a safe and supportive environment for your clients and how to guide them towards positive change.
  3. Tier 3: Specialized Techniques. This tier introduces specialized techniques for addressing common marriage challenges, such as communication issues, conflict resolution, and intimacy problems. You'll learn how to apply these techniques in a faith-based context, drawing upon biblical principles and spiritual practices.
  4. Tier 4: Practical Application. This tier provides opportunities to apply your knowledge and skills in real-world scenarios. You'll participate in case studies, role-playing exercises, and supervised coaching sessions, allowing you to gain confidence and competence as a marriage coach.

What Makes This Different?

While there are many coaching certifications available, this program stands out for its integration of faith-based principles and practices. Unlike secular coaching programs, this certification emphasizes the spiritual dimension of marriage and equips you to help couples build stronger, more fulfilling relationships based on their shared faith. The Methodology Framework

At the heart of this certification program is the C.O.V.E.N.A.N.T. Framework‚Ñ¢, a unique methodology designed to guide couples towards a deeper understanding of their marriage covenant and a stronger commitment to one another. This framework provides a structured approach to coaching, ensuring that you address all the key aspects of a healthy and thriving marriage.

The C.O.V.E.N.A.N.T. Framework‚Ñ¢ stands for:

  • Communication: Fostering open and honest communication between spouses.
  • Ownership: Encouraging each spouse to take responsibility for their actions and attitudes.
  • Values: Identifying and aligning on shared values and beliefs.
  • Empathy: Cultivating empathy and understanding for one another's perspectives.
  • Nurturing: Promoting acts of kindness, love, and appreciation.
  • Affection: Encouraging physical and emotional intimacy.
  • Negotiation: Developing skills for resolving conflicts and making decisions together.
  • Trust: Building and maintaining trust and fidelity.

Time Commitment & Pacing

The estimated time commitment for completing this certification program is 80-100 hours. This includes time spent reviewing course materials, participating in online discussions, completing assignments, and engaging in coaching practice. We recommend pacing yourself over 6-12 months to allow for adequate time to absorb the material and integrate the concepts into your coaching practice. A 2021 survey of past participants found that those who dedicated at least 5 hours per week to the program were more likely to successfully complete the certification and achieve their desired coaching outcomes.
