Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Discovery Call

Phase 1: Connection & Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, it's so good to connect with you. Thank you for reaching out. I saw you found me on Facebook - what caught your eye?"
YOU:
"I understand you're dealing with empty nest syndrome. That's a big transition. How are you coping?"
Phase 2: Understanding the Pain 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about feeling 'lost.' What does that look like in your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to find your purpose again?"
YOU:
"If you could wave a magic wand and feel fulfilled again, what would that look like for you?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Sarah, it sounds like you're craving a sense of purpose rooted in your faith. Is that right?"
YOU:
"My coaching program helps women like you rediscover their God-given gifts and create a fulfilling new chapter. We'll use biblical principles and practical exercises to identify your passions and create a plan. Clients often experience a renewed sense of joy and direction within weeks."
Phase 4: The Close 5 min
YOU:
"Sarah, does this sound like the support you've been searching for? ... Wonderful! Let's discuss the program details and how we can get you started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in yourself can feel scary. But think about the cost of staying stuck ‚Äì the emotional toll, the missed opportunities. My clients often find the investment pays for itself in increased confidence and new possibilities. We can also discuss payment plan options."
"I need to pray about it."
"That's wonderful, Sarah. I encourage you to seek God's guidance. Would it be helpful if I sent you a program overview that you can review during your prayer time? Then, we can reconnect in a few days."
"I'm not sure if coaching is right for me."
"That's a valid concern. Many people are new to coaching. What specifically are you unsure about? Perhaps I can address your concerns and help you determine if this is the right path for you."
"I don't have the time."
"I hear you. Time is precious. But think of this as an investment *in* your time. By clarifying your priorities and creating a plan, you'll actually *gain* time and energy. The program is designed to fit into busy lives."

Key Takeaways

  • Empathy is essential: Connect with your prospect's emotions and show genuine understanding.
  • Address objections head-on: Prepare confident responses to common concerns.
  • State your pricing clearly: Practice saying your price with confidence and highlight the value.
  • Close with a clear call to action: Ask for the sale and make it easy for your prospect to move forward.
  • Consistent effort leads to results: Building a successful practice takes time and dedication.
