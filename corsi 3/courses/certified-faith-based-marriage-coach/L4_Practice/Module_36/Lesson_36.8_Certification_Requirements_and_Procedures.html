Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the initial phase of a discovery call?

The key elements include expressing genuine interest in the prospect, actively listening to their story, and finding common ground to establish a connection.

2. How can you effectively address the "I need to pray about it" objection while still moving the conversation forward?

Acknowledge and respect their need to pray, then inquire about specific areas they are seeking guidance on. Offer to provide additional information that may be helpful in their decision-making process.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key. Understand your prospect's needs and motivations beforehand.
  • Active listening and empathy are crucial for building trust and rapport.
  • Confidently present your program as a valuable solution to their specific challenges.
  • Be prepared to handle common objections with grace and understanding.
  • Believe in the transformative power of your coaching and its potential impact on your clients' lives.
