Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Qualify 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I enjoyed meeting you at the health fair! To start, can you tell me a bit more about what you're hoping to achieve?"
YOU:
"That's helpful. On a scale of 1 to 10, how committed are you to making these changes in your health right now? ... Okay, that's great to know."
Phase 2: Deep Dive into the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned brain fog and fatigue. Can you describe a typical day for me?"
YOU:
"And what specific approaches have you tried so far to manage your Hashimoto's? What were the results?"
YOU:
"What are the biggest frustrations you've experienced in trying to find solutions?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution & Value 10 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, Susan, it sounds like you're looking for a personalized, root-cause approach to managing your Hashimoto's symptoms and regaining your energy. Is that right?"
YOU:
"My program is designed to do just that. We use advanced lab testing and a holistic approach to identify the underlying factors contributing to your symptoms. We then create a customized plan to address those root causes, rather than just masking the symptoms. Clients typically start seeing improvements in their energy levels and cognitive function within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"One of my clients, Maria, 48, had similar symptoms. After working together, she reported a 60% reduction in fatigue and significant improvements in her thyroid lab markers. She now has the energy to enjoy her hobbies again. "
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on our conversation, do you feel like this program could be a good fit for you?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me explain how the program works and what's included, and then we can discuss pricing and payment options."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time for this right now."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. The program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. We can schedule sessions at times that work best for you, and the action steps are manageable and sustainable. Think about how much time you're currently spending feeling unwell. This is an investment in reclaiming that time and energy."
"I need to research this more."
"Absolutely. What specific information are you looking for? I'm happy to provide you with resources, case studies, or research articles that support the effectiveness of my approach. I can also send you testimonials from past clients who have experienced similar challenges."
"I'm worried it won't work for me."
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific results, I can tell you that I have a proven track record of helping clients with Hashimoto's improve their symptoms and quality of life. My approach is highly personalized, and I'm committed to working with you every step of the way. We also track your progress with objective lab markers, so we can see the positive changes happening in your body."
"Can I just try one session to see if I like it?"
"I appreciate the interest, however, the program is designed as a comprehensive 12-week journey. It's through that consistent, dedicated work that we see real, lasting results. What I *can* offer is a satisfaction guarantee. If, after the first 30 days, you don't feel that you're receiving value, I'll happily refund your investment."

Check Your Understanding

What are the key elements of a successful sales conversation?

Building rapport, identifying needs, presenting your program as the solution, handling objections, and confidently presenting pricing.

How can you handle the objection "I need to research this more"?

Ask what specific information they're looking for and offer to provide resources, case studies, or testimonials.

Key Takeaways
