Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan! Thank you for scheduling this call. I'm excited to learn more about what's going on with you. To start, can you tell me what prompted you to reach out?"
YOU:
"I understand you saw my ad on Facebook. What specifically resonated with you about it?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling sluggish and gaining weight. When did you first notice these symptoms, and how are they impacting your daily life?"
YOU:
"Can you elaborate on your family history of thyroid issues? Has anyone been diagnosed, and what treatments have they pursued?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to address these issues? What were the results?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, it sounds like you're experiencing symptoms that could be related to thyroid dysfunction, especially given your family history. My approach focuses on identifying the root causes of these symptoms through comprehensive lab testing and a personalized plan."
YOU:
"Unlike conventional medicine, which often focuses on medication to manage symptoms, I use a holistic approach that incorporates nutrition, lifestyle modifications, and targeted supplementation to support optimal thyroid function."
YOU:
"My program involves a 12-week commitment, including regular check-ins, personalized recommendations, and ongoing support. Many clients report significant improvements in energy levels, weight management, and overall well-being within the first few weeks."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I believe my program could be a great fit for you. Are you interested in learning more about how we can work together to address your symptoms and achieve your health goals?"

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"It's a lot of money."
"I understand that it's an investment. However, consider the long-term costs of not addressing these issues. How much are you already spending on treatments that aren't working? My program is designed to provide lasting results, potentially saving you money and improving your quality of life in the long run."
"I'm not sure I have the time."
"I appreciate that you're busy. However, think about how much time you're currently spending feeling tired and unwell. My program is designed to be manageable and sustainable, with flexible scheduling and personalized support to fit your lifestyle. Imagine how much more productive you could be with increased energy and improved health."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can assure you that I'm committed to providing you with the best possible support and guidance. My program is based on evidence-based practices, and many clients experience significant improvements. I'm confident that if you follow the plan and actively participate in the process, you'll see positive changes."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can provide more information, or we can schedule a follow-up call to address your concerns?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's problem, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the sale.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's a lot of money"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the investment in terms of long-term value and potential savings compared to ineffective treatments.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful responses.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and its potential impact on the client's health.
  • Confidently present your pricing and close the sale.
  • Remember that consistent effort and effective strategies can lead to a thriving practice.
