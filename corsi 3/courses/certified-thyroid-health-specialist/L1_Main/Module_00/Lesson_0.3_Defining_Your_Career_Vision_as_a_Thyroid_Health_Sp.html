The Methodology Framework

The methodology framework we use is based on the principles of integrative and functional medicine. We emphasize a holistic approach that considers the interconnectedness of various systems in the body. This framework is designed to help you identify the underlying causes of thyroid imbalances and develop personalized intervention strategies. The key components of the framework include:

  1. Comprehensive Assessment: Gathering detailed information about the client's health history, lifestyle, and symptoms.
  2. Advanced Diagnostic Testing: Utilizing specialized laboratory tests to identify specific thyroid imbalances and related factors.
  3. Personalized Intervention Strategies: Developing customized plans that address the client's unique needs and goals.
  4. Ongoing Monitoring and Adjustment: Tracking the client's progress and making adjustments to the intervention plan as needed.
