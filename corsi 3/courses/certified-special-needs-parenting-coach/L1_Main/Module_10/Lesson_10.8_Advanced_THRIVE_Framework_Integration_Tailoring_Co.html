Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate Diane's feelings of overwhelm before offering solutions?

Validating her feelings builds trust and rapport. It shows her that you understand her challenges and are not simply dismissing them. This makes her more receptive to your coaching.

2. What are some specific ways you can tailor organizational strategies to Diane's ADHD?

Consider strategies that address her specific challenges with attention, impulsivity, and executive function. This might include breaking down tasks into smaller steps, using visual timers, and creating a designated workspace with minimal distractions.

Key Takeaways

  • Always validate the client's feelings and acknowledge their struggles before offering solutions.
  • Tailor coaching strategies to the client's specific challenges and individual needs.
  • Recognize the intersection of ADHD and parenting challenges for parents of children with special needs.
  • Frame coaching as a complementary approach to medication, providing skills and support that medication alone can't offer.
