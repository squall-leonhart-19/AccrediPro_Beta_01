Effective time management is the cornerstone of a successful and sustainable coaching practice. Start by identifying your most productive hours and scheduling your most demanding tasks during those times. Prioritize tasks based on their importance and urgency, using methods like the Eisenhower Matrix (urgent/important). And donâ€™t forget to schedule regular breaks to recharge and prevent burnout.

Research shows that individuals who use effective time management techniques experience a 20% reduction in stress levels. (American Psychological Association, 2020)
