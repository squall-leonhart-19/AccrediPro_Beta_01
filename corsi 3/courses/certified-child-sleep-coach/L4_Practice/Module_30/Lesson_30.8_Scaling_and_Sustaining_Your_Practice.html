Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Connect 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for reaching out! I know how challenging sleep deprivation can be. First, tell me a bit about [child's name] and what a typical night looks like."
YOU:
"I understand. It sounds like you're really struggling. I'm a mom myself, so I know how tough this can be."
Phase 2: Explore the Problem in Detail 10 min
YOU:
"When did these sleep issues start? What have you already tried?"
YOU:
"What impact is this having on you, personally, and on your family?"
YOU:
"Imagine [child's name] is sleeping soundly through the night. How would that change your life?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the problem, presenting your solution, and closing confidently.

2. How should you handle budget concerns during a discovery call?

Acknowledge their concern, highlight the value of your program, and explore possible payment options.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script until it feels natural and conversational.
  • Address objections with empathy and confidence.
  • Clearly communicate the value of your program and its potential impact.
  • Be prepared to discuss pricing and payment options.
  • Believe in your ability to help families achieve better sleep!
