Online Courses & Workshops

Packaging your expertise into an online course or workshop allows you to reach a wider audience than individual consultations alone. Think of it as scaling the 'E' in the D.R.E.A.M. Frameworkâ„¢ â€“ establishing routines not just for individual clients, but for entire groups of parents.

Income Example: Sarah, a former teacher, created a 6-week online course on toddler sleep. She sells it for $297. With just 20 sales per month, she generates nearly $6,000 in passive income.

Consider these steps:

  1. Identify a Niche Topic: Based on your ideal client (from Lesson 1), what's a burning question they have? Example: "Surviving the 4-Month Sleep Regression."
  2. Structure Your Content: Break down your knowledge into modules with clear learning objectives. Use a mix of video lectures, downloadable worksheets, and Q&A sessions.
  3. Choose a Platform: Platforms like Teachable, Thinkific, and Kajabi make it easy to host and sell your course.
  4. Promote Your Course: Leverage the social media strategies we discussed in Lesson 3 to drive traffic to your course.

E-books & Guides

E-books and guides are another excellent way to monetize your expertise. They're relatively easy to create and can be sold on your website, Amazon Kindle, or other online marketplaces. Think of this as refining the 'A' in the D.R.E.A.M. Frameworkâ„¢ â€“ offering actionable advice in a readily accessible format.

Income Example: Michael, a sleep consultant, wrote an e-book titled "The No-Cry Sleep Solution for Infants." He sells it for $19.99 on Amazon. With an average of 50 sales per month, he earns almost $1,000 in passive income.

Here's how to get started:

  1. Choose a Specific Topic: Focus on a niche area of sleep consulting. Example: "Potty Training and Sleep: A Comprehensive Guide."
  2. Write High-Quality Content: Provide valuable, actionable advice that readers can implement immediately.
  3. Format Your E-book: Use a professional template and ensure your e-book is easy to read on various devices.
  4. Market Your E-book: Promote your e-book through your website, social media, and email list.

Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing involves partnering with companies that sell products or services related to sleep. You earn a commission for every sale made through your unique affiliate link. This leverages the 'R' in the D.R.E.A.M. Frameworkâ„¢ â€“ recommending trusted resources to support healthy sleep.

Income Example: David, a sleep consultant, partners with a company that sells blackout curtains. He earns a 10% commission on every sale made through his affiliate link. If he generates $5,000 in sales per month, he earns $500 in passive income.

Here's how to find affiliate opportunities:

  1. Identify Relevant Products: Think about the products you already recommend to your clients. Examples: white noise machines, weighted blankets, organic mattresses.
  2. Research Affiliate Programs: Many companies offer affiliate programs. Search for "[product name] affiliate program" on Google.
  3. Promote Affiliate Links: Share your affiliate links on your website, social media, and in your email newsletters.

Case Study: The Sleepytime Academy

Client: Anya Sharma, a pediatric sleep consultant, wanted to diversify her income streams.

Presenting Problem: Limited time for one-on-one consultations; desired to reach a wider audience.

Intervention: Anya created "The Sleepytime Academy," a membership program offering weekly sleep tips, monthly live Q&A sessions, and a private support group. She also developed a mini-course on toddler sleep regressions.

Outcomes: Within six months, Anya gained 85 members paying $59/month, generating over $5,000 in recurring revenue. The mini-course added an additional $2,000/month in sales.
