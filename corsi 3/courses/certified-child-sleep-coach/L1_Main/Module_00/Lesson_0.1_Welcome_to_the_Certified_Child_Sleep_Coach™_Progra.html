What You'll Learn & Become

This certification program is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of child sleep science, coaching techniques, and business strategies. You'll gain the knowledge and skills to confidently guide families towards better sleep and build a successful coaching practice.

Here's a glimpse of what you'll learn:

  1. The Science of Sleep: Understand the biological and developmental factors that influence sleep in infants and children.
  2. Assessment & Evaluation: Learn how to conduct thorough sleep assessments, identify underlying sleep issues, and develop personalized sleep plans.
  3. Coaching Techniques: Master effective communication, motivational interviewing, and behavior modification strategies to support families through the sleep coaching process.
  4. Sleep Training Methods: Explore a variety of evidence-based sleep training methods and learn how to choose the best approach for each family's unique needs and preferences.
  5. Business Development: Gain practical knowledge of marketing, sales, and client management to build a thriving coaching practice.

By the end of this program, you will be able to:

  • Confidently assess and address a wide range of sleep issues in infants and children.
  • Develop personalized sleep plans that align with each family's values and goals.
  • Provide compassionate and effective coaching support to families.
  • Build a successful and sustainable child sleep coaching practice.

This is more than just a certification; it's a pathway to a fulfilling and impactful career.

Your Cohort & Community

One of the most valuable aspects of this program is the community of like-minded individuals you'll be joining. Your cohort is a group of fellow students who are on the same journey as you. You'll have the opportunity to connect with them, share experiences, and support each other along the way.

We've created several channels for you to connect with your cohort and the broader community:

  • Private Facebook Group: A dedicated online forum for sharing questions, insights, and resources.
  • Weekly Group Coaching Calls: Live sessions with Sarah and guest experts to address common challenges and provide personalized guidance.
  • Peer Coaching Practicums: Opportunities to practice your coaching skills with fellow students and receive valuable feedback.

Research consistently demonstrates the power of social support in achieving goals. A 2018 study published in the *Journal of Applied Psychology* found that individuals who participated in group coaching programs were 30% more likely to achieve their goals compared to those who received individual coaching only (Ibarra et al., 2018). Your cohort will be your sounding board, your support system, and your cheerleaders!

First Steps

To make the most of this program and set yourself up for success, here are a few important first steps to take:

  1. Introduce Yourself: Head over to the Facebook group and introduce yourself to your cohort. Share your background, your goals, and what you're hoping to get out of the program.
  2. Review the Course Materials: Familiarize yourself with the course syllabus, schedule, and resources. This will give you a clear roadmap for the journey ahead.
  3. Attend the Welcome Call: Join Sarah for a live welcome call where she'll provide an overview of the program and answer any questions you may have.
  4. Set Realistic Goals: Think about what you want to achieve in the program and set realistic goals for yourself. Break down your goals into smaller, manageable steps.

Remember, this is a marathon, not a sprint. Be patient with yourself, celebrate your progress, and don't be afraid to ask for help when you need it. We're all here to support you!
