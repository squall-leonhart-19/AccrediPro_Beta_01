Your Coaching Script

Here's how to start this conversation during the "Monitor & Maintain" phase. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, it's completely normal to experience regressions, especially after illness or travel. The fact that you're reaching out proactively is fantastic. Let's troubleshoot together."
MARIA:
"I feel like I failed. I was so proud of how far we'd come."
YOU:
"You haven't failed at all. Think of sleep coaching like tending a garden. Sometimes weeds pop up, and we just need to pull them. We have the tools; we just need to reapply them."
YOU:
"Let's start by reviewing what's changed in the last two weeks. Can you walk me through a typical day and night since the travel?"
MARIA:
"Okay, well, since we got back..."
YOU:
"Great. As you're explaining, I'll be listening for any deviations from the original plan, and we'll pinpoint the most likely triggers. Sound good?"
