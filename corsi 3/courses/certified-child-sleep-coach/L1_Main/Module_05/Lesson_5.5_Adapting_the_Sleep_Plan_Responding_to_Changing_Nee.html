Recognizing When to Adjust the Sleep Plan

Life happens! Several factors can disrupt a previously successful sleep plan. Being able to identify these triggers is the first step in providing effective support. Here are some key scenarios:

  • New Siblings: The arrival of a new baby can disrupt the sleep of older children due to changes in parental attention and household routines.

Emphasize that setbacks are normal and temporary. The goal is not to achieve perfect sleep every night but to establish healthy sleep habits that can be maintained over time.
