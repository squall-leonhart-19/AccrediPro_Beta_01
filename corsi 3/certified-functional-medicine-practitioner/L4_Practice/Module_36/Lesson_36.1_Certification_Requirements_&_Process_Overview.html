Certification Renewal Process

To maintain your certification, you will need to renew it every two years. The renewal process ensures that you stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in functional medicine and maintain a high level of professional competence.

  1. Continuing Education Credits: Complete a minimum of 30 hours of continuing education credits (CEUs) in functional medicine or related fields within the two-year renewal period.
  2. Renewal Application: Submit the online certification renewal application form, providing details of the CEUs you have completed.
  3. Renewal Fee: Pay the certification renewal fee.
  4. Certification Renewal Approval: Your renewal application will be reviewed by the AccrediPro Academy Certification Board, and upon approval, your certification will be renewed for another two years.

Ethical Guidelines & Professional Standards

As a Certified Functional Medicine Practitionerâ„¢, you are expected to adhere to the highest ethical standards and professional guidelines. These guidelines are designed to protect your clients, maintain the integrity of the profession, and ensure that you provide safe and effective care.

  • Client Confidentiality: Maintain strict confidentiality of client information, in accordance with HIPAA regulations and other applicable laws.
  • Informed Consent: Obtain informed consent from clients before initiating any treatment or intervention.
  • Scope of Practice: Practice within the scope of your training and expertise, and refer clients to other qualified healthcare professionals when necessary.
  • Professional Conduct: Maintain a professional demeanor in all interactions with clients, colleagues, and other healthcare professionals.
  • Conflict of Interest: Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts to clients.
  • Advertising and Marketing: Ensure that all advertising and marketing materials are accurate, truthful, and not misleading.

The AccrediPro Academy has a zero-tolerance policy for ethical violations. Any reported violations will be thoroughly investigated, and appropriate disciplinary action will be taken, up to and including revocation of certification.
