Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's problems, presenting your approach, and confidently closing the call.

2. How can you handle the objection "It's too expensive?"

Empathize, then reframe the investment in terms of the long-term value and results the client will achieve. Highlight the ROI and offer payment options.

Key Takeaways
