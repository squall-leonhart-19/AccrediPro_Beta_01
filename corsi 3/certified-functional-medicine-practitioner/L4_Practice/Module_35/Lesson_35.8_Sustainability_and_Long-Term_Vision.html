Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand. It‚Äôs an investment in your well-being. My program is designed to fit into your busy life. Most clients spend about 3-4 hours a week on the plan, including meal prep and check-ins. And honestly, how much time are you already spending feeling unwell?"
"I need to research this more."
"That's a great idea, Susan. What specific information are you looking for? I can send you some resources or testimonials from past clients. Also, what will be your measure of success when researching?"
"What if it doesn't work for me?"
"That's a valid concern. While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can tell you that I have a proven track record. Over 85% of my clients report significant symptom improvement within 12 weeks. If you fully commit to the program and follow the plan, I'm confident you'll see results. Plus, I offer ongoing support to make sure you feel heard and supported."
"Can I just try a few sessions first?"
"I understand the desire to test the waters. However, my program is designed as a comprehensive 12-week journey to ensure sustainable results. A few sessions wouldn't give us enough time to address the root causes and create lasting change. I'm happy to answer any specific questions you have to help you feel more confident in committing to the full program."
