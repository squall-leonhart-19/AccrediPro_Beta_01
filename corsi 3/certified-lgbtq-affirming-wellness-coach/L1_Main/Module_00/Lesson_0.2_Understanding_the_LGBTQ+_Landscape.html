The "Can I Actually Make Money?" Conversation

Hi, it's Rachel! I know what you're thinking. You're passionate about wellness, you're excited about supporting the LGBTQ+ community, but you're also wondering: "Can I actually make a good living doing this?"

It's a valid question! Many of you are career changers, maybe even pivoting after years in a completely different field. The thought of starting over, building a business, and relying on it for income can feel daunting. Imposter syndrome might be whispering in your ear, telling you it's too late, too risky, or too difficult.

I'm here to tell you: it's absolutely possible. Not only is it possible, but with the right training, support, and a solid plan, you can create a fulfilling and financially rewarding career as an LGBTQ+ Affirming Wellness Coach. Let's dive into the possibilities...

8 Career Paths (With Income Ranges)

The beauty of this certification is the flexibility it provides. You're not limited to one specific path. You can tailor your business to your strengths, interests, and lifestyle. Here are eight common career paths our graduates pursue, along with realistic income ranges:

  1. Private Practice (1-on-1 Coaching): Offering personalized coaching packages to individual clients.
    • Income: $5,000 - $20,000/month (depending on client load and package pricing)
    • Example: Offering a 3-month coaching package at $600/month, with 10 consistent clients, generates $6,000/month
  2. Group Coaching Programs: Leading group coaching sessions focused on specific topics (e.g., stress management, healthy eating, self-esteem).
    • Income: $3,000 - $15,000/month (depending on group size and program duration)
    • Example: Running a 6-week group coaching program for $497/person, with 20 participants, generates $9,940/program
  3. Corporate Wellness Workshops: Delivering workshops and seminars to companies on LGBTQ+ inclusive wellness topics.
    • Income: $1,000 - $5,000/workshop (depending on company size and workshop duration)
    • Example: Conducting 2 corporate workshops per month at $2,500/workshop generates $5,000/month
  4. Online Courses & Programs: Creating and selling self-paced online courses on various wellness topics.
    • Income: $500 - $10,000+/month (depending on course popularity and pricing)
    • Example: Selling an online course for $197, with 50 sales per month, generates $9,850/month
  5. Speaking Engagements: Presenting at conferences, events, and organizations on LGBTQ+ wellness and inclusion.
    • Income: $500 - $5,000/speaking engagement (depending on event size and speaker experience)
    • Example: Securing 1 speaking engagement per month at $3,000/engagement generates $3,000/month
  6. Writing & Content Creation: Writing articles, blog posts, and creating content for wellness websites and publications.
    • Income: $200 - $2,000+/month (depending on writing volume and publication rates)
    • Example: Writing 4 articles per month at $500/article generates $2,000/month
  7. Affiliate Marketing: Partnering with wellness brands and promoting their products/services to your audience.
    • Income: $100 - $5,000+/month (depending on affiliate commission rates and sales volume)
    • Example: Earning 10% commission on $20,000 in sales through affiliate links generates $2,000/month
  8. Combining Paths: Many coaches successfully combine multiple income streams for a diversified and stable income.
    • Income: Varies widely depending on the combination of income streams.
    • Example: Offering 1-on-1 coaching, running a group program, and selling an online course can generate a combined income of $8,000+/month

These are just examples, and your actual income will depend on your effort, marketing skills, and the value you provide to your clients. However, these figures demonstrate the real potential for building a thriving coaching business.

Realistic Timeline to Income

Let's be realistic: you're not going to become a millionaire overnight. Building a successful coaching practice takes time and effort. Here's a general timeline to consider:

  • Month 1-3: Complete the certification program, define your niche, create your website and social media profiles, and start networking.
  • Month 3-6: Offer free introductory sessions, build your email list, create valuable content (blog posts, videos, etc.), and start marketing your services.
  • Month 6-12: Refine your coaching packages, gather testimonials, increase your marketing efforts, and start building a consistent client base.
  • Month 12+: Scale your business by offering group programs, online courses, or other services. Consider hiring a virtual assistant or team member to help with administrative tasks.

During the initial months, focus on building your skills, creating valuable content, and establishing yourself as an expert in your niche. Don't get discouraged if you don't see immediate results. Consistency and persistence are key.

A 2022 study by the ICF (International Coaching Federation) found that new coaches typically require 6-12 months to establish a sustainable income. The same study also showed that coaches with specialized certifications earn, on average, 20% more than those without.

The Math That Makes Sense

Let's break down the numbers to show you how achievable your income goals are. Here are a few examples:

  • Scenario 1: You charge $150/session and see 10 clients per week. That's $1,500/week, or $6,000/month.
  • Scenario 2: You offer a 3-month coaching package for $1,800 and sign up 5 clients per month. That's $9,000/month.
  • Scenario 3: You run a group coaching program for $497/person, with 15 participants. That's $7,455 per program.

As you can see, even with relatively modest pricing and client volume, you can generate a significant income. The key is to find a pricing model that works for you and consistently market your services.

A 2023 survey of wellness coaches found that the average hourly rate for coaches with 2-5 years of experience is $125-$250. This highlights the potential for increasing your income as you gain experience and expertise.

Your 90-Day Vision Exercise

Now it's time to get clear on your own vision for your coaching practice. Grab a pen and paper (or your favorite note-taking app) and answer the following questions:

  1. What is your ideal coaching niche within the LGBTQ+ community?
  2. What types of services do you want to offer (1-on-1 coaching, group programs, online courses, etc.)?
  3. How many clients do you want to work with in the next 90 days?
  4. What is your target monthly income in the next 90 days?
  5. What are 3 specific actions you can take this week to move closer to your vision?

This exercise will help you clarify your goals and create a roadmap for success. Remember, your vision is unique to you. Don't compare yourself to others. Focus on creating a business that aligns with your values, strengths, and lifestyle.
