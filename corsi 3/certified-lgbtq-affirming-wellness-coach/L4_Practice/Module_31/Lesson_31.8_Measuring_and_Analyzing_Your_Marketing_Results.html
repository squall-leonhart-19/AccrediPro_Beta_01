Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, it's so great to finally connect! Thank you for reaching out. I saw you found me through the local LGBTQ+ Facebook group. Are you active in the community?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! I'm also involved with [mention a local LGBTQ+ organization]. So, Susan, what prompted you to schedule this call today? What's been on your mind?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Needs 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling lost and overwhelmed. Can you tell me more about that? What specific challenges are you facing right now?"
YOU:
"What does 'feeling confident in your identity' look like for you? What would be different in your life if you felt that way?"
YOU:
"And what have you tried already to address these feelings? Have you sought support groups, therapy, or other resources?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, thank you for sharing all of that. It sounds like you're seeking support, community, and guidance in navigating this new chapter. Is that a fair summary?"
YOU:
"My Affirming Wellness Coaching Program is designed to provide exactly that. Over 12 weeks, we'll work together to explore your identity, build resilience, connect you with LGBTQ+ resources, and create actionable goals for your future. I've helped many women in their 40s and 50s navigate similar journeys, and they've found tremendous value in the personalized support and community connection."
YOU:
"For example, I worked with Maria, 55, who felt completely isolated after coming out. Through the program, she not only gained confidence in her identity but also joined a local hiking group for lesbians and made lasting friendships."
Phase 4: Closing & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I truly believe this program can be a game-changer for you. Does this sound like the kind of support you've been looking for?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me briefly explain how the program works and then we can discuss the investment."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in yourself is a big decision. Let's talk about the value you'll receive. This program isn't just about coaching; it's about building a foundation for your happiness and well-being. I also offer payment plans to make it more accessible. Would you like to explore those options?"
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the time commitment, the financial investment, or something else? I want to make sure you have all the information you need to make an informed decision."
"I'm worried it won't work for me."
"That's a valid concern. I understand you might be skeptical. But I want to assure you that this program is highly personalized to your unique needs and goals. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied after the first two weeks, I'll refund your investment. How does that sound?"
"I'm too busy to commit to a 12-week program."
"I hear you. Life can be hectic. But the program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. We can work around your commitments and find times that work best for you. And remember, investing in your well-being is never a waste of time. It's an investment in your future happiness and fulfillment."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport during the initial minutes of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves finding common ground, actively listening, and demonstrating empathy. It's about creating a safe and comfortable space for the prospect to share their needs and concerns.

2. How can you address a prospect's concern about the financial investment of your coaching program?

Address financial concerns by highlighting the value they'll receive, offering payment plans, and emphasizing the long-term benefits of investing in their well-being. Also, share success stories from past clients to demonstrate the potential ROI.
