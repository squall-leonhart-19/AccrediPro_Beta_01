Certification Requirements

To become a Certified LGBTQ+ Affirming Wellness Coach, you must meet the following requirements:

  1. Complete all modules and associated assignments.
  2. Participate in at least 80% of live Q&A sessions.
  3. Pass the certification exam with a score of 80% or higher.
  4. Submit a case study demonstrating your application of the P.R.I.D.E. Frameworkâ„¢.
  5. Provide proof of professional liability insurance (if applicable in your jurisdiction).

The case study should detail your work with a fictional or anonymized client, highlighting your assessment process, intervention strategies, and client outcomes. It should demonstrate your ability to apply the principles and techniques learned throughout the program.

Case Study Example

Case Study: Alex (Age 28)

Presenting Symptoms: Alex, a 28-year-old non-binary individual, presented with feelings of anxiety and isolation related to workplace discrimination and difficulty navigating romantic relationships. They reported struggling with internalized transphobia and a lack of supportive community.

Intervention: Using the P.R.I.D.E. Frameworkâ„¢, we began with Personal Assessment to identify Alex's strengths and challenges. We then focused on Resilience Building through mindfulness exercises and cognitive reframing techniques to address internalized transphobia. Identity Exploration involved exploring Alex's gender identity and values, leading to a Defined Goal of finding a more inclusive workplace and building stronger relationships. Finally, Empowered Living involved creating an action plan to achieve these goals, including networking with LGBTQ+ organizations and practicing assertive communication skills.

Outcomes: Over six months, Alex reported a significant reduction in anxiety and isolation. They successfully transitioned to a more inclusive workplace and began building meaningful relationships within the LGBTQ+ community. Alex also reported increased self-esteem and a stronger sense of identity.
