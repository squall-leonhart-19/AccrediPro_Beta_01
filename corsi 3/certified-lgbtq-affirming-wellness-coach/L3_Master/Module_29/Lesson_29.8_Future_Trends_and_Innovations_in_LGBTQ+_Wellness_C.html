Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to address secondary trauma in new practitioners?

Secondary trauma can lead to burnout and decreased effectiveness, impacting both the practitioner and their clients. Addressing it proactively helps ensure long-term sustainability in the field.

2. How can you help a mentee clarify their scope of practice?

Review the certification guidelines, discuss specific scenarios, and provide referral resources. Role-playing challenging situations can also be beneficial.
