Advanced TCM diagnostics involve not only pulse and tongue diagnosis, but also a deep understanding of the Five Element theory and the Eight Principles.

Advanced acupuncture techniques include:

  • Distal Acupuncture: Treating conditions by needling points far from the site of pain or dysfunction.
  • Scalp Acupuncture: Stimulating specific areas of the scalp to address neurological conditions.
  • Auricular Acupuncture: Using points on the ear to treat a wide range of ailments, often used for addiction and pain management.
