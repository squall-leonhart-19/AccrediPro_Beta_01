Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, it's so great to connect with you! Thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah Mitchell, and I'm excited to learn more about what brought you here. To start, what resonated with you about my Facebook ad that made you want to book this call?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! I understand hormonal imbalances and brain fog can be incredibly frustrating. Before we dive into the details, tell me a bit about yourself and what you're hoping to achieve with your health."
Phase 2: Uncover Challenges & Goals 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, can you elaborate on the hormonal imbalances and brain fog you're experiencing? When did these symptoms start, and how are they impacting your daily life?"
YOU:
"I understand. It sounds like you've been through a lot. What specific treatments or approaches have you tried in the past, and what were the results?"
YOU:
"And what are your biggest health goals? If you could wave a magic wand and transform your health, what would that look and feel like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Unique Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're dealing with persistent hormonal imbalances, brain fog, and low energy, and you're seeking a holistic, personalized approach to address the root causes. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My integrative health program is designed to do just that. Instead of solely focusing on symptoms, we'll conduct comprehensive assessments to identify the underlying factors contributing to your imbalances. We'll then create a customized plan that may include targeted nutrition, lifestyle modifications, botanical medicine, and stress management techniques. My program is 12 weeks long with weekly 1:1 sessions, unlimited email support, and personalized resources. Most of my clients report a significant improvement in their energy levels, mental clarity, and overall well-being within the first few weeks."
YOU:
"How does this approach resonate with you, Susan?"
Phase 4: Invitation & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Wonderful! Based on our conversation, I believe my program could be a great fit for you. Would you like to learn more about the investment and how we can get started on your journey to optimal health?"

Handling Objections with Confidence

Common Concerns & Your Empowered Responses

"I need to think about it and do some research."
"I completely understand, Susan. It's a significant decision. To help you with your research, what specific questions or concerns do you have that I can address right now? I want to ensure you have all the information you need to make an informed choice."
"It's a bit more expensive than I anticipated."
"I appreciate your honesty, Susan. Let's explore the value you'll receive in my program. We're not just addressing symptoms; we're identifying and correcting the root causes of your imbalances for long-term results. Consider the potential cost savings from reduced doctor visits, fewer medications, and increased productivity. What value would you place on regaining your energy, mental clarity, and overall well-being?"
"I'm not sure if I have the time commitment right now."
"I understand that time is valuable, Susan. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your lifestyle. We'll work together to create a schedule that works for you, and the weekly sessions are just one hour. Think of this as an investment in your long-term health and well-being. By prioritizing your health now, you'll likely have more energy and focus to accomplish your other goals."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, Susan, I offer a payment plan to make the program more accessible. Instead of paying the full amount upfront, you can split it into smaller monthly installments. Would you like me to provide you with more details about the payment plan options?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's challenges and goals, presenting your unique approach, and inviting them to take the next step.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, explore the value they'll receive, and consider offering payment options. Focus on the long-term benefits and potential cost savings.

3. What is the importance of practicing your pricing presentation?

Practicing your pricing presentation builds confidence and helps you communicate the value of your services effectively. It ensures that you can confidently state your price and address any questions or concerns.

Key Takeaways

  • Master the Discovery Call: A structured discovery call is crucial for understanding your prospect's needs and presenting your services effectively.
  • Handle Objections with Confidence: Prepare for common objections and craft empowered responses that address concerns and highlight the value of your program.
  • Present Pricing with Poise: Practice stating your price with conviction and emphasize the long-term benefits and return on investment.
  • Embrace Your Income Potential: Recognize the realistic income opportunities available to integrative health practitioners and set ambitious goals for your practice.
  • Believe in Your Value: Have unwavering confidence in the transformative power of your services and your ability to help clients achieve optimal health.
