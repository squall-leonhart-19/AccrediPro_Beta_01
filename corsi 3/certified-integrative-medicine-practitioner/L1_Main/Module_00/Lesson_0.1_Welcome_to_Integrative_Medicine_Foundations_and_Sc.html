Case Study: Maria, 42, Overcoming Chronic Fatigue

Client: Maria, a 42-year-old marketing executive.

Presenting Symptoms: Persistent fatigue, brain fog, digestive issues (bloating, constipation), and difficulty sleeping.

Intervention:

  • Comprehensive health assessment, including stool testing and micronutrient analysis.
  • Personalized nutrition plan focusing on whole foods, gut-healing nutrients, and anti-inflammatory foods.
  • Supplementation to address nutrient deficiencies and support gut health (probiotics, L-glutamine, vitamin D).
  • Stress management techniques, including mindfulness meditation and yoga.
  • Sleep hygiene education and strategies to improve sleep quality.

Outcomes: After 3 months, Maria reported significant improvements in her energy levels, cognitive function, and digestive health. She was sleeping better, experiencing less bloating, and feeling more like herself again. She was able to return to her active lifestyle and enjoy her work and family life.
