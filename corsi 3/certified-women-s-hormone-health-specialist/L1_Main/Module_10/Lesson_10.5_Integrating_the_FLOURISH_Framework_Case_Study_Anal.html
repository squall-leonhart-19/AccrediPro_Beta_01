Case Study 1: Sarah, 42 - Perimenopause and Gut Dysbiosis

Client: Sarah, 42 years old

Presenting Symptoms: Irregular periods, hot flashes, night sweats, anxiety, bloating, and fatigue.

Medical History: History of antibiotic use for recurrent UTIs, diagnosed with IBS five years ago.

Lab Results: Elevated FSH, low estrogen, low progesterone, elevated cortisol, signs of gut dysbiosis on stool test.

Intervention:

  • Foundation Assessment: Comprehensive hormone panel, gut microbiome analysis, lifestyle assessment.
  • Lifestyle Optimization: Improved diet with focus on fiber and phytoestrogens, regular exercise, stress reduction techniques (mindfulness, yoga), and improved sleep hygiene.
  • Overcome Obstacles: Addressed stress and anxiety through cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) techniques.
  • Understand Hormonal Cycles: Educated Sarah on the stages of perimenopause and what to expect.
  • Restore Gut Health: Probiotics, prebiotics, and a gut-healing diet to address dysbiosis.
  • Individualized Supplementation: Targeted supplements to support hormone balance (e.g., black cohosh, magnesium, vitamin D).
  • Support System: Encouraged participation in a support group for women experiencing perimenopause.
  • Hormone Harmony: Regular monitoring of hormone levels and adjustment of treatment plan as needed.

Outcomes: Sarah reported a significant reduction in hot flashes and night sweats, improved mood and energy levels, and reduced bloating. Lab results showed improved hormone balance and gut microbiome composition.

After 3 months, Sarah reported a 60% reduction in hot flashes and a 40% improvement in sleep quality.

Case Study 2: Emily, 35 - PCOS and Insulin Resistance

Client: Emily, 35 years old

Presenting Symptoms: Irregular periods, acne, hirsutism, weight gain, and difficulty conceiving.

Medical History: Diagnosed with PCOS five years ago, family history of diabetes.

Lab Results: Elevated androgens, high insulin levels, low SHBG.

Intervention:

  • Foundation Assessment: Comprehensive hormone panel, glucose tolerance test, lifestyle assessment.
  • Lifestyle Optimization: Low-glycemic diet, regular exercise (including strength training), stress management techniques.
  • Overcome Obstacles: Addressed emotional challenges related to infertility and body image.
  • Understand Hormonal Cycles: Educated Emily on the impact of insulin resistance on her hormonal cycles.
  • Restore Gut Health: Focus on reducing inflammation in the gut through diet and targeted supplements.
  • Individualized Supplementation: Targeted supplements to improve insulin sensitivity and support hormone balance (e.g., inositol, berberine, spearmint tea).
  • Support System: Encouraged participation in a support group for women with PCOS.
  • Hormone Harmony: Regular monitoring of hormone levels and adjustment of treatment plan as needed.

Outcomes: Emily experienced more regular periods, improved skin, and weight loss. Lab results showed improved insulin sensitivity and hormone balance. She was also able to conceive naturally after six months of treatment.

After 6 months, Emily's fasting insulin levels decreased by 25%, and she lost 10 pounds.
