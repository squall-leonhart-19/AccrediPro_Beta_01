Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key differences between selling individual coaching versus group programs?

Group programs emphasize community, social proof, and leveraged time, while individual coaching focuses on personalized attention and tailored solutions.

2. How can you address budget concerns when selling a group program?

Highlight the value proposition (what's included), offer payment plans, and emphasize the long-term benefits and potential cost savings compared to ongoing symptom management.

Key Takeaways

  • Highlight the community aspect of group programs to attract clients seeking connection and support.
  • Address budget concerns proactively by showcasing the value and offering payment options.
  • Emphasize the efficiency and impact of group sessions to overcome time constraints.
  • Use social proof (testimonials) to build trust and demonstrate the program's effectiveness.
