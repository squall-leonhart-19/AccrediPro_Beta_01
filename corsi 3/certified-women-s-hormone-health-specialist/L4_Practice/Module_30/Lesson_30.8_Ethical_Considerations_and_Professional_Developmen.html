Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Intro 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thanks for scheduling this call! I'm Sarah. I know you're here because you're experiencing some challenging perimenopause symptoms. I'm excited to hear more about what you're going through."
YOU:
"Before we dive in, can you tell me a little bit about what prompted you to reach out to me specifically? What resonated with you on my Instagram?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Symptoms 10 min
YOU:
"Let's talk about these symptoms. When did you first notice them? How have they been impacting your daily life, your work, your relationships?"
YOU:
"I understand. Hot flashes and insomnia are truly disruptive. On a scale of 1-10, how would you rate the severity of these symptoms?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to manage these symptoms? HRT, supplements, lifestyle changes? What has worked, and what hasn't?"
Phase 3: Present Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're really struggling with these symptoms and you're looking for a natural and effective solution. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach is different because I focus on identifying the root causes of your hormonal imbalances. I don't just mask the symptoms. I use a comprehensive assessment, including lab testing, to create a personalized plan that addresses your unique needs. This includes diet, lifestyle, and targeted supplementation."
YOU:
"I work with clients over 12 weeks, and most start to see significant improvements within the first month. A recent study showed that 85% of women following a similar protocol experienced a reduction in hot flashes by 50% or more."
Phase 4: Closing the Call 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this approach sound like something you'd be interested in exploring further?"
YOU:
"Great! Let me tell you about the program and how we can get started..."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand that you're busy, Susan. However, think about how much time you're currently spending dealing with these symptoms. My program is designed to be efficient and effective, and I'll work with you to create a schedule that fits your lifestyle. It's an investment in your future health and well-being."
"I've tried so many things before, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration. It's disheartening to feel like you've exhausted all options. My approach is different because I focus on personalized solutions based on your unique hormonal profile. We'll use lab testing to identify the root causes of your imbalances and create a targeted plan that addresses those specific issues. Plus, I offer ongoing support and guidance throughout the program."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific outcomes, I can promise that I will provide you with the tools, knowledge, and support you need to achieve hormonal balance. My clients who fully commit to the program and follow my recommendations consistently experience significant improvements in their symptoms and overall well-being. I am confident that if you invest in yourself and follow my program, you will see results."
"I need to think about it."
"I understand. What specifically do you need to think about? Perhaps I can answer any remaining questions or concerns you have right now. I want you to feel 100% confident in your decision."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the prospect's pain points, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the call.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, then reframe the value of your program by highlighting the long-term benefits and potential return on investment.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script regularly to build confidence and fluency.
  • Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful responses.
  • Present your pricing with conviction and highlight the value of your program.
  • Remember that client acquisition is a skill that improves with practice.
  • Believe in the value of your services and the positive impact you can make on your clients' lives.
