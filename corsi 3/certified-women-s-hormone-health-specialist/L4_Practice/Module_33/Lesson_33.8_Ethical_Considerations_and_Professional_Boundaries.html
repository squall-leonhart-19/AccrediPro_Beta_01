Check Your Understanding

1. What are the four phases of a successful discovery call?

The four phases are: Build Rapport, Understand the Problem, Present Your Approach, and Close.

2. How should you respond to the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then ask clarifying questions: "What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or something else?"

Key Takeaways

  • Mastering client acquisition is essential for a thriving practice.
  • A structured discovery call helps you understand your prospect's needs and present your solution effectively.
  • Handling objections with empathy and confidence builds trust and increases your chances of closing the sale.
  • Clearly communicating the value of your program justifies your pricing.
  • Consistent effort in client acquisition leads to a predictable income stream.
