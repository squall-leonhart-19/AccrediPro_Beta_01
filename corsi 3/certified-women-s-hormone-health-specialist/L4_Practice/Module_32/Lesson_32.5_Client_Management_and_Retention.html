  • Feedback Forms: Provide clients with feedback forms after each session to capture immediate impressions and suggestions.

Case Study: Sarah's Retention Success

Client: Sarah, 42 years old

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah was experiencing persistent fatigue, mood swings, and irregular periods. She had been a client for six months but expressed uncertainty about continuing due to feeling overwhelmed by the lifestyle changes.

Intervention: The practitioner implemented a personalized retention strategy, including:

  • A check-in call to address Sarah's concerns and provide additional support.
  • A tailored plan focusing on small, achievable steps to manage her symptoms.
  • A loyalty program offering a discount on her next consultation.

Outcomes: Sarah felt heard and supported, leading her to renew her treatment plan. Her symptoms gradually improved, and she became a strong advocate for the practice.
