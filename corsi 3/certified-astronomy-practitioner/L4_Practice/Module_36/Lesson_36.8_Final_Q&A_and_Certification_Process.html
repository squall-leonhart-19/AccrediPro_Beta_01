Your Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call: A Structured Approach

Phase 1: Connection & Context 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Sarah, and I'm an Astronomy Practitioner. I help people like you navigate life transitions using astrological insights. I appreciate you reading my blog post. What resonated with you the most about it?"
YOU:
"That's interesting! So, what prompted you to book this call? What are you hoping to achieve?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned feeling lost. Can you tell me more about that? What areas of your life feel most affected?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address these feelings? What worked, and what didn't?"
YOU:
"If things continue as they are, where do you see yourself in a year? And if you could wave a magic wand and create your ideal life, what would that look like?"
Phase 3: Introducing Your Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, from what you've shared, it sounds like you're seeking clarity, purpose, and a renewed sense of direction as you navigate this new phase of life. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My approach uses your unique astrological blueprint to uncover your inherent strengths, karmic lessons, and optimal timing for major life decisions. YOU:

Phase 4: Invitation & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Based on our conversation, Susan, I genuinely believe I can help you gain the clarity and direction you're looking for. Does this sound like the support you need? ... Wonderful! Let me briefly share the program details and how we can begin."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the "Connection & Context" phase of the discovery call?

The primary goal is to build rapport with the potential client and understand their initial motivation for scheduling the call.

2. How should you handle the objection, "I'm not sure if I believe in astrology"?

Acknowledge their skepticism and explain that your approach is less about prediction and more about understanding their inherent patterns and potential. Frame it as a tool for self-discovery, not a rigid belief system.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call process builds trust and allows you to understand the client's needs deeply.
  • Confidently address objections by empathizing with the client's concerns and highlighting the value of your program.
  • Clearly present your pricing structure, emphasizing the return on investment (ROI) for the client.
  • Practice your call-to-action to confidently invite clients to embark on a transformative journey with you.
  • Remember, consistent effort in marketing and sales will lead to a thriving practice.
