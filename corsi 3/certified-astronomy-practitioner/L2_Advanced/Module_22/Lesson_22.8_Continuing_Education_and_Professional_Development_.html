Clinical Reasoning Process

Ethical Considerations in Eleanor's Case

Step 1: Scope of Practice
As a Certified Astronomy Practitionerâ„¢, you can address Eleanor's well-being using celestial insights. However, prescribing hormone therapies or diagnosing medical conditions is outside your scope. Emphasize that you are not a substitute for a medical doctor or licensed therapist.
Step 2: Dual Relationships
If Eleanor becomes a close friend or business partner, it could create a conflict of interest. Maintain professional boundaries to ensure objectivity and avoid exploiting the therapeutic relationship. Document all interactions and decisions.
Step 3: Informed Consent
Eleanor must understand the potential benefits and risks of integrating astronomy into her wellness plan. Explain that astral insights are not guarantees and that her active participation is crucial for success. Obtain written consent before starting any intervention.
Step 4: Confidentiality
Protect Eleanor's personal information and chart details. Discuss confidentiality policies upfront and obtain her permission before sharing any information with other healthcare providers. Use secure platforms for communication and data storage.
Step 5: Values Conflicts
If Eleanor's beliefs or decisions conflict with your personal values, address it professionally. Focus on her well-being and goals, while respecting her autonomy. If you cannot provide unbiased support, refer her to another practitioner.

Differential Considerations

Potential Ethical Dilemmas

1

Predictive Dilemmas

Eleanor asks for specific predictions about her fertility window or career success based on planetary transits. Avoid making definitive claims, as these can create false expectations and undermine her agency. Focus on exploring potential opportunities and challenges.

2

Emotional Dependency

Eleanor becomes overly reliant on your guidance and seeks constant reassurance. Encourage her to develop self-reliance and seek support from other sources, such as family, friends, or a therapist. Set clear boundaries on communication and availability.

3

Financial Conflicts

Eleanor offers you a business opportunity or asks for financial advice. Decline any offers that could compromise your objectivity or create a power imbalance. Maintain transparency about fees and avoid exploiting the therapeutic relationship for personal gain.

Key Takeaways

Key Ethical Considerations

  • Prioritize client well-being and autonomy in all decisions.
  • Maintain clear boundaries to avoid conflicts of interest.
  • Obtain informed consent and protect client confidentiality.
  • Recognize the limits of your scope and refer when necessary.
  • Continuously reflect on your ethical practice and seek supervision when needed.

Check Your Understanding

1. A client insists that you interpret their chart to predict the exact date they will meet their soulmate. How do you respond ethically?

Explain that while astronomy can offer insights into relationship patterns and timing, it cannot predict the exact date of meeting a soulmate. Emphasize the importance of personal agency and active participation in creating meaningful connections.

2. You notice a client becoming increasingly dependent on your astrological guidance, calling you daily for reassurance. What ethical steps should you take?

Acknowledge the client's reliance and gently set boundaries on communication. Encourage the client to develop self-reliance and seek support from other sources, such as friends, family, or a therapist. Explain the importance of developing their own intuition and decision-making skills.
