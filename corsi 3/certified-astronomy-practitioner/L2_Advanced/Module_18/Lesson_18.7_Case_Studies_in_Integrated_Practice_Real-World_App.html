Presenting Symptoms: Feeling unfulfilled in current administrative role, unsure of career path, seeking more meaningful work.

Celestial Context (C): Eleanor's birth chart reveals a strong emphasis on her Venus in Taurus, indicating a need for stability and creative expression. Her Saturn in Capricorn suggests a drive for achievement but also a fear of failure. We established a connection by validating her feelings of stagnation and acknowledging her inherent creative potential.

Orbital Observation(O): We examined Eleanor's progressed chart, noting Venus entering Gemini, signaling a shift towards more communicative and versatile career options. We also observed Saturn's transit through her 10th house, indicating a period of career evaluation and potential restructuring. This observation was key to understanding the timing of her career dissatisfaction.

Stellar Strengths (S): Eleanor's innate strengths lie in her artistic abilities (Venus) and her organizational skills (Saturn). We identified opportunities to leverage these strengths in a career that combined creativity with structure, such as event planning or interior design.

Milestone Mapping (M): We used Eleanor's solar returns to identify key years for career progression. We pinpointed a solar return with a strong Jupiter conjunction to her Midheaven as a year ripe for career advancement.

Optimizing Orbit (O): We explored Eleanor's vocational asteroids, discovering a strong affinity for the asteroid Pallas, associated with strategic thinking and craftsmanship. This suggested a potential career path involving problem-solving and creative design.

Sustainable Starlight (S): We advised Eleanor to pursue a career that aligned with her values and provided a sense of purpose. We recommended volunteering in areas she was passionate about to gain experience and build her network.

Outcome: Eleanor enrolled in an online interior design course. Within a year, she secured a part-time position with a local design firm, reporting increased job satisfaction and a renewed sense of purpose. She is now earning supplemental income while transitioning to her new career.

Outcome: The Millers attended couples therapy, incorporating the insights from their astrological charts. They reported improved communication, reduced conflict, and a stronger emotional connection. They learned to appreciate each other's differences and work together as a team.
