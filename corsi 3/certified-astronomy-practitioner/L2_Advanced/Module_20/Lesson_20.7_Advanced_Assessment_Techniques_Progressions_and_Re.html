Understanding Secondary Progressions

Secondary progressions are a symbolic time-lord technique that equate each day after birth to one year of life. This allows us to see how the natal chart evolves over time, mirroring psychological and spiritual development. Progressions can reveal shifts in values, relationships, and career paths, offering a dynamic view of the client's journey.

Key aspects to consider when interpreting secondary progressions include:

  • Progressed Sun: Indicates evolving identity and life purpose.
  • Progressed Moon: Reflects changing emotional needs and patterns.
  • Progressed Ascendant: Shows how the client presents themselves to the world over time.
  • Planetary Aspects: Highlights periods of integration, challenge, or transformation.

Understanding these progressions allows you to advise clients on how to best navigate their personal evolution, aligning their actions with their evolving cosmic narrative as defined by the C.O.S.M.O.S.M.O.S. Frameworkâ„¢.

Analyzing Solar and Lunar Returns

Solar and lunar returns provide snapshots of the astrological climate for a specific year or month, respectively. The solar return chart is cast for the moment the Sun returns to its natal position each year, while the lunar return chart is cast for each time the Moon returns to its natal position each month. These charts highlight prevailing themes and potential challenges, helping clients prepare for upcoming events and opportunities.

When analyzing solar and lunar returns, pay attention to:

  • Ascendant and Midheaven: Indicate the focus and direction of the year or month.
  • Planetary Placements: Highlight areas of life that will be emphasized.
  • Aspects: Reveal potential conflicts or harmonious influences.
  • Houses: Show where events are likely to unfold.

By interpreting these charts, you can provide clients with actionable strategies for maximizing their potential and mitigating risks, further supporting their journey towards Optimizing their Orbit.

Combining Techniques

To provide the most comprehensive assessments, it's crucial to integrate progressions and returns with other astrological techniques, such as transit charts and synastry charts. Transits provide a real-time view of planetary influences, while synastry charts examine the dynamics between individuals. Case Study: Life Purpose and Direction

Case Study: Sarah, 38

Client: Sarah, a 38-year-old marketing executive, felt unfulfilled in her career and sought clarity on her life purpose and direction.

Assessment:

  • Natal Chart: Indicated a strong potential for creative expression but a tendency to prioritize security over passion.
  • Secondary Progressions: Showed her Progressed Sun entering Leo, suggesting a growing need for recognition and self-expression. Progressed Moon in the 10th house highlighted a desire for career fulfillment.
  • Solar Return Chart: The current solar return had Mars conjunct the Midheaven, indicating a year of taking action in her career.
  • Transits: Showed Saturn transiting her 5th house of creativity, indicating a period of discipline and structure in her creative pursuits.

Intervention: Based on the analysis, Sarah was advised to explore creative outlets related to her marketing skills (e.g., content creation, branding). She was encouraged to take a course in creative writing and to seek opportunities to showcase her work.

Outcome: Within six months, Sarah launched a successful blog focused on marketing and creativity. She received positive feedback and began to feel more aligned with her life purpose. She eventually transitioned into a more creative role within her company, feeling more fulfilled and engaged.

Case Study: David, 45

Client: David, a 45-year-old teacher, was experiencing significant challenges in his relationships and sought guidance on how to navigate these difficulties.

Assessment:

  • Natal Chart: Showed a strong emphasis on partnerships but also a tendency to avoid conflict.
  • Secondary Progressions: Revealed his Progressed Venus squaring his natal Saturn, suggesting a period of relationship challenges and re-evaluation.
  • Lunar Return Chart: The current lunar return had the Moon in the 7th house of partnerships, highlighting the importance of addressing relationship issues.
  • Transits: Showed Uranus transiting his 7th house, indicating unexpected changes and disruptions in his relationships.

Intervention: David was advised to focus on open communication and setting healthy boundaries in his relationships. He was encouraged to seek couples counseling with his partner and to address any underlying issues that were contributing to the conflict.

Outcome: Through therapy and conscious effort, David and his partner were able to improve their communication and resolve many of their conflicts. David reported feeling more secure and fulfilled in his relationships.
