Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on identifying Stellar Strengths. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your story. Many people find themselves at similar crossroads, especially after significant life changes. It takes courage to acknowledge that something feels 'missing'. Let's explore what might reignite your inner spark."
MARIA:
"I appreciate that. It's hard to admit that I'm not completely happy when I have so much to be grateful for."
YOU:
"Absolutely. And acknowledging that feeling is the first step. I'd like to guide you through an exercise to uncover your Stellar Strengths ‚Äì those innate talents and passions that make you uniquely you. How does that sound?"
MARIA:
"That sounds intriguing. I'm open to it."
YOU:
"Great. We'll focus on identifying activities and experiences that bring you a sense of joy, flow, and effortless energy. Think back to different stages of your life ‚Äì childhood, adolescence, early career ‚Äì and consider what truly lit you up. We're not looking for 'shoulds' or expectations, just pure, unadulterated joy."

Check Your Understanding

1. What's the primary goal of this coaching session with Maria?

The primary goal is to help Maria uncover her Stellar Strengths ‚Äì those innate talents and passions that make her uniquely her. This will help her rediscover her passions and create a more meaningful life.

2. What's a common mistake to avoid when coaching someone in Maria's situation?

A common mistake is jumping to solutions without first exploring her strengths and passions. It's important to focus on understanding her Stellar Strengths before suggesting activities or changes.

Key Takeaways

  • Focus on validating the client's feelings and acknowledging their desire for change.
  • Emphasize the importance of identifying Stellar Strengths as a foundation for rediscovering passions.
  • Avoid imposing your own values and passions on the client.
  • Encourage the client to explore interests from different stages of their life.
  • Help the client translate their passions into concrete actions and goals.
