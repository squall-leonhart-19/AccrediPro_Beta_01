The 4-Tier Transformation

Our program is structured around a 4-tier transformation designed to take you from foundational knowledge to practical mastery. Here's what you can expect at each level:

  1. Tier 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15): This is where you'll build your core understanding of astronomy principles and our unique methodology. You'll learn the fundamentals and develop a solid base for your practice.
  2. Tier 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23): In this tier, we delve into more complex cases and clinical applications. You'll gain a deeper understanding of advanced techniques and strategies.
  3. Tier 3: Master (Modules 24-29): Here, you'll refine your skills to the level where you can train others and supervise practices. This is about becoming a leader in the field.
  4. Tier 4: Practice (Modules 30-36): This final tier is all about building your business and attracting clients. You'll learn the practical aspects of running a successful Astronomy Practitioner business.
