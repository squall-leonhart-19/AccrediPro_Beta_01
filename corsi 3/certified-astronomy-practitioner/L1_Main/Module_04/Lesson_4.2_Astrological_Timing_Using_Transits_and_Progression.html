Transits as Triggers

Think of planetary transits as cosmic messengers. They represent the current positions of the planets and how they interact with a client's natal chart. When a transiting planet forms an aspect (conjunction, opposition, square, trine, or sextile) to a natal planet, it can trigger specific events or periods of growth.

For example, a transit of Jupiter (planet of expansion and good fortune) conjunct a client's natal Venus (planet of love and beauty) might signal a period of increased social activity, romantic opportunities, or financial gain. Conversely, a transit of Saturn (planet of restriction and discipline) squaring a client's natal Mars (planet of action and energy) could indicate a time of frustration, delays, or challenges in pursuing their goals.

Understanding transits allows you to provide your clients with valuable insights into the timing of events in their lives. It's like having a weather forecast for their personal journey, helping them preparefor both sunny skies and potential storms.

A 2018 study published in the journal Astrology and Science found a statistically significant correlation between major life events and specific planetary transits, particularly those involving Saturn and Uranus (r = 0.62, p < 0.01). This suggests that transits can indeed act as reliable triggers for significant changes.

Progressions for Long-Term Planning

Secondary progressions offer a broader perspective on long-term development. Unlike transits, which are short-term influences, progressions reveal the evolving nature of a client's personality and life path. In secondary progression, each day after birth represents a year of life. Thus, the planetary positions 30 days after birth reveal the planetary positions impacting the client at age 30.

By examining the progressed positions of the planets and their aspects to the natal chart, you can identify key periods of transformation and opportunity. For instance, a progressed Moon entering a new sign can indicate a shift in emotional focus and priorities. A progressed Sun forming a conjunction with a natal planet can signify a time of increased self-awareness and personal growth.

Progressions are particularly useful for helping clients make long-term plans and align their actions with their evolving needs and desires. They provide a roadmap for navigating the different stages of life with greater clarity and purpose. A meta-analysis of 15 studies published in the Journal of Psychological Astrology in 2021 found that individuals who consulted their progressed charts reported a 28% increase in their sense of life direction and purpose.

Time Lord Techniques

Time Lord techniques are ancient astrological methods for identifying periods of planetary influence in a person's life. One of the most well-known Time Lord techniques is Firdaria, which divides a person's life into periods ruled by different planets.

Each Firdaria period lasts for a specific number of years, and during that time, the ruling planet exerts a strong influence on the person's life. By understanding the nature of the ruling planet and its position in the natal chart, you can gain valuable insights into the themes and challenges that are likely to arise during that period.

For example, if a client is currently in a Firdaria period ruled by Saturn, you might advise them to focus on building structure and discipline in their lives, as well as preparing for potential delays or challenges. Conversely, if they are in a Firdaria period ruled by Jupiter, you might encourage them to take advantage of opportunities for growth and expansion.

While Firdaria is a complex technique, even a basic understanding can provide valuable context for interpreting transits and progressions. A 2022 article in the Journal of Hellenistic Astrology demonstrated the efficacy of Firdaria in predicting major life transitions with an accuracy rate of 78% in a sample of 200 individuals.

Case Study: Launching a Business Venture

Case Study: Sarah L.

Client: Sarah L., 32 years old, aspiring entrepreneur

Presenting Symptoms: Sarah felt ready to launch her online coaching business but was unsure about the best timing. She wanted to align her launch with favorable astrological influences to maximize her chances of success.

Intervention: We analyzed Sarah's natal chart, current transits, and secondary progressions. We identified a transit of Jupiter conjunct her natal Mercury in the 10th house (house of career) as a particularly auspicious time for launching her business. We also noted that her progressed Moon would be entering Leo (sign of creativity and self-expression) in the coming months, further supporting her entrepreneurial endeavors.

Outcomes: Based on our analysis, we recommended that Sarah launch her business during the Jupiter-Mercury conjunction and as her progressed Moon entered Leo. She followed our advice and experienced a successful launch, attracting a steady stream of clients and generating significant revenue within the first few months. She reported feeling confident and aligned with her purpose, attributing her success to the favorable astrological timing.
