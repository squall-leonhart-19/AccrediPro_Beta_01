Secondary Progressions

Secondary progressions are a symbolic technique where each day after birth represents a year of life. They reveal the inner development and evolution of a person's character and life direction. As a reminder from previous lessons, this involves advancing the planets and angles in the natal chart according to their daily motion, providing a symbolic representation of inner growth and unfolding potential.

Exploring Solar Arc Directions

Detailed Analysis of Transits

Transits involve observing the current positions of planets in the sky (the transiting planets) and their aspects to the natal chart. Transits reveal the timing of specific events and experiences, highlighting periods of challenge, opportunity, and transformation. We'll now delve into how to interpret these transits with precision, aligning with the Timing Triggers aspect of the C.O.S.M.O.S. Frameworkâ„¢.

  • Influence of Transiting Planets on Natal Planets and Angles:
    • Each planet brings a unique energy when it transits a natal planet or angle. For example, a transit of Jupiter can bring expansion and opportunity, while a transit of Saturn can bring restriction and challenge.
    • Pay attention to the nature of the aspect (conjunction, opposition, square, trine, sextile) as this will influence how the energy manifests.
  • Outer Planet Transits:
    • Transits of the outer planets (Uranus, Neptune, Pluto) are particularly significant as they tend to bring long-lasting and transformative changes.
    • These transits often correlate with major life events and shifts in consciousness.

Interpreting Solar Returns

A solar return chart is a chart cast for the moment each year when the Sun returns to its exact natal position. This chart provides a snapshot of the themes and energies that will be prominent in the year ahead, offering a detailed forecast of potential events and experiences.

  • Calculating the Solar Return Chart: Use astrological software or online calculators to cast the solar return chart for the location where the person will be on their birthday.
  • Identifying Key Themes and Potential Events:
    • Pay attention to the ascendant and midheaven of the solar return chart, as these indicate the overall focus and direction for the year.
    • Note the house placements of the planets, as this reveals areas of life that will be emphasized.
    • Look for aspects between planets in the solar return chart, as well as aspects to the natal chart. These aspects can highlight potential events and challenges.
