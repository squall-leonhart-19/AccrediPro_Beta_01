Differential Considerations

What Else Could Be Going On?

1

Adrenal Fatigue/Dysregulation

Chronic stress and insomnia can lead to adrenal fatigue, which can exacerbate migraines, anxiety, and energy depletion. Consider adrenal support strategies.

2

Nutrient Deficiencies

Long-term medication use (Amitriptyline, Famotidine) can lead to deficiencies in B vitamins, magnesium, and other essential nutrients. Consider nutrient testing and supplementation.

3

Underlying Autoimmune Condition

Fibromyalgia can sometimes be associated with underlying autoimmune conditions. Monitor for signs and symptoms of autoimmune disease and consider referral for further evaluation if indicated.

4

Hormonal Imbalances

At age 48, hormonal fluctuations related to perimenopause could be contributing to migraines, insomnia, and anxiety. Consider hormone testing and support strategies.
