Handling Objections

Navigating Concerns with Confidence

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand. Many of my clients are busy professionals. Let's look at your schedule and see if we can find a time that works. Remember, investing in yourself is an investment in all areas of your life. Consider this self-care, not just another chore."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What aspects do you need to think about? I want to be sure I've provided you with all the information you need to make an informed decision. Perhaps we can address those concerns now?"
"I've tried other programs before, and they didn't work."
"I hear your frustration. What was missing from those experiences? The Certified Energy Healing Practitioner approach is different because it's tailored to your unique energy signature. We focus on identifying and releasing the root causes of your challenges."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of a discovery call?

The primary goal is to determine if your services are a good fit for the prospect's needs and to build rapport. It's about understanding their challenges and desires, not just selling your program.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then gently inquire about what specific aspects they need to consider. Offer to provide additional information or address any remaining concerns.

3. Why is it important to practice your pricing presentation?

Practicing your pricing presentation builds confidence and ensures you can clearly communicate the value of your services. This helps prospects feel comfortable with their investment.

Key Takeaways

  • Discovery calls are about building genuine connections and understanding client needs.
  • Confidently address objections by acknowledging concerns and providing valuable solutions.
  • Practice your pricing presentation to communicate the value of your services effectively.
  • Client acquisition is a skill that improves with practice and consistent effort
