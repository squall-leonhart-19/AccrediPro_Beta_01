Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's pain points, presenting your solution, and confidently closing the call.

2. How should you handle the objection "It's too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern, reframe the value of your program, and ask about their previous investments. Highlight the potential ROI in terms of improved well-being and reduced stress-related costs.
