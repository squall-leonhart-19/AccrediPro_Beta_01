Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on releasing old energy and rebalancing. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your story. It sounds like you've been carrying a heavy load. In our work together, we'll focus on releasing the energy of the past and rebalancing your energy field to attract the future you desire."
MARIA:
"That sounds wonderful, but I'm not sure I understand what you mean by 'releasing energy'."
YOU:
"Think of it like this: emotions and experiences can leave energetic imprints on your body and energy field. We'll use specific techniques to gently clear those imprints, allowing you to feel lighter and more free. It's like decluttering your inner space."
MARIA:
"Okay, that makes more sense. So, how do we 'rebalance' my energy?"
YOU:
"Rebalancing involves restoring harmony to your energy centers, also known as chakras. When these centers are balanced, energy flows freely, supporting your physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being. We'll use techniques to activate and align these centers."
MARIA:
"This all sounds very promising. I'm ready to try."
YOU:
"Great! Let's begin with a simple exercise to identify where you're holding onto the most tension in your body. Close your eyes, take a few deep breaths, and notice where you feel the most tightness or discomfort..."

Common Mistakes to Avoid

‚ùå Using jargon or overly technical terms
‚úì Keep your language simple and easy to understand. "Energetic imprints" is better than "karmic residue."
‚ùå Dismissing or minimizing the client's past experiences
‚úì Acknowledge her pain and validate her feelings. "It sounds like you've been through a lot."
‚ùå Jumping to solutions before building rapport
‚úì Take the time to listen and understand her story. Ask open-ended questions.
‚ùå Making promises you can't keep
‚úì Be realistic and honest about what energy healing can and cannot do. Focus on empowering her to take control of her healing journey.
‚ùå Forgetting to ground the client
‚úì Always end a session by guiding the client back into their body and the present moment.

Check Your Understanding

1. What's a good analogy for explaining "releasing energy" to a client?

Decluttering your inner space. Clearing energetic imprints left by emotions and experiences.

2. How can you help a client identify where they're holding onto tension in their body?

Guide them through a simple exercise: close their eyes, take deep breaths, and notice where they feel the most tightness or discomfort.

Key Takeaways

  • Release involves clearing energetic imprints from past experiences.
  • Rebalancing restores harmony to the energy centers (chakras).
  • Use simple language and avoid jargon.
  • Validate the client's experiences and feelings.
  • Empower the client to take control of their healing journey.
