Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to explain the concept of an energy field to a new client before beginning an assessment?

Explaining the concept builds trust and helps the client understand the rationale behind the assessment. It also addresses potential skepticism and encourages openness to the process.

2. What is the key difference between using an energy field assessment and making a definitive diagnosis?

An energy field assessment provides insights and starting points for exploration, while a definitive diagnosis is a medical conclusion. The assessment informs the coaching process, but does not replace medical advice.

Key Takeaways

  • Always explain the energy field concept clearly and simply.
  • Address any skepticism or lack of understanding with patience and validation.
  • Connect the assessment to the client's specific goals and challenges.
  • Use the assessment as a starting point for further exploration and discussion.
  • Practice the scanning process silently to develop your sensitivity and intuition.
