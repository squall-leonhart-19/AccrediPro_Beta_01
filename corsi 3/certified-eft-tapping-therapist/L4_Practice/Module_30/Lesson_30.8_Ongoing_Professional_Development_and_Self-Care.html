Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I believe in tapping."
"I understand your skepticism. Many people feel that way initially. EFT is based on the principles of acupressure and psychology. While it may seem unusual, numerous studies have demonstrated its effectiveness. For example, a study published in the Journal of Nervous and Mental Disease found that EFT significantly reduced anxiety symptoms (Gaudry et al., 2016). I can share some of this research with you if you'd like. Are you willing to try it and see if it works for you?"
"I'm too busy to commit to weekly sessions."
"I appreciate your honesty. Many of my clients have demanding schedules. The sessions are designed to be highly efficient and focused on your specific goals. We can also explore flexible scheduling options to fit your needs. Remember, investing in your well-being is an investment in your productivity and overall performance. What small changes could you make in your schedule to prioritize your mental health?"
"I've tried therapy before, and it didn't work."
"I'm sorry to hear that. EFT is different from traditional talk therapy. It combines cognitive restructuring with somatic techniques to address the emotional roots of your issues. It‚Äôs a more direct approach that can often yield faster results. What specifically didn't work for you in therapy before? Perhaps EFT can address those areas more effectively."
"I need to think about it and compare other options."
"That's perfectly reasonable. What specific aspects are you comparing? I can provide you with more detailed information about my program and answer any remaining questions you have. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not satisfied after the first month, I'll refund your investment. How does that sound?"

Case Study:Client Sarah, Anxiety Reduction and Career Advancement

Client: Sarah, 35, Marketing Manager

Presenting Symptoms: Generalized anxiety, difficulty sleeping, procrastination, and fear of public speaking, impacting her ability to present at conferences and advance in her career.

Intervention: 12-week EFT tapping program, focusing on identifying and addressing specific anxiety triggers related to work performance and public speaking. Sessions included cognitive restructuring, somatic experiencing, and personalized tapping scripts.

Outcomes: Sarah reported a 60% reduction in anxiety symptoms based on the GAD-7 scale. She successfully presented at two industry conferences and received positive feedback. Sarah also reported improved sleep quality and increased confidence in her abilities. Follow-up assessments at 6 months showed sustained improvements.

What are the four key phases of a successful sales call, according to the script provided?

Confidence in presenting your pricing conveys the value of your services and inspires trust in potential clients. It reinforces that you believe in the worth of what you offer.
