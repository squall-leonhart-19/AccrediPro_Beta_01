Discovery Call Script (30 Minutes)

Your Call Structure

Phase 1: Warm Welcome & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, thank you so much for scheduling this call. I'm Maya, and I'm excited to chat with you. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me on Instagram. What caught your eye?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Her Challenges 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the burnout and anxiety. When did you start feeling this way?"
YOU:
"What does a typical day look like for you?"
YOU:
"On a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the highest, how would you rate your stress levels?"
YOU:
"What have you tried in the past to manage your stress and anxiety?"
Phase 3: Presenting EFT as a Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Sarah, it sounds like you're carrying a lot on your shoulders. EFT (Emotional Freedom Techniques), or tapping, is a powerful tool that can help you release stress and anxiety by targeting the body's energy system."
YOU:
"Unlike other methods, EFT addresses the root cause of your emotions, providing long-term relief. My 8-week program is designed to help you learn EFT and apply it to your daily life."
YOU:
"Many of my clients, like you, start experiencing significant relief within the first few sessions. They report feeling calmer, more focused, and more in control."
Phase 4: Invitation to Work Together 5 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, Sarah, I truly believe EFT can help you. Would you like to learn more about my program and how we can get started?"

Handling Objections

Common Concerns & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time for this."
"I understand. The program requires about an hour a week for our sessions, plus 15-20 minutes a day for tapping. Think of it as an investment in your well-being. What if, by reducing your stress, you actually became MORE productive and had MORE time for everything else?"
"I'm worried it won't work for me."
"That's a valid concern. EFT has a high success rate. A 2016 meta-analysis in the Journal of Nervous and Mental Disease found that EFT significantly reduces anxiety symptoms (p < 0.001). I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy after the first two sessions, I'll refund your money."
"Can I pay in installments?"
"Yes, absolutely. I offer a payment plan where you can pay in two installments. This makes it more accessible for everyone."
"I need to think about it."
"Of course. What specifically do you need to think about? Is it the financial investment, the time commitment, or something else? Knowing this will help me provide you with the information you need to make the best decision for yourself."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's challenges, presenting EFT as a solution, and inviting them to work with you.

2. How can you address the objection "I'm not sure if I have the time for this"?

Frame the program as an investment in their well-being and suggest that reducing stress can actually increase productivity and free up more time.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Prepare confident responses to common objections.
  • Clearly and confidently present your pricing and payment options.
  • Focus on the value and transformation you offer to potential clients.
  • Remember, every "no" brings you closer to a "yes."
