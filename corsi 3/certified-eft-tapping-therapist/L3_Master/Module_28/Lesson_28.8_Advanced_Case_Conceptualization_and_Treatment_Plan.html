Check Your Understanding

1. Why is collaborative care important when working with complex trauma cases?

Collaborative care ensures clients receive comprehensive support from a team of professionals, addressing various aspects of their well-being and preventing burnout for the practitioner.

2. What are some key self-care strategies you would recommend to a new EFT practitioner?

Recommend regular breaks, mindfulness practices, setting clear boundaries with clients, seeking supervision, and engaging in activities that promote personal well-being and relaxation.

Key Takeaways

  • Supervision provides crucial support and guidance to new practitioners.
  • Prioritize validating the mentee's experience and normalizing challenges.
  • Emphasize ethical boundaries and the importance of scope of practice.
  • Teach practical skills like grounding techniques and collaborative care strategies.
  • Encourage self-care practices to prevent burnout and maintain well-being.
