The Methodology Framework

At the heart of this certification is our unique methodology framework, designed to guide you through every step of the narcissistic abuse recovery process. This framework is evidence-based, client-centered, and adaptable to a wide range of individual needs.

The framework is comprised of 5 key components:

  1. Assessment: Thoroughly evaluating the client's experiences, identifying patterns of abuse, and assessing the impact on their mental and emotional health.
  2. Education: Providing clients with a comprehensive understanding of narcissistic abuse, its dynamics, and its long-term effects.
  3. Empowerment: Helping clients reclaim their sense of self, build self-esteem, and develop healthy boundaries.
  4. Skills Development: Equipping clients with practical coping mechanisms, communication strategies, and conflict resolution skills.
  5. Integration: Supporting clients in integrating their experiences, healing from trauma, and building a fulfilling life free from abuse.

Throughout the program, you'll learn how to apply this framework effectively, tailoring it to each client's unique circumstances and goals.
