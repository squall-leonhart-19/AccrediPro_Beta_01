Clinical Reasoning Process

Applying the F.A.I.T.H. Framework‚Ñ¢

Step 1: Identify Core Issues
Eleanor's core issues include spiritual disconnection, chronic stress, and physical symptoms that are likely interconnected. The long-term use of PPIs may be contributing to nutrient deficiencies and exacerbating her symptoms.
Step 2: Explore Root Causes
Explore how Eleanor's demanding career and lack of work-life balance contribute to her stress and spiritual disconnection. The GERD may be related to dietary habits, stress, or H. pylori infection (even if previously treated). Iron deficiency could be worsened by PPI use.
Step 3: Develop a Christian Coaching Strategy
Use the F.A.I.T.H. Framework‚Ñ¢ to guide Eleanor toward a deeper understanding of her calling, aspirations, and potential for transformation. Encourage introspection through prayer, journaling, and biblical reflection.
Step 4: Prioritize Interventions
Address the spiritual and emotional aspects of Eleanor's challenges alongside practical lifestyle modifications. Begin with simple, sustainable changes to her diet, sleep hygiene, and stress management practices.

Differential Considerations

What Else Could Be Going On?

1

Subclinical Hypothyroidism

Fatigue, anxiety, and headaches can be symptoms of thyroid dysfunction. Consider a full thyroid panel including TSH, Free T4, and Free T3.

2

Adrenal Fatigue/Dysregulation

Chronic stress can lead to adrenal dysregulation, affecting energy levels, sleep, and overall well-being. Consider assessing cortisol levels.

3

Nutrient Deficiencies

Long-term PPI use can impair the absorption of B12, iron, magnesium, and calcium. Evaluate nutrient status through lab testing.

4

Depression

Anxiety and feelings of overwhelm can sometimes be symptoms of underlying depression. Assess Eleanor's mood and consider referral to a mental health professional if needed.

When to Refer

Red Flags ‚Äî Refer to MD or Specialist

  • Unexplained weight loss or gain may indicate underlying medical conditions.
  • Severe abdominal pain or bleeding requires immediate medical attention.
  • Persistent headaches unresponsive to treatment need neurological evaluation.
  • Signs of clinical depression or suicidal ideation necessitate mental health referral.
  • Iron deficiency anemia unresponsive to supplementation warrants further investigation by a physician.

Key Takeaways

  • Complex cases require a holistic approach that addresses the interconnectedness of spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being.
  • The F.A.I.T.H. Framework‚Ñ¢ provides a valuable structure for guiding clients toward spiritual growth and transformation.
  • Long-term medication use can have unintended consequences and should be carefully evaluated.
  • Christian Life Coaches must recognize their scope of practice and refer clients to other healthcare professionals when necessary.
  • Small, sustainable lifestyle changes can have a significant impact on overall health and well-being.
