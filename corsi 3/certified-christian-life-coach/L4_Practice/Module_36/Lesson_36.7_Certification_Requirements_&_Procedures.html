Certification Maintenance

Maintaining your certification is crucial for staying current with best practices and upholding the ethical standards of Christian life coaching. Certification maintenance involves several key components, ensuring you continue to Honor (H) your commitment to excellence.

  1. Continuing Education: You are required to complete a minimum of 20 hours of continuing education every two years. These hours should be related to Christian life coaching, personal development, or related fields.
  2. Ethical Conduct: Adherence to the ethical guidelines outlined in the course is mandatory. Any breaches of ethical conduct may result in suspension or revocation of your certification.
  3. Renewal Fee: A renewal fee is required every two years to maintain your certification status.

Continuing education not only keeps you updated but also enhances your coaching skills and expands your knowledge base.
