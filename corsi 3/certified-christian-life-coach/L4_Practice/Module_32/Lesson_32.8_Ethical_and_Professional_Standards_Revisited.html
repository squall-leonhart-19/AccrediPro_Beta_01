Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Connection 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan! It's so nice to finally connect. Thank you for taking the time to speak with me. I'm Grace, and I'm a Certified Christian Life Coach. I help women like you rediscover their purpose and live a faith-filled life. To start, tell me what prompted you to reach out?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful that you saw my ad on Facebook. What resonated with you?"
Phase 2: Explore Her Challenges & Desires 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about this feeling of being lost. When did you start feeling this way? How is it impacting your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to reconnect with your faith and find your purpose? What has worked, and what hasn't?"
YOU:
"If nothing changes, where do you see yourself in a year? And if you could wave a magic wand and create your ideal life, what would that look like?"
Phase 3: Introduce Your Coaching Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, here's what I'm hearing: [Summarize her pain points and desires, focusing on faith and purpose]. Does that resonate with you?"
YOU:
"My Christian Life Coaching program is designed to help you connect with God's plan for your life. Together, we'll explore your spiritual gifts, identify your core values, and create a personalized action plan to live a more fulfilling life. It's a 3-month commitment with weekly 1:1 sessions, personalized resources, and ongoing support."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the support and guidance you've been looking for? ... Wonderful! Let me share the details of the program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Empathetic Responses

"I'm not sure I have the time right now."
"I understand. Time is precious. However, consider this: What if investing a few hours each week in coaching could actually *save* you time in the long run by providing clarity, focus, and a renewed sense of purpose? Many of my clients find they become more efficient and productive in all areas of their lives."
"I need to pray about it."
"That's wonderful, Susan. I encourage you to seek God's guidance. Would it be helpful if I sent you some information about the program that you can review during your prayer time? Also, feel free to ask God for clarity and discernment. Remember, God often uses people and resources to answer our prayers."
"It's more than I was expecting to invest."
"I appreciate you sharing that. Let's break it down. Think of this as an investment in yourself, your faith, and your future. What is it worth to you to find your purpose, live a life that honors God, and feel truly fulfilled? Also, I do offer a payment plan to make it more accessible."
"I'm not sure coaching really works."
"I understand your skepticism. Many people are unsure about coaching initially. However, Christian Life Coaching is based on biblical principles and proven strategies for personal growth. I have several client testimonials I can share with you, and I offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied after the first month, I'll refund your investment."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of building rapport in the initial phase of a discovery call?

Building rapport involves creating a personal connection by expressing genuine interest, actively listening, finding common ground, and showing empathy. In a Christian context, this can also involve sharing your faith journey (if appropriate) and praying for the client.

2. How can you effectively address a client's concern that your coaching program is "too expensive"?

Acknowledge their concern and reframe the investment as an opportunity for transformation and long-term gains. Highlight the value they'll receive (e.g., finding purpose, improving relationships, growing closer to God) and offer payment plans or financing options to make it more accessible.

3. What are some compelling closing lines you can use to encourage a prospect to enroll in your Christian Life Coaching program?

Use closing lines that express your belief in their potential and the transformative power of coaching. Examples include: "I truly believe God has brought us together for a reason. Are you ready to take the next step?" or "I'm confident that with God's guidance and my support, you can achieve your goals. Are you ready to start living the life God has called you to?"

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is Key: Thoroughly research your prospect and understand their needs before the discovery call.
  • Empathy and Connection: Build a genuine connection by actively listening, showing empathy, and understanding their pain points.
  • Value-Based Pricing: Present your pricing with confidence, highlighting the transformative value of your coaching program.
  • Objection Handling: Anticipate common objections and prepare thoughtful, empathetic responses.
  • Faith-Based Approach: Integrate your faith into your coaching practice by praying for your clients and seeking God's guidance.
