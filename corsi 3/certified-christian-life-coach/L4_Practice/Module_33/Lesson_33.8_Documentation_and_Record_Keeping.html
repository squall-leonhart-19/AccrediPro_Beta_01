Discovery Call Script

30-Minute Discovery Call Structure

Phase 1: Rapport & Intro 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm Grace Williams, and I'm excited to learn more about your career goals. To start, what prompted you to reach out for career coaching?"
YOU:
"That's interesting. What aspects of your current role are no longer fulfilling?"
Phase 2: Problem & Vision 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about feeling 'stuck'. What does that look like in your daily life?"
YOU:
"What have you tried so far to address this feeling?"
YOU:
"If you could paint a picture of your ideal career, what would it look like? What kind of work would you be doing, and how would you feel?"
Phase 3: Solution & Value 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're seeking clarity, confidence, and a strategic plan for your career transition. Is that accurate?"
YOU:
"My coaching program is designed to help you achieve just that. We'll identify your strengths, values, and passions, and then create a roadmap to align your career with your ideal vision. Many of my clients secure new roles or launch fulfilling businesses within 3-6 months of completing the program."
YOU:
"Specifically, my approach involves [mention 2-3 key aspects of your coaching methodology, e.g., values assessment, skills inventory, networking strategies]."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, does this sound like the support you've been looking for? ... Great! I have a few options available to get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Confident Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in yourself is a big decision. Let's look at the potential ROI. How much is staying in your current, unfulfilling role costing you in terms of happiness, energy, and potential income? My clients often find that the investment pays for itself through increased earning potential and improved quality of life. I also have payment plan options available."
"I'm too busy to commit to coaching."
"I hear you. Life gets busy. However, think of coaching as an investment in streamlining your efforts. We'll focus on the most impactful strategies to get you results faster. Plus, the program is designed to fit into your schedule with flexible session times. What if investing a few hours a week could save you months or even years of feeling stuck?"
"I've tried other things before, and they didn't work."
"I appreciate you sharing that. It's understandable to feel skeptical. What was missing in those previous experiences? My approach is different because [highlight your unique methodology or point of differentiation]. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee, so you can feel confident in your investment."
"Can you guarantee I'll get a new job?"
"While I can't guarantee a specific outcome like a new job, I *can* guarantee that you'll gain clarity, confidence, and a strategic plan to move forward. My clients consistently report significant improvements in their self-awareness, networking skills, and job search effectiveness, which dramatically increases their chances of success. I equip you with the tools and support you need to achieve your goals."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key phases of a successful discovery call?

The key phases are: building rapport, understanding the problem and vision, presenting your solution and value, and closing with clear next steps.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need to think, then gently inquire about the specific concerns they have. Offer to provide more information or address any remaining questions.

3. What is the importance of practicing your pricing presentation?

Practicing your pricing presentation builds confidence and ensures you can clearly articulate the value of your services. It helps you avoid hesitation and present your prices with conviction.

Key Takeaways

  • A structured discovery call process is essential for effective client acquisition.
  • Understanding your prospect's needs and pain points is crucial for tailoring your pitch.
  • Handling objections with empathy and confidence can turn potential roadblocks into opportunities.
  • Practicing your pricing presentation builds confidence and ensures you communicate your value effectively.
  • Realistic income goals are achievable with consistent effort and a strategic approach.
