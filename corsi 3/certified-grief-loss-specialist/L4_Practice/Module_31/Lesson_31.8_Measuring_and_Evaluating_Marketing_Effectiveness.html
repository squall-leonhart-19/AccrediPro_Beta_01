Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Welcome 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Susan, thank you for reaching out. I'm Olivia, and I'm so glad you're here. How are you doing today?"
YOU:
"I saw you found me through Facebook. What caught your eye in particular?"
Phase 2: Understanding & Empathy 10 min
YOU:
"I understand you lost your mother six months ago. That must be incredibly difficult. Can you tell me a bit about what that's been like for you?"
YOU:
"What are some of the biggest challenges you're facing right now as you navigate this grief?"
YOU:
"It sounds like you're carrying a heavy load. What does 'feeling like yourself again' mean to you?"
Phase 3: Presenting Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Susan, what I offer is a structured, HEART-centered approach to grief transformation. We'll Honor your grief, Explore your loss, Assess your needs, Restore hope, and Transform your grief. We'll work together to create a safe space for you to process your emotions, find healthy coping mechanisms, and ultimately, honor your mother's memory in a way that feels authentic and healing. Does that sound like something that resonates with you?"
YOU:
"My clients often tell me that they feel a sense of relief and renewed purpose after just a few sessions. We'll focus on practical strategies you can implement immediately to start feeling more grounded and connected."
Phase 4: Invitation & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Susan, based on what you've shared, I truly believe I can help you navigate this journey. Are you open to learning more about my program?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of an effective discovery call?

An effective discovery call focuses on building rapport, understanding the client's needs, presenting your approach with empathy, and confidently inviting them to take the next step.

2. How should you handle budget objections?

Acknowledge the client's concern, explore the value they'll receive, and discuss payment options to make the program more accessible.

Key Takeaways

  • Empathy is Essential: Connect with your prospect on a human level before diving into business.
  • Value Over Price: Focus on the transformation you offer, not just the cost.
  • Confidence is Key: Practice your script and pricing until you feel comfortable and authentic.
  • Objections are Opportunities: View objections as chances to address concerns and build trust.
  • You're Making a Difference: Remember the impact you can have on someone's life through your work.
