Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements to include in a 30-minute discovery call?

Building rapport, understanding the client's needs, presenting your approach, and closing the call.

2. How should you handle the objection "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge their need for time, ask what specific aspects they need to consider, and offer a follow-up to answer any remaining questions.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script to build confidence and fluency.
  • Prepare for common objections and craft thoughtful responses.
  • Present your pricing with assurance and highlight the value of your services.
  • Remember that every "no" brings you closer to a "yes."
