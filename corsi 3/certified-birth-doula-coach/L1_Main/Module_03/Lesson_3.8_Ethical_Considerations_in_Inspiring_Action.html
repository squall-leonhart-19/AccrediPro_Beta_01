Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate a client's feelings before offering solutions?

Validating feelings builds trust and rapport. It shows the client that you understand their experience, making them more receptive to your guidance.

2. What is the risk of immediately offering productivity tips to a client struggling with procrastination?

It can minimize their struggles and make them feel misunderstood. Procrastination often stems from deeper emotional issues, and ignoring those can lead to ineffective solutions.

Key Takeaways

  • Prioritize building rapport and validating your client's experiences.
  • Explore the emotional landscape behind procrastination, not just the surface-level behavior.
  • Ask open-ended questions to encourage clients to share their thoughts and feelings.
  • Clearly set the direction for the coaching session to ensure a focused and productive conversation.
