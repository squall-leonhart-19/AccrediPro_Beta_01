The G.U.I.D.E. Elements Explained

The G.U.I.D.E. Frameworkâ„¢ consists of five key elements, each building upon the last to create a holistic coaching experience:

  1. Gather Information: This initial phase involves collecting relevant data about the doula's current situation, goals, challenges, and strengths. This includes understanding their experience level, client base, marketing strategies, and overall business operations.
  2. Uncover Potential: Here, you work with the doula to identify hidden opportunities and untapped potential. This involves exploring new skills, services, markets, and collaborations.
  3. Inspire Action: This is the phase where you motivate the doula to take concrete steps toward their goals. This involves setting realistic targets, creating action plans, and providing ongoing encouragement and accountability.
  4. Develop Systems: This element focuses on creating sustainable systems and processes to support the doula's long-term success. This includes streamlining workflows, implementing effective marketing strategies, and building a strong support network.
  5. Evaluate & Evolve: This final phase involves regularly assessing progress, identifying areas for improvement, and adapting strategies as needed. This ensures that the doula remains on track and continues to grow and evolve over time.
