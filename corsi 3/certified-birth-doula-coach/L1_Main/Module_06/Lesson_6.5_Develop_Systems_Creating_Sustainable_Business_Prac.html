Case Study: Streamlining Client Onboarding

Case Study: Streamlining Client Onboarding

Client: Sarah Miller, a new birth doula coach

Presenting Problem: Sarah was struggling to manage client inquiries and onboard new clients efficiently. She was spending hours answering emails, scheduling consultations, and sending out welcome packets.

Intervention: Sarah implemented a streamlined client onboarding process using Calendly for scheduling, a pre-designed welcome packet with FAQs, and a client intake form that automatically populated her CRM.

Outcome: Sarah reduced her onboarding time by 70% and improved client satisfaction by creating a seamless and professional experience. She also reported feeling less overwhelmed and more focused on coaching.

Case Study: Improving Financial Tracking

Case Study: Improving Financial Tracking

Client: Emily Davis, an experienced birth doula coach

Presenting Problem: Emily was having difficulty tracking her income and expenses, making it challenging to assess the profitability of her coaching practice. She was using a combination of spreadsheets and manual calculations, which was time-consuming and prone to errors.

Intervention: Emily implemented QuickBooks Online to track her income, expenses, and generate financial reports. She also set up automated bank feeds to streamline transaction reconciliation.

Outcome: Emily gained a clear understanding of her financial performance and identified areas for improvement. She was able to make informed business decisions, such as adjusting her pricing and reducing her expenses, which led to increased profitability.
