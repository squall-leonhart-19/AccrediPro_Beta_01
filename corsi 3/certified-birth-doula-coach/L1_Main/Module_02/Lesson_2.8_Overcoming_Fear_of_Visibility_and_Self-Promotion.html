Your Coaching Script

Here's exactly how to open this conversation, focusing on uncovering potential. Practice saying these words out loud:

YOU:
"Maria, thank you for sharing your story with me. It takes courage to make big changes, and it sounds like you've been through a lot. I want you to know that feeling 'stuck' after a major life transition is incredibly common. Many women find themselves exactly where you are, and it's a perfect time to uncover new possibilities."
MARIA:
"It's just so overwhelming. I feel like I've lost my identity now that I'm not 'Teacher Maria' anymore."
YOU:
"I understand. That sense of identity is powerful, and it's natural to grieve that loss. But I also believe that you have so much more to offer the world, and we're going to explore what those untapped talents and passions might be. Think of this as a chance to redefine yourself, not just replace your old role."
YOU:
"Before we start brainstorming new careers, let's focus on you. What activities have brought you the most joy and fulfillment in the past, even outside of teaching?"
MARIA:
"Well, I've always loved animals. That's why I volunteer at the shelter. And I used to be really into photography. I haven't picked up my camera in years, though."
YOU:
"Those are wonderful clues! Volunteering with animals and photography‚Äîthese are passions that can be woven into a fulfilling and potentially income-generating path. Let's explore those further. What specifically about animals do you enjoy, and what kind of photography did you do?"

  • Maria visibly brightens when discussing her past passions.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to validate a client's feelings of loss before exploring new potential?

Validating their feelings creates trust and allows the client to feel understood, making them more receptive to exploring new possibilities.

2. What are some effective strategies for uncovering a client's hidden passions and talents?

Ask about past hobbies, activities that brought joy, and values that are important to them. Look for common threads and potential combinations.

Key Takeaways

  • Uncovering potential involves helping clients redefine their identity after major life transitions.
  • Validating feelings of loss and grief is crucial for building trust and rapport.
  • Focus on aligning passions and values with potential income-generating opportunities.
  • Brainstorm unique combinations of interests to create fulfilling and meaningful work.
