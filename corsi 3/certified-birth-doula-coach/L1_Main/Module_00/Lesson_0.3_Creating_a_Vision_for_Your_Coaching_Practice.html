The 4-Tier Transformation

Think of this certification as climbing a beautiful, rewarding mountain. Each tier builds upon the last, taking you to new heights of knowledge and skill:

  1. Level 1: Foundation (Modules 1-15). This is where we lay the groundwork. You'll learn the core methodology and fundamental principles of birth doula coaching. It's like learning the scales before you compose a symphony.
  2. Level 2: Advanced (Modules 16-23). Here, we delve into more complex cases and explore the clinical depth of our methodology. Think of it as mastering advanced techniques to create truly bespoke care.
  3. Level 3: Master (Modules 24-29). This is where you become a leader. You'll learn how to train others and develop supervision skills. It's about sharing your wisdom and empowering the next generation of coaches.
  4. Level 4: Practice (Modules 30-36). Finally, we focus on building your business and attracting clients. This is where you put your knowledge into action and create a thriving practice.
