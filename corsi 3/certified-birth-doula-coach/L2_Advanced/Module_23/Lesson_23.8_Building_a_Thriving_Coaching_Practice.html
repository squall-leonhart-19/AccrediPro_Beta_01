Differential Considerations

Exploring Other Possibilities

1

Pelvic Floor Dysfunction

Beyond IC, consider pelvic floor muscle imbalances contributing to pain and urinary symptoms. Referral to a pelvic floor physical therapist may be beneficial.

2

Hormonal Changes

At age 62, hormonal shifts can exacerbate bladder sensitivity and contribute to insomnia and anxiety. Consider testing hormone levels (estradiol, FSH) to assess hormonal status.

3

Mast Cell Activation Syndrome (MCAS)

Chronic pelvic pain, anxiety, and gut issues can sometimes be linked to MCAS. Consider MCAS if symptoms are triggered by multiple foods or environmental factors.

4

Medication Side Effects

Amitriptyline can cause or worsen constipation, fatigue, and weight gain. Explore alternative pain management strategies with her physician.

Key Takeaways

  • Complex cases require a systems-thinking approach to identify interconnected issues.
  • Prioritize interventions based on the client's most pressing symptoms and potential root causes.
