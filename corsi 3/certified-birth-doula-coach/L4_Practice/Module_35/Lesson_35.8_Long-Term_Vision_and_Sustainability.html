Your Prospect

üë©

Sarah, 42

Seeking a birth doula coach. Found you on Instagram. Anxious about her first birth.

Her Situation
First pregnancy, feeling overwhelmed, wants a natural birth experience.
Budget Concern
"I'm not sure I can afford a doula coach, but I know I need the support."
Decision Style
Emotional, values connection, needs reassurance and personalized attention.
Her Goal
"I want to feel confident and empowered during my labor and delivery."

Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, thank you for scheduling this call. I'm so excited to chat with you! First, congratulations on your pregnancy! How are you feeling?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! So, what made you reach out to me specifically? What are you hoping to gain from working with a birth doula coach?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about what's making you feel anxious. Is it the pain, the unknown, or something else?"
YOU:
"What does your ideal birth experience look like? What are your biggest fears about labor and delivery?"
YOU:
"If you don't address these anxieties, what impact will that have on your birth experience and your early motherhood journey?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Sarah, it sounds like you're really craving a sense of control and empowerment during your birth. Is that a fair assessment?"
YOU:
"My coaching program is designed to help you achieve exactly that. We'll work together to create a birth plan that aligns with your values, learn relaxation techniques to manage pain, and build your confidence so you can advocate for yourself during labor. Many of my clients say they feel significantly calmer and more prepared after just a few sessions."
YOU:
"Unlike a traditional doula, I empower YOU with the skills and knowledge to navigate your birth experience, offering ongoing support and guidance throughout your pregnancy and postpartum period. "
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"Sarah, based on what we've discussed, do you feel like this is the type of support you've been looking for? ... Wonderful! Let me share the details of my program and how we can get started."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in yourself and your birth experience is a big decision. Let's talk about the value you'll receive. Think about the peace of mind, the reduced stress, and the positive impact this will have on your birth and early motherhood. I also offer payment plans to make it more accessible."
"I need to talk to my partner about it."
"That's perfectly fine. I'm happy to answer any questions your partner might have. Would it be helpful to schedule a brief call together, or would you prefer I send you some information you can share with them?"
"I'm already taking childbirth classes."
"That's great! Childbirth classes provide valuable information, and my coaching program complements that by offering personalized support, emotional preparation, and practical tools to help you navigate your unique birth journey. It‚Äôs the difference between knowing the steps and having a coach guide you through the dance."
"I'm worried I won't have time for coaching sessions."
"I understand that you're busy. My program is designed to be flexible and fit into your schedule. We can schedule sessions at times that work best for you, and I also offer email support in between sessions. The time you invest now will pay off in a more confident and empowered birth experience."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key elements of a successful discovery call for a birth doula coach?

Building rapport, understanding the client's needs and concerns, presenting your approach confidently, and handling objections effectively are all crucial.

2. How can you address a client's concern about the cost of your coaching program?

Emphasize the value they'll receive, discuss the long-term benefits, and offer flexible payment options to make it more accessible.

Key Takeaways

  • Practice your discovery call script out loud to build confidence and fluency.
  • Address client objections with empathy and provide solutions that demonstrate the value of your services.
  • Present your pricing confidently and highlight the unique benefits of your coaching program.
  • Remember that every "no" gets you closer to a "yes." Stay persistent and believe in the value you offer.
