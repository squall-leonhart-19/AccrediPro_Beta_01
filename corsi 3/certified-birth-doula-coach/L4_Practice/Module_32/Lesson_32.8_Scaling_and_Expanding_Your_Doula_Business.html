"My coaching program is designed to help doulas like you overcome these challenges. We focus on attracting ideal clients, crafting a compelling brand, setting sustainable prices, and building a thriving business without burnout. Many of my clients double their income within 6 months."
YOU:
"I've helped doulas transition from feeling overwhelmed and underpaid to confidently running successful, impactful businesses. Does this sound like the kind of support you're looking for?"
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"Great! Let's talk about how the program works and how we can get you started on the path to your dream doula business."

Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure I can afford it right now."
"I understand. Investing in yourself is a big decision. Let's look at it this way: how much money are you *losing* by not having a clear client acquisition strategy? What's the cost of staying stuck? My program is designed to pay for itself within a few months through increased client bookings and higher rates. We can also discuss payment plan options to make it more manageable."
"I'm too busy to add another thing to my plate."
"I hear you! Time is precious. The thing is, this program is designed to *free up* your time by helping you work smarter, not harder. We'll streamline your processes, attract the right clients, and set boundaries so you can focus on what you love: supporting birthing families. Think of it as an investment in your long-term sustainability."
"I'm not sure if coaching is right for me."
"That's a valid concern. Coaching isn't for everyone. It's for people who are ready to take action and invest in their growth. I offer a very personalized approach ‚Äì this isn't cookie-cutter advice. I'll be your partner, your accountability buddy, and your biggest cheerleader. If you're truly committed to building a successful doula business, I'm confident I can help you get there."
"Can you guarantee results?"
"While I can't guarantee specific income numbers (because your success depends on your effort and commitment), I *can* guarantee that you'll receive a proven framework, personalized support, and actionable strategies to attract more clients and build a sustainable business. My clients consistently report significant improvements in their confidence, client bookings, and income. I'm confident you'll see a positive return on your investment if you fully engage with the program."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are three key questions to ask during the "Uncover Needs" phase of a discovery call?

Possible answers: What are your biggest challenges right now? What inspired you to become a doula? What does your dream doula business look like?

2. How would you respond to the objection, "I'm not sure if coaching is right for me?"

Acknowledge their concern, emphasize the personalized approach, highlight your commitment to their success, and position coaching as an investment in their growth.

Key Takeaways

  • Preparation is key. Know your ideal client and their pain points.
  • Structure your discovery call for maximum impact.
  • Practice handling common objections with confidence and empathy.
  • State your price clearly and confidently, highlighting the value and ROI.
  • Believe in your ability to help your clients achieve their goals.
