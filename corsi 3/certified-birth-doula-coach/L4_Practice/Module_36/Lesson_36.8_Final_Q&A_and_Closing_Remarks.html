Handling Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm not sure if I have the time."
"I understand completely. As a mother myself, I know how packed your days are. This program is designed to fit into your life, not take over it. We can work together to find times that suit you, and the strategies you'll learn will actually *save* you time and energy in the long run by reducing your anxiety."
"It's a big investment, and I need to discuss it with my partner."
"That's perfectly reasonable. What specific questions do you think your partner will have? Perhaps I can address them now, or I'm happy to send you some information to share. We can also schedule a brief call with both of you if that would be helpful. Ultimately, this is an investment in your well-being and the well-being of your family. What value do you place on feeling like yourself again?"
"I'm worried it won't work for me."
"I appreciate your honesty. It's natural to feel uncertain. My program is based on proven coaching techniques and personalized to your specific needs. I also offer a satisfaction guarantee. I'm confident that if you commit to the process, you'll see results. Can you tell me more about what specifically makes you worried it won't work?"
"Can I get a discount?"
"I understand the need to be mindful of your budget. While I don't offer discounts, I do have a payment plan available to make it more manageable. I want to ensure you receive the full value of the program, which is why I focus on providing comprehensive support and personalized guidance. Let's discuss the payment plan options and see if we can find a solution that works for you."
