Workshop Discovery Call Script

Your 20-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Connection & Intent 3 min
YOU:
"Hi Sarah, thanks for your interest in the Mindful Motherhood workshop! I'm so glad you reached out. What resonated with you about the workshop description?"
YOU:
"My intention for this call is to see if the workshop is a good fit for you, and answer any questions you have. Does that sound good?"
Phase 2: Uncover Challenges 7 min
YOU:
"Tell me a bit more about what a typical day looks like for you. What are the biggest challenges you face as a mom?"
YOU:
"What have you already tried to manage the stress and overwhelm?"
YOU:
"And what's the impact on your family when you're feeling stressed and disconnected?"
Phase 3: Present Workshop Solution 7 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, it sounds like you're craving more calm, connection, and joy in your motherhood journey. Is that right?"
YOU:
"In the Mindful Motherhood workshop, we'll explore practical mindfulness techniques, create personalized self-care strategies, and build a supportive community of like-minded moms. You'll leave with tools you can implement immediately to reduce stress and connect more deeply with your children."
YOU:
"Does this sound like something that could help you, Sarah?"
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 3 min
YOU:
"Great! The workshop is [Date] from [Time] to [Time]. The investment is $97. Does that work for you?"
YOU:
"Perfect! I'll send you the registration link and I look forward to seeing you there!"

Handling Workshop Objections

Common Objections & Your Responses

"I'm too busy. I don't have time for a workshop."
"I totally understand. As a mom, your time is precious. This workshop is designed to give you practical tools to *create* more time and ease in your life. Think of it as an investment in your well-being, so you can be more present and effective in all areas of your life. Plus, it's just 2 hours!"
"I can probably find this information online for free."
"That's true, there's a lot of information out there. This workshop offers a curated, step-by-step approach, personalized guidance, and a supportive community. It's about implementation, not just information. It will save you hours of sifting through articles and videos and gives you the accountability to actually make changes."
"I'm not sure if it's worth the money."
"I understand. Think about the cost of *not* addressing the stress and overwhelm. What's the impact on your health, your relationships, your overall happiness? This workshop is an investment in your well-being and your family's well-being. And if you're not completely satisfied, I offer a full money-back guarantee."
"Can I get a discount?"
"I appreciate you asking! The price reflects the value of the content and personalized support. However, I do offer an early bird discount [mention discount and deadline] if you register by [date]."

Check Your Understanding

1. What are the key components of a successful workshop discovery call?

Building rapport, uncovering challenges, presenting a solution, and closing with clear next steps.

2. How can you handle the objection "I'm too busy" when promoting a workshop?

Reframe the workshop as an investment in time-saving tools and increased efficiency. Highlight the specific time commitment and the long-term benefits.

Key Takeaways

  • Workshops are a great way to generate income and attract new clients.
  • Focus on solving a specific problem for your target audience.
  • Practice your discovery call script to confidently enroll attendees.
  • Address common objections with empathy and value-based responses.
  • Promote your workshops through social media, email marketing, and collaborations.
