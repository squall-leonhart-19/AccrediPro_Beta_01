❓ Questions? We've Got Answers

Do I need prior experience or a medical background?
Not at all! We start from foundations and build your knowledge step by step. About 40% of our students have zero healthcare background — former teachers, corporate professionals, stay-at-home parents. If you can follow clear instructions and care about helping people, you can do this.
How long does the certification actually take?
Most students complete it in 30-45 days at 1 hour per day. But you have lifetime access, so you can go slower (or faster!). Some finish in 2 weeks working intensively, others take 3-6 months while working full-time. There's no deadline or pressure.
Can I do this while working a full-time job?
Yes! The training is 100% self-paced and online. Study at night, on weekends, or during lunch breaks. Most of our students keep their day jobs while building their coaching practice on the side until they're ready to transition.
Is this certification legitimate? Can I actually work as a coach?
Absolutely. You'll earn the FM-FC™ credential verified by AccrediPro Standards Institute (ASI). This is your first step on the path to Board Certified (BC-FMP™). You'll work as a Health & Wellness Coach offering educational and lifestyle guidance.
What if I don't pass the final exam?
You get unlimited exam attempts, plus Sarah's direct support to help you succeed. If you complete the full program, attempt the exam at least 3 times, and still don't pass — we refund you 100%. That's our 30-Day Certification Guarantee.
Can I really charge $75-200 per hour?
Yes. Most certified functional medicine coaches charge $100-150 per session, with experienced coaches at $175-250. Your pricing depends on your niche, location (or online reach), and experience level. We teach you exactly how to position yourself and price confidently in the business modules.
What if I need a refund?
Our 30-Day Certification Guarantee protects you: Complete the program + attempt the exam 3 times → If you don't pass, you get a full refund. We also work with students facing genuine hardship — Sarah personally reviews every case with care and empathy. See our full refund policy at accredipro.school/refund-policy
