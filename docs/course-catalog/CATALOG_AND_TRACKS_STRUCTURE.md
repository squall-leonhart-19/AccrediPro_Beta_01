# Catalog & Tracks Structure 2.0 (The "Empire" Model)

This document outlines the restructured pricing model, Career Roadmap, and Catalog organization implemented on Dec 22, 2025.

## 1. The "Big Three" Offers
We have shifted from a high-ticket single purchase to a tiered "Ascension Ladder" to maximize front-end conversions and back-end LTV.

| Offer Name | Price | Purpose | Key Content |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Certified Practitioner** | **$197** | The "No-Brainer" Entry | Full Certification, Practitioner Status, 21 Modules. |
| **2. Pro Accelerator Upgrade** | **$397** | The "Completeness" Upgrade | Advanced + Master Specialist Tracks, 20+ Advanced Modules. |
| **3. 10-Client Guarantee** | **$497** | The "Risk Reversal" | Mentorship until 10 clients are secured. "Safety Net". |

## 2. Career Roadmap Structure
The Roadmap (`/tracks/functional-medicine`) has been actively updated to guide users through these steps.

### **Step 1: Certified Practitioner ($197)**
*   **Focus:** "Get Credentialed"
*   **Status:** The base certification everyone starts with.
*   **Replaces:** Old $997 Certification.

### **Step 2: Pro Accelerator Upgrade ($397)**
*   **Focus:** "Be The Expert"
*   **Content:** Unlocks the "Advanced" and "Master" content libraries (e.g., Autoimmune, Hormones, Gut Health).
*   **Replaces:** Old $1,997 "Practice & Income Path".

### **Step 3: 10-Client Guarantee Mentorship ($497)**
*   **Focus:** "Get Paid" (The Safety Net)
*   **Content:** Direct mentorship and accountability to get the first 10 paying clients.
*   **Replaces:** Old $2,997 "Advanced & Master".

### **Step 4: Business Empire (Application Only)**
*   **Focus:** "Scale & Freedom"
*   **Content:** High-ticket backend offers ($5,000+), Done-For-You infrastructure, hiring, and scaling.
*   **Status:** Application/Call required.
