such a good question Lisa and this is honestly where a lot of new practitioners get stuck ðŸ’•

here's the framework I use when clients have multiple system dysfunctions:

**Always start with gut foundation first** - even if thyroid or adrenals seem like the "main" issue. Why? Because if the gut is leaky and inflamed, nothing else you do will stick. nutrients won't absorb, hormones won't balance, detox pathways won't work

So week 1-4 is typically: remove triggers (food sensitivities, infections), repair gut lining, restore beneficial bacteria

**Then address adrenals before thyroid** - this is critical. if someone's in adrenal dysfunction and you try to support thyroid, you can make them feel worse. the adrenals need to be stable first

Week 5-8: support stress response, balance cortisol patterns, rebuild adrenal reserve

**Then thyroid support** - once gut is healing and adrenals are stable, thyroid protocols actually work

Week 9-12: thyroid-specific nutrients, conversion support, address any remaining barriers

but here's the thing... you're not IGNORING the other systems while you focus on one. you're just prioritizing the sequence while keeping the whole picture in mind

does that help clarify?
