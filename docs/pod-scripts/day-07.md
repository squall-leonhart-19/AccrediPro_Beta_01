okay I want to share something that took me YEARS to figure out and I wish someone had just told me upfront

when a client comes to you with multiple symptoms - let's say fatigue, brain fog, joint pain, and skin issues - the temptation is to try to address everything at once

don't do that

here's the framework I use now:

**Step 1: ALWAYS start with gut**
even if gut isn't their main complaint. 70% of the immune system is in the gut. if the gut is inflamed, nothing else will work long-term. fix the gut first

**Step 2: Then stress/adrenals**
chronic stress messes with everything - hormones, digestion, sleep, immune function. once gut is stabilizing, address the stress response

**Step 3: Then specific systems**
NOW you can look at thyroid, hormones, detox pathways etc. but only after the foundation is solid

I used to try to do everything at once and my clients would get overwhelmed and not follow through. or they'd get temporary results that didn't stick

this order works. trust it

you'll learn this in the modules but wanted to give you the cliff notes ðŸ’•
