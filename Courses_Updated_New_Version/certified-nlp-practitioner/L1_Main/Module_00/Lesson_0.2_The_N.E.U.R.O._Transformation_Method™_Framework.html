Your Realistic Timeline to Income

I won't promise you $10k next week. That's not how a real, legitimate career is built. Here is the path most of our successful 40+ women take:

Months 1-3: The Foundation

Focus on your certification. Practice NLP techniques on friends and family. This is where you build your "confidence muscle." You might take on 2-3 "beta" clients at a reduced rate ($50-$75/session) to get your first testimonials.

Months 4-6: The First Paying Clients

With your certification in hand, you set your professional rates. You aim for your first 5 consistent clients. Income target: $1,500 - $3,000/month.

Months 7-12: Building Momentum

Referrals start to kick in. You refine your "niche" (e.g., NLP for Burned-out Nurses, NLP for Empty Nesters). You increase your rates. Income target: $5,000 - $10,000/month.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is NLP coaching generally priced higher than general "life coaching"?

NLP is "results-based" and focuses on rapid, measurable neurological shifts. Because it solves specific problems faster than traditional methods, clients perceive it as a higher-value investment.

2. What is the "Hybrid Model" of earning?

The Hybrid Model combines active income (1-on-1 coaching) with scalable or passive income (group programs or online courses) to maximize revenue without requiring more of the practitioner's time.

3. According to the "Math of Success," how many clients paying $600/month does it take to reach a $100k+ annual income?

Approximately 14-15 clients (15 clients x $600 = $9,000/month, which is $108,000 per year).

4. What is the primary focus of Months 1-3 in the career timeline?

Building the "confidence muscle" through practice, obtaining certification, and gathering initial testimonials from beta clients.
