Standardized Ethical Guidelines & Professional Boundaries

As an L1 Practitioner, you are a "Mindset Architect," not a licensed mental health counselor. It is vital to maintain these boundaries to ensure both your safety and the client's well-being.

The L1 Boundary Rule: We work with the "Worried Well." If a client presents with clinical depression, active trauma/PTSD, or personality disorders, your ethical duty is to refer them to a licensed therapist. NLP is an accelerant for growth, not a replacement for clinical psychiatric care.
