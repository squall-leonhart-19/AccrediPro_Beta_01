Common Rookie Pitfalls (And How to Avoid Them)

  • The "Over-Explainer": Trying to explain the science of NLP for 30 minutes. Fix: Focus on the results the client wants, not the mechanics of the amygdala.
  • The "Discounted Coach": Lowering your price the second a client hesitates. Fix: Offer a payment plan, but never devalue your transformation.
  • The "Fear of the Ask": Ending a great call without asking for the sale. Fix: Use the "Next Tuesday at 10:00 AM" closing script provided above.
  • The "Prep-Hole": Spending 3 weeks "preparing" your office instead of talking to people. Fix: Your practice starts the moment you tell someone "I can help you with that."
