The Power of the Niche: Why Generalism Fails

In the LGBTQ+ space, this is even more pronounced because the community is actively searching for practitioners who "get it" without needing an explanation of basic terminology.

Crafting Your 'Affirming Promise' (UVP)

Your Unique Value Proposition (UVP) is a clear statement that describes the benefit of your offer, how you solve your client's needs, and what distinguishes you from the competition. In this certification, we call this your Affirming Promise.

The Formula:
"I help achieve using the [P.R.I.S.M. Pillar] so they can [Manifested Authenticity Goal]."

Example:
"I help trans men navigate the metabolic changes of HRT using Strategic Wellness protocols so they can achieve body euphoria and peak physical performance."
