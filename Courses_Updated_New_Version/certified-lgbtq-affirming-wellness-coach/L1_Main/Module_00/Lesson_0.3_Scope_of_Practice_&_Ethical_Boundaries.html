The 4-Tier Transformation

This isn't just a course; it's a phased evolution. We move from the "what" to the "how" to the "business." Unlike many programs that leave you with a certificate but no clue how to find a client, our 4-Tier system ensures you are ready to launch the day you graduate.

Tier Focus Outcome
L1: Foundation (Mod 1-15) Methodology, LGBTQ+ Health Basics, Ethical Care Fluency in affirming terminology and wellness fundamentals.
L2: Advanced (Mod 16-23) Complex Cases, Trauma-Informed Care, Clinical Depth Ability to handle high-stakes client scenarios with confidence.
L3: Master (Mod 24-29) Supervision Skills, Group Coaching, Mentorship Mastery of the coaching container and leading communities.
L4: Practice (Mod 30-36) Business Setup, Marketing, Client Acquisition A fully-operational business and your first paying clients.

Don't rush Tier 1. It‚Äôs tempting to skip to the "Advanced" stuff, but Tier 1 is where you build the neural pathways that make you a great coach. If your foundation is shaky, your business will be too.

The P.R.I.S.M. Framework‚Ñ¢

Throughout every module, you will see the P.R.I.S.M. Framework‚Ñ¢. This is our proprietary methodology for client success. It ensures you never "blank out" during a session because you always know exactly where the client is in their process.

  • P - Presence & Psychological Safety: Creating the "Affirming Container."
  • R - Root Cause Recognition: Looking past symptoms to the systemic barriers.
  • I - Integrative Intervention: Evidence-based wellness protocols.
  • S - Sustainable Systems: Helping clients build habits that stick.
  • M - Mastery & Maintenance: Empowering the client to become their own advocate.

The "Coach-First" Methodology

One of the biggest hurdles for women our age is Imposter Syndrome. We think we need one more book, one more degree, or one more year of study before we can help someone. We solve this through Practice Labs.

Starting in Module 5, every lesson includes a simulation. You'll be given a "Client File" (a fictional but realistic LGBTQ+ individual) and a specific coaching challenge. You'll record your response, write out your protocol, and compare it to our Master Coach keys. By the time you reach Tier 4, you will have "coached" over 50 simulated clients.

Coach Tip

Your previous life experience (as a nurse, mom, or teacher) is an asset, not a distraction. You already have "soft skills." We are just giving you the professional framework to apply them to this specific niche.
