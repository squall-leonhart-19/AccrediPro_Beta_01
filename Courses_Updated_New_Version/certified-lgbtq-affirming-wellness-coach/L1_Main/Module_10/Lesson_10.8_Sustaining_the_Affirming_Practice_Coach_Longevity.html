Common Mistakes & Success Markers

As you transition into professional practice, watch out for these common "New Coach" tendencies.

4 Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • The "Fix-It" Reflex: Jumping straight to suggesting a new gym or app before exploring the identity-based barriers (The "Reflect" stage).
  • Over-Identification: Saying "I totally get it, I feel that way too!" too early. While empathy is good, keep the focus on her specific experience of being a queer woman in leadership.
  • Ignoring the Age Factor: Forgetting that perimenopause and aging play a massive role in wellness for a 52-year-old.
  • Polite Avoidance: Being afraid to use words like "Lesbian" or "Queer" if the client has used them. Use the client's language!
