My Journey to Coaching

Before I became a Conscious Parenting & Birth Support Coach, my life felt like it was falling apart. Five years of infertility nearly destroyed me. Every baby shower was torture. Every "when are you having kids?" felt like a knife. When IVF finally worked, I was so traumatized I couldn't enjoy the pregnancy.

It didn't end there. A traumatic emergency C-section led to severe postpartum depression. I couldn't bond with the baby I'd prayed for. I yelled at my toddler and felt like a monster. Nothing about motherhood looked like what I'd imagined. I felt like a failure as a woman and a mother.

Everything changed when a postpartum doula helped me heal my birth trauma, and conscious parenting work helped me break generational patterns. I realized millions of women needed support through this journey‚Äînot judgment, not unsolicited advice‚Äîbut real, trained, trauma-informed support.
