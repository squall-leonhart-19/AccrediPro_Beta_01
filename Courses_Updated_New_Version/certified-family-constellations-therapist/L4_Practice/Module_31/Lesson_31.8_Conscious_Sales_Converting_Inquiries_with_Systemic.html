1. Your Prospect Profile: Meet Elena

Before we jump into the script, let's look at who you are talking to. Most of your clients will be women in their 40s and 50s who have "tried everything else."

Prospect Profile: Elena, 52

Background: Elena is a high-school administrator. She is exhausted. She has a "difficult" relationship with her adult daughter and chronic back pain that doctors can't explain.

The Hook: She saw your post on LinkedIn about Mother-Daughter entanglements and it resonated deeply. She’s skeptical but desperate for a shift.

Her Secret Fear: "Is this just another 'woo-woo' thing that won't actually change my life?"

2. The 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

A successful call follows a specific energetic flow. You are the Facilitator from the moment they get on the phone.

Phase 1: Rapport & The "Systemic" Opening (0-5 mins)
YOU:
"Hi Elena, I'm so glad we could connect. Before we dive in, I like to take a breath and acknowledge that you're here not just for yourself, but for your whole family system. What was it about my post that made you say, 'I need to talk to her'?"
Sarah's Tip

Don't small talk about the weather. In Family Constellations, we go deep quickly. By acknowledging her "system," you immediately establish yourself as a specialist, not just a general life coach.

Phase 2: Uncovering the Pattern (5-15 mins)
YOU:
"You mentioned the tension with your daughter. If we look at this as a repeating pattern in your lineage, who else carried this burden? Your mother? Your grandmother?"
ELENA:
"Actually, my mother and grandmother didn't speak for ten years. I never realized I was doing the same thing."
Phase 3: The Prescription (15-25 mins)
YOU:
"Elena, what you're describing is a classic systemic entanglement. This won't be fixed by 'talking' about it more. We need to do the work of re-ordering the system. I recommend my Systemic Healing Intensive—it's 6 sessions over 3 months where we untangle these specific knots."

3. Handling Objections Systemically

In our work, an objection is often a "loyalty" to the old way of being. We handle them with grace and firmness.

The Objection The Systemic Response
"It's too much money." "I hear you. Sometimes our system resists investment because it feels like 'leaving' the struggle of our ancestors. Is the cost the real hurdle, or is it the fear of actually being free?"
"I need to ask my husband." "I honor your partnership. I'd love for you to share with him that you're doing this for the health of the entire family. If he says no, how does that affect your growth?"
"I'm not sure I'm ready." "The soul is never 'ready' for a leap, but the system is tired. What happens if we wait another year to address this back pain?"

4. Stating Your Price with Authority

Imposter syndrome usually shows up as a "shaky voice" when stating the price. Practice these lines out loud until they feel like a fact, like stating the time of day.

Sarah's Tip

Never say "It's only $1,500." The word "only" sounds defensive. Just say the number and stop talking. The silence is where the client decides.

The Pricing Script

"The investment for the 3-Month Systemic Intensive is $1,800. This includes our deep-dive constellation sessions, integration support, and the legacy work for your daughter. We can start next Tuesday. Does that work for you?"

5. Income Potential: The Reality for Practitioners

Let's look at what this looks like for a practitioner working part-time (10-15 hours a week), similar to how I started while still working as a teacher.

Scenario: The "Boutique" Practice

  • Program Price: $1,500 (6 sessions)
  • New Clients per Month: 4
  • Monthly Revenue: $6,000
  • Annual Gross: $72,000

Note: This assumes a 25% conversion rate on discovery calls. You only need to talk to 16 people a month (4 a week) to hit these numbers.

Sarah's Tip

As you gain experience, your "Intensive" price will naturally rise to $2,500 - $3,500. A full-time practitioner (8-10 clients a month) can easily cross the $150k+ mark while working fewer hours than a traditional 9-to-5.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of Phase 1 (Rapport) in a Constellations discovery call?

The goal is to move beyond "small talk" and establish a systemic container by acknowledging the client's family system and lineage immediately.

2. Why should you avoid using the word "only" when stating your price?

Using "only" sounds defensive and diminishes the value of the work. Stating the price as a neutral fact demonstrates authority and confidence in the transformation you provide.

3. Systemically, what might a "money objection" actually represent?

It often represents a "loyalty" to the struggle or suffering of ancestors. The client may subconsciously feel that paying for healing is "betraying" those who suffered before them.

4. To earn $6,000/month with a $1,500 program, how many new clients do you need?

You need 4 new clients per month. At a 25% conversion rate, this requires roughly 16 discovery calls (4 per week).

Key Takeaways for Your Practice

  • The discovery call is the beginning of the healing process, not just a sales pitch.
  • Focus on the "Systemic Pattern" rather than just the symptoms to build immediate authority.
  • State your pricing with "The Power of the Pause"—silence after the price is your strongest tool.
  • A boutique practice with just 4 new clients a month can replace a full-time professional salary.
  • Objections are invitations to look deeper into the client's systemic loyalties.
