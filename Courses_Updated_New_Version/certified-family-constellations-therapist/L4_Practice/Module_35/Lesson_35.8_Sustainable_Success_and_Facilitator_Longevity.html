Sarah's Scaling Secret

When you state your price, stop talking. Silence is where the client processes the value. If you rush to justify the price, you signal that you don't believe in it yourself. Let the silence hold the space for their transformation.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "Transformation Pricing" (Packages) better for scaling than hourly rates?

Packages provide predictable income, reduce "no-shows," and most importantly, ensure the client stays committed long enough to see the systemic shift, leading to better testimonials and more referrals.

2. What is the "Systemic Interpretation" of a client saying they need their spouse's permission?

It often reflects a "loyalty bind" where the client subconsciously feels that healing or spending money on themselves might upset the family balance or "orders of love." Your job is to help them see healing as a gift to the whole system.

3. According to Sarah, what should you do immediately after stating your price?

Stop talking. Use silence to hold the space for the client to process the value and the commitment.

4. How much can a "Scaled Expert" potentially earn annually with 8 clients and a monthly workshop?

Based on the income scenario table, approximately $162,000 per year by leveraging both high-ticket packages and group leverage.

Key Takeaways for Scaling

  • Discovery calls are a sacred invitation, not a high-pressure sales pitch.
  • Scaling requires shifting from "selling time" to selling a specific systemic outcome.
  • Handle objections by looking at the systemic fear beneath the surface.
  • Financial freedom comes from a tiered model of packages and group work.
  • Your authority as a Certified Practitioner is what allows you to charge premium rates.
