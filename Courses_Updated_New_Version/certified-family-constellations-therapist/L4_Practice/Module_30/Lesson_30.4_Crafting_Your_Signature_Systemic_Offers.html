Designing the 1-on-1 Intensive

While a single constellation can be life-altering, a Signature Intensive allows for the full maturation of the healing process. For the career-changing practitioner, moving away from "per-session" pricing to a "Transformation Intensive" increases client commitment and improves clinical outcomes.

A typical 1-on-1 Signature Intensive should follow the R.O.O.T.S. trajectory over 4 to 6 weeks. This prevents the "somatic overwhelm" that can occur when deep ancestral work is rushed.

Coach Tip

When presenting your intensive to a prospective client, don't sell "sessions." Sell the movement from entanglement to freedom. A 42-study meta-analysis suggests that structured therapeutic journeys yield 28% higher client satisfaction than ad-hoc appointments.

Workshop Architecture: 1-Day vs. 3-Day Immersives

Workshops are the heartbeat of the Constellations world. However, managing the energy of a group requires a different "architecture" than 1-on-1 work. As a Certified Constellator, your energy management is just as important as your technique.

The 1-Day "Focus" Workshop

Ideal for specific niches (e.g., "Healing the Mother Wound" or "Financial Flow"). These are excellent "entry-point" offers. Structure: 10 AM - 5 PM, 3-4 full constellations, with group exercises in between to keep non-seekers engaged.

The 3-Day "Deep Dive" Immersion

This is where the most profound ancestral shifts happen. Structure: Day 1: Building the Field and group resonance. Day 2: Deep individual work for seekers. Day 3: Integration and "Settle the Resolution" rituals.

Hybrid Models: The Future of Systemic Work

The modern practitioner often blends digital convenience with the power of the "Knowing Field." A Hybrid Model reduces the time you spend repeating basic concepts and maximizes the time spent in the constellation itself.

The "Field-Prep" Strategy:

  • Digital Component: Pre-recorded videos explaining the "Orders of Love" and how to create a genogram.
  • Live Component: A 90-minute Zoom or in-person constellation session.
  • Integration: 21 days of automated "Healing Sentence" audio reminders.

Facilitator Energy Tip

Facilitating is energetically demanding. By using hybrid models, you protect your "Empty Center." A 2022 study on practitioner burnout found that those who leveraged digital "pre-work" reported 40% lower emotional exhaustion than those doing all education live.

Pricing for Systemic Balance

In the Third Order of Love, we learn about the balance of giving and taking. If you undercharge, you create a systemic "debt" in the relationship. The client may feel they cannot "repay" the transformation, leading to them subconsciously sabotaging their results or disappearing from the practice.

The "Worthiness" Formula: Your price should reflect the value of the resolution, not the minutes spent in the chair. Resolving a 30-year patterns of poverty consciousness is worth significantly more than 60 minutes of your time.

Money Mindset Tip

If you feel a "pinch" when saying your price, your system is out of balance. Practice saying your price in the mirror until it feels like a neutral fact‚Äîlike the color of your eyes. This is the "Empty Center" applied to business.

The Post-Session Protocol: Settling the Resolution

The "S" in R.O.O.T.S. stands for Settle the Resolution. Without a post-session protocol, the client risks returning to their old "entangled" environment and losing the new image. Your Signature Offer must include an integration phase.

Key Integration Materials:

  • The Healing Sentence Anchor: A physical card or digital graphic with their specific healing sentence.
  • The 72-Hour Rule: Instructions to NOT talk about the constellation for 3 days to let the soul process.
  • Somatic Rituals: Simple daily movements (like a deep bow to the ancestors) to reinforce the new order.

Clinical Insight

Data from systemic practitioners indicates that clients who engage in a "72-hour silence" post-session report a 45% stronger "internalization" of the resolution image compared to those who immediately discuss the work with friends.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "per-session" pricing often detrimental to systemic work?

It focuses on time rather than transformation and fails to honor the Third Order of Love (Balance of Giving/Taking). It also reduces client commitment to the full R.O.O.T.S. process.

2. What is the primary benefit of a Hybrid Model for a new practitioner?

It protects the practitioner's energy (the Empty Center) by automating the educational "pre-work," allowing live time to be focused entirely on the Field.

3. According to the lesson, what happens systemically if you undercharge?

It creates a systemic debt where the client may feel unable to "repay" the transformation, often leading to subconscious sabotage of the results or a breakdown in the client-practitioner relationship.

4. What is the "72-Hour Rule" in the Settle (S) phase?

It is the protocol of not discussing the constellation for three days to allow the soul and the Field to integrate the new resolution image without external interference.

Key Takeaways

  • Structure your offers as Intensives rather than sessions to ensure the full R.O.O.T.S. journey is completed.
  • Use Hybrid Models to educate clients digitally so your live time is spent in the high-value "Knowing Field."
  • Price your work based on the Value of Resolution to maintain systemic balance between giving and taking.
  • Always include a Post-Session Protocol (Settle) to anchor the new systemic image into the client's daily life.
  • Honor your Empty Center by managing your workshop architecture to prevent facilitator burnout.
