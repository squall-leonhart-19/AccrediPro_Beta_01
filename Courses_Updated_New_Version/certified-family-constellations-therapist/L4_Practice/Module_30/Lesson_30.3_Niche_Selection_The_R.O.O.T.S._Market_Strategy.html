Market Research: The R.O.O.T.S. Framework

Effective market research for a Constellator isn't just about Google keywords; it's about observing the Knowing Field of Commerce. Use this 5-step framework to validate your niche:

  • R - Research the Field: Where is the greatest "unmet systemic need"? Look at communities where conventional therapy has plateaued (e.g., chronic grief or recurring business failure).
  • O - Observe the Demand: Use social listening in forums (Reddit, specialized Facebook groups) to find the "language of the soul" your clients use. Do they talk about "feeling stuck" or "carrying a weight"?
  • O - Order your Offerings: Ensure your niche doesn't violate your own systemic capacity. If you have unresolved trauma in a specific area, it may not be the right niche yet.
  • T - Target the Entanglement: Define exactly which systemic law is being most frequently broken in this niche (e.g., Exclusion in corporate settings).
  • S - Settle the Strategy: Choose one primary platform where your target demographic "belongs."

A "Starving Niche" is one where the pain is high, but the awareness of systemic solutions is low. Your job is to bridge that gap with education. The 'Order the System' (O) Business Model

In Module 3, we learned the Second Order of Love: Precedence. This applies to your business too. To "Order the System" of your practice, you must align your offerings with your energy and lineage.

  • Individual Intensive (The Deep Dive): High-touch, high-transformation. Ideal for complex ancestral trauma. (Recommended: $250 - $500 per 90-min session).
  • Group Workshops (The Collective Field): Leverages the power of representatives. (Recommended: $75 - $150 per participant).
  • Signature Programs (The Systemic Journey): A 3-6 month container focusing on a specific niche. This creates predictable income. (Recommended: $2,500 - $5,000+).
Coach Tip

Don't forget the Third Order of Love: Balance of Giving and Taking. If you undercharge, you create a systemic debt with your clients. Charging a professional fee allows the client to "take" the healing fully because they have "given" an equal exchange of energy (money).
