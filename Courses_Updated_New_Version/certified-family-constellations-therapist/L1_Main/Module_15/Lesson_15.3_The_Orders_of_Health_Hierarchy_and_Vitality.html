The Internal Hierarchy: Ordering the Biological System

In Family Constellations, we know that the Order of Precedence is vital: those who came before have priority over those who came after. In the Orders of Health, we apply this same logic to the body's internal architecture. Health is a state where the "elders" of our biology (the brain, the heart, the DNA) are respected, and the "younger" or peripheral systems are supported by the core.

When a system is entangled, this hierarchy is often reversed. For example, the immune system (a protective, defensive system) might become "parentified," taking on the role of the primary driver of life, leading to autoimmune conditions where the body "attacks" itself in a misguided attempt to maintain an order that has been lost in the family soul.

Coach Tip

When a client presents with a "hyper-vigilant" symptom (like chronic inflammation or anxiety), ask yourself: Is this system trying to do the job of a 'parent' who was absent or overwhelmed?

Parentification of the Symptom

A specific and heart-wrenching dynamic is when a child‚Äôs illness serves the parents' marriage. This is the Parentification of the Symptom. If a marriage is failing, the systemic "soul" of the family may produce a symptom in a child (or the adult child‚Äôs body) to give the parents a common focus, thereby preventing a separation.
