Epigenetics: The Biological Ledger of Ancestry

For decades, science believed that we were merely the sum of our genetic code‚Äîthat DNA was a fixed blueprint. Modern epigenetics has shattered this myth. We now know that while the DNA sequence remains the same, the "expression" of those genes is highly plastic.

Trauma creates chemical tags (methyl groups) that can silence or activate specific genes. Crucially, these tags can be passed down to offspring.

Distinguishing Individual vs. Systemic Trauma
