Understanding the L1 Scope of Practice

As an AccrediPro L1 Practitioner, your focus is the Core Family System. This includes the client, their siblings, their parents, and their grandparents. While the field may occasionally pull in deeper ancestral lines, L1 mastery is defined by resolving entanglements within these three generations.

L1 practitioners focus on:

  • Exclusions: Identifying who has been forgotten or pushed out of the immediate family.
  • Parent-Child Dynamics: Restoring the "Flow of Life" from parents to children.
  • Sibling Order: Ensuring everyone is in their correct hierarchical place.
  • Burdens: Identifying "I do it for you" loyalties to parents or grandparents.
Coach Tip: Staying in Scope

It is tempting to dive into "past lives" or "karmic loops" early on. However, 90% of systemic resolution occurs by simply fixing the relationship with the mother and father. Stay grounded in the L1 core; it is where the most profound healing lives.
