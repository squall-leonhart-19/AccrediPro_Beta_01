1. What is the primary indicator that Elena's financial issue is systemic rather than just a "bad habit"?

The "unexplained" nature of the anxiety and the fact that it triggers specifically when she surpasses her family's historical financial baseline, suggesting a "Hidden Loyalty" to her grandmother's suffering.

Because Elena is using her "forward-moving" energy to look backward and support her elders. Systemically, energy must flow from the ancestors *to* the descendant. When it flows backward, the descendant becomes "stuck" in their own life.

3. Which "Red Flag" would require an immediate referral to a medical professional?

4. What is the purpose of Phase 3 (Integration) in the protocol?

To ground the "soul movement" into practical, physical reality. Without an earthly action (like sending an invoice), the systemic shift may not fully take root in the client's daily life.
