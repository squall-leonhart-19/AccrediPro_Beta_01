Managing Representative Fatigue

In long-term programs, participants often represent multiple times per day. While representing is a primary vehicle for Reveal and Observe, it can lead to Representative Fatigueâ€”a state where the participantâ€™s own system becomes overloaded by the "borrowed" emotions of the field.

To ensure safety, you must implement "Field Hygiene" protocols:
