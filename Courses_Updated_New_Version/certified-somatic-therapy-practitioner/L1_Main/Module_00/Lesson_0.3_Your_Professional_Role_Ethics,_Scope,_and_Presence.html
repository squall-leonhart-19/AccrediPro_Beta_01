The 4-Tier Transformation

This certification is not a "weekend workshop." It is a comprehensive professional training program designed to take you from the basics to mastery. We have organized the curriculum into four distinct tiers of growth:

Tier Focus Area Key Outcome
L1: Foundation Modules 1-15: Somatic Fundamentals You understand the "Why" and the basic science of the nervous system.
L2: Advanced Modules 16-23: Complex Clinical Cases You learn to handle deep-seated trauma and "stuck" client patterns.
L3: Master Modules 24-29: Supervision & Leadership You develop the eye of a master practitioner and learn to guide others.
L4: Practice Modules 30-36: Business & Client Acquisition You build your brand, set your rates, and sign your first 5 high-paying clients.
Coach Tip: Dealing with Imposter Syndrome

It is completely normal to feel like you don't know enough yet. Remember: Tier 1 is designed to be your safety net. You don't need to be an expert on day one. Your only job in Tier 1 is to be a curious student. The "expert" version of you is waiting in Tier 3!

The E.M.B.O.D.Y. Method‚Ñ¢

The backbone of this entire program is our proprietary E.M.B.O.D.Y. Method‚Ñ¢. This is the framework you will use with every single client. Instead of guessing what to do next, you will follow this 6-step clinical protocol:

  • E - Establish Safety: Learning how to create a "neuro-chemically safe" environment for the client.
  • M - Map the Nervous System: Using our assessment tools to see where the client is "stuck" (Fight, Flight, Freeze, or Fawn).
  • B - Breath & Boundary: Implementing foundational somatic regulation tools.
  • O - Observe Sensation: Developing the client's interoceptive awareness (their "inner felt sense").
  • D - Discharge Energy: Techniques to safely release stored survival energy from the tissues.
  • Y - Yield to Integration: Ensuring the changes "stick" and become the client's new baseline.

By Module 5, you will already be practicing the "E" and "M" components of this method. We don't wait until the end of the course to teach you how to coach; we start immediately.

Practical Coaching & Practice Labs

One of the biggest complaints about online certifications is that they are "all theory and no practice." We have solved this with Practice Labs. Starting in Module 2, every lesson includes a practical component:

  • Client Scripts: Word-for-word guides on what to say during difficult sessions.
  • Demonstration Videos: Watch me work with real clients so you can see the E.M.B.O.D.Y. Method‚Ñ¢ in action.
  • Peer Practice: Opportunities to connect with other students to practice your skills in a safe, low-stakes environment.
Coach Tip: The "Messy" Middle

In the Practice Labs, you will get things "wrong." Good! That is where the best learning happens. I would much rather you make a mistake in a Practice Lab with a peer than with a paying client later. Embrace the messiness of learning.

Time Commitment & Pacing

I know you are likely balancing this certification with a job, a family, or other major commitments. This program is designed for the busy professional woman. Here is what we recommend for a successful journey:

  • The "Steady" Path: 3-4 lessons per week (approx. 5 hours total). This will lead to certification in 6-7 months.
  • The "Fast Track": 1 module per week (approx. 10 hours total). This will lead to certification in 4 months.
  • The "Life Happens" Path: Move at your own pace. You have lifetime access to the materials, so there is never any pressure to "keep up" if a family emergency or busy work season arises.
Coach Tip: Schedule Your "CEO Time"

Treat this certification like a high-level appointment with your future self. Block off 60 minutes on your calendar, 3 times a week. Put it in your phone as "CEO Training." When you treat this with professional respect, your results will follow suit.

Certification Requirements

To maintain the high standard of the Certified Somatic Therapy Practitioner‚Ñ¢ credential, there are specific milestones you must achieve. This ensures that when you put that seal on your website, it actually carries weight in the industry.

  1. Module Completion: View all video lessons and complete the required reading in Tiers 1 through 4.
  2. Knowledge Checks: Pass the end-of-module quizzes with a score of 80% or higher (you can retake these as needed).
  3. Final Case Study: Submit a written summary of one "Practice Lab" session where you applied the E.M.B.O.D.Y. Method‚Ñ¢.
  4. Professional Ethics Exam: A brief assessment ensuring you understand the scope of practice and client confidentiality.

Don't worry about creating your own intake forms or legal disclaimers. In Tier 4, we provide you with a "Business-in-a-Box" toolkit. This includes your client contracts, assessment PDFs, and even email templates to help you get your first clients.
