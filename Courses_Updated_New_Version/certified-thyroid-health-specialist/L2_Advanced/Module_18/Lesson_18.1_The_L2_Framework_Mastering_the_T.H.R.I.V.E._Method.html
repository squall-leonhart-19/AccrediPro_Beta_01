1. What is the primary difference between L1 foundational support and L2 systemic resolution?

High systemic inflammation increases oxidative stress within the thyroid gland. Introducing stimulating nutrients like iodine in a high-inflammation environment can exacerbate tissue damage and elevate thyroid antibodies.

The three phases are Assessment (gathering data/labs), Intervention (pillar-specific rebalancing), and Optimization (fine-tuning for metabolic resilience).
