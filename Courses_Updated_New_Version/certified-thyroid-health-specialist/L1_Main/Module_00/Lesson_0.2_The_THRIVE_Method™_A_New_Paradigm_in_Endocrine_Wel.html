The Math of a $100K Practice

Many practitioners struggle because they think they need hundreds of clients to be successful. In the thyroid niche, depth is more profitable than breadth. Because thyroid issues are complex, clients are willing to pay for comprehensive, multi-month support.

The "Solopreneur" Path to $100,000/Year:

  • Option A (High Touch): 8 clients at a time on a 4-month "Thyroid Mastery" package for $4,000. (Total: $32,000 every 4 months = $96,000/year).
  • Option B (Hybrid): 4 private clients ($1,000/mo) + 15 group program members ($300/mo). (Total: $8,500/month = $102,000/year).
