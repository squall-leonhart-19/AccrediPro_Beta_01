The Facilitator‚Äôs Mindset: Teacher vs. Guide

One of the most critical shifts you will make in this L1 Deep Dive is moving away from the "fixer" mindset. In the C.A.N.V.A.S. Framework‚Ñ¢, the client is the expert on their own imagery. Your role is to provide the scaffolding for their discovery.

Coach Tip: Language Matters

Avoid saying "That looks good." Instead, use process-oriented language like, "I notice you used very thick lines in this section. What was that experience like for you?" This keeps the focus on the internal experience.
