Common Mistakes & Success Markers

As you transition into your professional career, watch out for these common "new coach" traps that can hinder client progress.

The Rookie Mistake The Professional Correction
The "Information Dump": Giving the client 10 changes at once. The "Single Domino": Finding the one change that makes the others easier (e.g., protein at breakfast).
Solving the Symptom: Recommending a supplement for every complaint. Addressing the System: Looking at why the symptom exists in the first place (The Root Cause).
"I'm Not Sure" Energy: Fidgeting or over-apologizing when giving advice. The "Collaborative Expert": "I don't have that exact answer yet, but I will research it and get back to you by Tuesday."
Ignoring the "Why": Focusing only on the weight loss goal. Emotional Anchoring: Reminding Diane she's doing this to have the energy to travel.
