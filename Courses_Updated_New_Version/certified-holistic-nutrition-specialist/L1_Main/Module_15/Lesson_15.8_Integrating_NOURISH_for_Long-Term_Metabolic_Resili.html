Common Mistakes to Avoid

New coaches often fall into these traps during the first few sessions. Recognizing them is 50% of the battle.

  • The Data Dump: Explaining the entire Krebs cycle to a client who just wants to know why her jeans don't fit. Keep it simple!
  • The "Fix-It" Reflex: Jumping to supplements before addressing the fact that the client is only sleeping 4 hours a night.
  • Ignoring the Wine: Many women in Elena's demographic use wine to "relax" from high cortisol, but it further disrupts blood sugar. Address this with compassion, not judgment.
  • Imposter Syndrome Silence: Not charging what you're worth because you feel you "just started." Remember: Elena doesn't have the 100+ hours of training you now have.
