Check Your Understanding

1. Why might a "healthy" salad cause Diane more bloating than a cooked meal?

Raw vegetables require significant "digestive fire" (enzymes and HCL) to break down cellulose. If Dianeâ€™s digestion is sluggish or she is in a stressed "fight or flight" state, the raw food ferments in the gut instead of being digested, leading to gas.

2. What is the "Normalize and Validate" technique?

It is the coaching skill of telling the client their symptoms are common and not their fault. This reduces shame and builds the therapeutic alliance, making the client more likely to follow your protocol.

3. If Diane is constipated, why should we check her coffee intake?

While coffee can stimulate a bowel movement, it is also a diuretic and can lead to dehydration of the stool. Furthermore, relying on caffeine for motility ignores the underlying need for natural peristalsis and hydration.

4. Which "R" of the 5R framework addresses Diane's habit of eating quickly at her desk?

Remove. We are removing the "stressor" of rushed eating to allow the parasympathetic nervous system (Rest and Digest) to take over.
