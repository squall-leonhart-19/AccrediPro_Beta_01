The 4-Tier Transformation

We don't just dump information on you. Our curriculum is engineered through a scaffolded learning model designed to build your confidence alongside your knowledge. You will progress through four distinct levels:

L1

FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15)

Here, we build your core methodology. You‚Äôll master macronutrients, micronutrients, gut health, and the fundamentals of functional anatomy. By the end of this tier, you will understand the "why" behind every nutritional recommendation.

L2

ADVANCED (Modules 16-23)

We dive into complex clinical cases. You'll learn about hormonal cascades, autoimmune triggers, and metabolic dysfunction. This is where you move from "general advice" to "clinical depth."

L3

MASTER (Modules 24-29)

Focus shifts to advanced supervision skills. You'll learn how to manage long-term client cases, handle plateaus, and even how to train others or lead group programs.

L4

PRACTICE (Modules 30-36)

The Business Tier. We provide the "Business-in-a-Box" modules: how to get your first 10 clients, legal requirements, insurance, and marketing for the modern practitioner.

Sarah's Tip

Don't rush to Tier 4! I know you're excited to start your business, but the confidence to sell your services comes from the mastery you build in Tiers 1 and 2. Trust the process.
