Recognizing Vibrational 'Echoes' in the 7th Layer

In Level 1, we learned that the auric field consists of multiple layers. In advanced ancestral work, we focus our Frequency Assessment on the 7th Layer, also known as the Keteric Template or Causal Body. This layer holds the blueprint of the soul's journey and the "vibrational echoes" of the lineage from which the physical body was formed.

Ancestral patterns are rarely localized in the lower chakras alone. Instead, they appear during hand scanning as static or "cold spots" that feel ancient, heavy, and remarkably consistent. Unlike emotional trauma from the current life (which often feels "sharp" or "hot" in the 4th layer), ancestral echoes feel like a background hum that the client has mistaken for their own personality.
