When we reach the Crystal Selection (C) phase, we prioritize minerals that contain trace amounts of Lithium. Lithium is mineralogically unique; it is the lightest metal and has a powerful stabilizing effect on both neurological and energetic pathways.

1. Lepidolite (The Peace Stone)

Lepidolite is a potassium lithium aluminum silicate. Its monoclinic lattice structure is excellent for filtering out "energetic noise." Because it contains high concentrations of lithium, it acts as a natural "buffer" for an overstimulated nervous system, which is almost always a component of autoimmune flare-ups.

2. Amblygonite (The Integration Stone)

Amblygonite is a fluorophosphate mineral containing lithium and aluminum. It is particularly effective for aligning the energetic intentions with physical actions. In autoimmune cases, it helps the body "remember" that its own tissues are not the enemy.
