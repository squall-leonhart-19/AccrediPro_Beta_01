Check Your Understanding

1. Why did Elena likely have a negative reaction to Malachite early on?

Malachite is a high-copper, high-intensity mineral that acts as a powerful "drawer" of toxins and emotions. For a client with CFS and a taxed immune system, this "detox" happened too quickly, resulting in a physiological headache and rash (a healing crisis).

2. What is the primary reason for choosing Lepidolite in Phase 1?

Lepidolite contains trace amounts of lithium and has a very low, soothing frequency. It helps stabilize the nervous system and supports sleep, which is the "foundation" of healing for CFS and fibromyalgia.

3. If Elena reports her thyroid levels are "swinging" during treatment, what is your first action?

Immediately pause all mineral work near the Throat Chakra and refer her back to her endocrinologist. Mineral therapy can affect the biofield's regulation of the thyroid, and medical management must take precedence.

4. Why do we avoid "Vitality" minerals like Citrine in the early stages of CFS?

In CFS, the "battery" is empty. Using stimulating minerals is like trying to jump-start a car with a dead alternator; it creates a temporary surge followed by a severe crash (Post-Exertional Malaise). We must "recharge" the battery via stabilization first.

Key Takeaways for Practice
