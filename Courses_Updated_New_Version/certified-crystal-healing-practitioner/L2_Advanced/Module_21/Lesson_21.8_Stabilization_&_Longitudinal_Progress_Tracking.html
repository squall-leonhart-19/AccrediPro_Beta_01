Check Your Understanding

1. Why is grounding (Phase 1) prioritized over addressing Elena's Throat Chakra (Hashimoto's)?

Grounding provides the "energetic container" required to process higher-chakra shifts. Without a stable Root, opening the Throat can cause the client to feel unmoored, anxious, or physically overwhelmed (a healing crisis).

2. Which mineral property makes Lepidolite specifically useful for Elena's history of mild depression and stress?

The presence of Lithium. In mineral therapy, Lithium-bearing stones are utilized for their calming, mood-balancing, and anti-anxiety properties, which align with Elena's need for emotional regulation during retirement.

3. If Elena reports sudden heart palpitations during the second month, what is your immediate clinical action?

Immediate referral to her medical doctor. Heart palpitations are a "Red Flag" trigger, potentially indicating a thyroid medication imbalance or other cardiac issues that fall outside the scope of mineral therapy.

It refers to adults caring for both aging parents and their own children. This often leads to a "collapsed" Solar Plexus (Manipura), as the individual's personal will and needs are constantly sacrificed for others, leading to the "loss of spark" Elena described.
