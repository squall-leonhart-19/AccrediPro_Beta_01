Professional Documentation: Energetic SOAP Notes

In a clinical setting, memory is not a reliable tool. To provide "Premium" care, you must document every session. We use a modified SOAP Note format adapted for mineral therapy. This ensures that when a client returns for session 4, you can reference exactly which lattice structures were used in session 1.

  • S (Subjective): What the client reports. "Client feels 'heavy' in the chest and reports poor sleep."
  • O (Objective): What you observe energetically. "Frequency scan shows 40% depletion in Heart Chakra; Hand scanning detected 'heat' over solar plexus."
  • A (Assessment): Your energetic hypothesis. "Imbalance likely due to sympathetic nervous system dominance; resonance required for calming."
  • P (Plan): The minerals used and the grid design. "Used Rose Quartz (Hexagonal) for heart softening and Black Tourmaline (Trigonal) for grounding."

Designing Treatment Roadmaps

One-off sessions are rarely enough to facilitate permanent vibrational shifts. As a professional, it is your duty to recommend a Treatment Roadmap. Research in bio-energetic medicine suggests that the bio-field requires repetitive "entrainment" to hold a new frequency permanently.

The 3-Tier Roadmap Strategy:

  1. The Re-Alignment (3 Sessions): Best for acute stress or temporary energetic "fog." Focuses on clearing and initial stabilization.
  2. The Transformation (6 Sessions): Designed for deep-seated patterns (e.g., long-term grief or career transitions). Includes 3 weeks of clearing and 3 weeks of "Building" frequency.
  3. The Master Sovereignty (12 Sessions): A comprehensive 3-month journey. This often includes teaching the client self-care protocols and deep ancestral clearing work.

Effective Communication: Explaining Vibrational Logic

Clients are often intimidated by the "mystery" of crystal healing. To bridge the gap, use Vibrational Logic. This means explaining the why behind your crystal selection using mineral chemistry and geometry rather than just "intuition."

Example Script: "Today, I've selected Fluorite for your brow center. Because Fluorite has an isometric (cubic) lattice structure, it excels at bringing order to mental chaos. We are using its natural geometric frequency to help entrain your brainwaves into a more organized, focused state."

Time Management for Complex Grid Setups

As you progress to Level 2, your grids will become more complex, involving 20+ stones. This can easily eat into your "Transmutation" time if not managed properly.

  • Pre-Selection: Have your core "Stabilization" stones (like Smokey Quartz or Hematite) always ready in a dedicated tray.
  • The "Anchor" Technique: Place the four corner stones of your grid first. This establishes the perimeter of the healing field, allowing the client to begin relaxing while you place the more intricate interior stones.
  • Silent Transmutation: Do not feel the need to talk during the 25-minute Transmutation phase. Use a high-quality soundscape (432Hz or 528Hz) to maintain the frequency while you hold space.
