Crystal Selection (C) for Timeframes

The chemical composition and lattice structure of a stone determine how quickly it "talks" to the human bio-field. As explored in Module 9, the piezoelectric effect varies across crystal systems.

  • For Acute Intervention: Select stones with high Refractive Indices and Trigonal/Hexagonal lattices (like Clear Quartz or Citrine). These act as "high-speed processors" for energy, forcing a rapid shift in the field.
  • For Chronic Support: Select stones with Monoclinic or Triclinic lattices (like Selenite, Moonstone, or Labradorite). These have more complex, "slower" geometries that encourage the body to integrate changes over a longer period without "healing crises."

Managing Complex, Multi-Layered Histories

When a client presents with "everything is wrong," you must use Energetic Layering. Do not try to fix the gut, the heart, and the mind in one session.
