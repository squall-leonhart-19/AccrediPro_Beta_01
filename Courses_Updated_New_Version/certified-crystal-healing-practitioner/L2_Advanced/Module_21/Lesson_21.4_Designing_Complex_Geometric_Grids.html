The Hierarchy of Stones within a Grid

Professional treatment planning requires assigning specific "roles" to stones. You are no longer just placing "pretty rocks"; you are assigning functional tasks to each mineral based on its lattice structure and chemical composition.

  • Master Stones (The Hub): Usually the largest or most vibrant stone. It acts as the "CPU" of the grid, holding the primary intention and broadcasting the core frequency.
  • Way Stones (The Conduits): These stones direct the energy between the Master Stone and the Anchor Stones. Double-terminated points are ideal here to ensure bi-directional flow.
  • Anchor Stones (The Grounders): Placed at the feet or the perimeter, these stones ensure the energy shift is integrated into the physical body and "anchored" into reality.
