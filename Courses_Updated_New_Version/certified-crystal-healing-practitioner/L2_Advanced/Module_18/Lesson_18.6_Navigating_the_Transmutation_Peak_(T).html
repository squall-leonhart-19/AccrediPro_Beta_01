Recognizing the 'Vibrational Flip'

How do you know when the Transmutation phase is finished? We look for the Vibrational Flip. This is the moment when the client's field stops "resisting" the crystal's frequency and begins to "mimic" it (Entrainment).

Signs of the Flip:

  • The "Click" Feeling: A sudden sensation of the energy "locking" into place.
  • Breath Shift: The client takes a spontaneous, deep "involuntary" breath.
  • Aura Transparency: To the trained eye (or hand scan), the field feels clear and "glass-like" rather than "foggy."
  • Physical Stillness: Any twitching or REM eye movement stops abruptly.
Coach Tip

Once you recognize the Flip, do not stay in the Transmutation phase. Move immediately to Stabilization (S). Over-treating can lead to "vibrational fatigue," making the client feel groggy rather than refreshed.
