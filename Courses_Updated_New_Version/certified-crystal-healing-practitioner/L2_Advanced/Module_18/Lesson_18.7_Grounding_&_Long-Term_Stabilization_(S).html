Recognizing Incomplete Stabilization

Sometimes, despite our best efforts, the shift doesn't "take," or the client experiences Energetic Vertigo. You must be able to recognize these signs immediately.

Signs of Incomplete Stabilization:

  • Client is overly talkative or "manic" immediately after the session.
  • Visible swaying or lack of physical coordination.
  • Dazed expression or "glassy" eyes.
  • Reporting a feeling of being "outside their body."

Corrective Measures: If these signs are present, do NOT let the client drive. Administer a high-protein snack (nuts/seeds), apply heavy grounding stones to the lower chakras, and perform an additional 5 minutes of the Anchor Breath with physical pressure on the soles of the feet.
