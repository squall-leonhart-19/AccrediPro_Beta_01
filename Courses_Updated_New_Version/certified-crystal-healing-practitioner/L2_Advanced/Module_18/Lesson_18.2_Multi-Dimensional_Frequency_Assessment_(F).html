Acute Leaks vs. Chronic Stagnation

A critical skill in the "F" phase is distinguishing between an Energetic Leak and Vibrational Stagnation. Treating them the same way is a common beginner mistake that leads to poor results.

Acute Energetic Leaks: These feel like a "cool breeze" or a "pulling" sensation. The client is losing vitality. This often happens after surgery, a sudden breakup, or burnout. Intervention: Requires "filling" stones like Citrine or Carnelian.

Chronic Vibrational Stagnation: This feels like a "wall," "heat," or "pressure." The energy is present but not moving. This is common in long-term resentment or chronic inflammatory conditions. Intervention: Requires "breaking" stones like Malachite or Selenite.
