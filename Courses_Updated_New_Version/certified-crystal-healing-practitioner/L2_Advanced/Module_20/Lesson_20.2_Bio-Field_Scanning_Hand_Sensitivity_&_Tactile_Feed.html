The Sensitization Protocol

Before any session, you must "wake up" the sensors. Clinical observation suggests that practitioners who perform sensitization protocols report a 58% increase in signal clarity compared to those who do not.

  • Friction Generation: Rub your palms together vigorously for 30 seconds. This generates piezoelectric heat and stimulates blood flow to the nerve endings.
  • The "Invisible Ball" Exercise: Pull your hands apart 2 inches, then slowly push them toward each other without touching. Feel the resistance (the "bounce"). Repeat this, gradually widening the distance to 12 inches.
  • Temperature Contrast: Practice hovering your hand over a warm cup of tea vs. an ice cube to calibrate your "thermal sensors."

Layered Scanning Techniques

The human bio-field is not a single cloud; it is a multi-layered toroidal field. As a Level 2 Practitioner, you must distinguish between the physical and the subtle bodies.

1. The Etheric Body (1-2 inches from skin)

This layer mirrors the physical anatomy. When you scan here, you are looking for physical imbalances. Tactile feedback: Usually feels dense and "rubbery."

2. The Emotional Body (3-8 inches from skin)

This layer holds the frequency of feelings. Tactile feedback: Often feels like "pockets" of heat or cold. Swirling sensations here often indicate unresolved emotional processing.

3. The Mental Body (8-15 inches from skin)

This layer reflects thought patterns and beliefs. Tactile feedback: Usually feels like "static" or "cobwebs" around the head and throat chakras in clients with high anxiety or overactive minds.

Coach Tip: The "Hover" Height

Keep your palm flat and fingers slightly relaxed. If you move too fast, you'll miss the subtle layers. Think of your hand like a slow-moving metal detector scanning a beach.

The 'Pull and Push' Method

This advanced technique identifies the vector of energy flow. Is the client's energy pushing out too much (leaking) or pulling in too much (vampiric/depleted)?

Place your hand 6 inches above a chakra. Slowly move it toward the body (Push) and then away (Pull).

  • Push Resistance: If you feel a "wall" pushing your hand back, the chakra is overactive or "shielding."
  • Pull Suction: If you feel like your hand is being "sucked" toward the body, that area is desperately depleted and needs Frequency Infusion.
