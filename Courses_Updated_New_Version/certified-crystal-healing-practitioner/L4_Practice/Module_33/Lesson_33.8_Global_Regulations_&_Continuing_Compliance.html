Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to state your legal disclaimer at the BEGINNING of a discovery call?

It establishes you as a professional, sets clear boundaries immediately, and ensures the client understands the scope of your practice before they get emotionally invested in the solution.

2. If a client says, "I've seen crystals for $5 at the gift shop, why is your program $1,200?", what are they actually asking?

They are asking for the value of your expertise and the outcome. You aren't selling rocks; you are selling a protocol, accountability, and a specific health transformation.

3. According to the "Sarah Voice" tips, what should you do immediately after stating your price?

Stay silent. Allow the client to process the information. Apologizing or talking more often signals insecurity about your value.

4. What is the main benefit of "Package Pricing" over "Per Session Pricing"?

It ensures client commitment to the full protocol (leading to better results) and provides the practitioner with more stable, predictable income.
