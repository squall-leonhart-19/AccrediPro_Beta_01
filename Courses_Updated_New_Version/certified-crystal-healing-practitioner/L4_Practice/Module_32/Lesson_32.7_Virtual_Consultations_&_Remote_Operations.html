Shipping & Crystal Selection Logistics

A premium virtual practice often involves a physical component. Selling "Session Kits" is a significant revenue driver. A 2024 market analysis shows that "Wellness Subscription & Kit" models have a 35% higher LTV (Lifetime Value) than single-session models.

Professional Shipping Protocols:

  • Mineral Integrity: Use eco-friendly, high-impact padding. Stones like Selenite or Fluorite are prone to cleavage and must be triple-wrapped.
  • Energetic Cleansing: Every stone must be cleansed and stabilized (Module 6) before shipping. Include a "Care Card" explaining how the client should re-attune to the stones upon arrival.
  • International Compliance: Be aware of CITES regulations and customs restrictions on certain minerals (e.g., raw malachite in some regions).

Protecting Intellectual Property

As you scale, you will likely create digital guides, F.A.C.E.T.S.‚Ñ¢ grid templates, and recorded meditations. Protecting this Intellectual Property (IP) is vital for your business's valuation.

  • Watermarking: All custom grid diagrams should have your logo and a copyright notice.
  • Client Agreements: Your intake forms must include a clause stating that session materials and proprietary protocols are for personal use only and cannot be redistributed.
  • Secure Delivery: Use professional platforms (like a secure client portal) rather than sending raw PDFs via email.
