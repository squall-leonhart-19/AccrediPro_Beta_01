The FACETS‚Ñ¢ Assessment Rubric

To move from "woo-woo" to "wellness professional," you must quantify your findings. The FACETS‚Ñ¢ Assessment Rubric allows you to track client progress over 6-12 sessions, providing the "Proof of Concept" that many 40+ career-changing women find essential for their professional legitimacy.

During a scan, you will rate each major chakra or nodal point on a scale of 1-10 across three metrics:

  • Intensity: The "volume" of the energy. High intensity isn't always good (it can mean inflammation); low intensity often signals depletion.
  • Coherence: The "clarity" of the signal. Is it a steady hum or a jagged, inconsistent pulse?
  • Symmetry: Does the frequency feel the same on the left side of the body as the right? Asymmetry often points to structural or neurological imbalances.
