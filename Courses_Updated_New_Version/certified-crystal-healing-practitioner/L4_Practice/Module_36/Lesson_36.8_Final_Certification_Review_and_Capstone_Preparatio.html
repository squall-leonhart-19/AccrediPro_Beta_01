Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it more effective to sell a "12-week intensive" rather than single sessions?

Single sessions encourage a "quick fix" mentality. A 12-week package ensures client commitment, allows for true energetic stabilization (The 'S' in F.A.C.E.T.S.â„¢), and provides you with predictable recurring income.

2. What is the "Feel-Felt-Found" technique used for?

It is a psychological tool to validate a prospect's concerns (Feel), normalize them (Felt), and then pivot to a positive outcome based on past client success (Found).

3. According to sales data, what percentage of a discovery call should the practitioner be speaking?

Less than 35%. The prospect should do the majority of the talking so you can gather the data needed to present the right solution.

4. What should you do immediately after stating your price?

Practice "The Power of the Pause." Stay silent and allow the prospect to respond. This demonstrates confidence in your value and gives them space to make a decision.
