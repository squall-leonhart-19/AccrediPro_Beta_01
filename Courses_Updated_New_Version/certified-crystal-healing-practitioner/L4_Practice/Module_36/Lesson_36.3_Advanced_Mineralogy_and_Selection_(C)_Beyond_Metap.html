The Ethics of Sourcing & Mineral Integrity

A master practitioner's kit is only as effective as the integrity of its minerals. Vibrational Contamination is a real concern in professional practice. If a crystal was mined under conditions of suffering or treated with harsh chemicals to enhance its color, its foundational frequency is compromised.

Professional Sourcing Standards:

  1. Traceability: Know the mine of origin. Avoid "mystery" bulk lots.
  2. Non-Invasive Processing: Avoid irradiated or "aura" coated stones for deep clinical work. The metallic coating can interfere with the mineral's natural lattice oscillation.
  3. Energetic Hygiene: New minerals for your kit should undergo a 72-hour "neutralization" period before being introduced to clients.
