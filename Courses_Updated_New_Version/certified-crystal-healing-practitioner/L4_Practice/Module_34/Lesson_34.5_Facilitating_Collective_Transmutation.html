The Facilitator as 'Vibrational Governor'

In electrical engineering, a governor is a device that maintains the speed of a machine. As a Facilitator, you are the Vibrational Governor of the room. If the energy becomes too intense too quickly, participants may experience anxiety or physical discomfort. If it is too low, the transmutation will be incomplete.

You modulate the intensity through:

  • Physical Proximity: Moving closer to a struggling participant with a grounding stone (like Smoky Quartz) to "anchor" their field.
  • Breath Command: Using verbal cues to change the group's breathing pattern (e.g., "Take a deep breath in and sigh it out") to move stagnant energy.
  • Crystal Modulation: Adding or removing "Amplifier" stones (Clear Quartz) from the central macro-grid to turn the "volume" of the energy up or down.
Coach Tip: Managing Intensity

If you see multiple participants fidgeting or breathing shallowly, the energy is likely too "high." Immediately place a large piece of Black Tourmaline at the four corners of the room to ground the excess charge.

Intervention Protocols for 'Healing Crises'

A "Healing Crisis" (or energetic detox) occurs when the body releases stored trauma or toxins faster than the elimination systems can handle. In a group setting, this can look like sudden sobbing, nausea, or dizziness in a participant.

The "FACETS Safety Protocol":

  1. Containment: Quietly approach the participant. Do not draw the attention of the whole group.
  2. Physical Grounding: Place a grounding stone in their hands or near their feet.
  3. The "Anchor" Touch: With permission, a light hand on the shoulder can bring them back to their physical body.
  4. Hydration: Always have structured, mineral-infused water available for participants to sip immediately after a release.

Signs of Saturation:
