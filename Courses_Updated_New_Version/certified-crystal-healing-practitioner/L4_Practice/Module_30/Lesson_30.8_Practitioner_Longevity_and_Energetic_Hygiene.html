2. The 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

A successful call follows a specific arc. You must lead the conversation. If the prospect takes over, you lose the ability to diagnose effectively.

Phase 1: Rapport & Permission 5 min

YOU: "Hi Linda! I've been looking forward to our chat. Before we dive in, I'd love to hear‚Äîwhat was it about my profile or our initial message that made you feel like now was the right time to reach out?"

YOU: "Thank you for sharing that. My goal today is to understand what's going on with your energy and sleep, and see if my Mineral Therapy protocol is the right fit. If it is, I'll show you how we work together. If not, I'll try to point you to a better resource. Does that sound fair?"

YOU: "You mentioned feeling 'disconnected.' What does that actually look like on a Tuesday afternoon at 3:00 PM?"

YOU: "And how is this affecting your relationships or your plans for your new business? What is the 'cost' of not fixing this right now?"

Objections are not "no's." They are requests for more information. According to a 2023 study on consumer psychology, 74% of clients who raise an objection are actually highly interested but need to feel safe before committing.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary purpose of a Discovery Call?

To diagnose the client's needs and determine if your services are a proper fit for their specific problems, not just to "sell" a product.

2. Why should you use scientific terminology (like "mineral frequency") with a prospect like Linda?

It builds professional legitimacy and addresses the "skepticism" objection by grounding the practice in mineralogy and physics rather than just "magic."

3. What should you do immediately after stating your price?

Remain silent. Allow the client to process the investment and respond first.

4. How does a "Package Model" benefit the practitioner over an "Hourly Model"?

It focuses on the transformation (the result), allows for higher upfront revenue, and ensures the client stays committed long enough to see actual results.

Key Takeaways

  • Control the Frame: You are the expert. Lead the call with a structured 4-phase process.
  • Listen More: The prospect should be doing 70% of the talking in the discovery phase.
  • Value over Volume: You don't need 100 clients; you need 5 high-value clients who are committed to their healing.
  • Confidence is Currency: State your prices with authority and lean into the scientific backing of your certification.
