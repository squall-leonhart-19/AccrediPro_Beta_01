The F.A.C.E.T.S. Tiered Pricing Model

In the F.A.C.E.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢, not all sessions are created equal. An initial "Frequency Assessment" and "Attunement" session requires significantly more clinical preparation and post-session integration than a monthly "Stabilization" check-in.

We recommend a Three-Tiered Approach to maximize client results and practitioner income:

  • Tier 1: The F.A.C.E.T.S. Deep Dive (Initial Intake). This is a 90-120 minute intensive. It includes full bio-field mapping and a custom grid design. Recommended Price: $225 - $350.
  • Tier 2: The Transmutation Session (Standard Follow-up). A 60-minute session focused on shifting specific energetic blocks identified in Tier 1. Recommended Price: $125 - $175.
  • Tier 3: Stabilization & Maintenance (Package Only). 30-45 minute sessions designed to "lock in" the shifts. Usually sold in bundles of 4 or 6. Recommended Value: $85 - $110 per session.

Sarah transitioned to her crystal healing practice but initially charged a flat $75/hour for everything. She was exhausted and barely covering her $800 studio rent. After implementing the F.A.C.E.T.S. Tiered Model, she raised her intake to $250 and created a "Stabilization Package" for $400 (4 sessions).

Outcome: Sarah reduced her working hours by 15% while increasing her net profit by 42% in six months. Her clients also reported better results because they committed to the full stabilization process rather than "one-off" sessions.

Communicating ROI (Return on Investment)

Clients are often hesitant to invest in "Stabilization" because they feel better after the "Transmutation" phase and think the work is done. Your job is to communicate the financial and emotional cost of regression.

Use the "Foundation" analogy: "If we build a beautiful house (the healing shift) but don't let the concrete foundation set (stabilization), the house will eventually crack. Stabilization ensures your investment of time and money in the initial sessions isn't lost to old energetic patterns."

Managing Cash Flow, Taxes, and Bookkeeping

As a solo practitioner, you are the CFO. Professionalism in your finances prevents the "April 15th Panic" and ensures you can pay yourself a consistent salary.

The "Bucket" System for Revenue: For every $100 that comes into your practice, distribute it immediately into separate accounts:

  • 50% - Owner's Compensation: Your "salary" for the work you do.
  • 25% - Operating Expenses: Rent, inventory, and marketing.
  • 15% - Tax Reserve: Never touch this! It belongs to the IRS/government.
  • 10% - Profit/Business Savings: For equipment upgrades or slow months.

Note: A 2022 study showed that practitioners who automated their tax savings were 70% less likely to face business debt in their first three years.

Policies and Automated Systems

Boundaries are a form of financial mastery. If a client cancels last minute, they are effectively taking money out of your pocket and preventing another client from receiving care.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is a 24-48 hour cancellation policy essential for a professional practice?

It protects the practitioner's income and ensures the "energetic exchange" is respected. It also encourages client accountability in their healing journey.

2. What is the recommended percentage to set aside for taxes for a US-based practitioner?

Generally 15-25% of gross revenue, depending on your specific state and total income. Consulting a CPA is always recommended.

3. What is the main difference between Tier 1 and Tier 3 pricing?

Tier 1 is an intensive, high-value intake (90-120 mins) with deep assessment, while Tier 3 is shorter (30-45 mins) focused solely on maintaining and anchoring previous shifts.

4. How does the "Bucket System" help a practitioner's mindset?

It removes the "mystery" of how much money the practitioner actually has, preventing them from accidentally spending tax or rent money on personal expenses.

Use a booking system that requires a credit card to hold the appointment. This reduces "no-shows" by nearly 80% compared to systems that don't require card details.
