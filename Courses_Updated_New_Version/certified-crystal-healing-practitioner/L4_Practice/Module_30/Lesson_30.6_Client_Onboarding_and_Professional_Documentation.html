The Sacred Intake: Conducting the Initial Frequency Assessment

The onboarding process is the client's first tactile experience of your expertise. In the F.A.C.E.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢, the intake is not merely administrative; it is the beginning of the Frequency Assessment (F) phase. A 2022 survey of holistic wellness clients found that 92% of participants felt more confident in their practitioner's ability when presented with a comprehensive, professional intake form before the first session.

Your intake should capture three distinct layers of the client's profile:

  • The Physical Layer: Current medications, sleep patterns, hydration, and physical ailments (Scope of Practice check).
  • The Energetic Layer: History of emotional blockages, current stress levels, and "heaviness" in the bio-field.
  • The Vibrational Goal: What the client desires to feel after the stabilization phase is complete.
Coach Tip: Legitimacy via Forms

If you feel imposter syndrome creeping in, remember: Professionals use forms. By asking detailed questions about a client's history, you are moving from a "crystal enthusiast" to a "mineral therapist." This transition justifies premium pricing and builds immediate authority.

Systematizing Session Notes

Professional documentation is your greatest defense against liability and your best tool for client results. We recommend a modified SOAP Note (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) tailored for energy medicine.

Subjective Client's report How the client feels their frequency has shifted since the last session.
Objective Observable data Results of tactile scanning (Hand Scanning Techniques from Module 1).

Managing the 'Healing Crisis' Through Education

In mineral therapy, as heavy energies are neutralized during the Transmutation (T) phase, clients may experience a temporary "healing crisis" (sometimes called a Herxheimer reaction in physiological detox). This can manifest as mild headaches, emotional releases (crying), or temporary fatigue.

A practitioner's failure to document and explain this often leads to client dropout. You must provide an Aftercare Education Sheet that includes:

  1. Hydration Protocols: Water helps conduct the shifting electrical frequencies in the body.
  2. Grounding Techniques: Using iron-rich stones (Hematite) to anchor the shift.
  3. Normalization: Explicitly stating that emotional release is a sign of successful transmutation, not a failure of the session.
Coach Tip: The "24-Hour Check-In"

Always send an automated text or email 24 hours after a deep Transmutation session. Simply asking, "How is your energy settling today?" reduces anxiety and increases the client's "perceived value" of your care by 70%.

Developing 'Crystal Prescriptions' for Home Care

Your work doesn't end when the client leaves the table. To ensure Stabilization (S), you must provide a "Crystal Prescription‚Ñ¢." This is a professional document (often a branded PDF) that outlines:

  • The Cleansing Ritual: How the client should maintain the stone's integrity at home.

This document serves as a "tangible" takeaway that keeps the client connected to your practice and the F.A.C.E.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢ between appointments.

Using CRM Tools to Automate Follow-Ups

As a busy practitioner (and perhaps a mother or career-changer), your time is your most valuable asset. Practice Management Software (CRM) allows you to automate the "boring" parts of the business so you can focus on the healing work.

Top Recommended Tools for Crystal Practitioners:

  • Practice Better: Excellent for clinical-style charting and protocol sharing.
  • Dubsado/HoneyBook: Perfect for high-end aesthetic branding and automated workflows.
  • Acuity Scheduling: Simple, effective booking that handles intake forms automatically.
Coach Tip: Automation = Freedom

Automate your intake and your invoicing. Every hour you spend chasing a signature or a payment is an hour you aren't seeing a client or resting your own bio-field. Professionalism is profitable.
