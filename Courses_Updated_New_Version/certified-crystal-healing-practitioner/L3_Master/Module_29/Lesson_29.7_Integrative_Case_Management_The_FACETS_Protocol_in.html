Trauma-Informed Crystal Healing

Working at L3 requires a deep understanding of the nervous system. When we facilitate Transmutation, we are often touching upon stored emotional imprints. A trauma-informed approach ensures the client remains within their "Window of Tolerance."

Key adaptations for sensitive populations include:

  • Titration: Introducing mineral frequencies slowly rather than a full body grid immediately.
  • Pendulation: Moving between a "resource" stone (something that feels safe, like Rose Quartz) and a "work" stone (something that facilitates change, like Obsidian).
  • Consent-Based Placement: Always narrating where stones will be placed and offering "off-body" options for those with tactile sensitivities.
Coach Tip: Safety First

If a client begins to hyperventilate or "check out" during a session, immediately remove high-vibrational stones (Clear Quartz, Selenite) and replace them with heavy, iron-rich minerals (Hematite, Magnetite) at the feet to anchor the nervous system back to the physical plane.
