The 'Healing Crisis': Supporting Intense Releases

As a Master Practitioner, you must recognize the Healing Crisis (often referred to in clinical settings as a Herxheimer-like reaction). This occurs when the transmutation happens faster than the physical body can process the released toxins or emotional data.

Clinical Markers of Energetic Catharsis:

  • Physical: Sudden heat, localized "zapping" sensations, muscle twitching, or nausea.
  • Emotional: Unexplained weeping, sudden anger, or a "flood" of forgotten memories.
  • Autonomic: Changes in breathing depth, yawning (a sign of nervous system regulation), or shivering.

Amplification vs. Diffusion

Strategic mineral selection during the Transmutation phase depends on the state of the client's field. You must decide whether to boost a signal or break up a pattern.

Amplification is used when a client is "energetically depleted." We use minerals like Clear Quartz or Selenium-rich stones to boost the weak signals of the endocrine system. Diffusion is used when a client is "energetically congested." We use minerals with high iron content or complex lattice structures (like Obsidian or Black Kyanite) to absorb and break up the excess charge.
