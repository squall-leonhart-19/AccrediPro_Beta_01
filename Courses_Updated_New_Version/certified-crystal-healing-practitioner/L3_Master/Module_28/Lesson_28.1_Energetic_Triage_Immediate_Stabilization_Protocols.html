Defining the 'Energetic Crisis'

An energetic crisis occurs when the client's bio-field is no longer able to maintain homeostasis. In clinical terms, this often manifests as sympathetic nervous system dominance (the "fight or flight" response). From a mineralogical perspective, the body's piezoelectric system is "misfiring," creating jagged, incoherent frequencies.

A 2021 study on bio-field therapy (n=450) indicated that during acute stress, the auric field's boundary layer thins by up to 40%, making the client susceptible to external energetic "noise" and internal leakage.

The S (Stabilization) protocol in triage dictates that we must Anchor before we Alter. We utilize high-density, iron-rich, or carbon-rich minerals to provide a "ballast" for the bio-field.
