The 'Hook' Frequency: Identifying Cravings

Addiction creates a specific energetic phenomenon known as the "Hook" Frequency. This is not merely a thought; it is a bio-energetic resonance that typically anchors in the Solar Plexus (Manipura) and the Base Chakra.

When a client experiences a craving, the Frequency Assessment often reveals a "magnetic pull" outward from the solar plexus. This is the energy body attempting to "reach out" for the external substance or behavior to fill a perceived energetic void. A 2021 study on biofield therapies noted that targeted energy work could reduce perceived craving intensity by up to 42% in clinical settings.
