Managing Plateaus: When to Shift the Selection

A common frustration for career-changing practitioners is the Mid-Program Plateau. This occurs due to Frequency Habituation‚Äîthe client's bio-field has successfully entrained to the current crystals and no longer perceives them as a "corrective" stimulus.

Statistics from the 2023 Global Wellness Practitioner Survey indicate that 64% of clients experience a perceived plateau between weeks 6 and 8. To restart momentum:

  1. Change the Lattice System: If you have been using Hexagonal stones (like Apatite), shift to Isometric (like Garnet) to introduce a new geometric "logic" to the field.
  2. Alter the Mineral Chemistry: Shift from Oxide-based minerals to Silicates.
  3. Inverse Placement: Move the stones that were at the "crown" to the "root" to flush the energy in the opposite direction.

Ethical Boundaries & Psychological Support

Deep transmutation often brings up "shadow" material. While you are a healing practitioner, you must maintain Scope of Practice. If a client experiences a significant emotional release:
