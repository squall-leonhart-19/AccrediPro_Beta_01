    Identifying 'Energetic Seasons' in a Client's Life

    As women, we are naturally attuned to cycles. However, beyond the lunar or menstrual cycles, every client experiences "Energetic Seasons." These are periods of 3-4 months where the bio-field is predisposed to certain shifts.

    Common Energetic Seasons include:

    • The Shedding Season: Often occurs during life transitions (divorce, career change, empty nesting). The auric field appears "frayed" as old attachments are released.
    • The Incubation Season: A period of low outward energy but high internal restructuring. The field may appear dense but stable.
    • The Expansion Season: High vibrational output, wide auric reach, and rapid chakra spin-rates.
    Coach Tip

    When you identify a client's "Shedding Season," avoid aggressive T: Transmutation. Instead, lean into S: Stabilization. Forcing expansion during a shedding phase can lead to "healing crises" or energetic burnout.
