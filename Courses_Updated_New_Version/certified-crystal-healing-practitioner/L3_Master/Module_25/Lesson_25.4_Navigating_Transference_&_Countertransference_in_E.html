Recognizing Somatic Countertransference

Energy work is unique because the practitioner's body often acts as a secondary sensor. Somatic Countertransference is when you physically feel the client's blockages in your own body. For example, you may suddenly feel a sharp pain in your throat when the client is struggling to express a truth.

The danger is mistaking these signals as your own physical issues. A supervisor helps the practitioner distinguish between:

  • Personal Somatic Issues: Recurring physical patterns unique to the practitioner.
  • Informational Somatic Signals: Transient sensations that arise only during specific client interactions.
