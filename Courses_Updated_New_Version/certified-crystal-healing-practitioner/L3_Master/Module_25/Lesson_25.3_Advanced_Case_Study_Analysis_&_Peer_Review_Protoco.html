The F.A.C.E.T.S. Case Template

The following structure is required for all Level 3 supervision submissions:

  • Client Profile: Demographics, energetic history, and primary presenting imbalance.
  • Frequency Assessment (F): Initial bio-field scan data and identified dissonant nodes.
  • Attunement (A): Methods used to synchronize the practitioner and environment.
  • Crystal Selection (C): Mineralogical justification (Lattice system, chemistry, and Mohs scale).
  • Energy Placement (E): Specific anatomical landmarks and geometric grid logic.
  • Transmutation (T): The observed shift (or lack thereof) during the resonance phase.
  • Stabilization (S): Grounding protocols and client "homework" for integration.

The challenge in crystal healing is the subjective nature of "feeling" energy. To maintain professional standards, peer review protocols must focus on observable evidence and mineralogical logic rather than purely intuitive hits.
