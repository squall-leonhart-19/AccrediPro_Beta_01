Bio-Field Smoothing (Aura Combing)

Throughout the day, clients accumulate "energetic static"‚Äîresidual stress from electromagnetic fields, emotional interactions, and environmental noise. This static creates a "noisy" environment that can interfere with the precise oscillation of your crystals. Aura Combing is the technique used to remove this superficial debris.

The Technique:

  1. Position your hands 6-8 inches above the client's physical body.
  2. Cup your fingers slightly, as if holding a large comb.
  3. Using long, rhythmic strokes, move from the crown of the head down toward the feet.
  4. "Flick" your hands away from the client at the end of each stroke (toward the earth or a clearing salt bowl) to release the collected static.

Soft-Focus Visualization

While your hands feel the field, your eyes can learn to see it. We use a technique called Soft-Focus Visualization. Rather than staring intently at the client's skin, you allow your gaze to "relax" and focus approximately 2-3 inches past the physical body.

This engages the peripheral vision, which is more sensitive to light and movement. You may begin to notice:

  • Haziness: Areas where the energy is "muddy" or lacks clarity.
  • Breaks: Gaps in the auric field, often near old physical injuries.
  • Color Flashes: Brief perceptions of frequency (which we will map to specific chakras in Module 4).

Practitioner Tip

Don't force the vision. If you try too hard, your analytical brain takes over and the perception vanishes. Imagine you are looking at a "Magic Eye" poster from the 90s‚Äîrelax the muscles around your eyes and let the field reveal itself to you.

Finally, we must prepare the physical interface. Crystal healing is a bridge between the ethereal and the physical. If the skin is covered in heavy synthetic fabrics or if the energy center is "closed" due to physical tension, the crystal's piezoelectric effect is dampened.

Preparation Steps:

  • Fabric Check: Ensure the client is wearing natural fibers (cotton, silk, wool) if possible, as synthetics can hold static.

Ethics & Safety

Always ask for verbal permission before touching a client's physical body. If a client is uncomfortable with touch, all techniques in this lesson can be performed 2-3 inches above the body with equal efficacy.
