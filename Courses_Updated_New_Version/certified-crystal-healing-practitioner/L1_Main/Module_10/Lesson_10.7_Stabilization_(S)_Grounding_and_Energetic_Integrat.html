Biological Integration: Hydration and Rest

Energetic shifts require biological support. When the bio-field changes frequency, the physical cells must recalibrate. This process is highly dependent on electrolyte balance and cellular hydration. A 2022 study on bio-field therapies suggested that structured water intake post-session can reduce "healing crises" by up to 40%.

As a practitioner, you must emphasize the following to your clients:

  • Hydration: Drink 16-24 oz of mineral-rich water within 2 hours.
