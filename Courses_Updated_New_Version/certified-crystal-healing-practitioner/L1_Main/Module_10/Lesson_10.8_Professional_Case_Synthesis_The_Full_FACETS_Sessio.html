Check Your Understanding

1. How should you respond if a client says, "I don't feel anything happening"?

Validate their experience without panic. Say: "That's perfectly normal. Energy shifts can be subtle and often happen at a sub-sensory level first. Let's focus on your breath and see if any thoughts or images arise."

2. What is the most effective way to explain the "science" of crystals to a skeptic?

Use the concept of entrainment. Explain that crystals have a perfectly repeating geometric lattice (the most stable form of matter in the universe) which helps stabilize the more chaotic frequencies of the human body during stress.

3. Why is it important to suggest a follow-up session during the first session?

It establishes a therapeutic container. Healing is a process, not an event. Suggesting a follow-up shows you are committed to their long-term outcome, not just a one-time transaction.

4. What mineral property makes Citrine ideal for a "stuck" client like Elena?

Its Trigonal crystal system (focuses and directs energy) and its iron content (provides grounding and vitality to the solar plexus/digestive center).
