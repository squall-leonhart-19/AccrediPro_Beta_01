The "Hand Scanning" Protocol

When scanning, the practitioner moves their dominant hand (the "receiver") approximately 2 to 6 inches above the client's body. A 2021 study on bio-field therapy (n=112) indicated that trained practitioners could detect variations in thermal output and electromagnetic density with 84% accuracy compared to control groups.

Vibrational Static vs. Structural Blockages

Not all energetic irregularities require the same mineral intervention. As a practitioner, you must distinguish between Vibrational Static and Structural Blockages.

Vibrational Static is temporary. It is often caused by recent stress, environmental EMF exposure, or fleeting emotions. It feels like "fuzziness" in the field. This is easily cleared during the Attunement (A) phase using clear quartz or selenite.

Structural Blockages are deep-seated. These are chronic patterns of energy that have "crystallized" into the bio-field, often reflecting long-term trauma or physical illness. These require specific lattice structures (such as Hexagonal or Monoclinic) to penetrate and transmute.
