Check Your Understanding

1. Diane says she feels "like she's watching herself from the ceiling." What is the first thing you should do?

Instruct her to take a slow breath and focus on the physical sensation of her weight on the table and her feet on the anchors. Physical sensory input is the fastest way to reverse dissociation.

2. Why is Smoky Quartz preferred over Clear Quartz during the Stabilization phase?

Clear Quartz is an amplifier and can move energy upward/outward. Smoky Quartz is a grounding stone that draws energy downward into the physical body and the earth, which is the goal of stabilization.

3. A client starts talking rapidly about their "vision" during the stabilization phase. How do you handle this?

Gently acknowledge them ("That sounds like a powerful insight"), but immediately redirect them to their body: "Let's hold onto that thought. For now, just bring your awareness back to your breathing and the feeling of your feet."

4. What is a key physical sign that a client is NOT yet stabilized?

Glassy eyes, pale skin, cold extremities, or a feeling of lightheadedness/dizziness. They should not be allowed to stand up until these symptoms subside.
