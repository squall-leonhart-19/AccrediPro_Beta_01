Frequency Locking Breathwork

Breath is the bridge between the conscious and unconscious mind, and between the energetic and physical bodies. To "lock" a frequency, we use the 4-4-8 Pattern. This specific ratio shifts the client from the sympathetic (fight/flight) to the parasympathetic (rest/digest) state, where the body is most receptive to cellular repair.

  • Inhale (4 counts): Draw the crystal's frequency into the lungs and bloodstream.
  • Hold (4 counts): Allow the "stillpoint" to facilitate the transfer of vibrational information to the cells.
  • Exhale (8 counts): Release the "old" energetic signature and ground the new frequency into the nervous system.
Coach Tip

Instruct your client to visualize the breath as a "golden thread" weaving through the crystals on their body. This visualization increases the efficacy of the somatic integration by engaging the client's own intention.

Biocellular Support: Hydration and Mineralization

Energy requires a medium to travel through. In the human body, that medium is water. However, pure H2O is a poor conductor; it is the electrolytes (minerals) within the water that allow for the efficient transmission of bio-electrical signals.

Post-session, the client's "energetic conductivity" is at its peak. If they are dehydrated or mineral-deficient, the new frequency will "stutter" or fail to integrate. We recommend a Sole (pronounced So-lay) solution or high-quality mineral salts post-treatment.

Coach Tip

Always have a glass of "structured" water (water that has sat with a clear quartz point) ready for your client immediately after they sit up. This provides an immediate physical medium for the energetic shift.

Post-Session Movement Protocols

Energy that is not moved can become stagnant, even if it is "high-vibe" energy. Gentle movement helps distribute the newly shifted frequencies from the major chakras into the peripheral nervous system and the lymphatic system.
