The Square Formation

The square represents the element of Earth and the frequency of stability. By placing four identical grounding stones (such as Black Tourmaline or Hematite) at four points‚Äîabove the head, below the feet, and parallel to the shoulders‚Äîyou create a "containment box." The Cross Formation

The cross formation utilizes a central point (the client's heart or solar plexus) with stones placed at the four cardinal directions. This grid is specifically designed for balance. While the square contains energy, the cross distributes it evenly across the meridians, preventing "pooling" or "stagnation" of the new, higher frequencies we introduced during the session.
