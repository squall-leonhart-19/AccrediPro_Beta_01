F.A.C.E.T.S. Discovery Questions

In the F.A.C.E.T.S. Methodâ„¢, we don't just ask "how are you?" We use targeted inquiries designed to bypass the conscious mind and access the energetic narrative. Research in the field of narrative medicine suggests that the way a client describes their pain often correlates with the specific frequency of their bio-field distortion.

Use these Discovery Questions to uncover hidden patterns:

  • The Origin Inquiry: "If this sensation had a sound or a color when it first began, what would it be?"
  • The Boundary Question: "Where in your life do you feel you are 'leaking' energy or taking on the weight of others?"

  • The Chronological Anchor: "What major life transition occurred 6-12 months before this symptom manifested?"

The 4 Pillars of Energetic Health

A crystal can only do so much if the client's "biological battery" is drained. We assess four specific areas that anchor the human frequency:

1. Sleep Architecture

Does the client reach Delta-wave sleep? This is where the bio-field undergoes "energetic rinsing." Without it, the auric field becomes "sticky" with residual daily static.

2. Nutritional Density

High-vibrational foods (living, mineral-rich) support the crystalline structure of our own cells. Processed foods introduce "noise" into the cellular frequency.

3. Environmental Sanctity

Is the client's home or workspace a "Geopathic Stress" zone? High EMF exposure can override the subtle oscillations of healing crystals.

4. Emotional Equilibrium

Chronic cortisol production (stress) creates a jagged, "spiky" frequency in the auric field, making it difficult for the client to hold the steady resonance of a crystal.

Observing Non-Verbal Frequency Indicators

The body cannot lie. While the client speaks, you must observe their autonomic frequency. This provides immediate data on whether their bio-field is in a state of coherence or dissonance.

  • Pupil Dilation: Rapid dilation when discussing a specific topic indicates a "Sympathetic Surge" (stress response). This suggests an energetic trigger point.
  • Micro-Shifts: Leaning back or crossing arms when a certain crystal is mentioned suggests a subconscious frequency rejection.
  • Voice Tonality: A thinning or "breathy" voice often indicates a depletion in the Throat and Heart chakras.
Coach Tip

Watch for "The Sigh." A deep, involuntary exhale usually marks a moment of energetic release or the "dropping" of a frequency mask. Document exactly what was being discussed at that moment.
