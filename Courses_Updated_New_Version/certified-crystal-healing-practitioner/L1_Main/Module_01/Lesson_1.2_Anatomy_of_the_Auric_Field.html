Mapping Auric Pathology: Leaks & Congestion

In the Frequency Assessment phase, you are looking for two primary types of imbalance:

1. Auric Leaks (Energy Depletion)

Leaks are areas where energy is escaping the field. They often feel like "cool spots" or a "pulling" sensation during a scan. Causes: Physical trauma, surgery, sudden emotional shock, or chronic drug/alcohol use.

2. Auric Congestion (Energy Stagnation)

Congestion is "stuck" energy that has become dense and sluggish. It often feels like "heat," "static," or a "thickness" in the air. Causes: Suppressed emotions, environmental toxins, or repetitive negative thought patterns.

Coach Tip

Think of Leaks like a hole in a garden hose (loss of pressure) and Congestion like a kink in the hose (buildup of pressure). Crystals like Selenite "un-kink" the hose, while stones like Obsidian "patch" the holes.
