Safety & Contraindications

The Transmutation phase is the "active surgery" of the F.A.C.E.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢. Just as a surgeon wouldn't operate on an unstable patient, you must know when to Pause Transmutation and focus solely on Stabilization (Module 6).

  • Pregnancy: Avoid high-intensity transmutation near the abdominal area. Stick to gentle stabilization.
  • Pacemakers/Electronic Implants: High-iron stones (Hematite/Magnetite) can interfere with magnetic fields. Use non-magnetic alternatives.
  • Active Chemotherapy/Radiation: The body is already in a high state of toxic stress. Focus on Attunement and Stabilization rather than aggressive Transmutation.
  • Acute Psychosis or Severe Mental Instability: Rapid energetic shifts can be disorienting. Always work in conjunction with their mental health professional.
