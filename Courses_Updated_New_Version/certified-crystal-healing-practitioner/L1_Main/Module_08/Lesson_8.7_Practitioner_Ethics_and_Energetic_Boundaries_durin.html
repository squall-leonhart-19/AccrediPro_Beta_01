There is a fine line between facilitating a shift and imposing a shift. Ethical practitioners understand that the client's bio-field possesses its own innate intelligence. Our role is to provide the mineral frequencies that make a shift possible, not to force a specific outcome.

Professional Clinical Documentation

Legitimacy in the wellness economy is built on data. High-end practitioners do not rely on memory; they use Frequency Assessment Logs. This protects you legally and provides the client with a tangible "map" of their progress, which justifies your premium rates.

Essential Documentation Points:

  • Initial Frequency Assessment (F): Note tactile sensations (heat, cold, tingling) in each auric layer.
  • Mineral Selection (C): Document exactly which stones were used and why (e.g., "Lepidolite for high-amplitude anxiety").
  • Transmutation Response (T): Record any physical shifts (muscle twitches, temperature changes).

2. What is the main ethical difference between Intention-Setting and Manipulation?

Intention-setting is "invitational"‚Äîit offers frequencies for the client's innate intelligence to use. Manipulation is "coercive"‚Äîit attempts to force a specific outcome or push energy into guarded areas against the bio-field's current readiness.

The primary role is to hold a safe, grounded energetic space (the Pillar) and ensure physical safety. The practitioner should avoid "fixing" the emotion or providing unlicensed counseling, instead referring to a professional if necessary.

Documentation provides a legal shield, tracks client progress objectively, and demonstrates a level of professionalism that justifies higher certification-level rates ($200+ per session).
