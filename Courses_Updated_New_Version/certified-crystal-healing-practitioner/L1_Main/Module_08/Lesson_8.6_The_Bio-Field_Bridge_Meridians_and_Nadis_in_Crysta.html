Crystal Selection (C) and Meridian Resonance

The success of the Crystal Selection (C) phase depends on matching the mineral's chemical and vibrational profile to the element of the meridian. In TCM, meridians are categorized by the Five Elements: Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water.

  • Wood (Liver/Gallbladder): Resonates with green minerals like Malachite (Copper carbonate). Copper is highly conductive, helping to move "stuck" anger or frustration.
  • Fire (Heart/Small Intestine): Resonates with pink and red minerals like Rhodonite (Manganese silicate). Manganese is essential for metabolic fire and emotional warmth.
  • Earth (Spleen/Stomach): Resonates with yellow/brown minerals like Yellow Jasper. The iron content provides the "heaviness" required for grounding and digestion.
  • Metal (Lung/Large Intestine): Resonates with white/clear minerals like Clear Quartz or White Calcite. These amplify the "breath" of the bio-field.
  • Water (Kidney/Bladder): Resonates with dark blue/black minerals like Lapis Lazuli or Obsidian.
