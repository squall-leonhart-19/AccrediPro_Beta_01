Ethical Boundaries & Professional Scope

As a Level 1 Practitioner, your scope of practice is clearly defined. This protects both you and your clients, ensuring a professional standard that builds trust in the wellness industry.

Professional Ethics

Always state clearly: "I am a Certified Crystal Healing Practitioner. I do not diagnose, treat, or cure medical conditions. My work is intended to support the body's natural energetic balance."

  • Scope of Level 1: Stress reduction, energetic balancing, chakra alignment, and supporting emotional well-being.
  • Out of Scope: Medical diagnosis, prescribing minerals as a replacement for medication, or performing "psychic surgery."
  • Financial Integrity: Professionalism includes transparent pricing. In the US, Level 1 practitioners typically earn between $100 to $250 per session depending on location and experience.
Coach Tip

If a client asks for medical advice, always refer them to a licensed medical professional. You can say: "That is a question for your physician. My role is to help your energetic system stay as balanced as possible while you follow your doctor's plan."
