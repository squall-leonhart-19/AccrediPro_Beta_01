Amplification vs. Diffusion: Technical Intensity Control

In the F.A.C.E.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢, you have two primary "volume knobs" for energy: Amplification and Diffusion.

  • Amplification: Used when a client's energy is sluggish, depleted, or "empty." We use Terminated Points (like Clear Quartz or Citrine) pointed toward the body or a specific chakra to drive frequency inward.
  • Diffusion: Used when energy is congested, over-active, or "hot" (e.g., anxiety, inflammation). We use Rounded Stones or Clusters (like Amethyst or Selenite) to scatter the energy and break up the density.

A common mistake for beginners is to amplify everything. However, if a client has high anxiety, amplifying their field can lead to a panic response. Professionals diffuse first, then amplify.
