Mapping Vibrational Blockages

In the F.A.C.E.T.S.‚Ñ¢ framework, we categorize blockages into three specific "Vibrational States." Understanding these allows you to move into the "A" (Attunement) phase with precision.

1. Congestion (The "Wall")

Congestion feels like a thick, heavy energy. It often occurs in the lower chakras when a client is "holding on" to trauma or in the upper chakras during periods of intense mental overwork. Crystals with a Hexagonal lattice (like Quartz) are often needed here to restructure the flow.

2. Depletion (The "Sinkhole")

Depletion feels as if your hand is being pulled into a vacuum. There is no "bounce back" from the field. This requires Stabilizing minerals, often high in iron or grounding elements, to "plug" the leak before higher-frequency work begins.

3. Static (The "Fuzz")

Static is the most common presentation in our modern, tech-heavy world. It feels like a light prickling on the palms. This usually indicates that the client's bio-field is reacting to external environmental stressors (EMFs, toxic relationships, or chaotic environments).

Always scan the field at three levels: 1) The Physical Boundary (2-4 inches from the skin), 2) The Emotional Layer (1-2 feet), and 3) The Mental Layer (3 feet+). A blockage at the physical level often indicates an issue that has already manifested in the body.
