As a professional practitioner, you must maintain your own energetic hygiene. During the session, an energetic "cord" or resonance is established between you and the client. Stabilization requires the formal disconnection of this cord.

Strategic Aftercare & Homework

The 24-48 hours following a session are critical for cellular integration. You must provide your client with a clear roadmap for aftercare.

1. Biological Hydration

Energetic shifts move metabolic waste. Clients should increase water intake by at least 20-30% for two days. Suggest adding a pinch of Celtic sea salt to the water to provide the trace minerals (electrolytes) needed for electrical conductivity in the cells.

2. Movement & Earthing

Encourage the client to walk barefoot on grass or soil for 10 minutes. This provides a natural discharge for any residual high-frequency "static."

3. Energetic Homework

Provide a specific task. Example: "Whenever you feel your old stress pattern returning this week, hold this Smoky Quartz palm stone and take three deep breaths into your belly." This empowers the client and extends the value of the session.

Client Retention Tip

Send a "check-in" text 24 hours after the session. Ask: "How are you feeling in your body today?" This simple act of professional care builds immense trust and establishes you as a legitimate wellness authority.
