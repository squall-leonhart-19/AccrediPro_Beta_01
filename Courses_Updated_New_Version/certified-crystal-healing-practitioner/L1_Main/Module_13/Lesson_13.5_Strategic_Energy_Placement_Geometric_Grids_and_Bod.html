Clinical Safety & Professional Ethics

As a Certified Practitioner, safety is your first priority. Placing heavy or sharp minerals on the physical body requires strict adherence to the AccrediPro Safety Standards.

Key Safety Protocols:

  • Weight Distribution: Never place stones heavier than 200g directly on the throat or soft tissue of the abdomen.
  • Hygiene: Use a thin silk or linen barrier between the stone and the skin to prevent the transfer of oils or bacteria.
  • Termination Safety: Ensure terminated points are not placed where a client might roll onto them or where they could cause puncture if bumped.
