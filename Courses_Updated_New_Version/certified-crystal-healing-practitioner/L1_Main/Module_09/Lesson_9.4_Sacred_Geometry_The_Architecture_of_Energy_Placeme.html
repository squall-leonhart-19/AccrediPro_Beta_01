The Platonic Solids: Foundations of the Physical World

Sacred geometry is not a modern "New Age" invention; it is the study of the mathematical patterns that govern the structure of the universe. In mineralogy, these patterns manifest as lattice systems, which we explored in Lesson 2. However, at a deeper level, all matter is organized around five primary polyhedra known as the Platonic Solids.

To be classified as a Platonic Solid, a shape must have faces that are identical regular polygons, and the same number of faces must meet at each vertex. These shapes represent the "vibrational blueprints" of the physical elements.
