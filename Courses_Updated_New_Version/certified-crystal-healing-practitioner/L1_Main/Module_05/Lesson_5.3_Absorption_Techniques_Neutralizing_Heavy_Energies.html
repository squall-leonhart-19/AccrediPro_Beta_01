Zeolites and Shungite: The Molecular Traps

Zeolites are microporous, aluminosilicate minerals commonly used in industrial water purification. Energetically, their "cage-like" molecular structure acts similarly, trapping toxic energetic signatures within the crystal lattice.

Shungite is unique due to the presence of Fullerenes (C60). These carbon molecules are powerful antioxidants. In a healing context, Shungite doesn't just block energy; it neutralizes it. The 'Pulling' Protocol: Execution

Absorption is not passive. To be a top-tier practitioner, you must direct the flow of energy from the client into the mineral. This is achieved through the Direct Extraction Motion.

The 3-Step Extraction Process:

  1. Anchor the Crystal: Hold the absorbing crystal (e.g., Obsidian) in your dominant hand. Point the termination (if applicable) toward the area of density.
  2. The Visual Pull: As you inhale, visualize a dark, viscous liquid (the heavy energy) being drawn out of the client's body. As you exhale, see it entering the crystal and being neutralized by its core.
  3. The Physical Spiral: Move the crystal in a counter-clockwise spiral above the area. This "unscrews" the stagnant energy from the bio-field, making it easier for the crystal to absorb.

Handling Saturated Crystals & Satiation

A crystal is like a sponge; eventually, it becomes "saturated" and can no longer absorb. Using a saturated stone is not only ineffective but can actually "leak" density back into the client's field.

Signs of Crystal Satiation:

  • Physical Weight: The stone feels significantly heavier in your hand than when you started.
  • Temperature Change: The stone becomes uncomfortably hot or "clammy" to the touch.
  • Dullness: A visual loss of luster or "shine" in the mineral's surface.
