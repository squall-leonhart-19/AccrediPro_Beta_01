The Three Pillars: Absorption, Diffusion, and Amplification

The "T" phase of our method relies on three distinct mechanical actions that crystals perform when interacting with the human biofield.

1. Absorption (The Sponge)

Certain mineral structures, particularly those containing high concentrations of iron or aluminum (like Smoky Quartz or Black Tourmaline), act as energetic sinks. They draw in low-frequency, chaotic vibrations from the client's field. A 2021 study on bio-energetic remediation suggested that certain crystalline lattices can mitigate electromagnetic stress by absorbing chaotic wave patterns.

2. Diffusion (The Prism)

Stagnant energy is often "clumped" or localized. Diffusion is the process of breaking these clumps apart and spreading the energy evenly through the meridians. Stones with high light-refraction or fibrous structures (like Selenite or Fluorite) are excellent for this "shattering" of energetic blockages.

3. Amplification (The Speaker)

Once the field is clear and the blockages are diffused, we must raise the overall frequency. Amplification involves using the crystal's stable lattice to "broadcast" a higher frequency, forcing the client's field to rise to meet it. This is the Entrainment principle in action.

The Science of Lattice Stability vs. Chaotic Biofields

Why does a crystal change a human? The answer lies in the Solid State Physics of the mineral kingdom. A crystal is defined by its Lattice‚Äîa perfectly repeating, geometric arrangement of atoms. It is the most stable form of matter in the universe.

The human biofield, by contrast, is highly entropic (chaotic). Our thoughts, diet, and environment cause our vibrational state to fluctuate wildly. According to the Principle of Entrainment (first discovered by Christiaan Huygens in 1665), when two oscillating systems interact, the less stable one will eventually synchronize with the more stable one.

  • The Result: The client's biofield "locks in" to the crystal's geometry, creating coherence.
