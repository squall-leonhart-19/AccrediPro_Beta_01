Diffusion vs. Absorption: The Strategic Choice

One of the most common mistakes in crystal therapy is attempting to "absorb" energy that actually needs to be "diffused." Think of energy like water: absorption is what you do for a toxic spill; diffusion is what you do for a stagnant, mosquito-breeding pond. You don't want to remove the water (the life force); you want to get it moving again.

In the F.A.C.E.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢, we use Diffusion when the client presents with "energetic knots"‚Äîconcentrated areas of tension that have become fixed. These often manifest physically as recurring muscle knots or mentally as obsessive thought loops.
