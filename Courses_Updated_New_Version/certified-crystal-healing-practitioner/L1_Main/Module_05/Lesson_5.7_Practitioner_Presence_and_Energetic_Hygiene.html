The Neutral Witness Stance

In the F.A.C.E.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢, the practitioner acts as a "Neutral Witness." This is a state of active, non-judgmental observation. When a client is undergoing deep transmutation‚Äîperhaps releasing years of grief or trauma‚Äîthe practitioner‚Äôs bio-field must remain a stable reference point.

If you "feel" for the client too deeply (empathic identification), your frequency drops to match theirs. This stops the transmutation process because the Law of Resonance requires a higher, stable frequency to pull the lower frequency upward. By remaining neutral, you provide the "anchor" the crystals need to do their work.
