Sacred Geometry: Body Gridding Foundations

Body gridding is the art of placing stones in geometric shapes on the body to create a specific vector of force. Instead of treating points in isolation, we treat the relationship between them.

The Triangle (The Delta)

Used for manifestation or extraction. A triangle pointing toward the head "uplifts" energy; a triangle pointing toward the feet "grounds" or "extracts" heaviness. Common placement: Crown and two shoulders.

The Square (The Foundation)

Used for stability and protection. This is excellent for clients going through major life transitions (divorce, career change). Common placement: Two hip bones and two shoulders.

Safety Protocols: Weight, Temperature, and Sensitivity

As a professional practitioner, the physical safety of your client is paramount. Energy placement involves placing heavy, sometimes sharp, objects on the human body.

  • Weight: Never place large clusters (over 1lb) directly on the sternum or throat. Use smaller "tumbled" versions for these sensitive areas.
  • Temperature: Be cautious with stones that have been in the sun or a refrigerator. Extreme temperatures can cause skin irritation or even mild burns.
  • Skin Sensitivity: For clients with thinning skin (common in the 50+ demographic), place a thin silk or linen cloth between the skin and the stone to prevent "mineral abrasion."
Coach Tip #4: The "Hover" Test

ðŸ’¡ Before placing a stone, hover it 1-2 inches above the skin. If the client's skin "twitches" or the hair stands up, the area is hyper-sensitive. Use a "Bridge Stone" nearby instead of direct placement.
