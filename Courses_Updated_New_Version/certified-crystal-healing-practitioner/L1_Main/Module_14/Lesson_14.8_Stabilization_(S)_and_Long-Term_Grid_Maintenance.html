The "Imposter" Cure

If you feel nervous, remember: You are the expert in the room. The client isn't looking for perfection; they are looking for a guide. If you stumble on a word, just breathe and keep going. Your energy matters more than a perfect script.

Common Practitioner Pitfalls

Avoid these four common mistakes that can undermine your professional authority:

  • Over-Explaining: Don't get bogged down in too much history. Keep the focus on the client's transformation.
  • Weak Activation: If you move too fast during the activation ritual, the client won't feel the "weight" of the moment. Slow down.
  • Apologizing for the "Woo": Never say "I know this sounds crazy, but..." Own your modality!
  • Neglecting Grounding: If you aren't grounded, the client will feel your anxiety instead of the grid's stability.
Maintenance Tip

Always instruct your client on how to cleanse the grid without moving the stones (using sound or smoke). This ensures they feel empowered to maintain the energy after you leave.
