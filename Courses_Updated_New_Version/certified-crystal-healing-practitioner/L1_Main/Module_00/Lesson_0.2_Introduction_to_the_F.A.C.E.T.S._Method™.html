The Realistic Timeline to Income

Success doesn't happen overnight, but it happens faster than you think when you follow a proven system. Here is what a typical trajectory looks like for a dedicated practitioner:

The Practitioner Roadmap

From Student to Six-Figure CEO

  • Months 1-3: The Foundation. You are completing your certification, working with 5-10 "case study" clients for free or low cost, and setting up your legal/business structure. Income: $0 - $500/mo.
  • Months 4-6: The Launch. You begin charging professional rates ($125-$175/session). You sign your first 5-10 consistent clients. Income: $1,500 - $3,500/mo.
  • Months 7-12: The Momentum. Word of mouth spreads. You introduce a "Package" model (e.g., 5 sessions for $750). You host your first local workshop. Income: $4,000 - $7,000/mo.
  • Year 2+: The Scale. You have a waitlist for 1-on-1 work. You launch your first group program or online course. You may hire an assistant. Income: $8,000 - $20,000+/mo.
