Tier 1: The Etheric Blueprint (1-3 Inches)

The Etheric layer is the "bridge" between the physical body and the subtle field. Placement here is most effective for clients dealing with physical exhaustion, post-surgical recovery, or chronic pain. Because this layer is dense, it requires minerals with high specific gravity or strong grounding properties.

Mineral Selection for Tier 1

Minerals used here should have a direct resonance with the physical lattice. Black Tourmaline, Hematite, and Smoky Quartz are excellent for pulling stagnant energy out of the physical tissues and into the earth. Placement is usually done directly on the body or within the 1-3 inch "vitality zone."

Tier 2: The Emotional Resonance (3-8 Inches)

This is the "Mid-Field." Most emotional stagnation resides here. When a client says they feel "heavy-hearted" or "stuck," they are often sensing congestion in Tier 2.
