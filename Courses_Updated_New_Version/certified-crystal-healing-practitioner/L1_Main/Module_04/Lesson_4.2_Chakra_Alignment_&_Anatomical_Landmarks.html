Front-Body vs. Back-Body Protocols

Supine Positioning (The Front)

The front of the body is associated with the future, emotional expression, and the conscious mind. This is the standard position for most sessions, especially when addressing anxiety, emotional trauma, or manifesting new goals. It allows for direct access to the "input" side of the chakra system.

Prone Positioning (The Back)

The back of the body is associated with the past, ancestral patterns, and the subconscious. Prone placement is exceptionally powerful for:

  • Physical Pain: Placing crystals directly on the spinal nerve exits.
  • Grounding: When a client is "top-heavy" or over-intellectualizing.
  • Release: Letting go of old burdens (literally "getting things off their back").
