The S.O.L.A.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢

The backbone of this program is our proprietary framework: The S.O.L.A.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢. While traditional therapy often focuses on "processing the past," grief coaching is about integrating the loss into a meaningful future. Research shows that 60-70% of people experiencing "normal" grief benefit more from active integration than passive talk therapy (Neimeyer et al., 2021).

  • S - Story Reconstruction: Helping clients narrate their loss without getting stuck in the trauma loop.
  • O - Observation of Somatics: Identifying where grief is "stored" in the body.
  • L - Liminal Space Navigation: Mastering the "in-between" phase where the old life is gone but the new hasn't started.
  • A - Action-Oriented Rituals: Creating tangible ways to honor the deceased.
  • C - Connection Re-entry: Rebuilding social identity after loss.
  • E - Evolutionary Growth: Moving into Post-Traumatic Growth (PTG).

Clinical vs. Practical Labs

We believe that knowledge without application is just a hobby. In every module, you will find Practice Labs. These are not just "homework"; they are simulated client scenarios. A 2023 study in the Journal of Professional Coaching found that practitioners who utilized simulated case studies during training reported 45% higher "Day 1 Readiness" than those who only used text-based learning.

You will receive:

  • PDF Downloads: Worksheets you can print and use directly with your clients.
  • Client Templates: Intake forms, liability waivers, and session trackers.
  • Assessment Tools: Proprietary scales to measure a client's progress from "Acute Grief" to "Integrated Grief."
  • Scripts: Word-for-word guides for when you don't know what to say during a difficult session.

Coach Tip

Treat the Practice Labs as real sessions. Set a timer, sit in your coaching chair, and speak the scripts out loud. Your "practitioner voice" is a muscle‚Äîyou have to train it before you're in front of a paying client.
