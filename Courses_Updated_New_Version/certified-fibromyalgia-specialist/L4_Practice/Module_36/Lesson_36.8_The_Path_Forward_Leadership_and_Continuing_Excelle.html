Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary purpose of Phase 2 (The Gap Analysis) in the Discovery Call?

The purpose is to help the client realize that their current "symptom-chasing" approach isn't working and that a systematic approach (like RESTORE) is the missing piece.

2. How should you respond if a client says, "I've already spent so much on other things that didn't work"?

Empathize first ("I hear you, and that is so frustrating"). Then, pivot to how your certification allows you to look at the Root Assessment, which is usually what was missing in their previous attempts.

3. True or False: You should wait to mention the price until the very end of the call after they have committed to the 12 weeks.

True. You want to build the value and the vision of their "new life" before introducing the financial investment.

Approximately 3.5 to 4 clients per month at a $2,500 price point generates $100k-$120k per year.
