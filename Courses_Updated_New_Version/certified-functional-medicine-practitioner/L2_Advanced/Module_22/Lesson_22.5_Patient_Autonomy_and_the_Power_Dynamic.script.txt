And finally, don't forget the physiological barriers. If someone has severe dopamine resistance or crushing fatigue, they literally might not have the brain power to meal prep. In those cases, we have to lower the bar for success. We might focus on just one 5-minute habit, like drinking a glass of water before coffee, until their physiology shifts enough to handle more.
