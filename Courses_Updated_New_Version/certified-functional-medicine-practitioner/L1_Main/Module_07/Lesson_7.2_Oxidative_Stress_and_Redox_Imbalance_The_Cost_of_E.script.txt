So, let’s start with what I call the Redox Paradox. For decades, the narrative in the health industry was very black and white. Now, what happens when these shields fail? This is where we see the "Three Horsemen" of cellular damage. This damage isn't random; it follows very specific biochemical pathways that we can actually measure in the lab.

But we have to talk about another layer of this, which is Nitrosative Stress.
