The R.O.O.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢

Every great practitioner has a system. Without one, you'll feel overwhelmed by the sheer amount of data a client gives you. We teach you the proprietary R.O.O.T.S. Method‚Ñ¢, which serves as your clinical GPS:

  • R - Review: Comprehensive intake and history (The Functional Medicine Timeline).
  • O - Organize: Mapping symptoms to the Functional Medicine Matrix.
  • O - Optimize: Addressing the "Low Hanging Fruit" (Sleep, Hydration, Stress).
  • T - Treat/Target: Specific nutritional, botanical, or lifestyle interventions for root causes.
  • S - Support & Track: Ongoing monitoring and adjusting the "therapeutic lever."
