Now, as we move into the Target phase, we have to talk about safety. We have to be hyper-vigilant. Some of our very favorite "natural" tools can actually become dangerous when they are combined with specific pharmaceuticals. This is where your expertise really shines.

Even Berberine, which is one of my favorite tools for metabolic health, needs to be handled with care. It inhibits that same CYP3A4 enzyme we just talked about.
