Next, I want to introduce you to two concepts that are central to master-level work: Mediators and Perpetuators. In your earlier training, we talked a lot about Triggers. These are the events that started the illness, like a car accident, a divorce, or a bad bout of food poisoning. But in complex chronic cases, the trigger might have happened years ago.
