Practice Lab: The Discovery Call

In this scenario, you are speaking with Karen (44), a stressed mom whose 10-year-old son is "defiant and disrespectful." She's seen your post and wants to know "how this works."

The Script: Converting Curiosity to Commitment

YOU:
"Karen, I hear how exhausted you are. It sounds like you've tried every 'consequence' in the book, but the wall between you and your son is only getting higher. Does that feel accurate?"
KAREN:
"Exactly. I feel like a failure. I just want him to listen."
YOU:
"You aren't a failure; you're just using an outdated map. In my practice, we don't 'fix' the child; we heal the connection. When we shift your internal state, his behavior naturally follows. I have a 12-week Conscious Connection program designed for exactly this. Would you like to hear how we can start that shift today?"
Practice Exercise

Read that script out loud three times. Record yourself on your phone. Listen for a tone that is calm, authoritative, and compassionate. Notice how you didn't offer "tips"‚Äîyou offered a transformation.
