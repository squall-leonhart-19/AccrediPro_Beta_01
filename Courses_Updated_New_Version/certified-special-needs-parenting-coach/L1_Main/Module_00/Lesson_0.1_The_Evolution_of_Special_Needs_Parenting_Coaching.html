Let me be very clear: You are exactly who we need. In the world of special needs parenting, wisdom cannot be fast-tracked. Your years as a teacher, a nurse, a mother, or a wellness enthusiast are not "wasted time"‚Äîthey are your clinical hours. Professional Profile: Sarah, 52, Former Special Education Teacher (20 years)
