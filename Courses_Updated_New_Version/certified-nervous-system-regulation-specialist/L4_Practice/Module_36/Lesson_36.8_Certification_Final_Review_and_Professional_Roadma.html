Handling Objections with Regulation

When a prospect raises an objection, they are often experiencing a survival response (fear of change). Your role is to remain regulated and curious.

Objection 1: "It‚Äôs a lot of money."

Objection 3: "I need to talk to my husband."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of Phase 1 (Rapport) in a discovery call?

The goal is to establish co-regulation and safety. It is not just "small talk"; it is demonstrating that you are a regulated authority who can hold space for the client's vulnerability.

2. How should you respond when a client says, "I've tried everything and nothing works"?

Validate their experience and pivot to the mechanism. Explain that most "wellness" approaches target the mind or symptoms, while your approach targets the biological root (the nervous system), which is why their previous attempts likely failed.

3. Why is it important to stop talking after stating your price?

Silence allows the client to process the information and feel the weight of the decision. Talking over the silence usually stems from the practitioner's anxiety and can inadvertently signal a lack of confidence in the value provided.

4. If a practitioner wants to earn $120,000/year using the $2,500 model, how many new clients do they need per month?

They need 4 new clients per month. This averages out to one new enrollment per week, a highly achievable goal with consistent marketing and discovery calls.
Key Takeaways
  • Sales is Co-Regulation: Your state of being is your most powerful sales tool. If you are regulated, the client feels safe to invest.
  • Value the Transformation: You aren't selling hours; you are selling a life free from chronic dysregulation. Charge accordingly.
  • Structure Creates Safety: Following a proven script prevents you from "over-giving" or getting lost in the client's story.
  • Objections are Survival Responses: View objections as a sign that the client's system is nervous about change, and meet them with curiosity, not defensiveness.
  • Financial Freedom is Possible: With just 2-4 clients a month, you can replace a full-time professional salary while working part-time hours.
