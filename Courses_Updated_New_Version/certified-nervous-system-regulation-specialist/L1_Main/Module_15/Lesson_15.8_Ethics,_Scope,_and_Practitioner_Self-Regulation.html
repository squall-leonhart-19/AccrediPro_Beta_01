Common New-Coach Pitfalls

As you transition from student to practitioner, watch out for these four common mistakes:

  • Over-Explaining the Science: Clients want to know *how* it works, but they care more about *how they will feel*. Balance the biology with the benefits.
  • Under-Charging: When you charge professional rates (e.g., $150-$300+ per session), clients show up with more commitment and achieve better results.
  • Ignoring the DFY Tools: Don't reinvent the wheel. Use the assessment templates we've provided to maintain a professional standard from day one.
  • Imposter Syndrome: Remind yourself that you have completed 15 modules of rigorous, ASI-accredited training. You know more about the nervous system than 99% of the population.
