Navigating Your Time Commitment & Pacing

We understand that life happens. Many of you are balancing existing careers, family responsibilities, and personal commitments. This program is designed with flexibility in mind, while also providing structure to keep you on track.

  • Recommended Pace: We suggest aiming for 2-3 lessons per week. This pace allows you to absorb the material, complete practice labs, and reflect on your learning without feeling overwhelmed. At this pace, you can complete the full certification in approximately 9-12 months.
  • Flexibility is Key: There are no strict deadlines for individual lessons. You can pause, review, and adjust your schedule as needed. Our platform tracks your progress, allowing you to pick up exactly where you left off.
  • Strategies to Stay on Track:
    • Schedule Dedicated Study Time: Block out specific hours in your week, just like any other important appointment.
    • Break It Down: Don't try to consume an entire module in one sitting. Break lessons into smaller, manageable chunks.
    • Engage with the Community: Connect with fellow students in our online forums. Accountability partners can be incredibly motivating.
  • Support When You Fall Behind: If you find yourself struggling to maintain your pace, please reach out! Our student support team and dedicated mentors are here to help you strategize, offer encouragement, and connect you with resources to get back on track. We are invested in your success.
