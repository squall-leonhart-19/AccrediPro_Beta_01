1. If a client presents with severe gut issues and erratic blood sugar, which R.E.S.T.O.R.E. pillar should be prioritized first according to the clinical hierarchy?

False. In high-stress populations, Regulating the Nervous System (R) is a Level 1 or 2 Essential intervention, as the stress response will constantly sabotage metabolic and hormonal progress.
