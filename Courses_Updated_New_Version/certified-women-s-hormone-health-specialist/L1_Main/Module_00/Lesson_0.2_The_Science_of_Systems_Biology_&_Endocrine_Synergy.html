Your Realistic Success Timeline

I don't believe in "get rich quick" schemes. Building a reputable practice takes time. Here is the path most of our successful students follow:

Months 1-3: The Foundation Phase

You are immersed in the curriculum. You start "practice coaching" with friends or family (often for free or a low "beta" price) to build your confidence and gather testimonials. Income: $0 - $500.

Months 4-6: The Launch Phase

You have your certification. You launch your first official 1-on-1 package. You sign your first 3-5 paying clients at a professional rate. Income: $1,500 - $4,000 / month.

Months 7-12: The Momentum Phase

Referrals start coming in. You refine your "niche" (e.g., PCOS, Menopause, or Fertility). You might run your first small group program. Income: $5,000 - $10,000 / month.

Year 2 and Beyond: The Scale Phase

You are established. You have a waitlist. You increase your prices and move toward "one-to-many" models or passive income digital products. Income: $10,000 - $25,000+ / month.

Check Your Understanding

1. Which business model is typically recommended for new practitioners to build confidence and gather testimonials?

The Private Practice (1-on-1) model. This allows you to work deeply with individuals, refine your methodology, and secure the "proof of concept" needed to eventually scale to group programs.

2. True or False: You need a social media following of 10,000+ to earn $8,000 a month in this field.

False. As shown in the "Math of Momentum," you only need approximately 10 clients on a $800/month package to reach $8,000. High-ticket, specialized coaching relies on quality of impact, not quantity of followers.

3. What is the primary reason "Specialists" can charge more than general health coaches?

Specialists sell specific outcomes for high-stakes problems. When you specialize in a niche (like hormone health), you are perceived as a higher-value expert who can solve complex issues that generalists cannot.

4. According to the realistic timeline, when does a practitioner typically enter the "Momentum Phase"?

Between Months 7 and 12. This is when referrals begin to generate, the niche is refined, and income typically reaches the $5,000 - $10,000 per month range.
