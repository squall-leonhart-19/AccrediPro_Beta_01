Case Study: Diane’s Transformation

Client: Diane, 52 years old
Occupation: Career Nurse (United States)
Presenting Symptoms: Post-menopausal weight gain (15 lbs), "crashing" at 3 PM, and feeling like she's lost her "spark."
Background: Diane is high-achieving but exhausted. She’s tried keto and HIIT, which only made her more tired. She wants to feel vibrant again for her family and her career.

Your Goal: Use the R.E.S.T.O.R.E. framework to explain why her previous efforts failed and invite her into your 12-week Hormone Harmony program.

The Transition Script: From "Free Call" to "Paid Client"

One of the biggest hurdles for new practitioners is the "ask." We often feel like we’re "selling," but in reality, we are offering a solution to a woman who is suffering. According to a 2022 industry report, practitioners who use a structured closing script see a 64% higher conversion rate than those who "wing it."

Practice This Out Loud

YOU:

"Diane, thank you for being so open today. It’s clear that your body isn't 'broken'—it’s just communicating that it needs a different kind of support in this post-menopausal phase. Based on everything you’ve told me, I am 100% confident I can help you get your energy back and release that stubborn weight."

DIANE:

"I really hope so. I’ve just tried so many things that didn't work. How is this different?"

YOU:

"That’s a great question. Most programs use a 'one-size-fits-all' approach that ignores your unique hormonal blueprint. In my 12-week program, we use the R.E.S.T.O.R.E. framework. We’ll start with the DFY Intake Assessment I mentioned, which gives us a roadmap. We won't just guess; we'll address the root causes of your fatigue. Does that sound like the support you’ve been looking for?"

Coach Tip #1: The Power of Silence

After you ask, "Does that sound like the support you've been looking for?"—stop talking. Let the client process. In my practice, I’ve learned that the first person to speak usually loses the "lead." Give her space to say "Yes."

Final Readiness Check

1. A client says, "I love the idea, but I need to talk to my husband about the price." What is your best response?

"I completely understand, Diane. It's a big decision. What do you think he'll be most concerned about—the financial investment or the time commitment? Let's address that now so you have all the info he'll need." (This uncovers the real objection).

2. Which DFY resource should be sent immediately after a client signs their agreement?

The Comprehensive Intake Assessment and the First Week Success Guide. This creates immediate "momentum" and reduces buyer's remorse.

3. What is the primary purpose of the R.E.S.T.O.R.E. framework during a consultation?

To provide a logical, science-based structure that explains the "why" behind their symptoms and the "how" of your solution.

4. How soon should you aim to book your first "practice" client after certification?

Within 7 days. Implementation is the best cure for fear. Use the "Founding Client" script in your portal to fill your first 3 spots.
