The 4-Tier Transformation

This isn't just a course; it's a professional evolution. We have structured this curriculum into four distinct tiers to prevent overwhelm and ensure that each skill is built upon a rock-solid foundation. Unlike many programs that dump information on you, we focus on integration.

Tier Focus Area Modules Outcome
L1: Foundation Core Methodology & Fundamentals 1 - 15 Master the biology of trauma and narcissistic personality structures.
L2: Advanced Complex Cases & Clinical Depth 16 - 23 Handle high-conflict divorce, parental alienation, and CPTSD nuances.
L3: Master Supervision & Leadership Skills 24 - 29 Learn to lead groups and provide peer supervision.
L4: Practice Business Building & Client Acquisition 30 - 36 Launch your practice, set your rates, and get your first 3 clients.

You might look at Tier 2 or 3 and think, "I'm not ready for that!" That is normal. You aren't supposed to be ready for Tier 3 yet. Focus only on the module you are in. By the time you reach Module 16, you will have the foundation to handle it with ease.

Time Commitment & Pacing

We understand that you are likely balancing a job, a family, or your own recovery. This program is designed for maximum flexibility with a professional standard.

Recommended Pace: 2-3 lessons per week. At this pace, most students complete the full certification in 6-9 months. However, if you are in a "sprint" phase, you can move faster. If life gets messy, you can pause.

How to Stay on Track:

  • The 20-Minute Rule: If you can't find an hour, find 20 minutes. Consistency beats intensity every time.
  • The Community Hub: Never study in a vacuum. Use our private portal to ask questions when you're stuck.
  • Office Hours: Join our monthly live Q&A sessions to get direct feedback on your progress.
