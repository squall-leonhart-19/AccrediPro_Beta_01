Clinical Workflow Optimization

A professional practice requires more than just knowledge; it requires efficiency. The B.A.L.A.N.C.E. workflow follows a specific "Energy Descent" model:

  1. Observation (The 'B' Step): Begins the moment the client enters the room or joins the Zoom call.
