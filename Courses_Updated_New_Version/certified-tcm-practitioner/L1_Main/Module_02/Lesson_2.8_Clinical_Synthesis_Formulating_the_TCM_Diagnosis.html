Common Mistakes to Avoid

1

Using Too Much Jargon

If you tell a client their "Wood is insulting Earth," they will look at you like you're from another planet. Always translate to biological or emotional metaphors.

2

Over-Diagnosing

Focus on the primary disharmony first. Don't try to explain all 12 meridians in the first session. Keep it simple so the client doesn't feel overwhelmed.

3

Ignoring the Emotional Root

TCM is psychosomatic. If you only talk about the bloating and ignore the "stuck" feeling in Linda's life, you're only doing half the job.
