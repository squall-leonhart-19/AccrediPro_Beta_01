The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is structured into four distinct levels. This "staircase" approach ensures you never feel overwhelmed. You must master the foundations before moving into clinical complexity.

Tier Level Focus Area Key Outcome
L1: Foundation Modules 1-15: TCM Theory, Yin/Yang, Five Elements The ability to "speak the language" of TCM fluently.
L2: Advanced Modules 16-23: Complex Patterns, Tongue & Pulse Diagnostic precision and clinical depth.
L3: Master Modules 24-29: Supervision & Case Management The confidence to handle difficult, multi-layered cases.
L4: Practice Modules 30-36: Business, Marketing, & Legal A fully launched, legal, and profitable practice.

Don't rush to Tier 4! I see many students want to skip to "how to get clients" before they've mastered Tier 1. Trust the process. Your marketing becomes easy when your results are undeniable. Mastery is the best marketing strategy.

The B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢

This is the backbone of your training. While TCM is ancient, the way we apply it in a modern coaching context is unique. You will learn our proprietary 7-step framework for every client interaction:

  • B - Baseline Assessment: Establishing the client's current state.
  • A - Archetype Identification: Finding the Five Element pattern.
  • L - Lifestyle Integration: Making TCM practical for modern life.
  • A - Alignment Protocol: Selecting the right herbs, foods, or movements.
  • N - Nuance Check: Adjusting for the individual's unique constitution.
  • C - Consistency Coaching: Ensuring the client actually follows through.
  • E - Evaluation: Measuring progress and pivoting when necessary.
Sarah's Insight

The "C" (Consistency) is where most practitioners fail. You can give the best advice in the world, but if the client doesn't do it, they won't get better. We spend a significant amount of time teaching you behavioral psychology so your clients actually get results.

Time Commitment & Pacing

We designed this for busy women. Whether you are working full-time, raising a family, or both, this program fits into the "nooks and crannies" of your day.

The Average Path: Most students dedicate 3-5 hours per week and complete the certification in 9 to 12 months. However, the program is entirely self-paced. If you have more time, you can finish faster; if life gets messy, you can slow down.

Sarah's Insight

Consistency beats intensity. It is much better to study for 30 minutes every morning than to try and pull a 5-hour "cram session" on Sunday. Your brain needs time to marinate in these concepts.
