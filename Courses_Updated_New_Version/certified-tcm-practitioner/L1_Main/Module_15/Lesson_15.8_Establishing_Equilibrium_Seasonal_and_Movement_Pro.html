5 Common Mistakes New Coaches Make

Even the best students can stumble in the first 30 days. Avoid these pitfalls:

  1. Over-Explaining: Don't try to teach the client everything you know in the first 10 minutes. Focus on their goals.
  2. Discounting Prices: "Friends and family" rates often lead to uncommitted clients. Charge what you are worth.
  3. Skipping the Intake: Never hop on a call without a completed intake form. Information is your power.
  4. Waiting for "Perfect": Don't wait for a fancy website. You can book your first $2k client with just a Zoom link and an invoice.
  5. Neglecting Self-Care: You cannot pour from an empty cup. Practice the TCM principles on yourself first.
