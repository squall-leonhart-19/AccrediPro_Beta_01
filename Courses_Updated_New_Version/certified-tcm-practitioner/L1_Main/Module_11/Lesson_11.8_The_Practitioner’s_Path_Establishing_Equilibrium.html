Common Mistakes to Avoid

When you're deep-diving into Module 11 concepts, it's easy to get lost in the theory. Watch out for these 4 common pitfalls:

  • The "Textbook" Dump: Explaining the 5 Elements for 20 minutes instead of listening to the client. Keep theory to 10% of the session.
  • Ignoring the Emotional Root: Elena's bloating is tied to her career pivot. If you only give her ginger tea and don't acknowledge her fear of failure, the "Qi" will stay stuck.
  • Over-Prescribing: Giving a client 10 herbs, 5 lifestyle changes, and a new diet all at once. Success in coaching comes from incremental change.
  • The Jargon Trap: Using terms like "Damp-Heat" or "Zang-Fu" without explaining them in plain English. Use metaphors like "traffic jams" or "damp basements" instead.
