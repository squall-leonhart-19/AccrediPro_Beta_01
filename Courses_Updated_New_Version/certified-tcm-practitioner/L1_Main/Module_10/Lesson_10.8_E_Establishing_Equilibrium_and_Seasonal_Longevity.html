4. Common Coaching Pitfalls

Even experienced practitioners can fall into these traps. Awareness is 90% of the cure.

  • The "Information Dump": Trying to explain the entire Zang-Fu system in one go. Fix: Give one clear metaphor (like the traffic jam).
  • Fixing Instead of Coaching: Jumping to supplements before validating the client's feelings. Fix: Use the "Believe" step of B.A.L.A.N.C.E. first.
  • Ignoring the "Why": Telling Elena to stop drinking wine without explaining how it fuels "Liver Heat." Fix: Connect every action to her specific TCM pattern.
  • Under-earning: Feeling guilty about charging professional rates. Fix: Remember that Elena likely spends $200 on a single dinner; her health is worth far more.
