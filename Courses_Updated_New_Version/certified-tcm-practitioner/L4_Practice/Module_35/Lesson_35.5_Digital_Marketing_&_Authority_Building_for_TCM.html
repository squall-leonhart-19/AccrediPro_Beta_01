The Email Nurture: Educating on the B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢

Your email list is your most valuable business asset. Unlike social media, you own the relationship. An effective nurture sequence should guide a prospect through the six pillars of the B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢ over a 7-10 day period.

The "Authority Sequence" Structure:

  • Day 1: The Welcome & The 'B' (Body-Mind Assessment). Introduce the idea that symptoms are messengers, not enemies.
  • Day 3: The 'A' & 'L' (Analyze & Locate). Explain how you find the root cause of stagnation.
  • Day 5: The 'A' & 'N' (Align & Nourish). Share a practical tip on Five Element alignment or dietary energetics.
  • Day 7: The 'C' & 'E' (Circulate & Establish). Show them how long-term equilibrium is the goal of your work.
  • Day 10: The Invitation. Invite them to a "Clinical Strategy Session" or to join your program.
Coach Tip: The 80/20 Rule

In your emails, follow the 80/20 rule: 80% education and value, 20% invitation to work with you. If you provide enough "Aha!" moments regarding their health, they will naturally ask how to work with you.
