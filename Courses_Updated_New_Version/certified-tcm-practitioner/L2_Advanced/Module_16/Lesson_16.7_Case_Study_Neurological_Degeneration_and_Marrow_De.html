The Sea of Marrow: The Brain in TCM

In Traditional Chinese Medicine, the brain is not an independent organ but an "Extraordinary Fu Organ." It is known as the Sui Hai or "Sea of Marrow." The physiology is elegant: the Kidneys store Jing (Essence); Jing produces Marrow; Marrow ascends through the spine to fill the brain.

When we see neurological degeneration‚Äîsuch as the shrinking of the hippocampus in Alzheimer's or the loss of dopaminergic neurons in Parkinson's‚ÄîTCM views this as the Sea of Marrow becoming shallow.
