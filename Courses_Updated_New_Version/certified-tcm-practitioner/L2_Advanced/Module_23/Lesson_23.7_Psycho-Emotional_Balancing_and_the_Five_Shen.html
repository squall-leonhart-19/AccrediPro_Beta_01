The Seven Emotions (Qi Qing)

Emotions are normal, but when they are repressed or excessive, they become internal "evils." In the Locate Stagnation (L) phase, you must identify which emotion is knotting the Qi.

  • Anger: Makes Qi rise (Headaches, red eyes).
  • Joy (Excessive/Manic): Slows Qi down (Scattered focus).
  • Worry/Pensiveness: Knots Qi (Digestive stagnation).
  • Grief: Consumes Qi (Fatigue, weak voice).
  • Fear: Makes Qi descend (Incontinence, lower back weakness).
  • Fright (Shock): Scatters Qi (Palpitations, PTSD).
