Final Knowledge Check

1. What is the primary purpose of the "R" (Receive the Question) step in the framework?

To ensure the question is empowering and open-ended. Instead of "Will I get the job?", you refine it to "What can I do to stand out in this interview?" This shifts the client from a passive observer to an active participant.

2. If a client like Elena is skeptical, which step of the O.R.A.C.L.E. Framework is most critical?

O - Opening the Space. By defining the session as a "sanctuary" and a "mirror for inner wisdom," you remove the "fortune-teller" stigma and ground the session in psychological and spiritual safety.

3. What is a realistic income goal for a certified practitioner working 10-15 hours a week?

With a professional rate of $150/hour, 10 hours a week generates $6,000/month. Many of our advanced practitioners scaling into group programs reach the $10k-$30k/month range within 12-18 months.

4. How should you handle a card that you "blank" on during a session?

Go to L - Listen to Intuition. Describe the imagery on the card to the client and ask, "What is the first thing your eye is drawn to here?" This involves the client and often sparks the intuitive hit you need.
