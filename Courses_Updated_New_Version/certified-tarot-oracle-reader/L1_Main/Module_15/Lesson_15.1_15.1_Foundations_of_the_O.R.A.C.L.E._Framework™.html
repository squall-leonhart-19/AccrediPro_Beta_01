The 6 Pillars of the O.R.A.C.L.E. Framework‚Ñ¢

This is the birds-eye view of the journey you will take every client through. Each pillar is a distinct phase of the session:

  1. Orientation (O): Establishing the energetic baseline. We define the "Inquiry" and clear the field of noise.
  2. Resonance (R): Identifying the "pings." This is where the practitioner's intuitive signal aligns with the symbols.
  3. Archetypal Analysis (A): The structural core. We decode the universal patterns (The Fool's Journey, Suit elements) to find the psychological root.
  4. Clarity (C): Synthesis. We translate the "woo" into "mundane" language that the client's conscious mind can accept.
  5. Liberation (L): Identifying the shadow anchors. We pinpoint exactly what belief or energy needs to be released.
  6. Empowerment (E): Strategic action. The session ends not with a "good luck," but with a co-created integration ritual or goal.
Coach's Tip

In your first few sessions using this, keep a small index card with these 6 letters next to your deck. If you find yourself rambling or losing the thread, look at the card. Ask yourself: "Am I in Analysis or Clarity right now?" This simple check will keep your sessions tight and professional.

Ethical Guardrails

Professionalism is defined by boundaries. As a Certified Tarot & Oracle Reader‚Ñ¢, you are an Intuitive Consultant, not a medical professional, lawyer, or financial advisor. The O.R.A.C.L.E. Framework‚Ñ¢ includes built-in ethical "check-stops":

  • Non-Prescriptive: We never tell a client to stop medication or make a legal move. We explore the energy around those choices.
  • Agency-First: If a card suggests a challenge, we never present it as "inevitable." We present it as a "potentiality" that the client has the power to shift.
  • Referral Literacy: Part of being an L1 professional is knowing when a client needs a therapist instead of a Tarot reader.
Coach's Tip

A great way to handle "prediction" requests is to say: "The cards show the current trajectory if nothing changes. My job is to help you see that trajectory so you can decide if you want to stay on it or pivot." This puts the power back in the client's hands immediately.
