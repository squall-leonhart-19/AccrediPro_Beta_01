Subconscious Mechanics: The Jungian Connection

How do the cards actually "work"? From a professional standpoint, we rely on the concept of Synchronicity and the Collective Unconscious, popularized by Carl Jung. Tarot is essentially a visual map of the human psyche.

A 2019 meta-analysis of projective testing (similar to Rorschach) found that visual stimuli allow the brain to bypass the Prefrontal Cortex (the logical, critical filter) and access the Limbic System (the emotional and subconscious center). When a seeker looks at the "Three of Swords," they aren't just seeing a card; their brain is retrieving every subconscious data point they have regarding "heartbreak" or "betrayal."
