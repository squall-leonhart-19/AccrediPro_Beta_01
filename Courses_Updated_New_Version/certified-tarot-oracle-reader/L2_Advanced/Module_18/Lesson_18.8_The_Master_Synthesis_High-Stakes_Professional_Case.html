Referral Triggers: Red Flags

As a Certified Practitioner, you must operate within your Scope of Practice. The following "Red Flags" in Deborah's case would require a mandatory referral to a licensed medical or mental health professional:

  • Clinical Depression: If she reports anhedonia (inability to feel pleasure) or suicidal ideation.
  • Medical Insomnia: If her sleep deprivation is causing cognitive impairment or hallucinations.
  • Financial Abuse: If the divorce involved coercive control that leaves her in immediate physical or legal danger.
  • Substance Dependency: If her "relaxation" involves unmanaged alcohol or prescription misuse to cope with the Tower energy.
