Key Takeaways for Advanced Practice

  • Complex cases require "The Long View." Never rush a client in crisis into a "Predictive" answer.
  • Validate the physiological. Burnout is a physical event that limits a client's ability to process spiritual insights.
  • Scope of Practice is your shield. Referring out is a sign of expertise, not a lack of skill.
  • Structure builds trust. Clients in "Tower" moments crave the container of a professional 3-phase plan.
