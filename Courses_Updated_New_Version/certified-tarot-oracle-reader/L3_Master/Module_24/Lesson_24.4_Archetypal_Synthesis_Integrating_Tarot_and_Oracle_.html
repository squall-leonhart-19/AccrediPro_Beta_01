Multi-Deck Layering: Foundation and Flare

The most common mistake novice readers make is pulling too many cards without a hierarchy. Master Practitioners use the Foundation and Flare method. This creates a vertical depth to the reading that a single deck cannot achieve.

The Vertical Pull Technique

In this technique, you lay a Tarot card first to establish the situational reality. Then, you place an Oracle card directly on top (or adjacent) to reveal the spiritual or energetic lesson. For example, if a client pulls the Three of Swords (Tarot), the situation is one of heartbreak or mental clarity through pain. If the Oracle card layered on top is "Forgiveness," the synthesis is not just about the pain, but the specific liberation required to move past it.
