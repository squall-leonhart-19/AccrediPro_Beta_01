Orientation: Pinpointing the Soul Blockage

The Orientation (O) phase of the reading is critical. You must narrow the scope. Instead of asking "Who was I?", professional practitioners ask: "Which past-life imprint is most relevant to the client's current inability to [set boundaries/earn more/find love]?"

Use the Time-Anchor Technique: Before drawing cards, have the client hold their current blockage in their mind. This creates a "magnetic pull" for the cards to reflect the specific lifetime that holds the "Original Wound."

The Past Life Regression Spread

This 6-card spread is designed to move through the O.R.A.C.L.E. phases specifically for karmic inquiry. Use a mix of Tarot for the "What" and Oracle for the "Vibe/Lesson."

  1. The Anchor (Orientation): The current life situation that triggered this karmic memory.
  2. The Origin (Resonance): The specific past life/archetypal era of the imprint.
  3. The Wound (Archetypal Analysis): What happened? The core trauma or choice.
  4. The Contract (Clarity): The "lie" or vow the soul made (e.g., "I will never trust again").
  5. The Key (Liberation): What action or realization breaks the loop?
  6. The Integration (Empowerment): How to use this wisdom to fuel the current life mission.
Coach Tip: Somatic Resonance

During a regression reading, watch the client's body. If they suddenly take a deep breath or their eyes well up when you describe a card, you have hit Somatic Resonance. This is the moment the "karmic knot" begins to loosen. Stop talking and let them feel that resonance for a moment.

Liberation: Breaking the Soul Contract

Knowledge is not enough. To provide a $997+ value service, you must facilitate Liberation. This involves identifying the "Soul Contract"‚Äîthe subconscious agreement made during the past-life event.

Common Contracts include:

  • The Shield Contract: "If I stay small, I will stay safe" (Often from lifetimes of persecution).
  • The Debt Contract: "I must pay for my past mistakes through current suffering."
  • The Silence Contract: "My voice leads to destruction" (Often seen in former "whistleblowers" or healers).

To break these, we use Archetypal Reframing. We ask the client: "If this card (e.g., The Tower) was a lesson you have now GRADUATED from, what is your new diploma?" This shifts the energy from "active trauma" to "completed wisdom."

Coach Tip: Ethical Boundaries

Always remind clients that they have Free Will in the present. Karma is not "fate"; it is "momentum." Your job is to help them change the direction of that momentum, not to tell them they are doomed to repeat the past.

Empowerment: Integrating the Soul's Mission

The final step of the O.R.A.C.L.E. Framework‚Ñ¢ is Empowerment. Every past-life struggle carries a "Seed of Power." A soul that survived betrayal carries the seed of Discerning Leadership. A soul that lived in poverty carries the seed of Resourceful Creation.

Ask the client: "How does knowing you survived [Past Life Archetype] make you more capable of succeeding in your current business/relationship today?" This integrates the "shadow" of the past into the "light" of their current purpose.

Check Your Understanding

1. Which Major Arcana card most likely indicates a karmic imprint involving a "Vow of Silence" or institutional restriction?

The Hierophant. In a karmic context, this card often represents past lifetimes spent within rigid religious or social structures where individual truth was suppressed in favor of dogma.

2. What is the primary goal of the 'Orientation' phase in a Past Life reading?

To pinpoint the specific past-life imprint that is most relevant to the client's current life blockage, rather than just exploring history for curiosity's sake.

3. How does 'Liberation' differ from 'Clarity' in the O.R.A.C.L.E. Framework‚Ñ¢?

Clarity is understanding the soul contract; Liberation is the active dismantling of that contract through archetypal reframing and energetic release.

4. What is 'Somatic Resonance' and why is it important?

It is the physical reaction a client has (chills, tears, breath shifts) when a symbolic truth is revealed. It indicates that the reading has moved past the intellectual mind and is affecting the client's energetic/nervous system.
