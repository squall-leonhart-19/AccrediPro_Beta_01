The Meta-Inquiry: Identifying the 'Question Behind the Question'

Clients often arrive with a "surface question" born of anxiety or ego. "Will he come back?" or "Should I take this job?" To perform a Meta-Inquiry, you listen with what we call "The Third Ear." While the client speaks, you are scanning for:

  • The Void: What are they not mentioning? (e.g., asking about a job but never mentioning their family).
