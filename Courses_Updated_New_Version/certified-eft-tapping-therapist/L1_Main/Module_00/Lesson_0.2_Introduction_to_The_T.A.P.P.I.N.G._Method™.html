The "Can I Actually Make Money?" Conversation

Let's address the elephant in the room: Imposter Syndrome and the Money Block. Many women in our program come from "service" backgrounds‚Äînursing, teaching, or caregiving. You are used to giving of yourself, often for a fixed (and sometimes undervalued) salary. Transitioning to a fee-for-service model can feel daunting.

The global wellness market is currently valued at over $5.6 trillion, and the demand for stress-reduction techniques like EFT has skyrocketed by 300% since 2020. You aren't just "tapping on people"; you are providing a high-value clinical intervention for the #1 problem in modern society: chronic stress and emotional dysregulation.

Coach Tip

Realistic Timeline to Income

Financial freedom doesn't happen overnight, but with a professional credential, it happens faster than you think.

  • Months 1-3: The Foundation. Complete your certification, work with practice clients (case studies), and set up your professional digital presence. Income: $0 (Investment phase).
  • Months 4-6: The Launch. Begin taking paying clients at an introductory rate (e.g., $100-$125/session). Building word-of-mouth. Income: $1,000 - $3,000/month.
  • Months 7-12: The Momentum. Increase rates to professional standard ($150-$250/session). Start your first small group program. Income: $5,000 - $10,000/month.
  • Year 2+: The Scale. Implement the Hybrid Model. Launch a signature digital course or high-ticket mastermind. Income: $15,000 - $25,000+/month.
