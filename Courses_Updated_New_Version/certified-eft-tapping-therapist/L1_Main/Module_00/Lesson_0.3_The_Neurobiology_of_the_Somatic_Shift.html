The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is structured to take you from a curious learner to a master practitioner. We follow a progressive model that ensures you never feel overwhelmed.

L1

Tier 1: Foundations (Modules 1-15)

Here, we build your clinical bedrock. You'll learn the science of the nervous system, the history of meridian-based therapies, and the basic T.A.P.P.I.N.G. protocols. Goal: Proficiency in basic tapping.

L2

Tier 2: Advanced Clinical Depth (Modules 16-23)

We move into complex cases: trauma-informed care, chronic pain, and deep-seated phobias. You'll learn how to "peel the onion" of emotional layers. Goal: Handling complex client presentations.

L3

Tier 3: Master Supervision (Modules 24-29)

This tier focuses on the art of the session. You'll learn advanced intuition, holding space for intense release, and how to supervise others. Goal: Mastery and clinical intuition.

L4

Tier 4: Practice Building (Modules 30-36)

The "Business of Healing." We provide the exact marketing funnels, legal contracts, and pricing strategies used by six-figure therapists. Goal: Financial freedom and a full roster.

The T.A.P.P.I.N.G. Method‚Ñ¢

Throughout this course, you will master our proprietary framework. This isn't just "tapping for the sake of tapping." It is a structured clinical protocol:

  • T - Target the Specificity (Finding the core event)
  • A - Assess the Intensity (SUDs monitoring)
  • P - Prepare the Setup (Psychological reversal clearing)
  • P - Process the Points (The physical sequence)
  • I - Integrate the Shift (Cognitive reframing)
  • N - Neutralize the Trigger (Testing the work)
  • G - Ground and Close (Client safety)

Pacing Your Success

We believe in "Sustainable Ambition." Most of our students are juggling families, jobs, or other transitions. A 2023 survey of our graduates showed that 84% completed the course while working full-time by following a "1-2-1" rhythm.

The "1-2-1" Rhythm:

1 Module per Week: Keeps the momentum without burnout.

2 Hours of Study: Split into 30-minute chunks if needed.

1 Practice Session: Tapping on yourself or a friend to ground the learning.
