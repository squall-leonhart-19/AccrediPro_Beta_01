Preparing for Your Certification Exam

You have learned the science, the protocols, and the application. Now, it's time to formalize your expertise. The Level 1 Exam is designed to ensure you can safely and effectively guide a client through a standard EFT session.

Exam Blueprint

  • Format: 20 Multiple Choice Questions
  • Passing Score: 80% (16/20 correct)
  • Key Focus Areas: The 9 Tapping Points, The Setup Statement, Measuring SUDs, Tapping for Physical vs. Emotional Pain, and Ethical Boundaries.
  • Retake Policy: You may retake the exam if needed, but we recommend reviewing the Module 14 "Troubleshooting" lesson if you don't pass on the first try.
