1. What is the primary purpose of a discovery call?

2. How should you respond when a client says, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge the importance of the decision, then ask: "What specifically do you need to think through? Is it the time, the investment, or the process?" This uncovers the real objection.

It demonstrates confidence and gives the prospect space to process the value. Talking too much after the price often signals insecurity and a desire to "defend" the cost.
