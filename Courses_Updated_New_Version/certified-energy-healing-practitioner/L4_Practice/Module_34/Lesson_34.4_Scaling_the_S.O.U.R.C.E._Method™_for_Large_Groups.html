The C (Calibrate) phase in a large group is what we call "Setting the Frequency of the Chamber." Whether you are in a hotel ballroom or a Zoom room, the environment must be calibrated to support the specific outcome of your workshop.

  1. Intentional Staking: Before participants arrive, "stake" the four corners of the space with your intent (e.g., "This is a field of safety and rapid transformation").
