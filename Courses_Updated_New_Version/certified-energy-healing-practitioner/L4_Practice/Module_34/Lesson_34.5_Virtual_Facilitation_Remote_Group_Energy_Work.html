Technical Requirements for Professionalism

For a practitioner charging $997+ for a certification or workshop, "good enough" tech is a boundary violation. Your technical setup is the physical vessel for the energetic work. If your audio is grainy, the "Calibrate" (C) phase of your work will be less effective because the participant's nervous system is distracted by noise.

  • Audio: Use a dedicated USB condenser microphone. High-frequency sound healing (bowls, chimes) requires "Original Sound for Musicians" settings in Zoom to prevent the software from filtering out the healing frequencies as "background noise."

  • Platform: Zoom is the industry standard for breakouts, but ensure you are using the "Spotlight" feature correctly to focus the group's collective gaze during the Rejuvenate (R) phase.
