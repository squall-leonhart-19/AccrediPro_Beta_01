5. Objection Alchemy

An objection is not a "No." It is a request for more information or a manifestation of the client's fear. We use the A.C.E. Method: Acknowledge, Clarify, Explore.

Common Scenarios:

  • "I need to talk to my husband."
    Response: "I completely respect that partnership. When you talk to him, what are the main points you want to convey about why this is a priority for your health right now?"
  • "It‚Äôs just a lot of money right now."
    Response: "I hear you. If we set the money aside for a second, is this the support you feel you need to actually solve the burnout?"
Luna‚Äôs Insight

Never defend your price. Instead, defend the client‚Äôs potential. Remind them of what they stand to lose if they stay in their current state for another year.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary purpose of the "Deep Dive" phase (Phase 2) of the enrollment call?

The purpose is to move beyond surface symptoms and help the client realize the "Cost of Inaction"‚Äîunderstanding how their current energetic state is impacting their future health, career, and relationships.

2. Why is "Package Pricing" superior to "Hourly Pricing" for an energy healing practitioner?

Package pricing shifts the focus from "time spent" to "results achieved." It ensures client commitment over 12 weeks (which is necessary for energetic shifts) and provides the practitioner with predictable, higher-level income.

3. How should you respond when a client says, "I need to think about it"?

Acknowledge the decision, then clarify by asking, "Of course. Usually, when people need to think about it, it's either the money, the time, or they aren't sure I'm the right person. Which one is it for you?" This opens an honest dialogue.

4. What is the "Confidence Gap" in pricing?

It is the intentional silence you maintain after stating your price. It allows the client to sit with the investment without the practitioner "rescuing" them by lowering the price or over-explaining.
Luna‚Äôs Insight

Practice your price in the mirror. Say "$2,500" or "$3,000" until it feels as neutral as saying your phone number. Your nervous system must be regulated for the client's nervous system to feel safe investing.
