Gas Discharge Visualization (GDV) & Bio-Well

Developed by Dr. Konstantin Korotkov, Gas Discharge Visualization (GDV) is the modern, computerized evolution of Kirlian photography. It operates on the principle of "electron emission." When a high-intensity electromagnetic field is applied to an object (like a fingertip), it stimulates a "glow" of photons and electrons.

The Bio-Well device captures this emission from all ten fingers. These images are then mapped via complex algorithms to different organ systems based on the Korean Su-Jok meridian system. A 2020 study involving 1,200 participants demonstrated that GDV parameters significantly correlate with traditional physiological markers of stress and autonomic nervous system balance (p < 0.05).

It doesn't look inside your house, but it sees the atmospheric pressure and energy patterns surrounding it. We are looking at the 'energetic weather' of your body to see where storms are brewing before they turn into rain."

Polycontrast Interference Photography (PIP)

Polycontrast Interference Photography (PIP) is a non-invasive imaging system that allows us to see the light interference patterns surrounding the human body. Unlike standard photography, PIP uses a specialized software filter to reveal the "subtle light" (biophotons) emitted by the biofield.

In a clinical energy healing setting, PIP is used to visualize:

  • Congestion: Darker, "muddy" colors indicating stagnant energy (The "Open" phase focus).
  • Leaks: Sharp, jagged lines indicating boundary issues (The "Empower" phase focus).
  • Flow: Bright, symmetrical patterns indicating high-vibrational health.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary scientific principle behind Gas Discharge Visualization (GDV)?

The principle of "electron emission" or the "Kirlian effect," where a high-intensity electromagnetic field stimulates a glow of photons and electrons from the subject, which is then captured and analyzed digitally.

2. How does PIP imaging differ from standard photography?

PIP uses specialized software filters to reveal light interference patterns (biophotons) that are usually invisible to the naked eye, allowing for the visualization of congestion, leaks, and flow in the biofield.

3. If a client's scan shows low "Energy Symmetry," which phase of the S.O.U.R.C.E. Method is most likely indicated?

The "U" (Unify) phase, which focuses on energetic cohesion and harmonizing the different layers and sides of the biofield.

By stating there is an "energetic imbalance in the zone associated with the liver meridian," avoiding any physical medical diagnosis.
