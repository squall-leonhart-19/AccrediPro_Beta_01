Ethical Strategies for High-Vibrational Work

Maintaining boundaries in energy medicine is more complex than in massage or talk therapy because the work occurs in the Subtle Bodies. Use these three strategies to maintain professional distance while staying heart-centered:

  • The 'Empower' Anchor: End every session by giving the client a specific Daily Energetic Hygiene task. This shifts the focus from "what you did for them" to "what they are doing for themselves."

Check Your Understanding

2. You feel a deep physical ache in your own heart during a session with a client who has experienced grief. What is the ethical first step?

3. Why is the 'Unify' stage particularly prone to transference?

Because the 'Unify' stage creates field-cohesion and heart-centered resonance. This deep intimacy can be misinterpreted by the client's subconscious as a signal for a deeper personal or romantic connection.

4. What is the primary risk of the 'Vulnerability Loop'?

The primary risk is energetic dependency. It prevents the client from moving into the 'Empower' stage, leaving them reliant on the practitioner for their vibrational stability.
