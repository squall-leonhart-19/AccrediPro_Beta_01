Key Takeaways

  • Scope is Sacred: Never advise on medication, even if the client is a medical professional themselves.
  • Stabilization First: In complex trauma/medical cases, focus on grounding and "sealing" before deep clearing.
  • The Referral is a Tool: High-level practitioners use a "Care Team" approach for complex clients.
  • Dependency is an Ethical Failure: Our goal is to empower the client's own biofield resilience, not make them session-dependent.
  • Documentation is Protection: Always document your ethical conversations and referrals in your client notes.
