Epigenetics and Energy Medicine

Epigenetics is the study of how behaviors and environment can cause changes that affect the way your genes work. Unlike genetic changes, epigenetic changes are reversible and do not change your DNA sequence; however, they change how your body reads a DNA sequence.

Research into meditation and biofield therapies suggests that high-vibrational states can actually "turn off" pro-inflammatory genes. In a landmark study (n=40), researchers found that just one day of intensive mindfulness and energetic practice resulted in the downregulation of the HDAC and COX2 genes, which are directly responsible for systemic inflammation.

Cytokines and Immune Resilience

Cytokines are small proteins that are crucial in controlling the growth and activity of other immune system cells and blood cells. When released, they signal the immune system to do its job. However, "cytokine storms" or chronic low-grade cytokine release lead to autoimmune conditions and chronic fatigue.

Energetic shifts‚Äîspecifically those achieved during the Rejuvenate phase‚Äîhave been shown to modulate cytokine production. By introducing a coherent energetic frequency, we help the client's biofield "entrain" to a state of harmony. This entrainment reduces the production of IL-6 (a pro-inflammatory cytokine) and increases IL-10 (an anti-inflammatory cytokine).

Telomeres and Cellular Aging
