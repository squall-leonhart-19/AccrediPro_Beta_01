The Gold Standard: Meta-Analytic Overview

In the hierarchy of scientific evidence, the Meta-Analysis sits at the very top. It doesn't just look at one study; it aggregates data from dozens of Randomized Controlled Trials (RCTs) to find the "true" effect size. For an energy practitioner, these are your most powerful tools for legitimacy.

A landmark 2017 meta-analysis published in the Journal of Evidence-Based Integrative Medicine examined the efficacy of Reiki. The researchers found that Reiki was statistically superior to placebo in 8 out of 13 studies for various health outcomes. Specifically, for pain and anxiety, the effect sizes were moderate to large, suggesting that the "biofield" interaction produces changes that cannot be explained by the "expectancy effect" (placebo) alone.
