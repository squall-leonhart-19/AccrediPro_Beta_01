Check Your Understanding

1. Why must "Stabilization" occur before "Trauma Clearing" in a complex case?

If a client's energetic "container" is porous or leaking, clearing trauma releases high-intensity frequencies that the system cannot contain, leading to energetic exhaustion or physical symptom flare-ups (crashing).

2. What does a "Dorsal Heart Leak" typically indicate in a clinical assessment?

It usually indicates energy being "pulled" into the past, often associated with unintegrated grief, regret, or a refusal to let go of a previous version of one's life.

3. If a client asks if they can stop their medication because they "feel so much better" after your sessions, what is the required response?

"I am so glad you are feeling better! However, I am not a medical doctor and cannot advise on medication. Any changes to your prescription must be discussed with and managed by your prescribing physician."

4. How does polypharmacy (multiple medications) affect your energetic scan?

Medications can create a "chemical sheath" or dampening effect in the field, making the emotional and mental layers harder to read. Advanced practitioners learn to scan the "Etheric Template" which remains accessible beneath the chemical layer.
