The Vagus nerve controls the "freeze" response (Dorsal Vagal). If the nerve is in a state of shutdown, the body will reflexively tighten against any energetic intervention. Toning the Vagus nerve signals "safety," allowing the armor to dissolve.

The Schumann Resonance (7.83 Hz) is frequently used to ground the nervous system and provide a stable baseline for "opening" stubborn somatic blockages.
