Developing 'Inner Sight' for Deep-Tissue Assessment

Inner Sight is the ability to perceive the energetic "anatomy" beneath the skin without physical touch. This is developed through coherent visualization and the cultivation of the Ajna (Third Eye) center. In Level 2, we practice "X-ray Scanning."

To perform an X-ray Scan:

  1. Center yourself in the Heart-Brain coherence state.
  2. Softly gaze at the client's physical body, but allow your focus to "rest" about 2 inches inside the body.
  3. Note where the light seems "dimmed" or "opaque." These areas often correspond to physical congestion or incipient health issues before they manifest in blood work.
