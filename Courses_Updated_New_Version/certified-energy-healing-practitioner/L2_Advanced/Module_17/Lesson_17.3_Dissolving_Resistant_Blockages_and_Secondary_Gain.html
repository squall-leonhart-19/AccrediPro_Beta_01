The Mystery of Secondary Gain

In clinical energy medicine, Secondary Gain refers to the hidden benefits a client receives from their illness or energetic imbalance. This is not "faking it"; it is a sophisticated subconscious survival mechanism. For a woman in her 40s or 50s, illness may provide the only socially acceptable way to receive care, rest, or escape from toxic obligations.

When you perform your S: Scan, secondary gain often feels like a "repelling" force. As you move your hands through the auric field, you may feel a push-back, as if the field is actively defending the blockage. This is a sign that the client's ego-structure perceives the blockage as a protective shield rather than an impairment.

Coach Tip: Language Matters

Never tell a client they "want" to be sick. Instead, say: "I'm sensing a part of your energy field that feels very protective. It's as if this blockage is working hard to keep you safe from something. Does that resonate?" This invites the subconscious to cooperate rather than defend.
