Discernment: Mental Health vs. Energetic Attachment

The first and most critical step in the S.O.U.R.C.E. Scan is discernment. As a professional practitioner, you must never assume a spiritual cause where a biological or psychological one exists. However, research into the biofield suggests that external energetic "parasites" or dissonant thought-forms can indeed latch onto weakened areas of the auric field.

Coach Tip: The Professional Language

Avoid using "spooky" or alarmist language like "demons" or "curses" with clients. This creates fear, which lowers their vibration and makes clearing harder. Instead, use professional terms like "intrusive energetic frequencies," "dissonant thought-forms," or "non-beneficial attachments." This keeps the client empowered rather than victimized.

The Sovereignty Protocol: Utilizing Open and Unify

Once you have identified a non-beneficial attachment through your S.O.U.R.C.E. Scan, you must move into the Open and Unify phases with specific intent. This is not a "battle"; it is an assertion of the client's natural right to their own energetic space.

1. The 'Open' Phase: Creating the Ejection Pathway

Attachments often hide in "congested" energy. By using Meridian Flushing (Module 2, Lesson 4), you clear the stagnation that allows the attachment to remain anchored. During this phase, you are not attacking the attachment; you are removing the "food source" (stagnant, low-vibrational energy).

2. The 'Unify' Phase: The Sovereignty Command

In the Unify phase, you assist the client in bringing their awareness into their Heart-Centered Coherence. An attachment cannot remain in a biofield that is 100% unified and coherent. You will guide the client through a silent or spoken declaration of sovereignty: "I reclaim my field. Only that which is of my own light and for my highest good may remain."

Legal and Ethical Considerations: When to Refer Out

This is the most critical part of your professional standards. Energy healing is a complementary therapy, not a replacement for psychiatric care.

Referral Red Flags

If a client exhibits any of the following, stop the energetic clearing and provide a professional referral to a mental health specialist immediately:

  • Command Hallucinations: Voices telling them to harm themselves or others.
  • Disorganized Speech: Inability to maintain a coherent conversation.
  • Paranoid Delusions: Beliefs that the government or "the aliens" are tracking them via physical implants.
  • Sudden Personality Shift: If the client seems completely "gone" or replaced by another persona during the session.
