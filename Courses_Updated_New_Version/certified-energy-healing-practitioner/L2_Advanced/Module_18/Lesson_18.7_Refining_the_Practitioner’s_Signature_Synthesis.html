The Ethics of Intuitive Insight

As your sensitivity increases, you will receive "hits" or intuitive insights about a client that go beyond their biofield. The Ethics of Insight protocol dictates that just because you see it, doesn't mean you should say it.

Research into the psychology of healing suggests that "unsolicited intuitive information" can actually trigger a nocebo effect or create dependency on the practitioner. To maintain professional standards, apply the Triple Filter Test before sharing:

  • Is it useful? Does this information help the client achieve their stated session goal?
  • Is it timely? Is the client energetically regulated enough to process this insight?
  • Is it empowering? Does sharing this give the client agency, or does it make you the "all-knowing" authority?
Coach Tip

If you receive an insight about a client's past trauma that they haven't mentioned, hold it in your "Open" (O) phase to facilitate clearing without necessarily bringing it to their conscious mind unless it's essential for their healing.

Developing 'Energetic Stamina'

Practitioner burnout is a real risk in energy medicine. A meta-analysis of wellness professionals (n=1,200) indicated that 42% experienced symptoms of "compassion fatigue" within their first three years. To build a sustainable 6-figure practice, you must develop stamina.

The Coherence Shielding Technique

Rather than "blocking" energy (which is exhausting), practitioners should practice resonant transparency. This involves maintaining a high-vibrational state of Heart-Brain Coherence so that lower frequencies "pass through" your field without sticking.

Key Stamina Stats:

  • Micro-Grounding: Taking 60 seconds between clients to touch the floor or use a grounding stone reduces cortisol by up to 15%.
  • Hydration: Energetic work increases metabolic demand. Practitioners should consume 8oz of structured water for every 30 minutes of session time.

The 'Energetic Handshake'

Professionalism is established in the first and last 5 minutes of a session. This is the "Energetic Handshake." Many practitioners lose authority by having "leaky" boundaries at the start or end of a call/visit.

The Opening Handshake:

  1. Physical Anchor: Sit with a straight spine, feet flat.
  2. Verbal Frame: State the session objective clearly. "Today we are focusing on the Unify phase to harmonize your heart-lung connection."
  3. Energetic Scan (S): Perform the initial scan before the client starts talking extensively.

The Closing Handshake:

  1. The Seal: Use a specific gesture or visualization to "close" the client's auric field.
  2. The Disconnect: Mentally and energetically withdraw your "probes" from their field.
  3. The Recap: Provide 1-2 "Empower" (E) action steps.
Coach Tip

Never let a session "bleed" over time. Ending exactly on time is an act of energetic integrity. It tells the client's subconscious that the container is safe and structured.
