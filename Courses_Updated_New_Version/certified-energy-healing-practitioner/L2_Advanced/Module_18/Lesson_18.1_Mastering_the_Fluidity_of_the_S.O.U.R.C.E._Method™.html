Identifying Energetic Pivot Points

A Pivot Point is a moment during a session where the client's energy field signals that it is not ready for the next phase, or that a previous phase needs re-addressing. Recognizing these points prevents "energetic indigestion," where the client feels overwhelmed or "spaced out" after a session.

Current Phase The Signal (Pivot Point) The Necessary Loop-Back
Unify Field feels "brittle" or resists blending. Return to Open; there is still unresolved density.
Rejuvenate Energy feels "leaky" or won't hold. Return to Unify; the layers aren't cohesive enough to hold the charge.
Calibrate Client becomes restless or agitated. Return to Scan; a new frequency or trauma layer has surfaced.
