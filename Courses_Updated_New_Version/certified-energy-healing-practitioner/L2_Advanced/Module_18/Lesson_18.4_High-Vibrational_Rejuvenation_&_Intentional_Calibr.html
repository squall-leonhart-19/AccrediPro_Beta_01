Monitoring for 'Frequency Rejection'

In rare cases, especially with highly sensitive clients (HSPs), the introduction of high-vibrational energy can cause Frequency Rejection. This is not "bad" energy; it is the physical body's inability to process the rapid shift in voltage.

Signs of Frequency Rejection:

  • Sudden headache during the session.
  • Nausea or dizziness.
  • Acute irritability or emotional outbursts.

Safety First

If you notice signs of rejection, immediately move to the Empower phase (Module 6). Use heavy grounding techniques (Black Tourmaline visualization or manual foot pressure) to "drain" the excess voltage until the client stabilizes.
