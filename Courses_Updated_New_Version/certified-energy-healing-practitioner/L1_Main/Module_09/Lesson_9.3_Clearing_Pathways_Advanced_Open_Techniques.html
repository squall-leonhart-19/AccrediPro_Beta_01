The Sushumna Nadi is the central energetic spine. When this is closed, the client feels disconnected from their "Higher Self" or "Source." Opening the Sushumna is the ultimate goal of the 'Open' phase, as it allows the higher-dimensional energies of the 'Rejuvenate' phase to actually take hold.

Managing the 'Healing Crisis'

As you become more effective at "Opening," your clients will occasionally experience a Healing Crisis (also known as a Herxheimer-like reaction in energy medicine). This occurs when the physical body cannot process the volume of released energetic toxins as fast as you are moving them.

Statistics show that approximately 15-20% of clients undergoing deep energetic clearing will experience some form of temporary "detox" symptom, such as:

  • Mild flu-like symptoms or fatigue.
  • Unexpected emotional outbursts (crying or anger).
  • Vivid dreams or temporary insomnia.
  • Increased thirst or "heavy" limbs.
Coach Tip: Professional Communication

Always frame the healing crisis as a positive indicator. Tell the client: "Your body is successfully purging years of accumulated stress. This temporary discomfort is the physical evidence of your energetic breakthrough." This prevents the "imposter syndrome" fear that you've "hurt" the client.
