The Phantom DNA Effect

In 1995, Dr. Vladimir Poponin and his team conducted a groundbreaking experiment. They placed DNA in a vacuum tube and passed a laser through it. When they removed the physical DNA, the photons continued to behave as if the DNA were still there.

This "Phantom DNA" effect proves that matter leaves a permanent energetic footprint. For the energy practitioner, this explains why emotional trauma or physical injuries can continue to cause pain long after the physical wound has healed. The "phantom" of the trauma remains in the biofield, requiring an Open (O) and Clear technique to remove the informational imprint.

Clinical Differentiation: Physical, Etheric, and Astral

When you are working with a client, you must distinguish between these three "bodies" to choose the right Calibrate (C) tool:
