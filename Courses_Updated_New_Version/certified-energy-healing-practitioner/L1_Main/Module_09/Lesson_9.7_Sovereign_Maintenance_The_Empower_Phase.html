Client Education: When to Recalibrate

A key part of the Empower phase is teaching the client when they cannot do it themselves. This creates a professional relationship rather than a dependency.

The "Red Flag" List for Clients:

  • Chronic "Leak" Fatigue: Waking up tired even after 8 hours of sleep.
  • The "Emotional Mirror" Effect: Feeling an emotion that clearly does not belong to your current situation.
  • Physical Static: A feeling of "buzzing" or "fuzziness" in the head that doesn't clear with grounding.
  • Sudden Aversion: Feeling a sudden, intense need to avoid people or places that were previously fine.
