Repairing the Auric Membrane: Tears, Leaks, and Thinning

The auric membrane is the "skin" of the biofield. It serves as both a filter and a boundary. In a Deep Dive session, you will often encounter structural compromises in this membrane caused by trauma, surgery, or prolonged stress.

Identifying Structural Compromise

  • Tears: Sharp, jagged energetic signatures often found near old physical injury sites or areas of sudden emotional shock.
  • Leaks: Areas where energy is "bleeding" out, often felt as a cold draft or a "sinkhole" during your S.O.U.R.C.E. Scan.
  • Thinning: A general lack of density in the field, making the client "overly sensitive" to the environments of others (common in empaths).

To repair these, we use the Fibonacci Weave Technique. This involves moving your hands in a spiral pattern over the compromised area, "spinning" the etheric matter back into a cohesive web. This isn't just visualization; you are interacting with the bio-photonic emissions of the client's field to encourage structural re-patterning.
