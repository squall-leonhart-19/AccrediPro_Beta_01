Breathwork: The Sip Breath Technique

Breath is the bellows that fans the energetic flame. While most healing modalities focus on deep belly breathing, the Sip Breath is specifically designed for throughput‚Äîmoving large volumes of energy through the biofield quickly.

How to Perform the Sip Breath:

  1. Form your lips as if you are drinking through a very thin straw.
  2. Take a sharp, quick "sip" of air through the mouth.
  3. Immediately exhale slowly through the nose.
  4. Visualize the "sip" pulling energy from the Cosmic Reservoir down into your crown.
  5. Visualize the exhale pushing that energy out through your hands into the client's field.

Safety Protocols: Conduit vs. Battery

To remain a professional practitioner for decades, you must adhere to the Conduit Safety Protocol (CSP). This ensures that the energy moving through you doesn't "stick" to your own organs or deplete your nervous system.

  • The 10% Rule: Always keep 10% of the energy you channel for your own field. Think of it as a "tax" for the use of your physical vessel.
  • The Disconnect: At the end of every session, use a physical gesture (like washing hands or a "karate chop" through the air) to signal the end of the energetic connection.
  • Hydration: Energy flow uses electrolytes. A professional practitioner should consume 8-10oz of mineralized water between every session.
