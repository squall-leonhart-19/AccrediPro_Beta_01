Breath as the Bridge: The 5.5 Rhythm

The breath is the only physiological function that is both voluntary and involuntary. This makes it the perfect "bridge" between the conscious mind and the autonomic nervous system. To achieve unification, we utilize Coherent Breathing.

Research published in Frontiers in Psychology indicates that a rhythm of 5.5 breaths per minute (roughly 5.5 seconds in, 5.5 seconds out) maximizes Heart Rate Variability (HRV). This specific frequency synchronizes the heart, lungs, and brain into a state of "resonance."
