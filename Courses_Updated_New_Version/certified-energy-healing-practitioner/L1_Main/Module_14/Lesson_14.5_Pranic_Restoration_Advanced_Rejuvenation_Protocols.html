Identifying Energetic Depletion

How do you know when a client needs the 'Rejuvenate' protocol? Look for these "Subtle Markers":

  • Aura Transparency: The field looks "thin" or "washed out" during scanning.
  • Vibration Lag: When you move your hand over the body, the energetic response feels "slow" or "heavy."
  • Temperature Drops: The client may feel physically cold despite a warm room.
  • The "Grey Veil": A subtle greyish hue in the peripheral vision when viewing the client's field.
