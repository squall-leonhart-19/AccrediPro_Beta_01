Common Pitfalls for New Practitioners

When moving into advanced field dynamics, watch out for these 4 common mistakes:

1
Over-Explaining the Science

Diane doesn't need a PhD in quantum physics; she needs to feel better. Keep your explanations focused on her experience, not your textbook knowledge.

2
Matching the Client's Depletion

If the client is tired, don't lower your energy to match hers. Maintain your own high-frequency, grounded field. This is "Vibrational Leadership."

3
Using "Woo-Woo" Language with Professionals

Use words like "biofield," "structural integrity," and "resilience" rather than "aura sparkles" or "energy ghosts." It builds legitimacy and trust.

4
Forgetting the Business "Ask"

If a client has a breakthrough in a practice session, that is the best time to offer a long-term package. You are helping them, and you deserve to be compensated for that transformation.
