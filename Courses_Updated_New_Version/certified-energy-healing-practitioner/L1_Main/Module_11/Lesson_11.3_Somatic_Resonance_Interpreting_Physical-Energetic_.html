Translating Physical Sensations into Actionable Insights

When you perform a manual scan, your hands will receive different types of feedback. Interpreting these correctly is the difference between a general session and a Precision Deep Dive.

1. Thermal Feedback (Heat and Cold)

Heat: Usually indicates active inflammation or "hot" emotions like rage, acute anxiety, or a system trying to purge a toxin. It suggests the 'Open' phase needs to focus on venting and cooling the field.

Cold: Indicates depletion or withdrawal. This is common in cases of chronic fatigue or long-standing grief where the life force has retreated from the area. This requires the 'Rejuvenate' phase to bring "Liquid Light" back into the void.

2. Kinetic Feedback (Tingling, Pulsing, Magnetism)

Tingling/Electricity: Often signals that a blockage is currently breaking up. It is a positive sign of movement. Magnetism/Resistance: Feeling a "push back" from the field indicates a strong protective shield or a refusal to let go of a specific pattern. You must use the 'Open' phase gently here to avoid triggering the client's nervous system.
