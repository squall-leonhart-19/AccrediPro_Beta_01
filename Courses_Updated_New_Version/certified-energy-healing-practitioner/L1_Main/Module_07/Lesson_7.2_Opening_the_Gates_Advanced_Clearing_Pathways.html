Physiological Release Markers

How do you know if the clearing is working? You don't have to guess. The body provides Release Markers. As a professional practitioner, you must educate your client on these so they don't feel "weird" when they happen.

  • Yawning: This is the most common marker. It is a sign of the nervous system switching from Sympathetic (fight/flight) to Parasympathetic (rest/repair). It represents a "reboot" of the energetic system.
  • Involuntary Twitching: Often called Kriyas in Eastern traditions, these are fascial releases where the physical body lets go of stored kinetic energy from past trauma.
  • Temperature Fluctuations: Sudden heat (the "healing flush") indicates a surge of Qi/Prana through a previously blocked gate. Sudden cold often indicates the release of old, "frozen" emotional imprints.
  • Stomach Gurgling: This is a clinical sign that the body is moving back into "rest and digest" mode, indicating a successful 'Open' phase.
