Common Mistakes to Avoid

Mistake Why it Hurts Progress The "Empower" Correction
The "Rescue" Loop Client becomes dependent on you for every minor stressor. Ask: "What tool from our kit can you use right now?"
Over-Explaining Drowns the client in theory when they need practical action. Keep "Home Frequency" tips to under 3 minutes.
Ignoring Boundaries You take on the client's stress because you want them to "win." Model sovereignty by ending sessions on time and staying grounded.
Vague Homework "Just try to be mindful" results in zero action. Give a specific trigger: "When you turn on the coffee maker, do your grounding."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary shift in the "Empower" phase of coaching?

The shift from the practitioner being the "healer" to the practitioner being the "mentor/partner," and the client taking full ownership of their energetic maintenance.

2. Why is the "Golden Shield" or "Glass Wall" visualization effective for a client like Elena?

It provides a mental and energetic boundary that allows for compassion without "empathic distress" or energy absorption from her family.

3. What is a "Home Frequency Plan"?

A customized set of simple, repeatable energetic hygiene tools the client can use daily to maintain the results achieved during professional sessions.

4. How do you know a client is ready for the Empower phase?

When their acute symptoms have stabilized and they begin asking questions about how to "keep this feeling" or handle specific real-world stressors.
Key Takeaways
  • Empowerment is the Goal: Your success is measured by how little the client eventually needs you for basic maintenance.
  • Energetic Sovereignty: Teach clients that their energy is their most valuable currency and they are the only ones with the "PIN."
  • Practicality over Theory: A 2-minute tool used daily is better than a 60-minute meditation that never happens.
  • The Mentor Role: Transitioning to a mentor role builds legitimacy and professional respect, positioning you as a high-level wellness authority.
