The Science of Non-Locality

The most common hurdle for new practitioners (and their clients) is the question: "How can I feel someone's energy if they aren't here?" The answer lies in quantum non-locality. In physics, non-locality refers to the ability of objects to instantaneously know about each other's state, even when separated by large distances.

This is grounded in Bell's Theorem and subsequent experiments (notably by Alain Aspect in 1982), which proved that subatomic particles, once connected (entangled), remain connected regardless of distance. In the context of the S.O.U.R.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢, we view the client and practitioner as part of a unified field where distance is a physical construct, but not an energetic barrier.

The Surrogate/Proxy Protocol

When you are first learning remote scanning, using a physical surrogate can provide the tactile feedback your brain craves. A surrogate is a physical object‚Äîa doll, a crystal, or even your own body‚Äîthat acts as a "stand-in" for the client's energy field.

The 3-Step Proxy Setup

  1. Intentional Linking: Hold the surrogate and state clearly: "This [object] now represents the energetic biofield of [Client Name] for the purpose of this scan."
  2. Calibration: Perform a quick scan of the surrogate's "neutral" state before connecting to ensure you aren't picking up residual energy from the object itself.
  3. The Scan: Use the hand techniques learned in Lesson 1.4 on the surrogate. If you feel heat on the surrogate's "solar plexus," it corresponds to the client's actual solar plexus.

Mental Projection Techniques

As you progress, you will move beyond physical surrogates into Mental Projection. This involves using your "mind's eye" (Clairvoyance, as discussed in Lesson 1.3) to visualize the client standing before you.

A 1998 study published in the Western Journal of Medicine (Sicher et al.) demonstrated that distant healing significantly improved outcomes for patients with advanced AIDS, even when practitioners had no physical contact. This success begins with a clear mental projection during the scanning phase.

Coach Tip: The "X-Ray" Visualization

If you struggle to visualize, try the "X-ray" technique. Imagine the client is made of frosted glass. As you scan your "mental hands" over them, look for areas that appear darker, denser, or "flickering." These are your points of interest.

Time-Space Considerations

Energy is not bound by the linear "now." Often, during a remote scan, you may pick up an energetic imprint from the past. This usually manifests as a "ghost" sensation‚Äîsomething that feels significant but doesn't match the client's current physical state.

When you encounter this, use the Temporal Query Protocol:

  • "I am feeling a density in the heart space, but it feels 'older' than your current situation. Does this feel like a recent weight, or something from long ago?"

Establishing the Connection

Before any remote scan, you must establish a "Secure Line." This prevents you from picking up "cosmic noise" or the energy of other people in the client's vicinity.

The Remote Connection Checklist:

  • Permission: Never scan someone without their explicit consent. It is an ethical violation and produces inaccurate data.
  • The Anchor: Use the client's full name, birth date, or a recent photo to "tune" your frequency to theirs.
  • The Bubble: Visualize a shared energetic "container" that includes only you and the client for the duration of the scan.
Coach Tip: Professional Boundaries

Always "disconnect" after a remote session. Visualize yourself unplugging a cord or washing your hands in cool water. This prevents "energetic bleed" where you continue to feel the client's symptoms after the session ends.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the scientific principle that explains why energy can be felt across distances?

The principle is Quantum Non-Locality (or Quantum Entanglement), which suggests that particles/energy systems once connected remain linked regardless of physical distance.

2. When using a surrogate, why is "Calibration" a necessary step?

Calibration ensures you are not misinterpreting the natural energy of the object (the doll or crystal) as the client's energy. It establishes a "baseline" of neutrality.

3. What should a practitioner do if they feel an energetic imprint that doesn't match the client's current physical state?

Apply the Temporal Query Protocol. Ask the client if the sensation feels "recent" or "older," as you may be picking up a past energetic trauma or imprint.

4. True or False: It is ethically acceptable to scan a client's spouse if the client asks you to, even without the spouse's permission.

False. Explicit permission from the individual being scanned is required for ethical practice and to ensure clear, accurate energetic data.
