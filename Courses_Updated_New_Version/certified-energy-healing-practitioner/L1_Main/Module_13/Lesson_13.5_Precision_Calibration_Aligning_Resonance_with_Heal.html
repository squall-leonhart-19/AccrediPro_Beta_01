Identifying and Overcoming Frequency Resistance

One of the most common challenges in a deep-dive session is Frequency Resistance. This occurs when the client's subconscious mind or cellular memory clings to a lower-vibrational state because it feels "safe" or familiar.

Resistance often manifests as:

  • Physical Twitching: Sudden, involuntary movements as the nervous system resists the new frequency.
  • Emotional Deflection: The client suddenly changes the subject or uses humor to break the resonance.
  • Temperature Drops: A sudden chill in the room or the client's extremities, indicating a contraction of the biofield.

2. Pupil Dilation and Breath Patterns
