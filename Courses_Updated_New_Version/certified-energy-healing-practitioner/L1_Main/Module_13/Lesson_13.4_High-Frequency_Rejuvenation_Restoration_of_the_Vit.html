Comparative Analysis: Acute vs. Chronic Depletion

Not all depletion is created equal. Your approach in the Rejuvenate phase must be calibrated to the depth of the client's exhaustion. Using the same technique for a "tired mom" and a "burnout survivor" is a hallmark of an amateur approach.

Acute Energetic Fatigue

Typically caused by a recent stressful event (job deadline, minor illness). The biofield is "dim" but the structure is intact. Rejuvenation here is fast and often feels like a "recharge."

Chronic Life-Force Depletion (CLD)

Caused by years of over-extension, trauma, or toxic environments. The biofield is not just dim; it is "brittle" or "thin." This requires the Restoration of the Vital Life Force over multiple sessions, focusing on the Sushumna Nadi (Central Pillar).
