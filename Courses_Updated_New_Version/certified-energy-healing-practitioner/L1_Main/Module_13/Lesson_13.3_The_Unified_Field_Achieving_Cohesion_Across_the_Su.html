Mechanisms of the 'Unify' Phase

Coherence, in physics, refers to waves that are in phase with each other. In the human biofield, this means the frequencies of the physical body (dense) are harmonized with the frequencies of the spiritual body (subtle).

The unification process involves three primary energetic gateways:
