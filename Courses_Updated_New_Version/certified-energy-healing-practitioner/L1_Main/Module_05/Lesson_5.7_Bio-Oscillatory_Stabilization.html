The 'Rubber Band' Effect: Understanding Energetic Homeostasis

In biofield science, the "Rubber Band" Effect refers to the tendency of a complex system to return to its previous state of equilibrium, even if that state was dysfunctional. This is energetic homeostasis. A 2021 study on bio-electromagnetic fields noted that the body's "set point" is reinforced by years of repetitive neural and energetic firing patterns.

When we perform an energy clearing or rejuvenation, we stretch the client's frequency toward a higher state. However, without stabilization, the "elasticity" of their old habits, toxic environments, and limiting beliefs pulls them back. This isn't a failure of the healing session; it is a natural law of physics that must be managed through stabilization protocols.
