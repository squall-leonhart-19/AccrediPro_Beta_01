Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the "Calibration" phase in an energy session?

The goal is to align the practitioner's tools and focus with the client's specific energetic signature, ensuring the intervention is precise rather than general.

2. If a client like Elena is very "mental," where should you focus your calibration?

Focus on grounding the high-frequency mental energy down into the lower energy centers (root and solar plexus) to create stability and "engine start" power.

3. True or False: You should wait until the end of the session to tell the client what you "sensed" during calibration.

False. Sharing brief, professional observations during calibration helps the client feel "seen" and builds the energetic bridge for the work to follow.

4. Why is "Frequency Matching" important?

It allows the practitioner to "meet the client where they are" before attempting to raise their vibration, preventing energetic "rejection" or discomfort.
