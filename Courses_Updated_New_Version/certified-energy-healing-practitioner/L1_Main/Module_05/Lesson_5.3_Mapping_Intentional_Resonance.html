The Sympathetic Resonance Technique

In physics, sympathetic resonance occurs when a passive vibratory body responds to external vibrations to which it has a harmonic likeness. As a practitioner, you act as the "Master Tuning Fork."

To calibrate a client's field, you must first embody the frequency they require. If you are calibrating for "Calm," but your own field is frantic, entrainment will fail. This is why the C: Calibrate phase of the S.O.U.R.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢ requires the practitioner to hold a "Steady State."

The 3-Step Resonance Protocol:

  1. Internal Generation: The practitioner generates the target frequency within their own heart-brain coherence.
  2. Field Radiation: The practitioner expands their biofield to encompass the client's field.
  3. Harmonic Lock: The practitioner maintains the frequency until the client's biofield "locks" into the new rhythm through entrainment.
Coach Tip: Protecting Your Energy

Sympathetic resonance is a one-way street in professional practice. You are the transmitter, not the receiver. Always ensure you have completed your own "Open" and "Unify" steps before attempting to calibrate a client.

Distinguishing Ego Desires vs. Soul Alignment

A critical challenge in calibration is the difference between what the client thinks they want (Ego) and what the biofield needs for homeostasis (Soul Alignment). A 2021 study on biofield therapy outcomes noted that interventions aligned with the body's innate self-regulating mechanisms had a 40% higher success rate than those driven solely by symptom suppression.

Ego Desires are often "away-from" motivations. "I want this pain to go away so I can go back to working 80 hours a week." Calibrating for high energy in this state may actually cause more harm by bypassing the body's need for rest.

Soul Alignment is the "toward" motivation. It seeks the highest functional state of the organism. As a practitioner, you must scan (Module 1) to see if the client's goal is energetically sustainable. If a client asks for "Abundance" but their field is riddled with "Lack" patterns in the root chakra, you must calibrate the foundation first.
