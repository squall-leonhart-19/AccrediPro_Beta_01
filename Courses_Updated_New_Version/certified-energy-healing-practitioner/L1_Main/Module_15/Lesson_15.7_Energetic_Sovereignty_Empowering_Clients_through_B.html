Identifying 'Energy Vampires' and Field Integrity

In clinical energy medicine, "Energy Vampires" are individuals or environments that create a parasitic entrainment. This occurs when a person with a low-frequency, chaotic field unconsciously "hooks" into the coherent field of another to stabilize themselves.

Teach your clients to recognize the "Drop-Off" effect. If a client feels a sudden drop in their Calibration (mood or energy) within 5 minutes of interacting with someone, an energetic hook has likely occurred. This is often seen in high-stress corporate environments or toxic family dynamics.

The "Sacred No" is an energetic command that reinforces the Solar Plexus (Manipura) and the Throat (Vishuddha). When a client says "Yes" while their field is screaming "No," it creates a frequency dissonance.

By practicing the Sacred No, the client effectively "closes the circuit" of their field, preventing the loss of Prana (Vital Force).
