Frequency Modulation: Adjusting the 'Volume'

Not every client needs 100% intensity. A client with a highly sensitive nervous system (often referred to as an HSP or Empath) may find high-frequency rejuvenation overwhelming if it is too "loud."

Modulation Techniques:

  • Distance: Moving your hands further from the body (12-18 inches) softens the frequency.
  • Color Visualization: Visualizing Pale Blue light cools the energy; Golden-White light intensifies it.
  • Pulse: Rather than a steady stream, "pulse" the energy in 3-second intervals to allow the client's biofield to integrate the charge.
Coach Tip #4

If you see the client's eyelids fluttering rapidly or notice their breathing become shallow, you are likely "over-charging" their field. Dial back the intensity and focus on Grounding (E: Empower) before continuing.
