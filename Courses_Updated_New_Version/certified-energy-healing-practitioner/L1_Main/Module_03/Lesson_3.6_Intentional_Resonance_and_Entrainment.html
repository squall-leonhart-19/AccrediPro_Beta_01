The Physics of Resonance: The Tuning Fork Effect

In the world of physics, resonance occurs when a physical object or electronic circuit is exposed to a frequency that matches its own natural frequency. When two systems are in resonance, energy is transferred with maximum efficiency. In energy medicine, we utilize this as Intentional Resonance.

Think of two identical tuning forks. If you strike one and hold it near the other, the second fork will begin to vibrate at the same frequency without ever being touched. As a practitioner, your biofield is the first tuning fork. Through your training in the S.O.U.R.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢, you learn to "strike" your own frequency of coherence, which then invites the client's fragmented or "out-of-tune" energy to match that higher state.
