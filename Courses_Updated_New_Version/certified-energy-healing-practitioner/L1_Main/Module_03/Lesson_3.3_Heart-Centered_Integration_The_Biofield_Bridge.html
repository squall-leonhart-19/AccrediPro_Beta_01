The Heart-Lock Technique

The Heart-Lock Technique is a specific protocol used at the end of a session to "seal" the work. It prevents the client from immediately returning to old energetic patterns once they leave your office.

The Heart-Lock Protocol:

  1. Shift: Have the client shift their attention to the area of the heart.
  2. Breathe: Guide them to breathe slowly and deeply, imagining the breath flowing in and out of the heart center.
  3. Activate: Ask them to make a sincere attempt to experience a regenerative feeling, such as appreciation or care for someone or something in their life.
  4. Radiate: Instruct them to "lock in" this feeling by radiating it throughout their entire physical body and then into the space around them (the biofield).
