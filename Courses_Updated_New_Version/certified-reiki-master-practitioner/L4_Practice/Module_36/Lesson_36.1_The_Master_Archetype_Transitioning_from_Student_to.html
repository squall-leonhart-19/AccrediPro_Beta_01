Defining the Master Archetype

In Jungian psychology, an archetype is a universal, primordial symbol or pattern. The Master Archetype in Reiki is not about being "better" than others; it is about reaching a state of conscious competence where the healing energy and the practitioner's intent are indistinguishable.

Technical proficiency (knowing the symbols, the hand positions, and the history) is the foundation, but the Archetype is the structure. A Master Teacher does not just "do" Reiki; they are a living resonance of the Five Principles (Gokai). For the 40-55 year old professional, this often means unlearning the "performance-based" metrics of the corporate world and moving into a "presence-based" metric of success.
