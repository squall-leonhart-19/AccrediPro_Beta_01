Attracting 'Soul-Aligned' Clients

Marketing is simply the act of being found by the people who need you. For a Reiki Master, "loud" or "aggressive" sales tactics often backfire. Instead, focus on Educational Marketing.

A Master Practitioner doesn't sell "Reiki"; they sell solutions to energetic imbalances. Use the data you've learned about the biofield to write blog posts or social media updates. For example, instead of saying "Book a session," explain "How the Heart Gateway (U) affects your ability to set boundaries at work."

Coach Tip

Don't try to be for everyone. The more specific your niche (e.g., "Reiki for Women in Menopause" or "Reiki for Post-Surgical Recovery"), the more you can charge and the faster you will be recognized as an expert.
