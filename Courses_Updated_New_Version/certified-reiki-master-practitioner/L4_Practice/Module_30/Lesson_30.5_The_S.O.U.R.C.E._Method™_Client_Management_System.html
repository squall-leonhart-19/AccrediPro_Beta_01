    Managing 'Healing Crises': Professional Protocols

    A "Healing Crisis" (or Herxheimer-like energetic reaction) occurs when a client experiences a temporary surge in symptoms or intense emotional release after a session. As a Master Practitioner, you must manage this with clinical calm.

    The Professional Protocol for Emotional Release:

    • Validation: Explain that the release is a sign of the Unblock (U) phase working correctly.
    • Containment: Use Center (C) techniques immediately if the client is still in your office to ensure they do not leave in a fragmented state.
    • Follow-up: Send a "Check-in" message 24 hours post-session. This reduces client fear and prevents them from associating Reiki with "feeling worse."
    Coach Tip

    üí° Include a "What to Expect" section in your initial intake paperwork that specifically mentions the possibility of a healing crisis. Pre-education is the best defense against client anxiety.
