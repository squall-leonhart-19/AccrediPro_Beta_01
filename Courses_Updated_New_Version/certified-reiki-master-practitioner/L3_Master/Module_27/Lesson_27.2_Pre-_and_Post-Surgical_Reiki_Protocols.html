The Pre-Op 'Open' Phase: Calming the Storm

The primary goal of pre-surgical Reiki is to shift the patient from a Sympathetic (Fight/Flight) state into a Parasympathetic (Rest/Digest) state. High cortisol levels pre-surgery are associated with slower wound healing and increased post-operative pain. A 2021 study involving 120 surgical patients showed that those receiving 20 minutes of pre-operative Reiki reported a 34% reduction in pre-surgical anxiety compared to the control group.
