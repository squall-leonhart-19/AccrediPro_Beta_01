The Breaking Technique Protocol:

  1. Locate: Identify the dense Hibiki (sensation) during the Scan phase.
  2. Activate: Draw the Dai Ko Myo over the area and "push" it into the center of the cyst.
  3. Vibrate: Using a slightly cupped hand 2-3 inches above the body, use a subtle vibratory motion (Joshin Kokyu Ho breath synchronized).
  4. Dissolve: Visualize the high-frequency light of the Master symbol shattering the density into fine mist.
