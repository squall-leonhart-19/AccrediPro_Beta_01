4. Referral Triggers (Scope of Practice)

Your Reiki Master certification gives you immense power, but it does not make you a physician. You must refer Evelyn to her MD if you observe:

  • New Lymph Node Changes: Given her history of breast cancer, any new palpable mass in the axilla (armpit) or supraclavicular area requires immediate medical imaging.
  • Thyroid Storm Symptoms: If her "buzzing" turns into heart palpitations, tremors, or significant heat intolerance, her Levothyroxine dose may need adjustment.
  • Major Depressive Episode: If her grief over the divorce leads to an inability to perform daily functions (ADLs).
