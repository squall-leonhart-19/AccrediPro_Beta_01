The Bioenergetics of Anxiety

As a Reiki Master, your goal in the Center phase is to act as a lightning rod, drawing this volatile energy downward.

The Autonomic Nervous System & Reiki

The Autonomic Nervous System (ANS) is comprised of the Sympathetic (fight/flight) and Parasympathetic (rest/digest) branches. Chronic anxiety keeps the Sympathetic system on high alert. Reiki has been shown in clinical settings to increase Heart Rate Variability (HRV), a key indicator of parasympathetic activation.

Technical Application: Anchoring the Light

To master the Center phase for anxiety, you must move beyond general hand positions. We use the Power Symbol (Cho Ku Rei) to "seal" the energy into the physical structure of the lower body.

1. The Sole Connection

Place your palms directly on the soles of the client's feet. Visualize the energy flowing not just into the feet, but through them into the Earth. Mentally draw the Cho Ku Rei on the soles of the feet to "lock" the grounding in place.
