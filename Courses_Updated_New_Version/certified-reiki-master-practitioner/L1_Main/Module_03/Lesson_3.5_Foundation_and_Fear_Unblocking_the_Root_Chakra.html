The Primal Gate: Understanding Root Stagnation

The Root Chakra, or Muladhara, is located at the base of the spine. In the S.O.U.R.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢, we treat this area as the Primal Gate. While the Crown is our connection to the ethereal, the Root is our connection to the physical plane. When this gate is blocked, no amount of "upper chakra" work will stick‚Äîthe client will remain ungrounded and unable to integrate healing.

Stagnation here often feels different than in the Heart or Throat. Practitioners frequently report a dense, leaden, or "sticky" sensation. This is the energy of survival, safety, and tribal belonging. If a client is experiencing career transitions (like many of you), financial stress, or housing instability, the Root will likely present with significant Hibiki.
