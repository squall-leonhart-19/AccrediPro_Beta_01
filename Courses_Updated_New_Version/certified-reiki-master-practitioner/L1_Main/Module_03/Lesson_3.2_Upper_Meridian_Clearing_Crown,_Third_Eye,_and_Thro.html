The Crown: Releasing Mental Fatigue

In our modern, hyper-connected world, the Crown center (Sahasrara) often becomes overloaded with "informational debris." This manifests as mental fatigue, inability to focus, and a sense of spiritual isolation. As a Reiki Master, your role in the Unblock phase is to act as a grounding rod for this excess cerebral energy.

The primary hand position for Crown unblocking involves placing the hands gently on the top of the head, with the fingertips of both hands meeting at the midline. This position encourages the Hibiki (energetic sensation) to move downward, reconnecting the client with their physical body.

Coach Tip: The "Light Touch" Protocol

When working on the Crown, use the lightest touch possible. The Crown is highly sensitive to pressure. Think of your hands as hovering "leaves" on the surface of a pond. This prevents the client's ego-mind from "bracing" against the energy, allowing for a deeper release of mental stagnation.

The Third Eye: Enhancing Receptivity

The Third Eye (Ajna) is the seat of intuition and perception. Blockages here often feel like "static" or "clutter"‚Äîa flurry of racing thoughts that prevent the client from accessing their inner guidance. A 2022 study on biofield therapies noted that focused attention on the prefrontal cortex area (corresponding to the Third Eye) significantly improved alpha wave production, associated with relaxed alertness.

Techniques for Clearing Clutter:

  • The T-Position: One hand over the forehead (Third Eye) and the other at the base of the skull (Occiput). This "sandwiches" the energetic center, allowing stagnation to be processed through the back of the head.
  • Intuitive Sweeping: Gently moving the hands from the center of the forehead toward the temples, metaphorically "parting the clouds" of the mind.

The Throat: Authentic Expression

The Throat chakra (Vishuddha) is frequently blocked in women who have spent years "swallowing" their needs or opinions. Stagnation here often manifests physically as neck tension, thyroid issues, or a literal "lump in the throat."

Professional Insight

Hand Positions for the Throat:

  1. The V-Shape: Cup the hands around the sides of the neck without touching the windpipe. This provides a sense of safety and "containment" for the client's voice.
  2. The Bridge: One hand on the Throat and one on the Heart. This facilitates the flow of "heart-centered truth," ensuring that what is spoken is aligned with the client's core values.

Thermal Feedback: Heat vs. Cold

During the Unblock phase, the sensations you feel in your palms (Hibiki) provide a diagnostic map of the stagnation. Interpreting these sensations correctly is a hallmark of a Master Practitioner.

Sensation Energetic Interpretation Clinical Presentation
Intense Heat Active inflammation or "Over-active" stagnation. Acute stress, anger, or recent trauma.
Cold/Icy Chronic depletion or "Frozen" stagnation. Long-term suppression, depression, or grief.
Tingling/Electric Movement of energy; the "Unblocking" is occurring. Client may experience "sighs" or sudden muscle twitches.
Magnetic Pull The area is "hungry" for energy. Deep exhaustion or nutritional/emotional depletion.
Coach Tip: Managing the "Heat"

If you encounter intense heat in the Third Eye, do not pull away. Instead, visualize your hands as "cool marble." This allows the excess "fire" of the client's overactive mind to be absorbed and neutralized through your own grounded field.
