The 'Laser Focus' Technique: Mental Intention

In Japanese Reiki, the concept of Nen (focused thought/intent) is vital. If your hands are on the client but your mind is on your grocery list, the energy remains "ambient." To unblock stubborn Ki, you must employ Laser Focus.

This technique involves visualizing the Reiki not as a broad glow, but as a concentrated beam of white-gold light emanating from the center of your palms (the Laogong points). Imagine this beam gently vibrating at a high frequency, acting like an ultrasonic cleaner that breaks up the "calcium deposits" of energetic stagnation.
