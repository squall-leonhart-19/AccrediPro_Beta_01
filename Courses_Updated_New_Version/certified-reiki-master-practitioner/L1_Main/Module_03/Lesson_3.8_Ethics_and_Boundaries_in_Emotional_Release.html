  • Over-Talking: Trying to explain the history of Reiki during the unblocking phase. This keeps the client in their "head" rather than their "body."
  • Fixing vs. Facilitating: Feeling like you have to push the energy through. If you're straining, you're doing it wrong. Reiki flows; it doesn't push.
  • Absorbing the Blockage: Forgetting to ground yourself. If Diane releases sadness, you don't need to feel sad with herâ€”you need to be the stable ground she stands on.
