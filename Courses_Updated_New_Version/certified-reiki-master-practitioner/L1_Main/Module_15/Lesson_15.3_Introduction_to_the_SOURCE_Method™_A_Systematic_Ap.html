Integrating SOURCE with Usui Hand Positions

A common question is: "How do I do the SOURCE method while also doing the 12 hand positions?"

Think of the hand positions as the physical locations and the SOURCE method as the energetic intent. For example:

The "Unblock" Phase (U): While your hands are over the throat and heart (Positions 3 and 4), your focus is specifically on identifying and gently dissolving emotional congestion or "lumps" in the energy field.

The "Restore" Phase (R): Once the field feels "clearer," you shift your intent to saturation‚Äîfilling the space where the blockage was with gold or white light. This is often done at the solar plexus and lower abdomen (Positions 5 and 6).

Coach Tip: Transition Timing

In a standard 60-minute session, allocate approximately 10 minutes to S/O, 35 minutes to U/R, and 15 minutes to C/E. This ensures the client doesn't leave feeling "floaty" or ungrounded.
