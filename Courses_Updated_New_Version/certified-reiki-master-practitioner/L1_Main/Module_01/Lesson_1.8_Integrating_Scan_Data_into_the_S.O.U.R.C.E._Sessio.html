Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Medicalizing: Never say "I feel cancer" or "Your heart is failing." Use words like tension, heat, coolness, or heaviness.
  • Over-Apologizing: If you don't feel anything, don't say "I'm sorry, I'm not very good at this yet." Instead, use the "subtle energy" script from Exercise 2.
  • Rushing: The scan should take 3-5 minutes. Rushing makes the client feel like they are on an assembly line.
  • Lack of Eye Contact: Before the scan starts, make firm, warm eye contact. It builds the "trust bridge."

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to explain the Scan (S) to a client like Diane?

It establishes your professional authority, builds trust, and helps the client understand that the session is customized to their specific energetic needs.

2. What is the professional way to describe a "hot" sensation during a scan?

Describe it as an area where the body is "processing stress" or "holding significant energy," rather than giving a medical diagnosis.

3. If you feel nothing during a scan, what should you tell the client?

Frame it as the energy feeling "subtle" or "quiet," which indicates a need for grounding and gentle support to help the system open up.

4. How long should a professional Reiki scan typically take?

A thorough, professional scan usually takes between 3 to 5 minutes to ensure all major energy centers are assessed.
