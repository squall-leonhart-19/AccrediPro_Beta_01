Research into Biofield Stability suggests that centering the energy in the lower abdominal region helps stabilize the autonomic nervous system. A 2015 study on grounding (Earthing) showed that connecting the body's bio-electrical system to a stable point reduces cortisol levels and improves sleep (Chevalier et al., 2015).
