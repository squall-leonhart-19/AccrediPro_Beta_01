The Auric Field: The Three Primary Subtle Layers

The aura is not a single "bubble"; it is a multi-layered field of varying frequencies. In Level 1 Mastery, we focus on the three layers closest to the body, as these are most accessible during a standard Reiki session.

1. The Etheric Body (The Blueprint)

Extending 1-2 inches from the physical skin, the Etheric Body is the energetic "carbon copy" of the physical form. It contains the blueprint for every organ and cell. In the S.O.U.R.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢, when we perform the Scan (S), we are primarily interacting with the Etheric layer to find physical depletion.

2. The Emotional Body (The Feeling Layer)

Extending 2-4 inches out, this layer is fluid and changes color and density based on the client's current emotional state. Stored trauma often appears here as "heavy" or "sticky" energy that a practitioner can feel during the Unblock (U) phase.

3. The Mental Body (The Thought Layer)

Extending 4-8 inches out, this layer holds our belief systems, thought patterns, and mental constructs. Practitioners who are sensitive to this layer can often detect the "buzz" of an overactive mind (anxiety) before the client even speaks.
