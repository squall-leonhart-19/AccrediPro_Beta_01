The Vibrational Architecture of Cho Ku Rei

The Cho Ku Rei is frequently translated as "Place the power of the Universe here." However, from a master-level perspective, we look at its vibrational architecture. The symbol consists of a horizontal line (heaven/source), a vertical line (the grounding path), and a triple spiral.

In the Restore phase, the spiral acts as a centripetal force. Unlike centrifugal force which flings energy outward, centripetal force draws energy toward the center. When you draw the CKR over a client, you are creating a localized "vortex" that pulls universal life force into the cellular matrix.
