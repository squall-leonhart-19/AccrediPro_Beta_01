Reiki and the Strategic Inflammatory Response

We often think of inflammation as "bad," but acute inflammation is the body's first step in healing. It is the process by which the body sends white blood cells and growth factors to a site of injury. Reiki does not necessarily "stop" inflammation; rather, it optimizes the inflammatory cycle.

By applying Reiki during the Restore phase, we help the body move through the inflammatory stage more efficiently, preventing it from becoming chronic. This is achieved through the modulation of cytokines‚Äîsignaling molecules that coordinate the immune response. A 2022 study indicated that biofield therapies can significantly reduce pro-inflammatory cytokines (like IL-6) while boosting anti-inflammatory markers.

Heart Rate Variability (HRV): The Master's Secret

As a Master Practitioner, you may want to use objective data to validate your work. Heart Rate Variability (HRV) is the gold standard for measuring the health of the autonomic nervous system. It measures the variation in time between each heartbeat.

  • Low HRV: Indicates a stressed, sympathetic-dominant system (unhealthy).
  • High HRV: Indicates a resilient, parasympathetic-dominant system (healthy/restored).

Successful implementation of the Restore phase consistently results in a statistically significant increase in HRV. This provides "hard proof" that the Reiki session has successfully shifted the client's physiology into a state of deep restoration.
