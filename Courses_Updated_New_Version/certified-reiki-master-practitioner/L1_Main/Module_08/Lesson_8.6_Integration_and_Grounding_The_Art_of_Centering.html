Mastery of Kenyoku-ho (Dry Bathing)

Kenyoku-ho is the essential "energetic shower" for the practitioner. It is the technical method for disconnecting from the client's biofield. Without this, you may carry the client's emotional or physical "debris" home with you.

The Technical Steps of Kenyoku-ho:

  1. Right to Left: Place your right hand on your left shoulder. Sweep diagonally down across the chest to the right hip with a sharp exhale.
  2. Left to Right: Place your left hand on your right shoulder. Sweep diagonally down across the chest to the left hip with a sharp exhale.
  3. Repeat: Perform this three times in total.
  4. Arm Sweeps: Extend your left arm. Use your right hand to sweep from the shoulder down to the fingertips. Repeat on the other side.

This technique uses somatic interruption to tell your nervous system: "The session is over. I am back in my own space."
