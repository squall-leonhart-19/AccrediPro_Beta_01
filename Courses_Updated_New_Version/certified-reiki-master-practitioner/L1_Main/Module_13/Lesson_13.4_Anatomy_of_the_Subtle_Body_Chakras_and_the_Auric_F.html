Endocrine & Physiological Links

The relationship between chakras and the endocrine system is not coincidental. Each major chakra corresponds to a major endocrine gland. The endocrine system regulates hormones that control metabolism, growth, and mood. In Reiki practice, when we "Unblock" a chakra, we are effectively supporting the hormonal balance of the associated gland.

For example, the Heart Chakra is physically associated with the Thymus gland, which is central to the immune system (T-cell production). This explains why chronic emotional grief (heart blockage) is often scientifically linked to suppressed immune function.

Layers of the Auric Field

The Auric Field, or Biofield, consists of several layers that interpenetrate the physical body and extend outward. As a Reiki Master, you must learn to scan these layers for "tears" or "congestion."

  1. The Etheric Body (1-2 inches): The closest layer to the physical body. It acts as the blueprint for physical tissues. Congestion here often precedes physical illness.
  2. The Emotional Body (2-4 inches): Associated with feelings and moods. This layer is often "cloudy" in clients dealing with trauma or depression.
  3. The Mental Body (4-8 inches): Associated with thought patterns and beliefs. Rigid beliefs can cause "stiffness" in this layer of the field.
  4. The Spiritual Body (8+ inches): The outermost layer that connects the individual to the "Universal Source." In the Open (O) phase of SOURCE, this is where we establish the conduit.

The Lower Dantian (Hara)

While the chakras are the processors of energy, the Lower Dantian (located about two inches below the navel and two inches deep) is the battery. In Japanese Reiki traditions, this is called the Hara.

Mastering the Hara is essential for:

  • Grounding: Preventing the "spacey" feeling after high-frequency Reiki work.
  • Sustainability: Ensuring you are channeling Reiki from the Source, not using your own vital energy (which leads to burnout).
  • Power: A strong Hara allows for more potent energy transmission during the Restore (R) phase.

The SOURCE 'Unblock' Application

In the Unblock (U) phase of our method, we specifically look for stagnation. Stagnation occurs when the spin of a chakra slows or when the auric field becomes "dense" with unprocessed emotional data.

Signs of congestion during a Scan (S):

  • Heat: Indicates inflammation or over-activity (hyper-functioning).
  • Cold: Indicates depletion or lack of flow (hypo-functioning).
  • Tingling/Magnetism: Indicates active movement or "crying out" for energy.
