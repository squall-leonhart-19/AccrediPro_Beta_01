Kenyoku-Ho: The Art of Energetic Cleansing

Kenyoku-Ho, or "Dry Bathing," is a traditional Japanese technique used to purify the practitioner's energy field. Scientifically, this can be viewed as a method of resetting the biofield and disconnecting from external stressors.

The Technical Execution of Kenyoku-Ho

  1. The Right Shoulder: Place your right hand on your left shoulder. Sweep diagonally across the chest to the right hip with a firm, intentional motion while exhaling.
  2. The Left Shoulder: Place your left hand on your right shoulder. Sweep diagonally across the chest to the left hip.
  3. Repeat: Perform this three times (Right-Left-Right).
  4. The Arms: Extend your left arm. Use the right hand to sweep from the shoulder down to the fingertips. Repeat on the other side.
