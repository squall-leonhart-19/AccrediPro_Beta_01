Common Mistakes for New Coaches

  • Over-Explaining: Trying to explain the entire history of Mikao Usui during the intake. Keep it to 2-3 minutes max.
  • Apologizing for the Space: "Sorry it's small," or "Sorry about the noise." Own your space. Your confidence creates their comfort.
  • Asking "Do you feel that?" during the session: This pulls the client out of their meditative state. Save the discussion for the end.
  • Ignoring Physical Comfort: Not offering a bolster under the knees. If the client is in physical pain, they cannot receive the energy work effectively.
