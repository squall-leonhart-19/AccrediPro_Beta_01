The Auric Field: Your Energetic Armor

The aura is a multi-layered field of energy that surrounds the body. In Reiki Level 1, we focus on the three layers closest to the physical form:

  • 1. The Etheric Layer (The Blueprint): Extends 1-2 inches from the body. It mirrors the physical health. When you feel "heat" or "tingling" during a scan, you are often feeling the Etheric layer.
  • 2. The Emotional Layer: Extends 2-4 inches. This layer stores our feelings. It is fluid and changes color/density based on our mood.
  • 3. The Mental Layer: Extends 4-8 inches. This is the realm of thoughts, beliefs, and ideas. Chronic "over-thinkers" often have a very dense or "spiky" mental layer.

The Flow of Ki and the Nadis

If the chakras are the power plants, the Nadis are the electrical wires. Traditional Eastern medicine identifies 72,000 nadis in the human body. The three most critical are:

  1. Sushumna: The central channel running through the spine.
  2. Ida: The lunar, cooling, feminine channel (left side).
  3. Pingala: The solar, heating, masculine channel (right side).

Reiki works by clearing obstructions in these channels. When Ki flows freely through the nadis, the parasympathetic nervous system engages, lowering heart rate variability (HRV) and stimulating cellular repair.

Energetic Congestion: The Root of "Dis-ease"

Emotional trauma doesn't just "stay in the past." According to the Somatic Experiencing framework, trauma is stored as incomplete biological responses. In Reiki terms, this is Stagnant Ki.

Stagnation often feels like:

  • Coldness: A lack of vitality or "numbness" in a specific area.
  • Density/Stickiness: Energy that feels thick or hard to move through.
  • Pulsing: An over-active, inflamed area (often related to acute stress).
