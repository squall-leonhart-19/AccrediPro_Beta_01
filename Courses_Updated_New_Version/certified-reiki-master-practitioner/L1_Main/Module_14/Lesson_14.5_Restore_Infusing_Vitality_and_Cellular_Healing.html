The Physiology of Restoration

Restoration is not merely a "feel-good" state; it is a measurable biological shift. When we channel Reiki during the Restore phase, we are primarily interacting with the Autonomic Nervous System (ANS). Specifically, we are facilitating a transition from Sympathetic dominance (fight-or-flight) to Parasympathetic dominance (rest-and-digest).

A 2021 study published in the Journal of Alternative and Complementary Medicine observed that even 20 minutes of Reiki significantly increased Heart Rate Variability (HRV)‚Äîa gold-standard marker for parasympathetic tone and cellular resilience. When HRV increases, the body enters a state of "biochemical coherence" where cellular repair mechanisms, such as DNA methylation and mitochondrial ATP production, are optimized.

Harmonizing the Endocrine System

The 'Filling' Technique: Subtle Body Replenishment

While hand placements focus on physical organs, the 'Filling' Technique addresses the Etheric Template. Think of the etheric body as the "blueprint" for the physical body. If the blueprint has holes or "thin spots," the physical body will struggle to maintain health.

How to Execute the Filling Technique:

  1. Identify a "thin" area during your initial Scan (S) or Unblock (U) phase.
  2. Place your dominant hand over the area and your non-dominant hand on the Solar Plexus (to anchor the client's power).
  3. Set the intention: "I infuse this template with the frequency of wholeness."
  4. Maintain the position until the Hibiki (sensation) changes from a "pulling/vacuum" feeling to a "steady, radiating" warmth.

This technique is particularly effective for post-surgical recovery or after a significant emotional release, where the client may feel "hollow" or "raw."

Temporal Mastery: How Long is "Enough"?

One of the most common questions for new practitioners is: "How long should I stay in one spot?" In the S.O.U.R.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢, we move away from rigid timers and toward Bio-Feedback Mastery.
