The Five Levels of Hibiki

The sensations you feel in your hands during a scan are called Hibiki (echo or resonance). To the untrained hand, everything feels like "heat." To the Master Practitioner, there are five distinct levels of intensity that indicate the depth of the blockage.

1. Attraction Mild magnetism or "pulling" A healthy call for energy; minor depletion. General restoration.

Where you find Hibiki tells a story. As an expert, you should understand the Somatic-Emotional Mapping of the body:
