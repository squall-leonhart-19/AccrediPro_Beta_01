The Usui Legacy: Historical Roots

To be a Master Practitioner, you must understand the roots of your lineage. Reiki was rediscovered by Mikao Usui in Japan in the early 1920s. Following a 21-day period of fasting and meditation on Mount Kurama, Usui experienced a profound "satori" (enlightenment) where the symbols and frequencies of Reiki were revealed to him.

It is a common misconception that Reiki is a religion. Usui designed it as a "spiritual medicine for all diseases," accessible to anyone regardless of their faith. As a Level 1 practitioner, you are the first link in this modern chain of healing. Your ability to connect to this history provides the energetic weight and tradition that clients value.
