The Five Principles Deconstructed

  • Just for today, do not anger: Anger is often a secondary emotion covering fear or hurt. Energetically, it creates "Hibiki" (sensations) that feel like sharp, hot needles in the liver and solar plexus areas.
  • Just for today, do not worry: Worry is the misuse of imagination. In the 'S' (Scan) phase, chronic worry often manifests as a heavy, "cloudy" sensation around the Third Eye.

  • Just for today, be kind to others: This completes the circuit of energy. What we give out, we receive. Kindness ensures the 'Restore' phase remains stable.
