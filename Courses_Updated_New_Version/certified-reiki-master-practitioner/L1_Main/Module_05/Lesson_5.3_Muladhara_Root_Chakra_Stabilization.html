The Cho Ku Rei Anchor

The Cho Ku Rei (Power Symbol) is the Master Practitioner's primary tool for stabilization. While often used to "turn on" the light, its most potent use in the C: Center phase is to seal the energy.

The Sealing Protocol:

  1. Draw and Intone: Draw the Cho Ku Rei over the Root Chakra (base of spine) and then over each foot.
  2. The "Down and In" Visualization: As you draw the symbol, visualize the spiral pulling the expanded energy from the upper chakras down into the physical body.
  3. The Golden Seal: Visualize the symbol turning into a heavy, golden lead seal that "locks" the energy into the physical frame.
Master Secret

When drawing the Cho Ku Rei for stabilization, draw it clockwise to ground and manifest. This "pulls" the spiritual energy into the physical world, making the healing tangible for the client's daily life.
