Mastering the Discovery Call

The discovery call is NOT a free consultation; it is a qualification interview. Your goal is to determine if the client is a fit for your niche and if they are ready to invest in their health.

The 15-Minute "Vibe Check" Script

  1. The Connection (2 mins): Build rapport. "Tell me a little about what brought you to Ayurveda today."
  2. The Challenge (5 mins): Listen for symptoms (Vikriti). "How is this affecting your daily life?"
  3. The Goal (3 mins): Visualize the outcome. "If we worked together, what would success look like for you in 3 months?"
  4. The Alignment (3 mins): Explain your P.R.A.N.A. Method‚Ñ¢ briefly. "My approach focuses on root-cause identification rather than symptom suppression."
  5. The Close (2 mins): Logistics. "Based on what you've said, I believe I can help. Would you like to hear how my onboarding process works?"
Coach Tip

Avoid "giving away the farm" on discovery calls. If you start suggesting herbs or diet changes now, the client may feel they have enough info and never book the full consultation. Maintain the boundary: diagnosis happens after the intake form is reviewed.

Designing High-Impact Intake Forms

Your intake form is the foundation of the Prakriti Profiling process. It should be digital, secure, and completed at least 48 hours before the first session.

The 90-Minute Consultation Flow

The first deep dive is where you establish your authority. Using the P.R.A.N.A. Method‚Ñ¢, you move from the "What" (symptoms) to the "Why" (imbalance).

The Deep Dive Structure

  • 0-15 min: Review & Validate. Confirm the intake data. "I saw on your form that your digestion is sluggish in the morning; let's talk more about that."
  • 15-45 min: The Physical & Energetic Assessment. This includes visual observation (skin, eyes, nails) and detailed questioning to separate Prakriti from Vikriti.
  • 45-70 min: The P.R.A.N.A. Mapping. Explain their bio-energetic blueprint. Use analogies to make Doshas relatable.
  • 70-90 min: The Initial Protocol. Provide 2-3 "Quick Wins" (e.g., warm water in the morning, tongue scraping) and outline the long-term journey.
Coach Tip

Don't overwhelm the client with a 10-page protocol in the first session. Focus on Agni Optimization first. If the fire isn't working, the best herbs in the world won't be absorbed.

Automating the Onboarding Process

To scale your practice to a $100k+ professional level, you must stop being your own secretary. Automation ensures that no lead falls through the cracks.

Recommended Tech Stack for Ayurveda Practitioners:

  • Practice Better or Healthie: All-in-one HIPAA-compliant platforms for charting, billing, and intake.
  • Calendly or Acuity: For seamless scheduling that syncs with your personal calendar.
  • Stripe/PayPal: For secure, professional payment processing.
Coach Tip

Set a "Payment Before Booking" policy. This eliminated "no-shows" for 94% of practitioners in a 2022 wellness industry survey. Your time is your most valuable inventory.

Contracts and Financial Agreements

Clarity is kindness. A professional contract prevents misunderstandings and protects your energy.

Essential Clauses for Your Agreement:

  • Non-Medical Disclaimer: State clearly that you are an Ayurveda Practitioner, not a licensed medical doctor (unless you are).
  • Cancellation Policy: Require 24-48 hours notice for rescheduling.
  • Refund Policy: Be clear on whether services or packages are refundable.
  • Communication Boundaries: Define where and when clients can reach you (e.g., "Email only, 48-hour response time").
Coach Tip

As a woman in business, you may feel the urge to be "flexible" with boundaries. Resist this. Professionalism breeds respect. When you honor your own policies, your clients will honor your expertise.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary purpose of the 15-minute discovery call?

The primary purpose is to qualify the lead‚Äîdetermining if they are a fit for your niche, if you can genuinely help them, and if they are ready to invest in the process. It is not for providing treatment.

2. When should the client complete their intake forms?

Intake forms should be completed at least 48 hours before the initial consultation. This allows the practitioner time to review the data, identify patterns of Vikriti, and prepare for the 90-minute deep dive.

3. Why is "Agni Assessment" a critical part of the intake flow?

In the P.R.A.N.A. Method‚Ñ¢, Agni (digestive fire) is the gatekeeper of health. If Agni is impaired, the body cannot process nutrition or herbs. Identifying the state of Agni first ensures the rest of the protocol will be effective.

4. Which automation tool is considered "all-in-one" for wellness practitioners?

Practice Better and Healthie are the industry standards for all-in-one, HIPAA-compliant practice management, covering scheduling, charting, and billing.
