The Professional Intake & P.R.A.N.A. Framework

Your intake process is the "first impression" of your clinical authority. In the P.R.A.N.A. Method‚Ñ¢, the intake is not merely a data-collection exercise; it is a therapeutic intervention. A well-structured intake demonstrates to the client that you see them as a whole person, not just a collection of symptoms.

A professional initial consultation should typically last 75 to 90 minutes. This allows sufficient time to move through the five pillars of our framework without rushing the client.

Financial Architecture & Pricing Models

Many practitioners struggle with pricing due to "money blocks" or the desire to help everyone. However, an underfunded practice cannot provide high-quality care. To achieve financial freedom and sustainability, you must move beyond the "trading hours for dollars" trap.

1. The Initial Consultation Model

This is your entry point. A standard rate for a Certified Ayurveda Practitioner‚Ñ¢ (CAP) in the United States ranges from $175 to $350 for a 90-minute initial assessment. This includes the time spent reviewing their intake forms before the meeting and the time spent creating their protocol after the meeting.

2. The Transformation Package (The Gold Standard)

Research shows that clients who commit to a 3-month program have a 70% higher success rate in maintaining lifestyle changes compared to those who book session-by-session. Packages provide you with predictable income and provide the client with Sankalpa (firm resolve).

Practitioner: Sarah, 52, former School Teacher.

Year 1: Charged $100 per session. Struggled with "no-shows" and inconsistent income ($2,200/mo avg).

Year 2: Shifted to the "90-Day Agni Reset" package for $1,800. This included 1 initial consult, 5 bi-weekly follow-ups, and email support.

Outcome: Sarah now works with 15 active package clients at a time, generating $9,000 per month while working only 20 hours a week. Her clients report significantly better results because they are committed to the full P.R.A.N.A. journey.

Marketing with Authority & Integrity

Marketing is simply the act of letting people who are suffering know that you have a solution. As a career changer, your "previous life" (nursing, teaching, corporate) is a strength, not a weakness. It provides you with a unique "Niche Authority."

When using your Certified Ayurveda Practitioner‚Ñ¢ designation:

  • Lead with Education: Use social media or email newsletters to explain the why behind Ayurvedic concepts. (e.g., "Why your 3 PM energy crash is a Vata sign.")
  • The Scope Disclaimer: Always include a footer on your website stating: "I am a Certified Ayurveda Practitioner providing wellness education and lifestyle consulting. I do not diagnose, treat, or cure medical diseases." This builds trust and protects you legally.
  • Testimonials: Collect "Outcome Stories" (with permission). Focus on the shift in the client's quality of life, vitality, and digestive ease.
Coach Tip: Your "Previous Life" Advantage

üí° If you were a nurse, market to healthcare professionals burnt out by the system. If you were a teacher, market to busy parents. Your "old" community already trusts you; they are your first and best clients.

Digital Integration: The Modern Practice

In the post-2020 landscape, Tele-Ayurveda is no longer optional; it is a primary growth driver. Digital tools allow you to serve clients across the country from your home office, providing the flexibility many 40+ practitioners crave.

Essential Tech Stack for the CAP‚Ñ¢:

  • EHR/Practice Management: Tools like Practice Better or SimplePractice allow for HIPAA-compliant intake forms, scheduling, and secure messaging.
  • Telehealth: Use a HIPAA-compliant version of Zoom or the built-in video tools in your EHR.
  • Digital Dispensary: Partner with platforms like Fullscript or specialized Ayurvedic suppliers so you can "prescribe" high-quality herbs without carrying physical inventory. You earn a commission (typically 10-35%) on these sales, creating passive revenue.
Coach Tip: Remote Pulse & Tongue Analysis

üí° You can perform effective tongue analysis via high-resolution photos sent through your secure portal. For pulse, teach your clients to "observe their own rhythm" while you guide them via video. It becomes an empowering educational moment.

Scalable Practice Models: 1-on-1 to 1-on-Many

There are only so many hours in a week. To scale your impact and income, you must eventually move toward group-based education. This allows you to help more people at a lower price point while increasing your hourly "effective rate."

The Hybrid Model:

  1. Level 1: A 21-day "Seasonal Cleanse" group program (automated, $149).
  2. Level 2: A 6-week "Ayurvedic Foundations" group coaching program ($499).
  3. Level 3: High-touch 1-on-1 P.R.A.N.A. Method‚Ñ¢ Consulting ($2,500+).

By offering these tiers, you ensure that Ayurveda is accessible to those with lower budgets while reserving your 1-on-1 energy for those ready for a deep transformation.
