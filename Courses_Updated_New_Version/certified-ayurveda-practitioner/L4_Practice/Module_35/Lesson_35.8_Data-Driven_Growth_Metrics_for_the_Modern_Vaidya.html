The Scaling Discovery Script

30-Minute Group Enrollment Structure

Phase 1: Establish Authority 5 min
YOU:
"Donna, I'm so glad you enjoyed the webinar. Today is about seeing if the Ageless Vitality program is the right vehicle to get you from where you are now‚Äîfeeling disconnected and exhausted‚Äîto that vibrant mental clarity we talked about. Does that sound good?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Desires 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned the brain fog is affecting your new consulting business. If we don't get this under control, how does that impact your goals for the next year?"
YOU:
"And why now? What makes this the moment you're ready to invest in a structured Ayurvedic approach rather than just trying another supplement?"
Phase 3: The Group Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Based on what you've shared, you're a perfect fit for the group. We cover the exact hormonal balancing protocols you need, but you also get the benefit of hearing from four other women in your exact position. In my experience, the 'collective healing' in the group often leads to faster results than 1:1 because you realize you're not alone."
Phase 4: The Investment 5 min
YOU:
"The investment for the 8-week experience, including all materials and our weekly group coaching, is $1,800. We can do that in a single payment or a three-month plan. Which works best for your cash flow?"
Coach Sarah's Insight

Notice I didn't say "Is that okay?" after the price. I asked "Which works best?" This assumes the sale and positions you as a professional, not a pleader. Practice saying your price in the mirror until your voice doesn't shake!

Handling Group-Specific Objections

Objection 1: "I'm worried I won't get enough personal attention."

Your Response: "I hear that concern often. That's actually why I limit the group to 10 women. You get a dedicated 'Hot Seat' session every other week where we dive deep into your specific Dosha imbalances. Plus, you have 24/7 access to our private community where I answer questions daily. It's actually more support than my 1:1 clients used to get!"

Objection 2: "Is this just a pre-recorded course?"

Your Response: "Not at all. While there are foundational videos to save you time, the heart of the program is the live coaching. We meet every Tuesday to adjust your protocols based on how your body is responding in real-time. This is a high-touch transformation, not a DIY course."

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary benefit of a group program for a high-achieving client like Donna?

The structured roadmap to results and the collective wisdom/normalization of her symptoms through community support.

2. When presenting a $1,500+ price point, what is the most important vocal technique?

Stating the price clearly and then remaining silent (the "Power Pause"). Avoid justifying the price before the client responds.

3. True or False: You should wait until you have 50 private clients before launching a group.

False. You only need a proven process that has worked for 10-20 clients to have the confidence and data to build a successful group curriculum.

4. How does a "Hot Seat" session address the objection of "not enough attention"?

It provides a dedicated, high-intensity focus on one individual's needs while others learn from the application, mimicking the depth of a 1:1 session.
Coach Sarah's Insight

Your "Scaling" phase is where your imposter syndrome will flare up the most. Remind yourself: You aren't selling "time." You are selling a life-changing transformation. Ayurveda is a gift; don't be afraid to charge what that transformation is worth.

Key Takeaways

  • Scaling requires shifting from selling "hours" to selling "outcomes."
  • Group programs allow for higher profit margins and greater client impact through community.
  • Objection handling for groups should focus on the added value of the group, not just defending the lack of 1:1.
  • A hybrid model is the fastest path to a sustainable, 6-figure Ayurvedic practice.
