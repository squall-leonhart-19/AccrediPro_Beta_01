Clinical Practice Labs

One of the biggest complaints about online certifications is that students graduate with "head knowledge" but have no idea what to do when a real human is sitting in front of them. We solve this with Practice Labs.

Starting in Module 5, every module includes a Clinical Simulation. You will be given a fictional client profile‚Äîcomplete with their tongue photos, pulse descriptions (simulated), and health history. You will practice:

  • Identifying their Prakriti (original constitution) and Vikriti (current imbalance).
  • Drafting a 30-day "Path to Balance" protocol.
  • Practicing the "Consultation Script" to explain complex concepts in simple terms.

Success Pacing & Support

We know you are likely balancing this certification with a job, a family, or other commitments. This program is self-paced, but we provide a "Success Track" to keep you moving.

  • The "Steady" Pace: 1 module per week (approx. 8-10 hours). Graduation in 9 months.
  • The "Accelerated" Pace: 2 modules per week (approx. 15-20 hours). Graduation in 5 months.

If you fall behind, don't panic. You have lifetime access to the materials. We also host monthly "Office Hours" where you can bring your questions directly to the faculty.

Coach Sarah's Tip

Schedule your "Ayurveda Time" just like a doctor's appointment. Even 30 minutes a day is better than a 5-hour marathon once a month. Consistency is how you beat imposter syndrome.
