Check Your Understanding

1. What is the typical income range for an established 1:1 Ayurveda private practice?

Based on market data for certified practitioners, an established 1:1 practice typically generates between $5,000 and $20,000 per month, depending on the practitioner's niche and package pricing.

2. True or False: You must wait until you are fully graduated to start building your brand and audience.

False. The "Immersion Phase" (Months 1-3) is the perfect time to share your journey and build "pre-launch" interest, so you have a warm audience ready when you finish.

3. Why is "Outcome-Based Pricing" superior to "Hourly Pricing"?

Outcome-based pricing focuses on the value of the transformation (e.g., healing gut issues) rather than trading time for money. This allows for higher profit margins and better client commitment.

4. What is a "Hybrid Model" of revenue?

A hybrid model combines different revenue streams, such as high-ticket 1:1 consulting with more scalable group programs or digital courses, providing both stability and growth potential.
