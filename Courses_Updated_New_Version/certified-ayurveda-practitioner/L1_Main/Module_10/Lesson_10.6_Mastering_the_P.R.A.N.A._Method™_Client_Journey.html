Strategic Intake Questions

Instead of asking "What are your symptoms?", use these P.R.A.N.A.-specific prompts:

  • For Prakriti: "In your 20s, before life got stressful, how would you describe your natural energy and digestion?"

  • For Ama: "Do you wake up feeling refreshed, or do you feel like you have a 'heavy coating' on your mind and body?"

Determining the 'Pace of Protocol'

One of the most common mistakes new practitioners make is moving too fast. If you introduce Nature-Synced Nutrition (Phase N) while the client still has significant Ama (Toxins), they will not absorb the nutrients. This is known as "feeding the fever."

When to Advance from Phase A to Phase N?

Use this clinical checklist before introducing complex nutritional changes:

  • Tongue Analysis: Is the white coating (Ama) reduced by at least 50%?
  • Elimination: Is the client having a daily, easy bowel movement upon waking?
  • Appetite: Does the client feel "true hunger" (Vishwa-Agni) rather than emotional cravings?
  • Energy: Has the mid-afternoon energy crash subsided?
Coach Tip: The Agni Anchor

If a client pushes to "get to the diet part" early, explain it this way: "If we put high-quality fuel into a dirty engine, the car still won't run well. We are cleaning the engine (Agni) first so your body can actually use the premium fuel (Nutrition) we're about to give it."
