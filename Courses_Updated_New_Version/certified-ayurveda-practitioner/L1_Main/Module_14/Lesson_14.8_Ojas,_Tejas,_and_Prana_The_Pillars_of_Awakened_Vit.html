Common Pitfalls to Avoid

In my early days, I made these mistakes often. Watch out for these "Imposter Syndrome" markers:

  • The "Info-Dump": Trying to explain all 20 sub-doshas in the first 10 minutes. Keep it simple and relevant.
  • Over-Apologizing: Saying "I'm sorry, this might sound weird" before mentioning Ayurveda. Own the wisdom!
  • The "Fix-It" Rush: Interrupting the client's story to give a remedy. Let them finish; being heard is 50% of the healing.
  • Discounting Early: Lowering your price before they even ask because you feel "new." Stick to your professional fee.
