The Three Tides: A Hierarchy of Motion

As a Master Practitioner, your hands must become multi-dimensional. We no longer listen to just one rhythm; we listen to the entire symphony. The Primary Respiratory Mechanism (PRM) expresses itself through three distinct "Tides."

Cranial Rhythmic Impulse (CRI) 8-12 cycles/min Tissue, bone, and fluid dynamics Active listening/Doing
