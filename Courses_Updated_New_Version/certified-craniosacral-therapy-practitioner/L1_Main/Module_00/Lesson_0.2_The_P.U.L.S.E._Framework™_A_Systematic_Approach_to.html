The Realistic Income Timeline

Success doesn't happen overnight, but it happens faster than you think when you have a professional credential. Here is the trajectory most of our 40+ career changers follow:

  • Months 1-3: The Immersion. You are learning the techniques and practicing on friends and family. Total focus is on skill-building and gathering testimonials.
  • Months 4-6: The Launch. You take your first paying clients. At $150/session, just 5 clients a week brings in $3,000/month part-time.
  • Months 7-12: The Build. Word of mouth spreads. You increase to 12-15 clients a week. You begin to offer packages. Monthly income: $7,000 - $10,000.
  • Year 2+: The Scale. You increase your rates to $200+, launch a group program, or hire an assistant. Monthly income: $12,000 - $25,000+.
