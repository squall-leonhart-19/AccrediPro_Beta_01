Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Mistake 1: Making Medical Claims. Never say "This herb will cure your varicose veins." Instead, say "This herb supports the structural integrity of the venous walls."
  • Mistake 2: Ignoring Medication. Always ask about blood thinners or blood pressure meds before suggesting Hawthorn or Garlic.
  • Mistake 3: Over-complicating. Don't lecture the client on the "thoracic duct." Use the "slow stream" or "drainage pipe" analogies.
  • Mistake 4: Forgetting Lifestyle. Herbs work best when Sandra also elevates her feet for 10 minutes a day. Don't forget the "behavioral" part of coaching!
