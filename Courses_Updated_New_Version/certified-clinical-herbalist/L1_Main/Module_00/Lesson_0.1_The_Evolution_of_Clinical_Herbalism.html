The Pathway to Mastery

Over the coming months, we will take you through a 4-stage transformation:

  1. The Foundations: Understanding the history, philosophy, and basic botany of herbalism.
  2. The Body Systems: Diving deep into anatomy, physiology, and how specific plants interact with each system (Digestive, Nervous, Immune, etc.).
  3. The Clinical Protocol: Learning how to conduct an intake, assess a client, and build a safe, effective herbal plan.
  4. The Professional Launch: Building your business, understanding legalities, and finding your first clients.
