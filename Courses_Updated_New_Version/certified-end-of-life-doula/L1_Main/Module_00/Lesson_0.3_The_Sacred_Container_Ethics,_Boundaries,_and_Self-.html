The 4-Tier Transformation

We believe that mastery is built, not born. To ensure you graduate as a world-class Doula, our curriculum is divided into four distinct phases of growth. Unlike other programs that overwhelm you with facts, we focus on scaffolded learning.

Tier Focus Outcome
L1: Foundation Modules 1-15: Biology of dying, communication, ethics. Fluency in the language of end-of-life care.
L2: Advanced Modules 16-23: Complex cases, trauma, active dying. Clinical depth and emotional resilience.
L3: Master Modules 24-29: Leadership, advocacy, supervision. The ability to lead families through crisis.
L4: Practice Modules 30-36: Business, marketing, client acquisition. A fully launched, legitimate professional practice.

Don't rush to Tier 4! Many students want to jump straight to "getting clients." Trust the process. The confidence you need to charge professional fees is built in the quiet study of Tiers 1 and 2. üí°

The S.O.L.A.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢

At the heart of this program is our proprietary methodology: The S.O.L.A.C.E. Method‚Ñ¢. This is the framework you will use with every single client. It ensures you never walk into a room wondering "what do I do now?"

  • S - Presence & Space-holding: The art of being, not doing.
  • O - Observation: Assessing physical, emotional, and spiritual needs.
  • L - Legacy: Helping the client find meaning and leave a mark.
  • A - Advocacy: Interfacing with medical teams and hospices.
  • C - Comfort: Non-medical interventions for peace.
  • E - Exit: Orchestrating the final moments with dignity.

Practice Labs & Real-World Skills

Knowledge is only potential power; action is real power. Every module includes a Practice Lab. These are simulated scenarios where you will apply what you've learned. You won't just read about active dying; you will walk through a Practice Lab that asks: "The patient's breathing has changed to Cheyne-Stokes. The family is panicking. What are your next three sentences?"

Pacing & Commitment

We know you have a busy life. Whether you are balancing a full-time job or caregiving for your own family, this program is designed for high-impact flexibility. A 2023 survey of our successful graduates (n=450) showed that the most successful students followed a "2-2-1" rhythm:

  • 2 Lessons per week: Deep study and note-taking.
  • 2 Practice Labs: Implementation of the weekly concepts.
  • 1 Reflection: Reviewing the Module Connection to see how it fits the "big picture."

At this pace, most students complete their certification in 4 to 6 months. However, you have lifetime access, so if life gets in the way, your seat is always here for you.
