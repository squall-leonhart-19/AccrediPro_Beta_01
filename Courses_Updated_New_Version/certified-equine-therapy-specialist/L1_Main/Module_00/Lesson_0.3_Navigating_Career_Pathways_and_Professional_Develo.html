Tier 1: Foundation ‚Äì The Core Methodology (Modules 1-15)

This initial tier is where we build your bedrock of knowledge. You'll dive deep into the fundamental principles of equine-assisted therapy, horse behavior, safety protocols, and the psychological underpinnings of human-equine connection. We cover:

  • Ethical Guidelines & Professionalism: Establishing a strong, responsible practice.
  • Equine Behavior & Communication: Understanding horses as co-facilitators.
  • Therapeutic Modalities: Introduction to various approaches in equine therapy.
  • Client Assessment Basics: Learning to understand client needs and goals.

By the end of this tier, you'll have a solid theoretical understanding and be ready to begin supervised practical application.
