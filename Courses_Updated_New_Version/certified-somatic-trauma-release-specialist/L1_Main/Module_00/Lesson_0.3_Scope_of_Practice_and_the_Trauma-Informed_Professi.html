Time Commitment & Pacing

We've designed this for the busy woman. Whether you are working full-time, raising a family, or both, this program is flexible. However, consistency is the key to neuro-integration (learning).

The "Goldilocks" Pace: 3-5 lessons per week. This allows you to finish the certification in 6 months while still having time to digest the material. At this pace, you should expect to spend about 5-7 hours per week on the program.
