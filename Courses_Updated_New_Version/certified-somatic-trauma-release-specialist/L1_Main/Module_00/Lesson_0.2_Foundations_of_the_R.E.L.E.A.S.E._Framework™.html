    Check Your Understanding

    1. Why is somatic trauma release considered a "high-ticket" or premium service compared to general wellness coaching?

    Somatic work addresses the physiological root of trauma (the nervous system) rather than just surface symptoms. Because it provides deep, often permanent transformation for chronic issues, it is valued higher than general coaching.

    2. According to the "Success Math," how many private clients per week at $210/session are needed to reach a $10,000 monthly income?

    Exactly 12 clients per week. This allows for a part-time schedule while maintaining a high professional income.

    3. What is the primary focus of the "Months 1-3" phase in the income timeline?

    The focus is on learning, certification, and working with "beta" or practice clients to build confidence and gather testimonials, rather than immediate high-volume revenue.

    4. True or False: Somatic Specialists are limited to 1-on-1 private practice models.

    False. Specialists can thrive in group programs, corporate wellness, online courses, speaking, and integrative clinic environments.
