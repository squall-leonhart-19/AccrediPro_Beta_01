Realistic Timeline to Income

Success doesn't happen overnight, but it can happen faster than you think if you follow a proven framework. A 2023 industry survey of health and wellness specialists found that those with niche certifications reached profitability 40% faster than generalists.

  • Months 1-3: The Foundation. While completing your certification, you'll work with "beta" clients (often for free or low cost) to build your confidence and gather those all-important testimonials.
  • Months 4-6: The Launch. You start taking your first paying clients. At $150/hour, just 5 hours of client work a week brings in $3,000/month.
  • Months 7-12: The Momentum. You refine your "Signature Offer." You might move to packages (e.g., $1,500 for a 3-month support program). With 10 active clients, you are now at $5,000 - $8,000/month.
  • Year 2 and Beyond: The Scale. This is where you introduce group programs or digital products. This is how you break the "time-for-money" trap and move toward $10,000 - $25,000+ per month.
Coach Tip

Don't wait until you feel "perfect" to start. The most successful specialists are those who start sharing what they learn in Module 1 with their community immediately. Visibility creates opportunity.

The Math That Makes Sense

Let's break down the "Math of $100k." To earn $100,000 a year, you need to generate roughly $8,333 per month. Here are three ways to get there:

  1. The High-Touch Model: 14 clients paying $600/month for bi-weekly support. (Total: $8,400)
  2. The Leveraged Model: 20 people in a group program paying $300/month + 4 private VIP clients at $600/month. (Total: $8,400)
  3. The Hybrid Model: 8 private clients ($4,800) + 1 digital course selling 15 copies/month at $250 ($3,750). (Total: $8,550)
Coach Tip

Most career changers over 40 prefer the Hybrid Model. It offers the deep fulfillment of 1-on-1 work with the "breathing room" provided by digital products or group programs.
