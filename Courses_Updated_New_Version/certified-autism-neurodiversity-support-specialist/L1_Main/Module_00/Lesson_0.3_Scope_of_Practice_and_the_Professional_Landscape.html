The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is meticulously structured to move you from interested observer to expert practitioner. Unlike traditional academic courses that focus solely on theory, our 4-tier model ensures you possess both the knowledge and the clinical confidence to lead.

Tier Focus Modules Outcome
L1: Foundation Core Methodology & Fundamentals 1 - 15 Master the science of neurodiversity and the basics of support.
L2: Advanced Complex Cases & Clinical Depth 16 - 23 Learn to handle dual diagnoses, sensory processing, and co-occurring conditions.
L3: Master Supervision & Leadership 24 - 29 Develop the skills to train others and lead support teams.
L4: Practice Business & Client Acquisition 30 - 36 Launch your practice, set your rates, and get your first 5 clients.
Coach Tip

Don't rush to Tier 4! Many students want to jump straight to business building. Trust the process. Your legitimacy (and your ability to charge premium rates) comes from the deep work you do in Tiers 1 and 2.

Becoming a Practitioner by Module 5

One of the biggest hurdles for career changers is Imposter Syndrome. You might think, "Who am I to advise families?" We solve this through our Practice Labs.

Starting in Module 2, you won't just be watching videos. You will be engaging in simulated scenarios. By Module 5, you will have completed enough practical labs that you will begin to feel like a practitioner. We don't wait until the end of the course to teach you how to coach; we build the "coaching muscle" from day one.

Pacing & Time Commitment

We designed this for busy women. Whether you are working full-time, raising a family, or both, this journey is flexible. A 2023 survey of our successful graduates (n=450) showed that the most effective pacing was 3-5 hours per week.

  • The "Express" Path: 10-12 hours/week (Completion in 3-4 months)
  • The "Steady" Path: 4-5 hours/week (Completion in 6-8 months)
  • The "Life Happens" Path: 2 hours/week (Completion in 12 months)

Remember: This is a professional certification, not a weekend workshop. Respect the material, and it will reward you with a lifelong career.

Coach Tip

Block out your "Certification Power Hour" in your calendar now. Treating this like a scheduled appointment with your future self is the #1 predictor of graduation success.
