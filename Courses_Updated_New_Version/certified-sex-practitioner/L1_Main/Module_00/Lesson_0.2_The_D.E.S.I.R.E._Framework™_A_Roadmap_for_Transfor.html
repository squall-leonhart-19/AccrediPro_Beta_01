Realistic Income Timeline

Success doesn't happen overnight, but it happens faster than you think when you follow a proven framework. Here is what a typical transition looks like for our students:

  • Months 1-3: The Foundation. You are learning the science and the methodology. You might take on 2 "practice clients" at a reduced rate to build confidence and gather testimonials.
    Income: $500 - $1,000/mo
  • Months 4-6: The Launch. You receive your certification. You set your professional rates ($150-$250/hr). You start your first 1-on-1 packages.
    Income: $2,500 - $5,000/mo
  • Months 7-12: The Momentum. You have a steady stream of referrals. You might launch your first small group program. Your confidence is high, and your imposter syndrome is fading.
    Income: $6,000 - $10,000/mo
  • Year 2+: The Scale. You increase your rates. You add passive income streams (courses). You are recognized as a local or niche authority.
    Income: $12,000 - $25,000+/mo
Coach Luna's Tip on Timing

Don't quit your day job on Day 1! I recommend building your "Side Hustle" until your Sex Practitioner income covers 60% of your current bills. This removes the "desperation" energy from your client sessions.

The Achievable Math

To make this feel real, let's look at the numbers. Most practitioners fail because they don't have a "revenue map." Let‚Äôs look at how simple it is to hit $100,000 per year.

To earn ~$8,300 per month (which is $100k/year), you could choose one of these paths:

  • The 1-on-1 Specialist: 10 clients paying $830/month (typically 3 sessions/month).
  • The Group Leader: 17 people in a monthly group coaching program at $500/month.
  • The Hybrid: 5 1-on-1 clients ($4k) + 1 small group of 10 people ($4k) + 1 digital course sale per week ($300).
Coach Luna's Tip on Pricing

Never charge by the hour. Charge by the package. A 3-month "Intimacy Restoration Package" for $2,500 is much easier to sell and manage than 12 individual $200 sessions.
