Sonic Alchemy: Heavy to Light

The "T" phase requires a specific arc of instrumentation. We use Cathartic Instruments to surface the energy, followed by Harmonizing Instruments to transmute it.

  • Phase T1 (Surfacing): Use Gongs (especially with friction mallets) or deep, dissonant Himalayan bowls. The goal is to create a slight "friction" that encourages the body to release its grip.
  • Phase T2 (Transmuting): As you sense the release peak, transition to Perfect Fifths or Major Chords. Use Crystal Singing Bowls in F (Heart) or B (Crown) to lift the energy.
  • Phase T3 (Integration): High-frequency Koshi chimes or Tingshas to "sweep" the auric field and signal to the nervous system that the release is complete.
Coach Tip: The 70/30 Rule

During the peak of the release, keep the sound 70% grounding (low frequencies) and 30% elevating (high frequencies). Too much high-frequency sound during trauma release can cause "unrooting," making the client feel dizzy or fragmented.
