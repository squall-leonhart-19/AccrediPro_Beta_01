Vocal Toning: The 'LAM' Mantra

Sound is most effective when it is generated within the body. The bija (seed) mantra for the Muladhara is 'LAM' (pronounced LUM). This sound creates a specific vibration in the pelvic floor and the base of the spine.

The Mechanics of the LAM Tone:

  • The 'L' Sound: Places the tongue at the roof of the mouth, connecting the upper and lower energy circuits.
