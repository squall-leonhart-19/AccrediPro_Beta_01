The 'Sonic Sweep' Technique

The Sonic Sweep is a systematic movement used to clear the biofield. As a facilitator, your movement is just as important as the sound you produce. Follow this professional protocol:

  1. Positioning: Stand at the client's head. Begin with a high-frequency instrument (like a crystal pyramid).
  2. The Crown Activation: Strike the instrument 12 inches above the crown chakra. Allow the sound to bloom.
  3. The Lateral Sweep: Move the instrument in a slow, zig-zag pattern down the left side of the body, then the right side, maintaining a distance of 8-12 inches from the physical form.
  4. The Clearing Motion: Use a "flicking" motion with your wrist at the joints (shoulders, elbows, knees, ankles) where energy tends to pool.
  5. The Root Exit: Finish the sweep at the feet, using a lower-toned chime or bowl to "ground" the cleared energy into the earth.
Professional Presence

Maintain a "soft gaze" while performing the sweep. Don't stare at the client; instead, look at the space around them. You may notice subtle changes in the air density or the way the sound "bounces" off certain areas. This is your intuitive resonance developing!
