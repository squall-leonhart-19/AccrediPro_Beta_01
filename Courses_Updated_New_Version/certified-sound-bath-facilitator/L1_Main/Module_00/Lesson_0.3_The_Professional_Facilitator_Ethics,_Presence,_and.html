The 4-Tier Transformation

We believe that mastery isn't a single event; it's a progression. To move from "student" to "professional," you will travel through four distinct phases of growth. Each phase builds upon the last, ensuring you never feel overwhelmed but always feel challenged.

Tier Level Focus Area Outcome
L1: FOUNDATION (Modules 1-15) Core Methodology & Sound Science Understanding how sound heals and mastering your primary instruments.
L2: ADVANCED (Modules 16-23) Complex Cases & Clinical Depth Facilitating for trauma, chronic pain, and specific nervous system states.
L3: MASTER (Modules 24-29) Supervision & Space Holding Refining your "presence" and learning to lead large groups and retreats.
L4: PRACTICE (Modules 30-36) Business & Client Acquisition Building your brand, legalities, and getting your first 10 paying clients.
Coach Sarah's Tip

Don't rush to Tier 4! I know you're eager to start the business, but your confidence in Tier 4 is 100% dependent on the skills you build in Tier 1. Trust the process‚Äîthe "business" part becomes easy when you know your craft inside and out.

The R.E.S.O.N.A.T.E. Method‚Ñ¢

To ensure consistency and safety, you will be trained in our proprietary R.E.S.O.N.A.T.E. Method‚Ñ¢. This is the backbone of everything you'll learn. It is a 7-step framework that takes the guesswork out of facilitation.

  • Receive: Deep listening to the client's current state.
  • Evaluate: Assessing the energetic and physiological needs.
  • Select: Choosing the specific frequencies and instruments.
  • Orchestrate: The actual delivery of the sound bath.
  • Navigate: Managing the emotional "shifts" during the session.
  • Anchor: Grounding the client back into their body.
  • Transform: The post-session integration and coaching.
Coach Sarah's Tip

By Module 5, you'll have the "Receive" and "Evaluate" steps down so well that you'll already feel more professional than 90% of the practitioners out there. You'll stop "playing instruments" and start "facilitating transformation."

Time Commitment & Pacing

We know you are likely balancing this certification with a job, a family, or other commitments. This program is 100% self-paced, but we recommend the "Sustainable Momentum" track:

  • Recommended Pace: 2-3 lessons per week.
  • Weekly Commitment: 3-5 hours (including practice time).
  • Total Duration: Most students finish in 4-6 months.

If "life happens" and you need to step away for two weeks, your progress is saved. However, we provide Community Support through our weekly "Office Hours" to help you stay on track if you feel yourself falling behind.

Coach Sarah's Tip

Set a "non-negotiable" 1-hour block on your calendar twice a week. Treat it like an appointment with your future self. Consistency beats intensity every single time.
