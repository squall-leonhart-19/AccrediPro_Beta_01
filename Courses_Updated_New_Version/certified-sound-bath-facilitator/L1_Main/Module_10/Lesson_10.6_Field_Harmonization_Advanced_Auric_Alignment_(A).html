Identifying and Sealing Energetic 'Leaks'

An energetic "leak" is a localized area in the biofield where energy is dissipating rather than circulating. This often manifests to the practitioner as a sudden drop in temperature or a deadening of the instrument's tone when passing over a specific area.

The Sealing Technique

  1. Identify: Move your hand or a chime slowly over the client's body (about 12 inches away). Note where the sound seems to "fall flat" or "get sucked in."
  2. Saturate: Hold the instrument over this specific spot. Strike it repeatedly but gently, creating a constant "wall of sound."
  3. Spiral: Use a clockwise spiraling motion with the instrument to "knit" the energetic fibers of the field back together.
  4. Test: Sweep past the area again. If the tone remains bright and clear, the leak is sealed.
Coach Tip

Energetic leaks are common around the solar plexus in women who are "people pleasers" or around the heart in those recovering from grief. Be gentle; these areas can be emotionally tender as they begin to hold resonance again.

The Practitioner's Posture: Radiant Neutrality

You cannot align another's field if your own is chaotic. In the "A" phase, your physical and energetic posture is paramount. We teach a state called Radiant Neutrality.

Radiant Neutrality is the ability to hold a high-frequency, stable presence without "taking on" the client's released energy. It requires:

  • The Conduit Stance: Feet shoulder-width apart, knees slightly bent, spine elongated. Imagine energy flowing through you, not from you.
  • Soft Gaze: Keep your eyes slightly out of focus. This allows you to "see" the biofield with your peripheral vision and intuitive senses.
  • Breath Synchronization: Breathe in a 4-7-8 rhythm to keep your own heart rate variability (HRV) in a state of coherence.
