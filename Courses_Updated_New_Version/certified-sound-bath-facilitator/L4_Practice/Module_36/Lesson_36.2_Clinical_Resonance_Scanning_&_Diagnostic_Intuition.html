The Ethics of 'Reading' a Client

As your diagnostic intuition sharpens, you will start to "know" things about clients before they tell you. This power requires strict ethical boundaries. Level 4 certification requires mastery of "Energetic Consent."

Key Ethical Protocols:

  1. Obtain Explicit Consent: Always ask, "May I perform a resonance scan of your biofield today?"
  2. Scope of Practice: Never provide a medical diagnosis (e.g., "I feel cancer in your liver"). Instead, say "I am detecting significant energetic stagnation in this area."
  3. The 'Right to Privacy': Just because you can sense an emotional blockage doesn't mean the client is ready to talk about it. Use the sound to facilitate the release without forcing the conversation.
Coach Tip

If you sense something heavy, always ask: "I'm noticing some density in the heart area. Would you like to explore what's coming up for you, or shall we simply let the sound do the work?" This empowers the client and maintains professional distance.
