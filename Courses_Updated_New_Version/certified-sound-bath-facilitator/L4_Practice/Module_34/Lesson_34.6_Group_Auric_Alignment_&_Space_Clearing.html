Post-Session Venue Reset Protocols

Professionalism extends beyond the time the clients are in the room. If you are renting a yoga studio or a community center, leaving your "energetic footprint" behind is poor practice. Furthermore, you don't want to carry the group's "sloughed-off" energy home with you.

The Professional Reset Checklist

  • Physical-Acoustic Reset: Strike a large gong or deep bowl three times with the intent of "clearing the slate" for the next group.
  • Scent Clearing: If the venue allows, use a light mist of Palo Santo or Sage hydrosol (water-based) to refresh the air molecules.
  • Instrument Maintenance: Wipe down your bowls with a microfiber cloth. This physical act also serves as an energetic "wipe" of the instruments.
  • Facilitator Hand-Wash: A simple but vital clinical protocol. Washing your hands with cold water and sea salt after a session "breaks" the energetic link between you and the participants.
