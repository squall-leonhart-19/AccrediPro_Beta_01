Case Study: The 1920s Famine Imprint

Case Study: Sarah, 48 (High-Earning Nurse)

Presenting Problem: Sarah earned $140,000 annually but carried $60,000 in chronic credit card debt. Despite "knowing better," she felt an uncontrollable urge to spend every cent she earned, often on items she didn't need.

Soul Mapping (S) Discovery: Upon entering the Records, we identified a Lineage Loop originating in the 1920s. Sarah's great-grandmother lived through a severe famine and economic collapse. The "Soul Contract" Sarah inherited was: "Money is useless if it's not converted into something tangible immediately, because the value will disappear tomorrow."

Intervention: Using Healing Transmutation (H), we updated the contract from "Survival via Spending" to "Safety via Stewardship." We acknowledged the great-grandmother's trauma and released Sarah from the obligation to "spend before it's gone."

Outcome: Within 6 months, Sarah paid off $22,000 of debt. The "urge" to spend vanished because the energetic alarm bell (the famine imprint) was finally silenced.
