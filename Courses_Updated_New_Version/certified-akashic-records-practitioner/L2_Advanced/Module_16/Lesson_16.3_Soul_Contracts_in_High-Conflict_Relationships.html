No. It is a violation of sovereignty. Instead, you access the "Relational Field" or the shared contract between the two parties from the client's perspective.

Because the client needs 3D tools (like boundaries and somatic triggers) to prevent falling back into old behavioral habits even after the energetic contract is cleared.
