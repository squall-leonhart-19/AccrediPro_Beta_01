Practitioner Self-Assessment

You cannot audit a client if your own "instrument" is out of tune. Professional calibration involves the Bio-Energetic Tuning Fork method. Before every session, you must assess your own three centers:

  • The Intellectual Center: Is your mind quiet, or are you worrying about your own bills/schedule?
  • The Emotional Center: Are you in a state of "Neutral Compassion"?
  • The Physical Center: Is your body hydrated and grounded?
Coach Tip

If your own "Egoic Filter" is active, you will project your own life experiences onto the client's Records. Use the "Golden Shield" visualization learned in Module 1 to reset before the client arrives.

Subconscious Resistance Tools

Many clients consciously want answers but subconsciously fear the change those answers require. This creates a "frequency wall." To measure this, we use the Somatic Pendulum (using your own body as a dowsing tool) or Muscle Testing (Kinesiology).

Data indicates that 72% of clients experiencing "blockages" in the Records are actually manifesting a subconscious defense mechanism. Identifying this during the audit allows you to address the fear directly, rather than blaming the "Records" for being closed.

The 5-Step Audit Protocol

Integrate this checklist into your pre-session routine to ensure professional-grade results:

  1. Observation: Scan the client's auric field for "bright spots" (alignment) and "dark spots" (leakage).
  2. Somatic Inquiry: Ask the client, "Where in your body do you feel the most tension right now?"
  3. Frequency Check: Mentally assign a 1-10 scale to the client's current vibration (1=Heavy/Despair, 10=Aligned/Joy).
  4. Attachment Scan: Look for "hooks" or "cords" in the 7 primary chakras.
  5. Calibration Command: Use a silent invocation to bring both fields into a state of "Ready Receptivity."
Coach Tip

When you find resistance, don't judge it. Say to the client: "I'm sensing a protective layer around your heart today. Is it okay if we acknowledge that protection before we move deeper?" This builds immense trust.
