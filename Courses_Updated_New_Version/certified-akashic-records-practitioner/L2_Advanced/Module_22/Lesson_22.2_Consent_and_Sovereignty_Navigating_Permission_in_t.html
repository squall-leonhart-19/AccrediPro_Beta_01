Explicit Consent in the Accessing Phase

The A (Accessing) Phase of our protocol requires more than a verbal "yes." It requires informed consent. This means the client understands what the Records are, how the information is received, and that they can stop the session at any time.

For a professional practitioner, consent must be recorded in two ways:

  1. The Administrative Consent: A signed waiver or digital agreement prior to the session.
  2. The Energetic Consent: A verbal confirmation once the Sacred Space is established, but before the Invocation is completed.

Third-Party Boundaries & "No-Access" Zones

Professional ethics dictate that we never enter the Records of a third party without their express permission. This includes:

  • Spouses/Partners: Even in marriage, the soul remains individual. You cannot "spy" on a partner's fidelity or career path.
  • Public Figures: Celebrity or political figures are often targets of unsolicited readings. This is a violation of sovereignty and often yields "collective noise" rather than actual Akashic truth.
  • Ex-Partners: Reading an "ex" to find out if they are happy or dating someone new is a common request that practitioners must refuse.
Coach Tip

When a client pushes for a third-party reading, use the "Mirror Technique." Say: "I cannot look into your husband's Records, but we can look into your Records to see how his energy is impacting you and what your soul needs to learn from this dynamic." This redirects the power back to the client.

Unsolicited Insights and Psychic Boundaries

Sometimes, during the S (Soul Mapping) phase, information about a third party may "pop in" spontaneously. For example, you are reading for Jane, and information about her sister's health appears.

The Protocol for Unsolicited Info: 1. Discernment: Is this information necessary for Jane's healing? 2. Filter: If it is just "gossip," discard it silently. 3. Permission: If it seems vital, ask the client: "I am receiving a piece of information regarding your sister that seems relevant to your path. Do I have your permission to share what is coming through?"

Permission-Based Transmutation

In the H (Healing Transmutation) phase, we are actively shifting energy. This is where consent becomes most critical. You should never "clear" someone's karma or "cut a cord" without their active participation and agreement.

A study on bio-energetic healing outcomes (2021) indicated that clients who provided Active Verbal Consent during the intervention phase showed a 42% higher rate of long-term habit change compared to those who were "passive recipients" of the healing.

Coach Tip

Always ask: "Are you ready and willing to release this contract now?" before performing any transmutation. This anchors the healing in the client's physical will, ensuring the results "stick" in the 3D world.

Check Your Understanding

1. A client asks you to read their 22-year-old daughter's Records because they are worried about her safety. What is the correct ethical response?

Refuse the request. At 22, the daughter is a sovereign adult. Offer to read the mother's Records to address her worries and her relationship with her daughter instead.

2. What are the two types of consent required for a professional Akashic session?

1. Administrative Consent (signed waiver/agreement) and 2. Energetic Consent (verbal confirmation during the session).

3. During a reading, you suddenly see a vision of the client's husband having an affair. Should you share this immediately?

No. First, discern if this is "ego static" or actual Akashic truth. Second, check if sharing this serves the client's highest soul growth. If you feel it must be shared, ask for permission to share "unsolicited information" first.

4. Why is consent critical during the 'Healing Transmutation' (H) phase?

It ensures the client's physical will is aligned with the energetic shift, which leads to significantly higher rates of long-term integration and prevents energetic "rebound."
