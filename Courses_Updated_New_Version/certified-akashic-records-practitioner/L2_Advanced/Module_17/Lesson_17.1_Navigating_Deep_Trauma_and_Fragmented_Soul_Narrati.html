Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary difference between a standard Akashic session and a trauma-informed session?

Trauma-informed sessions focus on "titration" (processing in small amounts), establishing safe space anchors first, and prioritizing energetic integration over rapid narrative clearing to prevent re-traumatization.

2. What should you do if a client begins to experience a physical abreaction (e.g., shaking or hyperventilating)?

Immediately pause the inquiry, use your "Voice of the Records" to provide grounding, and guide the client to their pre-established Safe Space Anchor or use somatic grounding techniques (like feeling their feet on the floor).

3. Which signature in Soul Mapping indicates that energy is "stuck" at a specific age?

Temporal Displacement. This manifests as the client's energy or reactions being disproportionately aligned with a younger version of themselves rather than their current adult self.

4. Why is "Quantum Distance" used in the Healing Transmutation phase for trauma?

It allows the client to observe the traumatic narrative from a safe perspective (witnessing) rather than re-living it, which prevents the nervous system from becoming overwhelmed.
