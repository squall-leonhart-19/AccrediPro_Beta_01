The Ethics of Energetic Hygiene and Referrals

As a Certified Akashic Records Practitioner‚Ñ¢, you must know your scope of practice. While most energetic interference is minor and easily resolved through the Records, some cases involve deep-seated "dark" attachments or psychological disorders that mimic interference.

Red Flags for Referral:
