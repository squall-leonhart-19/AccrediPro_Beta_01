The 'Single-Party Resolution' Protocol

One of the most common questions from students is: "Can I heal the relationship if the other person isn't here?" The answer is a resounding YES. Because all souls are connected in the Field, shifting the vibration of one party fundamentally alters the "frequency" of the relationship.

The Mirror Principle: In the Akasha, relationships are often mirrors. When Client A resolves their "Victim" contract, they no longer provide the "hook" for Client B to be a "Perpetrator." Client B is then forced to either shift their behavior to match the new dynamic or exit the relationship because the energetic "match" is gone.

Practitioner Note

Always remind your client: We are not changing the other person's free will. We are changing your soul's reaction to them. This is the ultimate form of empowerment.

Anchoring Self-Sovereignty and Boundaries

The final phase of the protocol, Phase A (Anchoring), is critical. After a past-life trauma is resolved in the Records, the client must take Sovereign Action in the 3D world. Without this, the neural pathways of the old conflict will remain active.

Practitioners should help clients design a Soul-Led Action Plan (SAP) that includes:

  • Communication Boundaries: Setting specific times or methods for interaction.
  • Energetic Hygiene: Daily "Alignment" practices to keep their field clear.
  • Physical Sanctuary: Creating a space where the other person's energy is not allowed.

Karmic Loops are characterized by a "compulsive" or "magnetic" intensity that is disproportionate to the current situation, often involving repetitive patterns that feel impossible to break through logic alone.

If the underlying Soul Contract or "reason for the cord" isn't resolved, the energetic attachment will typically grow back because the soul still believes it "needs" the connection for a lesson or debt.

The absent partner is affected through the Mirror Principle. When one person changes their energetic frequency and resolves their contract, the "energetic match" for the conflict is removed, forcing the other person to either shift or exit the dynamic.

The Solar Plexus (associated with power and control) and the Sacral Chakra (associated with emotional and sexual attachment).
