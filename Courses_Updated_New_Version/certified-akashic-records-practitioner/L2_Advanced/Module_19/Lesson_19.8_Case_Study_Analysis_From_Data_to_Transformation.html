Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "triangulation" necessary in a complex clinical case like Elena's?

Triangulation ensures we aren't just looking at one data point. By combining Bio-History, Energetic Signature, and Ancestral Epigenetic Load, we avoid "spiritual bypassing" and address the root cause of symptoms that have both physical and energetic origins.

2. What is the "Ancestral Anchor" and why is it prevalent in women aged 40-55?

The Ancestral Anchor is a transgenerational trauma pattern where the client carries the unfulfilled desires or fears of previous generations. Women in this age group are often the first in their lineage to seek self-actualization, creating a conflict between their soul's purpose and their inherited "survival programming."

3. Which Phase 1 goal is most critical for a client with migraines and palpitations?

Nervous System Stabilization. We cannot do deep ancestral work if the client's body is in a state of high-alert (sympathetic dominance). Stabilization lowers cortisol and creates the "energetic safety" required for deep clearing.

4. If Elena's migraines increase in frequency during Phase 2, what is your first action?

First, refer her back to her MD to rule out a physiological shift. Second, pause the deep clearing work and return to Phase 1 (Stabilization) as the "healing crisis" may be overwhelming her current nervous system capacity.
