The Holographic Universe: The Part in the Whole

The Holographic Universe Theory, pioneered by physicist David Bohm, suggests that the universe operates like a hologram. In a physical photograph, if you cut it in half, you lose half the image. In a hologram, if you cut it in half, each piece still contains the entire image, just at a lower resolution.

This explains why, when you access a single "Soul Map," you can see the threads of the entire collective. Your client's individual struggle with "unworthiness" is a holographic fractal of the collective human struggle. When you heal a pattern in one person's Records, you are sending a "healing ripple" through the entire holographic field.
