Multi-Dimensional Soul Mapping

When we enter Level 2 work, we are often looking at Horizontal Timelines (past/future lives) and Vertical Dimensions (higher self/soul origin) simultaneously. To synthesize this, we use the Triangulation Method.

The Triangulation Method

Instead of looking at a past life in isolation, we look for the "Red Thread" that connects three points of data:

  1. The Historical Context: What happened in the past life? (e.g., A vow of silence in 14th-century Italy).
  2. The Current Manifestation: How is it showing up now? (e.g., A fear of public speaking or "throat chakra" blocks).
  3. The Soul Lesson: What is the overarching evolution? (e.g., Reclaiming the power of the Divine Voice).
