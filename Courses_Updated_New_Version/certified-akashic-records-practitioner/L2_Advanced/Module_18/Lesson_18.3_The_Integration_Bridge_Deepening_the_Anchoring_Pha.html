Overcoming Integration Resistance

The ego-mind often views high-frequency change as a threat. We call this Integration Resistance. Identifying this early allows you to coach your client through it rather than letting them fall back into old patterns.

  • The "Spiritual Bypass" Resistance: The client wants to stay in the Records and avoids the physical steps. (Solution: Emphasize that the Earth is a school of action.)
  • The "Somatic Flare" Resistance: The client experiences headaches or fatigue as the body purges old energy. (Solution: Explain the 72-hour settling period.)
  • The "Doubt Loop": 24 hours later, the client‚Äôs ego says, "That wasn't real; I made it up." (Solution: Use the SAP to provide physical proof of change.)
