Identifying the 'Council': Masters, Teachers, and Loved Ones

Upon accessing the Records, you aren't just reading a book; you are interacting with an intelligence. This intelligence manifests as the Masters, Teachers, and Loved Ones (MTLOs). Understanding their "energetic flavor" helps you discern the source of the information you receive.

1. The Masters

The Masters are the Keepers of the Records. They are non-physical beings who have never walked the earth in human form. Their energy is vast, neutral, and incredibly powerful. They provide the "big picture" soul contracts and karmic blueprints.

2. The Teachers

Teachers are specialized guides. They may have had human incarnations and often appear when a client needs specific knowledge (e.g., healing techniques, business strategy, or relationship dynamics). They are the "subject matter experts" of the Akasha.

3. The Loved Ones

These are the ancestors or individuals who have passed on but remain in the client's soul lineage. They provide emotional validation and healing for ancestral trauma. Note: They are not "ghosts"; they are the soul-essence of that person as it exists in the light.

Coach Tip

In a professional session, you don't always need to name which MTLO is speaking. Simply say, "The Records are showing me..." or "The Council is sharing..." This maintains your authority as the practitioner while honoring the source.
