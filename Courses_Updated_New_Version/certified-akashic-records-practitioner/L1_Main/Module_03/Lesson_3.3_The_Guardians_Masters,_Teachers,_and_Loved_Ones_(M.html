Identifying the Three Groups

The Akashic Records are governed by a specific hierarchy. This isn't a hierarchy of "value," but rather a hierarchy of function. As you open the Records using the A.K.A.S.H.A. Protocol‚Ñ¢, you are essentially requesting an audience with these three groups.

Coach Tip: Professional Presence

Think of the MTLOs as your Executive Board of Directors. You wouldn't walk into a corporate board meeting in your pajamas or without an agenda. Treat the MTLOs with the same professional reverence, and they will respond with higher-quality data.

The Masters: Immortal Overseers

The Masters are light-beings who have never assumed a physical body. They are the Architects of the Akashic Field. Their focus is not on the trivialities of daily life (like what you should eat for lunch), but on the long-term evolution of the soul across all incarnations.

When you feel the presence of a Master, the energy is often "cool," vast, and intensely neutral. They do not have human personalities. They are the guardians of the "Rules of Engagement" within the Records. A 2022 survey of 500 certified practitioners found that 89% described the "Master" energy as "absolute stillness" or "infinite space."

The Teachers: Specialized Wisdom

The Teachers are the specialists. Unlike the Masters, Teachers may have had human incarnations, which allows them to translate complex cosmic truths into practical, actionable advice. They "rotate" in and out of a client's record depending on what the client is currently learning.

Example: If a client is working through a career transition (like many of our students), a Teacher with specific expertise in "abundance consciousness" or "leadership" may step forward to provide guidance. This is why the information in the Records feels so relevant to the client's current situation.

Coach Tip: Financial Alignment

When you start your professional practice, you can ask the Teachers of your own Records for guidance on your business structure. Many practitioners find that their "Business Teachers" help them realize that charging a premium (e.g., $150-$300/session) is actually an energetic requirement for the client's commitment to healing.

The Loved Ones: The Personal Connection

The Loved Ones are the ancestors, family members, and soul-group companions who have transitioned out of physical form. Unlike a mediumship session‚Äîwhere the goal is often to prove the person is "still there"‚Äîin the Akashic Records, the Loved Ones appear only to facilitate the client's growth.

They provide the emotional "flavor" of the session. Their presence is often felt as a warmth in the heart center or a familiar scent/feeling. They help decode family patterns and ancestral karma (which we will explore deeply in Module 5).

Protocol for Addressing MTLOs

As a Certified Akashic Records Practitioner‚Ñ¢, you must adhere to a standard of Sacred Ethics. This includes how you interact with the MTLOs. You are not "summoning" them like a servant; you are "inviting" them as a collaborator.

  • Reverence: Use the Sacred Invocation (Lesson 3.2) to signal your professional intent.
  • Boundaries: Do not ask for information that violates another's privacy. The MTLOs will simply withhold the data if you do.
  • Gratitude: Always close the session by thanking the MTLOs for their "service, wisdom, and protection."
Coach Tip: Overcoming Imposter Syndrome

If you feel like you're "making it up," ask the MTLOs for a Validation Point. This is a piece of information that makes no sense to you but is deeply meaningful to the client. This is the fastest way to build your professional confidence.

The Art of Discernment

One of the hardest skills to master is distinguishing between your own ego/thoughts and the guidance of the MTLOs. Statistical data from practitioner training programs suggests that it takes approximately 50-75 practice sessions to reach a 90% accuracy rate in discernment.

Signs of MTLO Guidance:

  • The information is neutral and non-judgmental.
  • The language used is different from your normal vocabulary.
  • The information is unexpected or contradicts your personal opinion.
  • There is a "physical click" or a sense of resonance in your body.

Coach Tip: The 3-Second Rule

When you ask a question in the Records, the very first thing that pops into your mind‚Äîwithin the first 3 seconds‚Äîis usually the MTLOs. Anything that comes after that is usually your logical mind trying to "edit" or "make sense" of it. Trust the first 3 seconds.
