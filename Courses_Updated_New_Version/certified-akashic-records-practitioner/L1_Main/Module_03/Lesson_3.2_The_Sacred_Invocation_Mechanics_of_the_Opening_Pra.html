The Name Protocol: Legal vs. Soul Names

In the Akashic Field, your "file" is indexed by the current legal name. This is a point of confusion for many new practitioners. You might wonder: "What if my client prefers their nickname or a spiritual name they've adopted?"

The protocol is strict for a reason: The legal name represents the Soul's current contract with the physical 3D reality. It is the "social security number" of the energetic realm. Using a nickname is like trying to pick up a package at the post office without a valid ID.

  • Legal Name: Must be used for the initial opening. Use the name exactly as it appears on their current legal documents.
  • Soul Name: If a client has a "Soul Name" revealed through the Records, it is used for healing work (Module 5), but not for the initial opening of the file.
  • The "Three Times" Rule: The name is spoken three times within the invocation to anchor the frequency in the Physical, Mental, and Spiritual planes.
Coach Tip

If a client has recently changed their name (e.g., marriage or divorce), use the name they legally identify as at the moment of the reading. The Records are updated in real-time as the physical identity shifts.
