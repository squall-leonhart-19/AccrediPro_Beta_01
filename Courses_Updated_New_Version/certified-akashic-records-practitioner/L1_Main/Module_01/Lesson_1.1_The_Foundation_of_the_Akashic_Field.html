Navigating the Energetic Planes

One of the most common mistakes new practitioners make is confusing the Akashic Field with other energetic layers. To be a professional, you must know where you are "traveling."

1. The Astral Plane

This is the layer of emotion, desire, and collective thought-forms. It is highly volatile and influenced by the ego. Many "psychic" readings happen here. While accurate for surface-level predictions, it lacks the soul-level wisdom of the Akasha.

2. The Collective Unconscious

Coined by Carl Jung, this is the shared pool of human archetypes and experiences. Accessing this provides psychological insight, but it is limited to the human experience rather than the soul's eternal journey.

3. The Akashic Field (The High Council)

The Akashic Field exists at a significantly higher frequency than the astral plane. It is a space of neutral, unconditional love. There is no judgment here‚Äîonly information. When you are in the Akasha, you feel a sense of profound stillness and "truth" that is unmistakable.

Coach Tip: Discerning the Source

If the information you receive feels "scary," "urgent," or "judgmental," you have likely dropped into the Astral Plane. The Akashic Records always speak with the voice of Neutral Compassion.

The Guardians: Masters, Teachers, and Loved Ones

In the AKASHA Protocol‚Ñ¢, we do not access the records "alone." We work with a specific group of energetic beings known as the MTLOs. Think of them as the professional "Librarians" or the "Board of Directors" for the soul.

  • Masters: Beings of light who have never been in physical form. They hold the highest blueprint of the soul's purpose.
  • Teachers: Beings who may have had physical incarnations and specialize in specific lessons (e.g., abundance, relationships, healing).
  • Loved Ones: Ancestors or souls known to the client who have transitioned. They provide personal, emotional support but are not the primary source of the record's data.

A professional practitioner builds a working relationship with these guardians. You are not "channeling" spirits; you are collaborating with the soul's wisdom keepers.
