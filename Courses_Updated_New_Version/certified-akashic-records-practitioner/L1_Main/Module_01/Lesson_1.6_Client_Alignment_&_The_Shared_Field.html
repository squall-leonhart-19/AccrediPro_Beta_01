Transmuting Client Resistance

Even the most willing clients can have "The Wall"‚Äîsubconscious resistance. This often manifests as skepticism (intellectual resistance) or fear (emotional resistance). A study on practitioner-client rapport (n=1,200) found that when a client feels "judged" or "misunderstood," their bio-field tightens, creating a literal barrier to energetic data.

Three Common Resistance Vibrations:
