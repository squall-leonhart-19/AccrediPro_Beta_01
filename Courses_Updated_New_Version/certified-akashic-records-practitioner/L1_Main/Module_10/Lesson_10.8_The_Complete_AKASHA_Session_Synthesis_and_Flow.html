Common Pitfalls for New Coaches

In our practitioner community, we see these four mistakes most often in the first 20 sessions. Identifying them now will save you months of "imposter syndrome."

  • 1. Over-Explaining the Tech: Spending 20 minutes explaining how the Records work instead of what they are saying. Keep the "how" brief; the client is there for the "what."
  • 2. Fear of Silence: Trying to fill every gap with talking. The Records often communicate in the pauses. If you feel a lull, simply say, "I'm just listening for the next layer of information."
  • 3. Being the "Hero": Trying to "fix" the client. You are the facilitator. The healing comes from the client's Records and their own willingness to see the truth.
  • 4. Ignoring the Mundane: Giving only "cosmic" advice. If the Records tell a client to quit their job, your job as a coach is to ask, "What is the first practical step you need to take to do that safely?"

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it important to ask "How does your body feel?" after delivering an Akashic insight?

It moves the client from their logical/analytical mind into their energetic body, which is where the Akashic healing and integration actually occur. It bypasses the "ego" resistance.

2. What is the primary risk of "over-explaining" the mechanics of the Records to a client?

It keeps the session in the "mental" realm and takes away valuable time from the actual soul-level work. Most clients care more about the insights than the technical details of the "cosmic library."

3. In the case of Elena, why was "better time management" the wrong solution?

Because her obstacle wasn't a lack of skill; it was a soul-level safety mechanism. Her blueprint associated "freedom" with "danger." No amount of scheduling can fix a fear of being unsafe.

4. True or False: A successful session always ends with the client feeling happy.

False. Success is marked by shifts in energy, "aha" moments, and a physical release (like a sigh), which can sometimes be accompanied by grief or heaviness as old patterns are cleared.
