Boundaries of Consent: The Third-Party Rule

A common ethical dilemma occurs when a client asks: "Can you look into my husband's records to see if he's cheating?" or "Why is my adult daughter so angry with me?"

Within the A.K.A.S.H.A. Protocol‚Ñ¢, we adhere to the Universal Law of Sovereignty. Every soul has a right to privacy. Accessing someone else's records without their explicit permission is considered a "psychic intrusion."

Legal and Ethical Disclaimers: Protecting Your Practice

To be a Certified Akashic Records Practitioner‚Ñ¢, you must operate with the same professionalism as a therapist or consultant. This means having clear legal disclaimers. A 2022 survey of wellness professionals found that 64% of legal disputes could have been avoided with a clear "Informed Consent" document.

Your disclaimers should explicitly state:

  • Not Medical/Legal Advice: Akashic readings are for spiritual and educational purposes only.
  • Client Responsibility: The client is 100% responsible for any actions taken after a session.
  • No Guarantees: Spiritual work is subjective and results vary based on client integration.
  • Scope of Practice: You are not a licensed therapist (unless you are) and do not diagnose mental health conditions.
