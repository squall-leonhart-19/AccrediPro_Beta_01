Common Mistakes vs. Professional Shifts

The Rookie Mistake The Professional Shift Why it Matters
Using "I think" or "Maybe" too much. Using "The Records show..." or "The MTLOs say..." Establishes authority and separates your ego from the channel.
Rushing to fill the silence. Allowing 10-15 seconds of quiet to "listen." Silence is where the deepest information often drops in.
Trying to predict the future. Focusing on the current soul alignment. The Records are about empowerment, not fortune-telling.
Giving "advice" based on your own life. Asking the Records for a specific perspective. Prevents "Coach Projection" and keeps the session pure.
Sarah's Pro Tip

If you get "nothing" during the silence, simply describe the feeling in the room. Is it heavy? Light? Warm? Often, describing the energy is the "key" that unlocks the verbal message.

Check Your Understanding

1. When Elena expresses fear about her age, what is the most effective Akashic approach?

Instead of offering "cheerleading" advice, ask the MTLOs to show Elena her "Soul Timeline" or the value of her accumulated wisdom. This shifts the focus from linear time to soul readiness.

2. What should you do if you receive a symbol (like a "Golden Seed") but don't immediately know what it means?

Describe the symbol to the client first. Often, the client's own soul will recognize the symbol, and their reaction will provide the interpretation you need.

3. Why is it important to ask "How does that land?" after giving a message?

It facilitates somatic integration. It ensures the client isn't just "hearing" the information intellectually but is "feeling" the truth of it in their body, which leads to real transformation.

4. How do you handle a client who is skeptical during the session?

Don't try to "prove" the Records. Stay in your heart space, keep your frequency high, and provide the information exactly as it comes. The frequency of the Records usually softens skepticism better than any argument.

Success Markers: How to Know You Nailed It

  • Frequency Resonance: You feel a sense of "rightness" or "chill" when speaking the message.
  • Client Empowerment: Elena moves from "I can't" to "I see how my past serves my future."
  • Financial Alignment: You feel confident that the value provided is worth a professional fee (typically $150-$300 for a deep-dive session).
