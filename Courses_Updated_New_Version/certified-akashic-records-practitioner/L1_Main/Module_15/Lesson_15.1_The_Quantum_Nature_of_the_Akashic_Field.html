Non-Local Information Store

The core of Akashic work lies in the concept of non-locality. In classical physics, things only interact if they are close to each other. For centuries, the Akasha has been described as a "Hall of Records" or a "Great Library." While these metaphors are helpful for the human mind to grasp the concept, they are technically inaccurate. The Akasha is not a physical place you "go to."
