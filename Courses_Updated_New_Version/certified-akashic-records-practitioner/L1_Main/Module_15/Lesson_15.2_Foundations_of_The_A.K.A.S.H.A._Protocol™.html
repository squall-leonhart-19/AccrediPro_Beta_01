The Hierarchy of Support: Understanding MTLOs

When you enter the Akashic Records, you are not navigating a void; you are entering a structured library of consciousness. This library is stewarded by specific energetic frequencies known as Masters, Teachers, and Loved Ones (MTLOs).

1. The Masters

These are the "Librarians" of the Akasha. They have never inhabited a physical body. They hold the Universal Laws and ensure the integrity of the information. They are the source of the most profound, impersonal, yet deeply loving wisdom.

2. The Teachers

These are soul-specific guides. Unlike a "spirit guide" who might stay with a person for a lifetime, Akashic Teachers often cycle in and out based on the lessons the soul is currently navigating. They are specialists in specific soul-themes (e.g., abundance, grief, leadership).

3. The Loved Ones

These are individuals who have known the soul in this or past lifetimes. They provide the emotional bridge. However, in the Akasha, they do not speak from their "personality" (as in mediumship), but from their "Soul Essence," providing perspective on soul contracts and shared karmic loops.

The Law of Consent: Ethical Boundaries

In Level 1 practice, the Law of Consent is your primary ethical directive. In the quantum field, "looking" without permission is an energetic violation that creates karmic entanglement for the practitioner.

Coach Tip on Ethics

A professional practitioner never enters the records of a spouse, adult child, or celebrity without explicit, verbal, or written consent. This is the difference between a "hobbyist" and a "certified professional."

  • Direct Consent: The individual must give permission.
  • The "Minor" Exception: Parents may access records for children under 18, but only regarding their parenting of that child.
  • Business Entities: You may access the "Soul" of a business if you are the owner or have been hired by the owner.
