Realistic Income Timeline

Building a practice is a marathon, not a sprint. While some students land high-paying clients in their first month, most follow this professional trajectory:

  • Months 1-3: The Foundation. Focus on certification, practicing on "beta" clients, and setting up your basic business structure. Income: $0 - $1,000.
  • Months 4-6: Traction. Your first 5-10 paying clients. You begin to refine your niche (e.g., Records for Nurses, Records for Divorced Women). Income: $1,000 - $3,500.
  • Months 7-12: Momentum. Referrals start kicking in. You likely raise your rates and perhaps launch your first small group program. Income: $4,000 - $8,000.
  • Year 2+: Scaling. You are now a recognized expert. You may have a waitlist for 1-on-1 work and a suite of digital products. Income: $10,000 - $25,000+.

1. What is the estimated income for a "Hybrid Model" practitioner?
