Common Mistakes to Avoid

New practitioners often fall into these traps. Awareness is 90% of the cure.

  • The "I Think" Trap: Saying "I think the Records are saying..." instead of "The MTLOs are showing me..." (Own your authority!)
  • Over-Explaining: Trying to make sense of a symbol before sharing it. If you see a "purple umbrella," just say "I see a purple umbrella." The meaning often belongs to the client.
  • Energy Leakage: Getting emotional with the client. You can be compassionate without taking on their grief or fear.
  • Ignoring the Closing: Rushing the end of the session. The closing prayer is vital for "zipping up" your energy field.
Income Insight

Practitioners who master the "Authority Voice" (avoiding "I think") are able to charge 30-40% more because the client feels the weight of the divine truth being shared.

Success Markers

How do you know the session was a success? Look for these three signs:

  1. The "Akashic Sigh": The client takes a deep, involuntary breath. This is a sign their nervous system has integrated a soul-truth.
  2. Physical Shift: The client's face looks brighter or more relaxed than when they started.
  3. Clarity of Action: The client leaves with one specific "soul-aligned" action step, not a list of 50 chores.

Check Your Understanding

1. If you receive a symbol in the Records that makes no sense to you, what should you do?

Share the symbol exactly as you see/feel it without trying to interpret it first. The meaning is often specific to the client's journey, not yours.

2. What is the primary purpose of the "Pathway Prayer" in a Level 1 reading?

To shift the practitioner's consciousness from the mundane/ego level to the vibrational frequency of the Akashic Records, ensuring the information comes from the MTLOs.

3. A client asks, "Will I win the lottery next week?" How do the Records typically respond to "prediction" questions?

The Records focus on soul growth and possibilities, not fixed fortune-telling. A practitioner should pivot to: "What is the energy around abundance in your life right now?"

4. Why is the "Closing Prayer" considered a non-negotiable step?

It officially severs the energetic link between the practitioner and the client's Records, preventing "energy hangovers" and maintaining professional boundaries.
