Akashic Field vs. Astral Plane

One of the most common mistakes new practitioners make is confusing the Astral Plane with the Akashic Field. Understanding this distinction is what separates the Certified Practitioner from the amateur.

The Astral Plane is the realm of emotion, thought-forms, and collective ego. It is often "noisy," dramatic, and filled with "spirits" that may or may not have the client's best interest at heart. The Akashic Field, however, is a realm of Pure Information and Unconditional Love. It is quiet, neutral, and profound.

Coach Tip: Discerning the Source

Information from the Astral Plane often feels like a "judgment" or a "command" (e.g., "You must do this!"). Information from the Akasha feels like an "invitation" or a "revelation" (e.g., "This is the pattern that has been playing out.").
