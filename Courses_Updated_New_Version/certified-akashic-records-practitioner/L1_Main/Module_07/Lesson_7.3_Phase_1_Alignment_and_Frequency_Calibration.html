Identifying and Resolving Practitioner Resistance

Resistance is the "energetic friction" that occurs when your subconscious mind tries to protect you from the unknown. It often manifests as:

To resolve this, we use the Permission Protocol. You must explicitly ask your own subconscious for permission to "step aside" for the duration of the session. This acknowledges the ego's protective role while firmly asserting your role as a Professional Practitioner.
