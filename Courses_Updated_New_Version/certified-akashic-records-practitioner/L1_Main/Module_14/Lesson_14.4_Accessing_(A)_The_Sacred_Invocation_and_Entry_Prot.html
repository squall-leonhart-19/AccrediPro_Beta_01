The transition from the 3D physical world to the 5D Akashic Field requires a "frequency bridge." The invocation acts as this bridge by:

The Spoken Word vs. Silent Intent

While experienced practitioners can sometimes open the Records through silent intent, Level 1 Practitioners are required to use the Spoken Word. Why? Because the physical vibration of your vocal cords provides a "tether" to the physical body, preventing you from "drifting" too far into the astral plane. It keeps the session grounded and professional.

Recognizing the MTLOs

When you enter the Records, you are met by a collective of energy known as the Masters, Teachers, and Loved Ones (MTLOs). Understanding their distinct "signatures" is vital for discerning the source of your information.

  • The Masters: These are non-physical beings who have never had a human incarnation. Their energy is vast, neutral, and incredibly powerful. They provide the "Big Picture" soul perspective.
  • The Teachers: These may be beings who have had human lives and achieved mastery in specific areas (e.g., healing, teaching, art). They provide practical, "how-to" guidance.
  • The Loved Ones: These are the ancestors or departed souls connected to the client. Their energy feels familiar and emotional. They provide comfort and specific personal validation.
Coach Tip: Discerning the Signature

If the information feels judgmental or "scary," it is not from the MTLOs. The Akasha is a field of unconditional love. Any message that triggers fear is likely coming from the practitioner's or client's ego/shadow. Re-center and re-read the invocation if this occurs.
