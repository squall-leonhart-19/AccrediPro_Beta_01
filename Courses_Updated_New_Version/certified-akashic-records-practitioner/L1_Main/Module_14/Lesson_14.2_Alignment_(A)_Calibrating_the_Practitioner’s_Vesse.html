Neutralizing Egoic Static

The "Ego" is not the enemy, but in the Akasha, it acts as a filter that can distort the truth. Egoic static typically manifests in three ways:

  • The "Helper" Complex: Wanting to give the client "good news" so they like you.
  • Projection: Overlaying your own life experiences onto the client's Records.
  • Intellectualization: Trying to make sense of a metaphor before it is fully received.

To neutralize this, we use the "Observer Displacement" technique. Before opening the Records, visualize your personal opinions, worries, and desires being placed in a "sacred container" outside the room. You are not discarding them; you are simply setting them aside to ensure the vessel is empty and ready to be filled.

According to the HeartMath Institute, the heart's electromagnetic field is 60 times greater in amplitude than the brain's. In the A.K.A.S.H.A. Protocol‚Ñ¢, we use the heart as the primary "antenna."
