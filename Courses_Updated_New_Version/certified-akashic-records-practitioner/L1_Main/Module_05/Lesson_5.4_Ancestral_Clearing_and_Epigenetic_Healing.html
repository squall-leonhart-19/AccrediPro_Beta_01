The Seven Generations Principle

The Seven Generations Principle is a foundational concept in indigenous wisdom that finds its energetic proof within the Akashic Records. It posits that the choices we make today affect the seven generations following us, and conversely, we are currently carrying the energetic residue of the seven generations that preceded us.

In the Akashic Field, this appears as an Energetic Braid. While your Soul Record contains your individual journey, your "Lineage Record" is a sub-folder that contains the unresolved emotional data of your biological bloodline. When a trauma is too great for one person to process‚Äîsuch as war, famine, or systemic oppression‚Äîit is "packaged" and passed down the line, seeking a descendant with the capacity to finally transmute it.

The Science of Epigenetic Healing

While the Akashic Records are an energetic construct, the effects of lineage healing are often biological. Epigenetics is the study of how behaviors and environment can cause changes that affect the way your genes work. Unlike genetic changes, epigenetic changes do not change your DNA sequence, but they change how your body reads a DNA sequence.

A landmark study by Rachel Yehuda et al. (2015) demonstrated that the offspring of Holocaust survivors had different stress hormone profiles than a control group, specifically linked to the trauma their parents experienced. This is the Physical Anchor of the Akashic Record.

When we perform an Akashic clearing, we are essentially performing "Energetic Epigenetic Modulation." By clearing the emotional charge of the trauma in the field, we signal the client's nervous system that the "threat" is over, allowing their genes to express health rather than hyper-vigilance.
