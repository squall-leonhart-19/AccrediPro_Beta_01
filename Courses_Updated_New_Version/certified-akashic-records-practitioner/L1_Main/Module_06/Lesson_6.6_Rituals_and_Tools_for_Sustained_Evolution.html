Physical Anchors: Sensory Frequency Triggers

The human brain is highly associative. By using physical objects, we can create a "Pavlovian response" to the Akashic field. This is essential for clients who work in high-stress environments.

Coach Tip: Programming the Anchor

Instruct your client to hold their chosen anchor (e.g., a piece of Citrine) during the session's peak healing moment. Tell them: "As you feel this light, squeeze the stone. You are now 'uploading' this frequency into the crystal."

Common anchors include:

  • Crystals: Selenite for clarity, Black Tourmaline for grounding the 5D energy.
  • Scents: Frankincense or Palo Santo to trigger the "Alignment" phase of the protocol.
  • Symbols: A specific piece of jewelry or a geometric drawing that represents the client's Soul Origin.
