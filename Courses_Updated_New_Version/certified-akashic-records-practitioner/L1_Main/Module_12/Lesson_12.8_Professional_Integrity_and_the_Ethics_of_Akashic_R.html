  • The "Mirror" Test: Record yourself on your phone saying the opening prayer. Listen back. Is your voice shaky? Or is it a "Commanding Calm"? Aim for the latter.

Common Practitioner Pitfalls

The Pitfall Why It Happens The Professional Fix
Over-Explaining Nervousness or wanting to "prove" the Records are real. Give the insight, then stop talking. Let it breathe.
Filtering Ego fear that the information is "too weird" or "wrong." Share the raw image/word. Often the "weird" stuff is the most accurate.
Taking the Burden Thinking YOU have to fix the client's life. Remember: You are the straw; the Records are the water. You just facilitate.
Time Bleeding Poor boundaries; session goes 30 minutes over. Set a timer for 10 minutes before the end to start the "wrap up."
Sarah's Tip

If you get "stuck" or the information stops flowing, simply say: "Let's sit in the silence of the Records for a moment and see what else wants to come forward." Silence is a tool, not a failure.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary purpose of the "Pathway Prayer" in a client session?

To shift the frequency of both the practitioner and the client from the mundane 3D level to the 5D level of the Akashic Records, ensuring the information comes from the Soul level rather than the ego.

2. If a client like Diane starts crying during a reading, what is your first professional responsibility?

To hold the sacred space without rushing to "fix" or stop the crying. Acknowledge the emotional release as "healing energy moving through" and maintain your grounded presence.

3. What should you do if you receive an image that seems "silly" or makes no sense to you?

Share it exactly as you see it. In the Akashic Records, the practitioner is a translator. The client often holds the "key" to the meaning that you do not have.

4. Why is it important to ask "How does that land with you?" during the session?

It creates a collaborative coaching environment, ensures the client is integrating the information, and helps you calibrate the direction of the reading based on their resonance.
