Check Your Understanding

1. What should you do if you feel "blocked" immediately after opening Elena's Records?

Take a deep breath, acknowledge the block internally, and ask the Masters/Teachers: "What is the most important thing for Elena to know right now?" Trust the first thought that enters your mind.

2. Why is it important to ask Elena for permission before opening her Records?

Ethically, we never enter a soul's Records without explicit verbal consent. This maintains the energetic boundary and respects the client's free will.

3. Elena starts crying during the session. What is your role as a Level 1 Practitioner?

Hold the space. You don't need to "fix" her crying. Simply say, "I am holding this space for you. This is a beautiful release." The Records are often a place of deep emotional healing.

4. How do you know if the session was successful?

Success is measured by the client feeling "seen" and "heard" by their own soul. If Elena leaves with a sense of peace or a new perspective on her fear, the session was a success.
