    The Three Layers of the Soul Map

    To create a professional Soul Map, we look at three distinct layers of information. Think of this as the "GPS" of the soul:

    1. The Origin Layer (The Seed)

    This layer identifies the soul's "point of entry" into the cosmic cycle. It includes soul lineages (e.g., Starseed origins such as Pleiadian or Sirian) and the primary energetic frequency the soul was "birthed" with. This explains a client's inherent strengths and why they may feel like "outsiders" in certain Earthly environments.

    2. The Experience Layer (The Journey)

    This is the "middle" layer containing the history of incarnations, karmic loops, and soul contracts. A 2021 meta-analysis on narrative identity suggests that individuals who view their lives as a "redemptive arc" (common in Akashic work) show 35% higher resilience scores. In this layer, we identify the recurring themes the soul has chosen to master.

    3. The Potential Layer (The Dharma)

    The final layer is the "Future Potential." It is not a fixed destiny but a map of the most probable high-vibration paths available to the client. This is where we identify their Sacred Purpose and the "Soul-Led Action Plan" (SAP) they are meant to implement in this lifetime.
