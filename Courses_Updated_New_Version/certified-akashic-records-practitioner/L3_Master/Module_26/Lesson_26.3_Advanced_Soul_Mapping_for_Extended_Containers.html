Strategic Karmic Staging

In a long-term container, you must determine when to open specific files. Opening a heavy ancestral trauma file in the same week a client is launching a new business is poor "energetic staging."

Follow this A.K.A.S.H.A. Staging Protocol for 3-month programs:

  1. Month 1 (Alignment & Clearing): Focus on "Current Life Debris" and immediate energetic leaks. Clear the surface so the client has the energy for deeper work.
  2. Month 2 (Deep Soul Mapping & Contract Dissolution): Dive into the multi-lifetime Primary Soul Themes. This is the "heavy lifting" phase.
  3. Month 3 (Anchoring & Manifestation): Focus on the future trajectory. Use the Records to download "Soul-Led Action Plans" (SAP) for their business or life.
