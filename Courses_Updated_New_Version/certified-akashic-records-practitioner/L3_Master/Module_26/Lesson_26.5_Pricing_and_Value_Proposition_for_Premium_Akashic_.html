The Psychology of Premium Pricing

Most practitioners start with "hourly" thinking because it is the standard in the corporate world. However, in the Akashic Records, time is non-linear. A 15-minute breakthrough in the Records can resolve a 20-year karmic loop. If you charge by the hour, you are effectively being penalized for your efficiency and mastery.

Premium pricing is based on the Depth of Transformation. When a client pays a premium price, they are making a psychological commitment to their own healing. A study in the Journal of Consumer Research suggests that higher price points often correlate with higher "sunk cost" motivation, leading to better follow-through and results.

Coach Tip: The Commitment Mirror

Remember that your price is a mirror of the client's commitment. When you undercharge, you often attract "window shoppers" who aren't ready to do the deep integration work required by the A.K.A.S.H.A. Protocol‚Ñ¢.

In business, ROI is "Return on Investment." In the Akasha, we use Return on Intention. To charge premium prices, you must help the client see how soul-level healing manifests in their physical life (the 3D world).

Consider the following 3D translations of Akashic work:

Packaging the Soul-Led Container

A premium program is more than just a bundle of sessions. It is a comprehensive support system. Using the "Anchoring" phase of our protocol, you must include tools that help the client maintain their frequency between sessions.

Essential Premium Components:

  • The Core Curriculum: The 8-12 week journey through the A.K.A.S.H.A. Protocol‚Ñ¢.
  • Somatic Anchoring Tools: Custom meditations, frequency music, or breathwork protocols.
  • The Soul-Led Action Plan (SAP): A physical document mapping their 3D steps.
  • Voxer/Telegram Support: High-access "pocket coaching" for real-time integration.
  • Bonus Modules: Pre-recorded content on energetic boundaries or ego-clearing.
Coach Tip: The Value of "Between"

The most valuable part of a premium program is often the support between sessions. This is where the client actually lives their life and meets the resistance of their old patterns.

Managing High-Ticket Energetics

Many practitioners suffer from "imposter syndrome" when stating a price above $1,000. This usually stems from a Lack of Frequency Calibration. If you do not believe your work is worth $3,000, your client's nervous system will sense that incongruence.

To clear money blocks in the Records, use the following inquiry during your next personal session:

  1. "What is my current subconscious ceiling for receiving financial energy?"
  2. "Where in my ancestral line was it 'unsafe' to be both spiritual and wealthy?"
  3. "How does charging a premium price serve the highest good of my client?"
Coach Tip: The Neutral Ask

Practice saying your price in the mirror until it feels as neutral as stating your phone number. Emotionally charged pricing leads to "convincing" energy, which repels premium clients.

Tiered Akashic Program Models

Providing tiers allows you to serve clients at different stages of their journey while protecting your most valuable asset: your time.

The Three-Tier Framework

Silver Tier (The Self-Study + Group): Access to recorded curriculum + 2 group Q&A calls per month. Price: $997 - $1,497

Gold Tier (The Private Journey): Everything in Silver + 6-8 private 1:1 Akashic sessions. Price: $2,500 - $4,500

Platinum Tier (The Immersion): Everything in Gold + Unlimited Voxer support + 1-day private virtual retreat. Price: $5,000 - $10,000+

Coach Tip: The Anchor Price

Always present your Platinum or Gold tier first. This sets a "value anchor." Even if the client chooses the Silver tier, they understand the depth of the work available and the professional caliber of your practice.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "per hour" pricing considered a disadvantage for a master Akashic practitioner?

It penalizes efficiency. A master can often achieve in 20 minutes what a beginner takes 2 hours to do. Premium pricing should be based on the depth of transformation, not the minutes on a clock.

2. What is the definition of "Return on Intention" (ROI) in this context?

ROI is the translation of 5D energetic shifts (like clearing vows) into tangible 3D life improvements (like increased income, better health, or relationship stability) that the client can value.

3. Name one psychological benefit of a client paying a premium price for healing work.

It increases "sunk cost" motivation, leading to higher levels of commitment, better follow-through on integration steps, and ultimately, more profound results.

4. Which tier usually includes "unlimited pocket coaching" or Voxer support?

The Platinum (or highest) Tier, as it provides the most direct access to the practitioner's energy and real-time guidance during the integration process.

  • Your pricing is a frequency calibration; clear your own money blocks to hold the energy of high-ticket exchanges.
  • Offer tiered options to create a professional structure that scales with your availability.
