The Practitioner's Triage: Red Flags & Discernment

The most critical skill in L3 work is Triage. You must be able to discern, within the first 15 minutes of an inquiry, if the client is experiencing a Spiritual Emergence (an expansion of consciousness) or a Clinical Crisis (a psychological breakdown requiring medical intervention).

Informed Soul Consent

When a client is in a crisis state, their "3D ego" may be desperate for a "quick fix." However, the soul may have a different timeline. Informed Soul Consent is the practice of ensuring the client understands that Akashic work can temporarily increase sensitivity before it brings relief.

The L3 Consent Checklist:

  • Does the client understand that we are accessing deep-seated patterns?
  • Have they been informed that "healing crises" (temporary fatigue or emotionality) may occur?
  • Do they have a support system in place for the 48 hours following the session?
  • Are they aware that the Records provide *guidance*, not *medical advice*?
