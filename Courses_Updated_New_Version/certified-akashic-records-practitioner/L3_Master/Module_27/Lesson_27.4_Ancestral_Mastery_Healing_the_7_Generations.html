Identifying Epigenetic Imprints in the Akasha

Science is finally catching up to what Akashic practitioners have known for millennia: trauma leaves a mark. Epigenetics is the study of how behaviors and environment can cause changes that affect the way your genes work. Unlike genetic changes, epigenetic changes are reversible and do not change your DNA sequence, but they change how your body reads a DNA sequence.

In the Akashic Records, these imprints appear as energetic overlays on the Soul Map. A 2014 study by Dias and Ressler demonstrated that mice trained to fear a specific smell passed that fear down to two subsequent generations who had never encountered the smell. In humans, we see this in "inherited" anxiety, scarcity, or physical predispositions that have no immediate trigger in the client's current life.

Coach Tip: Identifying the Pattern

When a client says, "I don't know why I feel this way, my life is actually fine," you are likely looking at an ancestral imprint. Look for patterns that skip a generation or affect all siblings in a specific way.
