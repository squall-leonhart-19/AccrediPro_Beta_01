Limbic Resonance and Emotional Co-regulation

Limbic Resonance is the capacity for our nervous systems to "sync" with one another. In the context of the Emotional Co-regulation (E) pillar, this is our most powerful tool. Because humans are social mammals, our internal states are not autonomous; they are deeply influenced by those closest to us.

When one partner is dysregulated, the other's nervous system naturally begins to mirror that state. This is why "calmness is contagious," but so is "chaos." As a practitioner, you teach couples to use their own regulated state to act as a "tether" for their partner.

Practitioner Insight

Teach your clients the "Vagal Brake" technique. When one partner is spiraling, the other doesn't use words (Language Alignment can wait). They use proximity and slow, audible exhales. This utilizes limbic resonance to pull the partner back into the Window of Tolerance.
