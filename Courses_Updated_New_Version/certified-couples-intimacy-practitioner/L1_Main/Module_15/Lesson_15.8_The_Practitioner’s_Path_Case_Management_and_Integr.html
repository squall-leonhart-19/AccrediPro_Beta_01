The Practice Script: Closing with Confidence

Practice these lines out loud. Notice your tone‚Äîit should be steady, warm, and authoritative.

You (The Practitioner) "Elena, I've listened to what you've shared about your marriage, and I want to be very clear: what you're feeling is not a 'dead end.' It's a transition. Elena (The Client) "I want to believe that, I really do. But it's a big investment, and I'm not sure if now is the right time."
You (The Practitioner) "I completely understand. It is an investment in yourself and your marriage. My 12-week program is designed specifically for women in your exact position. We handle the forms today, and we start our first deep-dive session on Tuesday. Does that sound like the support you've been looking for?"
