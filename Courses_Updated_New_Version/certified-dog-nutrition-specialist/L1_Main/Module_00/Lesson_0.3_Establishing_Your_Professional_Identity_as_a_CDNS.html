The Four-Tier Transformation: Your Path to Mastery

Your journey with AccrediPro Academy is structured into four progressive tiers, each building upon the last to ensure you develop not just knowledge, but true mastery and practical application. Think of it as climbing a mountain: each tier brings you to a new vantage point, expanding your view and solidifying your footing.

Tier Modules Core Focus Key Outcomes
Tier 1: FOUNDATION Modules 1-15 Core Methodology & Foundational Science Master essential dog anatomy, physiology, macro/micronutrients, digestive health, and the AccrediPro methodology. Build confidence in basic nutritional assessment.
Tier 2: ADVANCED Modules 16-23 Complex Cases & Clinical Depth Address specific health conditions (allergies, obesity, kidney disease), interpret advanced diagnostics, and create personalized plans for challenging cases.
Tier 3: MASTER Modules 24-29 Advanced Coaching & Supervision Skills Refine your coaching techniques, learn to mentor other practitioners, handle ethical considerations, and develop your unique coaching style.
Tier 4: PRACTICE Modules 30-36 Building Your Business & Client Acquisition Develop a comprehensive business plan, master marketing strategies, set pricing, acquire clients, and establish your professional brand.

Your Pace, Your Progress: Navigating the Program

We understand that life happens, and flexibility is key, especially for career changers juggling existing commitments. This program is designed to fit into your busy life:

  • Recommended Pace: We suggest dedicating 5-7 hours per week, which typically allows you to complete 1-2 lessons. At this pace, most students complete the certification in 6-9 months.
  • "Life Happens" Flexibility: There are no strict deadlines for individual lessons. All content is available on-demand, allowing you to pause, catch up, or accelerate as your schedule allows.
  • Staying on Track: We provide weekly check-ins and progress trackers within the platform. Setting aside dedicated study blocks and engaging with the community forum can significantly boost your consistency.
  • Support When You Fall Behind: If you find yourself struggling to keep up, please reach out! Our student success team and I are here to offer guidance, help you create a revised study plan, and provide the encouragement you need to get back on track. You're never alone in this journey.
