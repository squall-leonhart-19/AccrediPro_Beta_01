A Personal Welcome From Sarah

As the lead coach and creator of this certification, I want to extend my warmest, most genuine welcome. My Story: Why I Created This Program

My journey into breastfeeding support began with my own struggles and triumphs as a new mother. It was a deeply personal experience that ignited a passion to ensure other mothers felt seen, heard, and expertly guided. I saw firsthand the immense gap in accessible, compassionate, and evidence-based support. There were incredible lactation consultants, but often long waitlists, limited availability, and a sometimes clinical approach that felt intimidating.

I realized that what many mothers truly needed was a supportive coach ‚Äì someone who could bridge the gap between clinical advice and real-life application, offering consistent encouragement, practical strategies, and unwavering belief. This realization became my mission.

I poured my heart and soul into creating this program because I believe deeply in the power of women supporting women. I wanted to build a curriculum that didn't just teach facts, but cultivated confidence, practical skills, and a professional identity. I wanted to empower women like you to step into this vital role, equipped with premium knowledge and the business acumen to build a thriving practice. This program is my love letter to every mother and every woman who dreams of helping them.
