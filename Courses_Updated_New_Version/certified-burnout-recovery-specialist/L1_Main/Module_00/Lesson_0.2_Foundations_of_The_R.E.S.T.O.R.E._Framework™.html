The Math of Success

Let's make this feel achievable. You don't need 1,000 clients to have a six-figure practice. You need a focused strategy. Look at how the numbers break down for a $10,000/month goal:

  • Option A: 10 private clients at $1,000/month (Premium 1-on-1 support).
  • Option B: 20 group program participants at $500/month (8-week burnout reset).
  • Option C: 1 Corporate Retainer at $5,000/month + 5 private clients at $1,000/month.
Sarah's Insight

Don't undervalue your time. A general life coach might charge $75 an hour. A Burnout Recovery Specialist sells a 90-day transformation. We teach you to price for the result (freedom from exhaustion) rather than the hour.

Realistic Income Timeline

Success doesn't happen overnight, but it happens faster than you think when you have a credential that people trust. Here is what a typical path looks like for our graduates:

Check Your Understanding

1. Why do specialized Burnout Recovery Specialists typically earn more than general health coaches?

Specialized practitioners solve a specific, high-stakes problem that has a massive economic and personal cost. This allows them to price based on the value of the transformation rather than just hourly rates. Industry data shows a 42% income premium for specialists.

2. What is the "Hybrid Model" of earning?

The Hybrid Model combines multiple revenue streams, such as 1-on-1 coaching (active income), group programs (leveraged income), and online courses or memberships (passive income), to create a diversified and stable financial base.

3. According to the Success Math, how many $1,000/month clients are needed to reach a six-figure annual income?

To reach approximately $100,000/year (approx. $8,333/month), you would need roughly 8 to 9 consistent private clients at a $1,000/month price point.

4. What is the primary focus of Months 1-3 in the realistic income timeline?

The focus is on "The Foundation": completing your certification, establishing your professional presence (like LinkedIn), and working with beta clients to secure your first testimonials and proof of concept.
