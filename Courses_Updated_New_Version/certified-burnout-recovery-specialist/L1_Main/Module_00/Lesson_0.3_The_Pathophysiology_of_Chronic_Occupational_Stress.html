Time Commitment & Pacing

We designed this for women with full lives. Whether you are working full-time, managing a household, or both, this program is flexible. A 2022 meta-analysis of online professional certifications found that consistent, small blocks of study (30-45 minutes) outperformed "marathon sessions" for long-term skill acquisition.

  • The "Steady" Path: 3-4 lessons per week. Completion in 4-6 months.
  • The "Accelerated" Path: 1 module per week. Completion in 3-4 months.
  • The "Life Happens" Path: 1-2 lessons per week. No expiration on your access.
