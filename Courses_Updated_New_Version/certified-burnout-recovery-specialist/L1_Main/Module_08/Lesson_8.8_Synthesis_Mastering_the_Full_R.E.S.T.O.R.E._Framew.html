Maintenance vs. Evolution

Before you practice the script, you must understand the shift in mindset. We aren't just "maintaining" a lack of burnout; we are "evolving" into a resilient leader.

Focus Area Maintenance Mindset (The Trap) Evolution Mindset (The Goal)
Boundaries Avoiding "too much" work. Actively curating high-value opportunities.
Self-Care Doing it so I don't crash. Doing it because I deserve vitality.
Identity "I am a person who recovered." "I am a person who prioritizes peace."
Warning Signs Fear of the "old me" returning. Curiosity about what the "new me" needs.

Common Coaching Pitfalls in Module 8
  • Mistake 1: Celebrating the Relapse. When a client says "I can handle more now!", new coaches often cheer. Correction: Metastasize the "yes" to see if it aligns with their new values.
  • Mistake 2: Ending the Engagement Too Early. "You're doing great, let's touch base in 3 months!" Correction: Explain that the Evolve phase is the most vulnerable time for "Autopilot" to take over.
  • Mistake 3: Becoming a "Friend" instead of a "Coach." Correction: Maintain professional distance to ask the hard questions about their boundaries.

Check Your Practice Lab Knowledge

1. Why is the "Evolve" phase considered the most vulnerable time for a client?

Because the client feels "good enough" and is tempted to return to old high-stress habits (Autopilot) without the immediate pain of burnout to stop them.

2. What is the primary goal of the "Future-Self" inquiry?

To help the client make decisions based on their long-term vision of vitality rather than their short-term desire to please others or prove they are "recovered."

3. According to Sarah, how should you handle a client who wants to stop coaching because they "feel fine"?

Educate them on the 63% relapse rate and transition them into a "Resilience Maintenance" or "Evolution Lab" package to protect their progress.

4. What is the difference between "Maintenance" and "Evolution" regarding boundaries?

Maintenance is about avoiding work to prevent a crash; Evolution is about actively curating high-value opportunities that align with one's peace and purpose.
