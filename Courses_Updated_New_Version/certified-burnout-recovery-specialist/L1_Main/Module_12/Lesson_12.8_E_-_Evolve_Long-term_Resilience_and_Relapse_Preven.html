Common Pitfalls for New Coaches

The "Fix-It Trap" is the urge to provide immediate tactical advice (like "take magnesium"). It should be avoided because it bypasses the validation and root-cause inquiry phases, often making the client feel unheard and failing to address the deeper physiological issues.
