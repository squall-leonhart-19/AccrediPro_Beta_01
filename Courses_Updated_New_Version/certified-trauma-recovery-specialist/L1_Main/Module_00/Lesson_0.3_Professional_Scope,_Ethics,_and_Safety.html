The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is not just a collection of videos; it is a structured metamorphosis. We have organized the 36 modules into four progressive tiers designed to build your confidence and competence simultaneously.

Tier Focus Outcome
L1: FOUNDATION (Mod 1-15) Neurobiology, Safety, & Core Methodology You understand how trauma lives in the body and brain.
L2: ADVANCED (Mod 16-23) Complex Cases & Clinical Depth You can handle "stuck" clients and multi-layered trauma.
L3: MASTER (Mod 24-29) Supervision & Teaching Skills You develop the "eagle eye" view needed for high-level results.
L4: PRACTICE (Mod 30-36) Business Building & Client Acquisition You transition from a student to a profitable business owner.
Coach Tip

Don't rush to Tier 4! Many students want to jump straight to "How do I get clients?" but your marketing will only be as strong as your Tier 1 confidence. When you know you can help someone, selling your services becomes an act of service, not a sales pitch.

The Applied Mastery Difference

Most online certifications are "content dumps." You watch 50 hours of video, take a multiple-choice quiz, and get a PDF. But knowledge is not the same as skill. A 2022 study on adult vocational training found that learners who engaged in simulated practice retained 75% more information than those who only used passive reading (Peterson et al., 2022).

That is why we use Practice Labs. In every module, you will encounter:

  • Simulated Client Scenarios: "What would you say if a client said X?"
  • Role-Play Scripts: Exact language for de-escalating a triggered client.
  • Assessment Drills: Practicing the intake process until it feels like second nature.

To ensure you never feel lost during a coaching session, we provide you with the A.N.C.H.O.R. Framework‚Ñ¢. This is the proprietary backbone of the AccrediPro methodology. While we will deep-dive into this in Module 2, here is what it represents:

Time Commitment & Pacing

We designed this for busy women. Whether you are balancing a full-time job, raising kids, or caring for aging parents, you can succeed here. Data from our successful graduates shows two primary paths:

  1. The Fast Track (3-4 Months): Requires 10-12 hours per week. Best for those currently in transition or working part-time.
  2. The Balanced Path (6-9 Months): Requires 4-6 hours per week. This is the most common path for career changers.

Life happens. If you need to take a month off because of a family emergency, your access doesn't expire. We are here to support your completion, not just your enrollment.

Coach Tip

I recommend scheduling "Certification Time" in your calendar just like a doctor's appointment. Even 30 minutes a day is more effective than a 5-hour marathon once every two weeks.
