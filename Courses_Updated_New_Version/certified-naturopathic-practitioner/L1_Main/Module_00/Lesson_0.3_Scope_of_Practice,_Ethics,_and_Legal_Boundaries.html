The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is not a "weekend workshop." It is a comprehensive professional training designed to move you through four distinct levels of expertise. A 2022 study on adult vocational training found that structured tiered learning increases retention by 64% compared to linear information delivery.

Tier 1: Foundation Modules 1-15: Anatomy, Physiology, & Core Naturopathy Understanding how the body works as a whole.
Tier 2: Advanced Modules 16-23: Complex Cases & Clinical Depth Connecting symptoms to root cause imbalances.
Tier 3: Master Modules 24-29: Supervision & Leadership Advanced protocols and client management.
Tier 4: Practice Modules 30-36: Business Build & Client Acquisition Launching your legal, profitable practice.

Practical Mastery & Labs

The biggest gap in most online certifications is the lack of application. You can read every book on naturopathy, but if you don't know how to talk to a client, you don't have a career. This is why we include Practical Mastery Labs in every module.

In these labs, you will:

  • Analyze real-world (anonymized) client intake forms.
  • Practice "The Discovery Call" using our proven scripts.
  • Draft protocol recommendations for specific case studies.
  • Review recorded sessions to see how master practitioners handle difficult questions.

Sarah's Insight

Treat these labs like your "clinical hours." The more you lean into the practice scenarios, the less imposter syndrome you'll feel when your first paying client sits across from you.
