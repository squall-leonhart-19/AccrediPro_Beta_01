Preparing for the Certification Exam

The final step to your credential is the AccrediPro CNP Exam. This is a 20-question, multiple-choice assessment designed to ensure you can safely and effectively apply naturopathic principles.

  • Format: Online, multiple choice.
  • Passing Score: 80% (16 out of 20 correct).
  • Retakes: You may retake the exam if needed, but we recommend reviewing the modules first.
  • Focus Areas: The 6 Naturopathic Principles, Gut Health (Module 5), Endocrine Balance (Module 8), and Scope of Practice/Ethics (Module 14).
Sarah's Coach Tip

Most students finish the exam in under 30 minutes. Take your time, read each question twice, and remember: you've already learned everything you need to know. You've got this!
