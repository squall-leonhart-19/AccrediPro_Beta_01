    Analyze Root Causes: The HLA-DR Defect

    Why do four people live in the same moldy house, but only one gets sick? The root cause often lies in the HLA-DR (Human Leukocyte Antigen) genetic complex. Approximately 24.7% of the population possesses "mold-susceptible" or "biotoxin-susceptible" genes.

    In a healthy individual, the innate immune system identifies a biotoxin (like a mycotoxin from mold) and "hands it off" to the adaptive immune system to create antibodies and clear it. In those with HLA-DR defects, the "hand-off" never happens. The biotoxins remain in circulation, continuously re-triggering the innate immune system, leading to a "cytokine storm" that never ends.
