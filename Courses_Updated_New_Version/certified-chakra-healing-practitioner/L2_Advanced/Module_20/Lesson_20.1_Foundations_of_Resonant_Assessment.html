Defining 'Resonant Assessment'

In the context of the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, assessment is not a one-time event but a continuous state of observation. The "R" (Resonant Assessment) is the diagnostic phase where the practitioner matches their frequency to the client's field to identify areas of dissonance.

True Resonant Assessment involves three distinct layers:

  • Informational: The data provided by the client (Intake Form).
  • Visual: Body language, posture, and auric color/density cues.
  • Vibrational: The subtle tactile "feel" of the chakra centers through hand scanning.

Ethics and the Scope of Practice

As a Certified Chakra Healing Practitioner‚Ñ¢, you occupy a unique space in the wellness industry. It is vital to understand that energetic assessment is not medical diagnosis. In many jurisdictions, using terms like "diagnose," "treat," or "cure" is restricted to licensed medical professionals.

The Practitioner's Boundary: You assess the energetic patterns that may correspond to physical or emotional symptoms. If a client presents with severe physical pain, your role is to assess the energetic stagnation while simultaneously recommending they consult a physician. This "Collaborative Care" model increases your professional legitimacy and protects your practice.
