The Solar Plexus is the seat of ego and identity. In a client with complex PTSD and recent trauma, opening this center without first grounding the Root Chakra can cause "energetic flooding," leading to increased anxiety, dissociation, or a physical hypertensive spike.

Key Takeaways

  • Clinical Neutrality: Always maintain a professional distance, especially when a client exhibits "savior" transference.
  • The 3-Pillar Approach: Advanced cases require Energy Work + Medical Oversight + Psychological Support.
  • Medication Boundaries: Never, under any circumstances, advise a client to alter or cease physician-prescribed medication.
  • Root-First Strategy: In cases of trauma or high stress, prioritize grounding (Root) before tackling identity (Solar Plexus) or spiritual (Crown) centers.
  • Documentation is Protection: Clear, professional clinical notes are the hallmark of a legitimate, high-level practitioner.
