When a client has a history in high-stress environments like ICU nursing, their Root Chakra is often in a state of "Functional Freeze." They can perform tasks, but their nervous system is not actually grounded. Always stabilize the Root before opening the Crown.

The Clinical Reasoning Process

In advanced practice, we use Layered Logic. We don't just see "blocked chakras"; we see a sequence of events. Diane's reasoning follows this path:

  1. The Foundation (Root): The divorce and career change shattered her sense of safety. Without a stable Root, the higher centers cannot hold a charge.
  2. The Power Leak (Solar Plexus): Her GERD is a somatic manifestation of "not being able to stomach" her new reality. Her power is leaking into her adult children's lives to avoid her own growth.
  3. The Protective Armor (Heart): The palpitations represent the Heart Chakra's attempt to "break through" the protective armor she built during her nursing years.

Differential Considerations

As a professional, you must distinguish between energetic stagnation and medical necessity. We use the Priority Ranking System:

  • Priority 1: Heart Palpitations. While Diane was cleared by a cardiologist, any recurrence during a session requires a pause. Is this a "Heart Opening" or a "Cardiac Event"? Clinical rule: If the client feels dizzy or has radiating pain, refer out immediately.
  • Priority 2: The Solar Plexus/GERD Link. Is the GERD purely dietary, or does it flare when she talks about her business? Diane's flares correlate with "visibility" tasks. This confirms an Energetic Root.
