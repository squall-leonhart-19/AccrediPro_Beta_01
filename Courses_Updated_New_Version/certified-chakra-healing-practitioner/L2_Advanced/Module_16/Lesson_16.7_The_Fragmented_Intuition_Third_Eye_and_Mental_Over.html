Discerning Anxious Chatter from Intuitive Guidance

One of the most valuable services you can offer a client is the ability to distinguish between the voice of the ego (fear) and the voice of the soul (intuition). As a Certified Chakra Healing Practitioner‚Ñ¢, you must teach the client to "feel" the source of the thought.

The Frequency Comparison

Anxious Chatter: Feels rapid, shrill, and originates in the "front" of the forehead. It usually uses "What if?" language and creates a tightening in the Solar Plexus.

Intuitive Guidance: Feels calm, "heavy" or grounded, and often originates from the "back" of the head or the heart. It uses "I am" or "It is" language and creates a sense of expansion.
