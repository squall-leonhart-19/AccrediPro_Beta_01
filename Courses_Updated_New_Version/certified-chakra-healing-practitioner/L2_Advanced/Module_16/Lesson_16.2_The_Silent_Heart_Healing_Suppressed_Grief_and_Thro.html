The "AH-HAM" Technique

This technique bridges the Heart (seed sound: YAM/AH) and the Throat (seed sound: HAM).

  1. Infusion: Have the client place one hand on the heart and one on the throat.
  2. Vibration: Inhale deeply into the heart. On the exhale, produce a long "AAAAH" sound, feeling the vibration in the chest.
  3. Transition: Halfway through the exhale, shift the sound to "HHHAAAMMM," feeling the vibration move up into the throat and mouth.
  4. Outcome: This creates a "vibrational bridge," physically and energetically clearing the path for the heart's energy to reach the throat.
