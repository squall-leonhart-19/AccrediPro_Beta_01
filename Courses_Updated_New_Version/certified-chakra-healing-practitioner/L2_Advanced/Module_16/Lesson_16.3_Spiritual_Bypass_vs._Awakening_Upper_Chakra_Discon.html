The 'Un-grounded Mystic' Profile

The "Un-grounded Mystic" is a clinical presentation characterized by an over-active Crown (Sahasrara) and Third Eye (Ajna), coupled with a severely deficient Root (Muladhara). In energy medicine, we call this a "top-heavy" energetic field.

Using the Resonant Assessment (R) phase of the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, you may notice these cues:

  • Visual Cues: A "glassy" look in the eyes, lack of eye contact, or a physical appearance that seems "ethereal" or frail.
  • Somatic Resonance: When scanning the field, the practitioner may feel a strong pull or buzzing around the head, but a complete "void" or coldness at the feet and base of the spine.
  • Language Cues: Excessive use of "we are all one," "it‚Äôs all an illusion," or "the universe will provide," especially when discussing practical problems like debt or health issues.

Mechanisms of Spiritual Bypass

Spiritual bypass, a term coined by psychologist John Welwood in 1984, is the use of spiritual practices and beliefs to avoid dealing with our painful feelings, unresolved wounds, and developmental needs. From a chakra perspective, this is a dysfunctional ascending current.

Feature Authentic Awakening Spiritual Bypass
Grounding Deeply rooted in the physical body and reality. Dissociation from the body; "escaping" to higher realms.
Emotions Welcomes all emotions (grief, anger, joy) as sacred. "Positive vibes only"; represses "lower" emotions.
Responsibility Takes radical ownership of life and choices. Uses "destiny" or "karma" to avoid accountability.
Shadow Actively integrates the dark parts of the self. Denies the existence of the shadow or ego.

A 2017 study published in Psychology of Religion and Spirituality suggests that individuals who utilize spiritual bypass often show higher levels of attachment anxiety and lower levels of emotional regulation (n=342, p < .05). This data confirms that the upper chakra "opening" is often a protective shield against lower chakra trauma.

Dissolving the Spiritual Ego

The Dissolving Blockages (D) phase for this client type focuses on the "Spiritual Ego." This is a sophisticated version of the ego that believes it is "beyond" human needs. This creates a massive blockage in the Throat (communication of truth) and Heart (authentic connection).

Techniques for Dissolving:

  • Shadow Mirroring: Asking the client to identify traits they find "un-spiritual" in others and finding where those traits live within themselves.
  • Somatic Truth-Telling: Having the client speak about a physical "shame" (e.g., financial debt, body dysmorphia) while maintaining hand contact with the Root chakra.

Anchoring Higher Frequencies

The Anchoring Frequency (A) phase is critical to prevent the "energetic floating" that leads to dissociation. Without anchoring, the healing session is just another "trip" that doesn't produce lasting change.
