The Mechanics of Spiritual Bypass

In L2 practice, we must look beyond the "beauty" of spiritual experiences to see the underlying energetic structure. Spiritual Bypass, a term coined by psychologist John Welwood, describes the use of spiritual ideas and practices to avoid facing unresolved emotional issues, psychological wounds, and unfinished developmental tasks.

Energetically, this manifests as a massive surge in the Ascending Current (the current of liberation) while the Descending Current (the current of manifestation) is blocked or weak. When a client spends all their time in the Crown (Sahasrara) and Third Eye (Ajna), they lose the "weight" necessary to navigate the 3D world. This is not enlightenment; it is fragmentation.
