Custom Energetic Hygiene Homework

Professionalism in chakra healing means providing clear, actionable homework. This is what separates a "woo-woo" session from a professional clinical intervention. Your "Energetic Hygiene" protocol should include:

  • Clearing: How to clear the energy of the day (e.g., salt baths, smoke cleansing, or visualization).
  • Protection: How to shield the newly opened chakras in crowded or toxic environments.
  • Feeding: Specific foods or colors that resonate with the chakra being treated.

Example: For a client with a sensitive Root Chakra, the homework might include "Barefoot Earthing" for 10 minutes daily and increasing the intake of root vegetables.

The Transition to Autonomy

The ultimate goal of a Certified Chakra Healing Practitioner‚Ñ¢ is to make yourself redundant. A client who is dependent on you for their energetic stability is not truly healed. Your treatment plan must have a clear "Exit Strategy."

This transition typically follows a 3-step sequence:

  1. Practitioner-Led (Sessions 1-4): You do 80% of the energetic work.

  3. Client-Sustained (Session 9+): You act as a "Consultant" or "Checker," while the client maintains their own field through the rituals you've designed.
