The 'Cloaking' Technique: Advanced Shielding

Standard "shielding" often involves imagining a bubble of light. While effective for beginners, the advanced practitioner requires a more sophisticated approach when entering high-density environments (hospitals, courtrooms, or sessions with highly toxic clients). Cloaking is the process of adjusting your external frequency to become "energetically invisible" to lower-vibrational interference.

Unlike a shield, which can sometimes create a "wall" that triggers resistance in others, a cloak allows you to move through heavy energy without it "hooking" into your field. This is achieved by vibrating at a frequency that does not provide a resonant match for the surrounding density.

Coach Tip

Think of cloaking like a stealth aircraft. You aren't fighting the radar; you are simply not reflecting it back. Use this when you feel a client is subconsciously "vampirizing" energy or when you are in crowded, chaotic public spaces.
