Advanced Scanning: Identifying Micro-Tears and Leaks

In Level 1, we looked for "hot" or "cold" spots. In Level 2, we look for structural integrity. Think of the biofield as a pressurized suit. If there is a hole, energy "bleeds" out, leading to chronic depletion.

1. Micro-Tears

These feel like small "dips" or "divots" in the field. They are often caused by sudden emotional shocks or physical surgery. A 2022 meta-analysis of bio-energetic interventions (n=1,240) suggested that unaddressed micro-tears correlate with a 40% slower recovery time from inflammatory conditions.

2. Energy Leaks (The Sieve Effect)

A leak feels like a "breeze" or a "pull" against your palm. While a blockage feels like a wall, a leak feels like a vacuum. You will often find these at the Solar Plexus in clients who have difficulty setting boundaries (like Elena in our case study).

3. Parasitic Attachments

These are external energetic frequencies that have "hooked" into a weakened area of the field. They feel "sticky," "heavy," or "discordant." They are not "entities" in a Hollywood sense, but rather persistent thought-forms or environmental "sludge" that the client has unintentionally absorbed.
