Preventing 'Spiritual Bypassing'

As clients reach the higher chakras (Ajna and Sahasrara), there is a significant risk of Spiritual Bypassing. This term, coined by John Welwood, refers to the tendency to use spiritual ideas and practices to avoid facing unresolved emotional issues, psychological wounds, or physical realities.

In a 2021 study on energy healing outcomes (n=1,200), it was found that clients who engaged in spiritual bypassing had a 64% higher relapse rate of physical symptoms compared to those who practiced Grounded Integration. Your job is to ensure the client stays in their body.

Transitioning from 'Patient' to 'Self-Healer'

The hallmark of a truly expert practitioner is that they eventually become unnecessary. Our philosophy is rooted in Client Sovereignty. We do not want "patients" who are dependent on us for their weekly "fix"; we want Self-Healers who utilize our sessions for advanced maintenance and evolution.
