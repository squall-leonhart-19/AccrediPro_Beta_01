    Seasonal and Lunar Synthesis

    Longevity is not a static state; it is a rhythmic one. A practitioner must teach the client to adjust their RADIANT maintenance protocol based on external cycles. As the seasons shift, so does the dominance of certain elemental energies.

    The Seasonal Shift

    • Winter (Root Dominance): Focus on warmth, cooked root vegetables, and deep stillness. Rituals should emphasize Anchoring (A).
    • Spring (Sacral/Heart Dominance): Focus on movement, greens, and "spring cleaning" the bio-field. Rituals should emphasize Dissolving (D).
