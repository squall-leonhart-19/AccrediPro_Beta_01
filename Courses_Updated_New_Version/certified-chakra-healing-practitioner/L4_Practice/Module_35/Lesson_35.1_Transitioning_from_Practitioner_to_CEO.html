Systems for the RADIANT Methodâ„¢

Scaling requires you to remove yourself as the "operator" of every small task. The Resonant Assessment (R) phase is often where practitioners lose the most time. By implementing a Client Relationship Management (CRM) system, you can automate the following:

  • Automated Intake: Clients receive their bio-energetic questionnaire immediately upon booking.
