Unlike 1-on-1 discovery calls, a group invitation call focuses heavily on the collective energy and the shared journey. Use this 20-minute structure.

YOU:

Phase 3: The Roadmap (6 Minutes)

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary psychological driver for women aged 45-55 to join a group healing program?

The primary driver is belonging and community. Research shows this demographic values shared experiences and social support as highly as the healing modality itself.

2. How does the "Invitation Call" for a group differ from a 1-on-1 Discovery Call?

The group call focuses on the exponential energy of the collective and the roadmap of the shared journey, rather than just the individual practitioner-client relationship.

3. If you enroll 10 students at a $497 price point for a 6-week program (90 mins/week), what is your approximate revenue per live hour?

Total revenue is $4,970. Total live hours are 9. This results in approximately $552 per live hour, demonstrating the massive scalability of the group model.

4. What is the best way to handle the "I won't get enough attention" objection?

By emphasizing intimacy through capped enrollment (e.g., limiting to 10-12 people) and highlighting dedicated Q&A time for every participant.
