Managing the 'Healing Crisis'

When you successfully dissolve a major blockage, the client will often experience a Healing Crisis (also known as a Herxheimer reaction in the medical world). This is a temporary worsening of symptoms as the body and biofield process the released "debris."

Common Symptoms of Energetic Detox:

  • Flu-like fatigue and muscle aches.
  • Sudden emotional "purges" (unexplained crying or anger).
  • Vivid dreams or temporary insomnia.
  • Skin breakouts or digestive shifts.

It is CRITICAL that you warn your clients about this before it happens. If they aren't prepared, they may think your treatment "made them worse" and stop coming. Reframe it as a sign of success: "Your body is finally strong enough to let go of what no longer serves you."
