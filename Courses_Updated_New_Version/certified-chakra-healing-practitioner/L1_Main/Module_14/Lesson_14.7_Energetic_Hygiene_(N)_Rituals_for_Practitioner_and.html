Cord Cutting and Boundary Setting

During deep energetic work, etheric cords can form between the practitioner's chakras and the client's. These are often "cords of sympathy" where the practitioner's energy is being siphoned to support the client's healing. While this may seem "kind," it is actually a violation of professional boundaries and stunts the client's ability to generate their own prana.

The Professional Cord-Cutting Protocol:

  • Recognition: Identify if you are thinking about a client more than 30 minutes after a session ends.
  • Visualization: See the cord (usually at the Solar Plexus) and visualize a pair of golden shears neatly severing the tie.
  • Affirmation: "I return your energy to you, cleansed and blessed. I call my energy back to me, whole and vital."
Coach Tip: The Email Boundary

Energetic hygiene includes digital hygiene. Set specific "office hours" for client communication. Responding to emotional client emails at 11:00 PM creates an open energetic cord that disrupts your sleep and Crown Chakra alignment.
