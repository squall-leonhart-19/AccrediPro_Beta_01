When a client experiences an emotional trauma or physical injury that they cannot fully process in the moment, the body‚Äôs compensatory mechanism is to isolate that energy. This is not merely "spiritual"‚Äîit is physiological. The piezoelectric nature of fascia means that mechanical stress (trauma) is converted into electrical signals. Managing the 'Healing Crisis'

As a professional practitioner, you must be prepared for the Herxheimer-like reaction of energetic clearing. When a blockage dissolves, the "toxins" (both energetic and biochemical) are released into the client's system for processing.
