The Conduit Principle: Channeling vs. Transfer

The hallmark of a master practitioner is the ability to move vast amounts of energy through the body without retaining "friction." In the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, we emphasize that the practitioner is a conduit, not a battery. When you attempt to "push" your own energy into a client, you risk compassion fatigue and energetic burnout.

Scientific research into biofield therapies suggests that when a practitioner enters a state of resonant coherence, they are not actually transferring their own ATP or cellular energy.
