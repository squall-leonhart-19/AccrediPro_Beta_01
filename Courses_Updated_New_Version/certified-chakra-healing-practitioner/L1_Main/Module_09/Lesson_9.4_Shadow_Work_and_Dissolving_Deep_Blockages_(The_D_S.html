In the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, the D step (Dissolve) addresses what biofield researchers often call an Energetic Cyst. This is not a physical cyst, but a localized area of high-density energy where a traumatic memory or repressed emotion has been "walled off" from the rest of the system.

A 2021 study on biofield physiology suggested that these areas of stagnation often correlate with fascial constriction and localized hypoxia (lack of oxygen) in the tissues. When energy cannot flow, the tissue literally begins to "harden."

Trauma-Informed Clearing: The Art of Titration

Dissolving deep blockages requires a trauma-informed approach. If you dissolve a blockage too quickly, the client may experience "flooding"‚Äîa state where the nervous system is overwhelmed by the sudden release of stored emotional charge.

We use a technique called Titration: releasing energy in small, manageable amounts. Think of it like opening a soda bottle that has been shaken; you twist the cap slightly to let the gas hiss out slowly, rather than removing the cap all at once.

Managing the 'Healing Crisis'

As you dissolve deep blockages, the body must process the released "waste"‚Äîboth biochemical and energetic. This is known as a Healing Crisis (or Herxheimer Reaction in medical terms). Research shows that up to 15-20% of clients may experience temporary "worsening" of symptoms before significant improvement.

Common Detoxification Symptoms:

  • Physical: Fatigue, mild headaches, changes in digestion, skin breakouts.
  • Emotional: Sudden weeping, vivid dreams, irritability, or feelings of "lightness" followed by "heaviness."
