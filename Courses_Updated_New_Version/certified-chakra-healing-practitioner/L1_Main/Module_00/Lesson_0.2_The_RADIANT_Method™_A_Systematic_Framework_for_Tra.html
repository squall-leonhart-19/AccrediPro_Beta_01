The Achievable Math

Income goals can feel abstract until you break them down into "Practitioner Math." Let's look at how simple it is to reach $8,000 per month (a common "freedom number" for our students).

  • Option A (1-on-1 Intensive): 10 clients paying $800/month for a premium transformation package. (Total: $8,000)
  • Option B (The Group Model): 16 students in a $500 Chakra Alignment course. (Total: $8,000)
  • Option C (The Hybrid): 5 private clients ($2,500) + 1 Corporate Workshop ($1,500) + 20 people in a small group ($4,000). (Total: $8,000)

Notice that none of these require 1,000 clients. You only need a small, dedicated "tribe" to build a thriving six-figure business.

Your 12-Month Professional Roadmap

Success doesn't happen overnight, but it happens faster than you think when you have a plan.

  1. Months 1-3: The Deep Dive. Complete your certification, practice on friends/family, and build your "Foundational Story."
  2. Months 4-6: The Beta Phase. Take on your first 3-5 paying clients at a "Charter Rate." Target Income: $1,500 - $3,000/mo.
  3. Months 7-12: The Launch Phase. Solidify your signature offer, start your marketing, and increase rates to professional standards. Target Income: $5,000 - $10,000/mo.
  4. Year 2+: The Scale Phase. Introduce group programs, workshops, or digital products. Target Income: $15,000+ /mo.
