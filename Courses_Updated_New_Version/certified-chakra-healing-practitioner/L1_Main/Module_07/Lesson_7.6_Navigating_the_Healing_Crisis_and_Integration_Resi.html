Softening Resistance to the 'New Self'

Integration resistance often stems from the Identity Protective Mechanism. The ego prefers a "known misery" to an "unknown joy." As the chakras align, the client may subconsciously sabotage their progress to return to their old, familiar vibration.

Tools for Softening the Ego:

  1. The "Future Self" Dialogue: Have the client speak from the perspective of their healed Heart Chakra to the "scared" version of themselves.
  2. Incremental Anchoring: Instead of asking for a 100% life change, focus on "micro-embodiments"‚Äîone 5-minute ritual per day.
