The Transformation Timeline: Breakthroughs vs. Evolution

Transformation occurs in two distinct ways: the Quantum Leap and the Incremental Evolution. As a practitioner, you must manage client expectations for both.

1. The Quantum Leap (Immediate Breakthrough)

This is the "Aha!" moment. A sudden release of a long-held blockage in the Heart Chakra might result in an immediate physical sensation of lightness or an emotional purge. This is the catalyst for transformation.

2. The Incremental Evolution (Long-term Recalibration)

This is the "T" phase in action. It happens over the 21-40 days following a deep session. It is the brain‚Äôs neuroplasticity catching up with the energetic shift. Research suggests that it takes approximately 66 days to form a new automatic habit; the same applies to vibrational habits.

Coach Tip: The 72-Hour Window

Always schedule a "check-in" 72 hours after a major infusion session. This is the critical window where the "old self" attempts to re-assert control. Your support during this window ensures the "T" phase takes root.
