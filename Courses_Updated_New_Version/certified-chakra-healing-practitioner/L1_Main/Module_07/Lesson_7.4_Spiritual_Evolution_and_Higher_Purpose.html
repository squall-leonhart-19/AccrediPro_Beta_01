Navigating Spiritual "Growth Spurts"

As clients reach the "T" phase of the RADIANT Methodâ„¢, they often experience rapid expansions in consciousness. We call these Spiritual Growth Spurts. While exciting, they can be disorienting. Symptoms often include:

  • Heightened Intuition: Suddenly "knowing" things about people or situations without logical proof.
  • Sensitivity to Environments: Feeling physically drained in crowded or chaotic spaces.

When a client feels "weird" or "too sensitive," explain it as a software upgrade. Their "hardware" (the nervous system) needs time to adjust to the new "software" (higher frequency). Refer back to Module 5: Anchoring Frequency to help them stay grounded during these spurts.

Practical Daily Integration

Spiritual evolution is useless if it doesn't change how a client handles a Tuesday afternoon. To integrate higher purpose, we must bridge the gap between the Sacred and the Secular.

The "Purpose Filter" Technique: Teach your clients to run every major decision through their Heart-Crown axis. 1. Crown Check: Does this choice align with the highest version of myself? 2. Heart Check: Does this choice expand my capacity to love and serve? 3. Root Check: Can I practically sustain this choice in the physical world?

Coach Tip: The 5-Minute Morning Alignment

Suggest a daily ritual: Hand on Heart (Anahata), focus on the top of the head (Sahasrara). Ask: "How may I be used today for the highest good?" This simple shift moves the client from a "to-do" list to a "to-be" list.
