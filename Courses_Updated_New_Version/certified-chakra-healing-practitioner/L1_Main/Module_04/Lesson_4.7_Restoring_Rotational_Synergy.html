Understanding 'Spin Rate' and Vitality

Chakras are not static pools of energy; they are dynamic vortices. In physics, a vortex's power is determined by its angular momentum. In energy medicine, we refer to this as the Spin Rate. A healthy chakra spins with enough velocity to pull Prana from the environment and distribute it to the corresponding endocrine and nervous systems.

When a client presents with chronic fatigue, "brain fog," or a general sense of being "stuck," the assessment often reveals a sluggish spin rate. These Hypo-rotational states mean the chakra is spinning too slowly to sustain the "Infusion" you are attempting to provide. It's like trying to charge a battery that has a high internal resistance.

Coach Tip: Somatic Detection

When scanning a sluggish chakra, your hand may feel a sense of "heaviness" or "static." A high-velocity chakra feels like a gentle, cool breeze or a vibrating magnetic repulsion. If it feels like "mud," the spin rate is insufficient.

Identifying and Sealing 'Energy Leaks'

Imagine pouring water into a bucket with a hole in the bottom. No matter how much Prana you infuse, the client will remain depleted if the Auric Field has fissures. These "leaks" are often found near the site of old surgeries, emotional scars, or areas of chronic physical pain.

Using the Somatic Resonance techniques from Module 1, you will scan the "outer shell" of the chakra's field (about 6-12 inches from the body). A leak feels like a "cold spot" or a sudden drop in pressure.

Coach Tip: The Sealing Technique

To seal a leak, use a "weaving" motion with your fingers, visualizing golden threads of light closing the gap. Follow this with a "Vibrational Seal" (Module 5) to ensure the repair holds.
