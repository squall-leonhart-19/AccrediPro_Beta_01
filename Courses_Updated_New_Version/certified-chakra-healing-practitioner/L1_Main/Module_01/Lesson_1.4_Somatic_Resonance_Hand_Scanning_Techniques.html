The Tactile Lexicon: Beyond Temperature

As your sensitivity increases, you will move beyond simple hot and cold. You will begin to notice textures in the air. These are categorized into the four primary tactile signatures of the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢:

  • Tingling (The "Static" Sensation): Often feels like tiny electrical sparks or "pins and needles." This usually indicates that energy is beginning to move or that the client is experiencing high levels of mental anxiety.
  • Pressure (The "Magnetic" Push): Feels like two magnets of the same pole repelling each other. This indicates a strong, protective "armor" around a chakra. The client may be defensive or highly guarded.
  • Stickiness (The "Taffy" Sensation): When you pull your hand away, it feels like it is moving through honey or thick syrup. This is a classic sign of energetic stasis or "sludge," often related to unproccessed grief or old emotional patterns.
  • Wind (The "Draft" Sensation): A feeling of air moving, even in a still room. This suggests a "leak" in the biofield where the client is losing energy to their environment or other people.
Coach Tip: The "Stickiness" Protocol

If you encounter "stickiness," do not try to pull through it quickly. Slow your hand movement down. This "viscosity" in the field is a signal that the client's nervous system needs a slower, more rhythmic approach to release the blockage.

The Byosen Scanning Method

Derived from traditional Japanese energetic practices, Byosen refers to the "frequency of the sickness" or "imbalance." In modern chakra healing, we adapt this into a systematic 5-step protocol:

  1. Centering: Perform the Friction Charge and take three deep breaths to ground your own field.
  2. The Hover: Place your hands 3-5 inches above the client's body, starting at the Crown.
  3. The Descent: Move your hands slowly down the midline of the body (The Sushumna Nadi).
  4. The Resonant Pause: Stop at each of the 7 major chakras for 10-15 seconds. Wait for the sensation to "bloom" in your palms.
  5. The Mapping: Mentally note the sensation (e.g., "Heat at Heart, Stickiness at Sacral") for your Alignment Strategy in Module 2.

Mapping Hand Sensations to Chakra Layers

The biofield is not a single flat layer; it is multi-dimensional. By changing the height of your hand, you can scan different aspects of the client's experience:

  • 1-2 Inches (Physical Layer): Sensations here relate to physical health, chronic pain, and cellular vitality. High heat here often correlates with actual physical inflammation.
  • 3-6 Inches (Emotional Layer): This is where you will feel "stickiness" or "pressure." It reflects the client's current emotional state and recent interpersonal stressors.
  • 6-12 Inches (Mental Layer): Sensations here (often "tingling" or "wind") relate to thought patterns, belief systems, and mental exhaustion.
Coach Tip: Professional Boundaries

Always maintain a "soft" hand. Do not tense your muscles while scanning. A tense hand is a "deaf" hand. Keep your fingers slightly separated and relaxed to allow the mechanoreceptors to function at peak capacity.
