Resonance vs. Dissonance

In bio-energetic assessment, we are looking for two primary states: Resonance and Dissonance. Understanding these is like a musician tuning an instrument.

Resonance is the state of a healthy, vibrant chakra. It feels like a smooth, consistent "hum" or a gentle outward pressure against your hand. It is coherent, meaning the energy waves are moving in sync.

Dissonance is the state of imbalance. It may feel like:

  • Static: A "prickly" or "fuzzy" sensation (Overactive/Inflamed).
  • Void: A cold or "sucking" sensation (Deficient/Depleted).
  • Density: A heavy, "thick" feeling (Blocked/Stagnant).
Coach Tip

When you first start, you might worry you're "making it up." This is normal! Trust the physical sensations in your own hands. If you feel a temperature change or a tingling, that is the bio-field interacting with your own nervous system.
