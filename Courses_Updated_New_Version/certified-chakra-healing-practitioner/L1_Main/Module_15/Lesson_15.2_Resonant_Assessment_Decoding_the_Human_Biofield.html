Scanning the Aura: Identifying Voids and Spikes

In the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, scanning is the tactile application of resonant assessment. We look for two primary types of data points in the biofield:

  2. Frequency Spikes (Hyper-activity): These feel like "heat," "pressure," "tingling," or a "pushing" sensation. This indicates inflammation, congestion, or an over-functioning chakra.

Fluctuations vs. Chronic Imbalance

A critical mistake new practitioners make is treating every energetic dip as a deep-seated trauma. We must distinguish between Temporary Fluctuations and Chronic Imbalances.

  • Temporary Fluctuations: Caused by a bad night's sleep, a recent argument, or caffeine. These usually resolve with simple grounding.
  • Chronic Imbalances: Persistent patterns that show up in the same location over multiple sessions. These are the "Root Causes" we address in the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢.
