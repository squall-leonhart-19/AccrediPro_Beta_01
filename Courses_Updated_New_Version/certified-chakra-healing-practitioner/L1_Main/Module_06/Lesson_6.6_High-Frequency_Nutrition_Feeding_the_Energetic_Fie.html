  3. Somatic Chewing: Chew each bite 20-30 times. This isn't just for digestion; it‚Äôs for sensing the vibration of the food.

Intermittent Fasting & Spiritual Clarity

While nutrition is about infusion, fasting is about purging. For the Crown Chakra (Sahasrara) to remain open and receptive to higher consciousness, the physical body must occasionally be free from the dense work of digestion.

Intermittent fasting (IF) triggers autophagy‚Äîa cellular "house-cleaning" process. Energetically, this clears the "static" from the auric field. A 16:8 fasting window (16 hours fasting, 8 hours eating) is often sufficient to heighten spiritual sensitivity without depleting the Root chakra's grounding.
