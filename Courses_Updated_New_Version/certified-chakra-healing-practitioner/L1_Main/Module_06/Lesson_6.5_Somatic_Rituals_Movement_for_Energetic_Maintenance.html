Dynamic Shaking: Dissolving Stasis in Real-Time

In the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, we use Dynamic Shaking as a primary nourishing ritual. This is modeled after "neurogenic tremors," a natural mammalian response to stress. When animals experience a threat, they shake to discharge the excess adrenaline. Humans, however, often suppress this, trapping the energy in the nervous system.

The Ritualized Shaking Technique

  1. The Base: Stand with feet hip-width apart, knees slightly bouncy (soft).
  2. The Pulse: Begin a gentle bounce from the heels, allowing the vibration to travel up the calves, thighs, and hips.
  3. The Release: Let the arms hang heavy. Shake the wrists, then the shoulders.
  4. The Sound: Add an audible exhale or "sigh" to release tension from the throat (Vishuddha).
