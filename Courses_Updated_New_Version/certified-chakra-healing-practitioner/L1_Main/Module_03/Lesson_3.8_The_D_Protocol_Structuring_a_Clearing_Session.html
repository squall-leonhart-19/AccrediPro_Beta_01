Common Mistakes to Avoid

As you begin your practice, watch out for these common "new coach" pitfalls:

  • The "Fix-It" Trap: Trying to make the client's pain go away immediately. Remember: Healing is a process of dissolving, not deleting.
  • Over-Explaining: Using too much "chakra jargon." If the client doesn't know what Manipura is, use words like "inner fire" or "confidence center."
  • Ignoring the Body: Focusing only on the client's story and forgetting to check their physical posture and breath.
  • Rushing the Release: Trying to force a breakthrough in the first 10 minutes. The energy moves when the client feels safe, not when you are on a schedule.

Success Markers

How do you know the session was effective? Look for these Success Markers:

  • Physical Shift: The client's shoulders drop, their breathing becomes deeper, or their skin tone flushes slightly (increased circulation).
  • The "Big Sigh": A spontaneous, deep exhale that indicates the autonomic nervous system has shifted from sympathetic (fight/flight) to parasympathetic (rest/digest).
  • Cognitive Clarity: The client says things like, "It doesn't feel as heavy now," or "I realized why I was holding onto that."

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is silence considered a "tool" in a chakra healing session?

Silence allows the client's biofield to settle and provides space for the subconscious mind to surface the root cause of the blockage without the interference of "talk therapy" logic.

2. What is a "Biofield Feedback Loop" in coaching?

It is the process of reflecting the client's physical sensations (like a lump in the throat) back to them, helping them connect their emotional state to their physical energy body.

3. If a client like Elena feels "invisible," which two chakras are most likely involved?

The Solar Plexus (personal power/identity) and the Throat (expression/being heard).

4. True or False: A practitioner's primary job is to "remove" the blockage for the client.

False. The practitioner facilitates the environment and awareness for the client to dissolve the blockage themselves, ensuring a permanent shift in the biofield.
