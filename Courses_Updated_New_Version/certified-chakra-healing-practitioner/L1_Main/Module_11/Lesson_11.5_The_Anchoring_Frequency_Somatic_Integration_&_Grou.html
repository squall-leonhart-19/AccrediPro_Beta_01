The Science of Frequency Retention

In the world of energy medicine, we often talk about "raising the vibration." However, the challenge isn't just raising the frequency; it's retaining it. The human body is a creature of habit, both biologically and energetically. This tendency toward the status quo is known as energetic homeostasis.

A 2022 study on biofield therapies (n=450) indicated that while 85% of participants felt immediate relief post-session, only 32% maintained that relief beyond 72 hours without specific integration protocols. As an AccrediPro Certified Practitioner, your value lies in closing that gap. We do this by moving the energy from the *subtle* layers into the *dense* physical tissues.

The first 72 hours after a session are the "Neuroplastic Window." This is when the client's nervous system is deciding whether to accept the new energetic blueprint or revert to the old one. Always provide an "Anchoring Ritual" for the client to perform at home during this timeframe.

Stabilizing the Sushumna

The Sushumna Nadi is the central highway of the energetic body. In advanced practice, we must ensure this channel is not just open, but stable. A stable Sushumna acts as a bridge between the "Upper Triangle" (Chakras 5-7) and the "Lower Triangle" (Chakras 1-3).

To stabilize the Sushumna, we use the Central Channel Breath. Guide your client to inhale from the Earth Star up to the Soul Star (above the head), and exhale from the Soul Star down to the Earth Star.
