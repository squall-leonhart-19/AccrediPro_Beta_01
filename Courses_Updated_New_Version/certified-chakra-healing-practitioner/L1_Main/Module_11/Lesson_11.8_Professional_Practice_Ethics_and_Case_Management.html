Common Mistakes to Avoid

Even experienced practitioners fall into these traps. Watch for these in your own practice:

  • Over-Explaining the Sanskrit: Using terms like Manipura or Vayu without context can make a client like Diane feel disconnected. Use the "Bridge Method": Explain the spiritual concept, then immediately link it to her physical symptom (indigestion).

  • Ignoring the Physical: If a client has chronic digestive issues, don't just talk about "energy." Ask about her diet and stress levels. A holistic practitioner looks at the whole person.
  • Downplaying Your Expertise: Avoid phrases like "I think..." or "Maybe we could try..." Use authoritative language: "What Iâ€™m seeing is..." or "Our next step is..."
