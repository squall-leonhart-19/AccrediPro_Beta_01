Success Markers

How do you know the Alignment Strategy worked? Look for these "Green Flags" in your session:

  • The "Sigh of Relief": The client takes a deep, spontaneous breath during the conversation.
  • Softened Features: Their jaw relaxes or their shoulders drop away from their ears.
  • Language Shift: They move from saying "I should" to "I feel" or "I want."
  • Physical Sensation: They report the "tightness" or "heaviness" has lessened by at least 2 points on a scale of 1-10.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "Bridge Language" important when working with professional clients like Diane?

It builds immediate credibility and safety. By using terms like "power center" or "stress response" alongside energetic concepts, you meet the client where they are intellectually while leading them toward energetic healing.

2. What is the "Sigh of Relief" a sign of in a session?

It is a physiological marker that the nervous system is shifting from Sympathetic (Fight/Flight) to Parasympathetic (Rest/Digest). This is a primary goal of the Alignment Strategy.

3. If a client is "scattered" and has "tightness in the chest," which two centers are likely misaligned?

The Solar Plexus (willpower/decision-making) and the Heart (purpose/emotional safety).

4. How much of a session should be spent on assessment vs. intervention?

Approximately 70% should be spent on assessment, listening, and validation. Rushing to the "fix" often bypasses the client's need to feel understood, which is essential for energetic shifts.
