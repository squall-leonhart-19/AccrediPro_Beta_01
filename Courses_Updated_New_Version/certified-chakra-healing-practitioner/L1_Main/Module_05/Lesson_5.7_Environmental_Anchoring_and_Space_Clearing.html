Clearing 'Vibrational Ghosts'

Spaces hold memory. A "Vibrational Ghost" is a residual energetic imprint left behind by intense emotions, trauma, or repetitive negative thought patterns in a physical room. If a client is trying to anchor "Abundance" in a room where they have spent years feeling "Scarcity," the walls themselves will attempt to entrain the client back to the lower state.

Professional space clearing involves three steps:

  • Break Up: Using sound (bells, drums, or clapping) to break up stagnant, "stuck" energy.
  • Clear: Using smoke (sage, palo santo, or incense) or salt sprays to neutralize the field.
  • Reset: Using a high-frequency intention or "sealing" the room with a crystal grid to set the new baseline.

Don't forget the corners! Stagnant energy (and dust) accumulates in 90-degree angles. When clearing a client's space, pay special attention to the corners of the ceiling and floor, as these act as "vibrational traps."
