The 'Body Lock' Technique

The "Body Lock" is a practitioner-led sequence designed to seal the meridians after an infusion of Prana (Module 4). In traditional yogic science, these are known as Bandhas, but in a clinical healing setting, we adapt them for the modern client.

1. The Mudra Seal

Mudras act as "circuit breakers" for energetic flow. By touching specific fingertips together, we create a closed-loop system that prevents frequency dissipation. For anchoring, we primarily use the Prithvi (Earth) Mudra: touching the tip of the ring finger to the thumb. This stimulates the Root frequency and provides a "heavy" anchor for higher-chakra work.

2. Postural Alignment (The Vertical Axis)

To anchor energy, the skeletal system must be aligned to handle the increased "voltage." We utilize a modified Tadasana (Mountain Pose) alignment, even if the client is lying down. Ensure the occipital ridge (base of the skull) is lengthened and the sacrum is heavy. This creates a clear "conduit" for the frequency to travel from the crown to the feet.

The 'Somatic Bridge' Exercise

The greatest challenge in frequency anchoring is the "Upper-Lower Split." Many clients have high-frequency upper chakras (intuition, heart) but "starved" lower chakras (legs, feet). The Somatic Bridge exercise creates a permanent pathway for energy to move from the ethereal to the material.

Exercise Protocol: The Somatic Bridge

Step 1: Have the client visualize the frequency in their Heart or Third Eye as a ball of light.

Step 2: On the exhale, guide them to "push" that light down the spine, through the hips, and into the large muscles of the thighs.

Step 3: Have the client flex their quadriceps and glutes for 3 seconds, then release. This "muscle firing" creates a neuro-muscular hook for the energy.

Step 4: Anchor the light into the soles of the feet, visualizing "roots" made of that specific frequency color entering the floor.
