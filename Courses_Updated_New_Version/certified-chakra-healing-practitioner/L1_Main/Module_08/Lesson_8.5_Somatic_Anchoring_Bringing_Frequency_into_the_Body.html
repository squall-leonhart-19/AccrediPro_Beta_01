Identifying Frequency Leakage

As a practitioner, you must educate your clients on how to maintain their "vibrational hygiene." Even the best session can be undone by "leaks." A 2022 study on biofield therapy efficacy noted that environmental stressors can degrade the "coherence" of a treatment within hours if the subject returns to a chaotic environment.

The 4 Primary Leakage Points:

  1. Digital Overload: Immediate exposure to high-EMF devices or social media post-session shatters the delicate integration of the Third Eye.
  2. The "Explanation" Trap: Trying to logically explain the "magic" of a session to a skeptical partner before the energy has anchored.
  3. Caffeine/Stimulants: Artificially spiking the nervous system before the new frequency has stabilized the adrenals.
  4. Boundary Collapse: Saying "yes" to a draining request immediately after a session, which "bleeds" the newly infused Prana.
