Daily Clearing Protocols: Pre and Post Session

Professional energetic hygiene requires a "clean in, clean out" approach. Just as a surgeon scrubs before and after an operation, an energy practitioner must scrub their field.

Pre-Session: The Activation Protocol

Before the client enters the room (or the Zoom call), perform the following 3-minute sequence:

  • The Pillar of Light: Visualize a column of white-gold light descending through your Crown and anchoring into the Earth. This establishes you as a conduit, not a source.
  • Setting the Intention: Explicitly state: "I am a clear channel. Only that which is for the client's highest good may pass through, and my energy remains my own."
  • The RADIANT Shield: Visualize your aura as a permeable membrane that allows information in but prevents emotional "hooks" from attaching.
Coach Tip

Always wash your hands with cold water up to the elbows before and after a session. Cold water helps break the electromagnetic "tethers" between you and the client's field.

Post-Session: The Reset Protocol

Once the session ends, it is vital to "disconnect." Failure to do so leads to energetic bleed, where you continue to process the client's issues hours later.

  • Cutting the Cords: Use a physical gesture (like a karate chop) across your Solar Plexus and Heart space to symbolically sever the session's connection.
  • Sound Clearing: Use a singing bowl or tingsha bells to "shatter" any stagnant energy remaining in your workspace.
  • Physical Movement: Shake your body for 30 seconds. This activates the lymphatic system and releases somatic resonance from the client's session.

Empathy vs. Energetic Merging

Many women entering this field are natural "empaths." While empathy is a gift, Energetic Merging is a professional liability. A 2021 study on wellness practitioners found that those who "merged" with client emotions had a 42% higher rate of secondary traumatic stress.

Professional Empathy: You understand and acknowledge the client's pain from a place of stable presence. You are the observer of the storm.

Energetic Merging: You feel the client's pain as your own. You are in the storm with them. In this state, you cannot heal them because you are now vibrating at the same frequency as the problem.
