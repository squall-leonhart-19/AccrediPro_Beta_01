Master Pendulum Protocols: Rotational Analysis

At the Master level, the pendulum is no longer just a "yes/no" tool; it is a precision instrument for Rotational Analysis. We are looking for three specific data points during the 'R' phase of the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢:

  1. Rotational Diameter: A small circle (under 2 inches) indicates Hypo-activity (Depletion). A wide circle (over 5 inches) indicates Hyper-activity (Congestion).
  2. Directional Integrity: Clockwise is the standard healthy flow. Counter-clockwise indicates an inversion, where the chakra is expelling energy rather than receiving it.
  3. Axis Wobble: If the pendulum creates an oval rather than a circle, it indicates Structural Instability in the chakra walls, often due to recent trauma.
