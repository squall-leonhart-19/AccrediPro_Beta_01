Advanced Infusion: The Maha Bandha Protocol

In Module 4, we introduced the basic Infusion of Prana. At the Master level, we utilize Maha Bandha (The Great Lock) to create a pressurized energetic environment. This pressure is not "force"; rather, it is the intentional containment of vital force to ensure it penetrates the deepest layers of the Sushumna Nadi (the central channel).

The Master Practitioner uses Maha Bandha to prevent pranic leakage during the infusion process. By engaging the pelvic floor (Mula Bandha), the abdominal lock (Uddiyana Bandha), and the chin lock (Jalandhara Bandha), you create a "closed-circuit" system that allows for a concentrated delivery of frequency.

This prevents the client from feeling "scattered" or "ungrounded" after a session.
