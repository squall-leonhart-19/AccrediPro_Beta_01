Advanced Diagnostics: Ghost Blockages

One of the most challenging aspects of master-level work is identifying Ghost Blockages. A ghost blockage is an energetic signature that appears in one chakra (e.g., the Solar Plexus) but is actually a secondary compensation for a primary "leak" elsewhere (e.g., the Root).

If you only treat the Solar Plexus, the client will feel better for 48 hours, but the symptom will return. Mastery involves tracing the "energetic thread" back to the origin. For example, chronic anxiety (Heart/Solar Plexus) is often a ghost blockage resulting from a lack of foundational safety in the Root chakra. As a Master Practitioner, you learn to look past the "loudest" symptom to find the "quietest" cause.
