Fascia is primarily composed of collagen and water, forming a liquid crystal matrix that is highly conductive of bio-electrical and bio-energetic signals.

In the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, we view the fascia as the bridge between the Annamaya Kosha (physical body) and the Pranamaya Kosha (energy body). Master Somatic Techniques

To stabilize a shift, we must engage the client's proprioception (sense of body position) and interoception (sense of internal state). Here are the three master-level anchoring techniques:

1. The Fascial Seal (Titration)

After a major chakra clearing, have the client focus on the physical area. Ask them to describe the physical sensation of the space (e.g., "warmth," "tingling," "expansiveness"). By naming the sensation, the brain creates a neural pathway to the energetic shift.

2. Tonal Anchoring

Using the client's own voice to vibrate the fascia. If you have worked on the Throat Chakra (Vishuddha), having the client hum a specific frequency while placing their hands on their chest allows the vibration to "scrub" the fascial layers, making the energetic change permanent.

3. Pendulation

This involves moving the client's awareness between the newly healed area and a "resource area" (a part of the body that feels safe and grounded, like the feet). This builds a bridge of safety, preventing the "healing crisis" or energetic overwhelm common in Level 3 work.
