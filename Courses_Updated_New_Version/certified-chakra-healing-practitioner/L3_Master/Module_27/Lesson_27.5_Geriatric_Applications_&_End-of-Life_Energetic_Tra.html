This suggests that as we age, our energy naturally begins to migrate upward in preparation for transition.

Palliative Care: Anchoring the Root

In palliative care, the primary energetic objective is the reduction of fear-based blockages. When a client faces terminal illness, the Root Chakra often goes into a state of "survival panic," which manifests somatically as increased pain perception and restlessness.

Using the A in the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, we focus on Somatic Anchoring. For the elderly, this often involves "Low-Frequency Entrainment." The Crown Chakra and Dementia

One of the most heartbreaking aspects of geriatric care is dementia. Energetically, dementia often presents as a fragmentation of the Crown Chakra. While the physical brain is deteriorating, the "Higher Self" remains intact. Our goal as practitioners is to maintain the bridge between the two.

Maintaining Dignity: Even when a client is non-verbal, their 7th chakra remains receptive. We use Lunar Rituals (Module 6) to calm the "energetic storm" that often leads to "sundowning" (late-day agitation). Gentle Crown clearing can reduce the frequency of these episodes by up to 30% according to anecdotal practitioner data.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the Root Chakra (Muladhara) the primary focus in palliative care?

The Root Chakra is the seat of survival and physical security. In terminal illness, "survival panic" or "death anxiety" creates massive blockages here, which can exacerbate physical pain and restlessness. Anchoring the Root provides the safety needed for the soul to begin its transition.

2. How does the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢ view dementia from an energetic perspective?

Dementia is viewed as a fragmentation of the Crown Chakra bridge. While the physical brain (hardware) is failing, the Higher Self (software) remains intact. Energetic work focuses on maintaining spiritual dignity and calming the "energetic storm" of the upper field.

3. What is the difference between a "Heart Connection" and a "Karmic Cord" in end-of-life care?

A Heart Connection is a healthy, frequency-based bond of love. A Karmic Cord is a heavy, tethering energy based on unresolved guilt, anger, or "unfinished business" that can prevent a peaceful transition. We clear the cords but leave the connections.

4. When should the practitioner shift focus from the "Descending Current" to the "Ascending Current"?

The shift occurs when the client is moving from the "struggle/survival" phase to the "active transition" phase. We stop anchoring them to the earth (Root) and begin facilitating their upward movement toward the Crown for the final exit.
