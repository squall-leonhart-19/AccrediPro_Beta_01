Managing Healer Burnout & Boundaries

Working with clients in chronic pain can be energetically taxing. As a practitioner, you may experience Compassion Fatigue or "Sympathetic Resonance," where you begin to feel the client's symptoms in your own body.

The 3 Pillars of Professional Boundaries:

  1. The Conduit Mindset: Remind yourself that you are the conduit, not the source of the healing energy. (RADIANT Step: Infusion).
  2. The "Glass Wall" Technique: During Resonant Assessment, imagine a clear glass wall between your field and the client's. You can see and feel their energy, but it cannot "stick" to yours.
  3. Post-Session Clearing: Use salt water or smoke cleansing (cedar/sage) between every client to reset your own field.
