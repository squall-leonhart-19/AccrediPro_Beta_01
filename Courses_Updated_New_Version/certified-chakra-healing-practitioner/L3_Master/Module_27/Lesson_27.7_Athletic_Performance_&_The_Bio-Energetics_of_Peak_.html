This cycle can lead to Adrenal Fatigue, where the body's cortisol rhythm becomes dysregulated.

A 2022 study on high-performance individuals found that those who practiced bio-energetic regulation (similar to our Nourishing Rituals) showed a 22% faster recovery of heart rate variability (HRV) compared to those who only used physical rest. Signs of Solar Plexus Imbalance in Athletes:

  • Hypo-active: Lack of "drive," premature fatigue, and slow recovery times.
