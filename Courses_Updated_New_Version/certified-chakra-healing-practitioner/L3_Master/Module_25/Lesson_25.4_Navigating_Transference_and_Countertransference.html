Managing Dependent Transference

Because energy work involves deep relaxation and a sense of being "held" energetically, clients may develop dependent transference. They may begin to see you as the sole source of their well-being, often triggered by imbalances in their Solar Plexus (willpower) or Root (safety) chakras.

Signs of dependent transference include:

  • Excessive emailing or texting between sessions.
  • Asking for your "permission" to make minor life decisions.
  • Expressing that they "cannot function" without their weekly session.
Coach Tip

When dependency arises, use the Nourishing Rituals (N) phase to hand the power back to the client. Assign them specific "Home Rituals" that emphasize their own agency. This prevents the "Guru-Disciple" trap that stalls long-term transformation.
