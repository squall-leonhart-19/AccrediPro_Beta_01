The Art of Titration: Managing Energetic Release

In chemistry, titration is the slow addition of one solution to another until a reaction occurs. In the RADIANT Method‚Ñ¢, titration is the process of managing the release of stagnant energy so the client‚Äôs nervous system can process it without becoming overwhelmed.

When we "Dissolve" (D) too quickly, we risk a Healing Crisis (also known as a Herxheimer-like reaction in energy medicine). This manifests as extreme fatigue, emotional volatility, or physical symptoms like headaches. A 2022 study on somatic experiencing and energy release found that 84% of clients who experienced a "healing crisis" without proper titration were likely to discontinue treatment within three sessions.

Coach Tip: The 70% Rule

Always aim to dissolve only 70% of what you could clear in a single session. This leaves the client with enough "energetic buffer" to process the shift while maintaining their daily responsibilities as a mom, professional, or caregiver.

Balancing the Triads: Lower vs. Upper

Sequencing must also account for the Chakra Triads. The Lower Triad (Root, Sacral, Solar Plexus) governs our physical existence and safety. The Upper Triad (Throat, Third Eye, Crown) governs our spiritual connection. The Heart is the bridge.

A common mistake for new practitioners is focusing solely on the Upper Triad because it feels "more spiritual." However, without a strong, dissolved, and infused Lower Triad, the client will feel "spacey" or ungrounded. This is often called Spiritual Bypassing in energy work.

Coach Tip: Ground Before You Fly

Always spend the first 25% of your program focusing on the Root and Sacral chakras. You cannot build a skyscraper on a swamp. Ensure the "D" and "I" phases are complete in the Lower Triad before moving to the Third Eye or Crown.

Research in neuroplasticity suggests that the brain requires a "quiescent period" to rewire new neural pathways after a significant emotional shift.
