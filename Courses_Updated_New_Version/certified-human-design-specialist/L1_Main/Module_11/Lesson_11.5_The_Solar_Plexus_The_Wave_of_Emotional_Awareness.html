The Dual Nature: Motor vs. Awareness

The Solar Plexus is unique in the Human Design system. It is currently undergoing an evolutionary transition. For most of human history, it functioned primarily as a Motor‚Äîa source of raw, biological power driving us toward food, sex, and survival. However, it is evolving into our third and most sophisticated Awareness center.

Unlike the Spleen (Body Awareness) or the Ajna (Mental Awareness), the Solar Plexus provides Emotional Awareness. This awareness is not instantaneous; it is a process that unfolds over time. As a Specialist, you must help your clients distinguish between the energy of the motor and the clarity of the awareness.

Specialist Insight

Remind your clients that "Emotion is just energy in motion." When the Solar Plexus is a motor, it feels like a physical "push" to do something. When it is awareness, it feels like a quiet "knowing" that only arrives after the wave has settled.

Defined Authority: No Truth in the Now

For the defined Solar Plexus, the mantra is simple but revolutionary: There is no truth in the now. Because the wave is always moving, any perspective gained at a single point in time is partial. Clarity is the sum of the entire wave.

When a client is at the "High" of the wave, the world looks perfect. When they are at the "Low," the world looks bleak. Neither is the truth. The truth is found in the neutral middle, which can only be reached by waiting. This is the "I" (Internalize Authority) stage of the DESIGN Framework‚Ñ¢.

The Open Solar Plexus: The Mirror and the Sponge

The 50% of the population with an Open Solar Plexus are not "unemotional." In fact, they often feel emotions more intensely than defined types because they are amplifying the emotions of those around them.

The "Avoidance of Confrontation" Trap

The most common Not-Self pattern for the Open Solar Plexus is avoiding truth and confrontation to keep the peace. Because they can feel the "heat" of someone else's anger or disappointment, they will lie, omit facts, or stay in toxic situations just to avoid the emotional backlash. This is a core part of the "E" (Evaluate Conditioning) phase.

Client Language

If you have an Open Solar Plexus client, ask them: "Are you making this choice because it's right for you, or because you're afraid of how the other person will react emotionally?" This question alone can break years of conditioning.

Coaching the Emotional Experiment

Navigating the 7-year deconditioning cycle (The "N" in DESIGN) requires practical tools. Here are three standards for your practice:

  1. The 24-Hour Rule: For minor decisions (purchases under $500, social invites), the client must wait one full sleep cycle.
  2. The Emotional Journal: For defined types, tracking the wave daily for 3 months to identify the "neutral point."
  3. The Aura Cleanse: For open types, spending 20 minutes alone in their own aura to "discharge" the amplified emotions of others.
Specialist Practice

Always check your own Solar Plexus definition before a session. If you are open, you will amplify your client's distress. If you are defined, you may be "conditioning" the client with your current wave. Awareness is the first step to professional neutrality.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the Solar Plexus considered a "dual" center?

It functions as both a Motor (biological energy/power) and an Awareness center (emotional clarity that develops over time). It is currently evolving from motor-dominance to awareness-dominance.

2. What is the primary frequency of the Tribal Wave?

The Tribal Wave operates on "Needs and Sensitivity." It ratchets up as needs go unmet and can lead to explosive releases if the "deal" or "bargain" in a relationship is broken.

3. What does "No Truth in the Now" mean for an Emotional Authority client?

It means that because their perspective is constantly changing based on their wave, any decision made in the moment is only based on a partial truth. Total clarity requires seeing the situation from the high, the low, and the middle.

4. How does the Open Solar Plexus typically behave in the Not-Self?

The Open Solar Plexus avoids confrontation and truth-telling to prevent feeling the amplified emotional "heat" or backlash from others. They become "people pleasers" to protect their own energetic system.
