The Physics of the Neutrino Stream

Human Design is often called the "New Astrology," but it is more accurately described as a mapping of subatomic imprinting. The primary agent of this imprinting is the neutrino.

Neutrinos are subatomic particles produced by stars (our Sun accounts for about 70% of the neutrinos hitting Earth). For decades, scientists believed neutrinos were massless. However, in 2015, Takaaki Kajita and Arthur B. McDonald were awarded the Nobel Prize in Physics for proving that neutrinos actually have a very tiny amount of mass.

Coach Tip: The Mass Factor

If something has mass, it can carry information. Think of neutrinos as an endless stream of "data bits" flowing through space. As they pass through planets, they pick up a "flavor" or frequency, which they then deposit into us as they pass through our bodies.

Because neutrinos are so small, they pass through nearly everything. Every second, trillions of neutrinos pass through every square inch of your body. This is the "Program"‚Äîthe constant stream of data that informs our consciousness and biological processes.
