Meet Your Client: Elena

Profile: Elena, 52, is a former high school teacher from Chicago. She recently "retired" but feels lost. She has spent her life following the rules but feels like she doesn't know who she actually is outside of being a teacher and a mother.

The Challenge: Elena is skeptical about Human Design. She wants "proof" that this isn't just another personality test. She is looking for a meaningful second career but lacks the confidence to start.

Goal: To see if a Human Design reading can give her the "permission" to do something radically different with her life.

Sarah's Insight

When working with women in their 50s, remember that they have spent decades conditioning themselves to meet others' expectations. Your job as a Specialist isn't just to read a chart; it's to help them unlearn who they were told to be.

Common Mistakes for New Specialists

The Mistake Why It Happens The Professional Correction
"Data Dumping" Over-explaining every gate and channel at once. Focus on Strategy and Authority first. Keep it actionable.
Ignoring the Client Reading the chart instead of listening to the person. Ask: "How does this resonate with your experience?" every 10 minutes.
Imposter Syndrome Feeling like you need to know 100% of HD before charging. You know 100% more than your client. You are the guide.
No Clear Next Step Ending a session without a follow-up plan. Always offer a 3-month "Integration Package" or a follow-up call.
