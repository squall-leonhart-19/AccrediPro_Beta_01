Key Takeaways

  • Strategy is the "How-To" manual for a client's energy; without it, the rest of the chart doesn't matter.
  • The goal of a Strategy session is to move the client from their "Not-Self" (Frustration) to their "Signature" (Satisfaction).
  • Effective coaching uses metaphors (like the tennis player or the boulder) to make complex concepts feel practical.
  • Practicing your script out loud builds the "muscle memory" needed to lead professional, high-value sessions.
