The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is structured into four distinct levels of mastery. We don't just dump information on you; we layer it so your confidence grows alongside your knowledge.

Tier Level Focus Area Professional Goal
L1: Foundation Modules 1-15: Core Methodology Understand the "What" and "How" of the chart.
L2: Advanced Modules 16-23: Clinical Depth Handle complex charts and specific life challenges.
L3: Master Modules 24-29: Supervision Refine your unique "voice" and specialist edge.
L4: Practice Modules 30-36: Business Build Launch your practice and sign your first 5 clients.
Sarah's Insight

Don't rush to Tier 4. Your imposter syndrome will fade naturally if you allow yourself to be a "beginner" in Tier 1. The most successful coaches are those who master the foundations before trying to build the business.

Not Just Theory: The Practice Labs

One of the biggest complaints about wellness certifications is that they provide plenty of "head knowledge" but zero "hand knowledge." You leave the course knowing the facts but terrified to talk to a client.

In this program, we use Practice Labs. Starting in Module 3, every lesson includes a real-world scenario. You will be given a chart and a client's specific problem (e.g., "I'm a Projector mom who is constantly exhausted"). You will learn to coach, not just recite a chart. By the time you reach Module 10, you will have "worked" with 20 virtual clients, making the transition to real people seamless.

The D.E.S.I.G.N. Framework‚Ñ¢

This is the backbone of your training. While others get lost in the "weeds" of Human Design, you will use our proprietary 6-step protocol to ensure every client session is transformational.

  • D - Decode: Translating symbols into human language.
  • E - Energy: Identifying where the client is leaking power.
  • S - Strategy: Giving them the "user manual" for their life.
  • I - Inner Authority: Teaching them how to make 100% correct decisions.
  • G - Guidance: The actual coaching intervention.
  • N - Nuance: Synthesis of the whole chart for a "wow" experience.
Sarah's Insight

Think of the D.E.S.I.G.N. Framework as your safety net. If you ever feel lost during a session, just go back to the next letter in the framework. It keeps you professional and focused.
