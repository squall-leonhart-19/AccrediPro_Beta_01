The Science of Neutrinos

How does the "imprint" actually happen? It isn't magic; it's physics. The information carrier is the neutrino. Neutrinos are subatomic particles produced by stars (including our Sun). They travel near the speed of light and have a tiny, infinitesimal mass.

Statistics to share with your clients:

  • Approximately 3 trillion neutrinos pass through every square inch of your body every second.
  • Because they have mass, they carry information.
  • As they pass through planets, they "pick up" the frequency of that planet and "stream" it into your body at the moment of birth (Personality) and the pre-birth point (Design).

This "Neutrino Stream" is the cosmic equivalent of a data download. It imprints your DNA with a specific energetic frequency that remains your "fixed" nature for your entire life.
