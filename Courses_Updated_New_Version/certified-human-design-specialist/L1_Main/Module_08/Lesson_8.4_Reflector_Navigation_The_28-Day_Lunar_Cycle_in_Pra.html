Mechanics of the Lunar Cycle

While the other four types are "Solar" beings, governed by the daily movement of the Sun, the Reflector is a Lunar being. Because a Reflector has no centers defined in their Bodygraph, they are inherently "open" to the transits of the planets. However, it is the Moon that provides their consistency.

The Moon moves through all 64 gates of the Human Design mandala approximately every 27.3 to 29.5 days. As the Moon enters a gate that connects to one of the Reflector's dormant gates, it creates a temporary definition. For a few hours, that Reflector might feel like a Generator, a Projector, or even a Manifestor.

Outer Authority & Sounding Boards

Reflectors do not have an Inner Authority (like a Sacral or Splenic response). Instead, they have Outer Authority. This is often the most difficult concept for new Specialists to teach.

A Reflector needs to hear themselves talk. They are not asking for your advice; they are using you as a mirror. When a Reflector speaks to a "Sounding Board," they are listening to the vibration of their own words as they bounce off the other person.

üí° When coaching a Reflector, your primary job is to be a clean mirror. Avoid saying, "I think you should..." Instead, say, "I heard you say [X] with a lot of energy, but when you mentioned [Y], your voice trailed off. Did you notice that?"

The Surprise Frequency

In Human Design, every type has a "Signature"‚Äîthe feeling that indicates they are on the right track. For the Reflector, that signature is Surprise. The Not-Self theme is Disappointment.

Because Reflectors are here to reflect the health of the "tribe" or environment, they are naturally delighted by the unexpected beauty of life. If a Reflector client says, "I was so surprised by how that meeting went!" they are in alignment. If they say, "I'm just so disappointed that people aren't better," they are likely absorbing the "Not-Self" of their environment.

Aura Health & Environment

A Reflector's physical health is almost entirely dependent on where they are and who they are with. Their aura is "resistant and sampling," meaning they don't take energy in as deeply as a Projector, but they are still highly sensitive.

The Importance of "The Place"

For a Reflector, the most important question is: "Am I in the right place?" If they are in the wrong place, they will meet the wrong people and have the wrong life. As a Specialist, you must help them evaluate their home, office, and even the grocery stores they frequent.

The Specialist's Guide to Reflectors

When you encounter a Reflector in your practice, they may feel like they are "too much" or "not enough." Your expertise provides the validation they have sought their entire lives. Remember, you are not just giving them a reading; you are giving them permission to slow down to a pace that the rest of the world finds "unproductive" but they find "essential."

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the 28-day cycle necessary for a Reflector's decision-making process?

The Moon moves through all 64 gates in approximately 28 days. This allows the Reflector to "sample" every possible energetic influence on their decision, ensuring that their final choice is not just a temporary reaction to a specific transit.

2. What is the primary role of a "Sounding Board" for a Reflector?

A Sounding Board acts as a mirror. The Reflector needs to hear the vibration of their own voice to find clarity. The Sounding Board should NOT offer advice, but rather reflect back what they hear the Reflector saying.

3. How can a Reflector determine if they are in the "wrong place"?

The feeling of chronic "Disappointment" is the primary indicator. If the Reflector feels drained, cynical, or let down by the people around them, it is a sign that the environment itself is unhealthy for them.

4. What is the "Sampling Protocol" in the context of a job offer?

Instead of saying "yes" or "no" immediately, the Reflector tells the employer they need time to consider it. Over the next 28 days, they observe how they feel about the job during different lunar transits, talking it out with others until a sense of "knowing" emerges.
