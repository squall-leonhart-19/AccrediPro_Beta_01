The Biology of Mitosis: Why Seven Years?

In Human Design, we often state that it takes seven years to decondition. This is not an arbitrary number. It is rooted in the biological reality of cellular renewal. While different cells in the body have varying lifespans‚Äîskin cells live for roughly 2-3 weeks, while red blood cells live for about 4 months‚Äîthe vast majority of the body's cellular structure, including the deeper tissues and skeletal framework, is replaced over a seven-year period.

By the end of seven years, the client is literally inhabiting a body that has never known the "Not-Self" frequency.
