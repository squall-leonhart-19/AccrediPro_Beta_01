The Reflector's 'Teasing and Sampling' Aura

Unlike the Projector's focused aura or the Generator's enveloping aura, the Reflector possesses a resistant and sampling aura. Because all nine centers in a Reflector‚Äôs bodygraph are undefined (white), they are constantly taking in the energy of those around them. However, their aura has a protective "teasing" quality that prevents them from being completely overwhelmed.

Think of the Reflector as a cosmic connoisseur. They don't just absorb energy; they sample it, much like a wine taster sips a vintage to judge its quality without swallowing the whole bottle. This allows them to act as the ultimate barometer for any environment or community.

Environment as Medicine

For a Reflector, where they are is who they are. Because they have no internal definition to "ground" them, the external environment provides the foundation for their health. If a Reflector is in the wrong room, they will be the first to get sick or feel depressed.

As a Specialist, your primary coaching question for a Reflector should always be: "Do you like the place where you are sitting right now?" If the answer is no, no amount of mindset work or strategy will help them until they change their physical location.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the Reflector's aura described as "sampling" rather than just "open"?

The "sampling" quality is a protective mechanism. It allows the Reflector to take in a "taste" of an environment or person without being completely conditioned or overwhelmed by it, enabling them to remain the "objective observer."

2. What is the minimum recommended time for a Reflector to wait before making a major life decision?

One full Lunar Cycle, which is approximately 28 days. This allows the Reflector to experience the decision through all possible temporary activations caused by the Moon's transit.

3. If a Reflector client is feeling "Disappointed," what is the first thing a Specialist should evaluate?

Their environment. Because Reflectors are mirrors, disappointment usually indicates they are in an unhealthy environment or surrounded by people who are out of alignment.

4. How does the Reflector find "clarity" if they have no Inner Authority?

Clarity is found through the process of time (the 28-day cycle) and by talking to others to hear their own voice reflected back to them in different energetic states.
