The Hierarchy of Inner Authority

In Human Design, authority is not a suggestion; it is a mechanical hierarchy. When multiple centers are defined, the bodygraph follows a specific order to determine which center gets the "final say" in decision-making. As a Specialist, your job is to ensure the client isn't following a "lower" authority while ignoring a "higher" one.

The hierarchy is as follows:

  1. Solar Plexus (Emotional) - If defined, this is always the authority.
  2. Sacral - Only if Solar Plexus is open.
  3. Splenic - Only if Solar Plexus and Sacral are open.
  4. Ego/Heart - Rare (Manifestors/Projectors).
  5. G-Center (Self-Projected) - Projectors only.
Specialist Insight

A common mistake for new practitioners is telling an Emotional Generator to "just follow their gut." For these clients, the gut gives the energy to respond, but the Solar Plexus determines the timing. Without waiting for clarity, the gut response is often just a reaction to an emotional high or low.

Solar Plexus: The Principle of "No Truth in the Now"

Approximately 50.8% of the population has a defined Solar Plexus. For these individuals, the "truth" is a moving target. Because the Solar Plexus is a motor that operates in a continuous wave, the perspective changes depending on where the individual is on that wave.

Sacral Authority: The Gut's Immediate Truth

If the Solar Plexus is undefined (open/white) and the Sacral Center is defined (colored in), the individual has Sacral Authority. This is the authority of the "Now." Unlike the emotional person, the Sacral person does have truth in the moment.

The Sacral response is a somatic, mechanical sound or movement:

  • Uh-huh: An upward, expansive sound/feeling (Yes).
  • Un-un: A downward, contracting sound/feeling (No).
  • Hmmm: A neutral state (Not now/Not enough information).
Professional Strategy

When coaching Sacral clients, avoid "Why" questions. "Why" activates the mind. Instead, use Yes/No questions. For example, instead of asking "What do you want to do for work?", ask "Do you want to start a coaching business?" Listen for the immediate gut sound before the mind can filter it.

Check Your Understanding

1. If a Generator has both a defined Sacral and a defined Solar Plexus, which one is their Authority?

The Solar Plexus. In the hierarchy, Emotional Authority always overrides Sacral Authority. They must wait for emotional clarity before acting on their gut response.

2. What is the "No Truth in the Now" principle?

It is the mechanical reality that for an emotional person, any decision made at the peak or valley of an emotional wave is likely to be distorted. Truth is only found through the passage of time.

3. How should you phrase questions for a Sacral Authority client?

Use binary "Yes/No" questions. This bypasses the mental processing of the Ajna and triggers the motor response of the Sacral Center.

4. What is the "I" in the D.E.S.I.G.N. Framework‚Ñ¢?

"Internalize Authority." It refers to the process of shifting the client's decision-making power from external logic and mental conditioning to their unique somatic Inner Authority.
