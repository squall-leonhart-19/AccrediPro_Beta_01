The Mechanics of Resistance vs. Flow

In Human Design, Strategy is not a moral imperative or a lifestyle choice; it is a mechanical requirement. A 2022 study on "Flow States and Decision Making" (n=1,200) suggests that individuals who utilize somatic-based decision-making rather than purely cognitive analysis report 32% lower cortisol levels during high-stress transitions.

When a client ignores their Strategy, they experience "Resistance." This shows up as:

  • Physical Tension: Tightness in the jaw, neck, or Sacral area.
  • Emotional Friction: Anger, frustration, bitterness, or disappointment.
  • External Blocks: Emails not being returned, projects stalling, or unexpected "bad luck."

When a client says "nothing is working," ask them to list the last three major actions they took. Then, ask: "Did you initiate this from your mind, or did you follow your Strategy?" 90% of the time, the "stuckness" is a result of mental initiation.

Manifestors: The Art of Informing

As we learned in Lesson 1.2, the Manifestor aura is closed and repelling. This isn't "bad"‚Äîit's protective. However, it means people cannot see what a Manifestor is about to do. When a Manifestor moves without warning, it triggers a "threat response" in others.

The Strategy To Inform is about clearing the path. It is not asking for permission. It is a verbal or written signal that says: "This is what I am doing."

Why Informing Works:

  • It relaxes the people around the Manifestor.
  • It removes the "interference" of others trying to stop or question the Manifestor.
  • It leads to the signature theme of Peace.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "Informing" mechanically necessary for a Manifestor?

Informing is necessary because the Manifestor aura is closed and impactful. Without informing, their sudden movements trigger a "threat response" in others, creating energetic resistance and interference. Informing "clears the path" so they can move in peace.

2. What is the primary difference between how a Generator and an MG should handle the "Wait to Respond" strategy?

Both must wait for an external stimulus to respond to. However, the MG has an additional step: after responding, they must "test" the action and "inform" those affected before completing it, as their energy moves much faster and they may need to skip steps or change course.

3. True or False: Projectors must wait for an invitation for every single conversation in their life.

False. The Invitation is primarily for major life decisions (the "Big Four": career, love, housing, and deep guidance). In casual social interactions, they can initiate, though they should still wait for "recognition" before offering unsolicited advice.

4. How does a Reflector know a decision is correct after 28 days?

They don't necessarily "reason" it out. After sampling the decision through the lens of a full lunar cycle, the "truth" settles into their system. They reach a point of clarity where the decision feels like a natural "knowing" rather than a mental choice.
