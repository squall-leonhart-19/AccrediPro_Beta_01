Mastering the Wait in Fast-Paced Roles

The modern workplace is designed for the Manifesting Generator and Manifestor‚Äîspeed is the default currency. For Projectors and Reflectors, this environment can feel like a constant state of "emergency."

The Projector's "Active Waiting"

A Projector in a high-stakes environment cannot simply sit in a corner. They must engage in Active Waiting. This involves mastering their craft and making their mastery visible so that the invitation is inevitable. In a meeting, this looks like asking the right questions rather than providing the answers too early.

The Reflector's "Lunar Buffer"

In corporate settings, a 28-day cycle for a decision is often viewed as impossible. The Specialist's job is to help the Reflector client build "Buffers." This involves negotiating timelines: "I need to run the numbers and see how this sits with the various departments; I'll have a final recommendation for you by the end of the month."
