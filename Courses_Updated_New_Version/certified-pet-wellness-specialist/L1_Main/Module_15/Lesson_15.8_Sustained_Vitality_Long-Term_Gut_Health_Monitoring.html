Common Coaching Mistakes to Avoid

The Mistake Why it Fails The Pro Fix
Over-Sciencing Using terms like "Short-Chain Fatty Acids" too early confuses the client. Use analogies (like the internet cable or telephone wire).
Promising a "Quick Fix" Microbiome repair takes 3-6 months, not 3 days. Set expectations early: "This is a journey, not a pill."
Ignoring the Parent Focusing only on the dog and ignoring Diane's stress. Validate the human: "I know how exhausting this is for you, too."
Supplement Dumping Recommending 10 products at once. Start with one foundational shift (e.g., adding a specific prebiotic).
