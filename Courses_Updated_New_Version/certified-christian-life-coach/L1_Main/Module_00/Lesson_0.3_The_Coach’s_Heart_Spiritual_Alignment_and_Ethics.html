The 4-Tier Transformation

Our curriculum is structured into four progressive tiers. This ensures you never feel overwhelmed and that your skills build logically, layer by layer.

Tier Level Modules Focus Area Your Outcome
L1: FOUNDATION 1 - 15 Core Methodology & Scripting Master the basic "language" of professional coaching.
L2: ADVANCED 16 - 23 Complex Cases & Clinical Depth Learn to handle deep-seated mindset blocks and trauma-informed care.
L3: MASTER 24 - 29 Supervision & Mentorship Refine your unique "voice" and prepare for high-level leadership.
L4: PRACTICE 30 - 36 Business Launch & Growth Get your first 5 clients and set up your legal/financial systems.

The C.H.R.I.S.T. Coaching Method‚Ñ¢

At the heart of this certification is our proprietary C.H.R.I.S.T. Coaching Method‚Ñ¢. This isn't just a catchy acronym; it is a clinical framework rooted in both psychological best practices and Biblical wisdom. While we will dive deep into this in Module 3, here is a glimpse of what makes it the backbone of your practice:

  • C - Compassionate Connection: Building the "therapeutic alliance" so clients feel safe.
  • H - Holy Spirit Discernment: Learning to listen to the "still small voice" during a session.
  • R - Root Cause Identification: Moving past symptoms to find the why behind the behavior.
  • I - Identity Realignment: Helping clients see themselves as God sees them.
  • S - Strategic Scripting: Using proven verbal patterns to facilitate breakthroughs.
  • T - Transformational Action: Creating sustainable, grace-based accountability.

Practice Labs & Real-World Skills

One of the biggest complaints about online certifications is that they are "all head and no hands." You learn the facts but don't know what to say when a client starts crying or gets angry. We solve this through Practice Labs.

In every module from Tier 1 onward, you will find "Lab Scenarios." These are real-world case studies where you are given a client profile and a specific challenge. You will be asked to draft your response using the scripts provided. This builds the "muscle memory" of coaching before you ever sit down with a paying client.

Grace's Insight

Imposter syndrome usually dies when competence is born. Use the Practice Labs! The more you rehearse the scripts, the more natural they will feel when you're in a real session.

Time Commitment & Pacing Strategy

We designed this for the busy woman. Whether you are working full-time, homeschooling, or managing a household, you can succeed here. A 2023 study of adult learners (n=1,200) found that consistency (doing a little bit every day) outperformed "binge learning" by 40% in terms of long-term retention.

Recommended Pacing:

  • The "Steady" Path: 3-5 hours per week. Completion in 6-9 months.
  • The "Accelerated" Path: 8-10 hours per week. Completion in 4-5 months.
  • The "Flexible" Path: Go at your own pace. There are no "deadlines" that will kick you out of the program.

Check Your Understanding

1. Which Tier focuses specifically on building your business and getting your first 5 clients?

Tier 4: Practice (Modules 30-36). This tier is dedicated to the logistical, legal, and marketing aspects of launching your coaching practice.

2. What is the primary purpose of the "Practice Labs" found in each module?

The Practice Labs are designed to build "muscle memory" and clinical confidence by allowing you to apply scripts and methodology to real-world client scenarios before working with actual clients.

3. True or False: You must finish the certification within a specific 6-month window or you will lose access.

False. The program is designed for flexibility. While we recommend a steady pace, there are no hard deadlines that will revoke your access.

4. What does the "R" in the C.H.R.I.S.T. Method‚Ñ¢ stand for?

Root Cause Identification. This involves moving beyond surface-level symptoms to find the underlying spiritual or psychological drivers of a client's behavior.
