Case Study: Career Pivot at 52

From Nursing to Faith-Based Coaching

Coach: Sarah M. (Former RN)

Challenge: Sarah felt "burnt out" after 30 years in healthcare. She loved helping people but hated the system. At 52, she feared she was too old to learn digital marketing or "start from scratch."

Transformation: Sarah leveraged her nursing background to specialize in "Christian Wellness Coaching" for women facing chronic illness. She didn't start from scratch; she built on top of 30 years of clinical empathy.

Outcome: Within 18 months, Sarah replaced her nursing salary, working 25 hours a week from her home office, earning $6,800/month.
