Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the "Hybrid Model" considered the gold standard for high-earning coaches?

The Hybrid Model combines high-ticket 1-on-1 coaching (for immediate cash flow) with scalable digital products or group programs (for passive income), allowing the coach to earn more without necessarily working more hours.

2. True or False: You need to work 40+ hours a week to earn a $100k salary as a sleep coach.

False. Because sleep coaches charge for outcomes (packages) rather than hourly rates, many coaches reach the six-figure mark working 20-30 hours per week.

3. What is the average price range for a standard 2-week private sleep coaching package?

Typically between $400 and $850, depending on the coach's experience, location, and the level of daily support provided.

4. How does a background in teaching or nursing benefit a new sleep coach?

These backgrounds provide "transferable skills" like empathy, clear communication, organizational ability, and authority, which allow career changers to command premium prices immediately.
