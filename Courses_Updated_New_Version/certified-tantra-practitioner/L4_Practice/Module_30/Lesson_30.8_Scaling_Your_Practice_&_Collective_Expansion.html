Section 2: The 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

A discovery call is not a free coaching session. It is a qualification process to see if you can help and if they are ready to invest. Use this structure to maintain professional authority.

Phase 1: Set the Container (0-5 mins)
Practitioner:
"Hi Sarah, I'm so glad we're connecting. My goal for today is to hear about where you are, where you want to be, and see if my 12-week Sacred Vitality program is the right bridge to get you there. Does that sound good?"
Phase 2: The Deep Dive (5-15 mins)
Practitioner:
"You mentioned feeling 'numb' in your application. Tell me, how is that showing up in your marriage right now? How does it feel when you wake up in the morning?"
Practitioner:
"And if we don't change this... if things stay exactly as they are for another year, what does that look like for you?"
Phase 3: The Prescription (15-25 mins)
Practitioner:
"Based on what you've said, Sarah, you don't need more 'information.' You need embodiment. In my program, we use Tantric breathwork and somatic clearing to move that 'numbness' out of your nervous system so you can actually feel pleasure again. I am 100% confident I can help you with this."

Section 3: Handling Objections with Grace

Objections are rarely about the money; they are usually about fear of failure. As a Certified Tantra Practitioner‚Ñ¢, your job is to hold space for their fear without taking it on yourself.

The Objection The Underlying Fear The Professional Response
"It's too expensive." "I'm afraid I won't get results." "I understand. Is it the total amount, or are you wondering if this investment will actually yield the change you're looking for?"
"Is this... sexual?" "I'm afraid for my safety/reputation." "That's a valid question. This is professional somatic therapy. All work is fully clothed and focused on your nervous system and energy. It is a safe, clinical-grade container."
"I need to ask my husband." "I'm afraid to prioritize myself." "I support that. When you talk to him, are you asking for permission, or are you inviting him to support your healing journey?"
Luna's Insight

Never defend your price. If they say it's expensive, agree! "Yes, it is a significant investment because the transformation we're doing is life-altering."

Section 4: Confident Pricing Presentation

Most new practitioners stumble here. They lower their voice or apologize. To Sarah, this signals a lack of confidence in your results. Practice saying your price in the mirror until it feels as natural as your name.

The Pricing Formula

For a 12-week "Signature Transformation" including 6 bi-weekly sessions and Voxer/Email support, a standard professional rate is $2,500 - $4,500.

The Close: Practice Out Loud
You:
"The investment for the 12-week Sacred Vitality Journey is $3,000. We can do that in one payment, or I have a monthly installment plan of $1,100. Which of those works best for your budget?"
Luna's Insight

After you say the price, STOP TALKING. The first person to speak usually loses the lead. Let Sarah process the numbers in the silence you've created.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the "Deep Dive" phase of a discovery call?

The goal is to understand the client's current pain points, the cost of staying the same, and their ultimate vision for the future, ensuring they are a fit for your specific program.

2. How should a practitioner respond to the "Is this sexual?" objection?

By validating the concern and clearly defining the professional, therapeutic, and non-sexual nature of the somatic container, emphasizing safety and clinical standards.

3. True or False: You should offer a discount immediately if a client says your price is too high.

False. You should first explore if the objection is about the actual dollar amount or a lack of certainty in the results. Discounting early devalues your expertise.

4. According to the Income Potential table, how many active clients are needed for a "Career Replacement" income (~$50k/year)?

Approximately 5 active clients at a time, assuming a signature program price point of $2,500 for a 3-month container.

Key Takeaways for Your Practice

  • Outcome Over Modality: Clients pay for the solution to their pain (reconnection), not the name of the technique (Tantra).
  • Professional Authority: Maintain a structured call to show the client you are a safe, capable leader for their journey.
  • Pricing is Energy: Stating your price clearly and without apology builds trust and demonstrates the value of the transformation.
  • The 2-Client Rule: You don't need a massive audience to be successful; you only need 1-2 new high-ticket clients per month to build a sustainable career.
