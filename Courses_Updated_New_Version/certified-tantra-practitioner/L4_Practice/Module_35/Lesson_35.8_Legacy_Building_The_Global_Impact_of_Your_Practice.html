Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary difference between selling a "session" and a "package"?

A session sells time and maintenance, whereas a package (high-ticket) sells a specific transformation and result. Scaling requires moving to a result-based model.

2. What should you do immediately after stating your price on a call?

Remain silent. This allows the prospect to process the investment and ensures you maintain the energetic lead of the conversation.

3. How do you handle the "I need to talk to my husband" objection somatically?

You coach them on the "permission" dynamic, asking if they would say yes if they knew their partner supported them, helping them reclaim their own agency.

4. Why is a group immersion considered a "scaling" move?

Because it allows you to serve multiple people in the same amount of time it takes to serve one, significantly increasing your hourly rate and impact.
