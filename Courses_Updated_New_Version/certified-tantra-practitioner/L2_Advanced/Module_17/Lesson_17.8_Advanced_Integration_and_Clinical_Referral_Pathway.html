3. Differential Considerations

Before proceeding with a Tantric protocol, we must consider what else could be driving Margaret's symptoms. This is where your clinical legitimacy is established. You are not just a "coach"; you are a practitioner who understands the body's complex signals.

  • Medical vs. Somatic: Is the pelvic pain purely somatic, or is there an undiagnosed case of endometriosis or uterine fibroids?
  • Psychological vs. Energetic: Is her anorgasmia a side effect of SSRIs (antidepressants) or a result of the "protective shielding" we identified?
