4. The 3-Phase Treatment Protocol

For a client with this level of complexity, we use a Phased Approach. Moving too quickly into "erotic expansion" will likely cause a re-traumatization or a deeper shutdown.

Phase I: Somatic Stabilization (Weeks 1-4)

Goal: Establish the "Internal Secure Base."

  • Interventions: Vagus nerve toning exercises, "Grounding" breathwork (4-7-8 technique), and boundaries/consent mapping.
  • Key Metric: Can Elena identify a "neutral" or "safe" sensation in her body for more than 30 seconds?

Phase II: De-Armouring & Integration (Weeks 5-12)

Goal: Gently melt the "Pelvic Shield."

  • Interventions: External pelvic de-armouring, vocal toning to release jaw tension (the jaw-pelvis connection), and grief ritual work for the betrayal trauma.
  • Key Metric: Reduction in chronic pelvic pain; ability to tolerate non-erotic touch without bracing.

Phase III: Erotic Reclamation (Weeks 13-20)

Goal: Rewiring the pleasure pathways.

  • Interventions: Tantric Breast Massage (for heart-womb connection), Yoni Mapping (clinical somatic touch), and Orgasmic Meditation.
  • Key Metric: Spontaneous desire returns; ability to reach "Expanded Orgasm" (somatic vs. peak).
