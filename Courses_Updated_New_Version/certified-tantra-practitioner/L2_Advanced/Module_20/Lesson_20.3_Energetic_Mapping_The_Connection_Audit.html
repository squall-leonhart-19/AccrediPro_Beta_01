The Art of Hands-Off Energetic Scanning

Before any physical touch occurs in a Tantric session, the practitioner must perform a Pranic Scan. This is not mystical guesswork; it is the systematic detection of the Human Energy Field (HEF). Research in biofield science suggests that the body emits low-level electromagnetic frequencies that can be detected by sensitive tactile receptors in the palms.

Detecting Density and Temperature

When scanning 2-4 inches above the body, you are looking for two primary indicators:

  • Pranic Density: Does the air feel "thick," "heavy," or "resistant"? High density often indicates emotional suppression or "armoring." A "hollow" or "thin" feeling may indicate depletion or exhaustion.
  • Thermal Variance: Areas of heat often correlate with "excess" or inflammation (physical or emotional). Cold spots usually indicate "stagnation" or areas where the client has "checked out" of their body.
