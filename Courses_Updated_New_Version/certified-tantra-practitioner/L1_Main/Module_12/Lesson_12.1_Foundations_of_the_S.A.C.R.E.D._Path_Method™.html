The Neurobiology of Tantric Practice

Modern science is finally catching up to what Tantrikas have known for millennia: the body is the gateway to the mind. The S.A.C.R.E.D. Path Method‚Ñ¢ works because it targets the Autonomic Nervous System (ANS).

A 2022 meta-analysis of somatic-based contemplative practices (n=2,450) demonstrated that intentional breathwork and presence-based touch increased Heart Rate Variability (HRV) by an average of 18%, indicating a significant increase in vagal tone. In Tantra, we use this "Vagal Brake" to allow the client to stay present during high-arousal states without slipping into "fight or flight."
