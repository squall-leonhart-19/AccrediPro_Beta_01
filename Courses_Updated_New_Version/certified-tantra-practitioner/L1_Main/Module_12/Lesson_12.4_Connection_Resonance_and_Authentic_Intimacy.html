Check Your Understanding

1. What is the "Third Presence" in a Tantric session?

The "Third Presence" is the unique energetic field created by the intersection of the practitioner's and client's bio-magnetic fields. It is a shared "We-Space" that facilitates healing.

2. How does mirroring signal safety to the client's brain?

Mirroring activates mirror neurons, which signals to the amygdala that the practitioner is "like" them, reducing the threat response and releasing oxytocin.

3. What is the primary difference between empathy and enmeshment?

Empathy (Resonance) is feeling with the client while maintaining your own energetic center; enmeshment (Absorption) is taking on the client's emotions as your own, leading to depletion.

4. Why is heart coherence important for a practitioner?

A practitioner in heart coherence acts as a biological "tuning fork," allowing the client's less-stable nervous system to entrain to a state of calm and safety.
