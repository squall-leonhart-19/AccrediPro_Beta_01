Common Practitioner Pitfalls

Even seasoned coaches can stumble when first integrating Tantra. Here are four things to watch for:

  • Over-Spiritualizing: Using too much Sanskrit or esoteric language before the client is ready. Meet them where they are.
  • Ignoring the Professional Background: If you were a nurse, use your medical knowledge! If you were a teacher, use your pedagogical skills. Don't hide your past; it's your legitimacy.
  • Lack of Boundaries: Feeling like you need to be the client's "friend" because the work is intimate. You are their practitioner, not their peer.
  • Rushing the Body: Trying to get a "breakthrough" in the first session. Nervous system regulation takes time, especially for women 40+.
