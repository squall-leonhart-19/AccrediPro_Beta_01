Energetic Mirroring & Mirror Neurons

Why do we "feel" what others feel? The answer lies in Mirror Neurons. These specialized brain cells fire both when an individual performs an action and when they observe the same action performed by another. In Tantra, we use Energetic Mirroring to facilitate expansion.

If you, the practitioner, embody a state of Ananda (bliss) or deep stillness, your client's mirror neurons will begin to simulate that state internally. This is why your personal Sadhana (practice) is your most important professional tool. You cannot take a client to a frequency you have not stabilized in yourself.
