Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • The "Fixer" Trap: Trying to take the client's pain away. Your job is to help them be with it until it transforms.
  • Rushing the Silence: New coaches are afraid of 10 seconds of silence. In Tantra, silence is where the energy moves.
  • Over-Explaining: Giving a 5-minute lecture on the nervous system mid-session. Keep explanations to 1-2 sentences.
  • Ignoring Your Own Body: If you aren't breathing, your client won't breathe. You are the "tuning fork."
Pro Tip

If you feel stuck, simply say: "Take a breath, and tell me what you feel in your body right now." It works 100% of the time to reset the session.
