The 'Open/Closed' Launch Model vs. Evergreen Enrollment

Choosing the right enrollment rhythm is critical for managing your energy and the group's container. In the fertility space, both models have distinct advantages depending on your business stage.

Feature Open/Closed (Cohort-Based) Evergreen (Rolling Enrollment)
Dynamics Everyone starts and finishes together. High camaraderie. New members join anytime. Ongoing community.
Marketing Focused "Launch" periods (2-3 times per year). Continuous, steady-state marketing.
Urgency Natural scarcity (Doors close on X date). Convenience-based (Start when ready).
Best For Building deep intimacy and high-priced programs. Lower-priced memberships or self-study hybrids.

Ethical Marketing: Confidentiality & Social Proof

In the fertility world, "social proof" (testimonials) is vital but legally and ethically sensitive. Many clients do not want their faces or full names associated with their fertility struggles on social media.

The De-Identification Protocol

To market ethically while maintaining Integrative Advocacy (Module 5), use the following strategies:

  • Initials and Age: Instead of "Jane Doe," use "J.L., Age 39."
  • The "Outcome-First" Headline: Focus on the physiological shift. Example: "From 60-day cycles to 29-day cycles in one cohort."
  • Screenshot Approval: If a client sends a celebratory text in your group app, ask: "This is so inspiring! May I share this anonymously with my community to give others hope?"
Coach Tip: Ethics Over Urgency

ðŸ’¡ Never use "countdown timers" that imply a woman will "miss her chance at motherhood" if she doesn't buy today. That is predatory. Instead, use timers to indicate when the bonus coaching calls or early bird pricing ends.

Pricing Strategies: Tiered Access

A group program allows you to serve multiple price points simultaneously. This is the key to financial freedom without burnout.

Tier Deliverables Price Point (Est.)
Self-Study Recorded modules + Digital workbooks only. $297 - $497
Group Cohort Modules + Weekly Group Q&A + Community Forum. $997 - $1,997
Hybrid VIP Group Cohort + (3) Private 1:1 Deep Dives. $2,997 - $4,997

By offering these three tiers, you ensure that you don't turn away a client who has a lower budget (Self-Study), while still capturing high-end clients who desire Functional Assessment (Module 1) at a deeper level (VIP).

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the "Open/Closed" launch model often recommended for new fertility coaches?

It creates natural urgency, allows for a high-intimacy "container" where everyone starts together, and helps the coach manage their energy by separating marketing phases from coaching phases.

2. Which lead magnet would most directly align with Module 6 (Lifestyle Alignment)?

A "Lifestyle Toxin Sweep" or "Endocrine Disruptor Checklist" for the home.

3. What is the most ethical way to share a client's success story without their full name?

Use initials and age (e.g., M.K., 41), focus on physiological outcomes (e.g., improved BBT charts), and always obtain written "screenshot approval" for anonymous sharing.

4. What is the primary benefit of the "Hybrid VIP" pricing tier?

It allows the coach to provide high-level, individualized support (like deep-dive functional lab reviews) at a premium price point while the client still benefits from the group community.
