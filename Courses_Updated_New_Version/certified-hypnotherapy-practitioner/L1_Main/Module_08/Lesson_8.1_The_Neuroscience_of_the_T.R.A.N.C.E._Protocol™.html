Biological Confirmation: How to Know It's Working

You don't need an EEG machine to know your client is in trance. Science provides us with reliable physiological markers:

  • Rapid Eye Movement (REM): Fluttering of the eyelids indicates the brain has entered a state similar to dreaming (Theta/Alpha).
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV): A successful induction increases parasympathetic tone, leading to a more rhythmic heart rate.
  • Muscle Tonus: A visible "drop" in the shoulders and jaw indicates the inhibition of motor neurons.
  • Respiratory Shift: Breathing moves from the upper chest to the diaphragm (abdominal breathing).
