Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the "Pre-Talk" in the T.R.A.N.C.E. Protocolâ„¢?

The primary goal is to address misconceptions, reduce anxiety (fear of the unknown), and establish the "Hypnotic Contract" where the client agrees to follow instructions.

2. If Elena continues to fidget during the induction, what should you do?

Utilize the "Pace and Lead" technique. Incorporate the movement into your suggestions, e.g., "And as you move your finger, you can imagine each movement releasing a bit more tension."

3. Why do we slow down our speech during the transition to induction?

To trigger the parasympathetic nervous system and model the state of relaxation we want the client to enter. This is part of "vocal pacing."

4. What is a "Success Marker" in a first session?

A success marker includes visible physiological changes: slowed breathing, muscle loosening in the jaw/shoulders, and the "hypnotic flush" (slight reddening of the skin).
