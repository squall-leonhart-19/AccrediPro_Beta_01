The AccrediPro Difference

Many certifications teach you "what" PCOS is. We teach you how to coach a woman through it. The difference lies in our Practice Labs.

In almost every module, you will find a "Lab" scenario. These are real-world client cases where you must apply the lesson's concepts. You aren't just memorizing that Inositol helps with insulin sensitivity; you're learning how to explain it to a skeptical client and how to troubleshoot when she forgets her dose.

The S.T.E.A.D.Y. Method‚Ñ¢

This is the "secret sauce" of our academy. The S.T.E.A.D.Y. Method‚Ñ¢ is a proprietary framework that ensures you provide consistent, high-level care every single time.

  • S - Symptom Mapping: Going beyond the diagnosis to see the whole woman.
  • T - Testing (Functional): Understanding labs (even if you can't order them, you can interpret them).
  • E - Environment: Addressing the endocrine disruptors in her home.
  • A - Adrenal Support: Managing the "Stress-PCOS" connection.
  • D - Dietary Bio-Individuality: No "one-size-fits-all" keto or low-carb.
  • Y - Yield: Tracking biomarkers to prove your coaching is working.

Time Commitment & Pacing

We designed this for busy women. Most of our students are working full-time or managing families while they study. A 2023 survey of our graduates showed that 84% completed the course while working 40+ hours a week.
