Check Your Understanding

1. Why is life experience considered a "superpower" in this specific field?

Because our target clients (women with PCOS and metabolic issues) value empathy, maturity, and the perspective of someone who understands the complexities of mid-life and hormonal health. It builds immediate trust and rapport.

2. What is the primary difference between a "generalist" health coach and a "specialist"?

A specialist has deep, focused knowledge in a specific area (like PCOS and Metabolic Health), allowing them to command higher rates, achieve better clinical outcomes, and stand out in a crowded market.

3. What should be your primary focus during the first few modules of the program?

Focus on clinical excellence and mastering the science. While the business side is important, your success as a practitioner is built on your ability to deliver real results for your clients.

4. What is the "Sarah's Vision" for this program?

To create a bridge between scientific expertise and a heart-centered practice, empowering women to reclaim their professional lives while helping other women reclaim their health.
