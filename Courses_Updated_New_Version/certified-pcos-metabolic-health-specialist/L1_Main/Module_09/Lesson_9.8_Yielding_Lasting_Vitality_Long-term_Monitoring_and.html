Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it a mistake to agree with a client that their metabolism is "broken"?

It reinforces a fixed mindset and biological hopelessness. Instead, a specialist should explain that the metabolism is "adapting" or "protecting" the body, which implies it can be retrained.

2. What is the metabolic significance of Elena's brain fog improving by 50%?

It indicates improved mitochondrial function and better glucose/ketone utilization in the brain. Since the brain is a high-energy organ, this is early physiological proof that metabolic repair is happening.

3. How should you handle a client comparing their results to a "friend on Keto"?

Acknowledge the friend's result but explain that "weight loss" is not the same as "metabolic health." Rapid weight loss often comes at the expense of muscle mass and metabolic rate, whereas your integrative approach is building long-term stability.

4. What is the "Specialist Tone" ratio recommended by Sarah?

70% Empathy (validating the client's struggle) and 30% Clinical Authority (providing the scientific explanation and the plan).

Key Takeaways for Practice Lab 9
