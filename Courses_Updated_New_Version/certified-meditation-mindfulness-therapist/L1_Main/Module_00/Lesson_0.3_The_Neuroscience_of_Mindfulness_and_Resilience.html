The S.E.R.E.N.E. Method‚Ñ¢

This is the backbone of our academy. While other coaches might wing it, you will use a proprietary, evidence-based framework that ensures consistent results for your clients. We will dive deep into this in Module 1, but here is your first look:

  • S - Stabilize: Regulating the nervous system through physiological anchors.
  • E - Enquire: Mindful investigation of thoughts and somatic sensations.
  • R - Release: Techniques for letting go of cognitive "loops" and emotional blockages.
