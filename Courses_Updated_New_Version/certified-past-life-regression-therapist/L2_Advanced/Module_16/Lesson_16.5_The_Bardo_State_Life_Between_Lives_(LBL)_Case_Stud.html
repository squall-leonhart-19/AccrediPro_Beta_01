The Death-to-Bardo Transition

Navigating the transition following a past life death scene is a critical skill for the Advanced Practitioner. Using the S.O.U.R.C.E. Framework‚Ñ¢, we move from the Somatic Release of the death scene into the Objective Observation of the soul's ascent.

In the Bardo, the client often experiences a shift from a heavy, localized physical sensation to an expansive, "weightless" state. This requires a specific linguistic shift from the practitioner to maintain subconscious stability. Instead of asking "What do you see?", we ask, "What is the quality of the light or energy around you now?"

Coach Tip: Navigating the Light

If a client experiences anxiety during the "ascent" phase, use Rhythmic Respiration. Remind them: "You are the observer now. The body has been left behind, but the consciousness is expanding. Breathe into the space between the stars."

Client: Elena, 48, Former Special Education Teacher

Elena, Age 48

Presenting Issue: Deep-seated unworthiness and resentment toward an emotionally distant mother.

During her LBL session, Elena transitioned from a 19th-century life as a pampered aristocrat into the Bardo. She observed her soul meeting with a "Guide" to plan her current life as Elena. She saw herself choosing her emotionally distant mother.

The Insight: Her soul chose this mother to force Elena to develop "Internal Validation." In her aristocrat life, she relied entirely on external praise. By choosing a mother who gave no praise, Elena was forced to find her own worth‚Äîa skill she now uses to empower special needs children.

Outcome: Elena's resentment vanished instantly, replaced by a profound sense of "mission accomplished." This shift allowed her to finally launch her private coaching practice, earning $350 per session by specializing in "Soul Contract Integration."

The Council of Elders

The "Council of Elders" (or Council of Wise Ones) is a recurring archetype in LBL research. These are highly evolved beings who assist the soul in reviewing its progress. Accessing this state requires the practitioner to hold a very high "holding space" frequency.

When the client is before the Council, the practitioner‚Äôs role is to facilitate a dialogue. We use the Uncovering the Narrative (U) phase to ask questions such as:

  • "What was the primary lesson the soul intended to learn in the life just reviewed?"
  • "How does the current life (the one the client is living now) serve the soul's long-term evolution?"
  • "What 'Specialization' is the soul currently training for?"
Coach Tip: The Practitioner's Presence

Clients often feel "judged" when they first see the Council. Remind them: "These beings are not judges; they are the ultimate supporters. They see your struggles with total compassion."

Identifying Soul Specializations

Just as we have professions on Earth, many clients discover they have "Soul Specializations" in the Bardo. This is particularly empowering for women in their 40s and 50s who feel they are "starting over" in their careers.

Common specializations include:

  • Healers: Souls who specialize in repairing energetic fractures (often nurses or therapists in current life).
  • Nurturers: Souls who specialize in the development of young souls (often teachers or mothers).
  • Guides/Messengers: Souls who bridge information between dimensions (often writers or public speakers).
  • Architects: Souls who design the "geometry" of future incarnations.

Stat Check: A 2022 survey of 500 LBL practitioners found that 82% of clients reported a "significant increase in life satisfaction" after identifying their soul specialization, even if they did not change their physical job.

Advanced Somatic Induction (S) for Inter-Life Frequency

Reaching the Bardo requires a deeper trance state than standard PLR. While PLR often exists in the Theta frequency (4-7 Hz), the LBL state often touches the Gamma frequency (30-100 Hz)‚Äîa state of high-level information processing and "Epiphany."

To facilitate this, we use Advanced Somatic Induction (S) techniques:

  1. The Somatic Bridge: Carry the sensation of "lightness" from the death scene and expand it into the entire body.
  2. Pineal Gland Focus: Direct the client's internal gaze to the "Third Eye" point to stimulate the visual cortex for higher-dimensional imagery.
  3. Vibrational Matching: Use your voice to match the "frequency" of the space the client describes. If the space is "vast," slow your speech and increase the space between words.
Coach Tip: Stability is Key

If the client begins to "drift" or lose the connection, use Somatic Anchoring. Ask them to feel the weight of their physical body in the chair while simultaneously observing the light of the Bardo. This "dual awareness" prevents the subconscious from snapping back into Beta state.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary difference in practitioner questioning between a Past Life scene and a Bardo/LBL scene?

In a Past Life scene, we focus on sensory details and narrative (Objective Observation of the environment). In a Bardo scene, we focus on the quality of energy, the "Why" behind choices, and the dialogue with higher consciousness (The Council or Guides).

2. Why might a client choose a life with significant physical or emotional challenges during the planning phase?

From the soul's perspective, challenges are "accelerators" for growth. A soul may choose a difficult life to master a specific virtue (like resilience or self-worth) that it failed to learn in a "comfortable" previous life.

3. How does identifying a "Soul Specialization" benefit a career-changing woman in her 50s?

It provides a sense of "Legitimacy" and "Continuity." It helps her realize that her previous career (e.g., nursing) was simply a physical expression of her soul's eternal specialization (e.g., Healer), making the pivot to PLR therapy feel like a natural evolution rather than a "start over."

4. Which brainwave state is most associated with the "Epiphany" and high-level information processing found in LBL sessions?

The Gamma frequency (30-100 Hz). While the client remains in a deep Theta trance, the "download" of soul information often triggers Gamma spikes.

Key Takeaways

  • The Bardo/LBL state provides the "Why" behind the "What" of past and current lives.

  • Advanced Somatic Induction must bridge the gap between deep relaxation (Theta) and high-level insight (Gamma).
