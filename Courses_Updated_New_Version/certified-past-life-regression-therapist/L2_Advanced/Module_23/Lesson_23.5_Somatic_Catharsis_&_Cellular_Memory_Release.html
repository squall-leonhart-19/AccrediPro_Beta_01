Managing High-Intensity Abreactions

An abreaction is the emotional and physical purging of a traumatic event. In Level 2 work, these can be intense. Safety is paramount. You must monitor the client's "Window of Tolerance."

  • Hyper-arousal: Rapid breathing, screaming, thrashing. Action: Use grounding touch (with prior consent) on the shoulder and slow the breathing.
  • Hypo-arousal: Dissociation, freezing, becoming "glassy-eyed." Action: Bring them back to the "Observer Stance" immediately.
