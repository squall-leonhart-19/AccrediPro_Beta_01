The 'Family Tree Karma' Audit

This is a critical distinction in modern therapy. Is the trauma Ancestral (DNA-based) or Karmic (Soul-based)? A 2020 meta-analysis of epigenetic studies (n=5,400) found that trauma can be passed down through at least three generations. However, Past Life Regression addresses the individual soul's journey.

Inherited vs. Incarnated Patterns

  • Inherited: Patterns shared by siblings and parents (e.g., a family-wide fear of poverty following a Great Depression experience).
  • Incarnated: Patterns unique only to the client (e.g., the client has an intense fear of fire, but no one else in the family does).

The KPI‚Ñ¢ includes a "Family Contrast" section where you document these differences. This helps the client realize that while they love their family, their soul's homework is uniquely theirs.
