The Transition: From 'R' to 'C'

In Module 4, we focused on Resolving the Karma (R) through cognitive reframing and understanding the "why" behind the patterns. However, understanding is rarely enough for permanent change. A client may know why they fear success (Resolution), but they may still feel a physical constriction in their throat when they try to speak up (Catharsis/Release).

The S.O.U.R.C.E. Framework‚Ñ¢ teaches that resolution is the *legal* settlement of the karmic debt, while clearing is the *physical* removal of the debt collector from the property. Without this soul-level clearing, the energetic "echo" of the past life continues to resonate in the client's current biofield.

Check Your Understanding

1. What is "Soul Shrapnel"?

Soul Shrapnel refers to lingering energetic fragments or frequencies of trauma (often from violent deaths) that remain embedded in the subtle body/somatic field of the client in the current life.

2. Why is the Higher Self's "witnessing" essential for catharsis?

The Higher Self provides validation that the karmic cycle is complete. This gives the subconscious mind permission to stop the repetitive processing (healing loops) of the trauma and allows for permanent integration.

3. True or False: Understanding the reason for a karmic pattern (Resolution) is always enough to stop the physical symptoms.

False. Resolution (R) provides the cognitive framework, but Cathartic Release (C) is required to clear the somatic and energetic imprints from the biofield.

4. What is the primary purpose of the verbal revocation formula?

The formula uses the client's sovereign will and conscious authority to formally cancel outdated soul contracts or vows that are still energetically active and causing stagnation in the present life.
