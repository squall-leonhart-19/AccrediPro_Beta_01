Check Your Understanding

1. Linda expresses fear that she "won't see anything." What is the best coaching response?

The best response is to normalize the fear and explain that the subconscious already knows the way. You should redirect her focus to a physical sensation (like the tightness in her chest) to bypass the logical mind.

2. According to the "Common Pitfalls" table, why should you avoid "Why" questions during a Deep Dive?

"Why" questions trigger the analytical, logical mind (Beta waves), which can pull the client out of the subconscious, experiential state (Theta waves).

3. What is the "Paced Exhale" technique?

It is the practice of the therapist speaking only while they are exhaling. This naturally slows down the speech and creates a calming, rhythmic cadence for the client.

4. If a client like Linda reports a physical sensation of "tightness," how can you use it in the regression?

Use it as an "anchor" or "doorway." Ask the client to focus on that specific feeling and let it carry them back to the very first time their soul experienced that exact sensation.
