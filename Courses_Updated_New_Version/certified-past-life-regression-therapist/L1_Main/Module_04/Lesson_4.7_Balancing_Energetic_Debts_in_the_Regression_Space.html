The 'Scale of Balance' Visualization

The Scale of Balance is a diagnostic and therapeutic tool used while the client is in a deep Theta state. It bypasses the logical mind's excuses and goes straight to the soul's perception of "fairness."

In the regression space, you ask the client to visualize a grand set of scales. One side represents the Action (the karma created), and the other represents the Resolution (the learning or restitution). Often, the client will see the scales heavily tipped, representing an energetic debt that they have been carrying into their current life.

Coach Tip

If the client sees the scales as stuck or rusted, this indicates deep-seated guilt or a refusal to forgive oneself. Use the Somatic Bridge (Module 1) to ask where that "rust" is felt in the physical body before proceeding with the balancing.

Symbolic Restitution: The Act of Service

Subconscious logic operates through symbols. If a client feels they "owe" something to a soul they harmed in a past life, simply saying "I forgive myself" is often insufficient. The psyche requires a symbolic act of restitution.

This is not about literal money or physical labor in the present; it is an energetic "payment" performed in the regression. Common forms include:

  • The Gift of Wisdom: Offering the "past self" the knowledge they lacked that led to the mistake.
  • Energetic Restoration: Visually rebuilding what was destroyed (e.g., a home, a reputation, a relationship).
  • The Soul Apology: A direct heart-to-heart communication between the client's soul and the soul of the "victim."

Cutting the Cords: Disconnecting from Tethers

Once the debt is balanced, the karmic tether must be severed. Many practitioners make the mistake of cutting cords before the lesson is learned. In the S.O.U.R.C.E. Framework‚Ñ¢, we only cut the cord after the Scale of Balance is level.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why must the 'Scale of Balance' be leveled before cutting a karmic cord?

Because cutting a cord without learning the lesson or balancing the debt often results in the "karmic momentum" recreating a similar situation in the client's current life. The lesson is the "key" that unlocks the tether.

2. What is the primary purpose of 'Symbolic Restitution'?

To satisfy the subconscious mind's need for justice and "fairness" through a symbolic act of service or giving, which allows the psyche to release the burden of guilt.

3. Who are the three archetypal figures typically found on the 'Karmic Board'?

Justice, Mercy, and Wisdom. These archetypes help provide an "authoritative verdict" that the client's debt is paid.

4. How does 'Energetic Sovereignty' protect the client after the session?

It anchors the realization that they are whole and complete, preventing them from entering into new "unbalanced" soul contracts or codependent energetic exchanges.
