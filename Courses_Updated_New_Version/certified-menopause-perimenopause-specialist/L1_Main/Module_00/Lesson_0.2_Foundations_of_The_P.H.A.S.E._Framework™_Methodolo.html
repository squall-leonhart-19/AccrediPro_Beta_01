The Math That Makes Sense

Many women feel overwhelmed by the idea of a "six-figure business." Let's break it down into bite-sized, achievable numbers. You don't need thousands of followers; you need a small group of women who value your expertise.

  • Low Volume / High Touch: 10 clients x $500/month = $5,000/month ($60k/year)
  • Moderate Volume: 15 clients x $700/month = $10,500/month ($126k/year)
  • The Group Model: 25 women x $400 (one-time program) = $10,000 per launch

Consider this: There are roughly 50 million women in the US currently in perimenopause or menopause. You only need 15 of them to reach a six-figure income. The math is on your side.

Realistic Timeline to Income

Building a professional practice takes time. Here is what a typical trajectory looks like for an AccrediPro student:

Months 1-3: The Foundation
Focus on your studies. Start "beta-testing" your knowledge with 2-3 practice clients (friends or family) for free or low-cost in exchange for testimonials.

Months 4-6: The Launch
Certification in hand. You land your first 3-5 paying clients. You are likely earning $1,500 - $3,000/month. You are refining your "Signature Offer."

Months 7-12: The Momentum
Referrals start coming in. You increase your rates. You may start your first small group program. Income stabilizes at $5,000 - $8,000/month.

Year 2+: The Scale
You are known as the go-to specialist in your niche. You may hire an assistant, launch a digital course, or partner with local doctors. Income potential: $10,000 - $25,000+/month.

Coach Sarah's Tip

The fastest way to reach the $5k/month mark is to pick a specific "niche within the niche." Are you the menopause specialist for runners? For corporate executives? For women with thyroid issues? Specificity equals higher fees.
