Common Practitioner Pitfalls

When you're starting out, it's easy to fall into "Old School" astrology traps. Avoid these four mistakes to maintain your professional standing:

  • The "Bad News" Tone: Saying "Oh, you have a square, that's going to be hard." This triggers the client's stress response and shuts down learning.
  • Over-Technicality: Spending 10 minutes explaining what a 90-degree angle is instead of explaining how it feels in the client's life.
  • The "Advice" Trap: Telling the client exactly what to do (e.g., "You should quit your job") instead of helping them use their own chart's energy to make the decision.
  • Ignoring the Signs: Focusing only on the planets and forgetting that a square in Fire/Water (like Elena's) feels very different than a square in Earth/Air.
