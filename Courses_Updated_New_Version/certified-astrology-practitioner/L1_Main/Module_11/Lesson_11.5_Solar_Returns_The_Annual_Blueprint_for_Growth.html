High-Impact Years: Planets on Angles

In astrology, the Angles (1st, 4th, 7th, and 10th house cusps) are the most sensitive points. When a planet in the Solar Return chart is within 5 degrees of an angle‚Äîeither an SR angle or a Natal angle‚Äîthat planet becomes a "Lead Actor" for the year.

  • Uranus on the Descendant (7th): Sudden changes or disruptions in partnerships and marriage.

Check Your Understanding

1. If a client is celebrating their birthday in a different city than their birth city, which location should you use for a professional Solar Return calculation?

Most professional practitioners use the client's current residence (or location at the time of the return) to ensure the house placements and angles accurately reflect the client's immediate environment.

2. What does a Solar Return Sun in the 10th House usually signify?

It signifies a year where career, public reputation, and professional achievements are the primary focus and source of vitality for the client.

3. A client has Saturn exactly conjunct the Solar Return Ascendant. What "persona" are they likely to adopt this year?

They are likely to adopt a serious, disciplined, and responsible persona. This is often a year of hard work, setting boundaries, and assuming new duties.

4. Why is the Solar Return considered a "software update" to the Natal Chart?

Because it doesn't change the underlying "DNA" (Natal Chart), but it provides a temporary set of themes, challenges, and opportunities that are specific to that 12-month cycle.
