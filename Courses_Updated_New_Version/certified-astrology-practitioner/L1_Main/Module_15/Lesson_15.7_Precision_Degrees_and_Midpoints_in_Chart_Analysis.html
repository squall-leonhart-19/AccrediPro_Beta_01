The Anaretic Degree (29¬∞): The Point of Crisis

In astrology, the 29th degree (29¬∞00' to 29¬∞59') is known as the Anaretic Degree. It represents the very end of a sign's journey. Think of it as the "Senior Year" of a zodiac sign‚Äîthe planet has learned all it can and is preparing to leap into a completely new elemental environment.

Clients with planets at 29¬∞ often exhibit a specific psychological profile:

  • Urgency: A feeling that time is running out to master that planet's function.

  • Indecision: Because the planet is about to change signs, the client may vacillate between the old sign's traits and the new one's promise.

Introduction to Midpoints: The Hidden Geometry

Midpoints are the mathematical "halfway points" between any two planets. If Planet A is at 10¬∞ Aries and Planet B is at 20¬∞ Aries, the midpoint is 15¬∞ Aries. While this sounds like simple math, in Professional Astrology, midpoints represent the synthesis of two energies.

As an expert, you use midpoints to find "hidden aspects." If a client has no direct aspect between Mars and Venus, but their Sun sits exactly on the Mars/Venus midpoint, that client will express their identity (Sun) through the blend of passion (Mars) and relationship (Venus).

Calculating the Sun/Moon Midpoint:
