The 4-Tier Transformation

We don't just teach you how to read a chart; we teach you how to be a practitioner. Our curriculum is divided into four distinct phases of growth, ensuring you never feel overwhelmed but always feel challenged.

Tier Focus Outcome
L1: Foundation (Mod 1-15) The "Alphabet" of the Stars. Symbols, signs, and planetary archetypes. You can explain a birth chart's core components with 100% accuracy.
L2: Advanced (Mod 16-23) The "Sentence Structure." Complex aspects, transits, and timing. You can identify "why" a client is experiencing a specific life challenge right now.
L3: Master (Mod 24-29) The "Nuance." Counseling skills, ethics, and intuitive synthesis. You move from "reading data" to "holding space" for profound client transformation.
L4: Practice (Mod 30-36) The "Business." Marketing, pricing, and client acquisition. You launch your professional brand and book your first three paid clients.
