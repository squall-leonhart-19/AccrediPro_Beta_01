    Realistic Income Timeline

    A career change doesn't happen overnight, but it can happen faster than you think. Here is the typical trajectory for an ambitious student in our program:

    • Months 1-3 (The Foundation): Focus is on certification. You may take "practice clients" for $50-$75 to build confidence. Revenue: $200-$500/mo.
    • Months 4-6 (The Launch): You are certified. You raise rates to $150/session. You sign your first 3-month package clients. Revenue: $1,500-$3,500/mo.
    • Months 7-12 (The Momentum): Word of mouth spreads. You increase rates to $250/session. You launch your first small group workshop. Revenue: $5,000-$8,000/mo.
    • Year 2+ (The Scale): You have a waitlist. You move to a "Package Only" model ($1,500+ per client) or launch a signature online course. Revenue: $10,000-$20,000+/mo.

    The Math of a $100k Practice

    Let's demystify the "six-figure" dream. It isn't magic; it's simple math. To earn $8,333 per month (approx $100k/year), you have several options:

    • Option A (High-End Consulting): 4 clients per month on a $2,100 "Transformation Package" = $8,400
    • Option B (The Volume Model): 42 individual sessions at $200 each = $8,400 (Approx 10 sessions a week)
    • Option C (The Hybrid): 15 individual sessions ($3,000) + 1 Group Program with 12 people at $450 ($5,400) = $8,400
    Sarah's Insight

    Option C is my favorite for beginners. It prevents burnout by mixing 1-on-1 work with the community energy of a group program. It also creates a "ladder" where group members eventually become high-paying private clients.

    Check Your Understanding

    1. Why is the "Hybrid Model" often recommended for practitioners transitioning from a full-time career?

    The Hybrid Model balances the steady income of 1-on-1 sessions with the scalability of group programs or digital products. This prevents burnout while allowing the practitioner to build multiple revenue streams and reach different price points in the market.

    2. True or False: You must wait until you are "an expert" with 10 years of experience before charging $200+ per session.

    False. Your value is based on the results and clarity you provide the client, not just your years in the field. Your previous professional life experience (nursing, teaching, etc.) adds immediate "expert" authority to your interpretations.

    3. What is the primary financial benefit of moving from "single sessions" to "3-month packages"?

    Packages create predictable recurring revenue and reduce the constant need for marketing. They also ensure better client outcomes because you have the time to go deeper into their chart and life transitions.

    4. Based on the "Math of $100k," how many $2,100 packages do you need to sell per month to reach a six-figure income?

    You only need to sign 4 clients per month to reach approximately $100,000 in annual revenue. This highlights why high-value packaging is the fastest route to financial freedom.
