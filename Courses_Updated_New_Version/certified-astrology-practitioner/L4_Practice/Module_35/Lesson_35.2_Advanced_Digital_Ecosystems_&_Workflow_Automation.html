Standardizing 'Plot Temporal Transits' Reports

Scaling requires moving from "bespoke" to "standardized excellence." In the 'P' phase (Plot Temporal Transits), automation allows you to provide ongoing value without manual checking of the ephemeris daily.

Dynamic Content Delivery: Using "conditional logic" in your email marketing software (like ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit), you can tag clients based on their planetary placements. When a major transit occurs (e.g., Saturn entering a new sign), the system automatically sends a personalized (but automated) briefing to all clients with that specific placement.

Coach Tip

Don't automate the interpretation, automate the notification. Let the system tell them the transit is happening, and invite them to a "Transit Deep Dive" session to discuss the personal impact. This creates a natural upsell loop.
