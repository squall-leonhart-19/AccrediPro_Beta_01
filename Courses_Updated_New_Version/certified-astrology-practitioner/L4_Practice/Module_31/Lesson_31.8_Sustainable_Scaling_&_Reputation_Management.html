Step 2: The 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

A discovery call is not a free reading. It is a consultation to see if you are a fit. Use this structure to stay in the driver's seat.

Phase 1: Rapport & Framing (0-5 mins)

YOU:

"Diane, it's so great to connect! I loved your question at the workshop about Saturn Returns. Before we dive in, let me share how this call works. I want to hear where you're at, what you're looking for, and if I think I can help, I'll explain how we can work together. If not, I'll point you toward someone who can. Does that sound fair?"

Phase 2: The Deep Dive (5-20 mins)

YOU:

"You mentioned feeling 'purposeless' in your corporate role. If we were sitting here a year from now and you felt completely lit up by your work, what would have changed?"

YOU:

"And what has stopped you from making that move already? Is it fear, or just not knowing which direction to point the car?"

Phase 3: The Prescription (20-25 mins)

YOU:

"Based on what you've shared, I'm confident I can help. In my Aligned Action Program, we don't just look at your chart; we use your specific planetary timing to map out exactly when you should launch this new venture. We'll move from 'stuck' to 'strategic' in 90 days."

Step 3: Handling Objections with Grace

When a client says "no" or "maybe," they are usually expressing a fear. Your job is to help them navigate that fear.

The Objection The Professional Response
"It's a lot of money right now." "I understand. Is it that the funds aren't available, or are you unsure if the investment will get you the result you need?"
"I need to talk to my husband." "Of course. If he says 'do whatever you think is best,' what would your gut tell you to do right now?"
"I've tried astrology before and it didn't help." "I hear you. Most readings are passive. My practice is active‚Äîwe use the chart to build a 90-day action plan. Does that feel different to you?"
Sarah's Insight

Never lower your price on the call. If they truly can't afford the package, offer a smaller "Starter Session" or a payment plan. Protect your value.

Step 4: Presenting Your Prices

Stop saying "I think I charge..." Instead, use the Power Pause. State your price and then stop talking.

The Pricing Dialogue

YOU:

"The investment for the 3-month Aligned Action container is $1,500. We can do that in one payment, or three monthly payments of $550. Which works better for your budget?"

(Then, stay silent. Let Diane speak first.)

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary purpose of a discovery call?

To determine if there is a professional fit and if you can provide the transformation the client is seeking, NOT to give a full astrology reading.

2. If a client says "I need to think about it," what is the best follow-up question?

"I completely understand. To help you think it through, is there a specific part of the program or the investment you're feeling hesitant about?"

3. Why should you avoid "discounting" your price on a call?

It devalues your expertise and creates an unequal power dynamic. Instead, offer payment plans or a smaller scope of work.

4. What is the "Power Pause"?

The act of stating your price clearly and then remaining silent to allow the prospect to process the information and respond first.

Key Takeaways

  • Transformation Over Information: Clients pay for the results you facilitate, not the astrological data you provide.
  • Control the Frame: Use a structured script to guide the prospect from pain to possibility.
  • Confidence is Currency: State your prices clearly and without apology; your certification is your proof of value.
  • Scalability: A thriving practice is built on packages (containers), not just one-off sessions.
