Planetary Pictures: The A/B = C Formula

A "Planetary Picture" occurs when a third planet (C) sits at the midpoint of two other planets (A and B). This is written as A/B = C.

Think of it as a chemical reaction:

  • Planet A: The Subject (What?)
  • Planet B: The Action (How?)
  • Planet C: The Result or Point of Manifestation

For example, if a client has Mars/Saturn = Sun, they possess a "hard-work" signature. Mars (energy) + Saturn (discipline) = Sun (identity). This person likely feels they must struggle to exist, or they possess extraordinary endurance. A 2022 survey of professional practitioners found that identifying just three key planetary pictures increased client "resonance" scores by over 40%.

Don't overwhelm the client with every midpoint. Focus on midpoints involving the Sun, Moon, Ascendant, and MC. These are the "Personal Points" that translate into immediate life experiences.
