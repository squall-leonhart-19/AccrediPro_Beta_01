Preparing for the Certification Exam

You are ready. The exam is the final "rite of passage" into your professional life. Here is what you need to know:

  • Format: 20 Multiple Choice Questions.
  • Passing Score: 80% (16 correct answers).
  • Focus Areas: Review the 4 Pillars of Inner Child Healing, the role of the Vagus Nerve, and the Reparenting Framework.
  • Strategy: Read each question twice. Often, the "most trauma-informed" answer is the correct one.
