Common Mistakes for New Coaches

  • Colluding with the client's fear: When a client says "I can't," don't say "I understand why you can't." Say, "I hear that your inner child feels like she can't. What does your Healed Adult know?"
  • Focusing on "How" too early: Manifestation is about the "What" and "Why." If you get bogged down in tech and logistics during a healing session, you lose the energetic momentum.
  • Ignoring the Body: If a client is saying manifestation affirmations but their shoulders are up to their ears, the nervous system is in a "freeze" state. Always bring it back to the breath.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why does Elena feel like a "fraud" despite having the credentials?

Her nervous system is experiencing "The Safety Snap-Back." Her inner child associates visibility with the criticism she received from her father, making professional success feel "unsafe."

2. What is the primary difference between Scarcity and Manifestation language?

Scarcity language is rooted in "if," "need," and "fear of lack." Manifestation language is rooted in "when," "am," and "abundance of value."

3. If a client is in a "freeze" state, what should the coach prioritize?

Prioritize somatic regulation (the body). Use breathwork or grounding exercises to bring the client out of the "freeze" state before attempting visualization or manifestation work.

4. What is a "Success Marker" in a manifestation-focused session?

A physiological shift (deepened breath), a language shift (using "when" instead of "if"), and a commitment to a small, "scary" action step.
