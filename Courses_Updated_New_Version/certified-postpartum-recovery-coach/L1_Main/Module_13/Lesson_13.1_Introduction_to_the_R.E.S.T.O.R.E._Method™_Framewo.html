The Evolution of Postpartum Support

Historically, postpartum care in the West has focused almost exclusively on infant survival. While infant mortality rates have improved, maternal health outcomes have stagnated or declined. A 2023 meta-analysis (n=12,400) revealed that up to 40% of mothers feel "abandoned" by the medical system after the 6-week checkup.

The R.E.S.T.O.R.E. Method‚Ñ¢ was developed to address the biopsychosocial gap in care. We are moving away from "bounce back" culture and toward regenerative recovery.
