Internal Coherence: The Heart-Brain-Gut Axis

In sound healing, coherence is not just a feeling; it is a measurable physiological state. When we apply harmonic resonance, we are essentially "tuning" the three primary intelligence centers of the body: the brain (cephalic), the heart (cardiac), and the gut (enteric).

A 2021 study published in the Journal of Psychophysiology demonstrated that specific low-frequency sound stimulation increases Heart Rate Variability (HRV), a primary marker of autonomic nervous system health. When HRV is high and rhythmic, the heart sends signals to the brain that "all is well," allowing the prefrontal cortex to come online while the amygdala (fear center) deactivates.
