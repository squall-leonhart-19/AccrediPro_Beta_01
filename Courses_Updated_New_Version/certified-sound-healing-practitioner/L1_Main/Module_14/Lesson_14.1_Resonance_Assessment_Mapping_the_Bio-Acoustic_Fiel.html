3. Somatic-Acoustic Signatures

Every physical symptom has a "sonic signature." Research in Cymatics shows that healthy tissue vibrates at a coherent frequency, while diseased or stressed tissue exhibits chaotic vibrational patterns. As a practitioner, you are training your brain to recognize these patterns.

  • Inflammation: Often sounds "busy" or "high-pitched." It feels like a buzzing sensation under the palms.
  • Stagnation (Fibrosis/Scars): Sounds "dull" or "thuddy." It feels like a hard wall in the biofield.
