Managing the 'Healing Crisis'

When you neutralize dissonance, you may trigger a Healing Crisis (sometimes called a Herxheimer-like emotional reaction). This is a sign that the intervention is working, but it requires skilled management to ensure the client feels safe.

Signs of an emotional release during a session include:

  • Rapid Eye Movement (REM) under closed lids.
  • Spontaneous sighing, yawning, or weeping.
  • Temperature fluctuations (sudden chills or heat).
  • Somatic shaking or "tremoring" (the body discharging stored survival energy).
