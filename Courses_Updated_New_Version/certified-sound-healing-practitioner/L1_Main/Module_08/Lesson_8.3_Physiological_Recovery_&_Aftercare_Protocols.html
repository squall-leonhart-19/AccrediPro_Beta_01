Check Your Understanding

1. Why is hydration considered the "non-negotiable" of sound healing aftercare?

Water is the primary conductor for sound. Dehydration increases "acoustic resistance" in the interstitial fluid, making it harder for the body to entrain to and integrate the frequencies.

2. What is the physiological cause of a "Sonic Detox"?

It is caused by the stimulation of the lymphatic system and the release of metabolic/cellular waste and stored emotional tension (amygdala release) during the session.

3. Which mineral is most critical for maintaining the "relaxed state" of the nervous system post-session?

Magnesium. It acts as a natural calcium-channel blocker, helping muscles and nerves remain in a parasympathetic (relaxed) state.

4. What is "Vibrational Shock"?

A state of physiological or emotional overwhelm that occurs when a client is ungrounded or dehydrated following a high-frequency intervention, causing the body to struggle with the new state of coherence.
