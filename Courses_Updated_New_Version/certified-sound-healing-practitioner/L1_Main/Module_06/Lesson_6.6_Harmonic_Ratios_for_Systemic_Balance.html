Creating Sonic Envelopes: Layering for Coherence

A Sonic Envelope is a technique where the practitioner layers three or more frequencies to create a "container" of sound that holds the client in a state of balance. Unlike a single tone, an envelope provides a rich spectrum that nourishes multiple systems simultaneously.

To create a standard Alignment Envelope, follow this layering protocol:

  • The Foundation (The Root): A low-frequency drone (e.g., 64Hz or 128Hz) to ground the physical body.
  • The Heart (The Ratio): A Perfect Fifth (3:2) layered over the foundation to trigger systemic homeostasis.
  • The Crown (The Shimmer): A high-frequency overtone (e.g., a 2:1 octave of the Fifth) to encourage spiritual expansion.
