Spinal Alignment Protocols

To achieve precise vertical alignment, we utilize both Weighted and Unweighted tuning forks. Weighted forks are used directly on the body for osteophonic (bone-conducting) resonance, while unweighted forks are used in the biofield.

The 3-Point Spinal Protocol:

  1. The Sacral Anchor: Place a 128Hz (weighted) fork on the sacrum. This grounds the vertical axis and stabilizes the Root.
  2. The Heart Bridge: Place the fork on the T12 vertebra (middle of the back). This integrates the lower and upper halves of the axis.
