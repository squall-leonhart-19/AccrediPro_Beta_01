Check Your Understanding

1. When Diane expresses skepticism about meditation, why do we pivot to the science of sound?

It removes the "burden of performance" from the client. By explaining that sound does the work via entrainment, you lower their anxiety and build your authority as a practitioner.

2. What is the "Rhythm of 60" in coaching?

Speaking at a pace of approximately 60 words per minute. This mimics a resting heart rate and helps the client's nervous system begin to co-regulate with your calm presence.

3. If a client like Diane mentions "tightness in the solar plexus," which technique from Module 11 should you apply?

Placement of a grounding instrument (like a weighted tuning fork or a small Himalayan bowl) near the area of tension to provide direct vibrational feedback to the tissue.

4. Why is it important to mention your fee ($150+) with confidence during the initial consultation?

Confidence in pricing reflects confidence in your results. For a client like Diane, a professional price point signals that you are a legitimate specialist who can provide the relief she hasn't found elsewhere.
