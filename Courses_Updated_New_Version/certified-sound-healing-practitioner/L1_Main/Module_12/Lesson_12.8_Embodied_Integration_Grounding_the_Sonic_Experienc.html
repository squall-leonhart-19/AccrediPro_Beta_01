Check Your Understanding

1. What is the "Bridge Technique" in a sound healing consultation?

The Bridge Technique is the practice of connecting scientific, physiological explanations (like the Vagus nerve or brainwaves) to the client's felt experience or spiritual goals to build credibility.

2. Why should you use the term "Professional Rate" instead of "Price"?

"Professional Rate" signals that you are a certified practitioner with specialized training, helping to combat imposter syndrome and establish your value in the wellness market.

3. According to the script, how do you explain the difference between music and sound healing?

By focusing on "physical vibration" and "entrainment," explaining that sound healing is a tactile, cellular experience rather than just an auditory one.

4. What is a "Type A" client's primary language during an intake?

Their primary language is "Function and Results." They respond best to explanations of the Parasympathetic Nervous System and measurable stress reduction.
