Mapping the Layers of the Biofield

To assess resonance accurately, you must know where to look. The biofield is organized into layers that correspond to different frequencies of human experience.

  • The Etheric Body (0-2 inches): The bridge to the physical. This layer holds the resonance of physical health, nutrition, and immediate vitality.
  • The Emotional Body (2-18 inches): The seat of feelings and relational energy. In this zone, practitioners often "feel" the heat or cold of suppressed emotions.
  • The Mental Body (18 inches - 3 feet): The layer of thought patterns and belief systems. Dissonance here often manifests as repetitive, intrusive thoughts or chronic anxiety.

Fascia: The Conductive Medium

How does a sound played 2 feet away from the body affect the cells inside? The answer lies in the Fascial System. Fascia is a web of connective tissue that wraps every muscle, organ, and nerve. It is composed primarily of collagen, which is a liquid crystal.

This allows the fascia to act as a high-speed communication network, transmitting vibrational information at speeds much faster than the nervous system's chemical signals.
