The 'N' in RESONATE: Decoding Tension

Neutralizing Dissonance is the fifth pillar of our framework. The Resolution Protocol

The goal of the R.E.S.O.N.A.T.E. Method‚Ñ¢ is never just to stir things up; it is to bring them back to Alignment. The Resolution Protocol follows three distinct phases:

  1. Identification: Locate the dissonance using a scanning instrument (usually a 512Hz fork or a high-pitched crystal bowl).
