Measuring Efficacy: HRV and Objective Data

To overcome imposter syndrome and establish professional legitimacy, you must use data. Heart Rate Variability (HRV) is the gold standard for measuring the health of the Autonomic Nervous System (ANS).

HRV measures the variation in time between each heartbeat. A high HRV indicates a resilient, flexible nervous system capable of shifting into the parasympathetic "rest and digest" state. Low HRV is associated with chronic stress, inflammation, and burnout.
