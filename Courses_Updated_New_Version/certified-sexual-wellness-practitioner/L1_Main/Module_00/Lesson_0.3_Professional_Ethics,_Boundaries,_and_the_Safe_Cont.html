Practice Labs & Real-World Skills

The biggest complaint about online certifications is that they are "all head, no heart." You might know the facts, but do you know what to say when a client starts crying? Or when a client says, "I just don't feel anything anymore"?

Beginning in Module 5, you will enter our Practice Labs. These are simulated coaching scenarios where you:

  • Analyze Scripts: Read real transcripts of coaching sessions and identify where the practitioner succeeded or missed a cue.
  • Role-Play Prompts: Receive "client cards" and record yourself responding to their concerns.
  • Peer Feedback: Engage with our community to refine your "bedside manner" in a safe, judgment-free space.
