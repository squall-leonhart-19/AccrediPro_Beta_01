Defining the Scope: Practitioner vs. Therapist

A common source of imposter syndrome for career changers is the fear of "playing doctor." It is essential to understand that your work is pedagogical and somatic, not clinical-psychological.

Sex Therapist

Focuses on diagnosing mental health disorders (DSM-5), treating clinical trauma, and resolving deep-seated psychological pathologies. Usually requires a clinical master's degree and licensure.
