Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary difference between "Information" and "Transformation" in the DESIRE Framework‚Ñ¢?

Information is the cognitive understanding of facts (Education), while Transformation occurs when that knowledge is integrated into the client's body (Somatic Awareness) and mindset (Inner Inquiry), leading to behavioral change.

While it shows up in Somatic Awareness (anxiety), the root cause is often in Education (myths about performance) or Inner Inquiry (sexual scripting and shame).
