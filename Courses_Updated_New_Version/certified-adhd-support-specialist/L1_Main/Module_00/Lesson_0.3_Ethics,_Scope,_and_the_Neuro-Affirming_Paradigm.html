The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is not a simple linear course; it is a transformation architecture. We have broken down the complex science of ADHD and the art of coaching into four distinct tiers that build upon one another.

Tier Level Focus Area Key Outcome
L1: FOUNDATION (Mod 1-15) Neurobiology, Executive Function, & ADHD Fundamentals You understand the "Why" behind ADHD behaviors.
L2: ADVANCED (Mod 16-23) Complex Cases, Comorbidities, & Clinical Depth You can handle difficult client scenarios with ease.
L3: MASTER (Mod 24-29) Supervision Skills & Advanced Behavioral Change You move from "knowing" to "mastering" the coaching flow.
L4: PRACTICE (Mod 30-36) Business Building & Client Acquisition Your practice is launched and you are getting paid.
Coach Tip

Don't rush to Tier 4! I know you're excited to start your business, but the clinical confidence you build in Tier 1 and 2 is what will make your marketing effortless later. When you know you can truly help someone, selling your services becomes a service in itself.

Practice Labs: Not Just Theory

Most certifications fail because they give you information but no integration. In this program, every module includes Practice Labs. These are simulated scenarios where you apply the concepts immediately.

By Module 5, you won't just be reading about executive function; you will be practicing how to explain it to a frustrated parent or a struggling executive. This "active recall" method ensures that the information moves from your short-term memory into your professional intuition.

The T.H.R.I.V.E. Method‚Ñ¢

The backbone of this entire certification is our proprietary T.H.R.I.V.E. Method‚Ñ¢. This is the framework you will use with every single client to ensure consistent, life-changing results.

  • T - Targeted Assessment: Identifying specific neuro-individual profiles.
  • H - Holistic Regulation: Addressing the biological "bottom-up" needs (sleep, food, movement).
  • R - Rewiring Habits: Utilizing neuroplasticity to build new executive pathways.
  • I - Identity Alignment: Moving from "I am broken" to "I have a brilliant, different brain."
  • V - Vitality Systems: Creating external structures that support internal weaknesses.
  • E - Environmental Mastery: Optimizing the client's home and work life for success.
