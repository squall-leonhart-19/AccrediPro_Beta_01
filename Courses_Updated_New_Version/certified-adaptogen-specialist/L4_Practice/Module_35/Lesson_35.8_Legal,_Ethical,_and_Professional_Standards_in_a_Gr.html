The Enrollment Conversation Flow: Your 30-Minute Blueprint

A well-structured discovery call guides the conversation naturally, building trust and clarity. Hereâ€™s a blueprint you can adapt for your own practice, focusing on a 30-minute timeframe.

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set the Stage 5 min

YOU:
"Hi Maria! Thank you so much for taking the time to chat with me today. It's wonderful to connect. Before we dive into what brought you here, I'd love to just take a moment to get to know you a bit. How did you hear about my practice?"
YOU:
"That's fantastic that [mutual friend's name] referred you. She's been on quite a transformative journey herself. So Maria, tell me â€” in your own words, what prompted you to reach out today? What's been going on for you?"

Phase 2: Deeply Understand the Problem & Vision 10 min

YOU:
"Thank you for sharing that. Let's talk more about this persistent fatigue and brain fog. When did it really start to become a noticeable issue? How does it impact your daily life, your work, and your time with your family?"
YOU:
"It sounds incredibly challenging. What have you tried so far to address these issues? What worked, even a little, and what felt like it didn't make any difference at all?"
YOU:
"Maria, if nothing changes and you continue on this path, where do you see yourself in six months or a year? And conversely, if you could wave a magic wand and achieve your ideal health, what would your life look like? How would you feel?"

Phase 3: Position Your Unique Solution 10 min

YOU:
"Maria, based on everything you've shared, here's what I'm hearing: you're a busy executive feeling overwhelmed by chronic fatigue, brain fog, and weight gain, despite trying various approaches. You're looking for an effective, personalized solution to regain your energy, mental clarity, and confidence. Does that feel accurate?"
YOU:
"The approach I use as a Certified Adaptogen Specialist is fundamentally different from what you've likely experienced. Instead of just managing symptoms, we focus on identifying and addressing the root causes of your imbalances using a holistic framework. We'll utilize targeted adaptogen protocols, alongside personalized nutrition and lifestyle adjustments, to support your body's natural resilience. My 12-week program is designed to guide you step-by-step. Most of my clients, especially those struggling with fatigue like you, start experiencing significant improvements in energy and mental clarity within the first 4-6 weeks."

Phase 4: Call to Action & Next Steps 5 min

YOU:
"Maria, based on what we've discussed, does this holistic, adaptogen-focused approach sound like the kind of comprehensive support and lasting solution you've been looking for?" (Pause for her response) "Wonderful! If you're ready to explore this further, let me tell you about how my program works and how we can get started on your journey to feeling like yourself again."

Coach Sarah's Insight

Your confidence is key. When you summarize their problem, you demonstrate deep listening. When you explain your solution, you offer hope and a clear path. Practice these phases out loud until they feel natural and authentic to your voice.

Confident Objection Handling

It's natural for prospects to have questions or concerns. View objections not as rejections, but as requests for more information or reassurance. Hereâ€™s how to gracefully handle common objections.

Common Objections & Your Empowering Responses

"I need to think about it."
"I completely understand, Maria. This is an important decision about your health and well-being. To help you think it through, could you tell me what specifically you need more time to consider? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or perhaps you have some unanswered questions about the process? I want to make sure you have all the information you need."
"It's too expensive / I've already spent so much money."
"I hear you, Maria, and I appreciate your honesty. It sounds like you've invested a lot in solutions that haven't delivered, and that's incredibly frustrating. Many of my clients felt the same way before working with me. What I can tell you is that my program is designed to address the underlying issues, providing a sustainable path to health, rather than just temporary fixes. We're investing in your long-term vitality, which often saves money in the long run from ineffective treatments and lost productivity due to symptoms. What value do you place on regaining your energy and mental clarity?"
"I've tried everything already, I'm not sure this will be different."
"Maria, I completely empathize with feeling like you've exhausted all options. It takes courage to keep seeking solutions. What sets my approach apart as a Certified Adaptogen Specialist is our focus on bio-individuality and precise adaptogen selection. We don't use a one-size-fits-all method. Through in-depth assessment, we tailor every recommendation to your unique body chemistry and specific imbalances. This personalized, evidence-based adaptogen integration is often the missing piece for those who haven't found relief elsewhere. It's about working with your body's innate wisdom, not against it."

Coach Sarah's Insight

When handling objections, always lead with empathy. Validate their feelings, then pivot to education and reassurance. Have your responses prepared, but deliver them authentically, not robotically. Practice makes perfect.

Presenting Your Value & Pricing with Confidence

This is often where new practitioners stumble. Remember, you are offering immense value â€“ a transformation in health and quality of life. Your pricing reflects your expertise, the depth of your support, and the results you help clients achieve.

Confidently Articulating Your Program & Investment

1. Lead with Value, Not Just Features

Instead of listing deliverables, describe the transformation. "My 12-week 'Reclaim Your Vitality' program isn't just about supplements; it's about guiding you from chronic exhaustion to sustained energy, from brain fog to crystal-clear focus, and from feeling stuck to feeling empowered in your body."

2. State the Investment Clearly & Without Apology

Once you've painted a clear picture of the value, state your price directly. "The investment for the 12-week 'Reclaim Your Vitality' program is $2,997. This includes..." (then list key components like 1:1 sessions, personalized protocols, ongoing support, educational resources). Avoid phrases like "It's only..." or "I charge..." â€“ use "The investment is..."

3. Offer Payment Options (If Applicable)

To reduce friction, consider offering a payment plan. "I also offer a convenient payment plan of three monthly installments of $1,050." This makes your program more accessible without devaluing it.

4. Reiterate the Return on Investment

Connect the price back to their deepest desires. "Maria, think about the cost of staying where you are: the lost productivity at work, missing out on quality time with your family, the emotional toll of constant fatigue. This investment is truly in your future health and happiness."

Coach Sarah's Insight

Practice saying your price out loud until it feels completely comfortable. If you hesitate, your prospect will sense it. Believe in the value you offer, and that belief will translate into confident delivery.

Check Your Understanding

Check Your Understanding

1. During a discovery call, what is the primary purpose of asking a prospect, "If you could wave a magic wand and fix this, what would your life look like?"

The primary purpose is to uncover the prospect's deepest desires and future vision. This helps you understand their true motivation for change and allows you to later frame your solution as the bridge to that desired future. It's about connecting with their aspirations beyond just their current pain points.

2. Maria expresses concern about the cost, stating, "I've spent so much money already." How should you best respond to this objection?

You should empathize with her frustration ("I hear you, and I appreciate your honesty. It sounds like you've invested a lot in solutions that haven't delivered..."), then pivot to highlighting the long-term, sustainable value of your personalized adaptogen approach as an investment that prevents future ineffective spending. You might ask, "What value do you place on regaining your energy and mental clarity?"

3. When presenting your program's pricing, what is a key strategy to ensure you communicate value effectively?

A key strategy is to lead with value and transformation, not just features. Describe the results and the improved quality of life clients will experience, rather than simply listing what's included. Frame the price as an "investment" rather than a "cost" or "charge," and state it clearly and confidently without apology.

4. What is the role of active listening in a successful enrollment conversation?

Active listening is crucial for genuinely understanding the prospect's pain points, past experiences, and desired outcomes. It allows you to tailor your responses, demonstrate empathy, and accurately summarize their situation, making them feel heard and understood. This builds trust and positions your solution as a direct answer to their specific needs, rather than a generic offering.
