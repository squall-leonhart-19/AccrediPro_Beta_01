The 4-Tier Transformation

This program is meticulously structured to move you from Foundation to Flourishing. We don't believe in "info-dumping." Instead, we use a tiered approach that builds clinical depth alongside business acumen.

What Makes This Different?

Most certifications are 90% theory and 10% application. We have flipped that script. To be a Certified Gestalt Therapy Practitioner‚Ñ¢, you must demonstrate competence, not just pass a multiple-choice quiz.

The Practice Lab Model

In every module, you will encounter Practice Labs. These are simulated client scenarios where you will apply the techniques you've just learned. You aren't just reading about "Empty Chair" work; you are watching it, deconstructing it, and then practicing the scripts provided.

  • Real Scenarios: No "perfect" clients. We use messy, real-world examples.
  • Coaching over Information: We teach you how to be with a client, which is the hardest part of Gestalt.
  • Module 5 Milestone: By the end of Module 5, you will have the tools to conduct a full introductory session.

Time Commitment & Pacing

We designed this for busy women. Whether you are working full-time, raising a family, or both, this program fits your life.

  • The "Steady" Pace: 3-4 lessons per week (Approx. 5-7 hours). Completion in 6 months.
  • The "Accelerated" Pace: 6-8 lessons per week. Completion in 3-4 months.
  • Flexibility: There are no "live" deadlines. If life happens and you need to take two weeks off, your progress is saved exactly where you left it.
Maya's Insight

Consistency beats intensity. It is better to do 30 minutes every morning than to try and pull a 5-hour "study marathon" on Sunday. Gestalt requires reflection time for the concepts to settle in your bones.
