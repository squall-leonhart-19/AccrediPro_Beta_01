Check Your Understanding

1. What is Maya Chen's primary background and specialty?

Maya is a Mindfulness & Emotional Freedom Specialist who overcame 15 years of chronic anxiety to build a $9,800/month practice.

2. Why is "life experience" considered a superpower in Gestalt therapy?

Gestalt relies on authentic presence and the "I-Thou" relationship. Older practitioners bring wisdom, empathy, and a capacity to hold space that is honed through decades of real-world experience.

3. What is the main difference between traditional coaching and Gestalt practice?

Traditional coaching focuses largely on future goals and surface habits, while Gestalt focuses on present-moment awareness, body integration, and root-level emotional patterns.

4. What is a "Practice POD"?

A small peer group of fellow students assigned to help you practice your skills and provide a support system throughout the certification process.
