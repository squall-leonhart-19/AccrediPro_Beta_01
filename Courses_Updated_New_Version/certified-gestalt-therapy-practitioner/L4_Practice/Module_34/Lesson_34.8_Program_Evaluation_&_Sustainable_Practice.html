1. What is the primary difference between a 1-on-1 discovery call and a group discovery call?

In a group call, you are screening for "field fit"â€”ensuring the prospect can handle the social dynamicâ€”and highlighting the unique benefits of collective healing (relationship-based healing) rather than just individual symptom relief.

Key Takeaways
  • Groups are Leverage: Transitioning to groups allows you to help more people while increasing your hourly rate significantly.
  • Presence Over Pitch: Your Gestalt presence on a discovery call is your best marketing tool. Mirror the client's energy to build trust.
  • Address the "Field": Always explain why a group is better than 1-on-1 for certain issues (e.g., isolation, social anxiety, shared life transitions).
  • Price Confidently: Use "Price Anchoring" by comparing the group cost to the much higher cost of equivalent 1-on-1 sessions.
  • Start Small: Your first group doesn't need to be perfect; it just needs to be an authentic container for awareness.
