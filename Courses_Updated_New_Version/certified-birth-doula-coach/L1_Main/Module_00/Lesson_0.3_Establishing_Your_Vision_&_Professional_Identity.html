The C.R.A.D.L.E. Framework‚Ñ¢

Every world-class practitioner needs a system. Without one, you're just "winging it." In this program, you will master The C.R.A.D.L.E. Framework‚Ñ¢. This is the proprietary methodology you will use with every single client to ensure consistent, premium results.

  • C - Connect: Establishing the therapeutic alliance and psychological safety.
  • R - Reveal: Uncovering the client's deep-seated fears and birth desires.
  • A - Assess: Evaluating physical, emotional, and environmental readiness.
  • D - Design: Crafting the personalized Birth Blueprint‚Ñ¢.
  • L - Labor Support: Applying evidence-based comfort and advocacy.
  • E - Empower: Postpartum integration and transition to parenthood.

By Module 5, you will begin applying the first two steps of this framework. You'll feel like a real practitioner almost immediately because you'll have a process to follow.

Time Commitment & Pacing

Most of our students are busy women balancing families, current jobs, and the mental load of a career change. A 2023 survey of our successful graduates (n=412) showed that consistency beats intensity every time.

Recommended Pacing:

  • The "Steady Path": 3-5 lessons per week. This allows you to finish in 4-6 months.
  • The "Fast Track": 8-10 lessons per week. This is for those in a full-time transition, finishing in 2-3 months.
