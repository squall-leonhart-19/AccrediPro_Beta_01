Endocrine Disruptors & Hormonal Resilience

Mastery requires Recognizing Stressors (R) that are chemical in nature. Endocrine Disrupting Chemicals (EDCs) like phthalates, parabens, and BPA are "hormone mimics." They can bind to glucocorticoid receptors, confusing the feedback loop between the hypothalamus and the adrenal glands.

For the long-term master, this means a "Clean Sweep" of:

  • Personal Care: Moving to fragrance-free and paraben-free products.
  • Kitchen: Eliminating plastic storage and non-stick cookware (PFAS).
  • Water: Using high-quality filtration (Reverse Osmosis or Berkey) to remove fluoride and chlorine, which compete with iodine in the thyroid.
