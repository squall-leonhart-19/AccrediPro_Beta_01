The 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

This script is a framework, not a rigid dialogue. Adapt it to your natural style, but ensure you hit these key points. The goal is to understand, connect, and qualify both the client and your program.

Your 30-Minute Call Structure for Jessica

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set Agenda 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Jessica! It's so wonderful to connect with you. Thank you for taking the time today. I'm Sarah, and I'm really looking forward to our chat. Before we dive into what brought you here, how did you hear about my practice?"
YOU:
"That's fantastic! I'm glad you found me. So, for our call today, my goal is to really understand what's going on for you, what you've tried, and what you're hoping for. If it feels like a good fit, I'll share how my program works and we can discuss next steps. Does that sound good?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem & Vision 10 min
YOU:
"Great. So, Jessica, in your own words, what led you to reach out to me today? What are the biggest challenges you're facing right now with your health?"
YOU:
"Thank you for sharing that. It sounds incredibly frustrating, especially dealing with hot flashes and brain fog on top of everything else. How long have you been experiencing these symptoms, and how do they impact your daily life, your energy, and your mood?"
YOU:
"And what have you tried so far to address these issues? What worked, and what felt like it didn't quite hit the mark?"
YOU:
"If we could wave a magic wand and you woke up tomorrow feeling exactly how you want to feel, what would that look like for you? What would be different in your life?"
Coach Sarah's Insight

During Phase 2, your primary job is active listening. Let Jessica speak, truly hear her frustrations, and reflect her feelings back to her. This builds trust and shows you understand her unique situation. The "magic wand" question is powerful â€“ it helps her articulate her deepest desires and gives you a clear picture of her desired outcome.

Phase 3: Present Your Approach & Solution 10 min
YOU:
"Jessica, thank you for sharing so openly. Based on everything you've told me, it sounds like you're feeling overwhelmed by your menopausal symptoms, frustrated with the lack of effective solutions, and you deeply desire to regain your vitality and mental clarity. Does that resonate with you?"
YOU:
"The approach I use is quite different from what you've likely encountered. Instead of just managing symptoms, we focus on identifying the root causes of your hormonal imbalances and systemic stress. Through a personalized plan involving targeted medicinal mushrooms, dietary adjustments, and lifestyle shifts, we work together to rebalance your body. Many of my clients, especially women navigating menopause, start experiencing significant improvements in energy, sleep, and mental clarity within the first 6-8 weeks."
YOU:
"My 'Menopause Mastery Program' is a 12-week journey where we meet bi-weekly. We'll utilize advanced assessment tools to pinpoint your specific needs, and then I'll guide you step-by-step in integrating powerful medicinal mushrooms like Reishi for stress and sleep, Lion's Mane for cognitive support, and Tremella for hydration and skin health, along with personalized nutrition. This is about empowering you with sustainable changes."
Phase 4: Close & Next Steps 5 min
YOU:
"So, Jessica, based on what I've shared about my approach and what you're looking for, does this sound like the comprehensive, personalized support you've been seeking?"

Confident Objection Handling

It's natural for prospects to have questions or concerns. Your ability to address them calmly and confidently will set you apart. Remember, an objection is often a request for more information or reassurance.

Common Objections & Your Empowering Responses

"This sounds great, but I need to think about it."
"I completely understand, Jessica. This is an important decision about your health and well-being. To help me understand better, what specifically do you need more time to think about? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or perhaps you have some lingering questions about the program itself? I want to make sure you have all the information you need."
(Listen carefully to her answer; it will reveal the true objection.)
"I've spent so much money on supplements and programs already, and nothing has worked."
"I hear your frustration, Jessica, and it's completely valid. Many of my clients come to me feeling the same way. The difference with my approach is that we don't just throw supplements at symptoms. We conduct thorough assessments to understand your body's unique imbalances, creating a truly personalized plan. This isn't a quick fix; it's a transformative journey designed to create lasting change, guided by a Certified Medicinal Mushroom Specialist. My program is an investment in a proven system, not just another product."
"The investment is higher than I expected."
"I appreciate your honesty, Jessica. It's true that investing in your health at this level requires commitment. What I've found, however, is that often, the cost of NOT addressing these issues â€“ in terms of ongoing symptoms, lost energy, and impact on quality of life â€“ far outweighs the investment in a program that truly delivers results. My clients often tell me they wish they had started sooner. We can discuss payment options, but first, let me ask: do you feel confident that this program can help you achieve the vibrant, clear-headed life you described?"
Coach Sarah's Insight

When handling objections, always lead with empathy and validation. Acknowledge their concern, then pivot to reiterate the unique value and benefits of your program. Don't be afraid to ask clarifying questions; they help you understand the real barrier and allow you to address it directly. Confidence in your value is magnetic.

Presenting Your Program & Pricing

Confidence in your pricing comes from confidence in your value. State your price clearly, without apology, and then immediately connect it back to the transformation your client will experience.

How to Confidently Present Your Investment

  • State the Price Clearly: "My 12-week Menopause Mastery Program is an investment of $X,XXX."
  • Pause: Allow the prospect to absorb the information. Resist the urge to fill the silence.
  • Immediately Reiterate Value: "This investment covers 6 personalized one-on-one coaching sessions, a comprehensive intake and assessment, customized medicinal mushroom protocols, tailored nutritional guidance, ongoing support, and access to exclusive resources. It's designed to give you the personalized support and tools you need to regain control over your hormonal health and feel vibrant again."
  • Offer Payment Options (if applicable): "I also offer a convenient payment plan of $XXX per month over four months, to make this accessible."
  • Connect to their Vision: "Jessica, think back to what you said about wanting to feel vibrant and clear-headed again, to have the energy to enjoy your grandchildren. This program is your path to achieving that."

Call-to-Action Practice: Closing Lines

Practice these lines out loud. Your closing should be clear, confident, and direct, guiding your prospect to the next step.

  1. "So, Jessica, are you ready to take this step and start your journey to feeling like yourself again?"
  2. "Based on what we've discussed, I truly believe my Menopause Mastery Program is exactly what you need. How would you like to move forward?"
  3. "If this feels right to you, we can get you enrolled today and schedule your first session. What questions do you have about getting started?"
  4. "I have a couple of spots opening in my practice next month. If you're committed to creating this change, we can secure your place today."
Coach Sarah's Insight

The closing isn't about being pushy; it's about being helpful. You've established rapport, understood their pain, and presented a solution. Now, it's your job to guide them to take action. Assume the sale, and be prepared to address any final concerns. Your confidence here is crucial!
