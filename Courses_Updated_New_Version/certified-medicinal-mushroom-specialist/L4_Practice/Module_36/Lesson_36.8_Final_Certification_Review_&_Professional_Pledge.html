Your Ideal Prospect: Meet Emily, 49

Found you through a local wellness fair. Booked a 30-minute discovery call.

Her Situation
Struggling with menopausal symptoms (hot flashes, sleep disturbances, brain fog) for 2 years. Feels dismissed by conventional doctors.

"I've tried so many supplements and remedies that didn't work. I'm hesitant to invest a lot more without a clear path."
Decision Style
Analytical, needs data and evidence. Values a personalized plan, not a "one-size-fits-all" solution. Wants to feel understood and supported.
Her Goal
"I want to feel like myself again. I miss having clear thoughts and sleeping through the night. I just want relief and to understand what's happening to my body."

Coach Tip

Remember, a discovery call isn't about selling; it's about discovery. Your primary goal is to deeply understand Emily's world, her pain points, and her aspirations. When you genuinely listen, the solutions you offer become naturally compelling.

The 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

This script provides a framework. Your authentic presence and active listening are your most powerful tools. Practice these phases out loud, adapting them to your natural voice.

Your 30-Minute Call Structure: Guiding Emily to Clarity

Phase 1: Build Rapport & Set the Stage 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Emily! Thank you so much for taking the time to chat with me today. I'm Sarah, a Certified Medicinal Mushroom Specialist‚Ñ¢. Before we dive into what brought you here, I'd love to just take a moment to get to know you a bit. How did you hear about my practice?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful! I love connecting with people at the wellness fair. So Emily, tell me ‚Äì what prompted you to reach out now? What's going on for you?"
YOU:
"Just to set expectations, this call is for us to explore if my approach is a good fit for your needs. There's no pressure at all. Does that sound good?"
Phase 2: Deep Dive into Her World 10 min
YOU:
"You mentioned menopausal symptoms ‚Äì hot flashes, sleep, brain fog. Can you tell me more about how these are affecting your daily life? How long have they been a challenge?"
YOU:
"And what have you tried so far to address these symptoms? What worked, even a little bit, and what really didn't make a difference?"
YOU:
"I hear your frustration. If nothing changes in the next year, how do you imagine your life will look and feel? And conversely, if you could wave a magic wand and truly resolve these issues, what would your ideal day look like? What would you be able to do that you can't now?"
Phase 3: Present Your Unique Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Emily, based on what you've shared, here's what I'm hearing: you're dealing with significant menopausal symptoms ‚Äì hot flashes, interrupted sleep, and brain fog ‚Äì that are impacting your quality of life. You've tried various solutions without lasting relief, and you're looking for a personalized, evidence-based approach to regain your energy, clarity, and well-being. Does that feel accurate?"
YOU:
"The approach I use as a Certified Medicinal Mushroom Specialist‚Ñ¢ is quite different from what you've likely experienced. Instead of just managing symptoms, we focus on identifying and addressing the root causes of your hormonal imbalances and systemic inflammation. I work with clients like you through my 12-week 'Myco-Balance Pathway' program. We start with a comprehensive assessment to understand your unique physiology, then create a highly personalized protocol using specific medicinal mushrooms, often combined with targeted nutritional and lifestyle adjustments. Most of my clients, especially those struggling with hormonal shifts, report noticeable improvements in energy and sleep within the first 4-6 weeks, and significant relief from hot flashes and brain fog by the end of the program."
YOU:
"This isn't about a quick fix; it's about empowering your body's innate ability to heal and find balance, using nature's profound wisdom found in medicinal fungi."

Coach Tip

When summarizing, use Emily's exact words where possible. This shows you've truly listened. When presenting your solution, connect it directly back to her stated desires. She wants relief from brain fog? Explain how specific mushrooms support cognitive clarity and neuroprotection.

Confidently Handling Objections

Objections are not rejections; they are simply requests for more information or reassurance. Your ability to respond with empathy and confidence is key.

Common Objections & Your Empowering Responses

"I need to think about it / Discuss with my partner."

"I completely understand, Emily. This is an important decision. To help you think it over, can I ask ‚Äî what specifically do you need to think about? Is it the investment, the time commitment, or perhaps you're wondering if this approach is truly different from what you've tried before? Knowing this helps me provide the information you need."

(If partner) "That's wonderful to involve your partner in such an important health decision. What specifically do you think they might want to know about the program? I'm happy to provide any information or even schedule a brief follow-up call with both of you to answer their questions."

"It's too expensive / I've spent so much already."

"I hear you, Emily. It sounds like you've invested a lot, both emotionally and financially, without getting the lasting results you deserve. My program is an investment, yes, but it's an investment in a foundational shift in your health. Consider the cost of continuing to feel unwell ‚Äì the missed work, the impact on your relationships, the ongoing cycle of trying things that don't work. My clients typically save money in the long run by avoiding ineffective treatments and regaining their vitality. What would it be worth to you to truly feel like yourself again, sleeping soundly and thinking clearly?"

"I'm skeptical / Medicinal mushrooms sound a bit 'out there' for menopause."

"I appreciate your honesty, Emily, and it's completely natural to feel skeptical, especially when you've been disappointed before. Many of my clients felt the same way initially. What I can tell you is that the use of medicinal mushrooms is backed by centuries of traditional use and a growing body of scientific research, particularly for hormonal balance and stress adaptation. We're not talking about magic; we're talking about potent bio-compounds that work with your body's natural systems. I provide you with all the evidence and support you need, and we start with a highly personalized plan to ensure comfort and efficacy. Would you be open to me sharing some relevant research summaries after our call?"

"I don't have enough time for a 12-week program."

"I understand that your time is precious, Emily, especially when you're already feeling drained. The 'Myco-Balance Pathway' is designed to integrate seamlessly into your life. Our calls are typically 60 minutes every other week, and the daily protocols are practical and easy to follow. Think of it as investing a small amount of time now to reclaim years of vibrant health. My clients find that as their energy improves, they actually gain more time and focus for what truly matters to them. What does your ideal time commitment look like, and how might we make this work for you?"

Presenting Your Value & Pricing

This is where many practitioners falter. Remember, you are offering a life-changing transformation, not just a product. Lead with the value, then present the investment.

Phase 4: Presenting Your Program 5 min
YOU:
"Emily, based on everything you've shared, I truly believe my 'Myco-Balance Pathway' program is exactly what you need to address your menopausal symptoms and regain your vitality. It offers the personalized, root-cause approach and dedicated support you've been looking for."
YOU:
"The program includes:
  • A comprehensive initial consultation (90 minutes) to deeply understand your health history, symptoms, and goals.
  • Five bi-weekly follow-up sessions (60 minutes each) for ongoing support, protocol adjustments, and education.
  • A personalized medicinal mushroom and supplement protocol tailored to your unique needs.
  • Customized dietary and lifestyle recommendations for optimal hormonal balance.
  • Direct messaging support between sessions for urgent questions.
  • Access to exclusive educational resources on medicinal mushrooms and women's health.
This entire 12-week program is designed to guide you step-by-step to feeling like yourself again."
YOU:
"The investment for the 'Myco-Balance Pathway' program is $2,997. I also offer a flexible payment plan of three monthly installments of $1,050. This investment covers your complete journey back to vibrant health."

Coach Tip

After stating the price, stop talking. Let the number sit. The first person to speak often loses. This allows the prospect to process and formulate their thoughts, which often leads to their primary objection ‚Äì a perfect opportunity for you to respond confidently.

Mastering the Call-to-Action

After presenting your solution and pricing, it's essential to guide the prospect clearly to the next step. Don't leave them guessing.

Phase 5: Closing the Call 5 min
YOU:
"So Emily, does this sound like the comprehensive, personalized support you've been looking for to finally address your menopausal symptoms and regain your well-being?"
YOU:
(If "Yes, it sounds great!") "Wonderful! I'm so excited for you to start this journey. The next step is to get you officially enrolled. I can send you the enrollment link right now, which includes the agreement and payment options. How does that sound?"
YOU:
(If "I need to think about it," or other objection, refer to Objection Handling above. If you've addressed the objection and she's still on the fence): "Emily, I truly believe in the power of this work, and I'm confident I can help you. Given everything we've discussed, what feels like the best next step for you today?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of the "Deep Dive into Her World" phase of the discovery call?

The primary goal is to deeply understand the prospect's pain points, current challenges, past attempts at solutions, and their ideal future state. This phase is crucial for building empathy and gathering information to tailor your solution.

2. Emily raises an objection: "I'm worried about the time commitment for a 12-week program." How should you respond, focusing on value?

A good response would be: "I understand that your time is precious, especially when you're already feeling drained. The 'Myco-Balance Pathway' is designed to integrate seamlessly into your life. Our calls are typically 60 minutes every other week, and the daily protocols are practical and easy to follow. Think of it as investing a small amount of time now to reclaim years of vibrant health. My clients find that as their energy improves, they actually gain more time and focus for what truly matters to them."

3. When should you state your program's price during the discovery call?

You should state the price after you have fully presented the value and components of your program, and ideally, after the prospect has confirmed that your solution sounds like what they're looking for. This ensures they understand the transformation they are investing in.

4. What is the key action you should take immediately after stating your program's price?

The key action is to stop talking and allow the prospect to process the information. This creates space for them to formulate any questions or objections they might have, which you can then address.

Key Takeaways

  • The discovery call is a critical opportunity to build rapport and deeply understand a prospect's needs.
  • Active listening and empathetic questioning are more important than talking about yourself.
  • Frame your medicinal mushroom expertise as a unique, root-cause solution to their specific problems.
  • Anticipate and confidently address objections by validating their concerns and reframing the value.
  • Present your program's value comprehensively before stating the investment.
  • Always provide a clear call-to-action to guide the prospect to the next step in their health journey.
