8 Profitable Career Paths

One of the beauties of this certification is the flexibility. You don't have to be stuck in a "dollars-for-hours" trap. Here are the eight most common ways our practitioners earn:

  1. Private 1-on-1 Practice: The foundation. Premium 3-month packages typically range from $1,500 to $5,000 per client.
  2. Group Coaching Programs: Scale your time. A 6-week "Libido Reboot" group with 10 women at $600 each equals $6,000 for just a few hours of work per week.
  3. Corporate Wellness: Companies are finally realizing that hormonal health impacts productivity. Retainers can range from $2,000 to $10,000/month.
  4. Integrative Clinic Teams: Work alongside GYNs or Functional Medicine doctors as their "Intimacy Specialist." Salary ranges: $70k - $140k.
  5. Digital Courses: Create once, sell forever. Passive income streams of $1k - $5k/month are common for established practitioners.
  6. Speaking & Workshops: Local events or virtual summits. Fees: $500 - $5,000 per engagement.
  7. Retreats: High-ticket "Intimacy Immersions." Profit margins are high, often netting $10k+ for a single weekend.
  8. Affiliate & Brand Partnerships: Recommending high-quality supplements, toys, or tech you already believe in.
