Welcome, Practitioner

Transitioning from a traditional career like nursing or teaching into high-ticket coaching requires a shift from "trading hours for dollars" to "selling outcomes." This lesson provides the blueprint for a funnel that does the heavy lifting for you, filtering for high-quality clients while you focus on transformation.

Learning Objectives
  • Design a "Libido Audit" lead magnet that utilizes the Discovery pillar to build immediate authority.
  • Construct an automated email sequence that addresses Exploration and Somatic Awareness blocks.
  • Outline a 45-minute "Ignition" webinar designed to convert viewers into high-ticket applicants.
  • Implement a vetting process that ensures Relational Alignment before the first consultation.
  • Select the optimal tech stack for a streamlined practitioner-client journey.

Section 1: The 'Libido Audit' Lead Magnet

In high-ticket coaching (services priced from $3,000 to $10,000+), the first touchpoint must provide massive value while highlighting a gap in the client's current approach. We use the Discovery pillar of our framework to create a "Libido Audit."

Unlike a generic PDF, a Libido Audit is a self-assessment tool. It allows the potential client to "discover" their own biological and psychological roadblocks. A 2022 study on digital health engagement found that personalized assessments increase user conversion rates by 42% compared to static informational downloads.

üí° Don't give away the solution in the lead magnet. Focus on the Discovery. Help them realize why they are struggling (e.g., "Your audit shows a high Inhibitory Response") so they seek your help to fix it.

Section 3: The 'Ignition' Webinar Structure

The "Ignition" Webinar is a 45-minute presentation that bridges the gap between the client's current pain and your high-ticket solution. Statistics show that 73% of B2B and high-level health consumers say webinars are the best way to generate high-quality leads.

The 45-Minute Blueprint:

  • 0-10 Min: The Hook & Empathy. Share your "why" and acknowledge their struggle (e.g., "The 3 Reasons Your Doctor Said Your Labs Are Normal But You Feel Broken").
  • 10-25 Min: The D.E.S.I.R.E. Methodology. Teach the framework. Show them the science of Responsive Desire vs. Spontaneous Desire.
  • 25-35 Min: The Case Study. Walk through a detailed transformation using the pillars.
  • 35-45 Min: The Vetting Call Invitation. Transition from teacher to practitioner. Explain that this is an application-only program.
Coach Tip

üí° Avoid the "Hard Sell." For a $997+ certification or high-ticket service, the webinar should feel like a professional consultation. Your goal is to invite them to an application, not a checkout page.

Section 4: Application & Vetting (Relational Alignment)

High-ticket coaching is a partnership. To maintain your professional integrity and ensure client success, you must vet for Relational Alignment. An optimized application process filters out "tire-kickers" and ensures you only speak with committed clients.

Required Application Fields:

  • Investment Readiness: "Are you prepared to invest financially and energetically in your sexual vitality?"
  • The 'Why' Factor: "On a scale of 1-10, how much of a priority is resolving this issue right now?"
  • Medical Clearance: "Have you consulted a physician to rule out acute pathology?"

Section 5: Essential Tech Stack

For a career changer, the "tech" can be the most intimidating part. However, a "lean" stack is often more effective than a complex one. A 2023 survey of independent wellness practitioners showed that practitioners using integrated CRMs saved an average of 12 hours per week on administrative tasks.

Category Recommended Tool Purpose
Funnel/Webinar Kajabi or ClickFunnels Hosting the landing pages and the Ignition webinar.
Email Automation ConvertKit or ActiveCampaign The 5-day nurture sequence and tagging leads.
Scheduling/Vetting Calendly + Typeform Automating the application and booking the call.
CRM/Payments Practice Better or Stripe Managing client records and high-ticket payments.
