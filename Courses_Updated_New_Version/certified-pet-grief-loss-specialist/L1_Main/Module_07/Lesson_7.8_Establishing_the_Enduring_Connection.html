Success Markers

How do you know the session is going well? Look for these "Shifts":

  • The Shoulder Drop: The client physically relaxes their posture as they feel "heard."
  • The "Right?" Response: The client says things like, "Exactly!" or "You hit the nail on the head."
  • Deeper Sharing: The client moves from talking about the death to talking about the relationship.
  • Decreased Shame: The client stops using words like "silly," "crazy," or "dramatic" to describe themselves.
