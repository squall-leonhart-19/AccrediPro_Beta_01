Common Pitfalls to Avoid

As you prepare for certification, watch out for these 4 mistakes that new coaches often make:

  • The "Fix-It" Trap: Trying to make the client stop crying too quickly. Grief needs to be witnessed, not "fixed."
  • Comparing Griefs: Never say, "I know exactly how you feel because I lost my cat." Keep the focus on their unique bond.
  • Over-Explaining: Using too much "science" or "theory" when the client is in an acute emotional state.
  • Neglecting Boundaries: Failing to use your Client Agreement templates (from the DFY Accelerator) to set professional hours.
