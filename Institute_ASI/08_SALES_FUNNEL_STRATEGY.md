## Market Positioning

### Target Audience

**Primary Avatar: "Sarah"**
- Age: 35-45 years old
- Gender: Female
- Location: United States
- Current Situation:
  - Unfulfilled in corporate career
  - Interested in health & wellness
  - Wants to help others
  - Seeking meaningful work
  - Wants flexibility/autonomy
- Pain Points:
  - Feels stuck in current career
  - Doesn't know how to transition
  - Worried about legitimacy/credibility
  - Overwhelmed by options
  - Concerned about income potential
- Desires:
  - Professional recognition
  - Clear career path
  - Ability to help others
  - Income potential
  - Work-life balance

### Positioning Statement

**Before (Course):**
> "Learn functional medicine online"

**After (Certification):**
> "Get professionally certified in Functional Medicine by AccrediPro Standards Institute - the trusted certification body for health and wellness professionals. Join 10,000+ certified practitioners building thriving careers."

### Key Differentiators

| Competitor | Their Approach | Our Advantage |
|------------|---------------|---------------|
| IIN | General health coaching | Specialized FM certification |
| HNBWC | One certification | Multiple specialties, career ladder |
| Random courses | No credential | Professional certification |
| Universities | Expensive, long | Accessible, practical |

### Messaging Pillars

1. **Credibility**: "Earn a real professional credential"
2. **Career**: "Build a thriving wellness practice"
3. **Community**: "Join thousands of certified professionals"
4. **Competence**: "Gain the skills employers want"
5. **Confidence**: "Know you're qualified to help"

### Webinar Structure (90 Minutes)

**Opening (0-5 min)**
- Welcome and housekeeping
- Promise: "By the end of this masterclass..."
- Credentials and authority

**Section 1: The Opportunity (5-20 min)**
- The wellness industry growth
- Why credentials matter now more than ever
- The "certification advantage"
- Story: How I got started

**Section 2: The Framework (20-45 min)**
- The 3 Pillars of Functional Medicine Success
  - Pillar 1: Clinical Knowledge
  - Pillar 2: Practical Skills
  - Pillar 3: Professional Recognition
- Teaching content (provide value)
- Case studies and examples

**Section 3: The Roadblocks (45-55 min)**
- Common mistakes people make
- Why most people fail
- The missing piece: Professional certification

**Transition (55-60 min)**
- "So at this point you have two options..."
- Bridge to offer

**Section 4: The Offer (60-75 min)**
- Introduce FM-CP™ Professional Certification
- Walk through everything included
- Stack the value
- Reveal the price
- Payment plan option
- Bonuses (for those who enroll today)
- Guarantee

**Q&A and Close (75-90 min)**
- Answer objections through Q&A
- Final CTA
- Urgency (cohort closes, bonus deadline)
