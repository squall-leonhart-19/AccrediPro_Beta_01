## Brand Guidelines

### ASI Logo

**Primary Logo**
- Full: "AccrediPro Standards Institute"
- Abbreviated: "ASI" (for badges/seals)
- Seal version for certificates

**Colors**
```
Primary Navy:     #1E3A5F
Accent Gold:      #C9A227
White:            #FFFFFF
Light Gray:       #F5F5F5
Dark Gray:        #333333
Success Green:    #2E7D32
```

**Typography**
- Headlines: Playfair Display (serif)
- Body: Open Sans (sans-serif)
- Credentials: Montserrat (sans-serif, bold)

### Credential Color Coding

| Level | Primary Color | Accent |
|-------|--------------|--------|
| Foundation (FC™) | Bronze #CD7F32 | Navy |
| Professional (CP™) | Silver #C0C0C0 | Navy |
| Board Certified (BC-™) | Gold #C9A227 | Navy |

## Production Specifications

### Digital Certificates (PDF)
- Resolution: 300 DPI
- Color: CMYK for print, RGB for digital
- Format: PDF/A for archival

### Physical Certificates
- Paper: 100lb cover stock, cream or white
- Finish: Linen texture
- Foil: Gold foil for seal (BC level)
- Size: 11" x 8.5"

### Wallet Cards
- Material: PVC plastic, credit card thickness
- Finish: Matte with gloss accents
- Size: 3.375" x 2.125" (CR80)
