## About ASI Certification

### What is ASI?

AccrediPro Standards Institute (ASI) is a professional certification body dedicated to establishing and maintaining standards for health and wellness professionals. Our certifications demonstrate that you have met rigorous standards of education, examination, and ethical practice.

### Why Get Certified?

| Benefit | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Credibility** | Demonstrate your competence to clients and employers |
| **Recognition** | Earn a credential recognized across the industry |
| **Career Advancement** | Open doors to new opportunities and higher income |
| **Professional Development** | Commit to ongoing learning and growth |
| **Client Trust** | Build trust through third-party validation |
| **Community** | Join a network of certified professionals |

### Our Credentials

| Credential | Level | For Whom |
|------------|-------|----------|
| **FC™** (Foundation Certificate) | Entry | Career explorers, support staff, foundation learners |
| **CP™** (Certified Professional) | Professional | Practitioners working with clients |
| **BC-™** (Board Certified) | Advanced | Practitioners establishing independent practices |

## Choosing Your Certification Path

### Foundation Certificate (FC™)

**Best for:**
- Exploring a new career in wellness
- Building foundational knowledge
- Support roles in wellness practices
- Preparing for professional certification

**Requirements:**
- 25 hours of training
- Knowledge assessment (70% pass)
- No prerequisites

**Cost:**
- Training: $397 (via approved provider)
- Exam: $195

**Timeline:** 4-8 weeks typical

---

### Certified Professional (CP™)

**Best for:**
- Working directly with clients
- Starting a wellness practice
- Professional recognition

**Requirements:**
- 75 hours of training
- 3 case studies
- Professional exam (75% pass)
- Foundation Certificate OR equivalent

**Cost:**
- Training: $2,497 (via approved provider)
- Exam: $395
- Annual Renewal: $149

**Timeline:** 3-6 months typical

---

### Board Certified (BC-™)

**Best for:**
- Establishing independent practice
- Leadership roles
- Teaching/mentoring others
- Maximum professional recognition

**Requirements:**
- 150 hours of training
- 10 supervised client cases
- Portfolio review
- Board exam (80% pass)
- Current CP™ credential

**Cost:**
- Training: $7,997 (via approved provider)
- Exam: $595
- Annual Renewal: $249

**Timeline:** 6-12 months typical

## Application Process

### Step 1: Complete Training

Enroll in an ASI-approved training program:
- AccrediPro Academy (primary approved provider)
- Other approved providers (listed on ASI website)

### Step 2: Meet Prerequisites

Ensure you meet all eligibility requirements for your chosen credential level.

### Step 3: Apply for Examination

1. Create an account at **portal.accredipro-institute.com**
2. Complete the certification application
3. Upload required documents:
   - Training completion certificate
   - Prerequisite documentation (if applicable)
   - Case studies (for CP™)
   - Portfolio (for BC-™)
4. Pay examination fee
5. Agree to Code of Ethics

### Step 4: Schedule Examination

Once approved:
1. Receive scheduling link via email
2. Select your preferred date/time
3. Complete system check (for online proctoring)
4. Take examination

### Step 5: Receive Results

| Exam Level | Results Timeline |
|------------|-----------------|
| Foundation | Immediate |
| Professional | Within 5 business days |
| Board | Within 10 business days |

### Step 6: Receive Credential

Upon passing:
- Digital badge (immediate)
- PDF certificate (within 24 hours)
- Credential ID number
- Directory listing (if opted in)
- Physical certificate (mailed within 2 weeks)

### Examination Day Checklist

**Before the Exam:**
- [ ] Test your computer and internet
- [ ] Ensure webcam and microphone work
- [ ] Have valid photo ID ready
- [ ] Find a quiet, private location
- [ ] Clear your desk of all materials
- [ ] Close all other programs
- [ ] Use the restroom beforehand
- [ ] Have water available (clear bottle only)

**During the Exam:**
- [ ] Follow all proctor instructions
- [ ] Read each question carefully
- [ ] Manage your time
- [ ] Flag questions to review
- [ ] Stay visible on camera
- [ ] Do not leave your seat

**Prohibited:**
- Cell phones or smart devices
- Notes, books, or study materials
- Other people in the room
- Headphones or earbuds
- Leaving the camera view
- Taking screenshots or photos

## After You Pass

### Your Credential

Upon passing, you receive:

**Digital Badge**
- Shareable on LinkedIn, email signatures, websites
- Links to verification page
- Downloadable in multiple formats

**Certificate**
- PDF certificate (immediate)
- Framed physical certificate (optional, mailed)
- Includes credential ID and dates

**Credential Use**

**Correct Usage:**
- Jane Smith, FM-CP™
- Jane Smith, BC-FMP™
- "ASI Certified" badge with credential

**Incorrect Usage:**
- Jane Smith, ASI (incomplete)

## Maintaining Your Credential

### Renewal Requirements

| Credential | CE Credits | Cycle | Fee |
|------------|------------|-------|-----|
| FC™ | None | Lifetime | None |
| CP™ | 20 | 2 years | $149 |
| BC-™ | 40 | 2 years | $249 |

### Continuing Education (CE)

**How to Earn CE Credits:**

| Activity | Credits | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| ASI-approved courses | 1/hour | Unlimited |
| ASI Annual Conference | 10 | Once per year |
| College courses | 15/course | Max 30/cycle |
| Publishing | 5/article | Max 10/cycle |
| Presenting | 5/presentation | Max 10/cycle |
| Peer supervision | 0.5/hour | Max 5/cycle |
| Self-study with quiz | 0.5/hour | Max 10/cycle |

**CE Guidelines:**
- CE must be relevant to your certification specialty
- Keep records of all CE activities
- Upload certificates to your ASI portal
- CE is audited randomly (10% of certificants)

### Renewal Process

1. **90 days before expiration**: Receive reminder email
2. **Complete CE**: Ensure all CE credits are entered
3. **Pay renewal fee**: Online or by phone
4. **Attest**: Confirm continued adherence to Code of Ethics
5. **Renewed**: Credential extended for 2 years

### Lapsed Credentials

| Time Lapsed | Reinstatement Process |
|-------------|----------------------|
| 0-30 days | Renew normally (grace period) |
| 31 days - 1 year | Pay renewal + $50 late fee + complete CE |
| 1-3 years | Above + reinstatement exam |
| Over 3 years | Must recertify (full exam) |

## Policies & Procedures

### Refund Policy

| Situation | Refund |
|-----------|--------|
| Cancel exam before scheduling | Full refund minus $25 admin fee |
| Cancel 7+ days before scheduled exam | Full refund minus $50 fee |
| Cancel 2-6 days before exam | 50% refund |
| Cancel less than 48 hours / No-show | No refund |
| Technical issues (ASI's fault) | Full refund or reschedule |

### Rescheduling

- Reschedule up to 24 hours before exam at no charge
- Less than 24 hours: $50 reschedule fee
- Maximum 2 reschedules per exam registration

### Testing Accommodations

ASI provides accommodations for documented disabilities:
- Extended time (50% additional)
- Separate room
- Screen reader compatibility
- Other accommodations as appropriate

**To Request:**
1. Submit request at least 14 days before exam
2. Provide documentation of disability
3. Specify accommodations needed
4. Await approval before scheduling

### Appeals

**Exam Results Appeal:**
1. Submit written appeal within 30 days
2. Pay $50 appeal fee (refunded if successful)
3. Specify grounds for appeal
4. Review by Examination Committee
5. Decision within 15 business days

**Ethics/Discipline Appeal:**
1. Submit written appeal within 30 days of decision
2. Pay $100 appeal fee
3. Appeals Board reviews
4. Decision within 30 business days
5. Decision is final

### Name/Information Changes

To update your name or information:
1. Log into your ASI portal
2. Submit change request
3. Upload supporting documentation (for name changes)
4. New credential/certificate issued
5. $25 fee for replacement certificate

### Duplicate Certificates

- Digital certificates: Free (download from portal)
- Physical replacement: $35 + shipping

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Application & Eligibility

**Q: Do I need a college degree to get certified?**
A: No. Foundation Certificate (FC™) has no educational prerequisites. Professional certification (CP™) accepts a high school diploma plus either the Foundation Certificate, a related degree, or documented experience.

**Q: Can I apply for CP™ without the Foundation Certificate?**
A: Yes, if you have a bachelor's degree in a health/wellness field OR 2+ years of documented professional experience.

**Q: How long do I have to complete the certification?**
A: Foundation: 12 months. Professional: 18 months. Board: 24 months from enrollment.

### Examination

**Q: How hard is the exam?**
A: Exams are designed to be challenging but fair. First-time pass rates are typically 75-85% for Foundation, 65-75% for Professional, and 60-70% for Board.

**Q: What if I fail the exam?**
A: You may retake after a 14-day waiting period. Foundation has unlimited free retakes. Professional includes 2 retakes, then $99 each. Board includes 1 retake, then $199 each.

**Q: Can I use notes during the exam?**
A: No. Foundation is honor code. Professional and Board are proctored with no materials allowed.

**Q: What ID do I need?**
A: A valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, state ID).

### Credentials

**Q: How do I display my credential?**
A: After your name: "Jane Smith, FM-CP™" or use the digital badge on your website/LinkedIn.

**Q: Can I use my credential internationally?**
A: Yes. ASI credentials are recognized internationally, though local regulations may apply.

**Q: What if my credential expires?**
A: You must complete CE and pay renewal before expiration. See "Lapsed Credentials" for reinstatement options.

### Continuing Education

**Q: What counts as CE?**
A: ASI-approved courses, conferences, publishing, presenting, and other activities. See CE section for details.

**Q: How do I report CE?**
A: Log into your ASI portal and enter activities. Upload certificates as documentation.

**Q: What if I can't complete CE in time?**
A: Contact ASI before your expiration date. Extensions may be granted for documented hardship.

### Costs

**Q: What are all the costs involved?**

| Item | Foundation | Professional | Board |
|------|------------|--------------|-------|
| Training | $397 | $2,497 | $7,997 |
| Exam Fee | $195 | $395 | $595 |
| Renewal | None | $149/year | $249/year |
| **Total Year 1** | **$592** | **$3,041** | **$8,841** |

**Q: Are there payment plans?**
A: Training programs often offer payment plans. Exam fees are due at time of scheduling.

**Q: Are there scholarships?**
A: Some approved training providers offer scholarships. Check with your training provider.
