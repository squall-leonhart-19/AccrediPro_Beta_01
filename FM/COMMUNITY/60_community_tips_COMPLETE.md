"Sarah, how do I structure a discovery call? What do I even SAY?"

**1. CONNECTION (5 minutes)**
"Thanks so much for chatting! Before we dive in, tell me what brought you to look for help right now?"

**2. DEEP DIVE (15 minutes)**
"Walk me through what you've already tried... what's been most frustrating... how is this impacting your daily life?"

**3. PAINT THE VISION (5 minutes)**
"If we worked together and 3 months from now you were feeling amazing ‚Äî what would that look like?"

Let THEM describe success. You're not selling ‚Äî you're listening.

**4. SHARE YOUR APPROACH (10 minutes)**
"Based on what you've shared, here's how I'd approach this... Does this resonate with you?"

Be specific. Connect their symptoms to root causes.

**5. DISCUSS INVESTMENT (5 minutes)**
"My program is [X] and includes [details]. What questions do you have?"

State price confidently. Then STOP TALKING. Let them respond.

**The secret sauce:**
You're not convincing them. You're discovering if you're a good fit for each other.

**Common fear:** "What if I freeze?"
