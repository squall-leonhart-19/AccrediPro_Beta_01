### Question 1 (Lesson 1.1)
**What term describes the emotional harm healthcare workers experience when forced to act against their values within a broken system?**

- A) Compassion fatigue
- B) Burnout syndrome
- C) Moral injury ✓
- D) Clinical depression

> **Correct Answer: C) Moral injury**
> 
> Moral injury is the clinical term for what happens when you're forced to act against your own values—when you KNOW there's a better way, but the system won't let you pursue it.

### Question 2 (Lesson 1.2)
**According to the 7 Body Systems Model, what percentage of your immune system is housed in your gut?**

- A) 30-40%
- B) 50-60%
- C) 70-80% ✓
- D) 90-100%

> **Correct Answer: C) 70-80%**
> 
> The gut houses 70-80% of your immune system, which is why Functional Medicine practitioners always start with gut health. Fix the gut, and many other problems often resolve.
