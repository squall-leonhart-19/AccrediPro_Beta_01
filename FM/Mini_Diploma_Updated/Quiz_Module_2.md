### Question 1 (Lesson 2.1)
**What was the recurring weekly experience Michelle described that signaled her unhappiness in nursing?**

- A) Monday morning meetings
- B) The Sunday Night Dread ✓
- C) Friday exhaustion
- D) Wednesday burnout

> **Correct Answer: B) The Sunday Night Dread**
> 
> Michelle described the "Sunday Night Dread"—a physical knot in her stomach starting around 4pm on Sundays as she anticipated returning to work. This is a common experience for healthcare workers trapped in broken systems.

### Question 2 (Lesson 2.2)
**What was the key question that Michelle learned to ask clients that conventional doctors typically ignore?**

- A) "What medications are you taking?"
- B) "When was the last time you felt truly healthy—and what happened next?" ✓
- C) "What are your symptoms today?"
- D) "Do you have a family history of this condition?"

> **Correct Answer: B) "When was the last time you felt truly healthy—and what happened next?"**
> 
> This "magic question" is the foundation of the Functional Timeline approach. It shifts focus from current symptoms to the story and triggers that initiated the illness.
