Content Marketing Strategy

Content builds trust before they become clients. Educate, don't just promote. Position yourself as the expert.

Content Types

Blog posts: SEO-driven, educational. Social media: Engaging, shareable. Videos: High trust-building. Podcasts: Deep connection with audience. Email: Nurture and convert. Webinars: Demonstrate expertise.

Social Media Strategy

  • Choose 1-2 platforms: Master them before expanding
  • Educational content: 80% value, 20% promotion
  • Consistency: Regular posting schedule
  • Engagement: Reply to comments, build community
  • Stories/Reels: Show personality and behind-the-scenes

Email Marketing

  • Lead magnet: Free guide, quiz, or resource to build list
  • Welcome sequence: Introduce yourself and your approach
  • Regular newsletters: Weekly or bi-weekly value
  • Promotional sequences: When launching programs
  • Segment by interest: Personalize based on their concerns

Referral Networks

  • Build relationships with complementary practitioners
  • MDs, naturopaths, chiropractors, acupuncturists
  • Personal trainers, health coaches, nutritionists
  • Offer to present at events or offices
  • Create a formal referral program for clients

1. What is the 80/20 rule for social media content?

80% of content should be educational/value-driven. 20% can be promotional. This builds trust and positions you as an expert before asking for anything. Too much promotion without value drives people away.

2. What is a lead magnet and why is it important?

A lead magnet is a free resource (guide, quiz, checklist) offered in exchange for an email address. It builds your email list, demonstrates your expertise, attracts your ideal clients, and starts the relationship before they become paying clients.
