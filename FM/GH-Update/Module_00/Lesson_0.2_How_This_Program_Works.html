Program Structure

This certification is designed for busy professionals who need flexibility without sacrificing depth. The program is 100% online and self-paced, meaning you can study when it fits your schedule‚Äîearly mornings, lunch breaks, or late evenings. There are no live sessions you must attend (though optional live Q&As are offered periodically).

The curriculum is organized into 15 modules, each containing multiple lessons, exercises, and assessments. Here's how the program flows:

1

Watch, Read & Learn

Each lesson includes rich written content (like what you're reading now), often accompanied by supporting videos, diagrams, and downloadable resources. Take notes in our built-in note-taking system.

2

Complete Knowledge Checks

At the end of each lesson, interactive questions help reinforce your learning. These are not graded‚Äîthey're learning tools to solidify concepts before moving forward.

3

Pass Module Quizzes

Each module concludes with a quiz covering key concepts. You need 70% to pass, and you can retake quizzes unlimited times. Passing unlocks the next module.

4

Apply What You Learn

Throughout the program, you'll complete practical exercises‚Äîcase study analyses, protocol designs, intake form practice‚Äîthat build real-world skills.

5

Pass the Final Exam

After completing all modules, you'll take a comprehensive final exam covering the entire curriculum. Pass with 70% or higher to earn your certification.

Assessment & Certification

Your certification is earned through demonstrated competence, not just completion. Here's how assessment works:

Module Quizzes

  • Format: Multiple choice and true/false questions
  • Passing score: 70% (you can retake unlimited times)
  • Purpose: Ensure mastery before advancing to new material
  • Feedback: Immediate results with explanations for incorrect answers

Final Exam

  • Format: 100 questions covering all modules
  • Time limit: 2 hours (most finish in 60-90 minutes)
  • Passing score: 70%
  • Retakes: Unlimited, with 48-hour waiting period between attempts

Your Certification

Upon passing the final exam, you receive:

  • Digital Certificate: Professionally designed, instantly downloadable
  • Certification Number: Unique ID verifiable on our website
  • Digital Badge: For your website, email signature, and LinkedIn
  • Alumni Status: Ongoing access to updates and community
