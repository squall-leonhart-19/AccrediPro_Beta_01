1. What is the central framework that ties all gut health interventions together?

The 5R Framework: Remove, Replace, Reinoculate, Repair, Rebalance. This provides the sequence and structure for all gut healing protocols. Each step builds on the previous one for optimal outcomes.

2. Why is the order of intervention important?

Sequence matters because you must remove pathogens and irritants before trying to rebuild. Adding probiotics to an infected gut feeds the problem. Repairing a leaky gut while still eating reactive foods won't work. Each step prepares for the next.
