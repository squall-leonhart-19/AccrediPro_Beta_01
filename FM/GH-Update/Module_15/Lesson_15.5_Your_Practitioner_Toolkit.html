‚úì Check Your Understanding

1. Why is having protocol templates valuable?

Templates save time, ensure consistency, reduce the chance of missing steps, provide a starting point to customize for each client, and help you appear more professional. You customize the template rather than starting from scratch each time.

2. What should a decision tree include?

Decision trees should map symptoms ‚Üí testing recommendations ‚Üí treatment pathways. They help guide clinical reasoning systematically. Include branches for different test results and when to refer. They're especially helpful for newer practitioners.
