Key Patterns Across Cases

The Coaching Difference

Notice what's missing: no lectures about the dangers of prediabetes, no explaining why stress eating is bad, no prescribed exercise routines. The wisdom and motivation came from the clients themselves. The coach simply created conditions for that to emerge.

Key Skills Demonstrated

Right-Sizing Goals

Adjusting goals until confidence is 7+ to set clients up for success.

Module 2 Summary
