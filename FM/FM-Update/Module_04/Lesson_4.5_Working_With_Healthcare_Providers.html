The Case for Collaboration

When health coaches and healthcare providers work together effectively, client outcomes improve dramatically. Chronic disease management becomes more effective. Patient adherence to medical recommendations increases. Healthcare costs decrease.

Understanding the Healthcare Landscape

Each type of healthcare professional has distinct training, perspectives, and communication preferences. Your role as a health coach is to complement medical care, not replace it.
