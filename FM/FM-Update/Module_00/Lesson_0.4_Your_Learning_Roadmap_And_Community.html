Navigating Challenges (They're Normal)

Every successful coach hit these bumps. Here's how to handle them:

Information Overwhelm

It feels like too much to learn. Solution: Focus on one lesson at a time. You're building frameworks, not memorizing textbooks. Trust the process.

Imposter Syndrome

"Who am I to coach others?" Solution: This feeling is universal and actually a sign you take this seriously. It fades as you gain experience.

Life Gets in the Way

Family, work, health‚Äîlife happens. Solution: Pause when you need to, but come back. A break isn't failure. Quitting is the only failure.

Difficult Material

Some concepts are hard. Solution: Read again, ask in the forum, attend Q&A. Confusion today becomes clarity tomorrow.
