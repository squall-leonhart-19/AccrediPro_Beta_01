Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a medical background?

No. While many students are nurses or RDs, the curriculum builds from the ground up. We teach you the science you need to succeed.

Is the program self-paced?

Yes! You have lifetime access. Most complete it in 6 months (5-7 hours/week), but there are no deadlines.

Can I practice internationally?

Yes. Our accreditations (IPHM, CMA) are recognized globally, allowing for insurance eligibility in 30+ countries including UK, EU, Canada & Aus.

Are payment plans available?

Yes! We partner with Klarna and Affirm, plus offer in-house financing starting at $199/month.

Will I be able to order labs?

Yes! Through our partnership with Rupa Health, graduates can order functional labs legally for clients.

Is there a refund policy?

We offer a 14-day risk-free trial. If it's not for you, get a full refund, no questions asked.

Are there live components?

Yes. We offer daily live office hours, Q&A sessions with faculty, and small group coaching circles. All sessions are recorded.

How does this differ from Nutrition programs?

We go beyond dietary theory. Functional Medicine addresses root causes like stress, sleep, toxins, and genetics using a systems-biology approach.

Is there post-grad support?

Absolutely. You retain lifetime access to the community and updates. Plus, access our Alumni Business Directory.

What are the tech requirements?

Just a computer/tablet with internet access. Our platform is cloud-based. PDF readers are recommended for resources.

Is there a deadline to finish?

No. You have lifetime access to the course materials. You can take as long as you need to complete the certification.

Can I access the course on mobile?

Yes, our learning platform is fully responsive and mobile-friendly, so you can learn on the go from your phone or tablet.

Is there a student community?

Yes! We have a vibrant private community where students share wins, ask questions, and form study groups.

How are exams structured?

Modules have short quizzes. The final is a 100-question open-book exam. You have multiple attempts to pass with 80%.

When is the next cohort?

Enrollment is currently open! You can start immediately upon registration. Cohorts for live calls form monthly.
