Attract Through Value

Content marketing attracts ideal clients by demonstrating expertise and providing value before they ever pay you. Great content builds trust, positions you as an expert, and warms up leads over time.

Content Pillars

3-5 Core Topics

  • Pillars are themes you consistently create content around
  • Related to your niche and ideal client's problems
  • Example for gut health practitioner:
  • 1. Gut health basics and education
  • 2. Food and nutrition strategies
  • 3. Lifestyle factors (stress, sleep)
  • 4. Client success stories / inspiration
  • 5. Behind the scenes / personal content

The 80/20 Content Rule

Value vs. Promotion

  • 80% value: Education, inspiration, entertainment‚Äîno ask
  • 20% promotion: Calls to action, offers, enrollment opportunities
  • Build trust before making the ask
  • Most practitioners invert this ratio and wonder why content doesn't work

Calls to Action (When You Do Promote)

  • Soft CTA: "DM me questions" / "Comment below"
  • Lead magnet: "Download my free guide"
  • Discovery call: "Book a free call"
  • Direct enrollment: "Enrollment is now open"

Content Calendar

Building a Sustainable Rhythm

  • Determine frequency: What can you maintain? (3x/week is good starting point)
  • Batch creation: Create a week's content in one sitting
  • Mix pillars: Rotate through your content pillars
  • Plan launches: Build content toward offers/enrollments
  • Tools: Notion, Trello, Asana, or simple spreadsheet

Sample Weekly Content Mix

  • Monday: Educational post (pillar 1)
  • Wednesday: Client story or case study
  • Friday: Personal or behind-the-scenes
  • Email: Weekly newsletter (repurpose best content)
