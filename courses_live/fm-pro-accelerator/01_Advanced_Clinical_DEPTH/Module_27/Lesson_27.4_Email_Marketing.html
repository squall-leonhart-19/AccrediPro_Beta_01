Own Your Audience

Social media reach can disappear overnight. Your email list is the only marketing asset you truly own. Email marketing consistently outperforms social in conversion rates and ROI.

Why Email Matters

Email vs. Social Media

  • You own the list: Algorithm can't take it away
  • Direct inbox access: More personal than feed
  • Higher conversion: Email converts 3-5x better than social
  • Longer format: Room for deeper connection
  • Segmentation: Target specific groups with relevant content

Building Your List

Lead Magnets That Work

  • Free guide/checklist: "7-Day Gut Healing Guide"
  • Quiz: "What's Your Hormone Type?"
  • Mini course: 3-5 email or video series
  • Challenge: "5-Day Sugar Detox"
  • Template/resource: Meal plan, shopping list

Growing Your List

  • Social media: Regular mentions of your freebie
  • Website opt-in: Pop-up, header, footer forms
  • Webinars: Registration requires email
  • Podcast/blog: Content upgrades for specific posts
  • In-person events: Collect emails at speaking gigs

Weekly Newsletters

Newsletter Best Practices

  • Consistent schedule: Same day/time each week
  • One main topic: Don't overwhelm
  • Personal tone: Write like you're talking to one person
  • Value first: Most emails = pure value, occasional pitch
  • Clear CTA: One action you want them to take

Newsletter Format Ideas

  • One tip: Weekly health tip with explanation
  • Q&A: Answer a reader question
  • Story + lesson: Personal anecdote with takeaway
  • Curated: Best things you read/listened to this week
  • Behind the scenes: What's happening in your practice

Subject Lines and Deliverability

Subject Lines That Get Opened

  • Curiosity: "The one food I removed that changed everything"
  • Specificity: "Why your fatigue won't go away (and what to do)"
  • Personal: Use first name; conversational tone
  • Short: 4-7 words optimal
  • Avoid spam triggers: ALL CAPS, excessive punctuation, "FREE"

Staying Out of Spam

  • Use confirmed opt-in: Double opt-in protects list quality
  • Clean your list: Remove inactive subscribers
  • Authenticate domain: SPF, DKIM, DMARC
  • Consistent sending: Don't go silent then blast
  • Easy unsubscribe: Required and keeps list healthy
