Communication Is Service

How you communicate is as important as what you know. Excellent communication builds trust, improves outcomes, and creates raving fans.

Active Listening

The HEAR Framework

  • H - Halt: Stop what you're doing, give full attention
  • E - Engage: Make eye contact, nod, show you're present
  • A - Anticipate: Be curious about what they'll say next
  • R - Replay: Reflect back what you heard

Reflection Techniques

  • Paraphrase: "So what I'm hearing is..."
  • Emotion reflection: "It sounds like that was frustrating..."
  • Clarifying questions: "Tell me more about..."
  • Summarize: "Let me make sure I understand..."

Setting Expectations

Expectation-Setting Best Practices

  • Timeline: Be realistic, not optimistic
  • Variability: "Most clients see... but everyone is different"
  • The journey: Prepare for ups and downs
  • Their role: What you expect from them
  • Your role: What you will and won't do

Under-Promise, Over-Deliver

  • Set conservative expectations
  • Then exceed them
  • Result: Delighted clients, not disappointed ones

Difficult Conversations

Common Difficult Situations

  • Non-compliance: Client not following recommendations
  • Poor results: Things not working as hoped
  • Scope: Client asking for things outside your expertise
  • Boundaries: Client overstepping
  • Cancellation: Ending the relationship

Framework for Difficult Conversations

  • State observation: "I've noticed that..."
  • Express impact: "This matters because..."
  • Ask for perspective: "What's your experience?"
  • Explore solutions: "What would help?"
  • Agree on next steps: Clear actions

Written Communication

Email Best Practices

  • Clear subject line: Include action if needed
  • Lead with the point: Don't bury the request
  • Keep it short: Busy clients skim
  • Action items: Make next steps clear
  • Professional tone: Warm but professional
  • Proofread: Errors undermine credibility

Protocol Summaries

  • Simple language: Avoid jargon
  • Visual organization: Headers, bullets, spacing
  • Prioritize: Most important first
  • Rationale: Brief why for each recommendation
  • Contact info: How to reach you with questions
