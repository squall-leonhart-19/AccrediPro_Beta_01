When Fatigue Won't Lift

Chronic Fatigue Syndrome (CFS/ME) and Long COVID share overlapping features: crushing fatigue, post-exertional malaise, cognitive dysfunction, and autonomic issues. These clients require careful, paced support that conventional medicine often fails to provide.

Understanding the Conditions

Chronic Fatigue Syndrome (ME/CFS)

  • Profound fatigue lasting >6 months, not improved by rest
  • Post-exertional malaise (PEM): Crash after physical/mental exertion
  • Unrefreshing sleep despite adequate hours
  • Cognitive dysfunction ("brain fog")
  • Orthostatic intolerance (can't stand for long)
  • Often triggered by viral infection, stress, or trauma

Long COVID

  • Symptoms persisting >4 weeks post-COVID infection
  • Many overlaps with CFS: fatigue, brain fog, PEM
  • May include: dysautonomia (POTS), shortness of breath, chest pain
  • Mechanisms: persistent inflammation, microclots, viral persistence, immune dysregulation

Pacing: The Non-Negotiable

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical Concept

Post-Exertional Malaise (PEM) means that pushing through fatigue makes these clients WORSE, not better. "Exercise" and "graded exercise therapy" can be harmful. Pacing is essential‚Äîstaying within the "energy envelope."

Energy Envelope Approach

  • Identify limits: What activity level triggers crashes?
  • Stay under threshold: Do 50-70% of what feels possible
  • Build slowly: Increase by 10% only after 2+ weeks of stability
  • Rest preemptively: Before feeling exhausted
  • Balance activity types: Physical, mental, emotional all count

Mitochondrial Dysfunction

Both CFS and Long COVID often involve impaired cellular energy production:

Mitochondrial Support Protocol

  • CoQ10 (ubiquinol): 200-400mg daily; critical for electron transport
  • D-ribose: 5g 2-3x daily; ATP precursor
  • L-carnitine: 1-2g daily; transports fatty acids into mitochondria
  • B vitamins: Complex for energy cycles, especially B1, B2, B3
  • Magnesium: ATP requires magnesium to function
  • Alpha-lipoic acid: 300-600mg; antioxidant for mitochondria

Additional Therapeutic Targets

Inflammation and Immune Modulation

  • Omega-3: 3g EPA+DHA for anti-inflammatory effect
  • Low-histamine diet: Many CFS/Long COVID have histamine intolerance
  • Quercetin: Mast cell stabilizer; 500-1000mg twice daily
  • Vitamin D: Optimize to 50-70 ng/mL

Autonomic/Dysautonomia Support

  • Salt loading: 5-10g sodium daily for POTS (with medical clearance)
  • Hydration: 2-3 liters daily, with electrolytes
  • Compression: Compression garments for blood pooling
  • Lying-down exercise: Recumbent bike, swimming when possible

Sleep and HPA Axis

  • Sleep optimization: Priority‚Äîmelatonin, magnesium, sleep hygiene
  • Adaptogens: Ashwagandha, rhodiola (start low, these are stimulating)
  • Adrenal support: Licorice root carefully if low cortisol

What NOT to Do

Avoid These Mistakes

  • Pushing exercise: Can cause severe setbacks lasting weeks/months
  • Too many supplements at once: Sensitivities are common; introduce slowly
  • Detox protocols: Often too aggressive for fragile systems
  • Telling them it's "just stress": Invalidates real pathophysiology
  • Expecting quick fixes: Recovery can take years; manage expectations
