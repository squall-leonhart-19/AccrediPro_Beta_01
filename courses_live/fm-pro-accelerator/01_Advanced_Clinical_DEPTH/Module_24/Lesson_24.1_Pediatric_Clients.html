Common Pediatric Presentations

Picky Eating

  • Normalize: Developmentally typical in toddlers/preschoolers
  • Rule out: Zinc deficiency (affects taste), gut issues (discomfort), sensory issues
  • Strategies: No-pressure exposure, involve kids in cooking, minimize snacking before meals
  • Supplements: Quality multivitamin as "insurance" during picky phases

ADHD and Focus Issues

  • Diet foundation: Blood sugar stability is critical‚Äîprotein at every meal
  • Eliminate: Artificial colors, flavors, preservatives; trial gluten/dairy-free
  • Key nutrients: Omega-3 (EPA/DHA), magnesium, zinc, iron (check ferritin)
  • Gut-brain: Address any GI issues‚Äîgut bacteria affect neurotransmitters

Recurrent Infections

  • Immune support: Vitamin D (optimize levels), zinc, vitamin C
  • Gut focus: 70% of immune system is in gut‚Äîprobiotics, diverse diet
  • History: Frequent antibiotics? May need microbiome restoration
  • Sleep: Ensure adequate sleep for immune function

Allergies and Eczema

  • Gut connection: Leaky gut often underlying‚Äîheal gut first
  • Identify triggers: Food diary, consider IgE testing for true allergies
  • Support: Omega-3s, vitamin D, probiotics (strain matters‚ÄîLactobacillus rhamnosus GG)
  • Topical: Reduce irritants, use gentle products (not your scope but can advise)

Working with Parents

Coaching the Whole Family

  • Parents implement: You coach the parent, not the child directly (especially young kids)
  • Family meals: Changes should apply to everyone, not single out the child
  • Manage expectations: Kids' palates develop over time; progress over perfection
  • Model behavior: Parents eating vegetables matters more than lectures
  • Reduce pressure: Power struggles backfire; maintain neutral stance on food

Red Flags Requiring Medical Referral

üö® Refer Immediately

  • Severe abdominal pain, bloody stool
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Severe food refusal (feeding disorder territory)
  • Signs of eating disorder in older children/teens
  • Depression, self-harm, suicidal ideation
