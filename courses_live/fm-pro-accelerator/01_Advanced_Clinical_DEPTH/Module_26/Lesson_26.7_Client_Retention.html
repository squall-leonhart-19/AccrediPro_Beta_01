The Client Lifecycle

Beyond the First Package

  • Active client: Currently in a program
  • Maintenance: Goals achieved, periodic check-ins
  • Alumni: Completed programs, not currently active
  • Returning: Re-engaging after time away
  • Each stage needs different touchpoints

Touchpoint Strategies

Active Clients

  • Between-session check-ins: Quick email or message
  • Celebrate wins: Acknowledge progress, no matter how small
  • Personalization: Remember their life details
  • Progress summaries: Monthly or quarterly recap

Maintenance Clients

  • Quarterly check-ins: Touchbase even without appointments
  • Seasonal recommendations: Immune prep, summer hydration, etc.
  • Lab check reminders: "Time for your annual labs!"
  • Group offerings: Challenges, workshops

Alumni/Past Clients

  • Newsletter: Stay on radar with valuable content
  • Anniversary touchpoints: "It's been a year since we worked together!"
  • New offerings: Alert them to relevant programs
  • Referral asks: Happy clients refer happily

Renewal Conversations

Timing the Renewal

  • Don't wait until the end: Begin conversation 2-3 sessions before package ends
  • Review progress: What have they achieved?
  • Identify remaining goals: What's left to accomplish?
  • Present options: Continuation, maintenance, new focus

Renewal Script Example

  • "We've made amazing progress on [specific wins]. I'd love to talk about what's next for you."
  • "There are a few options: continuing to work on [remaining goals], transitioning to maintenance, or addressing [new area you've noticed]."
  • "What feels right to you?"

Ongoing Offerings

Ways to Keep Clients Engaged

  • Maintenance packages: Quarterly check-ins at reduced rate
  • Group coaching: Lower-touch, lower-cost continuing support
  • Membership: Monthly access, Q&As, resources
  • Seasonal programs: Detoxes, challenges, resets
  • VIP days: Deep-dive intensives for returning clients

Reactivating Past Clients

Reactivation Strategies

  • Personal outreach: "I was thinking about you and wanted to check in"
  • New offering announcement: "I've got a new program you'd be perfect for"
  • Seasonal prompt: "As we head into [season], I wanted to touch base"
  • Limited-time incentive: Special rate for returning clients
