What to Automate

High-Impact Automations

  • Appointment reminders: Email/SMS 24 and 1 hour before
  • Intake form delivery: Auto-send when client books
  • Welcome sequences: Email series for new clients
  • Follow-up emails: Post-session check-ins
  • Payment invoicing: Recurring payments for packages
  • Calendar booking: Clients self-schedule

Sample Automation Flow

  • Trigger: Client books discovery call
  • Action 1: Send confirmation email with prep questions
  • Action 2: Add to CRM with "prospect" tag
  • Action 3: Send reminder 24 hours before
  • Action 4: After call, auto-send proposal or follow-up

Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

Why SOPs Matter

  • Consistency: Every client gets the same quality experience
  • Delegation: Eventually you can hand off tasks
  • Memory: You won't remember every step forever
  • Scaling: Required to grow beyond yourself

SOPs to Create

  • New client onboarding process
  • Session preparation checklist
  • Post-session documentation process
  • Follow-up scheduling process
  • Lab ordering and interpretation workflow
  • Weekly admin tasks checklist

Time Management

Time Blocking for Practitioners

  • Client sessions: Batch into 2-3 days (avoid scattered)
  • Admin time: Dedicated blocks (not stolen between clients)
  • Content creation: Specific day or morning
  • Learning: Protected time for professional development
  • Off days: Actual days off (burnout prevention)

Sample Weekly Structure

  • Monday: Admin, prep, planning
  • Tuesday-Thursday: Client sessions
  • Friday: Content, marketing, learning
  • Weekend: Off (no exceptions)

  • Time block to protect client time and admin time
