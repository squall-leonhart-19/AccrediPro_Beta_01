Ideal Client Avatar

Why Specificity Matters

  • "Everyone" is no one: Generic marketing attracts no one
  • Specific problems resonate: "Tired moms over 40" hits differently than "people who are tired"
  • Easier to find them: Know where they hang out online and offline
  • Content becomes easy: Speak directly to their struggles

Avatar Questions

  • Demographics: Age, gender, location, occupation, income level
  • Struggles: What keeps them up at night? What have they tried?
  • Desires: What transformation do they want?
  • Objections: Why haven't they solved this yet?
  • Language: How do they describe their problem?
  • Watering holes: Where do they spend time online?

Referral Strategies

Building a Referral Engine

  • Deliver exceptional results: Best marketing is a happy client
  • Ask for referrals: Most clients will if you simply ask
  • Create a referral program: Discount or gift for successful referrals
  • Stay in touch: Email past clients; they forget you exist
  • Make it easy: Provide share links, refer-a-friend cards

Professional Referrals

  • Build relationships with: MDs, chiropractors, acupuncturists, therapists, trainers
  • How: Send a thank-you note after mutual referrals
  • Co-marketing: Cross-promote each other's services
  • Lunch-and-learns: Educate local practitioners on what you do

Lead Magnets and Funnels

What's a Lead Magnet?

  • Free resource offered in exchange for email address
  • Demonstrates expertise and builds trust
  • Starts relationship before they're ready to buy
  • Allows you to nurture over time via email

Lead Magnet Ideas

  • Guides: "7-Day Gut Reset Plan," "Hormone Balancing Checklist"
  • Quizzes: "What's Your Fatigue Type?" (highly engaging)
  • Cheat sheets: "Anti-Inflammatory Foods Shopping List"
  • Mini courses: 3-5 video series on a specific topic
  • Challenges: "5-Day Sugar Detox Challenge"

Simple Funnel Structure

  • Step 1: Lead magnet offer (landing page)
  • Step 2: Nurture email sequence (5-7 emails)
  • Step 3: Offer discovery call or low-ticket entry point
  • Step 4: Sales conversation ‚Üí client
