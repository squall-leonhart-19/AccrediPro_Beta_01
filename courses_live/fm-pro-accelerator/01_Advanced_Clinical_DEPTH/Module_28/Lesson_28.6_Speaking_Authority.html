Position Yourself as the Expert

Speaking and authority-building activities position you as the go-to expert in your niche, attracting clients and opportunities that come to you.

Why Authority Matters

Benefits of Authority Positioning

  • Clients come to you: Less time chasing, more inbound
  • Premium pricing: Experts command higher rates
  • Opportunities open: Speaking, partnerships, media
  • Trust built faster: Third-party validation
  • Referrals increase: "She's the gut health expert"

Speaking Opportunities

Types of Speaking

  • Local: Libraries, community centers, gyms, churches
  • Professional: Conferences, associations, continuing education
  • Corporate: Lunch and learns, wellness workshops
  • Virtual: Summits, webinars, online conferences
  • Other people's audiences: Guest expert in groups/courses

Getting Booked

  • Create a speaker page: Topics, bio, photos, testimonials
  • Start small: Local, free, to build experience and reel
  • Pitch actively: Research events, reach out to organizers
  • Network: Other speakers refer each other
  • Deliver value: Word spreads when you're excellent

Podcast Guesting

Getting on Podcasts

  • Find relevant shows: Your niche, your ideal client's interests
  • Listen first: Understand the show before pitching
  • Pitch value: What will their audience gain?
  • Create a media kit: Bio, topics, headshot, sample questions
  • Services: PodMatch, Podcast Guests, direct outreach

On the Podcast

  • Provide massive value: Don't hold back
  • Tell stories: More engaging than pure information
  • Have a clear CTA: Where to find you, what to download
  • Promote the episode: Share when it goes live

Media Features

Getting Featured

  • HARO (Help a Reporter Out): Journalists seeking sources
  • Guest articles: Write for industry publications
  • Local media: Health segments, lifestyle features
  • Expert roundups: Industry blogs seeking quotes
  • Press releases: For notable events or milestones

Building Your Platform

Long-Term Authority Building

  • Consistent content: Blog, podcast, YouTube, newsletter
  • Book: Ultimate authority builder (even self-published)
  • Case studies: Document your results
  • Certifications: Continued education and credentials
  • Community: Build your following over time

‚ú® Key Takeaways

  • Authority positioning = clients coming to you
  • Speaking: Start local, build to conferences
  • Podcast guesting: Pitch value to their audience
  • Media: HARO, guest articles, local features
  • Long game: Consistent content and platform building
