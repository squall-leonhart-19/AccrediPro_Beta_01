Types of Online Courses

Course Formats

  • Mini course: $27-97, solves one specific problem, 3-5 modules
  • Signature course: $197-997, comprehensive transformation, flagship offering
  • High-ticket course: $1,000+, includes coaching, community, premium support
  • Tripwire: $7-27, low-cost entry to build trust, upsell to larger offers

Self-Paced vs. Live

  • Self-paced: Evergreen, sell anytime, lower touch, lower completion
  • Live cohort: Higher touch, better completion, launches only
  • Hybrid: Self-paced content + live Q&A calls

Course Creation Process

Step 1: Validate the Idea

  • What problem do they have? (Specific, painful)
  • What transformation do they want?
  • Would they pay for it? (Survey, pre-sell)
  • Do you have proven expertise? (Results from 1:1 work)

Step 2: Outline the Curriculum

  • Start with the end: What can they do/be after?
  • Work backwards: What steps get them there?
  • Modules: Big chunks (typically 4-8)
  • Lessons: Bite-sized within modules (5-15 min videos)
  • Less is more: Cut ruthlessly

Step 3: Create the Content

  • Video: Talking head, slides, screenshare
  • Audio: Optional, for on-the-go learners
  • Workbooks/PDFs: Implementation tools
  • Production: Good enough is good enough‚Äîperfectionism kills courses
  • Tools: Loom, Canva, basic webcam + mic

Platforms

Course Hosting Options

  • Teachable: Beginner-friendly, good marketing features
  • Thinkific: Similar to Teachable, good free tier
  • Kajabi: All-in-one (courses + email + website)
  • Podia: Simple, good for smaller creators
  • Practice Better: Integrated with practice management

Pricing Your Course

Pricing Factors

  • Value of transformation: What's it worth to solve this problem?
  • Depth of content: Mini vs. signature vs. high-ticket
  • Support included: Community, Q&A, 1:1 access
  • Your audience: What can they afford?
  • Test and iterate: Start lower, raise over time

Marketing Your Course

Launch Strategies

  • Webinar funnel: Free training ‚Üí course offer
  • Challenge: 5-day challenge ‚Üí course as next step
  • Email sequence: Nurture list, then open cart
  • Open/close cart: Limited enrollment windows
  • Evergreen: Available anytime with automated funnel
