Upon completion of the Master DEPTH Track, practitioners will:

1. **Analyze cases** using the complete DEPTH Model and 7 Master Patterns
2. **Build authority** through case documentation and unique clinical voice
3. **Communicate professionally** in complex and difficult situations
4. **Develop specialty expertise** in their chosen niche
5. **Master deep patterns** including HPA, gut, metabolic, autoimmune, hormone, toxicity, and inflammation
6. **Integrate all systems** for confident clinical decision-making
7. **Plan for lifelong mastery** and continued professional growth
