# Practice Path Track Curriculum
## Pro Accelerator™ - Modules 36-42

**Track Focus:** Practice operations, clinical protocols, case applications, business building, growth strategies, specialization, and certification completion.

**Prerequisites:** Foundation Track (Modules 1-21), Advanced Clinical DEPTH (Modules 22-29), Master DEPTH (Modules 30-35)

**Target Audience:** Practitioners ready to launch or scale their functional medicine practice.

## Track Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of the Practice Path Track, practitioners will:

1. **Operate a professional practice** with proper systems, documentation, and client journey
2. **Implement clinical protocols** for all 7 core treatment categories
3. **Apply protocols to real cases** across common presentations
4. **Build a sustainable business** with proper pricing, acquisition, and legal compliance
5. **Scale strategically** while preventing burnout
6. **Develop specialty expertise** in their chosen area
7. **Complete certification** and launch their practice

## Assessment & Certification

### Written Exam (50 questions)
- Multiple choice and short answer
- Covers core concepts from all 42 modules
- 80% passing score required

### Case Study Submission
- Complete case analysis demonstrating clinical reasoning
- Pattern identification and protocol selection
- Evaluated by clinical faculty

### Practice Plan Submission
- Business model and pricing
- Client journey documentation
- Systems and legal compliance
- 1-year growth plan

### Credential Awarded
- **AccrediPro Certified Functional Medicine Professional**
- Digital badge + printed certificate
- Listed in practitioner directory
- Alumni network access
