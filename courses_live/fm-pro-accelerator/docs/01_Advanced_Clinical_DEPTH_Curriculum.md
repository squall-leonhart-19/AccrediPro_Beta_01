## Track Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of the Advanced Clinical DEPTH Track, practitioners will:

1. **Assess multi-system clients** with confidence and systematic approach
2. **Interpret advanced functional testing** including stool, OAT, DUTCH, and comprehensive panels
3. **Recognize the 7 Master Patterns** and understand their interconnections
4. **Prioritize treatment** using decision tree logic and upstream/downstream thinking
5. **Implement core protocols** for HPA, gut, inflammation, detox, and hormones
6. **Manage complex cases** with appropriate treatment adjustments
7. **Handle difficult clinical situations** including non-responders and referrals
8. **Prepare for mastery** with integrated case analysis skills
