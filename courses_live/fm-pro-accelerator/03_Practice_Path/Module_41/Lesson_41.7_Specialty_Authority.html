Authority Building Strategies

  • Teaching: Train other practitioners in your approach

  • Collaborate with other experts in adjacent fields
  • Create a signature methodology or framework
  • Document case studies and success stories

Timeline

  • Year 1-2: Develop expertise, see cases, create content
  • Year 3-5: Publish, speak, become known locally
  • Year 5+: National/international recognition, teach others
