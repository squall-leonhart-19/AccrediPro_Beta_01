The Power of Specialization

Generalists compete on price. Specialists command premium fees and attract ideal clients who specifically seek their expertise.

Specialty Areas in Functional Medicine

High-Demand Specialties

  • GI/Digestive: SIBO, IBS, IBD, gut healing
  • Hormones: Women's health, menopause, PCOS, fertility
  • Autoimmune: Hashimoto's, RA, MS, lupus
  • Metabolic: Diabetes reversal, weight loss, longevity
  • Mental Health: Anxiety, depression, brain fog
  • Fatigue/Chronic Illness: CFS, fibromyalgia, long COVID

Choosing Criteria

  • Passion: What fascinates you clinically?
  • Personal experience: Have you or loved ones struggled with this?
  • Market demand: Are people searching for help?
  • Competence: Do you have or can you develop expertise?
