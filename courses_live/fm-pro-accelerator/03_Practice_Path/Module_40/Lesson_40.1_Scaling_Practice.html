Scaling Options

Option 1: Scale Income, Not Clients

  • Raise prices (premium positioning)
  • Add passive income products (courses, ebooks)
  • Affiliate relationships (supplement companies)

Option 2: Group Programs

  • One-to-many delivery = leverage your time
  • Foundation programs that prepare clients for 1:1
  • Condition-specific group protocols

Option 3: Build a Team

  • Hire support staff (admin, health coaches)
  • Bring on associate practitioners
  • Transition from practitioner to practice owner
