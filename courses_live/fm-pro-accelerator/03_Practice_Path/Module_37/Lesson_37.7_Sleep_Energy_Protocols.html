Restoring Rest and Vitality

Sleep is foundational‚Äînothing else works if sleep is broken. This lesson covers sleep optimization and energy restoration protocols that address the root causes of fatigue and insomnia.

Sleep Protocol

Sleep Hygiene (Non-Negotiables)

  • Consistent sleep/wake times (even weekends)
  • Dark, cool room (65-68¬∞F)
  • No screens 1-2 hours before bed
  • Morning light exposure within 30 min of waking

Sleep Support Stack

  • Magnesium glycinate: 300-400mg before bed
  • L-theanine: 200mg (calming without sedation)
  • Glycine: 3g (improves sleep quality)
  • Melatonin: 0.5-1mg ONLY (low dose; not long-term)
  • Phosphatidylserine: 100-200mg if cortisol is high at night

Energy Restoration Protocol

Mitochondrial Support

  • CoQ10: 100-200mg (ubiquinol form preferred)
  • PQQ: 10-20mg (mitochondrial biogenesis)
  • B-complex: Methylated forms
  • D-ribose: 5g 2x/day (ATP substrate)
  • Alpha-lipoic acid: 300-600mg (antioxidant, energy)
