Safe and Effective Detox

Detoxification done wrong can make people feel terrible. This lesson covers safe, phased detox protocols that open drainage first and support the body's natural elimination pathways.

Detox Prerequisites

‚ö†Ô∏è Critical: Open Drainage First

Never start aggressive detox with blocked drainage. Bowels should be moving 1-2x daily, kidneys functioning, liver supported BEFORE mobilizing toxins.

Phase 1: Drainage & Foundation (2-4 weeks)

  • Bowel regularity (magnesium, fiber, hydration)
  • Kidney support (hydration 2-3L water daily)
  • Lymph movement (dry brushing, exercise, rebounding)
  • Liver support (milk thistle, NAC, cruciferous vegetables)

Phase 2: Gentle Mobilization (4-8 weeks)

  • Binders (activated charcoal, bentonite clay, chlorella)
  • Glutathione support (NAC, liposomal glutathione)
  • Sweating (sauna if tolerated)
  • Continue drainage support

Phase 3: Deep Detox (4-12 weeks)

  • Chelation (under supervision for heavy metals)
  • Extended binder protocols
  • Targeted support based on toxin type
