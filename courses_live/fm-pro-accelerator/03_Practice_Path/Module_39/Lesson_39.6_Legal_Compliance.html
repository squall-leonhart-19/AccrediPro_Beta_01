Legal Essentials

Business Structure

  • LLC or PLLC recommended (liability protection)
  • Consult accountant for tax implications
  • Register with your state

Essential Documents

  • Informed consent: Scope of practice, what you do/don't do
  • Privacy policy: HIPAA compliance for health information
  • Terms of service: Payment, cancellation, refund policies
  • Client agreement: Expectations, communication policies

Liability Insurance

  • Professional liability (malpractice) insurance required
  • Coverage typically $1M-3M
  • Research options for your credential type
