Case: Sarah, 42F - Chronic Fatigue

Chief Complaint: "I'm exhausted all the time. Even 8 hours of sleep doesn't help."

History: Fatigue progressive over 3 years. Worse after COVID infection 2 years ago. Brain fog, afternoon crashes, wakes at 3am, weight gain 15 lbs.

Timeline: High-stress job for 10 years. COVID infection 2 years ago (never felt normal after). Digestive issues started 5 years ago.

Assessment Approach

Pattern Recognition

  • HPA dysfunction (10 years high stress, 3am waking)
  • Post-viral syndrome (COVID trigger)
  • Possible gut involvement (digestive issues)
  • Metabolic involvement (weight gain)

Testing Strategy

  • Comprehensive metabolic panel + insulin
  • Thyroid full panel (TSH, free T3/T4, rT3, antibodies)
  • Adrenal hormone panel (cortisol rhythm, DHEA)
  • Inflammation markers (hs-CRP, ferritin)
  • Consider: stool test, viral reactivation panel if indicated

Treatment Plan

Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Foundation

  • Sleep optimization (sleep hygiene + magnesium, L-theanine)
  • Blood sugar stability (protein at every meal, reduce sugar)
  • Foundation supplements (Big 5)
  • Stress management basics

Phase 2 (Weeks 5-12): Targeted Support

  • HPA support (adaptogens based on stage)
  • Mitochondrial support (CoQ10, PQQ, B-vitamins)
  • Address gut if testing reveals issues
  • Anti-inflammatory protocol
