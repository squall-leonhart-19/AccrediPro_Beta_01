Case: Jennifer, 38F - Hormonal Imbalance

Chief Complaint: "My hormones are a mess. PMS, irregular periods, can't lose weight."

History: Severe PMS (mood, bloating, breast tenderness), irregular cycles (28-42 days), weight gain around middle, fatigue, hair thinning.

Timeline: High-stress job for 5 years. Symptoms progressively worse. Came off birth control 2 years ago‚Äînever normalized.

Assessment & Testing

Pattern Recognition

  • Estrogen dominance (PMS, breast tenderness, weight at middle)
  • Low progesterone (irregular cycles, post-pill not normalized)
  • HPA involvement (high stress ‚Üí progesterone steal)
  • Possible metabolic involvement (central weight gain)

Testing Strategy

  • DUTCH test (full hormone panel with metabolites)
  • Full thyroid panel
  • Fasting insulin + metabolic markers
  • Adrenal panel (cortisol rhythm)

Treatment Plan

Phase 1: Address Upstream (Weeks 1-8)

  • HPA support (adaptogens based on stage)
  • Blood sugar balance (protein, reduce refined carbs)
  • Liver support (estrogen clearance)
  • Gut health (beta-glucuronidase, estrobolome)

Phase 2: Hormone-Specific Support (Weeks 9-16)

  • DIM 100-200mg (estrogen metabolism)
  • Calcium-D-glucarate 500mg (prevent recirculation)
  • Vitex 400mg (progesterone support) if needed
  • B6 100mg (luteal phase support)
