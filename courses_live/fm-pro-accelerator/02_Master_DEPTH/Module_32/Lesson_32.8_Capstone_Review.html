Module 32 Knowledge Synthesis

32.1: Professional Presence & Authority

  • Authority is humble; arrogance is insecure

  • First 5 minutes set the tone

32.4: Managing Difficult Client Dynamics

  • Difficult behavior often masks unmet needs
  • Set boundaries with warmth

32.6: Documentation & Professional Records

  • SOAP format: Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan
  • Document within 24 hours, retain 7+ years

32.7: Continuing Education & Growth

  • 80% depth, 20% breadth
  • Consistent weekly learning compounds
