The Hidden Driver

Toxicity‚Äîfrom mold, heavy metals, or environmental chemicals‚Äîis often the unseen driver behind cases that don't respond to standard protocols. This deep dive covers identification, testing, and treatment of environmental illness.

Common Toxic Exposures

Mold & Mycotoxins (CIRS)

  • Water-damaged buildings‚Äîhome, office, school
  • Symptoms: fatigue, brain fog, joint pain, respiratory, MCS
  • Testing: mycotoxin urine panel, VCS (visual contrast sensitivity)
  • Must get OUT of exposure before treatment works

Heavy Metals

  • Lead (old paint, pipes), mercury (fish, amalgams), arsenic (water, rice)
  • Chronic low-level exposure accumulates over years
  • Testing: provoked urine metals, blood for acute, hair for chronic

Environmental Chemicals

  • Pesticides, plasticizers (BPA), flame retardants
  • Endocrine disruption, inflammation, cancer risk
  • Testing: GPL-TOX, environmental pollutants panels
