The Ethics of Synthesis

As you integrate various modalities, you must remain tethered to the Ethical Standards established in Module 0. Synthesis is not an excuse for "eclectic drifting." Every intervention must be evidence-based and within the scope of coaching practice.

The 2022 Meta-Analysis by Fish et al. demonstrated that while integrated approaches are highly effective, they must maintain clear boundaries between coaching and therapy, especially when dealing with trauma. Synthesis in coaching focuses on functional growth and future-oriented resilience, whereas clinical synthesis often focuses on pathology and deep-seated healing.
