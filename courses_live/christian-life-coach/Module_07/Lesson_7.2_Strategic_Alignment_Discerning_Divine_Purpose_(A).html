2. The Charism Framework: Spiritual Gifts in Coaching

Strategic alignment is impossible without an inventory of Charisms (Spiritual Gifts). In the F.A.I.T.H. framework, we define Charisms as supernatural empowerments given by the Holy Spirit for the building of the Church and the service of the world (1 Corinthians 12).

As a coach, you must help the client distinguish between natural talents (learned or innate abilities) and spiritual gifts (divinely infused capacities). When a client operates within their Charism, they experience what psychologists call "Flow," but with a spiritual dimension of "Grace-enabled ease."

Did You Know? Research into "Gift-Job Fit" suggests that when employees utilize their primary strengths at work, they are 6x more likely to be engaged. In a kingdom context, this engagement is amplified by spiritual resonance (Gallup, 2023; Barna Group, 2022).
