Neurologically, sustaining transformation requires moving from conscious effort (prefrontal cortex) to unconscious automation (basal ganglia). While we rely on the Holy Spirit, we also respect the biological design of the brain. The "Discipline of Grace" involves structured habits like Prayer, Fasting, and Scripture Meditation, but applied through the lens of modern habit science.

Case Study: The Corporate Executive's "Relapse"

Client: David, 48, Executive Pastor/CEO.
Situation: After 6 months of successful stress management and spiritual alignment, David suffered a "burnout episode" leading to a week of isolation and neglected disciplines.

Outcome: David returned to his routines within 48 hours, whereas previous setbacks usually led to months of regression.
