Master Practitioner Terminology

Ventral Vagal Anchor

The physiological state of safety and social engagement that allows for higher-order cognitive functions.

Neuro-Cardiac Coupling

The phenomenon where the heart rate and autonomic rhythms of two individuals (practitioner and client) synchronize.

Allostatic Load

The "wear and tear" on the body which accumulates as an individual is exposed to repeated or chronic stress.

4. PFC-Amygdala Disconnect

When a parent "loses it," they are experiencing a physiological disconnect between the PFC (the "upstairs brain") and the Amygdala (the "downstairs brain"). In this state, the Respond (R) phase of RAISE is bypassed entirely. The amygdala hijacks the system, diverting blood flow away from the areas of the brain responsible for logic, empathy, and long-term consequences.
