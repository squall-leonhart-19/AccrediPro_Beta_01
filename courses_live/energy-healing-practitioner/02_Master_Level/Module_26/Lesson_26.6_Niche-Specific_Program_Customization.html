3. Emergency vs. Growth Protocols

One of the most critical decisions a practitioner makes is the Intensity Modulation of the program. Applying a high-frequency "Growth Protocol" to a client in an energetic crisis can lead to a "healing crisis" or further depletion.

The Emergency Protocol

Goal: Stabilization and Containment.

Used when Biofield Assessment (B) shows vitality scores below 3/10. Focus is on "plugging leaks" and gentle clearing. We avoid aggressive Lifeforce Activation (L) to prevent overwhelming the system.

The Growth Protocol

Goal: Expansion and Evolution.

Used when the client is stable (Vitality 6+/10). Focus is on high-frequency resonance matching, DNA activation themes, and significant Lifeforce expansion to reach new baselines of health.
