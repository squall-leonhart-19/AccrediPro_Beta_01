4. The Deep-Dive Intake: Identifying Root-Cause Blockages

In Level 3, the intake process is not just a form; it is a diagnostic ceremony. We use specific "root-cause" questioning to identify where the energy is stagnating, which directly informs the Neutralize Blocks (N) strategy.

The "Three-Layer" Questioning Technique

1. The Symptomatic Layer: "Where do you feel the tension in your body right now?"
2. The Narrative Layer: "When this tension arises, what is the story your mind tells about your safety?"
3. The Energetic Origin Layer: "If this sensation had a color and an age, what would they be?"

By identifying the "age" of a blockage, we often find it corresponds to a developmental period where the Lifeforce Activation (L) was suppressed. This allows for surgical precision during the healing sessions.

To bridge the gap between "woo-woo" and "wellness," we integrate objective energetic markers. This provides the "proof" that many high-level clients require to fully trust the process.

Subjective Marker
