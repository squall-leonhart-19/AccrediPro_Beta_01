Neutralization is not a single event; it is an archeological process. When developing a multi-month program, Phase 1 must systematically peel back the layers of resistance that prevent the client from holding a higher frequency. We categorize these into three distinct "energetic strata":

A. Current Life Interference

This is the most superficial layer, consisting of recent stressors, emotional residue from daily interactions, and digital fragmentation. Neutralizing this layer provides immediate symptomatic relief and builds client confidence.

B. Childhood & Developmental Imprints

These are the "foundational frequencies" established during the first seven years of life. Using the Haptic Scanning techniques learned in Module 1, you will often find these as dense "nodes" in the Solar Plexus or Heart centers.
