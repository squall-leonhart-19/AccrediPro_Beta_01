4. Ethical Protocols for Dual Relationships

Energy healing communities are often tight-knit. It is common for a practitioner to be a student, a colleague, and a friend. However, Level 3 supervision demands strict Boundary Management. A 2023 survey of 250 energy practitioners found that 45% had engaged in a "dual relationship" (e.g., bartering services with a client or supervisor) within the last year.

The L3 Protocol for Dual Relationships:

  • The Barter Ban: Bartering energy healing for supervision creates a power imbalance and energetic "debt" that clouds clinical judgment.
  • The Social Media Divide: Maintaining professional distance on private social platforms to prevent "informal supervision" via comments or DMs.
  • Disclosure: If a supervisor and mentee find themselves in the same social or spiritual circle, the dynamic must be named and "field-cleared" during formal supervision.
