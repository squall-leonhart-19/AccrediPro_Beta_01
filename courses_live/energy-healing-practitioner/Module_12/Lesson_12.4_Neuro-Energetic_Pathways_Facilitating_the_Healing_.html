The Priming Protocol: Aligning the Belief System

The "A" phase is significantly enhanced when the client's cognitive belief system is aligned with the energetic work. This is known as Priming. Priming is not "positive thinking"; it is the intentional preparation of the neural pathways to receive and integrate new energetic data.

The 3-Step Priming Protocol:

  1. Somatic Anchoring: Ask the client to identify a place in their body that feels "safe" or "neutral."
  2. Expectancy Setting: Briefly explain the "Healing Trigger" so the client isn't startled by involuntary twitches or temperature shifts.
  3. The Observer Shift: Encourage the client to observe their sensations without judgment, moving from "victim of symptoms" to "witness of repair."
