5. Assessing 'Spin' and Velocity

Energy centers (vortices) are not static; they are dynamic engines of frequency. In this assessment phase, we move the hand in a small circular motion approximately 3-4 inches above a chakra to sense its rotational velocity.

A healthy center exhibits a clockwise spin (from the practitioner's perspective looking at the client) with a consistent, moderate velocity.

  • Counter-clockwise spin: Often indicates energy being expelled or a "defensive" posture in that center.
  • Erratic spin: Indicates emotional instability or "fluttering" in the nervous system.
  • Zero spin (Stillness): Indicates a dormant or severely blocked center, often seen in long-term chronic conditions.
