As you neutralize deep blocks, the client may experience what is known as a Healing Crisis (or a Herxheimer-like reaction in the biofield). This occurs when stagnant energy is released into the system faster than the physical and energetic "waste" channels can process it.

Common Symptoms of a Healing Crisis:

For the most persistent blocks, we utilize the Neutralization Vacuum. This is a specific hand configuration (mudra) that creates a high-pressure energetic gradient, effectively "sucking" the density out of the client's field and into a neutralized state.

Technique Steps:

  1. The Setup: Fold the ring and middle fingers of your dominant hand into the palm, holding them with the thumb. Keep the index and pinky fingers extended (the "Antenna" position).
  2. The Engagement: Place the extended fingers 2-3 inches above the block site.
