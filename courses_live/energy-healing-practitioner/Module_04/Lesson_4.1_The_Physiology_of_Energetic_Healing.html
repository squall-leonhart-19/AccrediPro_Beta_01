6. Alignment vs. Active Induction

It is crucial for the professional practitioner to understand the difference between Module 2 (Align Chakras) and Module 4 (Activate Healing). While they often happen in sequence, their goals differ significantly.

Key Distinctions

Energetic Alignment (Module 2)

Focuses on the geometry of the field. Ensuring the vortices are spinning at the correct frequency and are centered. It is the "tuning of the instrument."

Healing Activation (Module 4)

Focuses on the output of the field. Inducing a specific physiological response like stem cell recruitment or cytokine modulation. It is the "playing of the music."

Without alignment, activation is less efficient because the "wires" of the energetic system are crossed. However, alignment alone may not be enough to overcome deep-seated chronic pathology. Activation requires the practitioner to hold a specific healing frequency that matches the tissue's resonant frequency for repair (which we will cover in Lesson 3 of this module).
