6. Maintaining Presence Without Depletion

A common mistake for new practitioners is "pushing" energy. This stems from a misunderstanding of the Observer Effect. Observation is an act of receptive focus, not of force. When you "push," you use your own metabolic energy (ATP), leading to exhaustion. When you "observe," you are simply the lens through which the universal lifeforce (Qi/Prana) is focused.
