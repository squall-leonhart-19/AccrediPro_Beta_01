5. Shielding vs. Closing: Trauma Responses

One of the most common mistakes new practitioners make is misidentifying a "closed" heart. In response to trauma, the energy field may react in two distinct ways:

  • Energetic Closing: A defensive withdrawal where the chakra "contracts" and becomes small and dense. This often results in emotional numbness and physical chest tightness.
  • Energetic Shielding: A protective layer around the heart. The chakra remains active, but a secondary "shell" of energy prevents outside influence from penetrating.

Understanding this distinction is vital for the Activate Healing (A) phase of our protocol. Forcing a closed heart to open can re-traumatize the client. Instead, we work with the shield first, acknowledging its protective function before inviting the core energy to expand.
