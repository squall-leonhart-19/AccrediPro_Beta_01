4. Mineral Intelligence: Crystals and Piezoelectricity

Crystals are not merely "pretty stones"; they are highly organized molecular lattices. In physics, quartz is known for its piezoelectric effect. Practitioners use crystals as frequency stabilizers. Placing a crystal on a chakra acts like a "tuning fork" that remains constant, helping the client's fluctuating energy field find its center.
