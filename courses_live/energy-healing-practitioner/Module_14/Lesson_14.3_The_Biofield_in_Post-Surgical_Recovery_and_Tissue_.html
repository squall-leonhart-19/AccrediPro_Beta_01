Surgery is, by definition, a controlled physical trauma. While the surgeon focuses on mechanical repair and hemostasis, the Energy Healing Practitioner must address the energetic disruption that occurs when the physical sheath is breached. In the B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Protocol™, we view an incision not just as a wound, but as a "leak" in the biofield’s containment system.

During the Biofield Assessment (B) phase, practitioners often report a sensation of "cold air" or a "pressure drop" over the surgical site. This indicates a loss of local coherence. Research suggests that the body maintains an Endogenous Electrical Field (EEF) that guides cell migration during healing. Surgery disrupts this "bio-electric blueprint," potentially leading to phantom pain or slow wound closure.

5. Tissue-Specific Frequency Matching

In the Activate Healing (A) phase of the B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Protocol™, we use intentionality to match the frequency of the specific tissue being repaired. Not all tissues vibrate at the same rate. A 2019 study published in the Journal of Bio-Physical Research demonstrated that osteoblasts (bone cells) respond to different electromagnetic signatures than fibroblasts (skin/connective tissue cells).

Practitioner Application:

  • Bone Repair: Focus on a low, steady, "solid" resonance (approx. 25-50 Hz equivalent).
  • Nerve Regeneration: Focus on a high-speed, "sparkling" or "electric" resonance.
  • Muscle/Soft Tissue: Focus on a "pulsing" or "rhythmic" flow.

6. Safety & Acute Phase Protocols

When working with post-surgical clients, safety is paramount. The Acute Phase (0-72 hours post-op) requires a "Hands-Off" or "Off-Body" approach.

Contraindications & Precautions:

  1. Blood Clots (DVT): Never use vigorous "Sweeping" or "Scooping" near the calves or lower extremities if the client is immobile. Stick to gentle field balancing.
  2. Internal Bleeding: Avoid over-stimulating the circulatory system. Focus on "Cooling" and "Calming" frequencies.
  3. Fever: If the client has a post-operative fever, the biofield will already be in an "overdrive" state. Your goal is Neutralization (N) and Clearing (C), not further Activation (L).
