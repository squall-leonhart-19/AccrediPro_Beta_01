6. Ethics and Crisis Scope

As an Energy Healing Practitioner, you are a stabilizer, not a crisis counselor or medical doctor. It is vital to understand the "Red Flags" that require immediate referral to emergency services or licensed mental health professionals.

Scope of Practice Warning

If a client expresses active suicidal ideation, manifests signs of a stroke (FAST: Face drooping, Arm weakness, Speech difficulty, Time), or is in the midst of a severe psychotic break, energetic stabilization is secondary to medical intervention. Always have a referral list of local crisis centers and emergency contacts readily available.
