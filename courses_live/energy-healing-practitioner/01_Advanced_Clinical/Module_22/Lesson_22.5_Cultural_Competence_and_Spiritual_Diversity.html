2. Cultural Appropriation vs. Appreciation

In the modern wellness industry, the line between honoring a tradition and appropriating it is often blurred. Cultural Appropriation involves taking elements of a marginalized culture without permission, credit, or understanding, often for profit or social status. Cultural Appreciation, conversely, involves deep study, acknowledging the source, and respecting the sacred nature of the tools used.

Trait Cultural Appropriation Cultural Appreciation
Source Acknowledgement Ignored or rebranded as "new discovery." Explicitly cited and honored.

Power Dynamics Dominant culture profiting from marginalized roots. Supporting the lineage and its practitioners.
Sacredness Treating sacred symbols as "trendy" decor. Maintaining ritual integrity and respect.
