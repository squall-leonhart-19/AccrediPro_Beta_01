Miasms: The Ancestral Energetic Footprint

The term "miasm" originates from Greek (miasma, meaning "stain" or "pollution"), historically used in homeopathy to describe inherited predispositions to disease. In Advanced Biofield Science, we define a miasm as a deeply embedded frequency pattern within the causal or mental layers of the biofield that predisposes the individual to specific physical or psychological pathologies.

Unlike transient blockages caused by daily stress, miasms act like "energetic templates." They are often inherited (epigenetic energetic imprints) or formed during significant early-life trauma. Detecting them requires scanning the outer subtle bodies (the 5th, 6th, and 7th layers) rather than just the etheric layer closest to the skin.
