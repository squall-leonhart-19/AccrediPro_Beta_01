5. Measuring Long-Term Success: Resilience Metrics

How do we know the Empowerment Model is working? We track Resilience Metrics. In energy medicine, resilience is defined as the speed at which a biofield returns to a state of coherence after a stressor.

Practitioners should track:

  1. Heart Rate Variability (HRV): An objective proxy for vagal tone and energetic adaptability.
  2. Recovery Time: How long does the client feel "off" after a stressful encounter? (Decreasing over time).
  3. Somatic Literacy: The client's ability to accurately identify where in their body they are holding energetic tension.
