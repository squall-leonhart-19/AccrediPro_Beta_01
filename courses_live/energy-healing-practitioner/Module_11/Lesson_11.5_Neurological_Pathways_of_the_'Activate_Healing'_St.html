The Biology of the 'Healing Crisis'

As a practitioner, you must understand the Herxheimer-like reaction, often called a "Healing Crisis." When the body moves from Survival to Repair, the sudden activation of the immune system and the clearing of energetic blocks can lead to temporary physical discomfort. This is not a sign of failure, but a sign of metabolic mobilization.

Biologically, this occurs because:

  1. Cytokine Release: As the biofield clears, dormant immune cells may activate, releasing cytokines that cause flu-like symptoms.
  2. Metabolic Waste: Improved circulation flushes stored cellular toxins into the bloodstream for processing by the liver and kidneys.
  3. Neurological Recalibration: The brain may struggle to interpret the sudden lack of "stress signals," leading to temporary fatigue or emotional volatility.
