INFORMED CONSENT FOR HEALTH COACHING

What is Health Coaching?

Health coaching is a client-centered process that supports individuals in achieving their health and wellness goals through behavior change, education, and accountability. As your health coach, I will work collaboratively with you to identify your goals, explore obstacles, and create actionable strategies for sustainable change.

What Health Coaching IS:

  • A supportive partnership focused on your wellness goals
  • Education about nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness practices
  • Guidance in setting and achieving realistic health objectives
  • Accountability and encouragement throughout your journey
  • A complement to your existing healthcare

What Health Coaching IS NOT:

  • Medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment of any kind
  • Psychotherapy, counseling, or mental health treatment
  • A replacement for care from licensed healthcare providers
  • Prescription or management of medications or supplements
  • Treatment for eating disorders or serious mental health conditions
⚠️ Important Medical Disclaimer: I am not a licensed medical professional. I do not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. Any recommendations I make are for educational purposes only and should not be considered medical advice. Always consult with your physician or healthcare provider before making any changes to your diet, exercise routine, or supplement regimen, especially if you have a medical condition or are taking medications.
