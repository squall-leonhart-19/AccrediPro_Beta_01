CANCELLATION & REFUND POLICY

Session Rescheduling & Program Terms

This policy outlines the terms for cancelling or rescheduling coaching sessions and the refund policy for our programs. By enrolling in our coaching services, you agree to the following terms.

Session Cancellation Policy

24-Hour Notice Required

All session cancellations or rescheduling requests must be made at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment time. This allows us to offer the time slot to another client and manage our schedule effectively.

Notice Given Policy
More than 24 hours Full reschedule with no penalty. We'll find a new time that works.
Less than 24 hours Session is forfeited. Counts as a completed session in your package.
No-show (no communication) Session is forfeited. Three no-shows may result in termination of services.
Emergency situations Handled on a case-by-case basis with documentation.

How to Cancel or Reschedule

To cancel or reschedule a session, please contact us via:

  • Email: [Your email address]
  • Scheduling system: [Your scheduling link]
  • Text message: [Your phone number, if applicable]

Please do not assume a message left via social media constitutes proper notification.

Coach Cancellations

In the event that I need to cancel a session, I will provide as much notice as possible and offer to reschedule at a mutually convenient time. Your session will not be counted as used if I cancel.

Program Refund Policy

Before Program Begins

If you wish to cancel before your first session, you may receive a full refund minus a $______ administrative fee.

After Program Begins

Due to the personalized nature of coaching services and the time invested in planning your program, all sales are final once the program begins. No refunds will be issued for:

  • Unused sessions
  • Sessions cancelled with less than 24 hours notice
  • Missed sessions (no-shows)
  • Early program termination by client choice
Extenuating Circumstances: We understand that life can be unpredictable. If you experience a significant life event (serious illness, family emergency, etc.) that prevents you from continuing the program, please contact us. We may be able to pause your program, extend your timeline, or make other accommodations on a case-by-case basis.

Package Expiration

All coaching packages must be used within the timeframe specified at purchase. Standard expiration periods:

  • Single sessions: Must be used within 30 days of purchase
  • Monthly packages: Valid for the calendar month purchased
  • Multi-month packages: Valid for the duration plus 30 days

Extensions may be granted in exceptional circumstances at our discretion.

Payment Plans

If you are on a payment plan:

  • All payments must be made as scheduled regardless of session attendance
  • Discontinuing the program does not cancel remaining payments
  • Failed payments may result in suspension of services
  • Accounts more than 30 days past due may be sent to collections

Termination by Coach

We reserve the right to terminate the coaching relationship and issue a prorated refund (minus completed sessions) if:

  • The client is abusive, threatening, or inappropriate
  • The client's needs are beyond our scope of practice
  • The client repeatedly fails to comply with program requirements
