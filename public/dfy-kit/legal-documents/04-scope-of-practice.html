My Role as Your Health Coach

As your health coach, I serve as a supportive partner in your wellness journey. I am trained to help you identify your health goals, explore obstacles, build sustainable habits, and create actionable strategies for positive change. I provide education, accountability, and encouragement—not medical treatment.

✓ What I DO Provide

  • Partner with you to set and achieve wellness goals
  • Provide education on nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness topics
  • Help you explore your relationship with food and health
  • Support behavior change and habit formation
  • Offer accountability and encouragement
  • Share evidence-based wellness information
  • Help you navigate stress management techniques
  • Guide you in making sustainable lifestyle changes
  • Refer you to appropriate professionals when needed
  • Maintain confidentiality of your information
  • Create personalized action plans with your input
  • Support your autonomy in health decisions

✗ What I Do NOT Provide

  • Medical diagnosis or treatment
  • Prescription or management of medications
  • Specific dosing of supplements
  • Treatment of eating disorders
  • Mental health therapy or counseling
  • Interpretation of lab results
  • Recommendations that contradict your doctor
  • Crisis intervention or emergency care
  • Claims to cure or treat disease
  • Advice outside my training and expertise
  • Services requiring a medical license
  • Replacement for professional medical care

Professional Boundaries

I am committed to maintaining appropriate professional boundaries in our coaching relationship. This means:

  • Our relationship is professional and focused on your wellness goals
  • Sessions are scheduled at agreed times with clear boundaries
  • Communication happens through agreed channels during business hours
  • I will refer you to other professionals when issues arise outside my scope
  • I will not provide advice on matters requiring licensure I do not hold

When I Will Refer Out

I will recommend you consult with appropriate professionals if you experience:

  • Symptoms requiring medical diagnosis or treatment
  • Mental health concerns beyond general stress management
  • Eating disorder behaviors or symptoms
  • Need for medication adjustments
  • Complex medical conditions requiring specialized care
  • Any situation beyond my training and expertise

Your Responsibilities

For our coaching relationship to be most effective, I ask that you:

  • Maintain relationships with your healthcare providers
  • Inform me of any diagnoses, medications, or health changes
  • Consult your doctor before making significant health changes
  • Communicate honestly about your needs and concerns
  • Take responsibility for your own health decisions

Questions?

If you have any questions about my scope of practice or qualifications, please don't hesitate to ask. Transparency and clear communication are foundations of our coaching relationship.
