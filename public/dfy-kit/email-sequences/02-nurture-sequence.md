# Nurture Email Sequence
# 8-Email Series to Build Trust & Warm Up Leads

---

## Email 1: Quick Win
**Subject Lines:**
- Try this 5-minute trick tonight
- One simple change that makes a big difference
- The easiest health hack you'll ever try

**Send:** 3 days after welcome sequence ends

---

**Hi [First Name],**

I've got a quick win for you today—something you can do in literally 5 minutes that can make a noticeable difference in how you feel.

**The tip:** [Share one simple, actionable tip that provides quick results]

**Why it works:**

[2-3 sentences explaining the science/reasoning behind it]

**How to do it:**

1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Step 3]

That's it! Simple, right?

Try it today and hit reply to let me know how it goes.

[Your Name]

---

## Email 2: Educational Deep-Dive
**Subject Lines:**
- What nobody tells you about [topic]
- The hidden connection between [A] and [B]
- Why your [symptom] might actually mean [this]

**Send:** Day 6

---

**Hi [First Name],**

Today I want to geek out a bit and share something that most people don't know about [topic].

**Here's the thing:** [Introduce the concept or connection]

[Educational content - 4-6 paragraphs that teach something valuable. Include:
- The science/research (simplified)
- Why this matters for them
- Practical implications
- 2-3 action steps they can take]

**The takeaway:** [One sentence summary]

**Your action step:** [Specific thing they can do this week]

Questions? Hit reply—I love nerding out about this stuff!

[Your Name]

---

I thought I'd switch things up today and share a little peek into my life.

[Your Name]

## Email 5: Case Study/Success Story
**Subject Lines:**
- From [struggle] to [success]: How [Name] did it
- "I wish I'd done this sooner" – [Client Name]
- Real transformation: [specific result]

**Send:** Day 18

---

**Hi [First Name],**

I want to introduce you to [Client Name].

**Before:** [Describe where they were - paint a picture of the struggle]

[Client Name] came to me feeling [emotions] and dealing with [symptoms/challenges]. [She/He/They] had tried [previous attempts] without lasting success.

**What changed:**

We focused on [2-3 key areas of your approach without giving everything away]:

1. **[Area 1]** - [Brief explanation]
2. **[Area 2]** - [Brief explanation]
3. **[Area 3]** - [Brief explanation]

**After:**

Within [timeframe], [Client Name] experienced:
- [Result 1]
- [Result 2]
- [Result 3]

Here's what [she/he/they] said:

> *"[Testimonial quote - 2-3 powerful sentences]"*

**What's the difference between [Client Name] and someone still stuck?**

[Insight about what made the difference - hint: it's usually getting proper support]

If you've been struggling with [similar issues], know that transformation IS possible.

[Your Name]

P.S. Want to explore what's possible for you? [Soft mention of discovery call or learn more link]

## Email 8: Invitation/Bridge to Sales
**Subject Lines:**
- I've been thinking about you, [First Name]
- Ready to take the next step?
- Let's make this personal

**Send:** Day 30

---

**Hi [First Name],**

Over the past few weeks, I've shared:

- [Summary of value provided in previous emails]
- Real stories of transformation
- My unique approach to [topic]

And through it all, I've been thinking about YOU.

**Where are you right now?**

Are you...
- Still struggling with [pain point 1]?
- Frustrated by [pain point 2]?
- Wishing you could [desired outcome]?

**If so, I want to help.**

Because the truth is, you can keep trying to figure this out on your own... or you can get expert support and get there faster.

**Here's how we can work together:**

[Describe your offer briefly - program, 1:1 coaching, etc.]

[BUTTON: Learn More / Book a Call]

**This is your invitation, [First Name].**

No pressure—just an open door whenever you're ready.

I'm here for you.

Warmly,

[Your Name]

P.S. Have questions before you take the leap? Just reply to this email. I'm happy to chat.

---

# CUSTOMIZATION NOTES:
- Adjust timing based on your audience and business
- Mix up content based on what resonates with your people
- Always provide genuine value before selling
- Test subject lines and track open rates
- Personalize examples to your specific niche
