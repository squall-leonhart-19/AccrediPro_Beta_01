Chronic stress affects every system in your body. Use these evidence-based techniques daily to activate your relaxation response and build resilience.

ü´Å 4-7-8 Breathing 2 min

  1. Exhale completely through mouth
  2. Inhale through nose for 4 counts
  3. Hold breath for 7 counts
  4. Exhale slowly through mouth for 8 counts
  5. Repeat 4 cycles

Best for: Acute stress, before bed, anxiety

üéØ Box Breathing 4 min

  1. Inhale for 4 counts
  2. Hold for 4 counts
  3. Exhale for 4 counts
  4. Hold for 4 counts
  5. Repeat 4-6 cycles

Best for: Focus, calming the mind, resetting

üßò Body Scan 10 min

  1. Lie down comfortably
  2. Close eyes, breathe deeply
  3. Focus on each body part (toes to head)
  4. Notice tension, breathe into it
  5. Release and relax each area

Best for: Physical tension, before sleep

üí™ Progressive Muscle Relaxation 10 min

  1. Starting with feet, tense muscles for 5 sec
  2. Release and notice relaxation for 30 sec
  3. Move up: calves, thighs, abs, chest...
  4. Include hands, arms, shoulders, face
  5. End with whole-body relaxation

Best for: Physical tension, insomnia

üåø Grounding (5-4-3-2-1) 2 min

Name out loud:

  • 5 things you can SEE
  • 4 things you can TOUCH
  • 3 things you can HEAR
  • 2 things you can SMELL
  • 1 thing you can TASTE

Best for: Anxiety, panic, overwhelm

üìù Journaling Prompts 5-10 min

  • What am I feeling right now?
  • What am I grateful for today?
  • What can I control? What can I release?
  • What would I tell a friend in this situation?
  • Brain dump: Write everything on your mind

Best for: Processing emotions, clarity
