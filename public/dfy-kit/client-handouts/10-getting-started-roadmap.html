Welcome to Your Health Journey!

This roadmap guides you through your first few weeks. Small, consistent steps lead to big transformation. You've got this!

1

Week 1: Foundation Building

Complete all intake forms
Health history, goals, and lifestyle assessment
Schedule your first session
Use the booking link provided in your welcome email
Start tracking current habits
Use the food & symptom diary (no changes yet - just observe)
Review program materials
Read through handouts and guides provided
2

Week 2: First Changes

Implement first dietary change
Based on Session 1 recommendations (start small)
Add one new daily habit
E.g., morning water, 10 min walk, stress practice
Begin supplement protocol (if applicable)
Follow the supplement guide with proper timing
Continue tracking
Note how you feel with new changes
3

Week 3: Building Momentum

Add second dietary change
Build on success from week 2
Establish sleep routine
Consistent bedtime, wind-down ritual
Daily movement goal
Find sustainable movement you enjoy
Weekly check-in with coach
Share wins, challenges, and questions
4

Week 4: Integration

Review progress
Complete progress tracker, note improvements
Identify what's working
Double down on successful habits
Plan for challenges
Troubleshoot obstacles with your coach
Set goals for next month
Build on foundation, add next phase
