Blood Sugar Balance Protocol

Metabolic Health & Glucose Optimization

Why Blood Sugar Matters: Blood sugar dysregulation is at the root of many health issues including weight gain, energy crashes, hormone imbalances, inflammation, and increased disease risk. Stabilizing blood sugar improves nearly every aspect of health.
Core Principles of Blood Sugar Balance
  1. Eat protein, fat, and fiber with every meal - These slow glucose absorption
  2. Never eat naked carbs - Always pair with protein or fat
  3. Eat in the right order - Vegetables ‚Üí Protein/Fat ‚Üí Carbs last
  4. Move after eating - A 10-15 minute walk reduces glucose spikes
  5. Don't skip meals - Consistent eating times stabilize blood sugar
  6. Prioritize breakfast - High-protein breakfast sets up the whole day
Food Choices for Blood Sugar Balance

‚úì Blood Sugar Friendly

  • Proteins: Eggs, fish, poultry, beef, legumes, tofu
  • Fats: Avocado, olive oil, nuts, seeds, coconut
  • Non-starchy veg: Leafy greens, broccoli, zucchini, peppers
  • Low-glycemic fruits: Berries, green apple, citrus
  • Fiber sources: Chia, flax, vegetables, legumes
  • Fermented foods: Sauerkraut, kimchi, yogurt
  • Vinegar: Apple cider vinegar before meals
  • Cinnamon: Add to foods - may improve sensitivity

‚ùå Minimize/Avoid

  • Sugar: All forms (white, brown, honey, agave)
  • Refined carbs: White bread, pasta, crackers
  • Sugary drinks: Soda, juice, sweetened coffee
  • High-glycemic fruits: Bananas, grapes, dried fruit
  • Processed foods: Most hidden sugars
  • Alcohol: Disrupts glucose regulation
  • Breakfast cereals: Even "healthy" ones spike glucose
  • Smoothie bowls: Concentrated fruit sugar

Ideal Plate Template

50% Veg
25% Protein
25% Carbs
  • 1/2 plate: Non-starchy vegetables
  • 1/4 plate: Quality protein (palm-size)
  • 1/4 plate: Complex carbs or starchy veg
  • Add: 1-2 tbsp healthy fat
  • Eat in this order: Veg first, protein, carbs last

Blood Sugar "Hacks"
  • ACV trick: 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar in water before carb-heavy meals
  • Veggie starter: Eat a salad or vegetables before the rest of your meal
  • Post-meal walk: Even 5 minutes helps clear glucose
  • Savory breakfast: Skip the sweet breakfast - protein and fat instead
  • Fat before carbs: Eat fat (like olive oil on salad) before carbs
  • Pair fruit: Always eat fruit with protein, fat, or after a meal
