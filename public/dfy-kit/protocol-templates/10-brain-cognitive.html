Blood Sugar & Brain Function
Blood sugar crashes = brain fog. The brain uses 20% of your glucose. Unstable blood sugar causes cognitive symptoms like:
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Brain fog and fatigue
  • Irritability and mood swings
  • Memory problems

Solution: Follow blood sugar balance principles - protein at every meal, avoid naked carbs, eat regularly, limit sugar and refined carbs. (See Blood Sugar Protocol)

Common Root Causes of Brain Fog
  • Blood sugar dysregulation - Crashes cause brain fog
  • Sleep deprivation - Brain can't clean itself
  • Thyroid dysfunction - Hypothyroid causes cognitive issues
  • Nutrient deficiencies - B12, iron, D3, omega-3
  • Gut issues/SIBO - Gut-brain connection
  • Chronic stress - Cortisol damages hippocampus
  • Inflammation - Neuroinflammation affects function
  • Hormonal changes - Perimenopause, low testosterone
  • Chronic infections - Lyme, EBV, mold illness
  • Medication side effects - Statins, PPIs, benzos, anticholinergics
