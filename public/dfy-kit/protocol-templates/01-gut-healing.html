Phase 2: REPLACE

Weeks 2-4

Replace digestive factors that may be lacking or insufficient.

Key Strategies

  • Support stomach acid production (apple cider vinegar in water before meals)
  • Digestive enzyme supplementation with protein-rich meals
  • Bitter foods to stimulate digestive secretions
  • Mindful eating practices - chew thoroughly, eat slowly
  • Avoid drinking large amounts of water with meals

Phase 4: REPAIR

Weeks 6-12

Support the healing and repair of the gut lining.

Healing Foods

  • Bone broth: Rich in collagen, glutamine, and minerals
  • Collagen-rich foods: Slow-cooked meats, fish with skin
  • Omega-3 fatty acids: Wild salmon, sardines, flaxseed
  • Zinc-rich foods: Pumpkin seeds, oysters, grass-fed beef
  • Vitamin A foods: Sweet potato, carrots, leafy greens
