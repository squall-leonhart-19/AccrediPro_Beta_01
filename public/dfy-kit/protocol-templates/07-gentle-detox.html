Managing Detox Reactions
Normal reactions (first few days): Headache, fatigue, mild nausea, skin breakouts, irritability, brain fog. Usually resolve in 3-7 days.

If reactions are severe: Slow down! Increase water, ensure bowels are moving, reduce supplements, add more binders (fiber, charcoal), and support with electrolytes.
