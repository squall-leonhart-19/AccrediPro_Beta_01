Why Level 0 Is Not Sufficient for Practice

  • Level 0 is an educational credential, not a professional license
  • Client work requires additional training in scope, ethics, and safety
  • Professional practice requires supervision and accountability structures
  • Charging fees without proper credentialing creates legal and ethical risks
  • Moving too fast without proper support leads to burnout and poor outcomes
