CASE STUDY: MICHELLE

MEET MICHELLE

42-year-old working mom. Two kids. Full-time job. Feels like she's running on empty every single day.

Michelle's Symptoms

  • ✗ Weight gain — especially around her midsection (20 lbs in 3 years)
  • ✗ Fatigue — constant, crushing, even after 8 hours of sleep
  • ✗ Anxiety — new in the last 2 years, feels like a low hum all day
  • ✗ Bloating — after almost every meal
  • ✗ Hair thinning — losing more every month
  • ✗ PMS — mood swings, cravings, headaches a week before her period
  • ✗ Insomnia — waking at 3am almost every night

What Her Doctors Told Her

"Your labs are normal."

"You're just getting older."

"Try eating less and exercising more."

"Here's a prescription for anxiety."

Sound familiar?

Michelle had seen 3 different doctors. None of them connected the dots.

WHAT FUNCTIONAL MEDICINE FOUND

When we actually looked — using functional lab testing and a root-cause approach — here's what we discovered:

GUT DYSFUNCTION

  • Low stomach acid (food wasn't being broken down properly)
  • SIBO — small intestinal bacterial overgrowth
  • Leaky gut — causing chronic low-grade inflammation

This explained the bloating, food sensitivities, and inflammation.

CORTISOL DYSREGULATION

  • High cortisol in the morning (wired but tired)
  • Cortisol spikes at 3am (classic stress pattern)
  • HPA axis dysfunction — her stress response was stuck

This explained the insomnia, anxiety, and weight around the midsection.

ESTROGEN DOMINANCE

  • High estrogen relative to progesterone
  • Poor estrogen detoxification through the liver
  • Sluggish Phase 2 liver detox pathways

This explained the PMS, weight gain, and hair thinning.

NUTRIENT DEFICIENCIES

  • Low B12 (energy, brain function)
  • Low ferritin (iron stores — hair, fatigue)
  • Low Vitamin D (immune function, mood)
  • Low magnesium (sleep, stress response)

This explained the fatigue, brain fog, and poor recovery.

THE PATTERN

Notice how everything connects?

Gut dysfunction → poor nutrient absorption → deficiencies → fatigue

Gut dysfunction → inflammation → immune activation → more fatigue

Chronic stress → cortisol dysregulation → insomnia → more stress

Poor liver detox → estrogen buildup → PMS, weight gain, hair loss

Michelle didn't have 7 separate problems.
She had ONE pattern with 7 expressions.

THE 12-WEEK TRANSFORMATION

Here's what we did — in the RIGHT order:

WEEKS 1-4: FOUNDATIONS + GUT

  • Removed inflammatory foods (gluten, dairy, sugar)
  • Added digestive support (stomach acid, enzymes)
  • Started gut healing protocol (L-glutamine, zinc carnosine)
  • Addressed SIBO with targeted antimicrobials
  • Established sleep hygiene basics

WEEKS 5-8: STRESS + DETOX

  • HPA axis support (adaptogens, nervous system work)
  • Liver support for estrogen detoxification
  • Added key nutrients (B12, iron, D3, magnesium)
  • Implemented stress reduction practices
  • Optimized sleep environment and routine

WEEKS 9-12: HORMONES + MAINTENANCE

  • Hormone-supportive nutrition (seed cycling, cruciferous vegetables)
  • Progesterone support through lifestyle and targeted supplements
  • Fine-tuning based on how she felt
  • Building sustainable long-term habits
