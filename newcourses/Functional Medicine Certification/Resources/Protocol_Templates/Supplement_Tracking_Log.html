Supplement Quality Checklist

When selecting supplements, consider:

☐ Third-party tested (NSF, USP, ConsumerLab, Informed Sport)

☐ GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) certified facility

☐ No unnecessary fillers or additives

☐ Appropriate form for absorption (e.g., methylated B vitamins)

☐ Reputable company with transparent sourcing

☐ Appropriate for client's dietary restrictions (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)

☐ Expiration date checked
