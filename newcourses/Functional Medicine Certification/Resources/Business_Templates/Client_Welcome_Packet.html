What to Expect

Our First Session

Your initial session is dedicated to getting to know YOU. We'll review your health history, discuss your goals, and begin crafting your personalized wellness plan. Come prepared to share openly—the more I understand about your life, the better I can support you.

Follow-Up Sessions

Each session builds on the previous one. We'll celebrate your wins, troubleshoot challenges, refine your action steps, and introduce new concepts as you're ready for them. Sessions typically follow this structure:

  • Check-in on how you're feeling
  • Review progress on action items
  • Explore challenges and solutions
  • Education on relevant topics
  • Set new goals and action steps

Between Sessions

This is where the real transformation happens! You'll practice new habits, track your progress, and notice how your body responds to changes. I'm here to support you between sessions via [email/text/app] for questions and encouragement.

2. Embrace the Process

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How is health coaching different from seeing a doctor or dietitian?

A: Health coaches focus on behavior change and lifestyle modification. We help you implement what you already know you should do and support you in building sustainable habits. We work alongside—not in place of—your healthcare team.

Q: What if I need to miss a session?

A: Please give at least 24 hours notice to reschedule. Life happens, and I'm happy to work with you to find a new time that works.

Q: How quickly will I see results?

A: This varies by individual and goals. Some clients notice improvements within weeks; others take longer. Sustainable change is our priority over quick fixes.

Q: What if I struggle with my action items?

A: That's valuable information! Struggles help us identify what's not working so we can adjust. Be honest about challenges—that's how we find solutions.

A: Yes! [Describe your between-session support policy]. I'm here to support you throughout your journey.
