Section 1: Nature of Health Coaching Services

Health coaching is a partnership between coach and client designed to support you in achieving your health and wellness goals through lifestyle and behavior change. As your health coach, I will:

  • Provide education about nutrition, lifestyle factors, and their impact on health
  • Help you identify and clarify your health goals
  • Support you in creating actionable plans to achieve those goals
  • Offer accountability, encouragement, and motivational support
  • Help you identify and overcome barriers to change
  • Track your progress and adjust strategies as needed
  • Provide resources and educational materials

Section 2: Scope of Practice & Limitations

Please Read Carefully:

I understand that my health coach is NOT a licensed healthcare provider. Health coaching services do not include and are not a substitute for:

  • Medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment – I will continue to work with my physician or other healthcare providers for all medical concerns
  • Psychotherapy or mental health treatment – If I experience mental health concerns, I will seek appropriate professional support
  • Nutritional therapy or medical nutrition therapy – Meal plans for medical conditions should come from a licensed dietitian
  • Prescribing or adjusting medications or supplements – All medication and supplement decisions should be discussed with my healthcare provider
  • Ordering or interpreting lab tests – My coach may educate me about labs, but interpretation must come from my provider

I agree to consult my healthcare provider before making any changes to my diet, exercise routine, or supplement regimen, especially if I have any medical conditions, take medications, or am pregnant or nursing.
