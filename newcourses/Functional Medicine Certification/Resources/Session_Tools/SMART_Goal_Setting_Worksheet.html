Understanding SMART Goals

SMART goals transform vague intentions into clear action plans. Each goal should meet these criteria:

Specific Clearly defined with precise details. Answer: What exactly do I want to achieve?
Measurable Quantifiable with concrete criteria. Answer: How will I track my progress?
Achievable Realistic given your current resources and constraints. Answer: Is this attainable?
Relevant Aligned with your values and larger objectives. Answer: Why does this matter to me?
Time-bound Has a specific deadline or timeframe. Answer: By when will I accomplish this?
