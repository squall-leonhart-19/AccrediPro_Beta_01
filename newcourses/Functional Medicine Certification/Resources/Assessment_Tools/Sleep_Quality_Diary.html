When to Recommend Medical Evaluation

Refer client to a healthcare provider if they report:

  • Loud snoring with gasping or choking
  • Witnessed breathing pauses during sleep
  • Severe daytime sleepiness affecting safety (driving, work)
  • Sleep problems lasting more than 3 months despite lifestyle changes
  • Symptoms of depression or anxiety affecting sleep
  • Unusual behaviors during sleep (sleepwalking, acting out dreams)
  • Chronic pain significantly impacting sleep
