Instructions

This assessment helps identify your current stress levels, stress sources, and resilience factors. Answer honestly based on your experience over the past 30 days. There are no right or wrong answers—this information helps create a personalized stress management plan.

Scoring Instructions:

For questions 4, 5, 7, and 8 (reverse scored): 0=4, 1=3, 2=2, 3=1, 4=0

Score Interpretation:

  • 0-13: Low perceived stress
  • 14-26: Moderate perceived stress
  • 27-40: High perceived stress

When to Refer for Mental Health Support

Recommend professional mental health evaluation if client reports:

  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide
  • Persistent feelings of hopelessness lasting more than 2 weeks
  • Severe anxiety interfering with daily functioning
  • Panic attacks
  • Symptoms of PTSD or trauma responses
  • Substance use to cope with stress
  • Significant changes in eating or sleeping patterns
  • Social withdrawal or isolation
  • Inability to perform work or daily responsibilities
