Discovery Call Script

Your 30-Minute Call Structure

Phase 1: Build Rapport 5 min
YOU:
"Hi Rachel! Thank you so much for taking the time to chat with me today. Before we dive in, I'd love to just take a moment to get to know you a bit. How did you hear about me?"
YOU:
"That's wonderful that [friend's name] referred you. She's been on quite a journey herself. So Rachel, tell me ‚Äî what made you decide to reach out now? What's going on?"
Phase 2: Understand the Problem 10 min
YOU:
"Tell me more about the fatigue. When did it start? How does it affect your daily life?"
YOU:
"And what have you tried so far? What worked, what didn't?"
YOU:
"If nothing changes, where do you see yourself in a year? And if you COULD wave a magic wand and fix this, what would your life look like?"
Phase 3: Present Your Approach 10 min
YOU:
"Rachel, based on what you've shared, here's what I'm hearing: [summarize her pain points]. Does that feel accurate?"
YOU:
"The approach I use is different from what you've tried before. Instead of just treating symptoms, we look at the ROOT causes. I work with you over 12 weeks to identify exactly what's driving your fatigue and create a personalized plan. Most of my clients start feeling significantly better within the first 4-6 weeks."
Phase 4: Close 5 min
YOU:
"So Rachel, does this sound like what you've been looking for? ... Great! Let me tell you about the program and how we can get started."

"It's too expensive."
"I hear you. Can I ask ‚Äî you mentioned you've spent $X on [supplements, doctors, etc.] that didn't work. What if we could actually solve this in 12 weeks? What would that be worth to you ‚Äî not just in money, but in energy, in quality of life, in being present for your family?"
"I need to ask my husband."
"Absolutely. Would it be helpful if I sent you some information you could share with him? Or, we could schedule a quick 15-minute call where he could ask any questions. What would work best?"
