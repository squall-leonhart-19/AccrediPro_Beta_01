The Momentum Principle

In behavioral psychology, the "fresh start effect" suggests that milestones (like receiving a certification) provide a surge in motivation and a temporary suspension of past limiting beliefs. A 2014 study by Dai et al. published in Management Science found that individuals are significantly more likely to pursue goals following temporal landmarks. As a new practitioner, you must capitalize on this 30-day window before the "routine" of daily life begins to dilute your professional drive.
