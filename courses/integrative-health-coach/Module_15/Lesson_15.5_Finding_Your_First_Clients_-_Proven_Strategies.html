The Founding Member Strategy

The "Founding Member" or "Beta" strategy is the most effective way to launch a new practice. It lowers the barrier to entry for the client while providing you with essential social proof and clinical experience. By framing your offer as a "Founding Member" opportunity, you create exclusivity and urgency.

The Founding Member Offer Structure:

  • The Discount: Offer 30-50% off your future standard rate.
  • The Limit: Only accept 3 to 5 people into this initial cohort.
  • The Exchange: Clearly state that in return for the lower rate, you require a detailed testimonial and permission to use their case study (anonymized if necessary).
  • The Timeline: Set a clear start and end date for the enrollment period.

The Discovery Call Blueprint

The discovery call is not a sales pitch; it is a mini-coaching session designed to determine if there is an ALIGN-ment between the client's needs and your services. A successful discovery call follows a specific arc:

  1. Empathy & Discovery (15 mins): Use open-ended questions to understand their current health status and frustrations.
  2. The Gap Analysis (10 mins): Help them see the distance between where they are and where they want to be.
  3. The Vision (5 mins): Ask them what life looks like once their health challenge is resolved.
  4. The Invitation (10 mins): "Based on what you've shared, I'm confident my [Program Name] can help you bridge that gap. Would you like to hear how we would work together?"
