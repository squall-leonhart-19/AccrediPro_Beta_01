When a client presents with 15+ symptoms across five different physiological systems, the most common mistake is attempting to address everything at once. This leads to therapeutic overwhelm, poor compliance, and potential Herxheimer (detox) reactions that can set progress back months.

The Layering Technique focuses on identifying the "Lead Domino"â€”the primary system that, when supported, creates a positive cascade across all other systems. In complex cases involving the HPA Axis, the Gut, and the Immune system, the order of operations is critical.
