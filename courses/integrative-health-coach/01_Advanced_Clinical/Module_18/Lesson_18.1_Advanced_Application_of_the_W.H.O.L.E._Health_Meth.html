3. Identifying 'Interference Fields' & Blocking Factors

An Interference Field is a biological or environmental factor that acts as a "hard stop" to the W.H.O.L.E. Method. You can have the perfect Holistic Plan (H), but if these fields are active, the client will not progress. A 2021 study on chronic illness recovery found that 22% of non-responders had hidden dental foci or environmental toxicant loads that were never assessed.

Common Interference Fields in Advanced Practice:

  • Dental Foci: Silent infections in root canals or "cavitations" that keep the immune system in a hyper-vigilant state.
  • Environmental Bio-toxins: Hidden mold (Mycotoxins) in the home or workplace that inhibit mitochondrial ATP production.
  • Scar Tissue: Physical scars can sometimes interfere with local autonomic nerve signaling (Bio-regulatory medicine concept).
  • EMF Sensitivity: For a subset of clients, high electromagnetic exposure acts as a cellular stressor that prevents the "Empower Self-Healing" (E) phase from initiating.
