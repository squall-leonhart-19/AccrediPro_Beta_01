The modern healthcare landscape is fractured. General practitioners (MDs), Functional Medicine practitioners, and Naturopathic Doctors (NDs) are often overwhelmed by the Implementation Gapâ€”the space between diagnosing a condition and the patient actually changing their life to manage it.

Your goal is not to "sell" to these practitioners, but to solve their biggest problem: patient non-compliance. When a doctor tells a patient to "reduce stress and fix their diet," the patient often fails because they lack the behavioral tools. As an AccrediPro certified coach, you are the solution to that failure.

The 'Integrative Bridge' Strategy

The "Integrative Bridge" positions the coach as the Implementation Arm of a clinical treatment plan. Clinical medicine is excellent at "what" (diagnosis, medication, surgery) but often poor at "how" (habit change, kitchen clean-outs, circadian rhythm resetting).

Reciprocal Agreements & Ethical Fee-Sharing

The 'Referral Toolkit' for Clients

Existing clients are your most potent marketing force. However, most people don't refer because they don't know how or when. You must provide them with a toolkit that makes the process frictionless.

Components of a Referral Toolkit
  • The "Friend Pass": A digital or physical card giving a friend a free 30-minute "Discovery Audit."
  • Social Proof Templates: Pre-written "I'm working with [Your Name]" posts for Instagram/Facebook.
  • The "Magic Email": A template they can copy and paste to introduce a friend via email.
  • Incentive Structure: A "thank you" gift (e.g., a free session or a high-quality supplement bundle) for every successful enrollment.
