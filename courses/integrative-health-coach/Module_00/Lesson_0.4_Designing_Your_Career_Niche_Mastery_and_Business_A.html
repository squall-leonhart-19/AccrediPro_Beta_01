4. Economic Modeling: Beyond the Hourly Rate

To build a sustainable business, you must decouple your income from your time. The value-based pricing model focuses on the ROI of the transformation you provide, not the minutes you spend on Zoom.

The ROI-Driven Pricing Formula

Consider the cost of the problem you are solving. If a client with chronic fatigue loses $20,000 in productivity annually, a $3,000 coaching program that restores their energy has a clear 6.6x ROI.

Key Economic Terms

Scalable Architecture

Business structures (like group programs or digital products) where increasing revenue does not require a linear increase in labor.

Value-Based Bundling

Pricing based on the total outcome (e.g., "The Gut Restoration Protocol") rather than individual sessions.
