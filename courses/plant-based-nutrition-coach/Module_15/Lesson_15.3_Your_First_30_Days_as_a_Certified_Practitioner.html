Avoiding Transition Pitfalls

According to data from the Small Business Administration (SBA), 20% of service-based businesses fail in the first year due to "Poor Systems." As an AccrediPro Practitioner, you are insulated from this by the P.L.A.N.T.S. methodology.

The "Over-Coaching" Trap: Do not give the client 50 changes at once. Stick to Therapeutic Whole Foods (T) first. Research shows that clients who make 1-2 structured changes have an 85% adherence rate, while those given 5+ changes drop to 15% (Gardner et al., 2018).
