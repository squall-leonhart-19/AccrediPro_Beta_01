Legal & Ethical Foundations

As an AccrediPro Certified Practitioner, you must operate within your Scope of Practice. This is critical for both client safety and legal protection. A 2022 survey found that 92% of legal issues in coaching arise from a lack of clear client agreements.

You ARE: An educator, a guide, a lifestyle architect, and a nutrition expert.
You ARE NOT: A doctor, a therapist, or someone who "prescribes" to "cure" a disease. Use language like "support," "optimize," and "align" rather than "treat" or "heal."

Legal Essential

Always ensure your client agreement includes a "Medical Disclaimer" stating that your coaching is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. This must be signed before the first session.
