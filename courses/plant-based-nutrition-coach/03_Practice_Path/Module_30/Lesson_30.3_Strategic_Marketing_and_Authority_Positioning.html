2. Content Marketing: SEO & The P.L.A.N.T. Pillars

Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is often misunderstood as a technical hurdle. In reality, it is about answering the questions your ideal clients are asking. Within the P.L.A.N.T. Nutrition Methodâ„¢, two pillars stand out as high-intent search drivers: Anti-Inflammatory Foods (A) and Nourishing Gut Health (N).

The "A" and "N" SEO Strategy

Clients rarely search for "Plant-Based Coach." Instead, they search for solutions to their symptoms. A strategic marketing plan targets these "long-tail" keywords:

  • The Anti-Inflammatory Pillar: Targeting terms like "Reducing joint pain with diet," "Foods to lower CRP levels," or "Plant-based anti-inflammatory meal prep."
  • The Nourish Gut Health Pillar: Targeting terms like "Microbiome diversity and fiber," "Bloating on a vegan diet," or "Prebiotic foods for SCFAs."

6. Leveraging Social Proof and Testimonials

Social proof is the "psychological shortcut" clients use to determine if you can help them. However, as discussed in Lesson 1 of this module, we must maintain strict ethical boundaries. You cannot share a client's full name or sensitive medical data without explicit, written HIPAA-compliant consent.

The "Outcome-Based" Testimonial Framework

Instead of "Jane Smith lost 20 lbs," focus on the functional outcome and the mechanism used:

Example of Ethical Authority Proof:
