2. Short-Form Video Mastery: Debunking the Myths

Short-form video (Reels, TikToks, Shorts) is currently the most efficient way to achieve "top of funnel" reach. For a plant-based coach, this is the ideal medium for Pattern Interrupt‚Äîstopping the scroll by addressing the most common barriers to entry: Protein, B12, and Iron.

The "Myth-Buster" Scripting Framework:

  1. The Hook (0-3s): "Stop worrying about your protein on a plant-based diet."
  2. The Pattern Interrupt (3-7s): Show a high-protein plant source (e.g., tempeh) with a surprising stat (31g per cup).
  3. The Education (7-20s): Briefly explain the Bioavailability Matrix (referencing Module 7).
  4. The Call to Action (20-30s): "Comment 'PROTEIN' for my plant-based amino acid guide."

3. Building and Moderating a 'High-Vibe' Private Community

While social media feeds are for attraction, private communities (Facebook Groups, Circle, or Slack) are for retention. A "High-Vibe" community is characterized by psychological safety, peer support, and direct access to your expertise.

According to the P.L.A.N.T. Method‚Ñ¢, sustainability (The 'T' for Thrive) is significantly enhanced by social proof. When a new client sees a "veteran" client sharing a successful meal prep (Module 1), the neurobiology of habit change is reinforced through social modeling.

Community Moderation Pillars:

  • The Welcome Protocol: Every new member must be tagged in a post asking them to share their "Plant-Based Why."
  • Evidence-Based Guardrails: Strictly moderate misinformation (e.g., "detox teas" or "anti-vax" rhetoric) to maintain your professional authority.
  • The "Win-Wednesday" Ritual: Fostering dopamine hits by celebrating non-scale victories (NSVs) like improved digestion or better sleep.

4. Engagement Strategies: Turning 'Likes' into Conversations

A common mistake is treating "Likes" as a vanity metric. In a premium coaching business, a like is a hand-shake. You must transition the interaction from the public square to the private DM (Direct Message) without appearing predatory.

The Consent-Based DM Framework:

A 2023 study in the Journal of Interactive Marketing found that personalized outreach increases conversion by 42% compared to automated sequences. Use the 3-step outreach:

Step 1: The Gratitude Opener
"Hey [Name], thanks for the love on my post about B12 absorption! I saw you've been following my page for a bit‚Äîwhat's been your biggest takeaway so far?"
Step 2: The Curiosity Bridge
"That's a great point. Are you currently eating 100% plant-based, or are you just exploring the transition?"
Step 3: The Low-Pressure Invitation
"If you're ever looking for a roadmap to simplify the 'Prepare & Plan' phase, I'm hosting a quick 15-min discovery call next week. Would you like the link to grab a spot?"
