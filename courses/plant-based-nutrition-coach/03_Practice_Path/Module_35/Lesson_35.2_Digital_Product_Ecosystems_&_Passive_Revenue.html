2. Developing Evergreen Courses: Modules P & L

Your most valuable assets are the "Prepare & Plan" (P) and "Learn Nutrients" (L) modules. These are the foundations of the P.L.A.N.T. Method‚Ñ¢ and are perfectly suited for evergreen online courses. Unlike live coaching, these products are recorded once and sold indefinitely.

Course Focus: Module P (Prepare & Plan)

Target the "Transition Phase" client. This product should focus on kitchen optimization, pantry stocking, and the logistics of plant-based living. It is a low-to-mid ticket entry point that solves immediate friction.

Course Focus: Module L (Learn Nutrients)

Target the "Health-Conscious" client. This is a deep-dive masterclass into B12, Iron, Protein, and Omega-3s. By positioning this as a "Nutritional Mastery" certification or intensive, you command a higher price point ($297‚Äì$497).

4. Sales Funnel Architecture: The "Sleep Sales" Machine

A digital product is only as good as the funnel that sells it. To scale, you must move away from manual "DM closing" and toward automated sales sequences. A standard high-converting funnel for a plant-based coach follows this architecture:

  1. The Lead Magnet (Top of Funnel): A high-value freebie (e.g., "The Plant-Based Bioavailability Checklist") that solves a specific problem related to Module L.
  2. The Welcome Sequence: 3‚Äì5 emails introducing the P.L.A.N.T. Method‚Ñ¢ and establishing authority.
  3. The "Problem/Agitation" Phase: Highlighting the risks of nutrient deficiencies or chronic inflammation.
  4. The Solution (The Offer): Introducing your evergreen course or membership as the definitive answer.
  5. Scarcity & Social Proof: Using deadlines and testimonials to drive conversion.

5. Intellectual Property (IP) Protection

As you scale, your frameworks‚Äîlike the P.L.A.N.T. Nutrition Method‚Ñ¢‚Äîbecome your most valuable business assets. Protecting your IP is non-negotiable for long-term growth. A 2023 survey of online educators found that 42% had experienced some form of content "piracy" or unauthorized redistribution.

The Three Pillars of IP Protection

  • Trademarking: Protect your brand name, logo, and unique framework titles (e.g., trademarking "The P.L.A.N.T. Method‚Ñ¢").
  • Copyrighting: Your course videos, PDF guides, and meal plans are automatically copyrighted upon creation, but formal registration provides stronger legal standing.
  • Client Agreements: Ensure every digital product purchase includes a "Terms of Use" that explicitly prohibits sharing login credentials or reselling content.
