High-Ticket Package

A comprehensive coaching program (typically 3-6 months) priced between $1,500 and $5,000, focusing on specific health outcomes.

Passive Revenue

Income generated from digital products, recorded courses, or memberships that do not require 1-on-1 time for every sale.

LTV (Lifetime Value)

The total amount of money a client spends with your practice over the entire duration of your relationship.

Billable Hours

The actual time spent in direct client consultation or specific plan creation that is directly compensated.

Operational Expenses & Margins

One of the greatest benefits of a nutrition coaching business is the high profit margin. Unlike a brick-and-mortar gym or a supplement company, a digital coaching practice has very low overhead. A typical monthly expense report for a full-time coach might look like this:

  • Practice Management Software (e.g., Practice Better): $50 - $100
  • Email Marketing (e.g., ConvertKit): $30 - $80
  • Video Conferencing (Zoom): $15
  • Professional Insurance: $20 - $40
  • Marketing/Ads (Optional): $200 - $1,000

In most cases, a plant-based nutrition coach operates at an 80-90% profit margin before taxes. This makes it one of the most financially efficient businesses to start and scale.
