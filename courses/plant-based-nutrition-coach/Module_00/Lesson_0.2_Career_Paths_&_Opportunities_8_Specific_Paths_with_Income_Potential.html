Path 2: Corporate Wellness Consulting

Corporations are losing billions annually due to "presenteeism" (employees present but unproductive due to poor health). A 2022 meta-analysis found that plant-based dietary interventions in the workplace led to significant improvements in HbA1c, LDL cholesterol, and psychological well-being.

As a consultant, you don't just coach individuals; you overhaul the corporate environment. This includes cafeteria consulting, lunch-and-learns, and "Longevity Challenges" for executive teams.

Path 3: Food Industry & Product Development

The "vegan junk food" era is fading. Consumers now demand Therapeutic Whole Foods (T) that are minimally processed. Food tech companies and restaurant groups need coaches to help them design menus and products that are actually healthy, not just animal-free.

Path 4: Specialized Performance & Longevity Coaching

Athletes are increasingly turning to plants to speed up recovery times. By modulating mTOR and AMPK signaling (Longevity Optimization - L), you can help athletes reduce oxidative stress post-training. This niche is underserved and highly lucrative.

Path 5: Specialized Culinary Arts & Meal Prep

Knowing what to eat is different from knowing how to prepare it. Coaches who combine nutrition science with culinary skills are in high demand as "Health Chefs" or meal prep consultants for busy high-net-worth individuals.

Paths 6-8: Scalable Models (Digital, Group, and Advocacy)

Path 6: Digital Content & Membership Sites

Create a "low-barrier-to-entry" membership (e.g., $49/month) where you provide monthly P.L.A.N.T.S.-aligned meal plans and live Q&A sessions. With 200 members, this is a $117,600 recurring revenue stream.
