4. Peer Review and Supervision Standards

Isolation is the enemy of clinical excellence. To maintain the integrity of the Level 3 designation, coaches must engage in Peer Supervision. This involves a formal process where cases are presented to a group of qualified peers to identify blind spots, ethical dilemmas, and opportunities for advanced optimization.

Supervision standards at AccrediPro Academy emphasize the "Reflective Practitioner" model. This means analyzing not just the client's data, but your own psychological response to the client (countertransference), which can significantly impact adherence and outcomes.
