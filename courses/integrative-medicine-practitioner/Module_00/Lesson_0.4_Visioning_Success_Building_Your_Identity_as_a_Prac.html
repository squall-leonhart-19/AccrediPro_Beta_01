Your identity is experienced by the client through the "journey" you design for them. A premium practitioner does not just provide "appointments"; they provide a transformative arc. Mapping this journey ensures that the client feels safe, seen, and supported.

Phase 1: The Discovery (Intake & Alignment)

Phase 2: The Deep Dive (Root Cause Analysis)
