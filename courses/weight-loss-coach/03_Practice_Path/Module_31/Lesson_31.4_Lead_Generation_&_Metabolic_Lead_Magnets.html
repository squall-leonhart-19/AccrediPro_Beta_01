2. Designing the 'Metabolic Health Audit'

The "Metabolic Health Audit" is your primary high-converting entry point. Instead of promising a quick fix, you are offering a diagnostic experience. This leverages the "Test Metabolism" pillar of our framework to provide immediate value while highlighting the client's need for professional guidance.

A successful Metabolic Health Audit should collect data on:

  • Subjective Metabolic Markers: Energy fluctuations, cold intolerance, and sleep quality (referencing Module 7: Optimize Lifestyle).
  • Hunger Signaling: Assessment of ghrelin/leptin balance through behavioral questions (referencing Module 6: Fix Hormones).
  • Inflammatory Triggers: Digestive symptoms and joint pain (referencing Module 3: Anti-Inflammatory Diet).

Practitioner Insight

The goal of the Audit is not just to give them information, but to create "Insight Gaps." By showing a client that their "stubborn fat" is actually a symptom of cortisol-driven insulin resistance, you move them from feeling hopeless to feeling that they finally have the right diagnosis.

5. Landing Page Architecture

A high-performing L4 intake form is not just a contact page; it is a psychological commitment device. According to the "Sunk Cost Fallacy" in behavioral economics, the more a lead invests (time/thought) into an application, the more they value the resulting conversation (Arkes & Blumer, 1985).

Essential Elements of the L4 Intake Form:

  • The Investment Question: "Are you prepared to invest financially and energetically into a 6-month metabolic transformation?"
  • The 'Why Now' Question: Identifying the emotional catalyst for change (Module 8: Mindset).
  • The Health History: A brief section on previous attempts to highlight the "yo-yo" cycle you are going to break.

6. Lead Scoring & Qualification

Not all leads are created equal. Lead scoring allows you to automate the prioritization of your time. In a professional coaching practice, you should only be speaking with "A-Grade" leads.

The Lead Scoring Matrix:

  • Score +20: Completed the Metabolic Health Audit and scored in the "High Resistance" category.
  • Score +15: Watched more than 50% of the Masterclass.
  • Score +10: Opened 3+ emails in the nurture sequence.
  • Score -50: Indicated they are looking for a "quick fix" or "under $100" solution.
