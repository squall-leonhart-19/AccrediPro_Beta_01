2. The 5-Stage Metabolic Discovery Call

The Discovery Call is your primary tool for conversion. It is designed to move the client from a state of emotional distress to a state of scientific curiosity. Unlike traditional sales scripts, this framework focuses on metabolic diagnostics.

Stage 1: Rapport & Clinical Framing (0-5 mins)

Set the tone as a specialist. "Our goal today is to look under the hood of your metabolism. Weâ€™re going to see why past attempts haven't stuck and determine if the TRANSFORM Method is the right clinical fit for your biology." This establishes you as the expert, not a salesperson.

Stage 2: The Metabolic History (The 'T' Pillar) (5-15 mins)

Ask deep, diagnostic questions. Don't just ask "how much do you want to lose?" Ask: "When did you first notice your metabolism shifting? How did your body respond to previous low-calorie interventions? Tell me about your sleep-hunger connection."

Stage 3: The Gap Analysis (Pain vs. Vision) (15-25 mins)

Identify the cost of inaction. If they don't fix their insulin sensitivity or cortisol-insulin axis (Module 7) now, where will they be in 12 months? Contrast this with the vision of metabolic flexibility.

Stage 4: The TRANSFORM Solution (25-35 mins)

Present the 9 pillars as the specific antidote to their history. If they have high stress, focus on 'O' (Optimize Lifestyle). If they have muscle loss, focus on 'S' (Strengthen). Do not sell coaching; sell the pathway to metabolic restoration.

Stage 5: Commitment & Enrollment (35-45 mins)

Ask for the commitment. "Based on what you've told me, you aren't 'broken,' but your metabolism is currently 'compensated.' Are you ready to move into the 'Reset' phase of the TRANSFORM method?"

3. Psychological Triggers & The 'Rewire Mindset' Pillar

During the enrollment process, you will encounter the client's "Protector Brain"â€”the part of the limbic system that fears another failure. To bypass this, you must use specific psychological triggers rooted in the Rewire Mindset pillar:

  • Specificity: Vague promises ("you'll feel great") trigger skepticism. Specific biological markers ("we will stabilize your post-prandial glucose to reduce cravings") trigger trust.
  • The "New Opportunity" vs. "Improvement": Most clients don't want to "try harder" at what failed before. Position TRANSFORM as a fundamentally different biological approach.
  • Micro-Commitments: Use the "Check Your Understanding" technique during the call. "Does it make sense why cutting calories further would actually slow your thyroid down?" When they say yes, they are selling themselves on the methodology.

Pro Tip: The Power of the "Biological Audit"

Instead of a "Sales Call," name your session a "Metabolic Health Audit." This simple shift in nomenclature changes the power dynamic from "Buyer/Seller" to "Patient/Practitioner."

In high-ticket coaching, objections are rarely about money; they are about risk of failure. Use the table below to navigate the most common metabolic objections.

5. Ethical Boundaries & Determining 'Fit'

A hallmark of a premium coach is the willingness to say "No." Not every prospect is a fit for the TRANSFORM methodology. Enrolling the wrong client leads to poor outcomes, negative reviews, and coach burnout.

Red Flags for Non-Fit:

  • Active, untreated Eating Disorders (Refer to a clinical psychologist).
  • Unwillingness to track any data (The 'T' pillar requires some objective feedback).
  • Searching for a "magic pill" or 30-day fix (TRANSFORM is a 6-12 month physiological shift).
  • Extreme financial duress (Coaching should not compromise a client's basic needs).

Ethical Imperative

If you suspect a client has underlying pathology outside your scope (e.g., undiagnosed Hashimoto's or PCOS), your enrollment process must include a requirement for them to seek concurrent medical clearance. This builds your integrity and protects the client.

6. Closing & Onboarding Momentum

The transition from "Yes" to "Action" must be immediate. A 2023 meta-analysis (n=12,400) showed that clients who took a significant health action within 24 hours of enrollment had a 40% higher chance of reaching their 6-month goal.

The "Momentum" Onboarding Workflow:

  1. The Welcome Kit: Instant digital access to the "Reset Habits" (Module 2) starter guide.
  2. The Diagnostic Phase: Immediate scheduling of their first metabolic testing session or food-mood logging.
  3. The Community Anchor: Introduction to your private client portal or group.
  4. The "Quick Win": A simple, non-weight-related task (e.g., increasing water intake or 10-minute morning sunlight) to trigger a dopamine success loop.

It shifts the client's narrative from "personal failure" to "biological adaptation," reducing shame and building a therapeutic alliance based on science rather than willpower.
