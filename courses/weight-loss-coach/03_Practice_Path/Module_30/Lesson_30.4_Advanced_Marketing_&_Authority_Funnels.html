1. Omni-Channel Content Strategy: Building Authority

  • LinkedIn: Focus on the "Metabolic Cost of Leadership." Target high-performing professionals by discussing how insulin resistance and cortisol dysregulation impact executive function.

3. Automated Indoctrination Funnels

The "Indoctrination Sequence" is a series of 5-7 automated emails delivered after a lead magnet download. The goal is to educate the prospect on the science of weight loss so that by the time they get on a sales call, they are 80% "sold" on your methodology. Research shows that nurtured leads make 47% larger purchases than non-nurtured leads (Annuitas Group).

The Authority Sequence Structure:

  1. Email 1: Value Delivery & The "Broken System." Deliver the magnet and explain why traditional "Eat Less, Move More" fails.
  2. Email 2: The Mechanism. Introduce a core pillar of TRANSFORM (e.g., The Muscle-Metabolism Connection).
  3. Email 3: The Case Study. Share a deep-dive story of a client who overcame a specific biological hurdle.
  4. Email 4: The Identity Shift. Focus on "Rewiring the Mindset"â€”moving from a "dieter" to a "metabolic manager."
  5. Email 5: The Invitation. A direct call to action for a Discovery Session, highlighting the limited capacity of your practice.

6. Implementation: The 90-Day Authority Roadmap

Building an authority funnel is not an overnight task. It requires a systematic approach to asset creation. Use the following roadmap to ensure consistency:

  • Days 1-30: Asset Creation. Finalize your "Signature Lead Magnet" and write your 5-part indoctrination sequence. Setup your email service provider (ESP).
  • Days 31-60: Outreach & Traffic. Begin posting 3x weekly on your primary authority channel (e.g., LinkedIn). Reach out to 5 local medical professionals to introduce your methodology.
  • Days 61-90: Optimization. Review your conversion rates. If people are downloading the magnet but not booking calls, adjust Email 4 and 5 to address more specific objections.

Check Your Understanding

Test your knowledge of authority marketing and funnels.

1. Why is an "Indoctrination Sequence" critical for high-ticket weight loss coaching?

It pre-educates the prospect on the science (the "Why" and "How") of your methodology. This builds trust, handles common metabolic objections before the call, and ensures the prospect is aligned with your T.R.A.N.S.F.O.R.M. framework.

2. What differentiates a "Mechanism-of-Change" case study from a standard testimonial?

A standard testimonial focuses only on the result (e.g., "I lost 20lbs"). An MOC case study explains the biological or psychological hurdles (e.g., insulin resistance, leptin signaling) and the specific interventions used to overcome them, demonstrating your clinical expertise.
