5. Scaling Your Methodology: Group & Digital

Scaling requires you to move from "Coaching the Person" to "Coaching the Process." The TRANSFORM method is uniquely suited for this because it is modular. You can deliver your signature framework in three distinct tiers:

  • The High-Touch Boutique (1:1): Full customization of all 9 pillars.
  • The Hybrid Group: Weekly masterminds focusing on one pillar of TRANSFORM per week for 9 weeks.
  • The Digital "Evergreen" Program: A self-paced course using your proprietary worksheets and video modules.

The key to scaling is Standardized Flexibility. You provide the standardized science (TRANSFORM), but the client applies it through your signature niche lenses. For example, your "Menopause Weight Loss Academy" might be a group program that follows the 9 pillars but uses content specifically tailored to hormonal fluctuations.
