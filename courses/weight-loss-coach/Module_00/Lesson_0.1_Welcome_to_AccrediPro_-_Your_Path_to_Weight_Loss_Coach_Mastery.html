Metabolic Flexibility

The capacity for the organism to adapt fuel oxidation to fuel availability; shifting between glucose and fatty acid oxidation.

Thermic Effect of Food (TEF)

The energy expenditure above the resting metabolic rate due to the cost of processing food for use and storage.

Hedonic Hunger

The drive to eat to obtain pleasure in the absence of an energy deficit, often triggered by environmental cues.

Circadian Alignment

The synchronization of behavioral patterns (eating/sleeping) with the internal biological clock to optimize hormonal output.

Scope of Practice & Ethics

As an AccrediPro Weight Loss Coach, you operate as a health educator and behavioral coach. It is critical to understand the boundaries of your profession to protect both your clients and your business.

A 2023 review in the Journal of Clinical Medicine emphasized that coaching interventions are most effective when they complement medical care rather than replace it. You do not diagnose disease, you do not prescribe medication, and you do not treat eating disorders (unless you hold additional clinical licensure). Instead, you provide the lifestyle architecture that allows the body's natural weight-regulation systems to function optimally.
