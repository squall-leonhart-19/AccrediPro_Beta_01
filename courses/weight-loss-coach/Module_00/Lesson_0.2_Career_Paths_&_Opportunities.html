However, the *type* of spending is changing. While spending on diet pills is stagnating, spending on professional services and behavioral coaching is skyrocketing.

Clinical & Medical Integration

There is a massive opportunity to work alongside medical professionals. Functional medicine doctors, endocrinologists, and bariatric surgeons are increasingly hiring "Health Coaches" to handle the "lifestyle prescription" part of the treatment plan.

In this role, you act as the bridge between the doctor's diagnosis and the patient's daily life. You ensure the patient follows the Metabolic Flexibility protocols designed to manage insulin resistance or PCOS. This path provides a steady stream of referrals and high professional credibility.

Corporate Wellness & B2B Solutions

Companies are losing billions in productivity due to obesity-related illness and absenteeism. A 2023 meta-analysis of 52 studies found that for every dollar spent on wellness programs, medical costs fall by about $3.27 and absenteeism costs fall by about $2.73.

As a METRIC-certified coach, you can offer corporate workshops or long-term "Metabolic Health Challenges." Companies value the Thermic Optimization (T) and Emotional Regulation (E) components of our method, as they directly impact employee energy levels and stress management.

Digital & Tech Consulting

The "Health Tech" sector is booming. From wearable devices (Oura, Whoop, CGM companies) to weight loss apps (Noom, MyFitnessPal), companies need experts to design content and coaching protocols. Career paths here include:

  • Content Strategist: Developing science-based curricula for health apps.
  • Product Consultant: Helping tech teams understand the psychology of Emotional Regulation in weight loss.
  • Virtual Head Coach: Managing a team of 50+ junior coaches for a digital platform.
