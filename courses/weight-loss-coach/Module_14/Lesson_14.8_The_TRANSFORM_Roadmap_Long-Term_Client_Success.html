4. Analyzing Long-Term Success Patterns

A 2021 meta-analysis of long-term weight maintainers found that three factors were non-negotiable for success past the 24-month mark. These are deeply embedded in the TRANSFORM methodology:

  1. High Physical Activity (NEAT + Resistance): Maintaining skeletal muscle mass is the primary defense against the metabolic slowdown that follows weight loss.
  2. Consistent Dietary Quality: Focusing on anti-inflammatory, nutrient-dense foods rather than just caloric restriction.
  3. Self-Monitoring Without Obsession: Using data (like the Deep Dive) as feedback, not judgment.

5. Ethics and the Professional Scope of Practice

As an AccrediPro certified coach, your ethical responsibility increases as your knowledge deepens. You must remain vigilant about:

  • Medical Referral: While you understand hormones (Lesson 6.1-6.4), you do not prescribe medication. You collaborate with the client's physician.
  • Disordered Eating: Long-term coaching requires identifying the thin line between "discipline" and "orthorexia."
  • Psychological Boundaries: You are a coach, not a licensed psychotherapist. If deep-seated trauma is the root of a client's "Rewire Mindset" block, referral is mandatory.
