The Data-Driven Practitioner: Tracking Your Own ROI

To scale to $10k-$30k per month, you must treat your practice as a laboratory. Track your "Conversion Rate" (Calls booked vs. Clients signed). A healthy conversion rate for a new practitioner is 25-30%. If you are below this, you need to revisit the "Emotional Regulation" (E) module to better understand your clients' psychological barriers during the sales process.
