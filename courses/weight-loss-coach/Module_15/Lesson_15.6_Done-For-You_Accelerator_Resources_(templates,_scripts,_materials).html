The Accelerator Philosophy: Efficiency vs. Friction

In the world of professional coaching, "friction" is the enemy of momentum. Friction occurs when you have to reinvent the wheel for every new client. Research in organizational psychology suggests that standardized workflows can increase service delivery efficiency by up to 40% while reducing practitioner burnout (Sikka et al., 2020).

The Accelerator resources are built on the principle of Decision Minimalism. By having your templates ready, you don't have to decide what to ask in an intake form or how to structure a contract. These decisions have been made based on the best practices of high-earning practitioners ($10k-$30k/month range).
