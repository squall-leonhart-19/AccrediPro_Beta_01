The Beta Launch Strategy

The fastest way to gain momentum is the Beta Launch. Instead of "selling coaching," you are "inviting 5-10 founding members to help pilot a new metabolic optimization program." This shifts the dynamic from a sales pitch to an exclusive opportunity.

The Beta Framework:

  • The Limit: Only 5-10 spots available.
  • The Discount: Offer a 30-50% "Founding Member" discount.
  • The Exchange: In return for the discount, the client agrees to provide a video testimonial and weekly feedback.
  • The Result: You get paid to learn, build your confidence, and generate the social proof needed for full-price launches.
