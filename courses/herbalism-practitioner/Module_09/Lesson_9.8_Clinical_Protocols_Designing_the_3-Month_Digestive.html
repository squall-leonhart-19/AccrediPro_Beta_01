5. Sustainable Practice: The "Kitchen Medicine" Transition

The mark of a master practitioner is not how many supplements a client takes, but how well they can maintain their health using Sustainable Practice (S). As the 3-month reset concludes, we transition the client from clinical tinctures to culinary integration.

The "Digestive Spice" Protocol: Teaching clients to use the "CCF" (Cumin, Coriander, Fennel) tea or incorporating warming spices helps maintain the "fire" without perpetual clinical intervention.

Check Your Understanding

1. Why is it contraindicated to start a client with high-dose bitters if they present with a bright red, "mapped" tongue and gastric burning?

Sustainable Practice (S). It ensures the client is not dependent on clinical products and can maintain health through daily, accessible "Kitchen Medicine."
