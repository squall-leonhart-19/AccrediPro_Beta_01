Your First 90-Day Roadmap

Success in this industry requires a systematic approach. Follow this roadmap to build your practice from scratch:

DAYS 1-30: FOUNDATION PHASE

Complete your exam and celebrate! Set up your business legal structure (LLC/Sole Prop). Most importantly, define your signature offer. What specific problem within the V.I.T.A.L. pillars do you solve better than anyone else?

DAYS 31-60: LAUNCH PHASE

Get your first 5 "Beta" clients. These can be from your existing network. Offer a "Founding Member" discount in exchange for a detailed testimonial. This builds the social proof necessary for premium pricing.

DAYS 61-90: GROWTH PHASE

Shift to marketing. Launch your social media strategy (3-5 posts/week focused on your niche). Start building an email list with a "Lead Magnet" (e.g., "The 7-Day Glucose Stabilization Guide for Perimenopause").
