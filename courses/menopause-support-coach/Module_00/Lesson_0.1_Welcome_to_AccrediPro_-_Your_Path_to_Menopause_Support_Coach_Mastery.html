The Global Menopause Revolution

We are currently witnessing a massive demographic shift. By 2025, it is estimated that 1.1 billion women worldwide will be postmenopausal. Despite this, menopause remains one of the most misunderstood and poorly managed biological transitions in human health. This represents not just a health crisis, but a significant opportunity for specialized support.

A 2023 study published in Mayo Clinic Proceedings estimated that menopause-related symptoms result in $1.8 billion in lost work productivity annually in the U.S. alone. When you add the direct medical costs, the economic impact is staggering. As an AccrediPro Menopause Support Coach, you are positioned at the intersection of health advocacy and economic stability.

Bridging the "Menopause Gap"

The "Menopause Gap" refers to the chasm between the symptoms women experience and the support they receive from the conventional medical system. Research indicates that while 80% of women experience symptoms that impact their quality of life, only a fraction receive evidence-based interventions. Furthermore, a survey of internal medicine residents found that only 20% felt adequately trained to manage menopause.

Conventional Approach

Symptom-suppression focused, often limited to a 15-minute consultation, with a "wait and see" mentality or reliance solely on HRT without lifestyle context.
