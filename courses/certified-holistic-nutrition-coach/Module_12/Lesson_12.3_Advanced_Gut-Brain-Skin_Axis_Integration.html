How do Short-Chain Fatty Acids (SCFAs) like Butyrate specifically protect the skin from inflammatory flares?

SCFAs travel systemically and induce the differentiation of T-regulatory (Treg) cells in the skin. These cells suppress overactive immune responses and enhance the expression of barrier proteins like filaggrin, reducing sensitivity and inflammation.

What is the relationship between "Metabolic Endotoxemia" and acne?

Metabolic endotoxemia occurs when Lipopolysaccharides (LPS) from the gut translocate into the blood due to intestinal permeability. These endotoxins bind to TLR4 receptors on skin cells, triggering pro-inflammatory cytokines that exacerbate acne and other skin conditions.
