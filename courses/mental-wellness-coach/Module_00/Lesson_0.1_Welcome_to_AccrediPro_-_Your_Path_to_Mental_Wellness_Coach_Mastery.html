Comparison: Coaching vs. Traditional Therapy

Traditional Therapy

Focuses on diagnosing and treating mental illness/pathology. Often looks at past trauma and "why" things happened. Relationship is clinical and hierarchical.

Mental Wellness Coaching

Focuses on optimizing mental health and performance. Looks at the "how" of moving forward. Relationship is a collaborative partnership based on action.
