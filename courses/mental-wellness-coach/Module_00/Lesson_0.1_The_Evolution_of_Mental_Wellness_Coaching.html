4. The Shift to Salutogenesis

The traditional medical model is built on pathogenesisâ€”the study of the origins of disease. While vital for medicine, it often ignores the origins of health. In this course, we embrace Salutogenesis, a term coined by Aaron Antonovsky, which focuses on factors that support human health and well-being rather than factors that cause disease.

Feature Pathogenesis (Traditional) Salutogenesis (Wellness Coaching)
Primary Focus Disease, symptoms, and risk factors. Health, resilience, and "Ease."
Goal Treat or cure the ailment. Optimize function and well-being.
Human State Binary: You are either sick or healthy. Continuum: We are all moving toward health.
Intervention Pharmacological or clinical therapy. Behavioral, lifestyle, and cognitive mapping.
