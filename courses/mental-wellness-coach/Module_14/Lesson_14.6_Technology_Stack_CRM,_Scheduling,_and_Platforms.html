Security, HIPAA, and Data Privacy

As a Mental Wellness Coach, you handle sensitive psychological data. A data breach is not just a legal liability; it is a profound violation of client trust. In the US, HIPAA compliance is the benchmark. Even if you are not a "covered entity" by strict legal definition, following HIPAA standards is a professional requirement for premium certification.

Your tech stack must include:

  • Encryption at Rest and in Transit: Data is scrambled so hackers cannot read it.
  • BAA (Business Associate Agreement): A legal document where the software provider agrees to protect your data.
  • Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Required for both you and the client to log in.
