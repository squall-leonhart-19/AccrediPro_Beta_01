3. Advanced KPI Tracking: Measuring the Invisible

To operate at a professional $997+ certification level, you must provide data-driven results. We categorize these into Objective Biomarkers and Subjective Psychometrics.

Objective KPIs

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV): A direct proxy for Autonomic Nervous System (ANS) flexibility.
  • Sleep Architecture: Tracking REM and Deep Sleep cycles via wearable technology (Oura, Whoop, etc.).
  • Blood Glucose Variability: Using Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGM) to link glycemic spikes to mood crashes.

Subjective KPIs

  • PROMIS-29: Patient-Reported Outcomes Measurement Information System (measures 7 domains).
  • WEMWBS: Warwick-Edinburgh Mental Wellbeing Scale (focuses on positive mental health).
  • Values Alignment Score: A proprietary M.I.N.D.F.U.L. metric measuring the gap between current behavior and core values.

Pro Tip: The Data-Narrative Bridge

Data alone is cold. A Level 2 Coach uses data to build a narrative. For example: "Notice how your HRV dropped on Tuesday (U)? That was the same day your blood sugar spiked after that processed meal (N), which triggered the 'I'm a failure' thought loop (M)."
