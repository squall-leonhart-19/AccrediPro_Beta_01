6. The Logistics of Architecture: Pacing and Energy

When designing your blueprint, you must account for the Cognitive Load of your participants. Wellness transformation is exhausting. If every week is "heavy," participants will drop out around Week 5 (The "Mid-Program Dip").

The Integration Week Strategy

For every 3 weeks of new content, schedule 1 "Integration Week." No new pillars. No new worksheets. Focus entirely on peer coaching, Q&A, and somatic processing of the previous 3 weeks. This prevents the "Information Overload" that triggers the sympathetic nervous system and halts neuroplasticity.
