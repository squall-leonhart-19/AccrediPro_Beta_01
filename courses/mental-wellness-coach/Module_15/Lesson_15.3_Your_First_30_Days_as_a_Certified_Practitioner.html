Week 1: Infrastructure & The DFY Kit

The biggest mistake new practitioners make is spending weeks on a website or logo. In Week 1, your focus is purely on Infrastructure Readiness. This means downloading and personalizing the resources in your portal.

The Week 1 Checklist:

  • Legal: Customize your Client Agreement and Liability Waiver (DFY Template).
  • Financial: Set up a simple payment processor (Stripe/PayPal) and link it to your booking page.
  • Logistics: Choose one video platform (Zoom/Google Meet) and one scheduling tool (Calendly).
  • Assessment: Print or digitize your "ASCEND Intake Form" for your first trial clients.

By utilizing the Done-For-You Accelerator Resources, you bypass the technical "setup fatigue" that causes many to quit before they start. These resources are designed to save you over 100 hours of administrative labor.
