Mitochondrial Rescue Protocols

In neurological autoimmunity, the "friendly fire" of the immune system often targets the mitochondria. When the brain's energy factories fail, neurons cannot maintain their membrane potential, leading to excitotoxicity. This is why Oxidative Stress Mitigation is the "O" in A.R.M.O.R.

Practitioner Tip: The BBB Window

Remember that the Blood-Brain Barrier (BBB) is most permeable during the acute phase of a flare. This is a "window of opportunity" where lipophilic (fat-soluble) nutrients like high-dose Omega-3s and Curcumin can most effectively reach the brain tissue. Once the "M" (Mucosal Integrity) phase seals the BBB, maintenance doses are sufficient.
