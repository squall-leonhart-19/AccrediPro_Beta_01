Digital Product Suites

Digital products serve two purposes: they provide passive income and act as a "warm-up" for your higher-priced services. In the autoimmune niche, information overload is a major pain point. Your digital products should provide clarity and "quick wins."

Key digital product types for Autoimmune Specialists include:

  • The "Quick Start" Guide: A $27-$47 PDF focused on a specific symptom (e.g., "The 7-Day Brain Fog Protocol").
  • Masterclass Series: A 3-part recorded video series on a specific pillar, like "Mucosal Integrity: Healing the Leaky Gate."
  • The Self-Paced Course: A comprehensive $297 course covering the entire A.R.M.O.R. Methodâ„¢ without live support.
  • Subscription Membership: A $49/month community for "Remission Maintenance," providing ongoing recipes, guest expert Q&As, and lab reviews.
