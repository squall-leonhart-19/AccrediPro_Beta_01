The 3-Circle Niche Model

To find your "Sweet Spot," evaluate your potential niche through these three lenses:

  1. Passion/Experience: Do you have a personal connection to a specific condition (e.g., Celiac, RA)?
  2. Market Demand: Are there enough people suffering? (Hint: With 50 million Americans affected, the answer is almost always yes).
  3. Profitability: Can the target audience afford high-ticket support?
CASE STUDY: The Power of Niching

Practitioner: Sarah J., Certified Autoimmune Specialist

Initial Approach: General Health Coach. Sarah struggled to find clients at $75/hour, making less than $2,000/month.

The Pivot: Sarah narrowed her niche to "Post-Partum Hashimoto's Recovery." She targeted women 0-2 years post-childbirth experiencing thyroid flares.

Outcome: By specializing, Sarah increased her package price to $2,500 for a 12-week program. She now maintains a waitlist of 15 clients and earns $12,500/month working 25 hours per week.
