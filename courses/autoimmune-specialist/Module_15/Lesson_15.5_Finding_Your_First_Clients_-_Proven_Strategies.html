Educational Marketing: The Authority Builder

Autoimmune clients are often highly educated about their conditions because they've had to be their own advocates. To win their trust, you must lead with education. This is known as Authority Marketing.

A meta-analysis of service-based business growth (n=4,500) showed that practitioners who utilized "Educational Webinars" or "Community Workshops" had a 31% higher retention rate than those using traditional advertising. By teaching the mechanism of "Molecular Mimicry" or the "Th1/Th2 Balance," you prove your expertise before a transaction ever occurs.
