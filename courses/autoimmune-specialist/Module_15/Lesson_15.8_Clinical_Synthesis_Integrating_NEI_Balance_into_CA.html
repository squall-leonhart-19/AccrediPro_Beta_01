Check Your Understanding

1. If a client has "flat-lined" cortisol and severe gut dysbiosis, why might you support the HPA axis before starting an intensive herbal antimicrobial protocol?

Because the body requires metabolic energy and a functional stress response to handle the "die-off" (endotoxemia) from gut treatments. Without NEI support, the client is likely to experience a severe flare during gut work.

2. What is the primary difference between a healing crisis and an immune flare in terms of biomarker trends?

In a healing crisis, inflammatory markers (like CRP or ESR) often stay stable or trend downward despite temporary symptoms. In an immune flare, these biomarkers typically spike alongside the symptoms.
