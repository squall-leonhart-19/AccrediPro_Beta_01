5. The Holy Spirit as Primary Architect

In Level 2 coaching, the planning process is a Pneumatic Process (Spirit-led). We incorporate prayerful discernment as a formal, documented step in the planning process. This distinguishes our methodology from "Christian-lite" coaching which uses secular tools with a verse attached at the end.
