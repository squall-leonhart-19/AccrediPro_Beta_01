5. Mapping the Kingdom Contribution Profile

The culmination of this lesson is the creation of the Kingdom Contribution Profile (KCP). This is a document you co-create with the client during the Align phase. It includes:

  1. Primary APEPT Function: Their operational mode.
  2. Sanctified Temperament: Their refined personality.
  3. Primary Charismata: Their 3-5 key spiritual gifts.
  4. The "Shadow Warning": The specific temptations they must watch for.
  5. The Vocation Bridge: How these show up in their 9-to-5.

Check Your Understanding

1. According to the lesson, what is the primary difference between how a Christian coach and a secular coach view "Temperament"?

The Christian coach views temperament through the lens of progressive sanctification and metanoia, seeing it as something that can be refined and "sanctified" by the Holy Spirit, rather than a static psychological trait.

2. What does the term "Shadow Side" refer to in the context of spiritual gifts?

The Shadow Side refers to the distortion of a spiritual gift when it is exercised without character, maturity, or the fruit of the Spirit, causing the strength to become a liability (e.g., leadership becoming dominance).
