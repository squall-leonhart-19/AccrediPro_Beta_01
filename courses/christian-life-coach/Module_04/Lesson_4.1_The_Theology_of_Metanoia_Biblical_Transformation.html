5. Identifying the 'Old Man' vs. the 'New Man' (Ephesians 4)

Ephesians 4:22-24 provides a three-step coaching framework for Metanoia: 1. Put off the Old Man (the former way of life). 2. Be renewed in the spirit of your mind. 3. Put on the New Man (created in true righteousness).

As a coach, you can use this as an assessment tool. When a client presents a challenge, ask:

  1. "What part of the 'Old Man' (old patterns, worldly logic, fleshly reactions) is currently driving this behavior?"
  2. "What scriptural truth is needed to renew the mind in this specific area?"
  3. "What does 'putting on the New Man' look like in your next practical step?"
