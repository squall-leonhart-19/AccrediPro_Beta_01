Scaling with Group Programs: Leverage & Community

Group coaching allows you to "Unload the Toxic Burden" (U) for many people simultaneously. This model is ideal for seasonal detoxes (Spring/Autumn) and niche challenges (e.g., "7-Day Liver Reset"). Research in the Journal of Medical Internet Research (2021) indicated that participants in group-based health interventions were 2.4 times more likely to complete a 30-day protocol compared to those working solo, due to the "community effect."

Earnings & Revenue Vision

As a Detox & Cleanse Specialist, your earning potential is directly tied to how you package your expertise. Avoid the "hourly trap" where you are only paid when you are physically working. Instead, sell outcomes.

Part-Time Practice (10-15 hrs/week)

Focus on 8-12 high-value clients and one small group program per quarter. Total: $3,200 - $8,000/month.

Full-Time Solo Practice (30-40 hrs/week)

Managing 25-40 clients with 3-month P.U.R.I.F.Y. packages. Total: $9,500 - $26,000/month.

Scaled Practice (Entrepreneur Model)

Utilizing automated courses, large group launches, and a small team. Total: $15,000 - $40,000+/month.

8 Career Paths for Specialists

Your certification opens doors beyond traditional private practice. Consider these specific opportunities:

1. Private Practice

The classic 1-on-1 model. Virtual or in-person. Income: $8k-$20k/mo.

2. Group Programs

Launch-based workshops or 6-week cleanses. Income: $5k-$45k per launch.

3. Corporate Wellness

Retainers for employee detox programs. Income: $2k-$10k/mo retainer.

4. Content Creation

Digital products, YouTube, and courses. Passive income: $1k-$20k/mo.

5. Product Formulation

Creating your own supplement line or tea blends. High-scale potential.

6. Writing & Publishing

Ebooks, health blogs, or traditional publishing. Advances: $5k-$100k+.

7. Teaching & Training

Educating other practitioners. Income: $50-$500/hr.

8. Integrative Teams

Working in clinics with MDs or DCs. Salary: $60k-$150k/year.

The 90-Day Practice Roadmap

Success in the first 90 days requires a focus on foundation, launch, and growth. Most AccrediPro graduates book their first paying client within 14 days of certification.

Days 1-30: Foundation

Complete certification, set up basic booking (Calendly/Practice Better), and define your signature P.U.R.I.F.Y. offer.

Days 31-60: Launch

Pricing Your Services for Profit

Premium practitioners do not compete on price; they compete on value. If you charge $50, you attract "tire kickers." If you charge $2,500, you attract committed clients who will do the work and get the results.

  • Single Consultations: $150 - $500 (Best for "Prepare the Pathways" assessments).
