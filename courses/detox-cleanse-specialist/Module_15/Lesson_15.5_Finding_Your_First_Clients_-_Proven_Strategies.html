Mastering the Discovery Call

The goal of a discovery call is not to "sell," but to assess readiness. If you approach the call as a clinical intake, the client naturally views you as a practitioner. A 2022 study on health coaching found that practitioners who used "motivational interviewing" techniques had a 40% higher client retention rate than those who used traditional sales scripts.

The 4-Step Discovery Call Framework

  1. The Gap: Where are they now vs. where do they want to be?
  2. The Root Cause: Briefly explain how toxicity/inflammation is causing their symptoms.
  3. The Bridge: Show how the P.U.R.I.F.Y. phases solve these specific issues.
  4. The Commitment: Ask, "Are you ready to commit to the preparation phase?"
