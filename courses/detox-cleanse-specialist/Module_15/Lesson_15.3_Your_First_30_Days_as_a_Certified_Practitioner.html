Week 3: Mastering the Intake (Restore Bio-Transformation)

By Week 3, you should have discovery calls on your calendar. This week is about conversion and assessment. You are not "selling" a detox; you are offering a transformation of their bio-transformation pathways.

During your discovery calls, use the Inquiry Framework:
1. Identify their "Toxic Load" symptoms (Brain fog, fatigue, skin issues).
2. Explain the P.U.R.I.F.Y. methodology briefly.
3. Bridge the gap between where they are and their "Clean Slate" health goals.

Week 4: Onboarding & Optimization (Intensify Gut Integrity)

Week 4 is about delivering the "First Session Experience." When a client pays, they immediately enter a state of "buyer's remorse" if communication stops. Your onboarding must be seamless.
