In the wellness industry, professionalism equals protection. Using "handshake agreements" is a recipe for liability. The Accelerator includes templates drafted to clarify your role as a practitioner (and not a medical doctor):

Includes clear terms on refunds, session cancellations, and the scope of practice. This protects your time and your income.

The Informed Consent & Waiver

A vital document that ensures the client understands the nature of detoxification and confirms they have cleared the protocol with their primary physician.
