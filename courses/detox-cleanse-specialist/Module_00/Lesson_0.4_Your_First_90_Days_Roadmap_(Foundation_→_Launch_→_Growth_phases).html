Why 90 days? Research in behavioral psychology suggests that three months is the "sweet spot" for significant habit integration and complex goal achievement. A 2021 study published in the Journal of Applied Psychology found that individuals who broke large professional goals into 90-day milestones were 76% more likely to achieve them compared to those with annual targets.

Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

The first 30 days are dedicated to internalizing the science and setting up the "clinical environment." You cannot guide a client through the P.U.R.I.F.Y. Protocol‚Ñ¢ if you are still searching for the definitions of Phase I and Phase II liver detoxification. This phase is about competence and compliance.

Key actions during this phase include:

  • Deep Dive Education: Complete the core modules on hepatic biotransformation, gut-barrier integrity, and lymphatic flow.
  • The "Self-Detox" Trial: Apply the P.U.R.I.F.Y. principles to your own life. You must be a product of the product to lead with authenticity.
  • Legal & Admin Setup: Secure your professional liability insurance, draft your client waivers, and set up a basic HIPAA-compliant intake system.
  • Niche Selection: Decide if you are the "Detox Specialist for High-Performance Executives" or the "Cleanse Coach for Postpartum Recovery."

Success Metrics & KPIs

You cannot manage what you do not measure. A 2023 meta-analysis of small service-based businesses (n=1,240) found that practitioners who tracked at least three KPIs were 3.4 times more likely to reach six-figure revenues within 18 months. For a Detox Specialist, we recommend tracking:

  • Inquiry-to-Intake Ratio: How many people who ask about your detox actually book an assessment? (Target: 30%+)
  • Client Symptom Reduction: Using a standardized "Toxicity Questionnaire" to show a 40-60% reduction in symptoms over 21 days.
  • Testimonial Capture Rate: What percentage of clients provide a written or video success story? (Target: 70%+)
