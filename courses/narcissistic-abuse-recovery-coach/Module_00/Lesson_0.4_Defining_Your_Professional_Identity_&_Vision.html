5. Professional Visioning & Goals

A professional vision is a detailed mental image of your practice in the future. To move beyond a "hobbyist" mindset, you must apply the S.M.A.R.T.E.R. goal framework (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound, Evaluated, Reviewed) to your career development.

The 12-Month Practice Roadmap

Research into entrepreneurship shows that practitioners who write down their goals are 42% more likely to achieve them (Matthews, 2015). Your roadmap should include:

  • Months 1-3: Completion of L1 Certification and establishment of ethical protocols.
  • Months 4-6: Niche selection and the launch of a "Beta" coaching program for 3-5 clients.
  • Months 7-12: L2 specialization and community impact (e.g., hosting a support group or contributing to a recovery blog).
