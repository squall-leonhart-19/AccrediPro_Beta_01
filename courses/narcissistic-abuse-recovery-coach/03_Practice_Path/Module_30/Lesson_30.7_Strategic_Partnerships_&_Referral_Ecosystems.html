What is the primary difference between a "Direct Referral" and "Co-Managed Care"?

A direct referral is simply sending a client to another professional. Co-managed care involves an ongoing, consented collaboration where the coach and the other professional (e.g., a therapist) align their strategies to ensure the client receives consistent, non-conflicting support.
