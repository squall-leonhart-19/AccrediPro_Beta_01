2. Future Pacing: The Ultimate Diagnostic Test

Future Pacing is the process of asking a client to vividly imagine a future scenario that previously would have triggered the negative emotion. It is the most robust way to check for "tail-enders" or missed aspects before ending a session.

The Practitioner's Script:
"I want you to close your eyes and step into a scene next week where this issue might normally come up. See what you see, hear what you hear. As you look at that person/situation, what do you notice in your body now?"
