3. The 'N' (Navigate) in Asynchronous Delivery

The most dangerous part of self-paced EFT is when a client hits a traumatic abreaction or a "moving aspect" without a practitioner present. In the T.A.P.P.I.N.G. Protocolâ„¢, Navigate Aspects (N) is the safeguard.

In digital products, you must build "Safety Exit Ramps." If a student reports a SUDS increase or a sudden "Vivid Flashback" (VAKOG aspect), your system must automatically trigger a "Grounding & Integration" (G) video. Never leave a student in the 'N' phase without a recorded pathway back to stability.

4. Technical Ecosystem & Automated Onboarding

Your digital transformation requires a robust Technical Stack. A professional practitioner should avoid "Frankenstein" systems (patching together 10 different tools) and instead aim for an integrated ecosystem.

The Recommended Practitioner Stack:

  • LMS (Learning Management System): Kajabi, Searchie, or Circle.so for hosting the T.A.P.P.I.N.G. modules.
  • CRM & Automation: ActiveCampaign or ConvertKit to send automated "Checking In" emails if a student hasn't logged in for 5 days (crucial for accountability).
  • Video Hosting: Vimeo (for privacy) or Searchie (which allows students to search for keywords like "headache" or "sadness" inside your videos).

Check Your Understanding

Test your knowledge on digital scaling strategies.

1. Why is the 'Navigate Aspects' (N) phase critical to handle differently in a digital course compared to 1:1 work?

In 1:1 work, the practitioner guides the client through shifting aspects in real-time. In digital delivery, the practitioner isn't there, so the course must include "Safety Exit Ramps" and pre-recorded "Grounding" (G) sequences to ensure the client doesn't get stuck in a high-intensity emotional state.

2. What is the primary benefit of the "Hybrid" coaching model over a standard DIY course?

The Hybrid model offers higher retention and better client outcomes (up to 72% higher) because it combines the scalability of digital content with the accountability and personalized guidance of live group support.
