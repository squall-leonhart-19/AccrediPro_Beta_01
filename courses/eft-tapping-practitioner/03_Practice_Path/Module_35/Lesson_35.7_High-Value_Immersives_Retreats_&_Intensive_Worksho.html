6. Post-Retreat Integration: The 90-Day 'G' Roadmap

The greatest risk of an intensive is the "re-entry shock." A client clears a massive "Table Leg" on Day 3, but returns to a toxic workplace on Day 6. Your immersive package must include a 90-day roadmap that utilizes the Personal Peace Procedure as a daily maintenance tool. This ensures the Cognitive Reframes achieved during the "Investigate" phase are solidified into permanent neural pathways.
