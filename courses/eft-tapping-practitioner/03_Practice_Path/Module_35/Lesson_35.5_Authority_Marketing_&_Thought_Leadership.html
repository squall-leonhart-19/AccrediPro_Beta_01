2. L4 Content Strategy & White Papers

In previous modules, we discussed social media and basic blogging. At Level 4, your content must shift toward Clinical Authority. This involves the creation of White Papers and Case Study Repositories. A 2022 survey found that 71% of B2B decision-makers use white papers to research purchasing decisions, and this applies equally to corporate wellness directors looking for EFT interventions.

Your content strategy should prioritize:

  • White Papers: Deep dives into specific issues (e.g., "The Impact of Somatic Tapping on Nursing Burnout: A 6-Month Analysis").
  • Case Study Repositories: Moving beyond "Client A felt better" to structured reports including SUDS/VOC data, duration of effect, and secondary physical improvements.
  • Clinical Data Synthesis: Translating peer-reviewed research (like the meta-analyses by Stapleton or Bach) into "Plain English" for stakeholders.
