4. The Financial Therapeutic Alliance

As a practitioner, your relationship with money sets the tone for the client's relationship with their own worth. Handling financial logistics professionally is part of the Ground & Integrate phase of your business. Ambiguity in pricing or "chasing" payments creates a rupture in the therapeutic container.

Essential Financial Policies:

  • The 48-Hour Rule: Cancellations within 48 hours are charged at 100%. This respects the practitioner's energy and the client's commitment.
  • Upfront Investment: Packages should be paid in full or via an automated payment plan (e.g., Stripe/PayPal subscriptions) before the first session.
  • The "No-Refund" Transformation: Once a package begins, refunds are generally not offered. This prevents the "Resistance Refund"â€”where a client wants to quit just as they hit a core trauma layer.

5. Calculating Practitioner Lifetime Value (PLV)

To build a sustainable practice, you must understand the Practitioner Lifetime Value of a client. This is the total revenue generated by a single client throughout their entire journey with you.
