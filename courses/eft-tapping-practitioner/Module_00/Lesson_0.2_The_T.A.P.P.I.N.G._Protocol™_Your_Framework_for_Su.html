3. The 7-Step Journey

The T.A.P.P.I.N.G. Protocolâ„¢ follows a logical sequence designed to safely down-regulate the nervous system while maintaining cognitive engagement.

T: Target the Issue

Isolate a specific event, emotion, or physical sensation. We aim for a "micro-moment" rather than a "macro-theme."

A: Assess Intensity

Using the 0-10 SUDS scale. This provides a baseline for the practitioner to measure progress and notice "shifts."

P: Prepare the Setup

The Setup Statement (even though I have this [Issue], I deeply and completely accept myself). This addresses Psychological Reversal.

P: Perform the Rounds

The actual physical application of tapping on the meridian endpoints while repeating the "Reminder Phrase."

I: Identify Shifts

Observing changes in the client's physiology (sighs, yawns, posture) and cognitive reframes ("It doesn't feel like my fault anymore").

N: Neutralize Remaining Triggers

"Chasing the pain"â€”finding the remaining pieces of the puzzle that keep the SUDS above a zero.

G: Ground and Integrate

Closing the session, ensuring the client is fully present in their body, and future-pacing their new emotional state.
