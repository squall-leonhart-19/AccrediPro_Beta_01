The first 30 days are about internal mastery and infrastructure. You cannot build a high-rise on a swamp. During this phase, your primary focus is the "N" (Nutrient-Density Optimization) and "U" (Understanding Bio-individuality) of the NURTURE Methodâ„¢.

Phase 2: Launch (Days 31-60)

In the second month, you move from the "lab" to the "field." This is where the "T" (Transitional Habit Formation) and "U" (Unified Family Integration) principles become your primary tools. You aren't looking for a full roster yet; you are looking for validation.

Case Study: The Beta Success

Practitioner: Elena R. | Strategy: 5-Client Beta Pilot

Scenario: Elena, a new coach, felt "imposter syndrome" and was hesitant to charge full price ($1,200) for her 3-month program.

Intervention: She launched a "Beta Pilot" for $497, limited to 5 families. She focused heavily on Unified Family Integration (U), coaching parents on modeling behaviors.

Outcome: 4 out of 5 families saw "dramatic improvement" in picky eating within 45 days. Elena gathered 4 video testimonials and 5-star Google reviews. She immediately raised her price to $1,500 for the next cohort, using the data from her Beta to prove value.

Phase 3: Growth (Days 61-90)

By day 60, you have data, testimonials, and confidence. Now, you focus on "R" (Resilient Immunity & Vitality) and scaling. This phase is about moving from 1-on-1 "hustle" to systematized growth.

During these 30 days, you will:

  • Referral Engine: Partner with 1-2 local pediatricians or occupational therapists.
  • Content Authority: Publish 2-3 deep-dive articles or videos on your niche topic.
  • Full-Price Transition: Sunset your beta pricing and launch your signature high-ticket package.
