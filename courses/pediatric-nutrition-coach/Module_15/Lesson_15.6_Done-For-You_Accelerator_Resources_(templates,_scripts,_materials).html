Marketing & Swipe Files

Marketing is often the biggest hurdle for pediatric coaches. Our "Swipe Files" are pre-written copy that you can "swipe" and adapt for your own use. These are based on Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) principles for the health industry.

Included in your Portal:

  • Email Booking Scripts: 5 templates for following up with "ghosted" leads, confirming discovery calls, and asking for testimonials.
  • Social Media Content Calendar: 30 days of "Education-First" posts (e.g., "The Truth About Toddler Milk," "3 Signs of Gut Dysbiosis").
  • The "First Client" Outreach Script: A low-pressure script to send to friends and family to announce your new certification.
  • Lead Magnet Templates: Canva templates for "The Picky Eater's Protein Guide" and "The Gut-Happy Lunchbox."

Tracking & Outcome Management

To build a high-value practice, you must prove results. Tracking is how you demonstrate the ROI (Return on Investment) to the parents. Your Accelerator includes:

  • The Practitioner Dashboard (Excel/Google Sheets): Track all active clients, their last session date, and their primary goal in one view.
  • The Progress Tracker: A simple "Before & After" assessment for parents to fill out every 4 weeks, rating their child's energy, sleep, and digestion on a scale of 1-10.
  • The Supplement Protocol Template: A professional way to present your recommendations (Resilient Immunity & Vitality) so they are easy for busy parents to follow.

Implementation Roadmap

Do not try to use all 50+ resources on day one. Follow this "Accelerator Roadmap" to avoid overwhelm:

Phase 1: The Essentials (Days 1-3)

Focus on the Legal Suite and Intake Forms. You cannot accept a client without these. Set up your digital signature tool (e.g., HelloSign or DocuSign) with our templates.

Phase 2: The Visibility (Days 4-7)

Use the Social Media Templates and Outreach Scripts. Announce your certification and offer 3 "Founding Member" spots at a discounted rate to get your first testimonials.

Phase 3: The Delivery (Ongoing)

Integrate the Progress Trackers and NURTURE Worksheets into your actual coaching calls to deepen the value and ensure long-term client success.
