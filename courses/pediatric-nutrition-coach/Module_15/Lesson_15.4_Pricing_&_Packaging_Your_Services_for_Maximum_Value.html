The Hourly Rate Trap

Many new practitioners enter the pediatric nutrition space with a "consultant mindset," charging by the hour (e.g., $100/hour). While this feels safe, it is a foundational error that limits both your income and your client's results. In pediatric nutrition, meaningful physiological changeâ€”such as microbiome restoration or habit formationâ€”rarely happens in a single 60-minute session.

When you charge by the hour, you inadvertently incentivize the client to spend *less* time with you to save money. This leads to incomplete protocols, poor compliance, and ultimately, a lack of results. A 2022 study on health coaching outcomes found that clients enrolled in structured, multi-month programs were 3.4 times more likely to achieve their primary health goals compared to those attending sporadic "a la carte" sessions.

Value-Based Pricing Principles

Value-based pricing is not about what it "costs" you to deliver the service; it is about what the transformation is worth to the parent. For a parent of a child with chronic eczema, severe picky eating, or digestive distress, the value is not "one hour of nutrition advice." The value is a child who sleeps through the night, a family dinner without tears, and the avoidance of long-term health complications.

To implement value-based pricing, you must shift your communication from *features* (what you do) to *outcomes* (what they get). Using the NURTURE Methodâ„¢, you are not just providing a meal plan; you are providing a Root-Cause Assessment and Transitional Habit Formation system that secures the child's future vitality.

The NURTURE Signature Package

Your "Signature Package" is your flagship offering. It should be designed to take a client from "Crisis/Confusion" to "Confidence/Vitality." A standard 12-week NURTURE Signature Package typically includes:

  • N - Nutrient-Density Optimization: Custom pantry makeover and nutrient-gap analysis.
  • U - Understanding Bio-individuality: Initial 90-minute deep-dive assessment.
  • R - Root-Cause Assessment: Interpretation of functional testing or symptom tracking.
  • T - Transitional Habit Formation: Bi-weekly 30-minute "Check-in" calls.
  • U - Unified Family Integration: A dedicated session for the spouse/caregivers.
  • R - Resilient Immunity: Targeted supplement protocol and seasonal adjustments.
  • E - Evidence-Based Application: Unlimited messaging support for 90 days.

Tiered Pricing Strategy

While your Signature Package is your core, offering tiers allows you to meet clients where they are while still maintaining high value. A "Good-Better-Best" model is standard in premium coaching.

Tier 1: The Intensive (Bronze)

A 30-day "Kickstart" focused on immediate symptomatic relief. Includes 1 deep-dive and 1 follow-up. Price point: $500 - $750.

Tier 2: The Signature NURTURE Transformation (Silver)

The 12-16 week flagship program described above. This is your most popular option. Price point: $1,500 - $2,500.

Tier 3: The Concierge/VIP (Gold)

6 months of support, including home visits (or virtual kitchen tours), direct text access, and coordination with the child's pediatrician. Price point: $4,000 - $6,000.

Handling Pricing Objections

When a parent says, "That's a lot of money," they are rarely saying they don't have it. They are usually saying, "I'm not sure if this will work for my child." Your job is to reframe the investment. Compare the cost of your program to the alternative:

  • The cost of 5 years of failed interventions and specialized co-pays.
  • The cost of lost school days and parental work productivity.
  • The "cost" of the child's self-esteem and long-term metabolic health.
