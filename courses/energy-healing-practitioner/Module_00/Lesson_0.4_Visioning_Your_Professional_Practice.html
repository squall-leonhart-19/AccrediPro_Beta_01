The Ideal Client Avatar (ICA)

Your ICA is a detailed profile of the person who benefits most from your specific application of the protocol. When defining your avatar, move beyond demographics (age/location) into psychographics and energetic states.

Ask yourself: "What is the specific energetic blockage my client is experiencing, and how does that manifest in their physical reality?" For example, a client with a "Sacral imbalance" may present with creative blocks, reproductive health issues, or an inability to process emotional transitions.
