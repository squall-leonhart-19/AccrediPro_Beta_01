5. Time-Independent Effects: Retrocausal Healing

Perhaps the most mind-bending aspect of non-locality is its relationship with time. In quantum physics, equations are often time-symmetric, meaning they work equally well forward and backward. Research into Retrocausality suggests that consciousness can influence events that have already occurred (in the past) or set the stage for events in the future.

A famous (and controversial) study by Leibovici (2001), published in the British Medical Journal, examined the effects of "retroactive" prayer. In a randomized controlled trial of 3,393 patients with bloodstream infections, a group was prayed for *after* they had already been discharged from the hospital. The "prayed-for" group (even though the prayer happened after the fact) had significantly shorter hospital stays and shorter durations of fever than the control group.
