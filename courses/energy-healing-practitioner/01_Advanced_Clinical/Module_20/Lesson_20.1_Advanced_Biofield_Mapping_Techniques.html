4. Scanning for Leaks vs. Congestions

In the Clear Energy Field (C) phase of the B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Protocolâ„¢, we must first assess the nature of the field distortion. Not all blocks are the same. Advanced practitioners distinguish between Energetic Leaks and Energetic Congestions.

Energetic Leaks (Breaches)

A "thinning" or hole in the outer layers (Secondary/Tertiary). Often caused by trauma or boundaries issues. The client feels "drained" by others or hypersensitive to environments.

Energetic Congestions (Density)

A "thickening" or accumulation of stagnant energy. Often caused by suppressed emotions or toxicity. The client feels "heavy," "stuck," or unable to move forward.

Advanced scanning technique involves the "Perpendicular Probe." Instead of moving parallel to the body, move your hand slowly toward the body from 1 meter away. Note the exact distance where the "resistance" begins. If the resistance is immediate but weak, you are dealing with a leak. If the resistance is delayed but hard/repulsive, you are dealing with congestion.
