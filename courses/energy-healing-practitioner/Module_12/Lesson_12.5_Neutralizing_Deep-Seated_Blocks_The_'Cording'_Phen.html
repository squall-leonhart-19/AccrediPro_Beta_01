2. The Psychology of Stagnation: Trauma as Density

Blockages are rarely just "bad energy"; they are frozen snapshots of unresolved psychobiological responses. When a client experiences a traumatic event, the nervous system may fail to complete the "threat response cycle." This incomplete physiological process leaves a residue in the biofield, characterized by high entropy and low coherence.

As a practitioner, you must address the psychological hook before or during the energetic neutralization to ensure the block does not simply reform.
