In the Align Chakras (A) step, we are not merely balancing spinning vortices; we are actively influencing Vagal Tone. The Vagus nerve wanders through the throat (Throat Chakra), heart (Heart Chakra), and solar plexus (Solar Plexus Chakra). When we align these centers, we reduce "neural noise" in the Vagal pathway. 3. Neutralizing the 'Freeze' Response (The 'N' Method)

One of the most complex challenges in energy healing is the Freeze Response. According to Polyvagal Theory, this is the Dorsal Vagal state—an evolutionary survival mechanism of "playing dead." Energetically, this manifests as a "cold," dense, or stagnant block, often located in the lower chakras or the sub-auric layers.

The Neutralize Blocks (N) step is critical here. Unlike a Sympathetic "Fight/Flight" block which feels "hot" and "jagged," a Freeze block is a protective shell. To neutralize it, the practitioner must use Phase-Reverse Frequencies. Instead of pushing energy into the block (which causes the client to retreat further into freeze), we use the Neutralization method to gently dissolve the "energetic armor."
