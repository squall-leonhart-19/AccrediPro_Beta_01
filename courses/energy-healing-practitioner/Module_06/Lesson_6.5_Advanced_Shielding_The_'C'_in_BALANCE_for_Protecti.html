  • Impermeable (The Wall): Necessary in cases of acute psychic attack or severe environmental toxicity. It is a temporary emergency measure.
  • Permeable (The Filter): The goal of the B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Protocol‚Ñ¢. This allows high-vibrational nutrients and love to enter while filtering out low-vibrational static.
