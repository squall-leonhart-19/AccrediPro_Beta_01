5. The 'Closing Circle' Ritual: Sealing the Field

One of the most common mistakes in energy facilitation is allowing participants to leave while their fields are still "wide open." This can lead to "energetic hangovers" or vulnerability to environmental stressors (EMFs, negative news, etc.).

The Closing Circle serves as the E (Empower Client) phase. It involves:

  1. Grounding: Visualizing roots extending from the feet into the Earth (neutralizing excess charge).
  2. Sealing: A collective "zipping up" of the central meridian.
  3. Disconnection: Formally intending the dissolution of the "group cord" so participants do not remain energetically tethered to one another.

Critical Safety Warning

Never end a high-vibration workshop abruptly. If a participant is "floating" (ungrounded), they are at a higher risk of accidents or emotional overwhelm upon re-entering the "real world." Ensure every participant has "landed" back in their physical body before dismissal.
