    4. Outsourcing & Team Building

    You cannot scale a "solopreneur" practice indefinitely without hitting the Energetic Wall. Outsourcing allows you to stay in your "Zone of Genius" (Healing and Protocol Development).

    When to Hire?

    A meta-analysis of small business growth (2021) suggests that hiring should occur when you are at 80% capacity. Waiting until 100% capacity leads to "Hiring in Crisis," which often results in poor culture fit and training errors.

    • The Virtual Assistant (VA): First hire. Handles inbox management, scheduling, and basic social media.
    • The Ads Manager: Hired once you have a proven "High-Ticket Package" (Lesson 6). They manage the flow of new leads.
    • Junior Practitioner: Hired to handle the "C" (Clear Energy Field) and "B" (Biofield Assessment) phases for entry-level clients, while you focus on L4 Master-level interventions.
