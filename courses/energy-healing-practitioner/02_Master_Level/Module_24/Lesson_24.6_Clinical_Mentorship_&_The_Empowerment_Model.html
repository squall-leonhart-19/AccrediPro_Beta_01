2. The Psychology of Secondary Gain in Energy Medicine

This refers to the subconscious benefits a client receives from remaining ill or energetically depleted. Energetically, secondary gain manifests as a "hook" or "anchor" in the Solar Plexus or Sacral chakras, where the client subconsciously resists the Neutralize (N) phase of the B.A.L.A.N.C.E. Protocol because the blockage serves a protective or social function.
