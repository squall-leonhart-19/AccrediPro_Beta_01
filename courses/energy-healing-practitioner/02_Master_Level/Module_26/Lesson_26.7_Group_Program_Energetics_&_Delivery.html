4. Maintaining Safety: Neutralizing Blocks (N) in Groups

One of the greatest risks in group energetics is Emotional Contagion. If one participant undergoes a massive emotional release (Neutralize Blocks - N), the "ripples" can trigger others in the group who may not be ready for that depth of clearing. As a practitioner, your priority is Energetic Containment.

Safety Protocols for the N-Phase:

  1. The "Bubble" Technique: Before any deep clearing, instruct participants to establish their own "Sovereign Field" boundary, ensuring they remain witnesses to the group rather than sponges.
  2. Muting/Privacy (Virtual): In virtual settings, encourage participants to turn off cameras if they feel a massive release coming, preventing visual triggers for the rest of the group.
  3. Grounding Anchors: Always end group sessions with a physical grounding exercise (e.g., foot stomping, drinking water) to "break the circuit" of the collective field.
