5. Safety Protocols: Recognizing 'Energy Overload'

Activation is a powerful tool, but it carries the risk of Energy Overload (sometimes called a "healing crisis" or "Kundalini spike"). Sensitive clients, particularly those with trauma histories or nervous system dysregulation, may find rapid activation overwhelming.

Practitioner Warning: Overload Signs

If a client exhibits rapid eye movement, involuntary muscle twitching, sudden nausea, or emotional flooding (uncontrollable weeping or anger) during the 'L' phase, immediately cease activation. Shift to the 'C' (Clear) or 'E' (Empower) phases to ground the energy.

Managing the Overload:

If overload occurs, use the 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Method or direct palm-to-earth contact. Statistics from clinical energy medicine trials show that approximately 4.2% of clients experience significant energetic discharge during the first activation session.
