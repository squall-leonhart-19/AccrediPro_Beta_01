5. Strategic Sequencing: Neutralize vs. Nourish

A common mistake is attempting to neutralize a block when the client's overall vitality is too low. If the client's lifeforce (L) is depleted, neutralizing a major block can lead to "healing crisis" exhaustion.

The practitioner must decide: Do I break the dam, or do I fill the reservoir first?
