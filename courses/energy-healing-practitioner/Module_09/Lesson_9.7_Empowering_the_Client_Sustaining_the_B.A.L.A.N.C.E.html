5. Motivational Interviewing in Energy Medicine

Motivational Interviewing (MI) is a collaborative, goal-oriented style of communication with particular attention to the language of change. In energy healing, we use MI to help clients overcome resistance to lifestyle changes that affect their biofield (e.g., diet, sleep, toxic relationships).

The OARS Framework for Energy Practitioners:

  • Open-Ended Questions: "How would your life look if your energy felt this clear every morning?"
  • Affirmations: "I noticed how committed you were to your grounding practice this week; that strength is showing in your field."
  • Reflective Listening: "It sounds like you feel a conflict between your social commitments and your need for energetic clearing."
  • Summarizing: "So, we've identified that your morning routine is your strongest asset, and we're looking for a way to bring that same calm to your 3 PM slump."
