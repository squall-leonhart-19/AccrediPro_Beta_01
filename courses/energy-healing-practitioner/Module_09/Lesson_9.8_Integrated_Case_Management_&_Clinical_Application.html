4. Ethical Boundaries & Referrals

As an Energy Healing Practitioner, you are a vital member of the integrative health team, but you must strictly adhere to your Scope of Practice. Energy medicine is a complementary modality, never a replacement for primary medical or psychological care.

Red Flags for Immediate Referral:

  • Medical: Sudden unexplained weight loss, localized heat/redness (potential infection), or neurological symptoms (numbness, slurred speech).
  • Psychological: Statements of self-harm, active hallucinations, or severe dissociative episodes during a session.
Ethics Warning

Never diagnose a physical or mental illness based on energetic findings. Instead of saying, "You have a liver problem," use clinical language: "I am detecting significant energetic density in the right hypochondriac region; I recommend a routine check-up with your physician to ensure physical homeostasis."
