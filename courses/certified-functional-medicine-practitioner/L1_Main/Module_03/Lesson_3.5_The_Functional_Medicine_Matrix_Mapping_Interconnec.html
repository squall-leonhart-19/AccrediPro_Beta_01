2. The Seven Physiological Nodes

The Matrix organizes human biology into seven "nodes." Unlike conventional medicine, which organizes by organ systems (Cardiology, Gastroenterology, etc.), Functional Medicine organizes by biological processes. This is why a "skin problem" might be mapped under "Defense and Repair" or "Assimilation."

Check Your Understanding

Both would be mapped under the "Communication" node, as they involve endocrine signals and hormonal balance.
