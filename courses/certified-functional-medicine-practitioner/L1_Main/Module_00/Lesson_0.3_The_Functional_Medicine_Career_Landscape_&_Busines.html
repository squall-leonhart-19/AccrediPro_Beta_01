Hi, I'm Sarah. I want to take a moment to acknowledge exactly where you are. If you are a nurse looking for a way to help people without the burnout, a teacher ready for a new chapter, or a wellness enthusiast who finally wants the "official" title to match your passion—you are in the right place.

I know the "imposter syndrome" can creep in. You might be wondering, "Can I really learn this? Will people actually pay me for my advice?" The answer is a resounding yes. This journey isn't just about reading textbooks; it's about building the confidence to change lives, starting with your own.

1. The 4-Tier Transformation

Our curriculum is meticulously designed to take you from "interested" to "expert" through a progressive 4-tier system. We don't just dump information on you; we build your clinical muscle memory step-by-step.

Tier Modules Focus Outcome
L1: Foundation 1 - 15 Core methodology, biochemistry, and the G.U.T.S. system. Understanding the "Why" behind chronic dysfunction.
L2: Advanced 16 - 23 Complex cases, advanced lab interpretation, and autoimmune protocols. Clinical depth and the ability to handle "mystery" cases.
L3: Master 24 - 29 Supervision skills, group coaching, and teaching others. Establishing authority and scaling your impact.
L4: Practice 30 - 36 Business setup, legalities, and client acquisition. A fully operational, revenue-generating practice.

2. What Makes This Different

Most certifications are "information-heavy and application-light." You finish the course with a 300-page manual but no idea what to say when a client sits across from you. AccrediPro is built on the "Practice Lab" philosophy.

Starting in Module 1, you will engage in simulated client interactions. You won't just learn about Vitamin D; you'll learn how to explain Vitamin D deficiency to a 45-year-old mother of three who is exhausted and skeptical. You aren't just gaining knowledge; you are gaining a professional identity.

4. Time Commitment & Pacing

We know you have a life. A 2023 survey of our successful graduates (n=450) showed that the most effective pace was 3-5 hours per week. This allows for "deep work" without leading to burnout.

If "life happens"—a move, a family illness, or a busy season at your current job—the curriculum is flexible. You have lifetime access to the materials. However, we recommend completing at least two lessons per week to maintain momentum and stay engaged with the community.

6. Certification Requirements

To earn your Certified Functional Medicine Practitioner (CFMP) credential, you must:

  1. Complete all video lessons and reading materials across all 4 Tiers.
  2. Pass the end-of-module quizzes with a score of 80% or higher.
  3. Complete the "Final Case Study" (Tier 3), where you will develop a mock protocol for a complex client.
  4. Submit your "Practice Readiness" checklist (Tier 4).
