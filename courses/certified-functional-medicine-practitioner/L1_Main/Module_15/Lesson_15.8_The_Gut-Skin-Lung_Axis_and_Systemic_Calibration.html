Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the GALT (Gut-Associated Lymphoid Tissue) significant in autoimmune cases?

Because 70-80% of immune cells reside there. If the gut barrier is compromised (leaky gut), these immune cells are constantly exposed to foreign particles, leading to systemic inflammation and potential molecular mimicry (autoimmunity).

2. In the 5R Protocol, what does "Reinoculate" typically involve?

Reintroducing beneficial bacteria through fermented foods and high-quality probiotics, as well as "feeding" them with prebiotics (fiber/polyphenols) to encourage a healthy microbial balance.
