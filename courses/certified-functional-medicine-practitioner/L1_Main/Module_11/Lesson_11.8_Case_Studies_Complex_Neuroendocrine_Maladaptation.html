Common Mistakes to Avoid

Even experienced practitioners trip up here. Watch out for these "traps":

  • The "Just Relax" Trap: Telling a high-achieving woman to "just relax" is dismissive. Instead, say: "We need to provide your nervous system with physiological safety."

  • Over-Supplementing: Don't try to fix a broken NEI trio with 15 different pills. Start with circadian rhythm and blood sugar stability.
