7. Troubleshooting Common Client Plateaus

When a client stops progressing, the novice coach changes the diet. The Expert Practitioner audits the V.I.T.A.L. pillars. Most plateaus are not "calorie problems"; they are "environment problems."

Consider the Toxic Load (Inflammation). If a client is losing fat, they are releasing stored fat-soluble toxins (POPs - Persistent Organic Pollutants) into the bloodstream. If the "Targeted Nourishment" doesn't include adequate Phase II liver support (sulforaphane, glycine, taurine), the body may stall fat loss to protect the brain from these circulating toxins. This is a physiological protective mechanism, not a failure of will.
