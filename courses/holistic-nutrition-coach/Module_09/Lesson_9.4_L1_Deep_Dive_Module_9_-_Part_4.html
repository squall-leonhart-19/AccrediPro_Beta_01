4. The Practitioner's Logic Flow: Troubleshooting Stagnation

When a client stops progressing, most coaches simply "cut calories" or "add cardio." The AccrediPro practitioner looks deeper. We use a hierarchical logic flow based on the V.I.T.A.L. pillars:

  1. Check Pillar A (Aligned Lifestyle): Is the client sleeping < 7 hours? High cortisol will override the most perfect diet.
  2. Check Pillar I (Inflammation): Is there a hidden food sensitivity or low-grade infection (e.g., H. Pylori)?
  3. Check Pillar T (Targeted Nourishment): Is the protein intake sufficient to maintain lean mass and thermogenesis?
  4. Check Pillar V (Vitality): Is the thyroid-adrenal axis compensated? (Check morning basal body temperature).
