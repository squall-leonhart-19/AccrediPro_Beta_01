Case Studies

Case Study 1: Sarah, 42, Chronic Fatigue

Client: Sarah, a 42-year-old female, presents with chronic fatigue, digestive issues, and brain fog. She reports a history of antibiotic use and a diet high in processed foods.

Assessment: Gut microbiome analysis reveals low diversity and an overgrowth of opportunistic bacteria. Bloodwork indicates elevated inflammatory markers.

Intervention:

  • Personalized meal plan emphasizing whole, unprocessed foods and prebiotic-rich vegetables.
  • Supplementation with a high-quality probiotic, omega-3 fatty acids, and adaptogenic herbs.
  • Stress reduction techniques, including mindfulness meditation and yoga.

Outcomes: After 3 months, Sarah reports significant improvements in energy levels, digestion, and cognitive function. Repeat gut microbiome analysis shows increased diversity and reduced levels of opportunistic bacteria. Her inflammatory markers also decreased.

Case Study 2: John, 55, Metabolic Syndrome

Client: John, a 55-year-old male, diagnosed with metabolic syndrome. He has elevated blood sugar, high blood pressure, and excess abdominal fat. He reports a sedentary lifestyle and a diet high in refined carbohydrates.

Assessment: Bloodwork confirms insulin resistance and elevated cholesterol levels. Dietary assessment reveals inadequate fiber intake and excessive sugar consumption.

Intervention:

  • Personalized meal plan focusing on whole grains, lean protein, and healthy fats.
  • Supplementation with berberine, chromium, and a fiber supplement.
  • Gradual increase in physical activity, starting with daily walks and progressing to resistance training.

Outcomes: After 6 months, John's blood sugar, blood pressure, and cholesterol levels have significantly improved. He has also lost weight and increased his muscle mass. He reports feeling more energetic and motivated.
