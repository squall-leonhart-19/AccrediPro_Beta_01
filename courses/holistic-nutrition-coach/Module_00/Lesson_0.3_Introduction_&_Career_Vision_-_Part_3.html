5. Ethics & Scope of Practice: Protecting Your Vision

To maintain a $997+ certification standard, you must operate within your legal and ethical boundaries. A Holistic Nutrition Coach does not diagnose or treat disease. Instead, we "support the body's innate ability to heal by addressing underlying nutritional imbalances."

Scope of Practice Check

Scenario: A client comes to you with a self-diagnosis of "Adrenal Fatigue" and asks for a prescription-strength supplement protocol. What is the correct professional response?

You must decline "prescribing" and instead explain that you will assess their Vitality (V) markers through a nutritional lens. You suggest they consult their physician for diagnostic testing while you focus on supporting their circadian rhythm and micronutrient status to optimize energy production.
