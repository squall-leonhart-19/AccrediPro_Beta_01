    4. Documentation Audit: Ensuring L4 Standards

    Professionalism at the L4 level requires impeccable record-keeping. In the event of a medical audit or legal inquiry, your documentation is your primary defense. A 2021 study on health coaching liability found that 62% of practitioners failed to maintain adequate "Scope of Practice" boundary documentation.

    The L4 Documentation Checklist
    • Informed Consent: Signed document explicitly stating you are not a medical doctor and do not prescribe or adjust medication.
    • SOAP Notes: Subjective, Objective, Assessment, and Plan notes for every session.
    • Medical Release: Signed HIPAA-compliant forms for any communication with the client's medical team.
    • Data Privacy: Verification that all digital storage (spreadsheets, apps) is encrypted and meets GDPR/HIPAA standards.
