Local Workshops & Micro-Events

In a digital world, local presence is a massive differentiator. Hosting a 45-minute talk at a local library, community center, or yoga studio positions you as the local authority. Use a "High-Value" title like: "The 3 Hidden Reasons Your Blood Sugar Won't Budge (And How to Fix Them Without Giving Up Fruit)."

At the end of every talk, offer a "Metabolic Audit" (a free 15-minute call). This is your lead-in to your full coaching program. Data shows that in-person speaking engagements convert to clients at a rate of 15-20%, compared to 1-2% for cold social media traffic.
