The Power of the Niche

The biggest mistake new practitioners make is trying to help "everyone with blood sugar issues." In a crowded marketplace, being a generalist is a race to the bottom on pricing. A niche is not a limitation; it is a clinical advantage. When you specialize, your marketing speaks directly to a specific person's pain, and your clinical outcomes improve because you see the same patterns repeatedly.

According to the 2023 International Coaching Federation (ICF) Global Coaching Study, specialized health coaches command fees 34% higher than general life or wellness coaches. By focusing on a specific population, you become the "go-to" expert in that sub-field.
