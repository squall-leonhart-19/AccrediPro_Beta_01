The Client Intake & Assessment Suite

Your first interaction with a client sets the tone for the entire relationship. A professional, thorough intake process communicates that you are a clinical expert, not a hobbyist. Our DFY suite includes a comprehensive 7-page Health History & Glycemic Baseline Form.

Key Components of the DFY Intake Suite:

  • Medical History & Medications: Essential for identifying "Red Flags" and coordinating with physicians.
  • STABLE Method Assessment: Pre-built questions to identify Glycemic Variability (S) and Circadian Disruptions (L).
  • Symptom Burden Tracker: A quantitative way to measure progress beyond just glucose numbers.
  • Client Readiness Scale: A psychological tool to assess the client's stage of change.

Clinical Tracking & Data Management

As a Blood Sugar Coach, your value lies in Data Integration (T in STABLE). We provide proprietary spreadsheets that allow you to import CGM data and automatically highlight patterns that the standard software might miss.

The Glycemic Variability Dashboard allows you to input daily readings and see a visual representation of the "Blood Sugar Rollercoaster." This makes it incredibly easy to show clients the "Food Order Effect" (E) or the impact of a poor night's sleep (L) on their morning fasting numbers.
