Week 3: Implementing STABLE Tracking

Once your founding clients are onboarded, Week 3 is about the Tactical Tracking (T) phase. This is where you differentiate yourself from "dietitians" by using real-time data integration.

A meta-analysis of health coaching interventions (n=12,430) published in The American Journal of Medicine showed that coaches who utilized objective data (like CGM or frequent FGM) saw a 22% greater reduction in A1c than those using subjective food diaries alone.

The Onboarding Sequence

1. Baseline Week: Client wears CGM with no dietary changes to identify "Glycemic Variability" (The S Phase).
2. Data Review: Use the STABLE Method dashboard to identify the top 3 triggers.
3. Intervention: Apply "Active Sensitization" (A) through post-meal movement.
