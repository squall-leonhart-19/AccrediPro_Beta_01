Data Infrastructure & Tech Stack

A professional coach is only as good as their data. In your first 90 days, you must master the "Digital Health Suite." A 2023 meta-analysis of n=4,120 diabetes patients showed that those using digital tracking with coach intervention had an average A1c reduction of 1.1% more than those using tracking alone (p < 0.05).

Your tech stack should include:

  1. CGM Portals: Professional accounts on LibreView (Abbott) and Dexcom Clarity.
  2. EHR/CRM: A HIPAA-compliant platform (like Practice Better or Healthie) for charting and billing.
  3. Communication: Secure messaging to prevent clinical boundaries from being crossed via personal SMS.

Key Lesson Takeaways

  • Phase 1 (Foundation): Focus on legal setup, tech infrastructure, and mastering Stabilization (S) and Tracking (T).
  • Phase 2 (Launch): Prioritize the "Founding 5" Beta clients to build your clinical confidence and data portfolio.
