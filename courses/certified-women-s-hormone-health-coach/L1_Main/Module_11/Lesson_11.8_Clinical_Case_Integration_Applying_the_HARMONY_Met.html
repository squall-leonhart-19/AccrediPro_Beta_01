Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "run more, eat less" often counterproductive for a 52-year-old client like Elena?

In perimenopause, high-intensity cardio and caloric restriction can spike cortisol. High cortisol triggers insulin resistance and signals the body to store visceral fat (the "meno-pot") for survival/hormonal backup.

2. What is the primary goal of the "Empathetic Pivot" in a coaching session?

To acknowledge and validate the client's emotional struggle (building trust) while simultaneously redirecting the conversation toward a tangible, science-based solution (establishing authority).
