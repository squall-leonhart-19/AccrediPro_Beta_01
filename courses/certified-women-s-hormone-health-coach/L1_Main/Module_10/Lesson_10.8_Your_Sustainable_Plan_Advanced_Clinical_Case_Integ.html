Check Your Understanding

1. A client has gone 11 months without a period and then experiences spotting. Is she post-menopausal?

No. Menopause is defined as 12 consecutive months. Any bleeding before the 12-month mark "resets the clock" and should be discussed with a medical professional to rule out other issues.

2. Why is resistance training prioritized in the "Second Spring" coaching model?

As estrogen declines, women lose bone density and muscle mass (sarcopenia) at an accelerated rate. Resistance training is the primary lifestyle intervention to maintain metabolic rate and skeletal integrity.
