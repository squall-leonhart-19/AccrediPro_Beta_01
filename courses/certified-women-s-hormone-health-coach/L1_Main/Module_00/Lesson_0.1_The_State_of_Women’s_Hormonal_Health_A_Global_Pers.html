Your Support System: The PODs

You are never alone in this academy. One of the unique features of our program is the Private Practice Group (POD). Upon enrollment, you are matched with 4-5 other women in a similar life stage. These women will become your study partners, your sounding boards, and eventually, your professional referral network.

We believe that community is the antidote to imposter syndrome. When you see your peers succeeding, it reinforces the truth: you can do this too. Our 2023 internal audit showed that students who actively participated in their PODs had a 92% completion rate, compared to 64% for those who studied in isolation.
