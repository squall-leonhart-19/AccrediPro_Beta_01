Check Your Understanding

1. Why is "eating clean" (like Diane's salad and coffee) often a root cause of hormone dysfunction in midlife?

It often lacks sufficient amino acids for neurotransmitter production, healthy fats for hormone synthesis, and enough calories to signal "safety" to the HPA axis, leading to chronic cortisol elevation and thyroid downregulation.

2. What is the most effective way to handle a client's fear of "eating more"?

Validate the fear, then pivot to the biological reality that their current restriction is causing the very symptoms (weight gain, fatigue) they are trying to avoid. Use "replenishment" language instead of "weight loss" language.

Key Takeaways

  • Replenishment is a Mindset: Shift the focus from what to remove to what to ADD for hormonal harmony.
