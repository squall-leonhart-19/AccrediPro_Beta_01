Case Study 1: The "Unexplained" Fatigue

Client: Elena, 42

Presenting Symptoms: Elena is a former teacher from Chicago. She presents with "unrelenting" fatigue, sudden weight gain (12 lbs in 6 months), and irregular cycles. Her doctor told her her labs are "normal."

Global Context: Elena lives in an urban environment with high exposure to xenoestrogens and works a high-stress corporate job after her career pivot.

Intervention: We focused on an anti-inflammatory protocol and a "home detox" to reduce endocrine disruptors. We also implemented a 10-minute morning sunlight routine to reset her circadian clock.

Outcome: Within 8 weeks, Elena reported a 60% increase in morning energy levels and her cycles regulated to a 28-day average.
