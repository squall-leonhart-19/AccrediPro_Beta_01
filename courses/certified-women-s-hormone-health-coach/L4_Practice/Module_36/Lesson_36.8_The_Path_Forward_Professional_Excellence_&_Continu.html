Check Your Understanding

1. What is the "Bridge Technique" in a discovery call?

The Bridge Technique involves explicitly connecting the client's specific symptoms (e.g., cortisol-driven belly fat) to the specific mechanisms of your program (e.g., adrenal nourishment phase), showing them exactly HOW your method solves their unique problem.

2. If a client says "I need to think about it," what is the most effective follow-up?

Ask an open-ended question to uncover the real concern: "I completely understand. Usually, when someone needs to think about it, it's either the time commitment or the financial investment. Which one is it for you?" This allows you to coach them through their fear.

Key Takeaways for Your Practice

  • Empathy is the Sales Engine: Your ability to reflect the client's pain back to them is more important than your price list.
  • The 30-Minute Rule: Spend 15 minutes listening and 15 minutes leading. Never pitch before you've diagnosed.
  • High-Ticket is High-Service: Charging $2,000+ allows you to give the client the deep support they actually need to see results.
  • Objections are Requests for Information: When a client objects, they are usually asking for more certainty that your program will work for THEM.
  • Consistency over Intensity: Reaching $10k/month is a result of consistent discovery calls, not a "lucky" viral post.
