Check Your Understanding

1. According to research, what is one of the primary clinical benefits of group-based health coaching compared to 1-on-1?

Group-based interventions often show higher adherence rates (up to 25% higher) due to social support, peer accountability, and the validation of shared experiences.

2. How should you handle a prospect who is worried that a group program won't be "personalized" enough for their complex issues?

Explain that the program covers the "Universal Pillars" that 90% of hormone issues share, while providing a private portal or "VIP upgrade" option for one-on-one questions and lab reviews.

Key Lesson Takeaways

  • Groups are Medicine: Don't apologize for the group format; sell it as a high-value feature of community and accountability.
  • The 80/20 Rule: 80% of hormone health foundations (diet, sleep, stress) are the same for everyone. Groups allow you to teach this once to many.
  • Confidence is Currency: When stating your group price, don't use "diminishers" like "It's just..." or "Only...". State the price and pause.
  • Scalability: A successful group coach can earn 3-5x more per hour than a 1-on-1 coach while helping more women.
