Check Your Understanding

1. A prospect asks: "Can you tell me if I have hypothyroidism?" What is the most compliant and professional response?

"I cannot provide a medical diagnosis. However, we can look at your symptoms and lifestyle markers, and I can help you prepare a list of specific thyroid tests to request from your doctor so you can get the answers you need."

2. Why is it important to state your disclaimer at the beginning of the call rather than the end?

Stating it early builds professional trust, establishes clear boundaries, and prevents the prospect from expecting a medical 'prescription' at the end of the call, which reduces friction during the pricing presentation.

Key Takeaways

  • Compliance = Confidence: A clear scope of practice protects you legally and positions you as a high-level professional.
  • Sell the Result, Not the Hours: Clients pay for the resolution of their brain fog and fatigue, not for 12 hours of your time.
