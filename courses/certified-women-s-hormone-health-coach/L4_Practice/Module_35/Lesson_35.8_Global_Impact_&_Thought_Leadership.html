Check Your Understanding

1. Why is the "Methodology Bridge" (Phase 3) critical for scaling?

It shifts the conversation from "selling sessions" to "selling a proven system." This builds authority and justifies high-ticket pricing by showing the client a clear path from their current pain to their desired outcome.

2. What is the most effective way to handle the "It's too expensive" objection?

By reframing the cost as an "investment vs. expense." Compare the cost of the program to the "Cost of Inaction" (e.g., lost work productivity, expensive ineffective supplements, or long-term health decline).

Key Takeaways

  • Structure = Success: Use a 30-minute timed script to maintain professional authority.
  • Identify the Gap: Your job is to show the client the distance between where they are and where they want to be.
  • Value over Volume: Focus on enrolling 5 high-value clients rather than 20 low-cost sessions to prevent burnout.
  • Confidence is Currency: State your price clearly and then stop talking. Let the client process the value.
