The 30-Minute Discovery Call Script

This structure is designed to move the client from Pain to Possibility.

Phase 1: Rapport & Permission (0-5 min)

YOU:
"Hi Linda! I've been looking forward to this. I've read your intake form, and I can see you've been dealing with a lot. Before we dive in, my goal today is to understand exactly what‚Äôs going on and see if I‚Äôm the right person to help. If I am, I'll tell you how that works. If not, I'll point you to someone who is. Phase 2: The Deep Dive (5-15 min)

YOU:
"You mentioned the brain fog is affecting your work. Can you give me a specific example of how that felt this week?" (Wait for the emotional answer). "And if we don't get this sorted, where do you see your health in 12 months?"

Phase 3: The Bridge (15-25 min)

YOU:
"Linda, what you‚Äôre experiencing isn't 'just aging.' It's a metabolic and hormonal shift that your current lifestyle isn't supporting. In my 'Hormone Harmony' program, we don't guess; we look at the root causes. We'll spend 12 weeks recalibrating your cortisol and insulin sensitivity so you can get your brain and your body back. Phase 4: The Invitation (25-30 min)

YOU:
"Based on our talk, I am 100% confident I can help you. The investment for the 3-month partnership is $1,997. We can get your first session booked for Tuesday. Would you like to get started?"

Handling the "Big Three" Objections

Objections are not "No's." They are requests for more information or safety.

"I need to talk to my husband/partner."
"I completely respect that. I want you to have full support. When you talk to him, what do you think his main concern will be? Is it the cost, or is it that he's seen you try things before that didn't work?" (This uncovers the real fear).
"It's just not the right time. I have a big project at work."
"I hear you. But let me ask‚Äîwill that project be easier or harder to finish with the brain fog and exhaustion you're feeling right now? There is never a 'perfect' time to heal, but there is a time when the cost of waiting becomes too high."
"That's more than I expected to spend."
"I understand. It is an investment. If we were sitting here in 3 months and you had your energy back, you were sleeping through the night, and you'd lost that 15lbs... what would that be worth to you?"

Check Your Understanding

1. What is the primary goal of Phase 2 (The Deep Dive) in the discovery call?

The goal is to help the client articulate "The Gap"‚Äîwhere they are now vs. where they want to be‚Äîand to uncover the emotional cost of staying the same.

2. If a client says "I've tried everything and nothing works," how should you respond?

Acknowledge their frustration first (empathy). Then, explain why your approach is different (e.g., "Most programs focus on calories; we focus on hormonal signaling"). This builds authority and hope.

Key Takeaways

  • Listen More, Talk Less: Aim for the client to speak 70% of the time during a discovery call.
  • Focus on Outcomes: Clients don't buy "12 sessions"; they buy "sleeping through the night" and "feeling like themselves again."
  • Confidence is Currency: Stating your price without stuttering or apologizing is essential for establishing professional authority.
  • Reframing Sales: View every call as an opportunity to help a woman navigate a difficult life transition.
  • Consistency is Key: Income growth follows the "Rule of 5"‚Äîtalk to 5 new prospects a week to maintain a full practice.
