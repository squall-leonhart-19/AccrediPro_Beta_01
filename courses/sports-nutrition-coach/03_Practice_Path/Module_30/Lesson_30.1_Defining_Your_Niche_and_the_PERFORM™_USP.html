1. The Rationale for Niching: Beyond Generalism

In the burgeoning field of sports nutrition, the "generalist trap" is a common pitfall for new practitioners. Attempting to serve "anyone with a fitness goal" often leads to marketing dilution and price commoditization. A 2022 survey of independent health consultants revealed that those with a defined niche commanded fees 38% higher than generalists and reported 22% higher client retention rates.

Niching is not about limiting your potential; it is about deepening your expertise. By focusing on a specific population, you master the unique physiological demands, psychological stressors, and cultural nuances of that group. This depth of knowledge allows for more precise application of the PERFORMâ„¢ methodology, particularly in the "P" (Periodization) and "R" (Results Tracking) phases.
