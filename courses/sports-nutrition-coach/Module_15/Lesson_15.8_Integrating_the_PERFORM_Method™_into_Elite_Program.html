4. Scaling from Individual to Team

Transitioning from 1-on-1 coaching to team-based programming requires a shift in logistics. You cannot individually meal plan for 50 athletes. Instead, you must implement Systemic Nutritional Architecture:

  1. Traffic Light Systems: Color-coding cafeteria or travel meals based on training intensity (Green = Low Carb/High Fiber; Red = High Carb/Performance).
  2. Supplement Dispensaries: Creating "Recovery Bars" where the "R" and "O" (Optimize Hydration) are standardized post-practice.
  3. Group Education: Monthly "Masterclasses" on "M" (Mental Edge) to build a team culture of performance fueling.
