Legal & Professional Foundations

Professionalism includes protection. You must operate within your Scope of Practice and ensure your clients understand the nature of the coaching relationship. A 2023 analysis of health coaching litigation showed that 85% of disputes could have been avoided with a clear, signed client agreement at the start of the relationship.

The Accelerator provides:
1. Coaching Agreement: Outlines payments, cancellations, and expectations.
2. Liability Waiver: Standard protection for nutrition recommendations.
3. Privacy Policy Template: For handling sensitive health data.

Your Implementation Roadmap

Do not try to use every resource at once. Follow this 3-step sequence to launch effectively:

Phase 1: The Foundation (Days 1-3)

Download the Client Agreement and Intake Forms. Add your logo and contact information. These are your "Non-Negotiables."

Phase 2: The Outreach (Days 4-10)

Select 3 Social Media templates and the "Founding Member" script. Post these to announce your certification and availability.

Phase 3: The Delivery (Ongoing)

As clients sign up, use the F.U.E.L.E.D. Tracking Spreadsheets to manage their data and the Email Scripts to keep them engaged between sessions.
