The P.R.I.M.E. Protocolâ„¢ Methodology

Throughout this course, you will master our proprietary methodology. This is the framework you will use to assess every client and create high-value protocols.

P - Physiological Profiling

Comprehensive assessment of biological markers, medical history, and genetic predispositions to identify barriers before they manifest.

R - Rhythmic Regulation

Restoring natural biological rhythms, including the menstrual cycle and circadian patterns, to optimize hormonal signaling.

I - Inflammatory Inhibition

Addressing systemic and localized inflammation to improve egg quality and create a receptive immunological environment for implantation.

M - Metabolic & Microbiome Mapping

Evaluating insulin sensitivity and the health of the gut and vaginal microbiomes to provide the critical resources for a healthy pregnancy.

E - Environmental & Emotional Equilibrium

Mitigating toxic endocrine disruptors and building emotional resilience to protect the epigenetic health of the future child.
