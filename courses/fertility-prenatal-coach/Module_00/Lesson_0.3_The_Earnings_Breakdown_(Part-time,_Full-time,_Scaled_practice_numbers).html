Scaling Beyond One-on-One

The "Scaled Practice" is the final evolution of the Fertility Coach. This is where you decouple your time from your income. By utilizing Environmental & Emotional Equilibrium (the 'E' in P.R.I.M.E.) in a digital format, you can serve hundreds of clients simultaneously.

A 2023 industry report on digital health coaching (n=1,200 practitioners) found that those with a "hybrid" model (online course + limited group support) had 42% higher profit margins than those doing pure 1-on-1 work. Scaling allows you to lower the entry price for clients who cannot afford high-ticket coaching, thereby increasing your impact while maintaining a high income.
