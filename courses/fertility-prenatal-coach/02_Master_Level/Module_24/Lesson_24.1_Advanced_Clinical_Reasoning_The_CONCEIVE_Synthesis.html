2. Identifying the "Lead Domino"

The "Lead Domino" is the primary physiological or psychological bottleneck that, when addressed, creates a cascade of improvement across all other pillars. In complex cases, you cannot fix everything at once. A Master Practitioner must decide where to strike first.

The Hierarchical Intervention Model

While every case is bio-individual, clinical data suggests a general hierarchy for prioritization:

  1. Safety & Drainage: Ensuring the body can actually eliminate toxins (Phase III Detoxification) before pushing metabolic or hormonal shifts.
  2. Metabolic Stability: Blood sugar regulation is the foundation of the HPO axis. If insulin is high, hormone optimization (O) will fail.
  3. The Nervous System: If the client is in a chronic "Sympathetic Dominance" (V & E pillars), the body will prioritize survival over reproduction, regardless of how many supplements they take.
