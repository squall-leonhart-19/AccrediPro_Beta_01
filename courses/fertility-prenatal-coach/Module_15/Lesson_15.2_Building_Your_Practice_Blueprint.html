Value-Based Pricing

Setting prices based primarily on the perceived or estimated value of the outcome to the customer rather than the cost of the service or hourly rates.

Scope of Practice

The specific procedures, actions, and processes that a healthcare or wellness professional is permitted to undertake in keeping with the terms of their professional certification.

Client Lifetime Value (CLV)

The total revenue a business can reasonably expect from a single client account throughout the professional relationship.

Pricing for High-Impact Results

The biggest mistake new practitioners make is trading hours for dollars. If you charge $100 per hour, you are capped by your time. If you charge $3,000 for a 12-week P.R.I.M.E. Transformation, you are rewarded for the result.

The Hourly Trap

"I charge $125 per session. If the client feels better after two weeks, they stop coming. I am constantly hunting for new leads. My income is volatile and my impact is shallow."

The Signature Blueprint

"I offer a $4,500 'Conception Catalyst' 4-month program. Clients are committed. I have predictable cash flow and can focus 100% on their success. My income is $15k/mo with only 4 clients."

The Client Acquisition Funnel

You do not need 10,000 followers to have a $20k/month practice. You need a targeted funnel. A 2022 study by the International Coaching Federation (ICF) found that 72% of high-ticket coaching sales happen through a "Discovery Call" or "Consultation."

Your blueprint for acquisition:

  • The Hook: A specific lead magnet (e.g., "The 7-Day Anti-Inflammatory Fertility Meal Plan").
  • The Nurture: 3-5 automated emails demonstrating your expertise in the P.R.I.M.E. pillars.
  • The Call: A 20-minute "Fertility Audit" where you identify their barriers and offer your signature program as the solution.

Operational Systems & Automation

To scale to $30,000/month, you must automate the "admin" so you can focus on the "coaching." Using a Practice Management Platform (like Practice Better, BetterPT, or Kajabi) is essential. A 2023 survey of health practitioners found that those using automation saved an average of 11 hours per week on administrative tasks.

Essential Tech Stack

  • Onboarding: Automated contract signing and initial payment.
  • Scheduling: Self-service booking to eliminate email tag.
  • Forms: Digital intake forms that automatically populate client charts.
  • Communication: A secure portal for client messaging (avoiding WhatsApp/SMS for boundaries).

Legal & Ethical Foundations

As a Fertility & Prenatal Coach, you are a wellness educator, not a medical doctor (unless you hold those credentials). Protecting your practice is non-negotiable. Statistics show that 1 in 15 health practitioners will face a "Scope of Practice" inquiry or dispute during their career.

Compliance Checklist

1. Client Agreement: Clearly state that you do not diagnose or treat disease.
2. Professional Liability Insurance: Ensure coverage for "Wellness Coaching" specifically.
3. HIPAA/GDPR: Use encrypted platforms for storing client health data.
4. Medical Disclaimer: Every email and PDF must contain a disclaimer stating your info is for educational purposes only.
