Defining Your Scope: The Ethical Boundary

As a coach, your role is to educate, empower, and investigate. You do not diagnose or treat medical conditions. Understanding this boundary is not a limitation; it is your professional protection. You are a specialist in Health Optimization. When you find a medical red flag (e.g., a suspicious ultrasound finding), your role is to facilitate the referral to an RE (Reproductive Endocrinologist), becoming a vital part of the client's medical team.
