Validating Your Niche

Before fully committing, you must validate that your chosen niche has "willingness to pay." Use the 3-V Validation Method:

  1. Volume: Are there enough people (e.g., Facebook groups, forums, subreddits) talking about this specific problem?
  2. Value: Is the problem "painful" enough that they are seeking professional help rather than just reading a blog post?
  3. Velocity: How quickly are people seeking solutions once the problem is identified? (In fertility, velocity is usually high due to the biological clock).
