Pricing is often the biggest hurdle for new coaches. However, when working with fertility, you must consider the Return on Investment (ROI). If your coaching helps a client avoid just one failed IVF cycle, you have saved them $20,000 and months of heartbreak. Your $3,500 package is, in fact, a bargain.

The Sales Psychology of Fertility

Selling high-ticket fertility services is not about "convincing"; it is about empathetic leadership. Research shows that fertility patients experience stress levels equivalent to cancer patients (Domar et al., 1993). Your sales process must be a safe space.

Focus your marketing on the Cost of Inaction. Use data-driven claims: "A 2021 study found that targeted nutritional and lifestyle interventions (similar to our P.R.I.M.E. pillars) can improve clinical pregnancy rates in ART cycles by up to 40%." When the client realizes that your service is an investment in their future child's epigenetics, the price becomes secondary.
