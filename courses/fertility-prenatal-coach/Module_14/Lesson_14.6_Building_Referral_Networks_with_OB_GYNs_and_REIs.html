There is virtually no time left for the deep work of Rhythmic Regulation or Environmental Equilibrium.

Professional Outreach Protocol

Cold calling is rarely effective in the medical world. Instead, follow this 4-step "Clinical Authority Sequence":

Step 1: The Patient-Led Entry

The easiest way to get a doctor's attention is through a shared client. Ask your current client for permission to send a "Co-Management Letter" to their OB/GYN. This introduces you as a professional already working on the case.

Step 2: The Evidence-Based Packet

Mail (yes, physical mail) a professional folder containing:

  • Your Professional Bio & AccrediPro Credentials
  • A 1-page summary of The P.R.I.M.E. Protocol‚Ñ¢
  • 2-3 brief Case Studies with data-driven outcomes
  • Sample "Progress Report" so they see how you communicate

Step 3: The "Value-First" Meeting

Request a 15-minute "Meet and Greet" or offer to bring healthy lunch for the staff (the "Lunch & Learn"). Your goal is not to sell, but to ask: "What is the one thing you wish your patients did better at home that they currently struggle with?"

Ethical Boundaries & Scope

To keep the trust of an MD, you must stay strictly within your Scope of Practice. Never "prescribe" or "diagnose." If a client‚Äôs labs look concerning, your role is to say: "These markers are outside the optimal range for our protocol; I recommend you discuss the possibility of [Condition] with Dr. Smith."

A 2021 study on collaborative care models (n=850) found that when non-clinical practitioners respected medical boundaries, physician trust increased by 74% over a 12-month period. This trust is the foundation of a $100k+ practice built on referrals.

Check Your Understanding

1

What is the "Implementation Gap" and why is it a selling point for your coaching services?

The implementation gap is the space between a doctor's medical advice (e.g., "reduce stress") and the patient's ability to actually change their daily habits. Coaches solve this by providing the time, accountability, and specific P.R.I.M.E. tools that doctors lack the time to implement.
2

True or False: The best way to get a referral from an REI is to explain your passion for helping women get pregnant.

False. REIs are data-driven. The best way is to present evidence-based outcomes (like improved egg quality or protocol adherence) and demonstrate how you improve their clinical success rates.

Key Takeaways

  • Efficiency is King: Doctors refer to people who make their jobs easier, not more complicated.
  • Speak the Language: Use clinical terms like "adherence," "biomarkers," and "outcomes" rather than "vibes" or "healing journeys."
  • The 90-Day Rule: Position your work as the "pre-medical" preparation phase to optimize the success of their interventions.
  • Documentation: "Closing the loop" with professional progress reports is the #1 way to ensure repeat referrals.
  • P.R.I.M.E. Authority: Use your certification methodology as proof of a standardized, professional approach to care.
