3. Email Nurture & Emotional Cycles

Fertility coaching is unique because the prospect's emotional state fluctuates based on their biological cycle. An advanced funnel uses Temporal Email Mapping. You should not send the same aggressive "buy now" emails during the "Two-Week Wait" (TWW) as you would during the follicular phase.

Your automation sequence should be divided into three tiers:

  1. The Indoctrination Sequence (Days 1-5): Establishing the science behind your method.
  2. The Empathy Sequence (Ongoing): Addressing the grief of a negative test (referencing Module 8: Embrace Journey).
  3. The Urgency Sequence (Cyclical): Offering "Cycle Start" intensives or limited-time audits.

4. Lead Scoring & Sensitive CRM Protocols

In the prenatal space, data privacy is paramount. Your CRM (Customer Relationship Management) system must not only track opens and clicks but also Lead Scoring based on "Fertility Readiness." A lead scoring model might look like this:

  • Download a PDF: +5 points
  • Watch 50% of Masterclass: +20 points
  • Visit "Investment" page twice: +15 points
  • Click on "PCOS Support" link: Tag as "PCOS Segment"

When a lead reaches 50 points, an automated "Personal Invitation" email is triggered. This ensures you are only spending your high-energy "Discovery Call" time with prospects who are pre-educated and highly motivated.

Key Takeaways

  • Personalization Wins: Use quizzes and audits to segment your audience by their specific fertility challenges.
  • Education as Sales: Use the "Masterclass" format to bridge the gap between "free information" and "high-ticket transformation."
