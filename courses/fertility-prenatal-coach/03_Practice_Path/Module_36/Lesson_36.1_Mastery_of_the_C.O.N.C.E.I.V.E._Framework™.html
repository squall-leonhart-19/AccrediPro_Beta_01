5. Identifying Bottlenecks in "Embrace Journey"

The final pillar, "Embrace Journey," is often where the most significant bottlenecks reside. Even with perfect nutrition and clean environments, a client stuck in Biological Urgency Mode (a chronic state of sympathetic dominance) will have reduced uterine blood flow and impaired implantation receptivity.

Master Practitioners look for these subtle bottlenecks:

  • The "Two-Week Wait" Crisis: Does the client's anxiety spike so severely that it disrupts the progesterone-driven luteal phase?
  • Identity Loss: Has the client stopped "living" in favor of "tracking"? This disconnect from the Intimacy pillar often causes a secondary hormonal crash.
  • Cognitive Dissonance: When a client does the physical work but subconsciously believes their body is "broken."
