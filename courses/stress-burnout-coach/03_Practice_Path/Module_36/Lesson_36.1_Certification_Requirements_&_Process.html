3. Case Study Portfolio Standards

You are required to submit three (3) distinct case studies. These are not merely summaries; they are deep-dive analytical papers that prove your clinical reasoning. One of these must be a Complex Case involving significant nervous system dysregulation or professional burnout.

Example Case Study Structure: "The High-Performance Burnout"

Client: "Elena," 42, Corporate Attorney.

Presentation: Stage 9 Burnout (Inner Emptiness), HRV consistently below 30ms, chronic insomnia, and high cynicism.

Intervention: Use of the RESTORE Protocol. Specifically, stabilizing the dorsal vagal response before attempting cognitive restructuring (Transform phase).

Outcome: 45% increase in baseline HRV over 12 weeks; return to work with established boundaries (Empower phase).

Note: For certification, this would require 1,500 words of analysis including "Why this intervention?" and "What would I do differently next time?"

4. The Practical Coaching Assessment

The final hurdle is the Live Practical Assessment. This is a 45-minute proctored session (conducted via Zoom) where you will coach a "client" (an AccrediPro examiner or a peer under observation) through a specific challenge.

Assessment Components:

  • Active Listening (10 mins): Demonstrating presence and mirroring.
  • The "Pivot" (15 mins): Moving from a client's "story" to a RESTORE-based intervention.
  • Technical Instruction (10 mins): Teaching a stabilization technique (e.g., Box Breathing or 4-7-8) with physiological rationale.
  • Closing & Goal Setting (10 mins): Ensuring the client has a tangible, empowered next step.

5. Grading Rubric & Evaluation

Your submissions are graded on a 4-point scale across five domains. A total score of 16/20 (80%) is required for immediate certification.

6. Submission & Appeals Process

Once you have completed all modules and gathered your documentation, you will access the Certification Portal. You should expect a review period of 14â€“21 business days as each portfolio is manually reviewed by our board of master practitioners.

What if I don't pass?

AccrediPro Academy views the certification process as a learning tool, not just a gatekeeper. If your portfolio or practical assessment falls below the 80% threshold, you will receive:

  • Detailed Feedback: Specific notes on which domains need improvement.
  • Remediation Plan: Suggested modules for review or additional coaching hours to focus on.
  • One Free Re-submission: You have 60 days to re-submit or re-take the practical assessment at no additional cost.

50 documented coaching hours are required, and the minimum passing grade is 80% (or 16/20 on the rubric).

What is the "Complex Case" requirement in the Case Study Portfolio?

One of the three required case studies must involve a client with significant nervous system dysregulation or advanced-stage burnout, requiring sophisticated application of the RESTORE Protocol.

Key Takeaways

  • Rigorous Standards: Certification is based on demonstrated mastery, not just attendance.
  • Evidence-Based: Your case studies must link interventions to the physiological and psychological mechanisms discussed in Modules 1-35.
