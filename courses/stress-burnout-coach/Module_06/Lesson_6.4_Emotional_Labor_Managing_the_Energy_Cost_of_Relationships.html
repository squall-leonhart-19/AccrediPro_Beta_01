Relational Energy Audits

To help clients manage their "Stress Shield," we must quantify their interpersonal interactions. A Relational Energy Audit categorizes people in the clientâ€™s life into three categories:

1. Energy Batteries (Renewers)

Relationships that are reciprocal. After an interaction, the client feels energized or calm. These require low emotional labor.

2. Energy Neutrals (Functional)

Transactional relationships (e.g., the barista, the dry cleaner). Low cost, low reward.

3. Energy Vampires (Depleters)

Relationships characterized by high emotional labor, one-sided venting, or constant crisis. These require high Expressive Suppression.

A 2022 study found that just one high-conflict relationship can negate the benefits of five high-quality relationships in terms of systemic inflammation (IL-6 levels). Coaches must help clients "prune" or "boundary" these depleting connections to allow the HPA-axis to downregulate.

The Compassion-Boundaries Paradox

Many clients in burnout are "highly sensitive" or "empathic." They fear that setting boundaries is "unkind." As an AccrediPro Coach, you must teach the difference between Empathy and Compassion.

Compassion (feeling *for* someone with a desire to help) activates the reward centers (ventral striatum).
