2. The Feedback Trifecta: Specificity, Balance, and Timeliness

To move beyond vague encouragement, supervisors must utilize the "Trifecta" approach. This ensures that the guidance is actionable and grounded in the RESTORE Protocolâ„¢ framework.

What is the "Parallel Process" in supervision feedback?

Parallel Process refers to the phenomenon where the dynamics between the coach and the client are "mirrored" in the relationship between the supervisor and the coach. For example, if a client is being resistant to the coach, the coach may subconsciously act resistant toward the supervisor's feedback.
