Identifying Your High-Value Niche

To build a blueprint, you must know who you are building it for. The "Allostatic Load" (A in ASCEND) of a stay-at-home parent is physiologically different from that of a Silicon Valley CEO, even if the symptoms of burnout look similar. Your niche should be at the intersection of your personal experience, professional background, and market demand.

Pro Tip: The "Niche Down" Formula

I help [SPECIFIC POPULATION] who are struggling with [SPECIFIC BURNOUT SYMPTOM] to achieve [DESIRED OUTCOME] using the [ASCEND METHODOLOGY] without [COMMON PAIN POINT].

The ASCEND Service Architecture

Your practice blueprint must structure the 6 pillars of the ASCEND Methodology into a logical client journey. We recommend a 12-week "Resilience Intensive" as your flagship offering. This provides enough time for physiological regulation (Somatic Regulation) and cognitive restructuring.

The "Hourly" Trap

Selling sessions one-by-one ($150/hr). Focuses on "fixing" immediate problems. Low client commitment. High practitioner burnout due to constant marketing.

The ASCEND Blueprint

Selling a 90-day transformation ($3k - $7k). Focuses on root-cause recovery. High client buy-in. Scalable income with fewer, higher-quality clients.

Pricing & Financial Modeling

Burnout is expensive. A 2022 study by Deloitte found that burnout costs employers roughly ¬£45 billion annually in the UK alone. When pricing your services, you are not charging for your time; you are charging for the restoration of their earning capacity, health, and relationships.

We recommend a three-tier model for your blueprint:

  1. The Intensive (Tier 1): A 90-day high-touch program including labs, weekly coaching, and somatic tools. ($3,500 - $7,500).
