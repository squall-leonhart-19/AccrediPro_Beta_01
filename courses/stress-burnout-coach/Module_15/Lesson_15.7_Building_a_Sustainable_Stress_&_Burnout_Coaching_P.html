4. Operational Systems & Automation

Administrative tasks are the "silent burners" of a coaching practice. To remain sustainable, you must implement a tech stack that handles the low-value tasks, allowing you to stay in your "Zone of Genius" (coaching). A 2023 industry report found that coaches using automated scheduling and billing systems saved an average of 9 hours per week.

Critical systems for a Stress & Burnout practice include:

  • CRM (Customer Relationship Management): Tracking lead journeys from first contact to graduation.
  • HIPAA-Compliant EHR (Electronic Health Record): Essential for protecting sensitive physiological data (e.g., cortisol labs or HRV logs).
  • Automated Onboarding: A sequence that sends the RESTORE Assessment, contract, and first invoice immediately upon signing.
