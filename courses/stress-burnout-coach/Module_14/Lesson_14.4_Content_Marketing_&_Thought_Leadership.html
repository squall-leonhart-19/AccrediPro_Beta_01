Platform-Specific Mastery

Not all platforms are created equal for burnout coaches. For high-ticket practitioners, the hierarchy of impact usually follows this order:

  1. LinkedIn: The "Professional Living Room." Ideal for B2B, corporate wellness, and high-level executive clients. Focus on "The Future of Work" and "Sustainable Performance."
  2. Email Newsletter: The "Inner Circle." This is where you nurture long-term trust. Share deep-dive case studies and personal reflections.
  3. Podcasting: The "Auditory Intimacy." Being a guest on podcasts allows you to borrow authority from established hosts and demonstrate your communication skills.
  4. Instagram/TikTok: The "Visual Hook." Best for somatic exercises (breathwork demos) and quick "myth-busting" videos.

The Content Funnel Architecture

Your content should move a stranger through a specific psychological journey. We call this the Authority Funnel:

1. Awareness (Top of Funnel): Short, punchy insights that stop the scroll.
Goal: Get the click or the follow.
Metric: Reach/Shares.

2. Education (Middle of Funnel): Deep-dive articles, newsletters, or videos that explain the "ASCEND" approach.
Goal: Establish the "Expert's Paradox" (showing them why their previous attempts at recovery failed).
Metric: Read time/Email opens.

3. Conversion (Bottom of Funnel): Case studies, testimonials, and direct invitations to a "Resilience Audit" or "Discovery Call."
Goal: Book a call.
Metric: Booked appointments.

Ethical & Legal Considerations

As a coach, you must maintain clear boundaries in your content marketing. It is vital to distinguish between education and medical advice. Always include a disclaimer on your website and in your email footers stating that your content is for educational purposes and does not replace the advice of a medical professional.

Furthermore, ethical thought leadership requires Intellectual Honesty. If a study you are citing has limitations (e.g., small sample size), mentioning those limitations actually increases your credibility with sophisticated clients. It shows you are a critical thinker, not just a marketer.
