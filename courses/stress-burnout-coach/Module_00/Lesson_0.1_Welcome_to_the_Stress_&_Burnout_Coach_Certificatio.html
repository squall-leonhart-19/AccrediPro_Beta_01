Critical Scope Boundary

As a coach, you do not diagnose or treat medical conditions. If a client expresses active suicidal ideation or exhibits symptoms of severe clinical disorders (e.g., Bipolar I, Schizophrenia), you must have a referral network of licensed mental health professionals ready.
