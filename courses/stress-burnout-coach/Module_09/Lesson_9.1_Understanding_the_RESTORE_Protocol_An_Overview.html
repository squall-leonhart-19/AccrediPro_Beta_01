Deciphering the RESTORE Acronym

The RESTORE Protocolâ„¢ is built upon a simple yet powerful acronym, with each letter representing a critical step in the process of nervous system regulation and stress recovery:

  • R - Recognize
  • E - Evaluate
  • S - Strategize
  • T - Tune
  • O - Optimize
  • R - Reintegrate
  • E - Empower

Let's briefly break down each component:

  1. Recognize: This initial stage involves cultivating self-awareness and identifying the specific stressors and triggers that contribute to dysregulation.
  2. Evaluate: During this phase, we assess the impact of stress on various aspects of the client's life, including physical health, emotional well-being, relationships, and performance.
  3. Strategize: Based on the evaluation, we develop personalized strategies and interventions to address the identified stressors and promote nervous system regulation.
  4. Tune: This stage involves actively engaging in practices that help regulate the nervous system, such as breathwork, mindfulness, and somatic experiencing techniques.
  5. Optimize: Here, we focus on optimizing lifestyle factors, such as sleep, nutrition, and movement, to support overall well-being and resilience.
  6. Reintegrate: This crucial phase involves reintegrating healthy habits and coping mechanisms into daily life, ensuring sustainable change.
  7. Empower: The final stage focuses on empowering clients to take ownership of their well-being and develop the skills and resources to navigate future challenges with confidence.
