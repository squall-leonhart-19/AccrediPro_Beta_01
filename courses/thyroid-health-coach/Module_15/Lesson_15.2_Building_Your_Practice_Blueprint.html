The THRIVE Client Journey

A professional practice requires a repeatable system. Your clients should feel held and supported through a structured 4-phase journey:

  1. Phase 1: The Deep Dive (Weeks 1-2): Targeted Biomarker Assessment and detailed health history. We identify the "H" (Hormonal Synergy) and "R" (Root Cause) issues.
  2. Phase 2: The Foundation (Weeks 3-6): Implementing "I" (Inflammation Modulation) and "V" (Vital Micronutrients). This is where clients see the fastest reduction in symptoms like bloating and brain fog.
  3. Phase 3: The Integration (Weeks 7-10): Focusing on "H" (Axis Balance) and "E" (Environmental Detox). We refine the protocol based on initial feedback and bio-individual responses.
  4. Phase 4: The Graduation (Weeks 11-12): Long-term maintenance and empowerment. We ensure the client has the tools to sustain their thyroid health independently.
