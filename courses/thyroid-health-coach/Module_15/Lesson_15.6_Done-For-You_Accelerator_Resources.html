Legal & Client Agreements

Operating without a solid legal foundation is a risk to your career. The DFY Accelerator includes Client Agreement Templates drafted to protect both you and the client. Key sections included in these templates are:

1. Scope of Practice: Clearly stating you are a coach/practitioner, not a medical doctor, to ensure compliance with health regulations.

2. Refund & Cancellation Policies: Protecting your time and income from last-minute changes.

3. Communication Boundaries: Setting expectations for between-session support (e.g., email-only, 24-hour response time).
