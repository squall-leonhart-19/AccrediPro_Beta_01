4. Legal & Liability: Navigating the Boundaries

One of the most significant risks in group coaching is the "Medical Advice" trap. As a coach, you are facilitating a process, not diagnosing or treating. In a group setting, this boundary becomes blurred when members ask specific questions about their medication dosages (e.g., Levothyroxine vs. NDT).

L4 Legal Safeguards

  • Group Disclaimers: Every session must begin with a verbal and visual disclaimer that the information is for educational purposes only.
  • Privacy (HIPAA/GDPR): Use secure platforms like Practice Better or Healthie. Ensure members sign a "Group Confidentiality Agreement" to protect each other's health data.
  • Scope of Practice: Never adjust medication. Always refer back to the prescribing physician while providing the questions the client should ask their doctor.
