Communication Without Chaos

Clients will want to contact you between visits. Without clear systems, you'll drown in emails and texts. Setting communication boundaries protects your time while still providing excellent care.

Communication Policy

Define Your Channels

  • One primary channel: email, practice portal, or secure messaging
  • NOT personal cell, text, or social media DMs
  • State clearly in onboarding: "Contact me through X"

Set Response Time Expectations

  • "I respond to messages within 24-48 business hours"
  • "For urgent issues, contact X or go to urgent care"
  • Under-promise, over-deliver (if you say 48h, respond in 24)

Between-Visit Support Models

  • Limited messaging: Included in package, brief questions only
  • Office hours: Weekly Q&A time for quick questions
  • Mini-consults: 15-min paid calls for concerns between visits
