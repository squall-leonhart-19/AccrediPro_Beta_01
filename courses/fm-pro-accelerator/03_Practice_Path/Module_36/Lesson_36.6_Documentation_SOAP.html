If You Didn't Write It, It Didn't Happen

Good documentation protects you legally, ensures continuity of care, and demonstrates professional competence. SOAP notes are the industry standard for health practitioner documentation.

The SOAP Format

S = Subjective

  • What the client reports: symptoms, concerns, experiences
  • "Patient reports fatigue improved 40% since last visit"
  • Include relevant quotes when appropriate

O = Objective

  • Observable/measurable data: labs, vitals, physical findings
  • "TSH 2.1 (previously 4.2), weight 165 lbs (previously 172)"
  • Include any test results or tracking data

A = Assessment

  • Your clinical interpretation and analysis
  • "Thyroid function improving with current protocol. Metabolic pattern resolving."
  • Document patterns identified and progress toward goals

P = Plan

  • What you're recommending and next steps
  • "Continue current supplements, add magnesium 400mg. Retest thyroid in 8 weeks."
  • Include follow-up timing
