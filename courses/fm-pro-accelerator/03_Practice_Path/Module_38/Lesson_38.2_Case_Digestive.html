Case: Michael, 35M - Chronic GI Issues

Chief Complaint: "Bloating after every meal. I don't know what I can eat anymore."

History: Bloating, gas, alternating constipation/diarrhea for 5 years. Progressively worse. Multiple food sensitivities developed. Previous antibiotic use for acne as teenager.

Assessment & Testing

Pattern Recognition

  • Post-infectious IBS/SIBO pattern (food poisoning trigger)
  • Antibiotic history ‚Üí dysbiosis risk
  • Progressive food sensitivities ‚Üí leaky gut likely

Testing Strategy

  • SIBO breath test (hydrogen/methane)
  • Comprehensive stool analysis
  • Consider: anti-vinculin/anti-CdtB antibodies (post-infectious autoimmunity)

Treatment Plan (5R Framework)

Phase 1: Remove (Weeks 1-6)

  • SIBO antimicrobial protocol (if positive)
  • Elimination diet (remove reactive foods temporarily)
  • Biofilm disruptors if needed

Phase 2: Replace & Repair (Weeks 7-12)

  • Digestive enzymes, HCl if needed
  • Gut healing: L-glutamine, zinc carnosine, bone broth
  • Prokinetic to prevent SIBO recurrence

Phase 3: Reinoculate & Rebalance (Ongoing)

  • Gradual probiotic introduction
  • Slow food reintroduction
  • Stress management (gut-brain axis)
