Case: Robert, 52M - Metabolic Syndrome

Chief Complaint: "My doctor says I'm prediabetic. I don't want to go on medication."

History: Weight gain over 10 years. Central obesity (BMI 32). Energy crashes. High blood pressure 138/88. Doctor recommending metformin.

Labs: Fasting glucose 112, HbA1c 5.9%, fasting insulin 24, triglycerides 245, HDL 38, LDL 142.

Assessment

Metabolic Pattern Analysis

  • Significant insulin resistance (fasting insulin 24)
  • Classic metabolic syndrome (central obesity, dyslipidemia, elevated glucose)
  • High triglyceride/HDL ratio = cardiovascular risk
  • Reversible at this stage with aggressive lifestyle

Treatment Plan

Dietary Intervention (Critical)

  • Low-carb/Mediterranean hybrid (<50g net carbs initially)
  • Eliminate sugar, refined carbs, seed oils
  • Time-restricted eating (16:8)
  • Protein at every meal (30g+)

Exercise Protocol

  • Resistance training 3x/week (builds insulin-sensitive muscle)
  • Daily walking 30+ minutes (especially after meals)
  • Start gradual‚Äîconsistency over intensity

Supplement Support

  • Berberine 500mg 2x/day (equivalent to metformin in studies)
  • Chromium 200-400mcg
  • Alpha-lipoic acid 300-600mg
  • Omega-3 3-4g (triglyceride reduction)
  • Magnesium 400mg
