Case: Lisa, 45F - Hashimoto's Thyroiditis

Chief Complaint: "I'm on thyroid medication but still feel terrible. My antibodies are sky high."

History: Hashimoto's diagnosed 5 years ago. On levothyroxine. Still fatigued, brain fog, weight gain. TPO antibodies 650.

Timeline: Mono in college. High-stress career. Symptoms worsened after second pregnancy 7 years ago.

Assessment

Autoimmune Triad Analysis

  • Genetics: Family history? (often present)
  • Leaky gut: GI symptoms? food sensitivities?
  • Triggers: EBV (mono history), pregnancy, stress

Testing Strategy

  • Full thyroid panel (TSH, fT3, fT4, rT3, TPO, TG antibodies)
  • Stool test (gut health, beta-glucuronidase)
  • Food sensitivity panel or elimination diet
  • Vitamin D, selenium, zinc levels
  • Consider: EBV reactivation panel

Treatment Plan

Non-Negotiables

  • 100% gluten-free (molecular mimicry with thyroid)
  • Vitamin D optimization 60-80 ng/mL
  • Selenium 200mcg (antibody reduction)

Phase 1: Remove Triggers (Weeks 1-8)

  • Gluten and dairy elimination
  • Address gut infections if present
  • Reduce inflammation (omega-3s, curcumin)

Phase 2: Heal & Support (Weeks 9-24)

  • Gut healing protocol
  • EBV support if reactivation present
  • Ongoing selenium, vitamin D, omega-3
  • Retest antibodies at 6 months
