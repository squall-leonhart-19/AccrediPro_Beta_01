Case: Karen, 55F - Non-Responsive

Situation: Karen has been your client for 6 months. Initial improvement, then plateau. Now seems worse. Multiple protocol changes haven't helped.

Frustration: She's considering stopping treatment. "Nothing is working."

Troubleshooting Framework

7 Reasons Cases Get Stuck

  • 1. Non-compliance (supplements, diet, lifestyle)
  • 2. Hidden infection (parasites, Lyme, EBV)
  • 3. Environmental exposure (mold, toxins)
  • 4. Structural issue (adhesions, dental infection)
  • 5. Psycho-emotional barrier (trauma, stress)
  • 6. Wrong diagnosis/pattern assessment
  • 7. Unrealistic timeline expectations

The Stuck Case Conversation

  • "I want to understand what's happening. Can we review together?"
  • Re-check compliance honestly (no judgment)
  • Re-examine the timeline‚Äîis there something we missed?
  • Discuss what hasn't been tested
  • Consider referral or second opinion

Knowing When to Refer

  • Outside your scope (psychiatric, structural, cancer concerns)
  • Not improving after appropriate interventions
  • Need for prescribing authority (LDN, bioidentical hormones)
  • Client needs more intensive support than you can provide
