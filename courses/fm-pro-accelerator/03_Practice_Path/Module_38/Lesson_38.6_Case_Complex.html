Multi-System Assessment

  • Inflammation/pain
  • SUSPECTED: Mold/CIRS (water damage timeline)

Priority Testing

  • Mycotoxin urine panel (HIGH priority given timeline)

  • Full thyroid panel with antibodies
  • Adrenal panel
  • Inflammation markers
