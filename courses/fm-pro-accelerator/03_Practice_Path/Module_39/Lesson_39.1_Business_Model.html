Clinical Excellence Meets Business Success

You can be the best practitioner in the world, but without a solid business model, you can't help anyone. This module builds the business foundation that allows your clinical skills to thrive.

Business Model Options

Model 1: One-on-One Premium

  • High-touch, high-fee individual packages
  • $3,000-10,000+ for comprehensive programs
  • 15-30 clients at a time (manageable caseload)
  • Best for: practitioners who love deep client relationships

Model 2: Group Programs

  • One-to-many delivery with some individual support
  • $500-2,500 per person
  • Leverages time: 10 people, one program
  • Best for: condition-specific or transformation programs

Model 3: Hybrid

  • Combination of 1:1 and group
  • Individual for complex cases, group for foundations
  • Most sustainable long-term model

Your Assignment

  1. Which business model fits your personality and goals?
  2. What's your ideal client load?
  3. What revenue target do you need to meet your lifestyle goals?
  4. Work backward: what pricing and volume gets you there?
