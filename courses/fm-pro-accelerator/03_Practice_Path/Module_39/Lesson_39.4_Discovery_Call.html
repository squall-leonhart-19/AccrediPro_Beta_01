Discovery Call Structure (15-30 min)

Opening (2-3 min)

  • Warm greeting, set the agenda
  • "Today is about understanding if we're a good fit for each other"

Understand Their Problem (10-15 min)

  • "What's going on that made you reach out?"
  • "How long has this been going on?"
  • "What have you tried?"
  • "What would life look like if this was solved?"

Present Your Approach (5 min)

  • Brief explanation of your functional approach
  • "Based on what you've shared, here's what I typically see..."
  • Connect their story to your solution

Invitation (3-5 min)

  • "Based on our conversation, I think I can help. Here's how we'd work together..."
  • Present your program clearly
  • State the investment confidently
  • Ask: "Does this feel like a fit for you?"

The discovery call is NOT free consulting. It's for both of you to decide if you're a good fit. Listen 80% of the time. When you present pricing, be confident and then PAUSE. Let them respond. Don't fill the silence with discounts.
