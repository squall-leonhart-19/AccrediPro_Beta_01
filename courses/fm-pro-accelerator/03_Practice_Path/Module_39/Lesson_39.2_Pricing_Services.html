Value-Based Pricing

Price Your Transformation, Not Your Time

  • What is solving their problem worth to them?
  • Reversing metabolic syndrome: worth $50,000+ in avoided medical costs
  • Getting their energy back: priceless quality of life
  • Price based on value delivered, not hours worked

Package Pricing (Recommended)

  • Example: 6-month comprehensive program = $4,500
  • Includes: initial consult + testing review + 6 follow-ups + email support
  • Client knows total investment upfront
  • You're paid for the whole journey, not nickel-and-dimed per visit

Pricing Psychology

  • People who pay more, pay attention more
  • Low prices attract low-commitment clients
  • Premium pricing positions you as expert
  • Never undercharge out of fear‚Äîit hurts your clients AND you
