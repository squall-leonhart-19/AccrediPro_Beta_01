Calming the Fire

Inflammation drives most chronic symptoms. This lesson covers core anti-inflammatory protocols that work across presentations, plus targeted immune support for autoimmunity.

Core Anti-Inflammatory Protocol

Foundation Anti-Inflammatory

  • Omega-3: 3-4g EPA+DHA (higher dose for active inflammation)
  • Curcumin: 500-1000mg liposomal or with piperine
  • Vitamin D: Optimize to 60-80 ng/mL
  • Quercetin: 500mg 2x/day (mast cell stabilizer)
  • Anti-inflammatory diet: Remove sugar, processed foods, seed oils

Autoimmune Support

  • 100% gluten-free (non-negotiable for autoimmune)
  • Vitamin D optimization (60-80 ng/mL)
  • Gut healing (leaky gut drives autoimmunity)
  • Omega-3 high-dose
  • Consider: low-dose naltrexone (LDN) with prescriber

SPM (Specialized Pro-Resolving Mediators)

  • SPMs help resolve inflammation (not just suppress)
  • Useful for chronic, stuck inflammation
  • 2-4 softgels daily for active inflammation
