Mental Health/Brain Specialty Overview

Common Conditions

  • Anxiety and panic
  • Depression (treatment-resistant)
  • Brain fog and cognitive decline
  • ADD/ADHD (nutritional support)
  • Insomnia and sleep disorders

Root Cause Drivers

  • Inflammation: Neuroinflammation drives depression, fog
  • Gut-brain axis: 90% of serotonin made in gut
  • Blood sugar: Dysregulation creates mood swings
  • Nutrient deficiencies: B12, folate, omega-3, D, iron
  • Toxicity: Mold, heavy metals impact brain

Adjunctive Support

  • Gut healing (serotonin production)
  • Anti-inflammatory protocols
  • Blood sugar optimization
  • Targeted supplementation (omega-3, B vitamins, magnesium)
  • Toxicity assessment
