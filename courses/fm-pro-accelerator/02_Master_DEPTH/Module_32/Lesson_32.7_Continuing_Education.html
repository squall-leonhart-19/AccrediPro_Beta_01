Never Stop Learning

The field evolves constantly. New research, new protocols, new understanding emerge every year. Lifelong learning isn't optional‚Äîit's what separates good practitioners from great ones. This lesson helps you build a sustainable learning practice.

Learning Strategies

The 80/20 Learning Rule

  • 80%: Deepen expertise in your core focus areas (patterns you see most)
  • 20%: Explore adjacent topics to expand your range
  • Better to know a few areas deeply than many areas superficially

Key Learning Resources

Structured Learning

  • IFM courses and conferences
  • Specialty certifications (SIBO, hormone, mold, etc.)
  • Peer learning groups and case review communities

Ongoing Resources

  • PubMed alerts for your key topics
  • Podcasts: FM-focused shows, researcher interviews
  • Books: 1-2 per quarter on clinical topics
  • Conferences: 1-2 per year (virtual counts)
