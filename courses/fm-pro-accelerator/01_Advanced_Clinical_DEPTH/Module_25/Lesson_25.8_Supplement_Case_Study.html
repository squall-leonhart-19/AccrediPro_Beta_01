Step 1: Identify Drug-Nutrient Depletions

Medication Analysis:

  • Omeprazole (PPI): Depletes B12, magnesium, calcium, iron, zinc
  • Lexapro (SSRI): May affect B vitamins; avoid St. John's Wort and 5-HTP
  • Must address PPI-induced depletions as priority

Instead of 18 supplements, we'll design a manageable 5-supplement core protocol:
