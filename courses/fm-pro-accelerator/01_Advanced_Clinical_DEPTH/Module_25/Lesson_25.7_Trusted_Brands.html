Practitioner vs. Retail Grade

Why Practitioner Brands?

  • Higher manufacturing standards: Beyond minimum GMP requirements
  • Third-party testing: More rigorous testing protocols
  • Better ingredient forms: Methylated vitamins, chelated minerals by default
  • Clinical dosing: Therapeutic doses, not just "dusting" for label claims
  • Clean formulations: Minimal fillers, no unnecessary additives
  • Distribution control: Sold through practitioners, not third-party sellers

Setting Up Dispensary Accounts

Online Dispensary Platforms

  • Fullscript: Largest selection; multiple brands; patient ordering portal
  • Wellevate (Emerson Ecologics): Good selection; competitive pricing
  • Natural Partners: Wide selection; practitioner-focused
  • Most are free for practitioners to set up
  • Clients order directly; shipped to their home
  • Practitioner earns percentage (typically 15-25%)

üí° Transparency Tip

Be transparent with clients that you may receive a percentage from supplement sales. This builds trust and is ethically required. Many practitioners use the margin to offset client costs or reinvest in education.

Ethical Considerations

Best Practices

  • Recommend what's best: Not what earns highest margin
  • Allow client choice: They can purchase elsewhere if preferred
  • Disclose financial interest: Be upfront about dispensary percentages
  • Don't oversupplement: Only recommend what's necessary
  • Review regularly: Discontinue supplements no longer needed

Acceptable Retail Options

When Retail Is Fine

  • Budget constraints: Some supplementation better than none
  • Simple nutrients: Vitamin C, basic magnesium, simple probiotics
  • Trusted retail brands: Garden of Life, NOW Foods (select products), Life Extension
  • ConsumerLab approved: Independently verified products

Always Avoid on Amazon/Retail

  • Practitioner brands sold by third-party sellers (may be counterfeit or expired)
  • No-name brands with too-good pricing
  • Products without clear third-party testing
  • Complex therapeutic formulations (buy through proper channels)
