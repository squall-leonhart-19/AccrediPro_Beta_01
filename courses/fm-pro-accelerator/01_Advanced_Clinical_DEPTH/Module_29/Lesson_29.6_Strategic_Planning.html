Design Your Business Intentionally

Without a plan, you're reacting to whatever comes. Strategic planning gives you direction and helps you build the practice you actually want.

Annual Planning

Yearly Vision

  • Revenue goal: What do you want to earn this year?
  • Client load: How many clients, what type?
  • Offerings: What will you offer/launch?
  • Lifestyle: How do you want to work? Time off?
  • Growth: Skills, certifications, team?

Backward Planning

  • Start with end-of-year vision
  • Work backward: Q4 ‚Üí Q3 ‚Üí Q2 ‚Üí Q1
  • What needs to happen each quarter to reach annual goal?

Quarterly Goals

90-Day Sprints

  • Focus: 1-3 major priorities per quarter
  • Revenue target: Monthly breakdown
  • Projects: What to launch, create, complete
  • Metrics: How you'll measure success

OKR Framework (Optional)

  • Objective: What you want to achieve (qualitative)
  • Key Results: How you'll measure it (quantitative)
  • Example: Objective = Launch group program / KR = Enroll 15 clients

Weekly and Daily Planning

Weekly Planning (CEO Time)

  • Review last week: What worked? What didn't?
  • Check quarterly goals: Are you on track?
  • Identify top 3: Most important tasks for the week
  • Block calendar: When will you do what?

Daily Planning

  • Morning review: 5 minutes to set the day
  • Top 3 priorities: What must happen today?
  • Time blocks: When for each task?
  • End-of-day review: Did you hit your 3?

Review Rhythms

Review Cadence

  • Daily: 5-min morning/evening bookends
  • Weekly: 30-60 min planning session
  • Monthly: Financial review, metric check
  • Quarterly: Goal review, adjust plan
  • Annually: Big picture visioning

Questions for Review

  • What went well this period?
  • What didn't go as planned? Why?
  • What did I learn?
  • What needs to change?
  • What am I most excited about next period?

  • Annual vision broken into quarterly sprints
  • 90-day focus: 1-3 priorities per quarter
  • Weekly planning: Top 3 priorities, block calendar
  • Daily: Morning setup, evening review
  • Review rhythms catch problems early and keep you on track
