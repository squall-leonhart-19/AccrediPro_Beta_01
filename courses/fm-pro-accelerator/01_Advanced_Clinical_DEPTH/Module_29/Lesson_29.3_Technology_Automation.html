Essential Practice Software

Practice Management Platforms

  • Practice Better: All-in-one for health practitioners (scheduling, forms, protocols, client portal)
  • Jane App: Popular for allied health, great interface
  • Simple Practice: Mental health focus, HIPAA-compliant
  • Healthie: Telehealth-focused, good for virtual practices

What to Look For

  • Client portal: Where clients access forms, protocols, resources
  • Scheduling: Self-booking with automatic reminders
  • Forms/intake: Digital intake, questionnaires
  • Telehealth: Built-in video conferencing
  • Payments: Invoice and payment processing
  • Documentation: Notes, protocols, client files

Automation Workflows

What to Automate

  • Appointment reminders: 24-hour and 1-hour before
  • Intake process: Form sent automatically after booking
  • Welcome sequence: Emails after someone books/enrolls
  • Follow-up: Check-in emails post-session
  • Re-engagement: Email past clients after X months
  • Review requests: Ask for testimonials after completion

Automation Tools

  • Zapier: Connect apps together (Practice Better ‚Üí email)
  • Make (Integromat): More complex workflows
  • ActiveCampaign: Email marketing with automation
  • ConvertKit: Simpler email automation for creators

Communication Tools

Client Communication

  • Secure messaging: Through your practice platform, not regular email
  • Video calls: Zoom, Practice Better built-in, Google Meet
  • Voxer/voice: For check-ins between sessions
  • Loom: Quick video explanations

Internal/Team

  • Slack: Team communication
  • Notion/Asana: Project management, SOPs
  • Google Workspace: Docs, sheets, shared drives

Security and Compliance

Non-Negotiables

  • HIPAA-compliant platforms: If handling PHI
  • Two-factor authentication: On all accounts
  • Strong passwords: Use a password manager
  • Encrypted communication: Not regular email for sensitive info
  • BAAs: Business Associate Agreements with vendors
  • Regular backups: Client data protected

‚ú® Key Takeaways

  • Practice management platform is your central hub
  • Automate: Reminders, intake, follow-ups, re-engagement
  • Secure communication: Not regular email for sensitive info
  • HIPAA compliance: Use compliant platforms, 2FA, strong passwords
  • Right tools save hours and improve client experience
