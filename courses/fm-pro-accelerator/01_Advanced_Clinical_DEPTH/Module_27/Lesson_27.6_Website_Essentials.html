Homepage Best Practices

Above the Fold

  • Headline: What transformation you provide ("From exhausted to energized")
  • Subheadline: Who you help and how
  • CTA button: Book a call, download freebie
  • Image: Professional photo of you or relevant imagery
  • Within 3 seconds: Visitor should know what you do and for whom

Below the Fold Elements

  • Problem agitation: "Are you struggling with..."
  • Solution: Brief intro to your approach
  • Social proof: Testimonials, logos, credentials
  • How it works: Simple 3-step process
  • About snippet: Brief intro, link to full about page
  • CTA: Repeated throughout the page

Website Platforms

Best Options for Practitioners

  • Squarespace: Beautiful templates, easy to use ($16-49/mo)
  • Showit: More design flexibility, popular with creatives
  • WordPress: Most flexibility, steeper learning curve
  • Wix: Beginner-friendly, drag-and-drop
  • Practice Better/Simple Practice: Basic site included

Common Mistakes

What to Avoid

  • Too much about you: Focus on the client's problem
  • No clear CTA: Every page needs a next step
  • Cluttered design: Clean, whitespace, easy to scan
  • Slow loading: Optimize images, minimize plugins
  • Not mobile-friendly: Most traffic is mobile
  • Hiding pricing: Be transparent or at least give ranges
  • No email capture: Lead magnet on every page
