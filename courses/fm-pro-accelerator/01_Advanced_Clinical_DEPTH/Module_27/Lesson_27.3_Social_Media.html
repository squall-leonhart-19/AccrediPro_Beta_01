Choosing Platforms

Go Where Your Clients Are

  • Master 1-2 platforms before expanding
  • Where does your ideal client spend time?
  • What format suits your strengths? (Video, writing, images)
  • Consistency on 1 platform > sporadic on 5

Algorithm-Friendly Strategies

What Algorithms Reward

  • Saves and shares: Content worth keeping or sending
  • Comments: Especially early engagement
  • Watch time: For video, keeping people watching
  • Consistency: Regular posting signals active account
  • Platform-native features: Use what they're promoting (Reels, Shorts)

Content That Gets Saved/Shared

  • Lists and checklists: "5 foods for gut health"
  • How-to guides: Step-by-step instructions
  • Relatable content: "POV: You're trying to eat healthy but..."
  • Surprising info: Myth-busting, counterintuitive facts

Engagement That Converts

Beyond Posting

  • Respond to every comment: Builds relationship and algorithm favor
  • Engage on others' content: Be visible in your niche
  • DM conversations: Where relationships and sales happen
  • Ask questions: Encourage comments with genuine prompts
  • Stories polls/questions: Easy engagement, conversation starters

Time-Efficient Social Media

Batching and Boundaries

  • Batch create: Make a week's content in one sitting
  • Schedule ahead: Later, Planoly, Meta Business Suite
  • Set time blocks: 30 min AM/PM for engagement
  • Avoid the scroll: Engage intentionally, then close
  • Repurpose: Same message, different formats
