Validated Questionnaires

MSQ (Medical Symptoms Questionnaire)

  • Overall symptom burden across 15 systems
  • Score 0-180+, lower is better
  • Every 4-6 weeks

PSQI (Sleep Quality Index)

  • Sleep quality past month
  • Score 0-21, <5 = good
  • Monthly
