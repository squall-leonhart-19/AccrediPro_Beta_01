Essential Documents

Required Intake Forms

  • Health History Questionnaire: Comprehensive health background
  • Informed Consent: Explains scope, what you do/don't do
  • Privacy Policy/HIPAA: How you protect their data
  • Financial Agreement: Payment terms, cancellation policy
  • Lab Release: If you'll be reviewing labs

Enhancing the Welcome Experience

  • Welcome video: Personal introduction, what to expect (2-3 min)
  • Welcome guide: PDF with how to prepare, what to bring, FAQs
  • Quick-start resource: Simple action they can take immediately
  • Scheduling link: Easy booking for their first session

Setting Expectations

What to Cover

  • How to reach you: Email, portal, response time expectations
  • Your role vs. their role: Partnership, not "fixing" them
  • Timeline for results: Realistic expectations (often 3-6 months)
  • Between-session expectations: Homework, food logs, etc.
  • Cancellation policy: 24-48 hours notice; rescheduling process

Managing Expectations Script

  • "Most clients start to notice shifts within 2-4 weeks, but lasting change typically takes 3-6 months."
  • "Your results depend heavily on your implementation between sessions."
  • "I'm your partner in this‚ÄîI'll guide you, but you do the work."

Automation for Efficiency

Tech Tools for Onboarding

  • Practice management: Practice Better, That Clean Life, Simple Practice
  • Scheduling: Calendly, Acuity, built-in practice management
  • Email automation: ConvertKit, ActiveCampaign, Mailchimp
  • Document signing: HelloSign, PandaDoc, DocuSign
  • Payment: Stripe, Square, PayPal

What to Automate

  • Welcome email sequence: Triggered on payment
  • Intake form delivery: Sent automatically after booking
  • Reminder emails: Before first session, if forms incomplete
  • Thank-you messages: After sessions

Creating Early Wins

Why Early Wins Matter

  • Builds confidence in the process
  • Reduces buyer's remorse
  • Creates momentum for harder changes
  • Gives them something to share (referrals!)

Quick Win Ideas

  • Sleep improvement: One simple sleep hygiene change
  • Energy boost: Blood sugar stabilization technique
  • Digestive relief: One elimination or addition
  • Stress reduction: Simple breathing practice

‚ú® Key Takeaways

  • Onboarding sets the tone for the entire client relationship
  • Essential documents: Health history, consent, financial agreement
  • Set clear expectations about timelines, roles, and communication
  • Automate welcome emails, reminders, and form delivery
  • Create an early win in session 1 to build momentum
