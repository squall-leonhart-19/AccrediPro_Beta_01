Key Success Factors

What Made Sarah Successful:

  • Didn't scale too fast: Mastered 1:1, then added group, then course, then membership
  • Built on what worked: Same curriculum across formats
  • Hired strategically: Buy back time, not just add expenses
  • Kept 1:1: Still offers premium 1:1 at highest rates
  • Multiple streams: Not dependent on any single source
  • Recurring revenue: Membership + supplements = predictable base
