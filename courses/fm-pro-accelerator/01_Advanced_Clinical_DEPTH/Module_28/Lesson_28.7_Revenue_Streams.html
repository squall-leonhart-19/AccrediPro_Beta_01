The Revenue Ladder

Low to High Touch

  • Free content: Social, blog, podcast ‚Üí builds audience
  • Lead magnet: Grows email list
  • Low-ticket: $7-97 digital products, mini courses
  • Mid-ticket: $200-1,000 group programs, courses
  • High-ticket: $1,500+ 1:1 packages, VIP intensives
  • Clients can ascend the ladder over time

Affiliate and Passive Income

Affiliate Opportunities

  • Supplement companies: Professional lines (Fullscript, Wellevate)
  • Lab companies: Referral programs
  • Software: Practice management, course platforms
  • Other practitioners' programs: Courses, certifications
  • Ethics: Only recommend what you genuinely use and believe in

Digital Products

  • Templates: Meal plans, tracking sheets, intake forms
  • eBooks: In-depth guides on specific topics
  • Printables: Planners, journals, checklists
  • Platforms: Gumroad, Payhip, your own site

When to Add New Streams

Timing Considerations

  • Master one stream first: 1:1 ‚Üí group ‚Üí course ‚Üí membership
  • Don't add too soon: Spreads focus thin
  • Add when current stream is stable: Consistent revenue
  • Build on what's working: Don't reinvent every time
  • Beware shiny object syndrome: New isn't always better

Signs You're Ready

  • 1:1 is full or nearly full
  • You have a proven transformation/methodology
  • You have an audience asking for other options
  • You have capacity (time or team) to create new offers
