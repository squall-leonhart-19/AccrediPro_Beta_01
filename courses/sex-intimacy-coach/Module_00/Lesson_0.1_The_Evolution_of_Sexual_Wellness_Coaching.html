2. Defining the Scope: Coaching vs. Sex Therapy

As an AccrediPro certified practitioner, your first responsibility is understanding your Scope of Practice. While coaches and therapists both help clients improve their intimate lives, their methodologies, goals, and legal boundaries differ significantly.

Clinical Sex Therapy

Focus: Past-oriented; healing trauma, diagnosing DSM-5 disorders (e.g., Vaginismus, ED), and treating mental health pathology.

Method: Clinical assessment, psychotherapy, and medical referrals.

Goal: Resolution of symptoms and psychological healing.

Sexual Wellness Coaching

Focus: Present and Future-oriented; goal setting, skill-building, mindset shifts, and somatic awareness.
