4. Recognizing Somatic Overwhelm vs. Healthy Expansion

Expansion is not about "more is better." It is about capacity. If a client pushes into expansion too quickly, they may experience "therapeutic recoil" or "arousal non-concordance."

Healthy Expansion

  • Deep, fluid breathing
  • Bright, present eyes
  • "Spacious" feeling in the chest
  • Ability to vocalize and communicate
  • Post-session: Energized and grounded

Somatic Overwhelm

  • Breath holding or gasping
  • Dazed or "checked out" look
  • Tightening of the jaw or throat
  • Sudden shivering or coldness
  • Post-session: Crashing or anxiety
