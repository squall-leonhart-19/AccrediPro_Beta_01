Evidence-Based Practice (EBP) strictly follows protocols proven in clinical trials. Evidence-Informed Practice (EIP) uses research as a foundational guide but allows for clinical flexibility, client values, and the integration of proprietary methodologies like I.N.T.I.M.A.T.E.‚Ñ¢ to meet the client's unique needs.
