1. Refining Your Signature Method

At the Master level, you are no longer merely a practitioner of a framework; you are the architect of an experience. While the I.N.T.I.M.A.T.E. Connection Method‚Ñ¢ provides the structural integrity of your work, your "Signature Method" is the aesthetic and intuitive layer that makes your coaching uniquely effective.

A signature method is not an abandonment of the evidence-based steps (Investigate, Normalize, Trust, Ignite, Mindful Presence, Attune, Transform, Expand). Instead, it is the weighted emphasis you place on specific elements based on your niche. For example, a coach specializing in postpartum intimacy may emphasize the "Normalize" and "Mindful Presence" phases, while a coach working with high-performance executives might focus on "Ignite" and "Expand."
