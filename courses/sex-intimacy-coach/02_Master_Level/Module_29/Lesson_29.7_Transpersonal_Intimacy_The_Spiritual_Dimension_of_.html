    5. The Shadow: Navigating "Spiritual Bypassing"

    As a Master Sex & Intimacy Coach, you must be hyper-vigilant regarding Spiritual Bypassing. This is the tendency to use spiritual ideas and practices to avoid facing unresolved emotional issues, psychological wounds, or basic biological needs.

    Warning: Signs of Bypassing in Clients

    1. Using "sacredness" to avoid discussing lack of physical attraction.
    2. Claiming "oneness" to ignore a partner's lack of consent or boundaries.
    3. Dismissing sexual dysfunction (like ED or Vaginismus) as purely "energetic" without addressing physiological or traumatic roots.

    The I.N.T.I.M.A.T.E. Method prevents bypassing by requiring Investigation (I) and Normalization (N) first. We cannot "ascend" to the transpersonal until the foundational nervous system safety and psychological integration are complete.
