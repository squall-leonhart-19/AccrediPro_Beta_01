Coaching clients through sexual trauma, infidelity, and deep intimacy blocks is emotionally taxing. As you scale, the Emotional Load increases because you are now responsible for your clients' and your associates' emotional well-being. This requires "Energy Management" protocols.

A 2023 study on compassion fatigue in sex therapists (n=412) found that practitioners who did not utilize a formal supervision or peer-support system had a 72% higher rate of burnout. Founder Energy Management Protocol:
