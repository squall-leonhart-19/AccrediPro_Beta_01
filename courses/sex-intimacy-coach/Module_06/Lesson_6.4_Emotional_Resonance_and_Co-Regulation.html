2. Emotional Leakage and the Pursuer-Distancer Dynamic

When emotional attunement fails, Emotional Leakage occurs. This is the phenomenon where unexpressed resentment, anxiety, or fear "leaks" into the sexual space, often appearing as physical tension or lack of desire. This frequently fuels the Pursuer-Distancer dynamic.

In this cycle, the "Pursuer" (often in a state of hyper-arousal) seeks connection to soothe their anxiety. The "Distancer" (often in hypo-arousal or overwhelm) perceives this pursuit as a threat and retreats to find safety. The more the Pursuer pushes for intimacy, the more the Distancer shuts down. As a Sex & Intimacy Coach, your goal is to interrupt this cycle not through sexual advice, but through co-regulation.
