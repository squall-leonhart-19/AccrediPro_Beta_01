2. The Microcosmic Orbit: Circulating the Charge

The Microcosmic Orbit is a Taoist energetic practice that finds its modern scientific parallel in the stimulation of the Vagus Nerve and the movement of cerebrospinal fluid. In coaching, we use this to prevent "energy stagnation" in the genitals, which often leads to premature climax or sensory numbness.

The Three Points of the Orbit

Coaching the orbit involves guiding the client's breath and attention through three primary gates:

  • The Perineum (Hui Yin): The root gate where sexual energy is concentrated.
  • The Spine (Du Mai): The ascending channel where energy is "drawn up" through breath and muscular contraction.
  • The Front Channel (Ren Mai): The descending channel where energy is "cycled back" to the lower abdomen (Dantian).

By moving the "charge" away from the genitals and into the torso and head, clients report a "cooling" sensation in the pelvic floor and a "blooming" sensation in the chest and limbs. This technique is essential for clients who experience the "brakes" (Dual Control Model) due to fear of losing control.
