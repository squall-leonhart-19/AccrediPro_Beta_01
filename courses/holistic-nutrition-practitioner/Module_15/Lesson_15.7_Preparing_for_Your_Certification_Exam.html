Exam Format & Logistics

The AccrediPro Academy Holistic Nutrition Practitioner exam is a rigorous assessment of your clinical and business readiness. To succeed, you must understand the mechanics of the test:

  • Question Types: A mix of multiple-choice, scenario-based applications, and methodology matching.
  • Passing Threshold: You must achieve an 80% (16/20 correct) to receive your certification.
  • Retake Policy: If you do not pass on your first attempt, we recommend reviewing the specific modules highlighted in your results before re-attempting.
  • The "Open Resource" Philosophy: In the real world, practitioners use resources. You may refer to your notes, but the time limit (60 minutes) is designed to ensure you have a fluent understanding of the material without relying solely on searching for answers.
