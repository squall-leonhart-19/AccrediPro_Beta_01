3. Performance Management & KPIs for Coaching Support

You cannot manage what you do not measure. In a high-performance team, Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) must be tied to client success, not just hours worked. A meta-analysis published in Management Science (2023) indicates that teams with clear, outcome-based KPIs show 28% higher engagement levels than those with activity-based metrics.

Recommended KPIs for coaching staff:

  • Sales Enroller: Lead-to-Close Ratio (Target: 25-35%) and "No-Show" Rate.
  • Operations Manager: System Downtime and Onboarding Speed (Target: < 24 hours from payment to portal access).
  • Support VA: Average Response Time (Target: < 4 hours during business hours) and Ticket Resolution Rate.

5. Legal Considerations: Independent Contractors vs. Employees

As you scale, the legal distinction between an Independent Contractor (IC) and a Full-Time Employee (FTE) becomes critical for risk management. Misclassification can lead to significant back-tax penalties and legal fees.

Feature Independent Contractor (1099) Full-Time Employee (W-2)
Control Controls how the work is done. Employer controls the how, when, where.
Tools Provides their own equipment/software. Employer provides all necessary tools.
Exclusivity Can work for multiple clients/coaches. Typically works exclusively for you.
Taxation Responsible for their own self-employment tax. Employer withholds income and payroll taxes.

Key Takeaways

  • Hire for the Gap: Identify whether you need an OM (Systems), a VA (Admin), or a Sales Enroller (Revenue) based on your current bottleneck.
  • The 4-Stage Funnel: Never hire based on an interview alone; use paid trial tasks to verify "Execution" capability.
  • Outcome-Based KPIs: Move away from tracking hours; track the metrics that directly impact client retention and revenue.
  • Leadership as Coaching: Use the Listen, Identify, Focus, and Execute pillars to manage your team just as you manage your clients.
  • Compliance is Mandatory: Regularly review the IC vs. Employee status of your team to ensure legal and tax compliance.
