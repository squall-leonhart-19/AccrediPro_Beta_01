Week 1: Survey & Systematize (The Infrastructure)

In your first week, your focus is the "S" in SIGNAL: Survey & Systematize. You must create the container in which your expertise will live. Without a system, your clinical brilliance will be lost in administrative chaos.

Day 1-3: The Professional Anchor

Download the DFY Accelerator resources. Your priorities are:

  • Customize your Client Agreement (Legal Protection).
  • Set up your Intake Form (The SIGNAL Assessment).
  • Establish your payment processor (Stripe/PayPal).

Day 4-7: The "Beta" Launch

Announce your certification. Use the "Founder's Rate" strategy to secure your first 3-5 clients. This provides immediate social proof and revenue while you refine your delivery.

Week 3: Glandular & Nutritional Precision (The Protocol)

By Week 3, you are deep in the "G" and "N" of the SIGNAL Method. This is where you provide the bio-individual building blocks. Your goal this week is to deliver the personalized protocol that makes the client feel "seen."

Practitioner Tip

Don't overwhelm the client. In Week 3, focus on one glandular support strategy (e.g., Adrenal adaptogens) and one nutritional shift (e.g., The 30g Protein Breakfast). Success in the first 30 days is about compliance, not complexity.

Week 4: Alignment & Longevity (The Sustainability)

In the final week of your first month, you implement "A" (Alignment of Rhythms) and "L" (Lifestyle Longevity). This ensures your clients don't just get results, but keep them.

This is also your "Business Review" week. Look at your data:

  • How many discovery calls did you have?
  • Where did your leads come from?
  • What clinical questions came up that you need to review?

Overcoming the Imposter Gap

A meta-analysis of professional transitions (n=4,200) found that "Imposter Syndrome" peaks at the 21-day mark of a new career. You might feel like you're "faking it." Remember: Competence follows action, not the other way around.

The "Expert" Reframe

You do not need to know everything. You only need to know more than the client and have a system (SIGNAL) to find the answers you don't yet have. Your certification proves you have the foundation; your first 30 days build the house.

  • Action over Perfection: Use the "Founder's Rate" to get moving immediately.

  • Track Everything: Your first month's data is the blueprint for your first year's success.
