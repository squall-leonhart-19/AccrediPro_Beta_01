4. Ethics and the Scope of Practice

As an AccrediPro Certified Practitioner, your power lies in your ability to coach behavior and interpret functional patterns. However, safety is paramount. You must refer to an Endocrinologist or Functional Medicine Physician when:

  • Lab values are in "Critical" ranges (e.g., TSH > 10 or < 0.1).
  • Sudden, unexplained weight loss or severe pelvic pain is present.
  • The client is suicidal or experiencing clinical depression (refer to a licensed therapist).
  • The client requires prescription adjustments (Coaches never alter medication).
