Avoiding the "Education Trap"

The "Education Trap" is a psychological phenomenon where a student uses "more learning" as a procrastination tool to avoid the fear of "more doing." Research in educational psychology (Hattie et al., 2021) suggests that active retrieval and application increase long-term retention by 40% compared to passive consumption.

To avoid this, we mandate a "Learn-Do" ratio of 1:1. For every hour you spend in the AccrediPro portal, you must spend one hour on your 90-day roadmap tasks. If you are studying thyroid health, spend the next hour drafting your "Thyroid Support Protocol" for your future clients. This ensures your knowledge is "sticky" and commercially viable.
