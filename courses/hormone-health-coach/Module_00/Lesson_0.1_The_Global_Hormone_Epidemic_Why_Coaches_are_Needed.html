3. The Bio-Psycho-Social Model: The 'Perfect Storm'

Hormonal dysfunction does not happen in a vacuum. It is the result of a Bio-Psycho-Social interaction. This model suggests that biology (genetics, gut health), psychology (chronic stress, trauma), and social factors (environmental toxins, socioeconomic status) converge to create endocrine disruption.

The Three Pillars of Disruption:

  • Environmental Toxins: Endocrine Disrupting Chemicals (EDCs) found in plastics, pesticides, and personal care products mimic estrogen, leading to "Estrogen Dominance."
  • The Cortisol Steal: Chronic activation of the HPA-axis (stress response) prioritizes cortisol production over progesterone and DHEA, leading to cycle irregularities.
  • Nutritional Insufficiency: Modern diets high in ultra-processed foods drive insulin resistance, which acts as a master-switch for hormonal chaos.

4. Defining Your Scope & The Referral-First Mindset

As an AccrediPro Certified Hormone Health Coach, your role is distinct and vital. You are an educator and lifestyle strategist, not a medical doctor. Understanding your legal boundaries is essential for a sustainable practice.

Scope of Practice Boundaries

  • WE DO: Suggest lifestyle modifications, educate on hormonal pathways, interpret functional labs (for educational purposes), and provide accountability.
  • WE DO NOT: Diagnose diseases, prescribe medications, or "treat" medical conditions.
  • THE REFERRAL-FIRST MINDSET: If a client presents with "red flag" symptoms (unexplained weight loss, severe pain, suicidal ideation), we refer to clinical specialists immediately.
