4. The Dual 'O' Strategy: Optimization vs. Overcoming

This is where the B.L.O.O.M. Methodâ„¢ diverges from generic coaching. We split the intervention into two simultaneous tracks: Systemic Optimization and Targeted Overcoming.

Pillar 3: Optimization (The Hormone Hierarchy)

We cannot fix sex hormones (Estrogen/Progesterone) if the foundation is crumbling. We follow a strict hierarchy:

  1. Blood Sugar & Insulin: The master controller. High insulin "steals" the precursors for sex hormones.
  2. Adrenals & Cortisol: The survival system. The body will always prioritize cortisol over progesterone.
  3. Thyroid: The metabolic engine.
  4. Sex Hormones: The "luxury" hormones.

Pillar 4: Overcoming (Acute Symptom Resolution)

While we work on the foundation, the client needs relief. This pillar uses targeted, evidence-based protocols for specific conditions like PCOS, Endometriosis, or PMDD. For example, a 2022 study showed that Myo-inositol supplementation was as effective as Metformin for improving insulin sensitivity in PCOS clients with fewer side effects (Greff et al., 2023).
