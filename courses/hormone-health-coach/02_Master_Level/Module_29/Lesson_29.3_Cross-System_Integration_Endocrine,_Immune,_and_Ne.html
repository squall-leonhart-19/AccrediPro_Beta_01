2. Psychoneuroimmunology (PNI) in Hormone Coaching

Psychoneuroimmunology (PNI) is the study of how psychological processes and the nervous and immune systems of the human body interact. For the Hormone Health Coach, PNI explains why Optimization (O) fails if the client is in a state of chronic sympathetic dominance.

The Cytokine-Hormone Connection

When the nervous system perceives a threat (real or imagined), it triggers the release of Corticotropin-Releasing Hormone (CRH). While CRH stimulates cortisol, it also activates mast cells. Activated mast cells release histamine and cytokines, which then travel back to the brain to further stimulate the HPA axis. This creates a self-perpetuating loop of stress and inflammation.
