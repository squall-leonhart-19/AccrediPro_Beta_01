5. Technical Setup & Ethical Scarcity

Technical friction is the "silent killer" of webinar conversions. For a professional hormone health practice, we recommend the following stack:

  • Platform: Zoom Webinar (for reliability) or WebinarJam (for marketing features like "live offers").
  • Audio: A dedicated XLR or high-quality USB microphone. Audio quality correlates with perceived authority.
  • Visuals: Clean, science-heavy slides with minimal text and high-quality anatomical diagrams.

Ethical Scarcity: In health coaching, we must use scarcity with integrity. Avoid "fake" countdown timers. Instead, use Capacity-Based Scarcity. "Because I review every client's Baseline assessment personally, I only have the bandwidth to take on 5 new clients this month." This is a true statement that respects the clinical nature of your work.

6. The 72-Hour Breakthrough Strategy

The majority of enrollments do not happen during the live webinar; they happen in the 72 hours following it. Your follow-up sequence must be rigorous:

The Follow-Up Timeline
  • Hour 0-2: Send the "Replay & Homework" email. Include a specific worksheet based on the "Baseline" (B) section of the webinar.
  • Hour 24: The "Case Study" email. Share a transformation story specifically related to the symptoms discussed in the Q&A.
  • Hour 48: The "Hormone Breakthrough Call" invite. Offer a 15-minute 1-on-1 "Pattern Recognition" call for the first 10 people who apply.
  • Hour 72: The "Doors Closing" email. Reiterate the capacity-based scarcity.
