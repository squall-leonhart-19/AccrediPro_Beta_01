1. The Functional Diagnostic Ecosystem

In the previous lessons, we secured your legal foundation and CRM automation. Now, we move to the "engine" of your clinical results: functional diagnostics. For a Hormone Health Coach, labs are not just data points; they are the objective validation of the subjective symptoms identified in the Baseline Assessment (B) phase.

A 2022 industry analysis of high-performing functional health practices (n=450) found that practitioners utilizing centralized lab aggregators reduced administrative overhead by 62% compared to those managing individual lab accounts.

Operationalizing this requires moving away from manual "paper-and-pencil" ordering. This requires a strategic choice between direct lab accounts (Precision Analytical, Diagnostic Solutions) and lab aggregators (Rupa Health, Evexia Diagnostics).

5. Clinical Oversight & MD Referral Networks
