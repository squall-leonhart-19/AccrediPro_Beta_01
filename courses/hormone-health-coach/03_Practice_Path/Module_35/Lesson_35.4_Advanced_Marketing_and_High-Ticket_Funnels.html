An evergreen webinar is a pre-recorded presentation that runs on autopilot, educating prospects while you sleep. For the Hormone Health Coach, the B.L.O.O.M. Methodâ„¢ provides the perfect narrative arc for this funnel.

The 4-Stage Webinar Architecture:

  1. The Hook (Baseline): Open with the "Global Hormone Epidemic." Validate their struggle using the symptom mapping concepts from Module 1.
  2. The Paradigm Shift (Listen & Optimize): Explain why standard labs (TSH only, etc.) fail them. Introduce the concept of "Functional Ranges" vs. "Normal Ranges."

5. Ethical Social Proof & HIPAA Compliance

In the hormone health space, trust is the primary currency. However, as an AccrediPro certified professional, you must maintain the highest ethical standards. HIPAA compliance (or local equivalent) is non-negotiable when sharing client wins.

Guidelines for Social Proof:

  • Anonymize Data: Use initials or change names (e.g., "Client A").
  • Focus on Labs: Share "Before and After" lab markers (with permission) rather than just "Before and After" body photos. This reinforces your status as a clinical expert.
  • Video Testimonials: Ensure you have a signed Marketing Release Form that specifically outlines where the video will be used.
  • The "Outcome" Focus: Instead of "She lost weight," use "She restored her cycle and regained her cognitive edge."

6. Scaling Economics: Understanding Your Numbers

To scale to a $1M+ practice, you must treat your marketing like a laboratory. You are looking for a Predictable Client Acquisition Cost (CAC).

If your program is $5,000 and your CAC is $1,000 (meaning you spent $1,000 in ads to get one client), you have a "Money Machine." You can confidently reinvest profits to grow. As you move into this tier, your focus shifts from "How do I coach?" to "How do I optimize the conversion rate of my funnel?"
