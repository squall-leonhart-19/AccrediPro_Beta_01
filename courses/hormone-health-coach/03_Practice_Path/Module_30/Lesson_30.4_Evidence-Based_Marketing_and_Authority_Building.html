2. Ethics & Compliant Messaging: Navigating the "Cure" Trap

As established in Lesson 2, your scope of practice prohibits diagnosing or "curing" disease. However, marketing requires you to speak to the results. The key is to shift from Disease-Centric Claims to Function-Centric Outcomes. You are not "curing Hashimoto's"; you are "optimizing thyroid-adrenal crosstalk to resolve fatigue."
