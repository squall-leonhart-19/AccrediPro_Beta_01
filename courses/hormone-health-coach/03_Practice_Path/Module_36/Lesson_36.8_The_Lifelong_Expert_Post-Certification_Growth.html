4. The Future of Hormone Health: Longevity & Nutrigenomics

The next decade of hormone health will be defined by Personalized Endocrinology. We are moving away from "population averages" and toward individual genetic blueprints.

Nutrigenomics: As a coach, understanding how genetic polymorphisms (like MTHFR or COMT) influence hormone metabolism will be essential. This isn't about "diagnosing" genes, but about using the Baseline phase to understand why one client thrives on a specific protocol while another does not.

Longevity Science: Hormones are the primary drivers of the "Hallmarks of Aging." Practitioners who understand the interplay between Growth Hormone, IGF-1, and mTOR will lead the way in helping clients maintain muscle mass and cognitive function well into their 80s and 90s.
