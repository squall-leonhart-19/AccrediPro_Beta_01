2. The B.I.R.T.H. Method Sales Psychology

High-conversion consultations follow a specific psychological arc. Instead of a linear list of services, we align the conversation with the five pillars of our methodology. This ensures the client understands the comprehensive value of your support, moving beyond the "labor coach" stereotype.

B.I.R.T.H. Pillar Sales Application Client Psychological Need
Breathe & Center Active listening and co-regulation during the call. Safety and Emotional Security.
Inform & Educate Demonstrating evidence-based knowledge (EBP). Competence and Trust in Expertise.
Reassure & Comfort Validating their specific fears/desires. Empathy and Personal Connection.
Transform the Experience Painting the vision of an empowered outcome. Self-Actualization and Autonomy.
Honor & Heal Discussing postpartum recovery and long-term wellness. Sustainability and Whole-Person Care.
