Check Your Understanding

Test your mastery of cultural humility and integration.

1. How does "Cultural Humility" differ from "Cultural Competence" in a clinical setting?

Cultural competence suggests an end-point of "knowing" a culture, whereas cultural humility is a lifelong process of self-reflection, acknowledging power imbalances, and viewing the client as the expert of their own experience.

2. According to global trends, what is a key factor in the effectiveness of doula support across different countries?

Effectiveness is highest when the support person is independent of the hospital staff and when the birth environment is culturally adapted to the birthing person's specific needs and traditions.
