  1. Identify the Trigger: Notice when you think, "I know exactly how they feel."

5. Advanced Questioning: Uncovering Subconscious Narratives

Standard coaching asks "Why?" or "How?". Advanced inquiry asks about the texture of the experience. This bypasses the logical brain and taps into the subconscious identity scripts formed by minority stress.

The "Essence" Questions:
  • "When you say you feel 'trapped' in that office, what is the *color* or *shape* of that trap?"
  • "If your gender identity was a landscape you were standing in, where is the sun located?"
  • "Notice the space between who you are and who you feel you *should* be. What does that space taste like?"

Note: These questions seem abstract, but for a client with trauma, they provide a safe metaphor to express what literal language cannot.
