Mastering the Client Journey

A marketing funnel is not just a sales tool; it is a psychological journey that transforms a stranger in pain into a committed client. In the sleep health industry, prospects are often exhausted, skeptical, and overwhelmed by "sleep hygiene" advice that hasn't worked. Your funnel must demonstrate empathy while positioning you as the specialized expert who understands the complex biological drivers of their insomnia. By the end of this lesson, you will have the blueprint to build an automated system that attracts and qualifies your ideal clients 24/7.

Top of Funnel (TOFU): Awareness & Lead Generation

The goal of TOFU is to capture attention and contact information. For a sleep practitioner, generic advice like "don't use your phone before bed" is insufficient. Your lead magnet must solve a specific, acute problem or provide a "lightbulb moment" regarding their physiology.

Weak Lead Magnet

"10 Tips for Better Sleep" (Too generic, low perceived value, easily found on Google).

High-Converting Lead Magnet

"The Cortisol-Caffeine Calculator: Stop the 3 AM Wake-Up Call" (Specific, physiological, high utility).

Data from AccrediPro's 2024 Practitioner Survey indicates that Quizzes (e.g., "What is Your Sleep Chronotype?") convert at a rate 40% higher than static PDF guides. Quizzes provide immediate, personalized feedback, which is highly engaging for a prospect looking for answers to their unique sleep struggles.

Middle of Funnel (MOFU): Nurturing Authority

Once you have the email address, the MOFU stage begins. This is where you implement the RESTOREâ„¢ Method education. Your nurture sequence should not just "check in"; it should dismantle the prospect's previous failures and reframe their understanding of sleep.

The 5-Day Nurture Blueprint

Day 1: Deliver the Lead Magnet + Welcome.
Day 2: The "Why You're Still Tired" (Address the S in RESTORE - Stress/Nervous System).
Day 3: Case Study (Social proof of a similar client's transformation).
Day 4: The "Master Clock" (Address the R in RESTORE - Rhythm Alignment).
Day 5: The Invitation (Direct CTA to book a Discovery Call).

Bottom of Funnel (BOFU): Conversion to High-Ticket

The BOFU stage is where the sale happens. For high-ticket sleep coaching ($2,500+), the primary conversion tool is the Discovery Call. This is a 30-45 minute session designed to assess fit, not to provide free coaching.
