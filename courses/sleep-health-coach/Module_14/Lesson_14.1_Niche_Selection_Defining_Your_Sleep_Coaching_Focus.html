The Generalist Trap

The "Generalist Trap" is the belief that by narrowing your focus, you are losing potential clients. In reality, the opposite is true. When you speak to everyone, you speak to no one. Your marketing becomes vague, your pricing becomes competitive (downward), and your clinical protocols remain surface-level.

High-Value Sleep Niches

Success in sleep coaching requires aligning your personal passion with market demand. Here are 6 high-demand niches where you can apply the RESTORE Methodâ„¢:

1. Executive & Corporate Performance

2. Pediatric & Toddler Sleep

Helping exhausted parents establish healthy circadian rhythms for children.

4. Tactical & Shift Work Optimization

Working with nurses, pilots, police, and first responders.

  • Pain Point: Circadian misalignment, chronic fatigue, safety risks.
  • RESTORE Focus: Timing & Consistency (T) and Recovery Tracking (R).
