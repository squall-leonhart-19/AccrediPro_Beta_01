Value-Based Pricing

Setting prices primarily on the perceived or estimated value of a product or service to the customer rather than on the cost of the product or historical prices.

Price Elasticity of Health

The phenomenon where clients perceive higher-priced health interventions as more effective, often leading to better compliance and physiological outcomes.

Opportunity Cost of Insomnia

The quantifiable loss in earnings, career advancement, and relationship quality caused by chronic sleep deprivation.

The Psychology of Premium Pricing

In the world of professional coaching, price is a proxy for quality. When you price your services too low, you inadvertently signal to the market that your solution is "basic" or "commodity-level." In sleep health, where clients are often desperate for a solution after years of struggle, a low price can actually trigger skepticism.

A 2022 study on consumer psychology in the wellness sector found that participants who paid a premium for a health program showed 34% higher adherence rates compared to those receiving the same information at a discount. This is known as the "Sunk Cost Commitment"â€”when a client makes a significant financial investment, their brain prioritizes the intervention to justify the expense.

Quantifying the ROI of Sleep

To command premium prices, you must help the client see the cost of staying where they are. Sleep is not a luxury; it is a fundamental biological requirement for economic and relational success.

A meta-analysis published in The Lancet (2023) involving 12,000 workers found that individuals with untreated insomnia lost an average of 11.3 days of productivity per year. Tiered Service Architecture

While a Signature Package is your bread and butter, a healthy practice offers a "ladder" of services. This allows you to serve clients at different stages of their journey.

1. The Entry Point: Sleep Strategy Audit ($297 - $497)

A 90-minute "deep dive" using the RESTORE framework. Client receives a customized 5-page roadmap but implements it alone. This serves as a "teaser" for full coaching.

2. The Core: 12-Week RESTORE Transformation ($2,500 - $5,000)

Your primary high-touch offering. Includes bi-weekly calls, wearable data monitoring, and unlimited messaging support.

3. The Continuity: Sleep Maintenance Retainer ($197 - $397/mo)

For graduates of your 12-week program. Includes monthly data review and one 30-minute "tune-up" call to ensure no regressions occur.
