Week 4: Analysis & Scaling

In the final week of your first month, you transition from a "newbie" to a "business owner." This involves reviewing the Recovery Tracking & Metrics (the 'R' in RESTORE) of your clients and your business. Are your clients seeing an increase in Deep Sleep or HRV? Are your discovery calls converting at 20% or higher?

A successful practitioner uses data to remove emotion from the business. If your conversion rate is low, you don't "suck at coaching"; you simply need to refine your Discovery Call script (found in the DFY Accelerator).
