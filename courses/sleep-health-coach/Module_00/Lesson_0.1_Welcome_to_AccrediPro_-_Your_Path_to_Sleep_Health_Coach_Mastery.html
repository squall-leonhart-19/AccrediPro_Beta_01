Suprachiasmatic Nucleus (SCN)

The "master clock" in the brain that regulates circadian rhythms based on light input.

Sleep Architecture

The structural organization of sleep stages (N1, N2, N3, and REM) throughout the night.

Homeostatic Sleep Drive

The internal pressure to sleep that builds up the longer we stay awake, primarily driven by adenosine.

Sleep Effort

The counterproductive psychological phenomenon of "trying" to sleep, which often fuels insomnia.
