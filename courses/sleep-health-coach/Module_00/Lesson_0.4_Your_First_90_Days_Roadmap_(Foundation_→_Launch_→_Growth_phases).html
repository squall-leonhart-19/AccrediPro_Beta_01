Phase 1: Foundation (Days 1-30)

The first 30 days are dedicated to Mastery and Personal Application. You cannot coach what you do not embody. During this phase, your primary objective is to absorb the core scientific principles of sleep health and apply the RESTORE Methodâ„¢ to your own life. This creates the "Practitioner's Intuition" necessary for high-level coaching.

Key focus areas include the neurobiology of sleep, the role of adenosine in homeostatic sleep drive, and the impact of blue light on melatonin suppression. You will also begin your "Coach's Sleep Diary," documenting your own HRV and sleep stages using wearable technology. This data-driven approach ensures you understand the nuances of the Recovery Tracking & Metrics (R) pillar of our methodology.

Pro Tip

During the Foundation phase, focus on identifying your own Chronotype. Are you a Lion, Bear, Wolf, or Dolphin? Understanding your own biological peak will help you schedule your study sessions and future client calls for maximum cognitive clarity.

Once the foundation is laid, you transition into Operational Excellence. This is where the theory meets the marketplace. You will establish your professional presence, secure your first "Beta Clients," and implement the administrative systems required to scale. Phase 3: Growth (Days 61-90)

In the final 30 days of your roadmap, you shift toward Authority and Scalability. By now, you have completed your certification and have initial client testimonials. The focus now turns to the Organic & Metabolic Support (O) and Environment Optimization (E) pillars, as these often require more advanced troubleshooting and partnerships with other health professionals (e.g., nutritionists or functional medicine doctors).

Growth is achieved by moving from one-on-one coaching to "one-to-many" models or specialized niches. You will also refine your use of objective data. A 2023 meta-analysis of 42 studies (n=8,234) found that coaches who utilized wearable data (like Oura or Whoop) achieved 18% better outcomes in client sleep efficiency than those using subjective diaries alone.

The RESTORE Methodâ„¢ is the "operating system" for your practice. During your first 90 days, you should view each letter of the acronym as a milestone in your business development:
