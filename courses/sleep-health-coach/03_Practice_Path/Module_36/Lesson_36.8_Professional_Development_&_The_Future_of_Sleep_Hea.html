A meta-analysis of 42 studies (n=8,234) found that lifestyle interventions led by coaches were significantly more effective at long-term habit maintenance than automated app-based solutions alone (Effect size d = 0.64). Professional Ethics Review

Test your grasp of the professional standards required for L4 Certification.

1. A client presents with chronic insomnia and mentions they frequently wake up gasping for air. According to the D.R.E.A.M. framework, what is your immediate professional responsibility?

This is a "Red Flag" symptom for Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA). Your responsibility is to refer to a sleep physician for a clinical diagnostic study (PSG). You may continue coaching on hygiene and environment, but you MUST NOT attempt to "treat" a suspected breathing disorder.

2. How does the "E" (Environment) in the D.R.E.A.M. Method differ from standard "Sleep Hygiene" checklists?

While hygiene is behavioral, Environment is structural and biological. It involves engineering the "Cool Sanctuary" (thermal regulation), eliminating melatonin suppression (photobiology), and managing auditory sleep thresholds. It is a proactive, science-based optimization of the sleep space, not just a list of "don'ts."
