Check Your Understanding

1. Why is value-based pricing more effective than hourly pricing for High-Net-Worth Individuals?

HNWIs value outcomes, time, and ROI over "time spent." By anchoring the price to the cost of the problem (e.g., lost executive productivity), the coaching fee becomes a minor investment compared to the financial gain of improved performance.

2. What is the primary difference between the 'Environment' (E) pillar in standard coaching versus the Sleep Concierge model?

In standard coaching, the coach provides advice and checklists for the client to implement. In the Concierge model, the coach/team performs in-home audits and often manages the physical installation or calibration of sleep technologies (white-glove service).
