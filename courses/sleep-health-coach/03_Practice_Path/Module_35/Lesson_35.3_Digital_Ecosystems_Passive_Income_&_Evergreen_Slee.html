5. Automating the Sales Funnel: Lead Magnets & Evergreen Webinars

Passive income requires automated traffic. You cannot manually pitch every digital course sale. The "Evergreen Funnel" is a 24/7 sales machine that follows a specific psychological path:

  1. The Hook (Lead Magnet): A free resource like "The 3-Minute Bedroom Audit" or "The Caffeine Half-Life Calculator."
  2. The Education (Email Sequence): 5-7 automated emails that explain the *science* behind the problem (e.g., the Melatonin-Cortisol Axis from Module 2).
  3. The Conversion (Evergreen Webinar): A pre-recorded masterclass that provides genuine value and ends with an offer for your digital course or membership.

Check Your Understanding

Why is the "Environment" (E) pillar of the D.R.E.A.M. method particularly well-suited for an evergreen digital course?

Environmental optimizations are largely objective and "set-and-forget" (e.g., changing light bulbs, setting thermostats). Unlike deep psychological root causes (A), environmental shifts don't require high-touch clinical nuance for every individual, making them highly scalable through recorded instruction.

What is the primary psychological purpose of a "Lead Magnet" in a sleep coaching funnel?

The lead magnet builds authority and trust by solving a small, specific problem for free (e.g., calculating caffeine timing). It moves the lead from "unaware" to "aware" of your expertise, allowing you to capture their email for further education.
