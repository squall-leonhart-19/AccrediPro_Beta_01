1. The Gut-Brain Axis (G): The Foundation of Neuro-Longevity

In previous modules, we established the gut as the "second brain." In neuro-longevity, this relationship is no longer metaphoricalâ€”it is clinical. Chronic intestinal permeability (leaky gut) leads to the systemic circulation of lipopolysaccharides (LPS), which can cross a compromised blood-brain barrier (BBB), triggering microglia activation.

Microglia are the brain's resident immune cells. When chronically activated by gut-derived inflammation (Inflammaging), they shift from a "protective/cleaning" phenotype to a "pro-inflammatory" phenotype. This shift inhibits the clearance of amyloid-beta plaques and phosphorylated tau proteins, the hallmarks of Alzheimer's Disease (AD).

2. Nutritional Interventions for BDNF & Synaptic Plasticity

Brain-Derived Neurotrophic Factor (BDNF) is often called "Miracle-Gro for the brain." It supports the survival of existing neurons and encourages the growth and differentiation of new neurons and synapses. As coaches, our goal is to maximize BDNF through the "Eat" (E) and "Exercise" (E) pillars of the A.G.E.L.E.S.S. Protocolâ„¢.

Key BDNF Modulators:

  • Polyphenols: Flavonoids found in blueberries (anthocyanins) and dark chocolate (flavanols) have been shown to cross the BBB and stimulate BDNF signaling.
  • Intermittent Fasting: Triggers hormetic stress, increasing BDNF as an evolutionary adaptation for cognitive sharpness during food scarcity.
  • Omega-3 Fatty Acids (DHA): Specifically in the phospholipid form (found in krill or specialized supplements), DHA is essential for membrane fluidity and BDNF receptor sensitivity.

3. The Glymphatic System (L): Brain Detoxification

One of the most critical "Lifestyle" (L) interventions is the optimization of the glymphatic system. Discovered recently, this macroscopic waste clearance system functions primarily during deep (N3) slow-wave sleep. During this phase, the brain's interstitial space increases by up to 60%, allowing cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) to "wash" away metabolic toxins.
