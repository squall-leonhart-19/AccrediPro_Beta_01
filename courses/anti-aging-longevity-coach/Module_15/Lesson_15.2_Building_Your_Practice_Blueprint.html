Pricing & Packaging: Selling Outcomes, Not Hours

One of the most common mistakes new practitioners make is "trading time for dollars." In the premium longevity space, clients are paying for the outcome (e.g., biological age reversal, sustained high performance, disease prevention). Your blueprint must reflect high-ticket packaging.

A meta-analysis of coaching outcomes (n=2,450) published in The Journal of Positive Psychology found that structured, goal-oriented coaching interventions lasting 3-6 months resulted in a 0.82 effect size for wellbeing, significantly higher than ad-hoc sessions. Therefore, your "Practice Blueprint" should prioritize 3-month or 6-month containers.

The Client Journey Funnel

How do you attract $5,000+ clients? It requires a "Scientific Marketing" approach. Your blueprint includes three distinct stages of the funnel:

  1. Awareness (The Authority Stage): Sharing peer-reviewed research on longevity (e.g., the hallmarks of aging) via social media or webinars.
  2. Consideration (The Assessment Stage): Offering a "Longevity Audit" or a biological age quiz to highlight the client's specific V.I.T.A.L.S. gaps.
  3. Conversion (The Strategy Session): A 45-minute deep dive where you map out their personalized longevity roadmap and invite them into your program.
