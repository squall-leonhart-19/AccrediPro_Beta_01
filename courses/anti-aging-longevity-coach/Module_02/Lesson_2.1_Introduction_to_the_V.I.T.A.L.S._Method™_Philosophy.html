Geroscience

The interdisciplinary field that aims to understand the relationship between the biology of aging and age-related diseases.

Senescence

The process by which cells cease to divide and enter a state of permanent growth arrest, often secreting pro-inflammatory signals.

Hormesis

A biological phenomenon where exposure to low doses of a stressor (that would be toxic at high doses) induces beneficial adaptations.

Inflammaging

Chronic, sterile, low-grade inflammation that develops during aging and contributes to the pathogenesis of age-related diseases.
