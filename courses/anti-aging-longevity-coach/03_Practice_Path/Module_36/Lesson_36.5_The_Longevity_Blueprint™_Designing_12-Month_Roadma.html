The Longevity Blueprint‚Ñ¢ utilizes a 12-month framework because it aligns with the natural cycles of cellular turnover and habit formation. By sequencing the pillars, we ensure that each phase builds a physiological platform for the next.

The A.G.E.L.E.S.S. Protocol‚Ñ¢ is not static. We build Pivot Points into the blueprint every 90 days. These are formal reassessment periods where the A (Assess) pillar returns to the forefront.

The Quarterly Review Checklist:

  • Biomarker Delta: Compare current blood work (HbA1c, ApoB, Hs-CRP) against baseline.

1. Why are "Supplements & Biohacks" (Phase 3) placed at the end of the 12-month roadmap instead of the beginning?

Supplements are enhancements that require an optimized biological terrain to be effective. By addressing gut health, diet, and exercise first, we reduce systemic inflammation and improve nutrient signaling (mTOR/AMPK), ensuring the body can actually utilize advanced interventions like senolytics or NAD+ boosters without interference from metabolic dysfunction.

2. What is a "Pivot Point" and how often should it occur?

A Pivot Point is a scheduled quarterly (every 90 days) reassessment period. It involves re-testing key biomarkers and functional metrics to determine if the current protocol is working or if the coach needs to "pivot" back to an earlier phase (e.g., if inflammation markers have spiked).
