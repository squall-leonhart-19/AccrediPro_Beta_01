3. Leveraging Junior Coaches & Automation

To scale beyond 50 active clients, you must distinguish between Clinical Strategy (which you provide) and Lifestyle Implementation (which your team provides). Junior Coaches, often trained in basic health coaching or nutrition, can handle the high-frequency, low-complexity tasks associated with the L (Lifestyle) and S (Social) pillars.

The Delegation Framework

  • The Master Coach (You): Analyzes bloodwork (A), designs the annual protocol, and leads high-level strategy calls.
  • The Junior Coach: Conducts weekly 15-minute "check-ins," monitors client wearable data (Oura/Whoop), and facilitates community engagement in the Social pillar.
  • Automation Tools: Use platforms like Practice Better or CoachAccountable to automate the delivery of pillar-specific education, supplement reminders, and progress tracking.
