Check Your Understanding

Why is the 'Social & Purpose' (S) pillar easier to implement in a group coaching model than in 1-on-1 coaching?

Group coaching naturally creates a "Blue Zone" effect, where clients provide peer-to-peer accountability and social connection, which are core components of the 'S' pillar. In 1-on-1 coaching, the social connection is limited to the coach-client relationship.

What is the primary role of a Junior Coach in a scaled longevity practice?

A Junior Coach handles high-frequency, lower-complexity tasks such as monitoring daily lifestyle data (sleep, steps, stress), moderating community forums, and ensuring basic protocol compliance, allowing the Lead Coach to focus on high-level biomarker interpretation and strategy.
