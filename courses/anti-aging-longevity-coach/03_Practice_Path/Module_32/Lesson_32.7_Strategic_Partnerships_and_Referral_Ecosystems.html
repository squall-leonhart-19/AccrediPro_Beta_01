2. Ethical Affiliate Ecosystems

As a Longevity Coach, your recommendations carry significant weight. Developing affiliate relationships with supplement and biohacking brands can provide a secondary revenue stream, but it must be managed with radical transparency.

Ethical affiliate management involves a three-tier vetting process:

  • Scientific Rigor: Does the brand provide third-party testing (COAs) and peer-reviewed data for their formulations?
  • Personal Experience: Have you or your team personally used the product for at least 90 days?
  • Client Benefit: Does the affiliate link provide a discount for the client, or just a commission for you?

4. Longevity Tech & Wholesale Mastery

To provide a premium experience, you should negotiate wholesale or "Pro" pricing for your clients on essential longevity tools. This includes Continuous Glucose Monitors (CGMs), Oura rings, biological age testing kits (e.g., TruDiagnostic), and red-light therapy devices.

The "White Glove" Tech Integration: Instead of telling a client to "buy a CGM," your business model should include the device, the interpretation, and the protocol as a bundled service. By purchasing 50 units of a biological age test upfront, you can often secure a 30-40% discount, allowing you to either increase your margin or offer the test at a lower price than the public can find, creating exclusive value.

6. Managing Your Referral Network

A referral network only works if it is reciprocal and organized. Use a CRM (Customer Relationship Management) tool to track who is sending you leads and who you are sending leads to.

The Ethical Referral Agreement: Ensure all referral fees are legal within your jurisdiction (some medical boards prohibit "kickbacks"). In many cases, it is better to structure these as "Marketing Fees" or simply "Value-Adds" for the client. Always prioritize the client's health outcomes over the referral commission.

Key Takeaways

  • Longevity is a "team sport"; building a Circle of Care with MDs and labs is essential for clinical depth and professional credibility.
