4. Environmental Toxin Audit: Endocrine Disruption

A longevity program is incomplete if the client is living in a "toxic soup." Endocrine-disrupting chemicals (EDCs) like phthalates, bisphenols (BPA/BPS), and PFAS are linked to accelerated biological aging and mitochondrial dysfunction.

The Three-Phase Toxin Audit Protocol
  • Phase 1: The Kitchen (Ingestion): Switch from plastic storage/non-stick pans to glass, stainless steel, and cast iron. Filter water with Reverse Osmosis (RO) to remove PFAS ("forever chemicals").
  • Phase 2: The Bathroom (Absorption): Audit personal care products using the EWG Skin Deep database. Focus on removing "Fragrance/Parfum" (the loophole for phthalates).
  • Phase 3: The Bedroom (Inhalation): Implement HEPA/Carbon air filtration and ensure "Off-gassing" mitigation for new furniture or mattresses.
