4. Time-Efficient Movement: The 15-Minute Minimum Effective Dose (MED)

The most common barrier to the E (Exercise) pillar in the C-suite is time. The longevity coach must move away from 60-minute gym sessions toward high-intensity, high-yield interventions.

The "Ageless Executive" MED Workout
  • 0-3 mins: Dynamic Warm-up (World's Greatest Stretch).
  • 3-10 mins: HIIT/VILPA (Vigorous Intermittent Leisure Physical Activity). 40 seconds max effort, 20 seconds rest (Kettlebell swings or Air Bike).
  • 10-15 mins: Isometric Holds (Plank, Wall Sit) to stimulate GLUT4 translocation without excessive CNS fatigue.

A 2023 study in Nature Medicine found that just 3 to 4 one-minute bursts of vigorous activity daily were associated with up to a 40% reduction in all-cause mortality. This data is a powerful tool for convincing time-poor clients that "short" is still "effective."

5. CGM for the Executive: Data-Driven Mental Clarity

Fluctuating blood glucose is the silent killer of executive productivity. The "afternoon slump" is often a hypoglycemic dip following a high-carb lunch. Using Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM), we provide real-time biofeedback to the executive.

The goal for the executive is "Glycemic Variability" (GV) reduction. High GV is more damaging to the vascular endothelium than consistently high (but stable) glucose. We aim for a standard deviation of < 15 mg/dL.

Why is Glycemic Variability (GV) arguably more important than average glucose for an executive?

High GV causes more oxidative stress and endothelial damage than stable glucose levels. For an executive, the "crashes" associated with high GV directly impair cognitive function, decision-making, and emotional regulation during the workday.

How does the "Travel Protocol" utilize the 'E' (Eat) pillar to entrain circadian rhythms?

By fasting during transit and eating only at the local mealtime of the destination, the executive uses "food-entrainable oscillators" (peripheral clocks) to signal the body's master clock (SCN) that it has arrived in a new time zone, accelerating adaptation.
