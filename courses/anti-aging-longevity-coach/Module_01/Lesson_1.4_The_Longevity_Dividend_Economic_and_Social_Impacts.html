Compression of Morbidity

The goal of longevity coaching is not just to add years to life, but to compress morbidity. In a traditional aging trajectory, a person might spend the last 15-20 years of their life in a state of declining health (expansion of morbidity). In the longevity model, we aim to keep the individual at peak function until the very end, followed by a very short period of decline.
