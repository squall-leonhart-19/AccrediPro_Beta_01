4. Advanced Longevity Nutriceuticals

While food is the foundation, advanced longevity coaching involves evaluating specific compounds that target the Hallmarks of Aging. As we age, NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) levels decline by approximately 50% every 20 years, leading to mitochondrial dysfunction.

Key longevity agents for the post-menopausal client:

  • NAD+ Precursors (NMN/NR): Support DNA repair via sirtuin activation. Essential for maintaining metabolic flexibility in the absence of high estrogen.
  • Resveratrol: A potent polyphenol that mimics the effects of caloric restriction and activates SIRT1. Clinical trials show it may improve bone mineral density in post-menopausal women.
  • Quercetin & Senolytics: Helps clear "zombie cells" (senescent cells) that secrete pro-inflammatory cytokines, often referred to as "inflammaging."
  • CoQ10 (Ubiquinol): Critical for cardiovascular support as the risk of heart disease rises post-menopause.
