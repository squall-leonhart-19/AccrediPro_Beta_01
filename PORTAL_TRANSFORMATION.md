## Credential Naming Convention

### Format
`ASI-[SPECIALTY]-[LEVEL]`

### Core Credential
- **ASI-CHC** - Certified Health Coach

### Specialty Credentials
- **ASI-FM** - Functional Medicine Specialist
- **ASI-WL** - Weight Loss Specialist
- **ASI-GH** - Gut Health Specialist
- **ASI-HH** - Hormonal Health Specialist
- **ASI-WW** - Women's Wellness Specialist
- **ASI-DC** - Diabetes Coach Specialist
- **ASI-MH** - Mental Health & Wellness Specialist
- **ASI-SN** - Sports Nutrition Specialist
- **ASI-SS** - Sleep Science Specialist
- *(50+ more specialties)*

### Levels
- **Foundation** (default, no suffix)
- **Advanced** (ASI-FM-A)
- **Master** (ASI-FM-M)
- **Practice** (ASI-FM-P)
