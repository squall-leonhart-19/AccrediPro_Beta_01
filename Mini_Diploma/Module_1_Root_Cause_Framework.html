THE 7 BODY SYSTEMS MODEL

Your body is ONE connected system — not isolated parts.

Traditional medicine treats symptoms.
Functional Medicine treats systems.

Here are the 7 systems that explain almost every chronic health issue:

SYSTEM 1: Gut & Digestive System

"If the gut isn't working, nothing is working."

Your gut controls:

  • Digestion and nutrient absorption
  • 70-80% of your immune system
  • Inflammation levels throughout your body
  • 90% of your serotonin (your "happy" chemical)
  • Detoxification pathways
  • Hormone processing and elimination

When the gut breaks down — hormones, energy, immunity, weight, and mood ALL fall apart.

This is why we always start here.

SYSTEM 2: Hormones

Hormones are your body's messengers. They tell every system what to do.

The big players:

  • Cortisol — your stress hormone
  • Insulin — your blood sugar regulator
  • Thyroid hormones — your metabolism controller
  • Estrogen & Progesterone — your cycle regulators
  • Melatonin — your sleep hormone

Symptoms like PMS, weight resistance, anxiety, insomnia, and hair loss?

They're rarely "hormone problems."
They're communication problems — signals getting lost or scrambled.

SYSTEM 3: Immune System & Inflammation

Chronic inflammation is behind:

  • Joint and muscle pain
  • Autoimmune conditions
  • Fatigue that doesn't improve with rest
  • Bloating and digestive issues
  • Brain fog and memory problems
  • Stubborn weight gain

Inflammation is your body asking for help. It's not a malfunction — it's a message.

SYSTEM 4: Detoxification (Liver + Lymph)

This isn't about "juice cleanses" — this is real biochemistry.

When your detoxification pathways are overwhelmed:

  • You feel puffy and swollen
  • You're chronically inflamed
  • You feel "heavy" in your body
  • You're exhausted for no clear reason
  • You develop food sensitivities
  • Your hormones become unstable

SYSTEM 5: Energy & Mitochondria

Your mitochondria are the power plants of every cell in your body.

When they crash:

  • You crash
  • Your energy dies
  • Your brain fog starts
  • Your mood drops

Most doctors completely ignore this system. We don't.

SYSTEM 6: Stress & Nervous System

Healing stops when stress is high.

Your nervous system holds:

  • Past trauma (even if you don't consciously remember it)
  • Chronic stress patterns
  • Burnout signatures
  • Emotional exhaustion

Women today live in survival mode. Our bodies are constantly in "fight or flight" when they should be in "rest and digest."

Functional Medicine brings the body back to safety.

SYSTEM 7: Structural / Movement System

Movement, muscles, fascia, breath, lymph flow.

Think of it as your body's communication highways — the physical pathways that everything else depends on.
